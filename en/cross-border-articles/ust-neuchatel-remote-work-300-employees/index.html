UST Neuchâtel: 300 employees in telework due to fire system malfunction

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 24 April 2026

Around 300 employees of the Federal Statistical Office in Neuchâtel are working remotely due to a malfunction in the fire system.

Staff were evacuated as a precautionary measure after a routine check last Friday revealed a malfunction in the fire safety systems. > "Unlike previous years, the audible alarms, automatic fire doors, and luminous signage were not functioning," explains Xavier Studer, spokesperson for the Swiss Federal Statistical Office (UST) for French-speaking Switzerland, to RTS's La Matinale. The UST immediately informed staff that they would need to stay home for safety reasons. The cause of the fault, known since Tuesday, is due to the failure of an 'electronic component' that controls the fire safety systems. ### Evacuation procedure and measures taken Precautionary evacuation of a building housing a federal service is not common.
