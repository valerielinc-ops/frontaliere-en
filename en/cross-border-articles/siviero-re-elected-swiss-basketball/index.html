The former president of Union Neuchâtel received 31 votes against 24 for his predecessor, Giancarlo Sergi. The vote took place during the federation's general assembly in Macolin. Siviero will remain in office for a four-year term. Despite his victory, Siviero had a complicated day: the 40-franc increase in licenses, which he supported, was rejected by the delegates, as was the budget.
