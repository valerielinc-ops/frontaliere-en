In brief - Sun Valley Festival in Malvaglia from May 22 to 24, 2026 - International artists and activities for all ages - Camping and food area available ## Key facts - What: Sun Valley Festival - When: May 22-24, 2026 - Where: Malvaglia, Canton Ticino - Who: Organized by Asilo Beach of Malvaglia - Tickets: Available for presale on ticketino.com The Sun Valley Festival returns to Malvaglia from May 22 to 24, 2026, promising three days of music and fun in the picturesque Boschetto area. Organized by the Asilo Beach association of Malvaglia, the event offers a unique opportunity to enjoy live performances and activities for all ages. The opening night will feature performances by Giovani Wannabe, Andrea Cerrato, Rumatera, Sgaffy, and DJ Feed, while on Saturday, May 23, the festival will kick into high gear with an explosive lineup including Dan and his brothers, Klischée, The Rockstar Show, DJ Gollum, Molella, and Edmmaro. On Sunday, the grand finale with Uncle Bard & The Dirty Bastards, The Rumpled, Simon & Mac, Ludwig, Babalos, and Gianni Tunga & Ciucio Jr. In addition to music, the festival will offer activities such as motorinata, children's party, and helicopter sightseeing flights. For those who want to fully experience the event, a camping area and a food area with numerous food trucks will be available. Free parking and nighttime shuttles to Biasca, Bellinzona, Faido, and Olivone will be provided for participants. Tickets are available for presale on ticketino.com and at the box office in the evening at a higher price, subject to prior sell-out. ### Program and activities The Sun Valley Festival is not just about music.
