Swiss Voice Tour returns to Tenero: singing contest at the mall (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 5 May 2026

From May 12 to 16, 2026, young talents and adults will compete to reach the finals of the contest, with Paolo Meneguzzi as a judge.

Contesto

In brief - Swiss Voice Tour returns to Tenero from May 12 to 16, 2026 - Two categories: youth up to 16 years and adults - Final on Saturday, May 16 at 14:00 ## Key facts - What: Swiss Voice Tour, singing competition - When: May 12 to 16, 2026 - Where: Tenero Shopping Center - Who: Aspiring singers and jury with Paolo Meneguzzi - Fee: Not specified Ticino is preparing to host the Swiss Voice Tour again, the largest singing casting in Switzerland. The event, which will take place at the Tenero Shopping Center from May 12 to 16, 2026, promises to be a must-attend for music lovers. Young talents and adults will have the opportunity to showcase themselves in front of an exceptional jury, aiming to secure a spot in the final stages of the competition. ### Two categories for all ages The competition is divided into two categories: one for youth up to 16 years and one for adults. Registrations are already open online at swissvoicetour.ch. The highlight will be the final on Saturday, May 16, scheduled for 14:00. Among the judges will be Paolo Meneguzzi, a well-known Swiss singer-songwriter. The event will be opened by a brief showcase from Luis Landrini, while the hosting will be entrusted to Riccardo Medri, a speaker from Radio Ticino. ### A parallel competition with prizes at stake In parallel, from May 5 to 16, a special competition with numerous prizes will also be active. This parallel competition adds an extra incentive for participants and the audience, making the event even more engaging. ### Opportunities for cross-border workers For cross-border workers living in Italy and working in Ticino, the Swiss Voice Tour represents an excellent opportunity to participate in a national-level event.
