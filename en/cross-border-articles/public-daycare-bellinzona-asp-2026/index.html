Public daycare centers in Bellinzona: Asp supports the initiative

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 25 April 2026

The Association for the defense of public services supports the initiative for more public daycare centers in Bellinzona, highlighting the importance of social equity and gender equality.

In a statement, Asp emphasized that public daycare centers are not only a response to an educational need but represent an essential tool for social equity, inclusion, and the promotion of gender equality. ### The current situation in Bellinzona Currently, in Bellinzona, there are not enough daycare places, with a shortage of about 60 places according to recent studies. Dettagli operativi

Practical implications for Bellinzona families The initiative for more public daycare centers in Bellinzona has several practical implications for families. Firstly, the increase in the number of available places would reduce the waiting list for access to daycare centers, allowing more families to benefit from these essential services. ### Affordable fees and social justice One of the main advantages of public daycare centers is the possibility of offering affordable fees, calibrated according to family income. This means that even families with limited resources can access quality educational services for their children. On the contrary, private facilities can have high costs that not everyone can afford, creating inequalities. ### Quality and regulation Public daycare centers are subject to regulations and controls that guarantee the quality of the educational offer. This includes the training of staff and the respect for the needs of children, ensuring that the services offered are of high quality. Private facilities, on the other hand, may not be subject to the same standards, risking offering a less reliable service. ### Work-life balance The availability of public daycare centers allows parents to participate in the labor market with greater peace of mind. This is particularly important for gender equality, as often it is women who have to reduce or interrupt their work activity to take care of their children. With more public daycare centers, families can find a better balance between work and family life. ## What to do to support the initiative The Asp invites the citizens of Bellinzona to sign the communal popular initiative 'More public daycare centers'.
