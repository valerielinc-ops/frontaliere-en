The occasion is also to celebrate the new live album 'DAJE!', released on June 19, 2026, which collects the tracks recorded during the celebratory tour of the 40th anniversary of the album 'Cuore'. A tour that delighted over 300,000 spectators all over Italy. A unique opportunity for fans The Antonello Venditti concert in Lugano, scheduled for July 10th at Piazza della Riforma, represents a unique opportunity for fans to relive the songs that have marked the career of the Roman singer-songwriter. The choice of only seated places, with a maximum capacity of 5,000 spectators, was made to ensure a more intimate and engaging experience, allowing the audience to fully enjoy the performance. Tickets are already on sale on the Ticketcorner website, with prices ranging from CHF 65 for the side tribune to CHF 120 for the central tribune. For residents of the municipalities of Lugano, Paradiso, Collina d'Oro, and Grancia, a 10% discount on the standard rate is provided. ### The supporting band The band accompanying Venditti on stage is composed of highly talented and experienced musicians. Alessandro Canini on drums, Amedeo Bianchi on saxophone, Danilo Cherni on keyboards, Angelo Abate on piano and Hammond organ, and Roberta Palmigiani on violin. Each member of the band has collaborated extensively with Venditti, contributing to creating a unique and recognizable sound. For example, Canini has played with Venditti in over 300 concerts in the last ten years, while Palmigiani participated in the recording of four albums by the singer-songwriter. ### The importance of 'Cuore' and 'Notte prima degli esami' The albums 'Cuore' and 'Notte prima degli esami' are considered among the most important in Antonello Venditti's career. 'Cuore', released in 1976, contains songs that have become real generational anthems, such as 'Notte prima degli esami', 'Sora Rosa', and 'Compagno di scuola'.
