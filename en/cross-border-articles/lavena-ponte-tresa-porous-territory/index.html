The Mayor of Lavena Ponte Tresa, Massimo Mastromarino, describes the 'porous territory' as a place where the border has never been a wall, but a membrane through which people, goods, capitals, and cultures flow daily in both directions. This phenomenon has deep historical roots and is measured in tens of thousands of workers. Mayor Mastromarino expressed concern over the health tax, which risks undermining decades of cooperation between Ticino Canton and the province of Varese. 'The porous territory' is a place where the frontier has never been an obstacle, but a bridge between two countries. This is the message we want to convey to our citizens and our Swiss fellow citizens.
