The act was submitted by the Geneva lawyer Romain Jordan, who represents some families of the victims. The decision is motivated by the direct damage caused to the State's assets due to the significant resources mobilized by the National Civil Protection Service for medical, psychological, and logistical assistance to the nationals involved.
