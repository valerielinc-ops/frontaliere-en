Contesto

In brief - Spring Pride 2026 unites Saronno, Caronno, Ceriano, and Solaro - Events from May 12 to 17 for inclusion and rights - International Day against Homophobia, Biphobia, and Transphobia on May 17 ## Key facts - What: Spring Pride 2026 - When: May 12-17, 2026 - Where: Saronno, Caronno Pertusella, Ceriano Laghetto, Solaro - Who: Involved municipalities, citizens - Cost: Not yet specified Spring Pride 2026 was presented in Saronno, a shared project among several municipalities to promote inclusion, respect, and rights. The initiative will accompany the territory towards the International Day against Homophobia, Biphobia, and Transphobia on May 17. The presentation was attended by Saronno's mayor Ilaria Pagani, councilor Lucy Sasso, Caronno Pertusella's councilor Alessandro Giudici, and Solaro's councilor Stefania Marruchi. Spring Pride was born from the collaboration between the municipalities of Saronno, Caronno Pertusella, Ceriano Laghetto, and Solaro, within the inter-municipal table dedicated to Equal Opportunities. The goal is to build a shared path capable of involving citizens through widespread events, moments of confrontation, and social opportunities. ### Event program Saronno will play a central role in the calendar, with initiatives scheduled for May 12 and between Saturday, May 16, and Sunday, May 17, in various places in the city and in the involved municipalities. Cultural meetings, presentations, exhibitions, moments of aggregation, and evening appointments are planned, designed to involve different audiences and promote the active participation of the community.
