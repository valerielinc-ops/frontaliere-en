What is the trial of Luido Bernasconi?
The trial of Luido Bernasconi is a judicial proceeding against the former director of RSI Radiotelevisione svizzera, charged with embezzlement.
Where will the trial take place?
The trial will take place at the Lugano court.
What can cross-border workers do in relation to the trial?
Cross-border workers do not need to take any action in relation to the trial of Luido Bernasconi. However, it is important to stay informed about the developments of the trial and any potential implications for RSI Radiotelevisione svizzera.
