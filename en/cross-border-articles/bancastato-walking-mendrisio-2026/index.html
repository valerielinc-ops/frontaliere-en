Dettagli operativi

The BancaStato Walking Mendrisio 2026 event is a unique opportunity to spend a day outdoors, surrounded by the beauty of the Ticino landscape. The routes have been designed to offer different experiences, suitable for all levels of physical preparation. The Family route, approximately 5 km long, is ideal for those who want to take a relaxing walk, with a mere 150 meters of elevation gain. The Salorino route, which winds for 12 km with a 400-meter elevation gain, is perfect for those seeking a more challenging experience, crossing the picturesque forests of Balerna and Salorino. The Castello route, with its 8 km and a 250-meter elevation gain, represents a good compromise for those who want to enjoy the landscape without overexerting themselves, passing near the Castello di Mendrisio. ### Walk&Dog: a unique experience for animal lovers The Walk&Dog category is a novelty that allows participants to bring their four-legged friends with them. This route is valid on all trails and includes a bib with the owner's and dog's names and a commemorative bowl. Registrations for this category are open until April 22, and the cost is 20 CHF for adults and 10 CHF for dogs. It is important to remember that dogs must be kept on a leash and have a microchip, as required by cantonal regulations. ### How to participate To participate in the event, you can register online at www.walkingmendrisio.ch by April 22 or on-site on Saturday, April 25 (2:30 PM - 5:00 PM) and Sunday, April 26 (from 8:00 AM). The event is open to everyone and represents an opportunity to spend a day in good company, focused on sports and fun. To participate in BancaStato Walking Mendrisio 2026, follow these steps: 1. Register online: Visit the website www.walkingmendrisio.ch and complete the registration process by April 22. The online registration fee is CHF 25 until March 31, and CHF 30 from April 1 to April 22. Payments can be made by credit card or PayPal. 2. Register on-site: If you prefer, you can register on Saturday, April 25, from 14.30 to 17.00 at the Mendrisio Exhibition Center or on Sunday, April 26, from 8.00 at the start of the routes. The on-site registration fee is CHF 35. 3. Choose your route: Choose from the three available routes based on your physical preparation and interests. The 10 km 'Panorama' route passes through the municipalities of Mendrisio, Morbio Inferiore, and Coldrerio, offering breathtaking views of the Alps. The 15 km 'Adventure' route includes a section through the Balerna forest and an ascent to Monte San Giorgio, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The 20 km 'Exploration' route passes through the municipalities of Mendrisio, Novazzano, Chiasso, and Morcote, with a stretch along Lake Lugano. 4. Join the warm-up: Before each start, join the warm-up to prepare for the walk. The warm-ups are led by qualified instructors and take place 30 minutes before each start. 5. Enjoy the event: Participate in the walk, visit the stands and exhibitors, and enjoy the lunch provided. The lunch includes traditional Ticino dishes such as cheese risotto, polenta, and bresaola, as well as vegetarian and vegan options. ### Use our calculator to plan your participation To help you plan your participation, use our calculator to estimate the time needed to complete your chosen route.
