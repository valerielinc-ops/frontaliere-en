Antiques Fair in Mendrisio: 160 exhibitors and over 10,000 visitors (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 7 May 2026

The 42nd edition of the Antiques, Art and Collectibles Fair will be held on Sunday, May 10th in Mendrisio, with over 160 exhibitors and a wide range of art and collectibles on offer

In brief - 42nd edition of the Antiques Fair in Mendrisio - Over 160 exhibitors from Switzerland and Italy - Over 10,000 visitors expected ## Key facts - What: Antiques, Art, and Collectibles Fair - When: Sunday, May 10, 2026, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM - Where: Historic center of Mendrisio - Who: Organized by a local team - Scale: Over 160 exhibitors and 10,000 visitors expected The 42nd edition of the Antiques, Art, and Collectibles Fair will take place on Sunday, May 10, 2026, in Mendrisio. The event, which runs from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, is one of the most significant in the sector, attracting over 10,000 visitors each year. This year, over 160 exhibitors from across Switzerland and Italy will bring the town's streets to life with a wide range of art and collectibles. ### A growing event "A result that highlights the growing interest in an event capable of evolving over time alongside the world of collecting," explain the organizers. In recent decades, art and antiques have expanded their boundaries to include new trends and attractive sectors, from mid-century modern to 20th-century design, to the phenomenon of Pokémon, now an integral part of contemporary collecting. ### What to find at the fair Alongside these, you'll find historic toys like porcelain dolls and Barbies, model kits, vintage electronics related to audio, video, and gaming, as well as coins, stamps, rare books, and curiosities from around the world. The displayed works tell the traditions and stories of their places of origin, giving each object a unique value. Among the stalls, you'll also find silverware, jewelry, glass, porcelain, vinyl records, books, sculptures, folk art, textiles, furniture, extra-European art, toys, iron objects, 20th-century design, and paintings from the 16th to the 20th c...

Dettagli operativi

Implications for cross-border workers The Mendrisio Antiques Fair represents a unique opportunity for cross-border workers living and working in Ticino. The event not only offers a wide range of art and collectibles but also provides an occasion to discover the cultural and historical richness of the region. For cross-border workers who are passionate about antiques, the fair is an opportunity to find unique and rare pieces that might not be available elsewhere. ### What to bring to the fair For those who decide to participate in the fair, it is advisable to bring with you a list of what you are looking for, a notepad to note the details of the items of interest and, of course, some cash. Many exhibitors might accept credit card payments, but it is always better to be prepared. Additionally, it is useful to bring a bag or a suitcase to transport any purchases. ### How to get to the fair Mendrisio is easily reachable by car, train, or bus. For those arriving by car, there are parking lots available near the historic center. Those who prefer public transport can take the train or bus to Mendrisio station and then walk to the fair. The central location of the fair makes it easily accessible from anywhere in Ticino. ### Tips for visitors For those attending the fair, it is advisable to arrive early to have more time to explore all the stands. It is also a good idea to take a lunch break at one of the local restaurants to enjoy Ticinese cuisine. Finally, don't forget to bring a bottle of water and wear comfortable shoes, as there will be a lot of walking. ### An event not to be missed The Mendrisio Antiques Fair is a must-attend event for anyone interested in the world of antiques and collectibles.
