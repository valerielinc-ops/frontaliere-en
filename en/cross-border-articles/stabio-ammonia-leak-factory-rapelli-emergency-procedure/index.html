Frequently Asked Questions
What are the symptoms of ammonia exposure?
Symptoms include eye burning, respiratory irritation, coughing, nausea, and in severe cases, breathing difficulties. If these symptoms occur, it is essential to leave the contaminated area immediately and contact the emergency services at 144.
How long will the ammonia leak alert in Stabio last?
The alert was issued on Thursday, April 16, 2026, and was managed by authorities by 11:30 PM the same day. However, no official confirmation has yet been provided regarding when the alert will be officially lifted or the area declared safe.
Can I cross the Gaggiolo border crossing during the emergency?
As of now, no closures or restrictions have been reported at the Gaggiolo border crossing. However, it is advisable to check official updates on the Ticino Civil Protection website before traveling.
What should a company do to prevent ammonia leaks?
A company must ensure regular maintenance of refrigeration systems, install automatic leak detection systems, and constantly update its emergency plans in collaboration with local authorities.
Where can I find official information about the incident?
Official updates are published on the [Ticino Civil Protection](nav:permits) website and the official social media channels of the cantonal authorities. Real-time updates can also be found through the Ticino Cantonal Police communications.
