Works on the Italian railway network: here's what changes for TILO (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 March 2026

From April 3rd, TILO S30 connections will undergo significant changes in circulation due to infrastructure works on the railway line between Pino Tronzano and Luino.

TILO S30 Train Services to Change Due to Italian Railway Network Works

Dettagli operativi

The changes to the TILO S30 connections will be in effect until December 12, 2026. Passengers will be able to use the substitute buses between Cadenazzo and Luino. It is recommended to check the updated schedules before departing. The RFI (Italian Railway Network) works are necessary to improve the Italian rail network and ensure its safety. The TILO connections are essential for the mobility of border workers and commuters traveling between Ticino and Lombardy. The TILO, the company that manages the rail connections between Switzerland and Italy, is working to minimize the inconveniences for passengers. The RFI works are part of a broader investment plan, which provides for interventions on the Italian rail network for a total amount of over 10 billion euros by 2025. This plan was defined in 2019 and also provides for the creation of new high-speed rail connections. For Ticinese commuters, the TILO connections are fundamental. For example, every day over 1,000 people travel from Bellinzona to Milan for work or study. The modification of the schedules and the replacement of trains with buses will have a significant impact on these commuters. The TILO has planned a bus substitution plan to ensure the continuity of service between Cadenazzo and Luino. Here are some useful information for passengers: - Updated schedules: it is possible to consult the updated schedules on the TILO website or on that of RFI. - Substitute buses: the substitute buses will be active between Cadenazzo and Luino, with a frequency of approximately 30 minutes. - Tickets: tickets for the substitute buses can be purchased online or on board. ⚠️ It is essential to note that the RFI works will not be completed by the end of 2026. Frequently Asked Questions
When will the works on the Italian railway network that will affect the TILO S30 connections begin?
Work will begin on Friday, April 3, 2024.
How much will it cost to use the replacement buses for the Cadenazzo-Luino route during the works?
Tickets for replacement buses are the same price as for TILO S30 train tickets. Prices start at €3.50 for a single journey, with day (€8.50) and monthly (€120) ticket options available online or on board.
Can I use my TILO travel book on substitution buses between Cadenazzo and Luino?
No, TILO travel booklets are not valid on replacement buses. For these services, you need to buy a specific ticket, which can be purchased online or at stations. Standard TILO tickets remain valid only for regular trains.
What are the public transport alternatives between Ticino and Lombardy during the works?
During the works, in addition to the TILO replacement buses, you can use the RegioExpress (RE) trains from Lugano to Varese, with a change in Mendrisio. Alternatively, there are scheduled buses (e.g., line 1 from Lugano to Como) or organized carpooling services.
How is luggage handled when travelling with replacement buses?
Luggage is handled in the same way as on trains: you can take it with you in the cabin without weight limits, as long as it does not take up space for other passengers. There are no specific luggage storage services on replacement buses, so it is recommended to travel light.
