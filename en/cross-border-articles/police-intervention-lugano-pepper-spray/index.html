Clash and police intervention with pepper spray in central Lugano

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

Two groups of young people clashed in central Lugano, leading to police intervention with pepper spray. Two people injured.

In brief - Two groups of young people clashed in central Lugano - Police intervention with pepper spray - Two people injured and rescued - 15 officers involved from Cantonal Police, Lugano City Police, and Ceresio Nord Police - Estimated medical expenses at CHF 2,000 for the injured ## Key facts - What: Clash between two groups of young people - When: Saturday, May 2, 2026, shortly after 22:30 - Where: Center of Lugano, Botta bus stop area - Who: Cantonal Police, Lugano City Police, Ceresio Nord Police - Amount: Two people injured, 15 officers involved - Regulation: Art. 234 of the Swiss Penal Code (simple injuries) The evening of Saturday, May 2, 2026, was eventful for the officers on duty in Lugano. Shortly after 22:30, two groups of young people clashed in the city center, necessitating a massive police intervention that had to use pepper spray. According to the first information gathered from witnesses, the two groups would be linked to the fan bases of Ticino teams. A first clash would have occurred in the afternoon in the Cassarate area; subsequently, the two groups would have met again in the evening, on the sidelines of the May Day concert, where the second episode of violence would have initially broken out in a bar in the lakeside area. During the morning, the cantonal police issued a statement stating that, together with the Lugano City Police, “based on a preliminary reconstruction and for reasons that will have to be established, the situation deteriorated, causing minor injuries to two people, who were promptly rescued.” On the scene, officers from the Lugano City Police intervened, subsequently supported by the Cantonal Police and the Ceresio Nord Police, as well as the rescuers from the Lugano Croce Verde.
