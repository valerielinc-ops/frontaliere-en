The decision concerns the Mountain House Madonna delle Nevi, located in Nante. The appeal was deemed unfounded, thus confirming the validity of the Municipal Council's vote. The State Council, chaired by Lorenzo Ombelli, declared that everything is in order and that there are no grounds to annul the decision taken by the Legislature of Monteceneri. The Mountain House Madonna delle Nevi will therefore remain operational according to the provisions established by the Municipal Council. This decision is important for the community of Monteceneri, as the Mountain House is a reference point for mountain and tourist activities in the area.
