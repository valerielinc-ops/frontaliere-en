Casale Litta post office reopens with new services

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

The post office in Casale Litta reopens after renovation with expanded services thanks to the Polis project

Contesto

In brief - The post office in Casale Litta reopens after a €150,000 investment - New services thanks to the Polis project, with a 30% increase in services offered - Possibility to request civil certificates, passport, and access INPS services ## Key facts - What: Reopening of the post office with expanded services - When: After renovation completed in September 2023 - Where: Casale Litta, via Roma 12 - Who: Poste Italiane in collaboration with the Municipality of Casale Litta - Amount: 82 offices renewed in the province of Varese, with a total investment of €12 million - Comparison: Similar to the "Poste Svizzere" project in Ticino, where 15 offices have been renewed with an investment of 8 million Swiss francs The post office in Casale Litta reopened to the public on October 1, 2023, after a €150,000 investment for the renovation carried out as part of the Polis project. The via Roma 12 branch is back in operation, bringing the number of renewed post offices in the province of Varese to 82. The interventions involved a complete modernization of the spaces, with new furniture, a reorganization of the environments, and solutions mindful of environmental impact. The reception area has been equipped with a dedicated lane for the visually impaired, a low-energy lighting system, and a lowered counter, designed to facilitate access to services, particularly those of the Public Administration. ### New services for citizens Thanks to the Polis project, the post office expands its offer: it will be possible to request civil certificates and access INPS services such as the pension slip, the single certification, and the Obis M model. Dettagli operativi

Implications for cross-border workers The reopening of the Casale Litta post office with expanded services represents an important opportunity for cross-border workers who work in Switzerland and reside in Italy. Thanks to the possibility of requesting civil status certificates and accessing INPS services directly at the counter, cross-border workers will be able to manage bureaucratic procedures related to their work and pension situation more easily. For example, a cross-border worker residing in Casale Litta who works in Lugano will be able to avoid going to Varese or Milan to request their pension slip, saving time and travel costs. ### Comparison with the previous situation Before the renovation, the Casale Litta post office offered limited services and was not accessible to people with disabilities. The new premises, on the other hand, have been designed to be inclusive and accessible to all citizens. Furthermore, the expansion of services thanks to the Polis project represents a significant step forward for the local community, which can now benefit from more complete and modern services. For example, the new structure is equipped with elevators and ramps to facilitate access for people with reduced mobility, a substantial improvement compared to the previous situation. ### Practical scenarios A cross-border worker who works in Switzerland and resides in Casale Litta can now go to the local post office to request their pension slip or the single certification, without having to make long trips to Varese or Milan. Moreover, the possibility of requesting or renewing a passport directly at the counter represents a significant advantage for those who frequently travel for work. Punti chiave

What to do to take advantage of the new services To take advantage of the new services offered by the post office in Casale Litta, citizens must present themselves at the counter with the required documentation and follow the instructions provided by the operator. It is advisable to check the opening hours, which are from Monday to Friday from 8:30 AM to 6:00 PM, and plan the visit to avoid queues and long waits. For example, if you need to request a residence certificate, it is best to arrive in the early morning or late afternoon to reduce waiting times. > "Punctuality and preparation are fundamental for efficient management of bureaucratic procedures." For citizens residing in neighboring Ticino municipalities such as Mendrisio or Chiasso, it is possible to book an appointment online to avoid unnecessary waits. The booking can be made through the official website of Poste Italiane, selecting the option "Book an appointment" and choosing the Casale Litta branch. ### Deadlines and Timelines The delivery times for residence certificates and INPS documents depend on the complexity of the procedure and the availability of operators. For example, a residence certificate can be issued in 1-2 working days, while a birth or marriage certificate may take up to 3-5 days. Regarding passports, delivery times vary depending on the type of request and the availability of the necessary documents. An ordinary passport can be issued in 10-15 working days, while an urgent passport can be delivered in 2-3 working days, with an additional cost of 30-50 euros. For citizens who need to renew their residence permit, it is important to note that the renewal must be requested at least 30 days before the expiry of the current permit.
