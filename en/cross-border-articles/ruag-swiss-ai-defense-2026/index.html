RUAG and Giotto.AI create Swiss AI for military data

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

RUAG is collaborating with Giotto.AI to develop a national AI for sensitive data, presenting LLARA in Thun.

Contesto

In brief - RUAG collaborates with Giotto.AI to develop a national AI for defense - LLARA, developed internally, is a virtual assistant for sensitive military data - Swiss AI operates offline, ensuring security and technological sovereignty - Project presented in Thun, with potential application in Ticino for local defense ## Key facts - What: Development of a national AI for defense - When: Recently presented, with developments expected by 2025 - Where: Thun, RUAG headquarters, with potential implementation in Ticino - Who: RUAG and Giotto.AI, with the support of the Federal Department of Defense - Budget: Estimated between 5 and 10 million Swiss francs RUAG, a Swiss leader in the defense sector, has announced a collaboration with the Lausanne-based startup Giotto.AI to create a national artificial intelligence capable of securely processing sensitive military data offline. This project was recently presented in Thun, at the RUAG headquarters, where employees were shown 'LLARA,' an internally developed conversational AI tool. Stephan Hirth, Vice President of Communications and Secure Sensors at RUAG, stated that AI will play a crucial role in the defense sector, bringing significant technological changes. ### Concrete applications Hirth envisions the use of LLARA in advanced military contexts, such as interacting with chatbots in tanks. Aldo Podestà, CEO of Giotto.AI, emphasized the importance of technological sovereignty, stating that complete control over AI is fundamental for a country. What is the main goal of the RUAG and Giotto.AI project?
The main goal is to develop a national artificial intelligence capable of processing sensitive military data securely and offline, ensuring Switzerland's technological sovereignty.
What is LLARA and how will it be used?
LLARA is a conversational artificial intelligence tool developed internally by RUAG. It will be used to support technological advancements in Swiss defense, with potential applications in advanced military contexts.
What are the advantages of the AI developed by Giotto.AI?
The AI developed by Giotto.AI is designed to operate in isolation, without an internet connection, consuming less data and ensuring complete control over the AI, which is fundamental for Swiss defense.
