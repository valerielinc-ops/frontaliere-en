Geopolitics and unions: new global balances

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

The general council of Fnp Cisl dei Laghi in Varese analyzes the impact of international tensions on work, pensions, and welfare.

In brief - The Fnp Cisl dei Laghi discusses the geopolitical impact on work and pensions - Europe must strengthen cohesion to face global crises - International tensions directly influence welfare and the healthcare system ## Key facts - What: Impact of geopolitical tensions on daily life - When: 29 April 2026 - Where: Varese - Who: Fnp Cisl dei Laghi - Amount: Not yet specified Geopolitics is no longer a distant matter, confined to international summits: it enters homes through the cost of energy, pensions eroded by inflation, and the resilience of the healthcare system. It is from this awareness that the general council of Fnp Cisl dei Laghi, convened in Varese, began its analysis of global scenarios and their concrete repercussions on local areas. Opening the proceedings was the general secretary Dario Grilanda, who highlighted the weight of ongoing transformations. From the war in Ukraine to energy security, to new challenges related to digital sovereignty and artificial intelligence. These are topics that increasingly impact social policies. The international landscape was outlined by journalist Marta Ottaviani, who described a global system that is now multipolar, marked by competition among great powers and the growing role of intermediate actors. Raw materials, migrations, trade routes, and demographics are the drivers of profound change. Hence the warning to Europe: strengthen its cohesion, because 'divided, we will get nowhere.' ### Responsibility and civil coexistence Central to the debate was also the intervention of the general secretary of Fnp Cisl, Roberto Pezzani, who emphasized the theme of responsibility. Impact on Pensions and Welfare Geopolitical tensions directly impact pensions and welfare, eroding the purchasing power of pension benefits due to inflation. This phenomenon is particularly evident in Italy, where high public debt limits the government's ability to mitigate the negative effects. ### Energy Security and Industrial Transformations Energy security is another crucial topic discussed during the general council. Energy crises directly influence the cost of living and access to resources, with significant repercussions on the labor market and citizens' living conditions. Industrial transformations, linked to digitalization and artificial intelligence, require a reform of the educational and training system to prepare new generations for a continuously evolving labor market. ### The Role of the Union The union plays a fundamental role in mediating between workers' needs and global challenges. Fnp Cisl dei Laghi is committed to representing workers and promoting social policies that guarantee equity and protection. This includes defending workers' rights, promoting sustainable welfare policies, and fighting against inequalities. ### Future Scenarios Future scenarios depend on Europe's ability to strengthen its cohesion and adopt common policies. A more integrated Europe can better face global crises and ensure adequate social protection for its citizens.
