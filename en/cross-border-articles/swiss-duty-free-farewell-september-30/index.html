Swiss to end duty-free sales from September 30, 2026

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

Swiss airline will discontinue its in-flight duty-free sales service starting September 30, 2026.

Contesto

In brief - Swiss to remove duty-free onboard from September 30, 2026 - Offer will remain available online via Miles & More - Decision follows analysis revealing a decrease in inflight purchases ## Key facts - What: Removal of onboard duty-free service - When: From September 30, 2026 - Where: On all Swiss flights - Who: Swiss International Air Lines - Amount: Not specified Swiss International Air Lines has announced that it will remove the onboard duty-free sales service starting from September 30, 2026. The decision was made following an analysis that revealed a decrease in inflight purchases. The current onboard offer includes items such as watches, jewelry, perfumes, cosmetic products, and various accessories. These products will still be available online on the Miles & More portal. The airline is following in the footsteps of its parent company Lufthansa, which already removed inflight duty-free services last September. Swiss's decision was communicated through an official announcement released on Wednesday. ### Implications for passengers Passengers who are used to shopping during Swiss flights will have to adapt to this new reality. The removal of onboard duty-free could influence the purchasing habits of frequent travelers with this airline. However, the option to buy duty-free products online via Miles & More remains a valid alternative. ### Comparison with Lufthansa Lufthansa has already implemented this decision a year ago, and now Swiss is following the same path. This indicates a trend in the aviation sector towards reducing onboard services, likely due to decreased demand. Frequently Asked Questions
What happens if I have already purchased duty-free products before September 30, 2026?
Purchases made before September 30, 2026 will not be affected by Swiss's decision. You can continue to use the products purchased as intended.
Can I still buy duty-free products online after September 30, 2026?
Yes, duty-free products will still be available online on the Miles & More portal after September 30, 2026.
What are the alternatives for purchasing duty-free products?
In addition to the Miles & More portal, you can purchase duty-free products at the airport or in local stores that offer duty-free services.
