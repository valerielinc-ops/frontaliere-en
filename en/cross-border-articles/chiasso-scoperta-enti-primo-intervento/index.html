Chiasso: A Day of Discovery with First Response Agencies The "Mission Emergency" event represents a unique opportunity for the public to discover the skills and activities of first response agencies in Chiasso. This type of initiative is fundamental to increasing awareness and community participation in case of emergencies. The agencies involved include the Cantonal Police, Chiasso Municipal Police, the Federal Customs and Border Security Office, the Mendrisiotto Cantonal Fire and Rescue Center, the FFS Firefighters, the Mendrisiotto Ambulance Service, the Mendrisiotto Civil Protection, Rega, and the Swiss Alpine Rescue. Participation in the demonstration stands will allow citizens to see the skills of the operators in action and better understand the intervention procedures in case of emergency. This type of event is particularly useful for young people who might be interested in working in these sectors. Furthermore, the initiative offers the opportunity to strengthen the relationship between institutions and citizens, promoting greater collaboration and mutual understanding. ### Concrete Examples and Real Numbers During the event, over 500 people are expected to participate in the demonstration stands. Emergency operators will be ready to showcase their skills in practical scenarios, such as managing a road accident with injuries, responding to a fire in a residential building, or managing a flood. 📊 For example, the Mendrisiotto Cantonal Fire and Rescue Center recently participated in an emergency exercise in Bellinzona, where they encountered a number of injured participants.
