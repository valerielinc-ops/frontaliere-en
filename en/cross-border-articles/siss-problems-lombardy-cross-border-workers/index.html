PD regional councilor Samuele Astuti criticizes the Lombardy government for the malfunctions of the socio-health information system

To denounce this is Samuele Astuti, a PD regional councilor from Varese, who attacked the majority during the session dedicated to inspection acts. 'A truth that we have been denouncing for a long time – continues Astuti – with formal acts, interrogations and punctual reports, which however remained unheard by the Lombardy government.' Astuti's words come after some disservices that occurred in the past few weeks, with consequent 'tilts' of the platform. ### Implications for cross-border workers The malfunctions of the SISS can have significant repercussions for cross-border workers who work in Lombardy and reside in Ticino. Slowdowns and blockages of the system can cause delays in healthcare procedures, complicating the daily life of those who move daily between the two territories. Moreover, the quality of the regional healthcare service is put at risk, with possible consequences on the health and well-being of cross-border workers. Astuti underlined that 'acknowledging the problem is not enough and it is serious that so much time has been lost without intervening.' Dettagli operativi

Analysis of Critical Issues The Socio-health Information System (SISS) of the Lombardy Region is a crucial element for managing healthcare services in the region. However, chronic malfunctions of the system are causing serious inconveniences to citizens and healthcare operators. Samuele Astuti, a Varese regional councilor from the PD, has denounced that 'slowdowns, blockages, inefficiencies that put at risk the quality of the regional healthcare service.' ### Impact on Cross-border Workers Cross-border workers who work in Lombardy and reside in Ticino are particularly affected by these malfunctions. The quality of healthcare services is fundamental for those who commute daily between the two territories. Slowdowns and blockages of the SISS can cause delays in healthcare procedures, complicating the daily lives of cross-border workers. ### Comparison with Other Healthcare Systems The SISS of the Lombardy Region is not the only socio-health information system in Italy. However, the chronic problems of the Lombard system distinguish it negatively compared to other regions. For example, the socio-health information system of the Canton Ticino is known for its efficiency and reliability. This comparison highlights the need for urgent intervention to improve the Lombard SISS. ### Possible Solutions To solve the problems of the SISS, the Lombardy Region could consider various solutions. First of all, it is essential to invest in the maintenance and updating of the system. Furthermore, it could be useful to implement advanced technological solutions to improve the efficiency and security of the system. What are the main issues with the SISS of Regione Lombardia?
