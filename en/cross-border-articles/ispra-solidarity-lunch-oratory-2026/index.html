Ispra: success at the second solidarity lunch in the oratory

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

Great turnout in Ispra for the solidarity lunch organized by the Pastoral Community and the Association of Ispra Volunteers.

Contesto

In brief - High turnout at the second solidarity lunch in Ispra - Organized by the Pastoral Community and Ispra Volunteers Association - Risotto prepared by volunteer chefs ## Key facts - What: Second solidarity lunch - When: Sunday, April 26, 2026 - Where: Ispra Oratory - Who: Pastoral Community "Blessed of the Cross" and Ispra Volunteers Association - Amount: Not specified The hall of the Ispra Oratory quickly filled up on Sunday, April 26, for the second appointment with the solidarity lunch dedicated to lonely people from Ispra and nearby towns. The initiative, organized by the Pastoral Community "Blessed of the Cross" and the Ispra Volunteers Association, confirmed the strong desire for sharing and closeness among citizens. The hall welcomed numerous people from Ispra and Cadrezzate with Osmate, creating a festive atmosphere, just like a big family. The absolute star of the table was an excellent risotto, carefully prepared by expert volunteer chefs, which warmed not only the palate but also the hearts of the diners. "A moment of celebration to be lived together – say the organizers – to break loneliness and feel part of a big family. A special thanks goes to all the volunteers from Ispra and Cadrezzate with Osmate who, with their commitment, made this wonderful day of solidarity possible." ### The importance of solidarity Solidarity and sharing are fundamental values that can make a difference in people's lives.
