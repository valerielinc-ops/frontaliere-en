In Brief - Varese Corsi Net Records 6,327 Enrollments in 2026 - 634 Courses Activated with 281 Teachers Across 52 Venues - New Courses: Flight Simulations and Vocal Fitness ## Key Facts - What: Presentation of New Varese Corsi Net Courses - When: May 5, 2026 - Where: Spazio Libero di Varese News - Who: Faculty, Institutions, Sponsors, and Businesses - Enrollments: 6,327 Varese Corsi Net has unveiled the new horizons of its project combining training, culture, and social aspects. The May 5, 2026, meeting brought together faculty, institutions, and businesses at Spazio Libero di Varese News, which has hosted part of the courses since 2025. The project, active since 1984, recorded 6,327 enrollments in the current season, with 281 teachers distributed across 52 venues in the province. Marcello Vitella of Yop Comunicazione outlined the year's progress, highlighting growth in the under-30 age group thanks to collaboration with the University of Insubria. Among the new courses presented for the next term are flight simulations and the 'Vocal Fitness' method proposed by Steve Depper for learning English phonetics. ### New Courses and Synergies The training plan aims to promote the culture of psychophysical well-being through proposals ranging from risotto-making to pilates, up to first aid courses. The meeting gave voice to various territorial realities, such as the Centro Gulliver, which will open its communities for therapeutic sports paths. Another example of integration between profit and non-profit comes from the Orti di Bregazzana, where cooking courses for parents and children are held to strengthen relationships through manual work.
