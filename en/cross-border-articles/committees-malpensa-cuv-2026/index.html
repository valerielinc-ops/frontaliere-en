Committees and environmental associations around Malpensa request a new CUV assembly for 2026

Contesto

TL;DR - Malpensa committees request CUV assembly for 2026 - Environmental and social issues at Malpensa airport - Nine municipalities affected by airport's impact ## Key facts - Cosa: Convocazione dell'assemblea CUV per il 2026 - Quando: 2026 - Dove: Malpensa, Cardano al Campo, Arsago Seprio, Somma Lombardo - Chi: Comitati e associazioni ambientali - Problemi: Aumento del traffico aereo, impatto ambientale, crescita della popolazione - Scadenza: 2026 Committees and environmental associations around Malpensa, including the municipalities of Cardano al Campo, Arsago Seprio, and Somma Lombardo, have sent a letter to the mayor of Cardano al Campo and president of the CUV, Lorenzo Aspesi, requesting a new convocation of the CUV assembly for 2026. The letter, published on Varese News, highlights the environmental and social problems related to the Malpensa airport operation and requests action to be taken for the annual assembly.

Dettagli operativi

This is the second year in which the committees request the convocation of the CUV assembly, which is the organization that brings together the nine municipalities most directly affected by the airport's impact. Punti chiave

The convocation of the CUV assembly for 2026 is an important step to discuss the problems related to the Malpensa airport operation and to find solutions that can satisfy the various interests involved. The committees and environmental associations around Malpensa are ready to work to find a meeting point and to convocate the CUV assembly for 2026. The problems related to the Malpensa airport operation are significant, including the increase in air traffic and the associated noise, the impact on the local environment, particularly on water and biodiversity, the growth of the population and economic activity in the area, and the need to expand airport infrastructure and reorganize local mobility. What is the main objective of the meeting of the CUV for 2026?
The main objective is to discuss the issues related to the airport operation and find solutions that can meet the different interests involved, such as reducing environmental impacts, the safety of passengers and workers and improving the quality of life for the inhabitants of neighboring municipalities.
