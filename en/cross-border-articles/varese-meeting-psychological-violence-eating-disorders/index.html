Varese: event on psychological abuse and eating disorders (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

On Friday, May 8 in Varese, an event to reflect on the link between psychological mistreatment and eating difficulties.

Contesto

In brief Public meeting on psychological violence and eating disorders in Varese Organized by Anemos Italy ODV and other local associations Featuring talks by experts and sector professionals ## Key facts - What: Public event "The Invisible Weight" - When: Friday, May 8, 2026, at 8:30 PM - Where: Sala Montanari, Varese - Who: Anemos Italy ODV, Lampi Blu, Ananke Family ETS Villa Miralago, Varese Inter-Agency Anti-Violence Network - Entry: Free admission until capacity is reached On Friday, May 8, 2026, at 8:30 PM, the public event "The Invisible Weight – When Psychological Violence Becomes Eating Disorder" will take place at Sala Montanari in Varese. The event is promoted by Anemos Italy ODV in collaboration with Lampi Blu, Ananke Family ETS Villa Miralago, and the Varese Inter-Agency Anti-Violence Network. The goal is to explore the connection between psychological abuse, emotional fragility, and eating behavior disorders. During the evening, professionals from various fields will speak, including Giorgia Carabelli, a nutritionist biologist; Nicoletta Manenti, a psychologist and psychotherapist; Maurizio Parisi, a general practitioner; Cinzia Fumagalli, president of Ananke Family APS; and Martina Minari, schools coordinator for Lampi Blu. The session will be moderated by Elena Bertolin, a legal consultant and relational coach. "Talking about psychological violence means giving voice to wounds that are often unseen but deeply affect mental and physical health," explains Anna Marsella, president of Anemos Italy. "This meeting aims to be a moment of listening, awareness, and prevention."
