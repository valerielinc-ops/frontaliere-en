Four gas stations in trouble: displayed prices different from actual ones (cross-border guide)

The Guardia di Finanza of Como discovered irregularities in four fuel distributors in Ticino. Here's what changes for cross-border workers.

Among the irregularities found are the discrepancy between the displayed prices and those actually applied, the failure to visibly display the differential between 'served' and 'self' prices, and two omissions in communicating price changes to the Ministry. Analysis of Irregularities The irregularities found in the four fuel distributors in Ticino were identified during intensified checks by the Guardia di Finanza of Como. The discrepancy between the displayed prices and those applied can indeed influence the cost of fuel, a crucial element for those who have to bear high transportation costs. Furthermore, the failure to communicate price changes to the Ministry can result in a lack of transparency that penalizes consumers. ### Authorities' Actions The Guardia di Finanza of Como has intensified checks on fuel distributors in the territory in response to recent price increases recorded at the national level and linked to international geopolitical tensions. Punti chiave

What to do in case of irregularities If a cross-border worker notices irregularities in the prices charged by fuel distributors, it is important to report the situation to the competent authorities immediately. The Guardia di Finanza of Como has set up a surveillance and prevention system to protect consumers, and citizens' reports can be crucial to combat speculative practices and ensure a competitive market. ### Reporting procedure 1. Identify the irregularity: Verify if the displayed price corresponds to the one charged. 2. Gather evidence: Photograph or record the displayed price and the one charged. 3. Contact the authorities: Report the irregularity to the Guardia di Finanza of Como or the Ministry of Enterprises and Made in Italy. 4. Provide details: Include precise information about the distributor, the date, and the time of the irregularity. ### Useful tools To report irregularities in fuel prices, you can use the "Osservaprezzi carburanti" portal of the Ministry of Enterprises and Made in Italy. This tool allows you to communicate the prices charged with updates at least weekly, ensuring constant monitoring. ### Conclusion The irregularities found in fuel distributors in Ticino underline the importance of constant vigilance and transparent communication of prices. Cross-border workers who use fuel distributors daily must be aware of their rights and the procedures for reporting any irregularities. In this way, it will be possible to guarantee a competitive and transparent market that meets the needs of all consumers.
