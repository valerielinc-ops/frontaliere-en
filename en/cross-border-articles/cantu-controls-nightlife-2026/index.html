Frequently Asked Questions
What happens if a cross-border worker is found working illegally?
If a cross-border worker is found working illegally, they may face severe penalties, including fines and the loss of work-related rights. Additionally, the employer may be subject to criminal sanctions. It is crucial to work in regular contexts to avoid these issues.
How can I verify the regularity of my employer?
You can verify the regularity of your employer by requesting documents such as the employment contract and checking the company's registration. You can also use online tools like public databases or company verification platforms.

If you suspect your employer is employing illegal workers, it is important to collaborate with local authorities. You can report your suspicions to the competent authorities or participate in voluntary inspections.
