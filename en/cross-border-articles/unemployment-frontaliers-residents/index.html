Frequently Asked Questions
What are the requirements to apply for unemployment benefits?
To apply for unemployment benefits, you must have worked for a minimum of 12 months in the last 24 months, be unemployed and available to seek new employment, and be registered in the unemployment insurance system.
What documents are required to submit the application?
The required documents include the unemployment certificate issued by the employer, the certificate of residence, the work permit, and the identity document.
What are the deadlines to respect?
You must submit the application within 30 days of the date of unemployment, report monthly job searches, and participate in any training or professional requalification programs.
