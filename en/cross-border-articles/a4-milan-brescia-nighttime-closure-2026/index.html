A4 closed between Sesto and Monza: affected nights

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

Nighttime closure of the stretch between Sesto San Giovanni and Monza for safety barrier work. Alternative routes indicated.

Contesto

In brief - Nighttime closure of A4 between Sesto San Giovanni and Monza - Safety barrier works - Alternative routes indicated ## Key facts - What: Nighttime closure of motorway A4 - When: 5-10 May 2026 - Where: Between Sesto San Giovanni and Monza - Who: A4 Milan-Brescia - Amount: Not specified On the A4 Milan-Brescia, to allow activities related to the enhancement and modernization of safety barriers, the section between Sesto San Giovanni and Monza, towards Brescia, will be closed on the following days and times: - On the two nights of Tuesday 5 and Wednesday 6 May, from 9:00 PM to 5:00 AM; - From 11:00 PM on Friday 8 to 6:00 AM on Saturday 9 May; - From 10:00 PM on Saturday 9 to 6:00 AM on Sunday 10 May. Alternatively, after the mandatory exit at the Sesto San Giovanni interchange, take the SS36 towards Lecco, merge onto the A52 North Ring Road towards Monza and re-enter the A4 at the Monza station. It is specified that the Sesto San Giovanni interchange will be simultaneously closed in both directions. Alternatively, it is recommended: towards Turin/Milan city: Cormano; towards Brescia: Monza. ### Implications for cross-border workers Cross-border workers who use the A4 motorway daily to travel between the Canton of Ticino and Lombardy should pay particular attention to these nighttime works. The closures could significantly affect travel times and travel planning. It is advisable to check the closure times and plan alternative routes to avoid delays. ### Recommended alternative routes For cross-border workers who need to reach the Canton of Ticino, it is advisable to use the SS36 towards Lecco and then the A52 North Ring Road towards Monza. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the exact dates and times of night closures on the A4 between Sesto San Giovanni and Monza?
The A4 will be closed between Sesto San Giovanni and Monza (towards Brescia) on the nights of 5-6 May (21:00-5:00), 8-9 May (23:00-6:00) and 9-10 May 2026 (22:00-6:00). The closures are due to modernization works on the safety barriers.
What is the recommended alternative route for cross-border commuters during night closures of the A4?
After the mandatory exit at Sesto San Giovanni, it is advisable to take the SS36 towards Lecco, take the A52 Tangenziale nord towards Monza and re-enter the A4 at Monza station. The Sesto San Giovanni junction will also be closed at the entrance.
How will the night works on the A4 affect the travel times of cross-border commuters between the Canton of Ticino and Lombardy?
The night closures of the A4 could significantly increase travel times for cross-border commuters. It's crucial to plan your trips ahead of time and consider increasing travel times, potentially by 30 minutes or more, to avoid delays.
How can I stay updated in real time on changes or extensions of work on the A4?
To stay up to date on any changes in the closure plans or extensions of the works, it is advisable to constantly monitor official updates and use navigation apps. These tools provide real-time information.
Why is the A4 motorway closed at night in the Sesto San Giovanni-Monza section?
The night closure of the A4 is necessary to allow activities to strengthen and modernize the safety barriers. These works are essential to improve the safety of the motorway section between Sesto San Giovanni and Monza, in the direction of Brescia, from 5 to 10 May 2026.
