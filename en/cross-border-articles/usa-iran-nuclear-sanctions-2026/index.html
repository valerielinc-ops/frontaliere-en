Baghaei emphasized that the USA is not serious about lifting sanctions and has not learned from past mistakes. He also stated that there is deep mistrust between the two countries and accused the United States of expecting its maximalist demands to be accepted. Iran, on the other hand, has always presented its proposals based on the end of the war and the establishment of a ceasefire. ### Mediations and negotiations Baghaei explained that many countries, with various intentions, have announced their willingness to mediate, but for Tehran the official mediator remains Pakistan. Trump suggested that negotiations are proceeding despite the public appearance of a stalemate, while acknowledging the uncertainty about Iran's leadership.
