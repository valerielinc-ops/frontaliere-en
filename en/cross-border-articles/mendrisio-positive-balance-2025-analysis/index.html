Mendrisio ends 2025 with 800k CHF surplus

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

For the fourth consecutive year, Mendrisio presents a positive balance with a surplus of 800,000 francs, despite a negative budget forecast.

Contesto

In brief - Mendrisio closes 2025 with a surplus of 800,000 francs - Negative budget of 2.3 million francs refuted by facts - Extraordinary revenues improve the result ## Key facts - What: 2025 Municipal Budget - When: 2025 - Where: Mendrisio, Canton Ticino - Who: Municipality of Mendrisio - Amount: 800,000 francs surplus For the fourth consecutive year, the City of Mendrisio presented a positive Consolidated Statement despite a negative Budget. However, the future remains uncertain. From an expected deficit of 2.3 million francs to a surplus of 800,000 francs. For the fourth consecutive year, 2025 also closed positively for the City of Mendrisio with a better financial result than initially estimated. 103 million in expenditures, 54 million in revenues, and a tax revenue of 49 million. 'The improvement is mainly due to the containment of expenses directly related to municipal autonomy and tax surpluses,' explains Mayor Samuele Cavadini. ### Extraordinary Revenues and Future Uncertainties Extraordinary revenues have been the cause of repeatedly cautious budgets later refuted by facts. 'Fortunately, they exist, but - warns Cavadini - it is not certain that they will be there in the future, which is not so reassuring.' Clouds on the horizon with a well-defined origin: Bellinzona. And a cause: the now chronic growth of cantonal contributions, particularly those related to the management of the elderly and social insurance. Which could further increase with the implementation of the two initiatives on health insurance premiums. ### Investments and Projects 2025 was also marked by significant investments in public construction for Mendrisio.
