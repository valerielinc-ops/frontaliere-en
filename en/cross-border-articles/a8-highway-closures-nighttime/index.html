A8: night closures between Busto and Gallarate, Castellanza entrance closed

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

New traffic changes on the A8 Milan-Varese for anti-noise barrier works. Night closures between Busto Arsizio and Gallarate and stop at Castellanza entrance.
