Five concerts to rediscover a gem to save

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

From May 9 to June 6, 2026, five ancient music concerts in the chapel of San Cassiano in Varese to support the restoration of the site

Contesto

In brief - Five concerts of ancient music in San Cassiano from May 9 to June 6, 2026 - Event promoted by the ProMusica Association to support the restoration of the church - Petition for FAI's 'I Luoghi del Cuore' ## Key facts - What: Ancient music review 'Musica a San Cassiano' - When: From May 9 to June 6, 2026, every Saturday at 17:30 - Where: Church of San Cassiano, Avigno, Varese - Who: ProMusica Association - Cost: Not yet specified The small church of San Cassiano, located in Avigno, Varese, is ready to welcome ancient music enthusiasts again with a new edition of the review 'Musica a San Cassiano'. Promoted by the ProMusica Association, the event will take place from May 9 to June 6, 2026, every Saturday at 17:30. This architectural gem, known for its intimate and cozy acoustics, will host five concerts spanning various genres of ancient music, with the aim of supporting the restoration of the church, which has infiltrations and cracks in the frescoed vault. The review, subtitled 'A harpsichord for a restoration', is accompanied by the quote from Italo Calvino 'Fantasy is a place where it rains inside'. This event not only offers an opportunity to enjoy high-quality music but also to contribute to the preservation of a precious cultural heritage. The organizers have chosen to involve FAI, which had already promoted the church as a 'Luogo del cuore'. The church has been nominated for the 13th Census 'I Luoghi del Cuore', promoted by FAI in collaboration with Intesa Sanpaolo, and during the events it will be possible to collect signatures to raise public awareness about the importance of restoration. ### Concert program The program of the review is varied and full of unmissable appointments. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the main goal of the "Musica a San Cassiano" review?
The main goal of the review is to support the restoration of the church of San Cassiano, which has infiltrations and cracks in the frescoed vault. The signature collection and the economic contributions collected during the events will contribute to the safeguarding of this cultural heritage.
How can I contribute to the signature collection for the church's restoration?
You can contribute to the signature collection by attending the events and signing the petition during the concerts. Additionally, you can vote for the church of San Cassiano online at the website www.iluoghidelcuore.it to support its candidacy for the 13th Census "I Luoghi del Cuore".
What concerts are scheduled in the review?
The review includes five concerts: "Dormi o fulmine di guerra" on May 9th, "...dialoghi amorosi..." on May 16th, "Music for these troubled times" on May 23rd, "Fantasie: musica e parole" on May 30th, and "In imitation of Birds" on June 6th. Each concert will take place at 17:30 in the church of San Cassiano.
