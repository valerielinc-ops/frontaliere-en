What are the Ticino municipalities that have invested in tourist infrastructure in recent months?
The Municipality of Surses has invested 8.9 million francs in the hotel Cube in Savognin, while the municipalities of Rheinwald, Andeer, Sufers, Ferrera, Muntogna da Schons, Zillis-Reischen and Rongellen have accepted a credit of five million francs to renew snowmaking facilities. What is the role of the Federal Law on Tourism in Public Investments in the Ticino tourist sector?
The Federal Law on Tourism provides state contributions to support public investment in tourist infrastructure, thus facilitating projects such as the purchase of the Cube hotel in Savognin or the renewal of snowmaking facilities in various gray municipalities.
What incentives do you receive a common Ticino to develop tourist infrastructure through federal law?
The Federal Law on Tourism allows the Ticino municipalities to receive state contributions up to 12.5% of their budget for investments in tourist infrastructure, promoting the development and improvement of local tourist services.
How do public investments affect tourism on job creation in Ticino?
Public investments, such as renewal of hotel facilities and facilities, stimulate the local economy by creating new jobs and increasing demand for services in the Ticino tourist sector.
What are the main types of tourist infrastructure financed by the Ticino municipalities?
Ticino's municipalities invest mainly in ski lifts, cross-country skiing, hotel facilities and support infrastructure to improve the tourist offer and hospitality.
