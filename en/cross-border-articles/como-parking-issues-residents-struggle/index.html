Cacia sent two PECs to the Municipality of Como to report the critical situation of the blue parking spots for residents, which are always full, while the non-ASA blue spots are free. This situation creates great difficulties for residents, who struggle to find parking even during normal hours. Cacia attached some representative photos of the situation to his report, particularly of via Salita Quarcino, which he considers significant. Municipal councilor Paola Ceriello has already addressed the issue before, denouncing the same problem. ### The resident's report Angelo Cacia, a resident in zone 1 of the Sant'Agostino Facilitated Parking Area, sent two PECs to the Municipality of Como to report the problem of the blue parking spots. In his report, Cacia highlighted that the current regulations have some incorrect elements, particularly regarding the limitation of residents' parking to only the currently designated streets. This results in great difficulty finding parking, even during normal hours, while some excluded streets have free or underutilized blue spots.
