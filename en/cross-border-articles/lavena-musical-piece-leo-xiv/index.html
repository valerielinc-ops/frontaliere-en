A musical tribute to Pope Leo XIV from Lavena Ponte Tresa (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

A musical piece dedicated to Pope Leo XIV will be performed for the first time on May 8th in Cremenaga.

Contesto

In brief - Musical piece in honor of Leo XIV - First performance on May 8 in Cremenaga - Don Giorgio Quaglia Polyphonic Choir ## Key facts - What: Musical piece "Lion of Christ" - When: May 8, 2026 - Where: Church of SS. Annunziata in Cremenaga - Who: Quattro Evangelisti Pastoral Community - Cost: Not yet specified On the occasion of the first anniversary of the election of the American Pontiff, Maestro Stefano Cropanese wanted to dedicate to the Holy Father the piece entitled "Lion of Christ," which will be performed for the first time on May 8 in the church in Cremenaga during an evening dedicated to prayer and spirituality. The Quattro Evangelisti Pastoral Community of Lavena Ponte Tresa, Cremenaga, and Ardena, on the occasion of the 1st anniversary of the election of Pope Leo XIV, next May 8, at 8:30 PM, in the Church of SS. Annunziata in Cremenaga, has organized an evening of prayer and spirituality in music, in collaboration with the Municipality of Cremenaga and the Reatium Cultural Association. ### The piece "Lion of Christ" The piece "Lion of Christ" was written for four uneven voices by Stefano Cropanese, on the verses of V. Politano and S. Cropanese. On this occasion, the Don Giorgio Quaglia Polyphonic Choir will be present, which includes about 40 singers not only from the four parishes of the Pastoral Community but also from some neighboring countries, as well as from neighboring Switzerland. In addition to the piece "Lion of Christ," the performance of the "Missa Brevis" for four uneven voices, composed by Cropanese himself, and other pieces inspired by the Holy Scriptures is planned. ### The evening of prayer and spirituality The evening of prayer and spirituality in music will feature the performance of pieces taken from the Gospel, chosen to meditat...

Practical Analysis: Implications for Cross-Border Workers The evening of prayer and spiritual music dedicated to Pope Leo XIV represents a unique opportunity for cross-border workers residing in the area of Lavena Ponte Tresa and surroundings. This event not only offers a moment of spiritual reflection but also an occasion to strengthen community ties between Italy and Switzerland. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation Before this event, no cultural initiatives of such magnitude were planned that directly involved cross-border workers. The presence of singers from Switzerland represents a new and significant element, which underscores the importance of dialogue and collaboration between the two communities. ### Concrete Scenarios Cross-border workers who decide to participate will be able to attend an evening of prayer and spiritual music, with the performance of sacred pieces and the reading of passages from the Gospel. This type of event can have a positive impact on the psychological and spiritual well-being of the participants, offering a moment of reflection and sharing. ### Concrete Procedures To participate in the evening, cross-border workers can go directly to the Chiesa SS. Annunziata di Cremenaga on May 8th at 20:30. No ticket or reservation is required, but it is advisable to arrive a bit early to secure a spot. It is important to check for any updates on the website of the Comune di Cremenaga or the Associazione Culturale Reatium for additional information. ### Practical Implications Participation in such events can have a positive impact on the quality of life of cross-border workers, offering moments of leisure and reflection that go beyond the work context.
