Frequently Asked Questions
What are the most common causes of fires in residential buildings?
The most common causes of fires in residential buildings include electrical system malfunctions, short circuits, improper use of appliances, and negligence in managing heat sources. It is crucial to perform regular checks and maintenance to prevent these incidents.
How can I prepare in case of a fire?
To prepare for a fire, it is important to know the escape routes in the building where you live and work, have a family emergency plan, participate in periodic drills, and have tools like the [fire risk calculator](nav:calculator) and the [insurance comparator](nav:health) at your disposal.
What are the emergency numbers in Italy?
In Italy, the general emergency number is 112. For fires, you can call 115, which is the number dedicated to the fire brigade. It is important to have these numbers at hand and know them by heart.
