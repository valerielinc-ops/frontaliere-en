Participating in the meeting can be a way to express your opinions and contribute to developing solutions that improve the quality of life for all citizens. ### Impact of Tourism in Como Tourism in Como has reached unprecedented levels, with over 1.5 million visitors per year. This increase has led to a growing impact on residential neighborhoods, particularly those near the lake. For example, in the Camerlata neighborhood, rents have increased by 20% in the last two years, making it difficult for local residents to find affordable housing. ### Comparison with Ticino In comparison, Ticinese municipalities like Lugano and Mendrisio have implemented policies to balance tourism with the needs of residents. In Lugano, for example, a 2.5% tourist tax has been introduced, which is reinvested in public services. Additionally, the canton has stipulated that 30% of new housing must be allocated to local residents. ### Regulations and Key Dates In Italy, Law No. 13 of 2017 introduced regulations for short-term rentals, but in Como, enforcement has been slow.
