Construction Work on Magliaso's New Primary School Has Begun (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 March 2026

The new primary school in Magliaso will be ready for the 2028/2029 academic year

Contesto

TL;DR - New elementary school construction starts in Magliaso. - 14.186 million francs allocated for the project. - School ready for 2028/2029, with 5 classrooms. ## Key facts - Cosa: Construction of new elementary school in Magliaso. - Quando: Construction started on March 26, 2024. - Dove: Magliaso, Switzerland. - Chi: Bonetti e Bonetti Architects. - Importo: 14.186 million francs allocated. - Scadenza: School ready for 2028/2029 school year. - Progetto: PIVOT project by Bonetti e Bonetti Architects. - Struttura: Two above-ground levels and one underground level. In the afternoon of Thursday, March 26, the first stone was laid, symbolically marking the start of construction of the new elementary school in Magliaso. The ceremony was attended by all the children of the Magliaso School Institute, who contributed to the ceremony by placing small stones, colored by themselves, in a pit, where a time capsule was also deposited, containing the notes of all the students, who expressed their thoughts to the future school generations. The Municipal Council had allocated a credit of 14.186 million francs for the costs of design and construction of this new school building. The 'PIVOT' project by Bonetti and Bonetti Architects was the winner of the architecture competition held by the Municipality in the autumn of 2019. The narrow and long building develops on two above-ground levels and one underground level. On the ground floor are located the entrance hall, the refectory, the teachers' room and the rooms for creative and music activities. On the first floor are the five classrooms. The structure will be ready for the 2028/2029 school year. Vice-Mayor Fè Fossati stated that 'we are building the future of our community.'
