Just Eat delivers Migros products in Ticino: over 10,000 items available

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 20 April 2026

Since May 4th, Just Eat has been delivering Migros products in Ticino, Valais, and Geneva with delivery in less than an hour. Prices are identical to those in stores.

Contesto

TL;DR - Just Eat delivers Migros products in Ticino, Valais, Geneva from May 4, 2026. - Over 10,000 items available with 60-minute delivery from Migros supermarkets. - Delivery costs CHF 4.90 for orders under CHF 30, free for orders over CHF 50. - Service available from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, 7 days a week. ## Key facts - Service Start: May 4, 2026 - Coverage: Cantons of Ticino, Valais, and Geneva - Delivery Time: Within 60 minutes - Delivery Cost: CHF 4.90 for orders under CHF 30 - Free Delivery: For orders over CHF 50 - Service Hours: 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM - Just Eat Partners: 7,000 partners and 1.5 million active customers - Migros Supermarkets: Lugano, Bellinzona, and Locarno among the first ## Just Eat delivers Migros products in Ticino: over 10,000 items available Starting from May 4, 2026, residents of the cantons of Ticino, Valais, and Geneva will be able to order over 10,000 Migros products via the Just Eat app. This new home delivery service represents an important novelty for those who wish to purchase food and consumer goods without having to physically go to supermarkets. With the new delivery service, it will be possible to avoid these inconveniences and enjoy greater convenience. ### Practical scenarios Imagine a cross-border worker who works in Lugano and resides in Varese.
