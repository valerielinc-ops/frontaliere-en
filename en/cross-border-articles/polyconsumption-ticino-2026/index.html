Poly-drug use in Ticino: Ingrado's 2025 report (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

Ingrado's 2025 report confirms poly-drug use as the prevailing trend in Ticino, with a 16.4% increase in substance counseling services.

In brief - Polydrug use confirmed as the prevailing trend in Ticino - 16.4% increase in substance counseling centers - 37% more people welcomed at the Viganello day center ## Key facts - What: 2025 Drug Report in Ticino - When: Published in 2026 - Where: Ticino - Who: Ingrado-Servizi Foundation - Amount: +16.4% users in substance counseling Polydrug use is confirmed as the prevailing trend in Ticino in 2025. This is what emerges from the report published by the Ingrado-Servizi foundation for addictions, which presents the activities carried out in over 5 cantonal sites (Bellinzona, Biasca, Locarno, Lugano and Chiasso). A significant increase in cases has been observed in the alcohol counseling centers, dedicated to those with alcohol problems, especially in the Sopraceneri area. For the substance counseling centers, an increase of 16.4% was also observed, with over 620 users. The Viganello day care center welcomed 37% more people than the previous year and the outreach service managed to reach approximately 400 people in the area. ### Implications for cross-border workers The polydrug use and the increase in behavioral disorders represent a significant challenge for the Ticino community, including cross-border workers. Dettagli operativi

Practical Analysis The polyconsumption of drugs and the increase in behavioral disorders represent a complex challenge that requires an integrated and multidisciplinary approach. For cross-border workers living and working in Ticino, it is essential to be aware of the available services and resources they can access if needed. ### Support Services The Ingrado-Servizi foundation offers a range of services to combat the spread of damage related to substance use. Among these, the distribution of kits to reduce the risks associated with drug use, with a 41% increase compared to the previous year. Additionally, the 'housing first' model, which provides housing and support in managing addiction, is a concrete response to the spread of 'crack houses' and the housing crisis. ### Behavioral Disorders An emerging phenomenon concerns the Behavioral Disorders Sector (Gat-p), which has recorded 87 new reports. The evident growth of requests related to the use of the internet, video games, and online betting, linked to traditional pathological gambling, represents a new challenge for authorities and organizations operating in the sector. For cross-border workers, it is important to be aware of the risks associated with these behaviors and the resources available to address them. ### Certifications and User Satisfaction Compared to 2025, the association has maintained the quality certifications already obtained, updating the regulations and internal directives. A satisfaction questionnaire saw over 80% of users satisfied, with evident positives regarding reception, confidentiality, and the absence of perceived judgment. Concrete action For cross-border workers living and working in Ticino, it is essential to be aware of the available services and resources they can access if needed. Here are some concrete steps that can be taken: 1. Inform yourself about the services offered by the Ingrado-Servizi foundation: Visit the foundation's website to get detailed information about the available services and contacts. 2. Participate in prevention and harm reduction programs: Use the kits distributed by the foundation to reduce the risks associated with drug use. 3. Access the housing first model: If you are in a housing crisis, contact the foundation for information on how to access housing and support in managing addiction. 4. Participate in support programs for behavioral disorders: If you are affected by behavioral disorders related to internet use, video games, or online betting, contact the Behavioral Disorders Sector (Gat-p) for information on available support programs. ### Useful tools For further information and support, you can use the salary calculator to compare wages in Ticino and Italy, and the permit comparator to better understand local regulations. ### Conclusion The polyconsumption of drugs and the increase in behavioral disorders represent a significant challenge for the Ticino community. Frequently Asked Questions
What services does the Ingrado-Servizi foundation offer?
The Ingrado-Servizi foundation offers a range of services to combat the spread of damage related to substance use, including the distribution of kits to reduce the risks associated with drug use and the housing first model, which provides housing and support in managing addiction.
How can I access support services for behavioral disorders?
To access support services for behavioral disorders, you can contact the Behavioral Disorders Sector (Gat-p) of the Ingrado-Servizi foundation for information on the available support programs.
What are the new developments for 2026?
The new developments for 2026 include the inauguration of the new operational headquarters of the Medicine of Addictions in Lugano and the recognition of the foundation as a Fmh Training Entity for the training of assistant doctors in the psychiatry department.
