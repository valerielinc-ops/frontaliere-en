Four goats appeared near the border crossing, attracting the attention of the customs authorities. According to the Federal Customs Administration and Border Security (UDSC), the goats risked causing two incidents, endangering traffic. The UDSC explained that their teams intervened to secure the animals in a pen located under the customs parking area. 'Near the customs office in Gandria, four goats attracted the attention of our colleagues. On two occasions, they risked causing an accident and endangering traffic,' reads the post. The authorities' intervention was timely, avoiding potential negative consequences for road safety.
