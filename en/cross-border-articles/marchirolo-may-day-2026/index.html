Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main events of May Day in Marchirolo?
The main events of May Day in Marchirolo include a procession starting from Borasio square, accompanied by the municipal musical band, followed by institutional speeches and theatrical readings. Additionally, certificates of merit are awarded to local citizens and entities.
How can I participate in local events as a cross-border worker?
To participate in local events as a cross-border worker, you can inform yourself through the event calendar of your municipality of residence, attend the events, and actively participate in the proposed initiatives. Additionally, it is useful to create bonds with the local community and reflect on your contribution to the territory.
What are the benefits of participating in local events for cross-border workers?
Participating in local events offers cross-border workers the opportunity to better understand the local culture, create bonds with the community, and reflect on their work and contribution to the territory. These events promote integration and a sense of belonging, which are fundamental elements for those who live and work between two countries.
