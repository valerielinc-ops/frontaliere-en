Contesto

In brief - Artificial intelligence and advanced robotics enter SMEs - Confcommercio conference in Varese with VHIT Spa case studies - Presentation of humanoid and quadruped robot prototypes ## Key facts - What: TerzIArio Donna 5.0 conference on artificial intelligence and robotics - When: Monday, May 4, 2026 - Where: Confcommercio Provincia di Varese headquarters - Who: Confcommercio Provincia di Varese, VHIT Spa, Terziario Donna - Cost: Not yet specified Artificial intelligence and advanced robotics are no longer scenarios confined to industrial giants but are useful tools for redefining managerial and productive processes even within small and medium-sized enterprises. This is the central theme of the TerzIArio Donna 5.0 meeting – Let's talk about Artificial Intelligence, organized by the Terziario Donna group of Confcommercio Provincia di Varese, which took place on Monday, May 4, at the Varese section headquarters. The session offered a practical overview of technological evolution: starting with the opportunities offered by linguistic models and AI agents, then moving on to the demonstration of some robot prototypes destined to change the face of manual and monitoring work. The strategic importance of data and the evolution of robotics were at the heart of the morning. The experience of VHIT Spa was one of the central points of the morning. Corrado La Forgia, CEO of the company, reviewed the technological and managerial transformations that allowed the company (born within Bosch and then passed to the Chinese group Weifu) to double its turnover while maintaining its workforce through the adoption of technologies that have optimized procurement management, digitized data analysis, prevented errors, and reduced maintenance interventions.
