Lombardy highway tolls: end of free travel for cross-border workers

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

From 1 May 2026, mandatory tolls on the Corda Molle with 50% discounts for residents of 22 municipalities. Concessions coming for commuters

Contesto

In brief - Mandatory toll on the Corda Molle from May 1, 2026 - 50% discount for residents of 22 municipalities - Benefits coming for commuters ## Key facts - What: Introduction of highway toll - When: From May 1, 2026 - Where: Corda Molle Highway, Lombardy - Who: Province of Brescia, Autovia Padana - Amount: 50% discount for residents After just two months of free access, travel on the Corda Molle highway in Lombardy becomes toll-based for everyone. The decision, official as of April 27, 2026, comes into effect on May 1, 2026. A bitter turn for users, following hypotheses of extending the free access. The long standoff between the Province of Brescia, mayors, and industry associations concludes with the introduction of the toll, albeit with significant benefits. ### Benefits for residents The concessionaire Autovia Padana will apply a 50% reduction on the toll cost for all residents of the 22 municipalities located along the route or within a two-kilometer radius of the infrastructure. This benefit, equal to that already provided for citizens of Brescia, will remain valid for the entire duration of the concession, i.e., until 2043. To take advantage of the discounted rate, resident citizens must follow a specific procedure: registration on the official Autovia Padana portal, verification of data and residence in the territory of the agreed municipalities. The discount is applicable to holders of tele-toll contracts or those who activate the License Plate Account service. Those who do not have these systems can pay the toll within 15 days via the FreFlow A21 app or on the concessionaire's website. The possibility of cash payment at the dedicated Infopoint remains active. ### News for commuters While the toll becomes a reality, the Province of Brescia is working to...

Frequently Asked Questions
Who is eligible for the 50% discount on tolls?
All residents of the 22 municipalities located along the route of the Corda Molle motorway or within a two-kilometer radius of the infrastructure are eligible for the 50% discount. This benefit is valid for the entire duration of the concession, i.e., until 2043.
How can I activate the toll discount?
To activate the discount, you need to register on the official Autovia Padana portal, verify your data, and confirm your residence in the territory of the partner municipalities. The discount is applicable to holders of tele-toll contracts or those who activate the License Plate Account service.
Are there any benefits for commuters?
The Province of Brescia is working to introduce a discount model based on the frequency of crossings, similar to that already adopted for other national infrastructures. This model rewards those who use the motorway more frequently, offering discounts proportional to the number of crossings made in a month.
