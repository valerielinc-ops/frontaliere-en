Salary adjustments and improved working conditions can have a direct impact on the quality of life of cross-border workers, who often have to manage high costs related to commuting and the cost of living in Switzerland. ### Tax Impact For cross-border workers who work in Switzerland and reside in Italy, it is important to consider the tax implications of salary adjustments. With withholding tax, cross-border workers pay taxes in Switzerland but can benefit from bilateral agreements that avoid double taxation. It is crucial for cross-border workers to understand how these adjustments can affect their net income and plan accordingly. ### Comparison with Other Healthcare Facilities The Moncucco Hospital Group is not the only healthcare facility in Ticino to report positive results.
