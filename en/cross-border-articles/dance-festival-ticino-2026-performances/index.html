Dancing Festival 2026: Ticino moves with shows and participation

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

From May 6 to 10, 2026, over 40 Swiss cities host the 21st edition of the Dancing Festival, with Lugano as the beating heart of the events.

Contesto

In brief - 21st edition from 6 to 10 May 2026 - Over 40 cities involved - 11 shows and 2 workshops in Ticino ## Key facts - What: Festa Danzante 2026 - When: 6-10 May 2026 - Where: Ticino (Ascona, Chiasso, Lugano, Locarno, Mendrisio, Montagnola) - Who: Local and national organizers - Cost: Pass for 25 francs (free for under 16s) The 21st edition of Festa Danzante will take place from 6 to 10 May 2026, involving over 40 cities and towns across Switzerland. In Ticino, the event will take place in six locations: Ascona, Chiasso, Lugano, Locarno, Mendrisio (with Ligornetto) and Montagnola. Lugano will be the heart of the program, with events taking place in symbolic venues such as LAC and Teatro Foce, as well as in parks, squares and museums. The program opens on 6 and 7 May in Mendrisiotto with two 'Dance on Tour' events: an inclusive workshop at the Museo Vincenzo Vela and the performance 'Le Cycle' in the gardens of the m.a.x. museum. On Friday 8 May, Lugano will host immersive performances at Parco Ciani and a Balkan party at Studio Foce. On Saturday 9 May, the festival will extend between Lugano and Locarno with workshops, classes and participatory creations, culminating in an event at the EXTRA_G platform at Teatro Foce. The grand finale will take place on Sunday 10 May between Ascona, Locarno and Mendrisio, with site-specific performances and moments between live art and music. In Ticino, 11 shows, 2 workshops, dance classes and two parties are planned. A pass for 25 francs, free for under 16s, will give access to events throughout Switzerland. ### Detailed programme The programme for Festa Danzante 2026 in Ticino is packed with events. How to Participate To participate in the Festa Danzante 2026, you can purchase a pass for 25 francs, which grants access to all events across Switzerland. The pass is free for those under 16 years old. You can buy the pass online or at authorized sales points. ### Detailed Program For a complete overview of the program, you can visit the official Festa Danzante website.
