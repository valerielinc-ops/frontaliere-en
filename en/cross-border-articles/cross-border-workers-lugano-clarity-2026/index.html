The request concerns clarity on potential privileges for former employees using municipal properties. The case of Castagnola has been cited as an example of a situation requiring greater transparency. The Lega asks the Municipality to provide details on any rental contracts with former employees and the criteria used for granting such contracts. The current situation has raised concerns about possible privileges and the need for more transparent management of municipal properties. ### Implications for cross-border workers Cross-border workers employed in Lugano may be affected by this interpellation, as transparency in the management of municipal properties could influence future housing policies. Additionally, the situation may have repercussions on housing opportunities for transborder workers, making the criteria for access to municipal properties clearer. The Lega has emphasized the importance of fair and transparent management of municipal properties, especially in a context where housing demand is high and availability is limited.
