Vocational training week: 30 events in Ticino

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

From May 4 to 8, over 30 events on radio, TV, and in-person to discover professions in Ticino

Contesto

In brief - Vocational training week from May 4 to 8 - Over 30 events across radio, TV, and in-person - Teleticino kicks off with a special on SwissSkills 2025 ## Key facts - What: Vocational training week - When: May 4-8, 2024 - Where: Ticino, Switzerland - Who: Swiss Conference of Vocational Education and Training Offices, SEFRI, DECS Ticino - Amount: Over 30 events From May 4 to 8, 2024, the Vocational Training Week returns to Ticino. The initiative, promoted by the Swiss Conference of Vocational Education and Training Offices with the State Secretariat for Education, Research, and Innovation (SEFRI), involves the main radio and television broadcasters in the canton. In Ticino, the Week is coordinated by the Millestrade project of the Department of Education, Culture, and Sport (DECS). ### Special programming Teleticino symbolically opens the week already on Friday, May 1, with the special 'When I grow up,' which includes a documentary about young people from Italian-speaking Switzerland who are protagonists at SwissSkills 2025. On the radio front, RSI Rete Tre will offer every morning the special edition of 'A life as...', dedicated to five professions told by those who live them firsthand. RSI Rete Uno, on the other hand, will host on Thursday, May 7, in the Casa Svizzera program, the LIFT project, which supports middle school students in approaching the world of work. ### News and participation Among the novelties of this year, the involvement of Radio Scuola media Besso, which will offer a monographic live broadcast on orientation designed and realized by students. Radio 3i and Radio Ticino will accompany the entire week with interviews and testimonies on the opportunities and challenges of vocational training. ### Events on the territory In addition to media...
