Dettagli operativi

Agrinatura is no longer just an agricultural fair, but a place where food – thanks to its direct producers – becomes a tool for storytelling, synthesis, and perspective for contemporary agriculture. "AgriNatura demonstrates how agriculture can be told and experienced through food, which is the first tool for relating to citizens," emphasizes Fortunato Trezzi. "In these first two days, we have seen extraordinary participation and increasing quality of the proposals: the kitchen thus becomes the most direct language to enhance the work of our companies and to transmit the value of the short chain and the territory." ### Workshops and Tastings The educational workshops and tastings have been a highlight of the event. From the dishes of chef Roberto Conti, with his risotto with wild garlic and Gorgonzola from Comasca, to the preparations of the farmer chefs, every moment has been an opportunity to deepen the knowledge of the short chain and local agricultural production. The informative meetings on cheeses and biodiversity have offered an effective balance between education and show, involving the public in a unique experience. ### The Role of Coldiretti Como Lecco Coldiretti Como Lecco has curated the central arena, ensuring constant and qualified participation. The cooking shows and workshops, often sold out, have demonstrated the public's interest and attention towards agricultural and gastronomic themes. The large celebratory cake, made for the occasion, has symbolized the success and importance of this event, which has consolidated its role over a quarter of a century. ### Conclusion Agrinatura 2026 has confirmed its value as a high-level agro-food destination, capable of combining dissemination, agricultural identity, and contemporary cuisine. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the Agrinatura 2026 program?
The Agrinatura 2026 program includes educational workshops, tastings, cooking shows, and informative meetings on cheeses and biodiversity. The event takes place from May 1 to 3, 2026.
Who are the main figures of Agrinatura 2026?
The main figures of Agrinatura 2026 are renowned chefs like Roberto Conti, Simone Nicolosi, and Giuseppe Crimaldi, along with farmer chefs Giulia di Scanno and Roberto Mauri.
Where does Agrinatura 2026 take place?
Agrinatura 2026 takes place in the central arena curated by Coldiretti Como Lecco. The event is a unique opportunity to discover the agricultural and gastronomic excellences of the territory.
