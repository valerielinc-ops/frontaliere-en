Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main benefits of artificial intelligence in companies?
The main benefits of artificial intelligence in companies include the automation of repetitive processes, optimization of internal resources, reduction of errors and bottlenecks, and freeing employees from the most tedious tasks, allowing them to focus on more rewarding and productive activities.
How can a company start integrating artificial intelligence?
A company can start integrating artificial intelligence by analyzing workflows to identify areas where the introduction of an algorithm can bring real economic and operational returns. Subsequently, it is important to choose a technology partner that offers scalable solutions and measurable economic goals.
What are the areas of application of artificial intelligence in companies?
The areas of application of artificial intelligence in companies include customer service, where virtual assistants can handle ordinary requests, marketing and sales, where predictive data analysis can guide the development of targeted commercial campaigns, and the administrative area, where intelligent reading systems can automatically archive, verify, and extract data from contracts or invoices.
