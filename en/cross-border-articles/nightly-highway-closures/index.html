Nighttime Closures on A9 Highway The nighttime closures on the A9 highway are necessary to allow maintenance work on high-voltage cables by the Terna Company and for the transit of exceptional transports. These works are essential to guarantee the safety and efficiency of the electrical network and transportation in the region. As an alternative, the following routes are recommended: for the closure of the Como Centro-Chiasso section, after the mandatory exit at the Como Centro junction, take the ordinary road towards Chiasso, until you reach the city customs office. For example, if you are coming from Lugano, you can take the A2 highway to Como, then take the exit for Chiasso, and follow the signs to the customs office. The additional travel time may vary from 15 to 30 minutes, depending on traffic. For the closure of the Lago di Como-A59 Tangenziale di Como junction section, after the mandatory exit at the Lago di Como junction, take the ordinary road towards Lainate/Milan and re-enter the A9 highway at Fino Mornasco. A concrete example is the following: if you are coming from Mendrisio, you can take the A2 highway to Lago di Como, then take the exit for Lainate, and follow the signs to the A9 highway. The additional distance may vary from 10 to 20 km, depending on the chosen route. It is essential to note that the nighttime closures are scheduled from June 1 to September 30, with closure hours from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM. The Terna Company has announced that the maintenance work will be carried out according to current regulations, such as the Legislative Decree of May 30, 2008, no. 117, which regulates the safety of electrical installations.
