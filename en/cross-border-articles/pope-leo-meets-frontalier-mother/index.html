The meeting took place in the Naples Cathedral, the first stop of the Pope's pastoral visit to the Neapolitan capital. The woman, introduced to Pope Leo XIV by the city's archbishop, Cardinal Mimmo Battaglia, was accompanied by her eldest daughter. During the meeting, Patrizia Mercolino donated a photo of little Domenico and a copy of the book 'A Burned Heart', written with her lawyer, Francesco Petruzzi. ### The significance of the meeting The meeting between Pope Leo XIV and Patrizia Mercolino holds particular significance for the cross-border worker community, who often face similar challenges to those experienced by the Caliendo family.
