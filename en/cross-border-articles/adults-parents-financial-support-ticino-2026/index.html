Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main reasons for financial support within families?
The main reasons for financial support within families are the high cost of living, accumulated debts, and health issues. These factors make it difficult for many young people to achieve financial independence and force them to rely on their parents to cover monthly expenses.
How can I manage my debts effectively?
To manage debts effectively, it's important to prioritize payments, focus on the most urgent and high-interest debts, and negotiate with creditors for better conditions. Additionally, it's crucial to create a monthly budget and stick to it to avoid unnecessary new expenses.
What are the local regulations I should know to optimize my financial situation?
In Ticino, it's important to know the rules related to withholding tax, AHV, and the Pension Fund (LPP). These aspects can have a significant impact on personal finances and must be managed carefully to avoid paying too much in taxes.
