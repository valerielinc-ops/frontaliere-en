The subsidy system for daycare centers in Bellinzona is at the center of a heated debate between the Municipality and the Department of Education. Matteo Cheda, representing the Municipality, has criticized the model adopted by the Department of Education, Culture, and Sport (DSS), calling it 'discriminatory'. According to Cheda, the current system does not guarantee equity among citizens. On the other hand, the Department has responded by ensuring that the criteria applied are 'objective' and based on clear parameters. The debate emerged following a series of complaints from parents, who lament disparities in the treatment of subsidies. ### Matteo Cheda's Criticisms Matteo Cheda has expressed concern that the subsidy system is not fair. 'The model adopted by the DSS is discriminatory and does not take into account the real needs of families', Cheda declared. In his view, the current system favors certain categories of citizens to the detriment of others, creating unjustified disparities.
