Some employees have sent an anonymous letter to the Municipality of Lugano, expressing dissatisfaction with the management of director Christian Castelli. The news was reported by La Regione and has led the Executive to initiate immediate checks. Filippo Lombardi, head of the Lugano Airport department, confirmed that a similar document had already arrived at the Municipality about a year ago. This time, however, the situation seems more serious, requiring the intervention of the Canton Labor Inspectorate, which will conduct interviews with all collaborators in the coming weeks. ### Checks underway The Canton Labor Inspectorate has initiated a round of interviews with all collaborators of the airport to clarify the situation. This process has been initiated to address the concerns raised in the anonymous letter and to ensure a serene and productive work environment. The checks have been decided to prevent the dissatisfaction from negatively influencing the operations of the airport. ### Impact on staff The dissatisfaction among employees could have significant repercussions on the morale and productivity of the staff.
