Como Municipality: 12 counters by appointment only from May 4, 2026

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

From May 4, 2026, 12 services of the Como Municipality will be available by appointment only, including the issuance of the Electronic Identity Card (CIE).

Contesto

In brief - From May 4, 2026, 12 services of the Municipality of Como will be by appointment only - 60 weekly tickets for the release without appointment of the CIE - Appointments bookable only for the month of May ## Key facts - What: Municipal services by appointment - When: From May 4, 2026 - Where: Municipality of Como - Who: Municipality of Como - Amount: 60 weekly tickets for CIE without appointment The Municipality of Como has announced that, starting from Monday, May 4, 2026, twelve services will be provided by appointment only. Among these, the service for issuing the Electronic Identity Card (CIE), certifications, transfer of vehicle ownership, authentication of signatures and copies of documents, substitute declarations, civil status certificates, historical civil status certificates, issuance of electoral certifications, issuance of new electoral card, issuance of duplicate electoral card, registration/cancellation of the list of scrutineers and presidents of polling stations. Appointments can be booked only for the month of May, while dates for subsequent months will be made available by May. For the issuance of the CIE, only for the month of May, up to 60 tickets will be available for the release without appointment every Monday from 15:30 to 19:30, exclusively for residents or inhabitants who need to replace the Paper Identity Card with the CIE. Furthermore, it is possible to obtain the issuance of the CIE for urgent reasons, duly documented or self-certified, such as travel, health, participation in public tenders/competitions, theft or loss, enrollment in schools, universities or other courses, and change of personal details. Frequently Asked Questions
Which services will be available by appointment only?
As of May 4, 2026, the following services will be available by appointment only: issuing of CIE, certifications, vehicle ownership transfer, signature authentication, document and photo copies, self-certifications, civil status certificates, historical civil status certificates, issuance of electoral certifications, issuance of new electoral cards, issuance of duplicate electoral cards, registration/cancellation of poll workers and election officers.
How can I book an appointment?
Appointments can only be booked for the month of May through the official website of the Municipality of Como. Dates for subsequent months will be made available by May. It is important to carefully read the instructions on the website and the vademecum for issuing the CIE.
What are the reasons for which I can obtain the CIE without an appointment?
It is possible to obtain the CIE without an appointment for duly documented or self-certified urgent reasons, such as travel reasons (for study, work, health), health reasons, participation in public tenders/competitions, theft or loss, enrollment in schools, universities, or other courses, and change of personal details.
