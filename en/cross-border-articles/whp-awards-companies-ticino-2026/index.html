WHP awards 90 Ticino companies for workplace health

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 21 April 2026

ATS Insubria and Confindustria Varese recognize companies that promote health in the workplace with the WHP program

Contesto

TL;DR - 90 companies awarded for WHP program adherence - WHP promotes health and well-being in workplaces - Award ceremony held in Varese for cross-border companies ## Key facts - Program: WHP (Workplace Health Promotion) - Organizers: ATS Insubria and Confindustria Varese - Number of companies: 90 - Location: Varese, Italy - Benefits: Fitness programs, health training, psychological support - Target: Cross-border workers in Ticino - Impact: Reduced absenteeism, increased productivity - Certification: Companies must submit application and pass evaluation ## Awarding of Virtuous Companies ATS Insubria and Confindustria Varese have awarded nearly 90 companies in the area for their adherence to the WHP (Workplace Health Promotion) program. This program aims to promote health and well-being in the workplace, an initiative that involves both Italian and Swiss companies, particularly in the Canton of Ticino. ### The WHP Program The WHP program is an initiative that encourages companies to implement health and well-being policies for their employees. The awarded companies have demonstrated a concrete commitment to improving working conditions by introducing well-being programs, physical activities, and psychological support. This recognition is an important signal for companies operating in the border area between Italy and Switzerland, where collaboration between public and private entities is fundamental. The awarded companies come from various sectors, including manufacturing, services, and commerce. The award ceremony took place in Varese, with the participation of representatives from local institutions and the awarded companies. Implications for cross-border workers For cross-border workers who commute to Ticino and reside in Italy, joining the WHP program represents an important opportunity to improve their quality of life and work. Companies that join the program offer a series of benefits to their employees, including fitness programs, health training courses, and psychological support services. ### Benefits for employees Employees of companies that join the WHP program benefit from a series of initiatives aimed at improving their health and well-being. These programs include: - Fitness programs and physical activities - Health and well-being training courses - Psychological support services - Initiatives for stress prevention and management - Healthy eating and nutrition programs These benefits not only improve the quality of life for employees but also help reduce absenteeism and increase job satisfaction. For cross-border workers commuting to Ticino, these programs represent an important opportunity to improve their quality of life and work. ### Comparison with the previous situation Before the introduction of the WHP program, many companies did not have health and well-being policies for their employees. This led to a series of problems, including increased absenteeism, reduced productivity, and greater job dissatisfaction. The introduction of the WHP program represented a significant step forward in improving the quality of life and work for employees. What to do to join the WHP program To join the WHP program, companies must follow a series of steps. These steps include submitting an application, evaluating the health and well-being initiatives implemented, and participating in a certification process. ### Steps to join 1. Submit the application 2. Evaluation of the health and well-being initiatives implemented 3. Participation in the certification process 4. Recognition and award Companies wishing to join the WHP program must submit an application to the Confindustria Varese or ATS Insubria website. The application must include a description of the health and well-being initiatives implemented by the company. ### Evaluation of initiatives The health and well-being initiatives implemented by the company will be evaluated by a commission of experts. The commission will evaluate the quality and effectiveness of the initiatives implemented, as well as their impact on the health and well-being of employees. Companies that achieve a sufficiently high score will be invited to participate in the certification process. ### Certification process Companies participating in the certification process must demonstrate that they have implemented a series of health and well-being initiatives for their employees. These initiatives must be consistent with the criteria of the WHP program and must be positively evaluated by the commission of experts. Companies that obtain certification will be awarded during an official ceremony. ### Recognition and award Companies that obtain WHP certification will be awarded during an official ceremony. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the benefits of the WHP program for employees?
Employees of companies that join the WHP program benefit from a series of initiatives aimed at improving their health and well-being. These programs include physical activity, health training courses, psychological support services, initiatives for stress prevention and management, and healthy eating and nutrition programs.
How can I join the WHP program?
To join the WHP program, companies must submit an application to the website of Confindustria Varese or ATS Insubria. The application must include a description of the health and well-being initiatives implemented by the company. The initiatives will be evaluated by a commission of experts and companies that achieve a sufficiently high score will be invited to participate in the certification process.
What are the evaluation criteria for the WHP program?
The evaluation criteria for the WHP program include the quality and effectiveness of the health and well-being initiatives implemented by the company, as well as their impact on the health and well-being of employees. Companies must demonstrate that they have implemented a series of initiatives consistent with the criteria of the WHP program and have achieved positive results in the evaluation by the commission of experts.
