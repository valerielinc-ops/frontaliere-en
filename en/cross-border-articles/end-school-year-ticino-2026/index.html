Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main dates for school holidays in Ticino for 2026?
The 2026 school holidays in Canton Ticino include breaks for Christmas, Carnival, Easter, and the summer break which starts around mid-June. These dates are official and published by the cantonal Education Department.
How do school holidays affect the lives of cross-border workers in Canton Ticino?
School holidays influence the work and family planning of cross-border workers, often requiring leave permits and careful management of the G permit, LAMal insurance, and source deductions.
What procedures should cross-border workers follow to manage absences during school holidays?
Cross-border workers should consult the official calendar, request leave from their employer, verify LAMal coverage, consider crossing times at border posts, and coordinate tax management with consultants.
