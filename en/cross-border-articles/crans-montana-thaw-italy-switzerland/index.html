Among the guests of the show hosted by Reto Ceschi, Cornado recalled how institutional dialogue has never ceased, even after months marked by polemics, misunderstandings, and very harsh positions taken by Italy. At the center, once again, the consequences of the fire that occurred at the bar Le Constellation, with 41 people losing their lives and more than a hundred injured. According to Cornado, the meeting between the Confederation's president Guy Parmelin and the Council's president Giorgia Meloni represented a turning point compared to the polemics of the past weeks. A political step that, in the words of the diplomat, could mark the beginning of a different phase in the relations between Rome and Berna, beyond the dossiers and bilateral agreements that have continued their course, as he himself explained, without interruptions. ### Cornado's statements The Italian diplomat first toned down the rhetoric regarding the possible investigation into the rescue operations during the night of the incident.
