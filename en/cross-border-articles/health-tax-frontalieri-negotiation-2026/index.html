"If Forza Italia wants to review the measure, we are here. We need a real debate and not propaganda," Astuti affirmed. The regional councilor of Forza Italia Giuseppe Licata reiterated the openness to dialogue, underlining the necessity to address the theme with seriousness and without slogans. "Today, as it stands, we are faced with confusing and inconsistent rules, as is already happening between different Regions, without national coordination and without a real parliamentary and institutional debate," Licata declared. Astuti criticized the approach of the majority, accusing them of not having activated a real process of sharing with local administrators, unions, and worker representatives. "In the regional council, we have asked several times to open a serious and structured discussion, but the majority, every time, has decided not to listen to us," Astuti added.
