Dettagli operativi

The transformation of the old FART building in Camedo into a hub for regional promotion represents a strategic opportunity to revitalize the local economy and capitalize on a key location in the Locarno region. This project, with an estimated investment of 1.2 million Swiss francs, was approved by the Municipality of Centovalli in 2022 under the Cantonal Economic Promotion Law (LPrEco), which allows up to 25% contributions for regional development initiatives. The former buffet, which served railway travelers for years, will be replaced by a modern, multifunctional facility. The new building will include modular meeting rooms capable of hosting up to 150 people, a fully equipped kitchen for tailored catering services, and a large parking area with 50 spaces. This setup will enable the organization of corporate conferences, training seminars, and small international congresses. For instance, a company based in Bellinzona could use the venue for a two-day seminar, taking advantage of the natural surroundings for team-building activities. Camedo's strategic location, just 20 minutes by car from Locarno and close to the Italian border, is another key advantage. Its proximity to the Centovallina railway, which connects Domodossola to Locarno, ensures sustainable mobility for both participants and organizers. In line with sustainability goals, the project includes the installation of solar panels on the roof, supporting Ticino's objective to reduce CO2 emissions by 50% by 2030. 📊 Practical comparison: Currently, many SMEs in the region travel to Lugano or even beyond the Gotthard for events, incurring logistical costs that can exceed 5,000 CHF for venue rentals and services. Camedo, the buffet ready to become the perfect event location For event organizers, choosing Camedo as a venue could prove to be both a captivating and strategic decision. Located in the heart of Centovalli, at the border between Switzerland and Italy, this small village offers a unique combination of breathtaking natural landscapes and modern facilities, perfectly suited for medium-sized events. ### A mix of nature and functionality Camedo’s main facility, formerly known for its railway buffet, has recently been expanded to include a 120-square-meter multifunctional hall, ideal for corporate meetings, workshops, or private celebrations. The hall is equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi, state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment, and a catering area with a professional kitchen, making it perfect for events requiring customized dining services. For those seeking a more intimate setting, a smaller 40-square-meter room is also available, designed for small meetings or training sessions. ### An economical and original option One notable aspect is the competitive cost compared to other venues in Canton Ticino. For example, renting the multifunctional hall for a day costs approximately 500 CHF, with an additional 25 CHF per person for buffet services. By comparison, a similar-sized venue in Locarno could cost up to 1,000 CHF per day, excluding catering. The availability of an outdoor area with stunning mountain views makes Camedo an especially attractive option for spring and summer events. ### Regulatory and practical aspects Organizing an event in Ticino requires compliance with specific regulations. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the current state of the transformation works of the old FART building in Camedo?
Work began in January 2023 and should continue until the end of spring 2024.
What is the potential economic impact of the new structure in Camedo on the local economy?
The new structure could generate an annual income of more than 500,000 francs between hospitality, catering and related services, as evidenced by similar examples in Ticino.
What are the regulations to organize an event in Camedo with more than 100 participants?
Organizers must notify the presence of more than 100 participants in the Municipality of Centovalli at least 15 days before the event. This is necessary to ensure the management of safety and viability, in accordance with cantonal regulations on large events.
How can I book the rooms for an event in Camedo?
Reservations for rooms in Camedo are open from January 2024. Organizers must make the reservation in advance and request a quote for catering within 30 days of the event to ensure the availability and the necessary services.
What sustainability measures have been implemented in the new structure of Camedo?
The new structure of Camedo includes the installation of solar panels on the roof, in line with the objective of Canton Ticino to reduce CO2 emissions by 50% by 2030. This initiative highlights the project's commitment to sustainable practices.
