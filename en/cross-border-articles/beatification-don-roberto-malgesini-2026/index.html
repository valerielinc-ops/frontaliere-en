Beatification process for Don Roberto Malgesini begins

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

The Diocese of Como has started the process for the beatification of Don Roberto Malgesini, who was killed in 2020.

Contesto

In brief - Diocese of Como starts beatification process for Fr. Roberto Malgesini - Edict signed by Bishop Oscar Cantoni on April 30, 2026 - Community involvement for testimonies and documents ## Key facts - What: Start of beatification process for Fr. Roberto Malgesini - When: April 30, 2026 - Where: Diocese of Como - Who: Diocese of Como, Bishop Oscar Cantoni - Amount: Not yet specified The Diocese of Como has officially started the process for the beatification of Fr. Roberto Malgesini, the priest killed in 2020 and remembered for his commitment to the poor. With an edict signed by Bishop Oscar Cantoni and released on April 30, 2026, the diocesan phase of the process that could lead to his beatification and canonization has been opened. The document, dated April 30, 2026, outlines the steps that led to this decision, which came after the approval of the Dicastery for the Causes of Saints and the favorable opinion of the Lombard Episcopal Conference. A significant moment for the Church of Como and for those who knew Fr. Malgesini. The edict emphasizes how the decision to start the cause entails 'grave responsibility' and requires direct involvement from the community. For this reason, an explicit invitation is extended to anyone who can provide useful elements. In particular, those aware of facts that could challenge the priest's reputation for holiness are asked to report them to the bishop or the postulator of the cause. Similarly, those in possession of writings, documents, or testimonies related to Fr. Malgesini are invited to submit them to the Curia of Como. The document will be posted for four weeks in various locations within the diocese: from the Curia of Como to the parishes linked to Fr. Malgesini's ministry, including those in Como, Gravedona, Lipomo,...

Implications for the Community The initiation of the beatification process for Don Roberto Malgesini represents a significant moment not only for the Diocese of Como but also for the local community and for all those who knew and appreciated the priest. The collection of testimonies and documents useful for reconstructing his life and work implies active and participatory involvement from the faithful and citizens. ### Procedure and Deadlines The document will remain posted for four weeks in various locations within the diocese, including the Bishop's Curia of Como and the parishes linked to Don Malgesini's ministry. This period is crucial for gathering all the necessary information to proceed with the beatification process. Publication in the official Ecclesiastical Bulletin represents a further step towards transparency and community participation. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation Before the initiation of this process, Don Roberto Malgesini was already held in high esteem and respect for his social and religious commitment. However, the official start of the beatification process represents a formal and institutional recognition that could lead to a greater dissemination of his message of charity and solidarity. ### Future Scenarios If the beatification process concludes positively, Don Roberto Malgesini could become a reference model for the Christian community and for all those engaged in social work.
