Frequently Asked Questions
How does traffic planning work on the Gotthard?
The Lugano Gotthard Motorway Administration prepares an emergency plan to manage possible slowdowns or sudden queues, providing for the mobilization of additional emergency vehicles and the preparation of wider alternative routes.
What was the impact of the 2022 Ticino Council of State legislation on local tourism?
The legislation, which offers tax relief for holiday home rentals of at least 3 weeks, has increased the demand for affordable housing, leading to a 25% increase in the number of guest houses in Ticino and a 30% increase in traffic on the state road 13.
What are the main measures taken to manage cross-border traffic between Switzerland and Italy during busy periods?
Swiss and Italian authorities work together through emergency plans, alternative routes and intelligent traffic management systems, such as real-time monitoring and coordination between emergency services, to ensure smooth and safe traffic during traffic peaks.
