Varese: 109 athletes at Salewa Cube for Italian Lead Cup

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

On Saturday 2nd and Sunday 3rd May, Salewa Cube in Varese hosts the second stage of the Italian Lead Cup with 109 athletes among the best in the nation.

Contesto

In brief - 109 athletes at the Salewa Cube for the Italian Lead Cup - Qualifiers on Saturday, May 2, finals on Sunday, May 3 - Claudia Ghisolfi and Davide Colombo among the favorites ## Key facts - What: Second stage of the Italian Lead Cup - When: May 2-3, 2026 - Where: Salewa Cube, San Fermo, Varese - Who: 109 athletes, including Claudia Ghisolfi and Davide Colombo - Size: 1000 m² area with 132 climbing routes The splendid structure of San Fermo hosts the second stage of the Italian Lead Cup: today at 10 am the men's and women's qualifiers, tomorrow morning the semifinals and then at 4 pm the finals. Vertical challenge and emotions at the Salewa Cube in San Fermo, Varese. Chalk on the hands, eyes upward, harness ready to let the rope slide to the highest carabiner, concentration combined with the right dose of tension: these are just some of the ingredients for the stage of the Italian Lead Cup, which will take place today, Saturday, May 2, and tomorrow, Sunday, May 3, in Varese, at the brand new Salewa Cube facility. This event will feature 109 athletes, among the best in the nation. It is the second leg of the circuit, which had its debut in April at the Climbing District in Pero, and will conclude in Campitello di Fassa, at the Adel Climbing Wall, on June 13-14. However, do not overlook the most anticipated national event: the Italian Lead Championship, which will take place on May 16-17 at the Federal Technical Center in Arco (TN). Supporting FASI in the organization of this competition is ASD Rock Brescia, while the hosting structure, recently opened, boasts over 1000 m² of area, with 132 lines designed for various levels, and represents a modern and high-quality scenario for the climbers who will be engaged on its walls. ### Athletes in competition Among th...
