One of the main changes concerns the obligation to provide more detailed information about their financial activities in Italy and Switzerland. This includes declaring income and financial assets in both countries, as well as submitting additional documents that may be requested by tax authorities. Another important aspect is the increased scrutiny by tax authorities. With access to more complete and detailed data, Swiss and Italian authorities will be able to conduct more accurate and targeted tax audits. This could lead to an increase in tax checks for cross-border workers who are not compliant with current regulations. To address these new challenges, it is essential that cross-border workers prepare adequately.
