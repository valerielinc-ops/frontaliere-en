Implications for cross-border workers Astuti's criticisms raise important questions for cross-border workers who work in Switzerland and reside in Italy. The health tax, introduced in 2021, is a sensitive measure that can have a significant impact on the finances of cross-border workers. For example, a worker earning 4,000 CHF per month must pay approximately 100 CHF in health tax, a cost that can increase based on income and family situation. Uncertainty in relations with Switzerland and the management of refunds can also influence the economic stability of border areas, such as Chiasso, Chiavenna, and Campione d'Italia.
