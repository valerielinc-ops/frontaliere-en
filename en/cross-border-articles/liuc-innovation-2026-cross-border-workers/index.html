LIUC: Innovation Week 2026 focuses on AI and startups (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 6 May 2026

From May 12 to 15, 2026, LIUC in Castellanza hosts Innovation Week with workshops on AI, startups, and new skills demanded by the market.

Contesto

In brief - Innovation Week at LIUC from May 12 to 15, 2026 - Workshops on AI, startups, and future skills - Participation of Daniele Cassioli and Elia Bombardelli ## Key facts - What: Innovation Week at LIUC - When: May 12-15, 2026 - Where: LIUC Università Cattaneo, Castellanza - Who: Gloria Puliga, Rector's Delegate for Innovation - Cost: Not yet specified The future is knocking at the doors of LIUC – Università Cattaneo. Indeed, Innovation Week is back, a three-day event of workshops and debates coordinated by Gloria Puliga, Rector's Delegate for Innovation, to explore the frontiers of Artificial Intelligence, funding channels for startups, and the skills required by tomorrow's market. The initiative is open to students, managers, and startuppers interested in navigating the opportunities of change. ### Detailed program The curtain rises on Tuesday, May 12 (11:00 AM, Bussolati Hall) with the event 'Build your future,' organized in collaboration with Intesa San Paolo. The meeting, designed to reflect on the challenges of tomorrow, will feature guests who have made resilience and communication their strong points: Paralympic athlete Daniele Cassioli, the famous 'web professor' Elia Bombardelli, and Antonello Nakhleh, who will share his journey from San Patrignano to a career in dog training. On Thursday, May 14 (2:30 PM, Auditorium), the focus shifts to the world of entrepreneurship with the 'Start Up Day.' One year after its launch, the university will take stock of the Business Angel Hub, a project born to connect high-potential ideas with investors, confirming LIUC's active role as an engine of the local innovation ecosystem.
