Dettagli operativi

Endometriosis is a medical condition that affects women, characterized by the growth of endometrial tissue outside the uterus. This tissue, normally present inside the uterus, can grow in other parts of the body, such as the ovaries, Fallopian tubes, or the pelvic region. Endometriosis symptoms can vary from woman to woman but often include pelvic, menstrual, or sexual intercourse pain, as well as chronic fatigue and fertility issues. Traditional endometriosis diagnosis can be complex and often requires invasive procedures, such as laparoscopy, which involves general anesthesia and an abdominal incision. These procedures can be costly and require significant recovery time. The saliva test for endometriosis, however, offers a less invasive and more accessible alternative. The test, developed by a team of Swiss researchers, analyzes the patient's saliva for specific biomarkers associated with endometriosis. The test results can help doctors confirm or rule out an endometriosis diagnosis, enabling timely and targeted treatment. The test has been validated through clinical studies and has proven accurate in identifying endometriosis at various stages of the disease. In Switzerland, the saliva test for endometriosis is available at some specialized centers and laboratories. The test's cost, which exceeds 800 Swiss francs, can be a significant barrier for many women. As mentioned earlier, Swiss health insurers generally do not reimburse this test, making it even more difficult for women with limited resources to access it. Frequently Asked Questions
How much does the saliva test for endometriosis cost in Switzerland and is it reimbursed by health insurers?
The saliva test for endometriosis costs over 800 Swiss francs in Switzerland and is currently generally not reimbursed by Swiss health insurers.
Can I take a saliva test for endometriosis in Italy and then ask for reimbursement in Switzerland?
No, Swiss health insurers generally do not reimburse tests carried out abroad, including saliva tests for endometriosis, unless specifically agreed or exempted by their insurance policy.
Is the saliva test for endometriosis also available in Italy near the border with Switzerland?
Yes, some Italian laboratories near the Swiss border, such as those in Lombardy (e.g. Varese or Como), offer saliva testing for endometriosis. However, the cost is similar (800-900 CHF/EUR) and is not reimbursed by the NHS. Check availability by contacting the centers directly.
Can I use Italian health insurance for the saliva test if I work in Switzerland?
No, the saliva test is not included in the Italian LEAs (Essential Levels of Assistance) and is not reimbursed by the NHS. If you are insured in Switzerland, the test will also not be covered by your Swiss health insurance, as indicated in the article.
What are the free or low-cost alternatives to diagnose endometriosis in Ticino?
In Ticino you can access gynecological examinations and pelvic ultrasounds through the Cantonal Health Service (SSC) or local associations such as 'Endometriosis Ticino'. These options are partially or totally free, but require a prescription.
