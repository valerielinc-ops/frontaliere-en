The deadline for submission is set for April 30, 2026, but it is possible to request an extension online. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the deadline for the delivery of the tax declaration 2025?
The deadline for the delivery of the tax declaration 2025 is 30 April 2026.
Can I use eTax 2025 even if I transferred home abroad in 2025?
Yes, you can use eTax 2025, but you must indicate the data until the end of the tax subjecting period in Switzerland.
What documents do I need to collect to correctly fill out the income statement 2025?
To correctly fill out the 2025 income statement, collect the following documents: single income certificate (CU), bank certificates, deduction certificates, insurance documents and the second and third pillar. These documents will help you to accurately report your incomes and deductions.
How can I apply for an extension for income statement 2025?
You can apply for an extension for the tax return of 2025 online through the official website of Canton Ticino. Use the QR code included in the material sent to quickly access the portal and forward your request for extension.
What expenditure can I deduct in my income statement 2025?
In your income statement 2025, you can include several deductible expenses, such as transport costs to get to work, voluntary contributions to the third pillar and other expenses recognised at cantonal and federal level. Check the deductions available to maximize your tax savings.
