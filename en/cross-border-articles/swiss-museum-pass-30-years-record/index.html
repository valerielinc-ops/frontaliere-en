Swiss Museum Pass: 30 years and a historic record The Swiss Museum Pass, also known as Museumspass, is celebrating its 30th anniversary this year with a historic record: over 100,000 passes sold annually. This tool, ideal for culture lovers and cross-border workers in Ticino, offers unlimited access to more than 500 museums across Switzerland, including numerous sites of interest in the Canton of Ticino. ### Purchase procedure 1. Visit the official website of the Museumspass Foundation. 2. Select the type of pass desired: - Individual: 120 CHF/year - Family: 180 CHF/year (2 adults + children up to 16 years) - Junior (6-16 years): 35 CHF/year 3. Complete the purchase online following the instructions. 4. Receive the pass via email or collect it at one of the participating museums, such as the Cantonal Art Museum of Lugano or the Museum of Cultures in Lugano. ### Costs and benefits The cost of the pass varies depending on the type chosen. The individual pass costs 120 CHF, while the family pass (for two adults and children up to 16 years) costs 180 CHF. The pass offers unlimited access to all museums in the network, making it a advantageous investment for those who frequently visit museums. 📊 Concrete example: - A family of four (2 adults + 2 children) that visits 10 museums in a year spends approximately 200 CHF without the pass (average 20 CHF per entry). With the family pass, the cost is 180 CHF, saving 20 CHF. ### Using the pass Once you have the pass, you can use it to visit any museum in the network. Simply present the pass at the museum entrance to gain access.
