Red alert on A2 between Chiasso and Lugano

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

Delays up to one hour and forty minutes between Chiasso and Lugano Nord due to a breakdown vehicle. Slowdowns also on cantonal roads.

At 6:00 AM, the agency reported that the situation was causing significant slowdowns. As of the last update at 7:53 AM, estimated delays on the A2 between Chiasso-Centro and the San Salvatore tunnel were around one hour. Slowdowns were also reported between Lugano south and Lugano north.
