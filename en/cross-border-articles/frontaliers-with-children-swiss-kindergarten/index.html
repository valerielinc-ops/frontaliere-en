Frequently Asked Questions
What are the costs for enrolling in a Ticino nursery?
The costs vary depending on the municipality and the type of structure chosen. In general, the costs are higher than those of Italian nurseries. However, frontier workers can benefit from subsidies and exemptions.
How does the waiting list for Ticino nurseries work?
The waiting list for Ticino nurseries can be long, so it is advisable to register in advance. In some municipalities, the waiting list can even be up to two years long. Therefore, it is important to find out in good time about the opportunities offered by the different structures.
Do frontier workers with children have the right to benefit from the same services as Swiss citizens?
Yes, frontier workers with children have the right to benefit from the same services as Swiss citizens. In particular, frontier workers with children can request the subsidy for the insertion of children in Ticino nurseries.
