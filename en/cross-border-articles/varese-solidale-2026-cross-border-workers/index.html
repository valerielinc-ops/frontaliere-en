Varese Solidale 2026: two days against healthcare poverty (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 7 May 2026

From May 15 to 16, 42 associations in Varese mobilize to combat healthcare poverty with conferences, screenings, and a solidarity dinner

Contesto

In brief - 42 associations in Varese united against health poverty - Conferences, screenings, and a solidarity dinner on May 15 and 16 - Focus on the Pharmaceutical Bank and the recovery of medicines ## Key facts - What: Varese is Solidale against health poverty - When: May 15 and 16, 2026 - Where: Varese, city center - Who: 42 associations in Varese, Caritas Zona Pastorale di Varese - Amount: Not yet specified 'Varese is Solidale' returns: on May 15 and 16, volunteers mobilize against health poverty. Two days of meetings, testimonies, prevention, solidarity, and fundraising to tell the story of Varese's volunteer network and put one of the most urgent needs of the territory at the center: health poverty. The 2026 edition celebrates its tenth anniversary involving 42 associations from the area united by a common goal: to stimulate attention towards those who have less and to build together concrete responses to the needs that emerge every day. The theme chosen for this edition arises from direct observation of the fragilities that emerge every day: 'More and more people find themselves in the condition of having to choose between eating or taking care of themselves.' ### The role of the Pharmaceutical Bank Among the protagonists of the event is also the Pharmaceutical Bank, which has been engaged for years in the fight against health poverty. Dina Ermolli explained the work carried out: 'For thirteen years, there has been an observatory on health poverty composed of experts who every year draft a report presented at the headquarters of Aifa. The Pharmaceutical Bank responds to these needs through the recovery of medicines and thanks to the collaboration with entities that already operate in the territory.' Frequently Asked Questions
What is Varese è Solidale?
Varese è Solidale is an annual event that involves numerous associations from Varese in the fight against health poverty. During the event, conferences, health screenings, informative exhibitions, and a solidarity dinner are organized to raise awareness and collect funds.
How can I participate in Varese è Solidale?
You can participate in the events organized during the manifestation, such as conferences, health screenings, informative exhibitions, and the solidarity dinner. Additionally, you can support the participating associations through donations, volunteering, and spreading the word.
What health services are offered during Varese è Solidale?
During the event, free health screenings, information on health services, and access to free or discounted medicines and health products are offered. The participating associations provide support and assistance to those in need.
