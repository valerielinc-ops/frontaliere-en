Stricter fuel checks in Como province (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

The Guardia di Finanza in Como has intensified checks on fuel distributors, issuing four fines for price irregularities.

Contesto

In brief - Guardia di Finanza checks fuel prices in Como - Four fines issued for irregularities found - Checks on distributors to protect consumers ## Key facts - What: Checks on fuel prices - When: May 2026 - Where: Province of Como - Who: Guardia di Finanza of Como - Amount: Four fines issued The Guardia di Finanza of Como has intensified checks on fuel distributors in the province, aiming to ensure transparency and correctness of pump prices. The operation, conducted by the provincial command of the Fiamme Gialle, resulted in the issuance of four fines following irregularities found in the prices applied. The verification activity comes amid rising fuel costs linked to international geopolitical tensions, directly affecting the cost of living and economic activities. The operation involved several distribution plants across the province, selected through risk analysis and joint work with the Price Supervision Authority and the Ministry of Enterprises and Made in Italy. The goal is to intercept and counter any speculative practices, ensuring that pump prices are correctly communicated and not altered by unfair practices. ### Irregularities found The Como Group identified several violations against four distributors. These included discrepancies between displayed and actual prices, failure to display the differential between 'served' and 'self', and omissions in communicating price changes to the ministry portal. The regulations require operators to periodically communicate prices through the 'Osservaprezzi carburanti' portal, a tool designed to ensure transparency and constant monitoring of market trends. Reporting irregularities If discrepancies are found between the displayed prices and those charged, the issue can be reported to the competent authorities. The Guardia di Finanza and the Price Surveillance Ombudsman are entities that can intervene to ensure price transparency and correctness. ### 3. Using the 'Osservaprezzi carburanti' portal The 'Osservaprezzi carburanti' portal is a useful tool for monitoring fuel prices and checking for any variations. Consumers can access the portal to check the prices charged by different distributors and report any irregularities. ### 4. Keeping evidence In case of irregularities, it's important to keep evidence, such as receipts or photos of the displayed prices. Frequently Asked Questions
What should I do if I notice irregularities in fuel prices?
If you notice irregularities in fuel prices, you can report the issue to the Guardia di Finanza or the Price Surveillance Commissioner. It's important to keep evidence of the irregularities, such as receipts or photos of the displayed prices, to support your report.
What are the penalties for distributors who charge incorrect prices?
Distributors who charge incorrect prices may face high fines from the Guardia di Finanza. Penalties can include fines and other corrective measures to ensure price transparency and accuracy.
How can I check fuel prices before refueling?
You can check fuel prices using apps or websites that monitor fuel prices. It's also useful to compare prices among different distributors and verify that the displayed prices match those actually charged.
