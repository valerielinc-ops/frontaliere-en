In an interview with the Neue Zürcher Zeitung, Cornado stated: 'I speak without mincing words. That's my style.' Italian Prime Minister Giorgia Meloni would have asked him to pay particular attention to the hospital bills related to the care provided to injured Italians. 'And that's exactly what I've done,' Cornado declared. In an interview with Ticinonews, Cassis had stated that the 'endless discussion' about the events in Crans-Montana (VS) was also fueled by Cornado. According to the FDFA head, it would be up to the ambassador to calm the tensions in bilateral relations. At the end of April, Meloni had been outraged that the hospital bills and the related copies had been sent from Switzerland to Italy. Such bills concerned the care provided to the victims of the fire that broke out on New Year's Eve at the Le Constellation bar in Crans-Montana (VS). ### Parmelin's statements The President of the Confederation, Guy Parmelin, assured that this will not happen again. The Federal Council will examine the issue of billing for hospital services among the interested states based on the legal provisions in force, Parmelin declared last Tuesday.
