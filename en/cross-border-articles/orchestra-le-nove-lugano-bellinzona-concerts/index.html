Di Valerie Linc · Published on 12 May 2026

Orchestra Le Nove performs Tchaikovsky in Lugano and Bellinzona on May 16 and 18, 2026. Here are the details on the programs and concert venues.

In brief - Double musical appointment on May 16 and 18, 2026. - Featuring Orchestra Le Nove with music by Tchaikovsky. - Concerts scheduled in Lugano and Bellinzona. - Debut in string orchestra formation. ## Key facts - What: Orchestra Le Nove concerts - When: Saturday, May 16 (11:00 and 20:30) and Monday, May 18 (20:30) - Where: Evangelical Church of Lugano and Teatro Sociale in Bellinzona - Who: Orchestra Le Nove - Program: Serenade for Strings and Souvenir de Florence by Tchaikovsky Orchestra Le Nove is preparing to return to the Ticinese stage with a program entirely dedicated to the Russian composer Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky. This choice of times aims to encourage the participation of different audience segments, from residents to workers who wish to dedicate their free time to culture. The concert series will conclude on Monday, May 18, at 20:30 at the Teatro Sociale in Bellinzona. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the dates and venues for the Orchestra Le Nove concerts?
Orchestra Le Nove will perform on Saturday, May 16, 2026, at the Evangelical Church in Lugano, with two shows at 11:00 AM and 8:30 PM. Subsequently, on Monday, May 18, 2026, the orchestra will hold a concert at 8:30 PM at the Teatro Sociale in Bellinzona.
What is the planned musical program?
The concert program is dedicated to the Russian composer Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky. Two main works will be performed: the Serenade for Strings and the Souvenir de Florence, pieces chosen for their expressive intensity and for highlighting chamber music.
What distinguishes this concert series by Orchestra Le Nove?
The uniqueness of these events lies in the debut of Orchestra Le Nove in a specific string orchestra formation. Furthermore, the orchestra has chosen to perform in intimate venues to foster a more direct and close relationship with the audience, a central objective of the new programming.
