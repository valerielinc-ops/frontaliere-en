The sixth edition of ComanoRun saw a record of 801 participants and new achievements in the men's and women's categories.

Contesto

In brief - Record of 801 participants at the ComanoRun 2026 - Tommaso Marani wins with 2'30" - Presence of Ramon Tanner and Joren van Pottelberghe ## Key facts - What: Sixth edition of the BancaStato ComanoRun - When: May 4, 2026 - Where: Comano, Canton Ticino - Who: Organized by BancaStato - Number of participants: 801 - Male winner: Tommaso Marani (2'30") - Female winner: Francesca Sabena (29'48") - Special guests: Ramon Tanner and Joren van Pottelberghe (HC Lugano) The sixth edition of the BancaStato ComanoRun set a new participation record with 801 registered participants. The event, held on May 4, 2026, saw the participation of athletes from various categories, including hockey players Ramon Tanner and Joren van Pottelberghe from HC Lugano, who signed autographs and posed for photos. In the men's category, Tommaso Marani from Sal Lugano won first place with a record time of 2'30" over the 7 km course. Andrea Alagona from Triathlon Team Ticino came in second with 23'48", followed by Evan Dingle from Cptc - Tracksmith with 25'7". Among the women, Francesca Sabena from Ocr Ticino won with a time of 29'48", ahead of Cristina Maggetti from SP Locarnese and Sara Solari from Sam Massagno. There was also great enthusiasm in the youth, popular, walking, and nordic walking categories. Complete rankings are available on the official event website, www.comanorun.ch. ### Implications for cross-border workers The sports event saw a record participation, but what does this mean for cross-border workers living and working in Ticino?
