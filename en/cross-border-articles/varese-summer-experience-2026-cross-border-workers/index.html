Wellness and Awareness Activities The Summer Experience courses offered by Varese Corsi provide a variety of activities dedicated to wellness and awareness, with a particular focus on outdoor experiences. Among these, Sunrise Yoga: waking up in the green, takes place every Saturday morning at 6:30 am at Villa Mirabello Park, with a cost of 15 CHF per session. > "This morning practice is perfect for starting the day with positive energy," says Laura, a regular participant. Sunset Yoga: relaxation at sunset, on the other hand, takes place every Thursday at 7:00 pm at Lake Varese, with a cost of 20 CHF per session. For those who prefer a gentler approach, Gentle Gymnastics: soft training & nature takes place every Tuesday and Friday at 10:00 am at Villa Estense Park, with a cost of 12 CHF per session. Finally, Mindful Walk: awareness paths, takes place every Sunday at 9:00 am at the Sacro Monte di Varese, with a cost of 18 CHF per session. These experiences are designed to slow down and find balance, taking advantage of the beauty of the parks and historic villas of Varese. For those who work in Ticino and wish to participate, it is important to consider the cross-border tax regulations. According to the 1983 Lugano Convention, cross-border workers are subject to taxation only in the country of residence, but must also declare their income in Switzerland.
