Contesto

In brief - Gastronomic walk in Cazzago Brabbia on Sunday, May 17, 2026 - 5 km route with enogastronomic stops - Tickets from 15 to 30 euros ## Key facts - What: Gastronomic and cultural walk - When: Sunday, May 17, 2026, from 11:00 to 17:30 - Where: Cazzago Brabbia - Who: Organized by local entities - Cost: 30 euros adults, 15 euros children On Sunday, May 17, 2026, in Cazzago Brabbia, the second edition of 'Camminan Mangiando' will take place, an event that combines a walk of about 5 kilometers with a gastronomic route and a rich calendar of cultural and recreational activities. The initiative is designed to be accessible to everyone and winds through the streets of the village and the surrounding nature, with walking stretches of about 20-30 minutes between one stop and another. The heart of the day is the itinerant lunch, which develops along the village with several stops. The start and the collection of gadgets will take place at Elicola Tabacchi Cartoleria Donata Maffioli. The stops include the aperitif at Bar San Carlo, the first course at Melamangio, the second course at Bon Bottega, the end of the meal at Oasi Bar Darsena 350, the snack again at Bar San Carlo and the collection of the final dessert at Elicola Tabacchi Cartoleria Donata Maffioli. The route is by reservation and includes a cost of 30 euros for adults and 15 euros for children up to 10 years, with reduced portions and non-alcoholic drinks. Tickets are available through the link on the dedicated Facebook page or by scanning the QR code on the posters. Reservations must be made by May 12, 2026, and places are limited. ### Activities and events scheduled Alongside the gastronomic route, the day is enriched by numerous cultural and recreational initiatives open to everyone.
