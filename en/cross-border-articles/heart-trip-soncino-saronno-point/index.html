Heartfelt trip to Soncino to support Saronno Point

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

A day of history, art, and solidarity to fund Saronno Point's activities for cancer patients.

Contesto

In brief - Heartfelt trip to Soncino to support Saronno Point - Participation fee: 70 euros, deposit of 35 euros - Registrations at Saronno Point's headquarters - Concrete example: in 2023, the trip raised 5,000 euros for home care projects ## Key facts - What: Heartfelt trip to Soncino - When: Sunday, May 17, 2026 - Where: Soncino, province of Cremona - Who: Saronno Point Odv - Fee: 70 euros per person, deposit of 35 euros - Registration deadline: Thursday and Saturday from 15:00 to 18:00 - Operational checklist: - Payment of 35 euro deposit - Completion of registration form - Check availability of seats (max 50 participants) A day between history, art, and solidarity to support those facing illness. Saronno Point Odv is organizing a new “Heartfelt Trip” for Sunday, May 17, an initiative open to the public with destination Soncino, in the province of Cremona, one of the most fascinating villages in Lombardy. The trip represents an opportunity to combine the recreational moment with the solidarity one, contributing directly to the activities of the organization. Discovering Soncino The chosen destination is Soncino, known for its Sforza castle and well-preserved medieval center. The day will allow participants to visit the village and spend time together, also enhancing the cultural heritage of the territory. For residents of Ticino, this trip represents an opportunity to explore a nearby but often overlooked destination, with a cost accessible compared to longer trips. For example, a similar trip to Lugano could cost up to 100 euros per person, while the trip to Soncino offers a similar experience at a lower price. Dettagli operativi

Implications for cross-border workers The 'Gita del cuore' represents a significant opportunity for cross-border workers employed in Ticino, offering them the chance to participate in solidarity initiatives without having to undertake long journeys. According to data from the Federal Statistical Office, in 2023 there were approximately 68,000 cross-border workers in Ticino, primarily from Varese, Como, and Novara. This initiative allows for combining a cultural experience with a concrete contribution to support activities for cancer patients and their families, such as the accompaniment services and complementary therapies offered by Saronno Point. ### Comparison with other initiatives Compared to other organized outings, this one stands out for its dual objective: offering a high-quality tourist and cultural experience while supporting an important social cause. The choice of Soncino as the destination adds extra value, allowing participants to discover a historic village of great charm. Unlike other initiatives, such as company outings that often limit themselves to recreational events, the 'Gita del cuore' integrates an element of solidarity, making it more meaningful. ### Practical scenarios Cross-border workers who wish to participate can plan their day to balance work commitments. For example, a cross-border worker employed in Lugano could leave early in the morning and return in the afternoon, taking advantage of rail connections such as the regional train that connects Lugano to Varese in about an hour and a half. The outing is an opportunity to spend a different day, away from the daily routine, while contributing to a charitable cause at the same time. How to Participate To participate in the "Gita del cuore" to Soncino, follow these simple steps: 1. Registration: Visit the Saronno Point office at via San Giuseppe 41 in Saronno on Thursdays and Saturdays from 3 PM to 6 PM. Cross-border workers from Ticino, such as those from Mendrisio or Chiasso, can plan their visit based on their work hours. For example, someone working in Lugano and returning home in the afternoon can easily stop by Saronno on their way back. 2. Payment: Pay a deposit of 35 euros at the time of registration and the remaining 35 euros later. Payment can be made in cash or by credit card. For cross-border workers, it's useful to know that the euro-Swiss franc exchange rate is currently favorable, with a rate of approximately 1.05 CHF for 1 EUR, making the trip more affordable. 3. Information: Contact the number 338 63774481 for more details. You can also send an email to info@saronnopoint.it to request specific information. ### Required Documents No specific documents are required for participation, but it is advisable to bring a copy of the payment receipt and, if available, a Saronno Point membership card. For cross-border workers, it is useful to have a valid ID document, especially if traveling in a group and needing to prove group membership. ### Deadlines Registrations are open until all spots are filled. It is advisable to book in advance to secure your participation. For example, for a similar trip organized last year, all spots were filled in less than two weeks. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can participate in the Gita del Cuore to Soncino?
The outing is open to everyone, residents and cross-border workers, who wish to support the activities of Saronno Point and spend a day between history, art, and solidarity.
How can I sign up for the outing?
Registrations are made at the Saronno Point headquarters in via San Giuseppe 41 in Saronno, on Thursdays and Saturdays from 15:00 to 18:00. A deposit of 35 euros must be paid at the time of registration.
What is the cost of participating in the outing?
The cost of participation is 70 euros per person, with a deposit of 35 euros at the time of registration.
