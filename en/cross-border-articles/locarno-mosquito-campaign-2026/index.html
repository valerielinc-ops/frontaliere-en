Locarno launches campaign against tiger mosquito (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 8 May 2026

Locarno Municipality is distributing Vectobac® G for free to combat the spread of the tiger mosquito. Find out how to participate.

Contesto

In brief - Locarno distributes Vectobac® G against the tiger mosquito - Two informative afternoons and a morning of consultation - Locarno Card required for collection ## Key facts - What: Awareness campaign and distribution of Vectobac® G - When: May 15, 22, 23, 2026 - Where: Locarno Ecocenter, via alla Morettina 6 - Who: Locarno Municipality and Public Green Service - Cost: Free Locarno is relaunching the campaign against the tiger mosquito in preparation for the summer season. The Municipality relies on the active collaboration of residents to protect neighborhoods and public health. The campaign includes the free distribution of the larvicide granule Vectobac® G, intended for use on private land in areas prone to water stagnation. This product is crucial to combat the spread of the tiger mosquito, an invasive insect that can only be effectively contained with the active participation of the population. The informative appointments will take place on Friday, May 15, and Friday, May 22, from 1:00 PM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday, May 23, from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM, at the Locarno Ecocenter. During these meetings, the Public Green Service team will be available to answer citizens' questions and provide practical instructions on the correct use of the product. To collect Vectobac® G, it is necessary to present the Locarno Card, the service card reserved for citizens residing in the city. Distribution is indeed limited to residents to promote thorough treatment of private areas, which are fundamental to limiting the reproduction of the tiger mosquito. The Municipality publicly thanks the neighborhood associations for the voluntary work done and reiterates that the fight against the tiger mosquito is effective only through a shared commitment. Dettagli operativi

The campaign against the tiger mosquito in Locarno represents an example of how collaboration between institutions and citizens can lead to concrete results in the prevention and containment of invasive insects. The tiger mosquito, known for its aggressiveness and ability to transmit diseases, is a problem that requires an integrated and coordinated approach. Vectobac® G, a natural larvicide based on Bacillus thuringiensis israelensis, is a safe and effective product for controlling mosquito larvae. When used correctly, it can significantly reduce the population of tiger mosquitoes in private gardens and green areas of the city. However, the effectiveness of the product depends on correct application and regular treatment. ### Good prevention practices In addition to the distribution of Vectobac® G, the awareness campaign includes practical guidelines to prevent the proliferation of the tiger mosquito. Among the recommended good practices are: - Eliminating water stagnation in flower pots, saucers, buckets, and other containers. - Covering water containers needed for irrigation or domestic use with fine mesh nets. - Regularly cleaning gutters and drainage channels to prevent water accumulation. - Treating wet areas and critical points where water collects with Vectobac® G. The active participation of citizens is fundamental for the success of the campaign. Every small gesture helps to reduce the spread of the tiger mosquito and improve the quality of life in the neighborhoods of Locarno. ### Impact on public health The tiger mosquito is not just a nuisance but can also transmit diseases such as dengue, chikungunya, and the Zika virus. Punti chiave

To participate in the campaign against the tiger mosquito in Locarno, residents must follow these simple steps: 1. Visit the Ecocentro: Appointments will be held on Friday, May 15, and Friday, May 22, from 1:00 PM to 5:00 PM, and on Saturday, May 23, from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM, at the Ecocentro di Locarno, via alla Morettina 6. 2. Show the Locarno Card: To collect the Vectobac® G, you must present the Locarno Card, the service card reserved for citizens residing in the city. 3. Receive practical instructions: During the meetings, the team of the Public Green Service will be available to answer citizens' questions and provide practical instructions on the correct use of the product. 4. Apply the Vectobac® G: Use the larvicide granules in areas prone to water stagnation to reduce the proliferation of tiger mosquito larvae. 5. Follow good prevention practices: Eliminate water stagnation, cover water containers with fine mesh nets, and regularly clean gutters and drainage channels. The active participation of citizens is fundamental for the success of the campaign. Every small gesture helps reduce the spread of the tiger mosquito and improve the quality of life in Locarno's neighborhoods. Frequently Asked Questions
What is Vectobac® G and how does it work?
Vectobac® G is a natural larvicide based on Bacillus thuringiensis israelensis, effective in combating the proliferation of tiger mosquito larvae. It works by inhibiting the development of larvae, thus reducing the population of adult mosquitoes.
Who can participate in the campaign against the tiger mosquito?
The campaign is reserved for residents of Locarno who own the Locarno Card. The distribution of Vectobac® G is limited to citizens domiciled in the city to promote thorough treatment of private areas.
What are the good prevention practices against the tiger mosquito?
Among the recommended good practices are the elimination of water stagnation, the covering of water containers with fine-mesh nets, the regular cleaning of gutters, and the application of Vectobac® G in critical points.
