AIL Arena in Lugano: open days on 30 and 31 May to discover the new home of FC Lugano

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 19 April 2026

On 30 and 31 May 2025, FC Lugano will open the new AIL Arena with two days of free events: guided tours, a Fan Village, and a competition with a Renault Clio up for grabs.

Contesto

TL;DR - AIL Arena opens May 30-31, 2025 in Lugano. - Free guided tours and Fan Village activities. - Free public transport for registered tour participants. - Prize draw with Renault Clio and season tickets. ## Key facts - Event: Grand opening of AIL Arena. - Date: 30 and 31 May 2025. - Location: Lugano, Switzerland. - Organizer: FC Lugano. - Prize Draw: Renault Clio and two season tickets for 2026/27. - Transport: Free public transport for registered tour participants. - Fan Village: Open to all, no registration required. - Season Tickets: Available for purchase for 2026/27 season. FC Lugano has announced that the grand opening weekend of the new AIL Arena will take place on 30 and 31 May 2025. This state-of-the-art facility will redefine sports and entertainment in Lugano. The two-day event is dedicated to the local community, fans, enthusiasts, and anyone curious to explore the new home of the Bianconeri for the first time. As stated in an official announcement, the occasion also serves as a tribute to the citizens who, in 2021, voted in favour of the project, making the Polo Sportivo e Eventi (PSE) a reality. ## A closer look at the new AIL Arena During the open-house weekend, visitors will have the chance to take a free guided tour of the AIL Arena, discovering some of its most iconic areas. Highlights include the buvette (refreshment area), mixed zone, press room, gym, players' zone, changing rooms, and hospitality areas. Registration will be required to join the tour, with sign-ups opening weeks in advance. Frequently Asked Questions
How can I sign up for the free tour of the AIL Arena?
Registrations for the free tour will open in the weeks leading up to 30 May 2025. You can register through the official FC Lugano channels, including the club’s website and social media. Once registered, you will receive a confirmation entitling you to free access to the public transport services of the Arcobaleno fare community for both days of the event.
Is registration required for the Fan Village?
No, the Fan Village is freely accessible to everyone without the need for registration. The area will be set up along the future Via Stadio and will feature activities for all ages, including season tickets for 2026/27, merchandise, food & beverage, and games.
Which public transport services are included in the free offer for tour participants?
Participants in the free tour of the AIL Arena will be entitled to free use of the public transport services of the Arcobaleno fare community (to and from the AIL Arena) on both days of the event. This includes trains, buses, and dedicated shuttles connecting different areas of the city.
What can I do at the Fan Village besides participating in the prize draw?
At the Fan Village, you can purchase season tickets for the 2026/27 Swiss Super League, buy merchandise, enjoy food & beverage offerings, take part in games and entertainment for all ages, and enjoy live music. The area is designed to provide a complete experience for all visitors.
Who are the partners of the AIL Arena inauguration event?
The event is presented in collaboration with the Gruppo Corriere del Ticino, which acts as the Presenting Partner. Additionally, AUTORS SA is the Business Partner providing the Renault Clio up for grabs in the prize draw.
