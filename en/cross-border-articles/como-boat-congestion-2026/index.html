Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main causes of the overcrowding of taxi boats in Como?
The main causes of the overcrowding of taxi boats in Como are the increase in the number of boats and the lack of sufficient piers to manage the traffic. The situation has worsened with the start of the good season and the increase in tourism.
What are the proposed solutions to solve the overcrowding problem?
The proposed solutions include the creation of new piers, the implementation of a booking system for taxi boats, and the introduction of docking shifts to better distribute the workload during peak hours.
How can cross-border workers be involved in solving the problem?
Cross-border workers can be involved in solving the problem by providing feedback on their experiences and participating in public consultations. Additionally, they can use advanced calculation tools to better plan their movements and reduce waiting times.
