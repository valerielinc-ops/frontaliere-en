Contesto

In brief - Edict for the canonization of Don Roberto Malgesini published - Diocese of Como initiates beatification process - Involvement of the People of God requested ## Key facts - What: Edict for the canonization of Don Roberto Malgesini - When: 2 May 2026 - Where: Diocese of Como - Who: Bishop Oscar Cantoni - Amount: Not specified The Diocese of Como has published the edict for the canonization of Don Roberto Malgesini, a diocesan priest brutally killed on 15 September 2020 in Piazza San Rocco. The text, signed by Bishop Oscar Cantoni, retraces the steps that led to the authorization to introduce the diocesan phase of the beatification process. The decision, made at the beginning of March, implies a serious responsibility and requires the involvement of the People of God. Bishop Cantoni invites those who are aware of obstacles to the fame of Don Roberto Malgesini's holiness to address him or the Postulator of the cause at the Curia Vescovile. Furthermore, those who possess writings or documents of the priest are invited to make them available to the Postulator. The edict will remain affixed for four weeks in various places in the Diocese, including the Curia Vescovile of Como and the parishes where Don Roberto carried out his ministry. ### Next steps The edict is a fundamental step in the canonization process. The diocesan phase involves the collection of testimonies and documents attesting to the holiness of Don Roberto Malgesini. The Diocese of Como has already obtained the favorable opinion of the Lombard Episcopal Conference and the nihil obstat of the Dicastery for the Causes of Saints. What is the edict for the canonization of Don Roberto Malgesini?
