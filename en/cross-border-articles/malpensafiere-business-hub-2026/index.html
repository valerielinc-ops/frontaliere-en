Frequently Asked Questions
What are the requirements to participate in the Focus Day Nuove Imprese 2026?
Participation is open to everyone, upon online registration on the website of the Varese Chamber of Commerce. There are no specific requirements, but it is advisable to prepare any documents related to your business idea.
What are the main sectors covered during the event?
The main sectors covered during the Focus Day include agriculture, home food, non-hotel accommodation facilities, and digital innovation. There will also be testimonials from Varese startups operating in various sectors.
How can I best prepare for the Focus Day?
To prepare yourself best, it is advisable to register online in advance, prepare any documents related to your business idea, plan your day based on the detailed program, and prepare a list of specific questions for the experts.
