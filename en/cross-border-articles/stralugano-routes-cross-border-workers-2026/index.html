For beginners or those who want to enjoy the experience without pressure, the 5 kilometers is the most accessible option. This route is ideal for those who want to enjoy the scenery and atmosphere of the race without the pressure of an overly ambitious goal. The 10 kilometers, on the other hand, is a balanced distance that requires basic preparation but remains within reach of many. It's perfect for those who want to challenge themselves and aim for a concrete target. For more ambitious runners, the half marathon of 21 kilometers represents a true personal challenge. It requires commitment and consistency, but it rewards with a great sense of accomplishment at the finish line. The relay allows you to run in a team, divide the route, and experience the event in a more collective way. This is ideal for those who want to share the effort and enthusiasm with friends or family. The Monte Brè Run, with its challenging climb immersed in nature, is designed for those seeking a different experience. The offers dedicated to children and families make StraLugano an inclusive event, capable of turning running into a moment of sharing. Punti chiave

To participate in the StraLugano 2026, you need to register through the official event website. During registration, you can choose your preferred route and add a 5-franc donation to support young runners. The discounted rate for those Over 65 is available at the time of registration; simply select the corresponding option. For those who wish to participate in the relay, you can form a team with friends or family and divide the route among team members. The Monte Brè Run, with its challenging climb, is ideal for those looking for a different challenge and want to test themselves in a natural setting. The proposals dedicated to children and families make StraLugano an inclusive event, capable of engaging everyone. For more information and to register, visit the official event website and follow the registration instructions. Don't miss the opportunity to participate in this unique sports event and live an unforgettable experience. Frequently Asked Questions
What routes are available at the StraLugano 2026?
The routes available at the StraLugano 2026 are the 5-kilometer, the 10-kilometer, the half marathon of 21 kilometers, the relay, and the Monte Brè Run. There are also proposals dedicated to children and families.
Is there a reduced rate for those over 65?
Yes, StraLugano offers a reduced rate to all those who have turned 65. It's a perfect opportunity to experience the event and share a day focused on sports and community.
How can I support young runners during registration?
At the time of registration, you have the opportunity to add a donation of 5 francs. This small gesture can represent a big step forward for emerging talents who want to grow and compete at the highest levels.
