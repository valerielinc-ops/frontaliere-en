Night closures Melide A2 southbound exit

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

The Melide motorway exit southbound will be closed from Sunday, May 3 to Friday, May 8, 2026, from 9:30 PM to 5:00 AM for noise-reducing pavement renewal works.

Contesto

In brief - Melide A2 south exit closure 3-8 May 2026 - Sound-absorbing pavement works - Traffic diverted into tunnel ## Key facts - What: Motorway exit closure - When: 3-8 May 2026, 21.30-05.00 - Where: Melide A2 southbound exit - Who: Federal Roads Office (FEDRO) - Cost: Not specified The Federal Roads Office (FEDRO) has announced that the Melide motorway exit on the A2 southbound will be closed from Sunday 3 May to Friday 8 May 2026, from 21.30 to 05.00. The closure is necessary for the renewal of the sound-absorbing pavement of the exit ramp, which runs from the Melide-Grancia Tunnel portal to the roundabout in front of Melide station. This is routine maintenance to restore full acoustic functionality and ensure the safety of a ramp particularly stressed by traffic. During the nights of closure, traffic in the tunnel will be reduced to one lane per direction, with traffic diverted into the south-north tube of the tunnel. The roundabout on the cantonal road will not be directly affected and will remain passable in all directions. FEDRO has specified that, while all possible measures are being taken to limit inconvenience, some disruptions may still occur. The Federal Office apologizes to those affected and thanks them for their understanding. ### Traffic impact The nighttime closure of the Melide exit will have a significant impact on traffic, especially for cross-border commuters who use this exit to reach the Canton of Ticino. During the nights of closure, drivers will need to follow the road signs and use alternative exits, such as Mendrisio or Lugano Nord, to reach their destinations. Traffic diversion in the tunnel could cause delays, especially during peak hours. FEDRO has invited motorists to plan their journey in advance and pay attention to road signs....
