Bottini: Quality jobs through temporary work (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 5 May 2026

The new collective agreement for temporary workers, renewed in July 2025, ensures quality employment with training and welfare.

Contesto

In brief,- New National Collective Labour Agreement (CCNL) for temporary workers,- Three-year validity, 1 million workers affected,- Training and welfare for quality employment

Dettagli operativi

Practical Analysis The new Ccnl for temporary agency workers introduces significant innovations that can have a major impact on cross-border workers who commute to Lombardy from Ticino. Among the main innovations is the investment in training and welfare, aimed at creating quality employment and combating job insecurity. ### Training and Professional Development Employment agencies offer specific training programs for temporary agency workers. These programs can include professional updating courses, technical and managerial training, and pathways for developing cross-cutting skills. For cross-border workers, this is an opportunity to improve their skills and increase their career prospects. ### Welfare and Protections The new Ccnl also provides a welfare package that includes healthcare, pension benefits, and other perks. This can be particularly useful for cross-border workers, who often face challenges related to double taxation and managing health insurance in two different countries. Furthermore, the collective agreement guarantees greater protections against unfair dismissal and offers more stable working conditions. ### Comparison with Other Forms of Precarious Work Compared to other forms of precarious work, such as fixed-term contracts or intermittent work, temporary agency work offers greater protections and opportunities for professional growth. The new Ccnl further strengthens these protections, making temporary agency work a more attractive option for cross-border workers seeking stability and economic security. ### Impact Scenarios A cross-border worker employed in Lombardy through a temporary agency can benefit from a specific training program to improve their technical skills. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main benefits of the new Collective Bargaining Agreement (Ccnl) for temporary workers?
The new Ccnl for temporary workers offers significant benefits, including specific training programs, a welfare package that includes health and pension assistance, and greater protection against unfair dismissal. These benefits aim to create quality employment and combat job insecurity.
How can I access the training programs offered by employment agencies?
To access the training programs offered by employment agencies, you need to identify the agencies operating in your area, contact them to request information on available courses, register for the courses, and actively participate in the programs offered. This can improve your skills and increase your career opportunities.
What are the main differences between temporary work and other forms of precarious work?
Compared to other forms of precarious work, such as fixed-term contracts or intermittent work, temporary work offers greater protection and opportunities for professional growth. The new Ccnl further strengthens these protections, making temporary work a more attractive option for cross-border workers seeking stability and economic security.
