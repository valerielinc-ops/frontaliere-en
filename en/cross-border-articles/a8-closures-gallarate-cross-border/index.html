Contesto

In brief - New nighttime closures on the A8 between Solbiate Arno and Gallarate from 6 to 8 May 2026 - Cancellations of planned closures for 4-5 and 7-8 May - Recommended alternative routes for travelers heading to Varese and Milan ## Key facts - What: Update of the schedule for noise barrier reinforcement works - When: From 6 to 8 May 2026, from 21:00 to 5:00 - Where: Section between the D08 junction and Cavaria, towards Varese - Who: Autostrade Society - Cost: Not specified Starting from 6 May 2026, commuters traveling on the A8 Milan-Varese highway during nighttime hours will face new closures. Autostrade Society has announced an update to the schedule for the reinforcement and modernization of noise barriers, which includes the cancellation of some previously announced closures and the introduction of new measures. The canceled closures will no longer take place at the beginning and end of the week. Specifically, the closures of the Solbiate Arno Albizzate-D08 junction section towards Milan, scheduled for the nights of 4 and 5 May, have been canceled. The closures of the D08 junction-Cavaria section towards Varese, planned for the nights of 7 and 8 May, have also been canceled. ### New measures towards Varese The new schedule includes the closure of the section between the D08 Gallarate-Gattico junction and Cavaria, towards Varese, from 21:00 on Wednesday, 6 May, until 5:00 on Thursday, 7 May. At the same times, the entrance ramp to the A8 for those coming from the D08 towards Varese will also be closed. For those heading to Varese, it is recommended to exit at Gallarate, take the SS341 towards Cavaria/Solbiate Arno, and re-enter the A8 at Solbiate Arno Albizzate. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the recommended alternative routes for those traveling to Varese?
For those heading to Varese, it is recommended to exit at Gallarate, take the SS341 towards Cavaria/Solbiate Arno, and re-enter the A8 at Solbiate Arno Albizzate. Those coming from the D08 can exit at Besnate, follow the SP26 towards Jerago, and take the SS341 until re-entering the highway at Solbiate Arno.
What are the recommended alternative routes for those traveling to Milan?
Motorists heading to Milan will have to exit at Solbiate Arno, follow the SS341 towards Gallarate and the Sempione State Road, and then re-enter the A8 at the Busto Arsizio exit via the SS336 of Malpensa.
Which closures have been canceled?
The closures of the Solbiate Arno Albizzate-D08 connection towards Milan scheduled for the nights of May 4 and 5 have been canceled. The closures of the D08-Cavaria section towards Varese planned for the nights of May 7 and 8 have also been canceled.
