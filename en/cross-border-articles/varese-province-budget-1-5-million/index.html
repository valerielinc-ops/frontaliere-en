Varese Province Budget: 1.5M for Investments

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

The 2025 budget for Varese Province closes with a surplus of 1.5 million, earmarked for investments in road infrastructure and cultural heritage.

Contesto

In brief - 2025 budget approved with a surplus of 1.5 million - Investments in road infrastructure and cultural heritage - 500,000 euros for the former Politeama cinema ## Key facts - What: Province of Varese 2025 Budget - When: April 30, 2026 - Where: Province of Varese - Who: President Magrini and Provincial Council - Amount: 1,594,244.40 euros surplus allocated to investments The 2025 budget of the Province of Varese closed with a positive result, respecting all major economic and financial balances. President Magrini expressed satisfaction with the solid accounts and the Entity's ability to transform resources into concrete interventions for the territory. The report showed a free administration surplus of over 17,009,730.80 euros and an investment surplus of 1,594,244.40 euros. ### Strategic investments The Provincial Council approved an amendment to the 2026-2028 budget forecast for 2026, aimed at financing interventions in strategic sectors for the territory. This maneuver fits into a broader strategy of the Provincial Administration aimed at strengthening the safety of the road network, supporting the Municipalities and enhancing the heritage as a lever for territorial development. Among the most significant investments, over 840,000 euros are allocated to the road connection in the Municipality of Travedona Monate, between the provincial road SP 32 and the state road 629. Furthermore, more than 175,000 euros have been allocated for the purchase of equipment for Civil Protection. Another important investment is represented by the 500,000 euros of free surplus destined for the enhancement of cultural assets, in particular for the redevelopment works of the former Politeama cinema in the Municipality of Varese. ### New funding Among the most significant res...
