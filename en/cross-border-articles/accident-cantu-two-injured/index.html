Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main causes of road accidents?
The main causes of road accidents include distraction while driving, excessive speed, the use of alcohol or drugs, lack of vehicle maintenance, and adverse weather conditions. It is important to adopt responsible behaviors while driving to prevent accidents.
How can I improve my road safety?
To improve your road safety, it is important to follow the rules of the road, maintain an adequate speed, avoid distractions while driving, always wear your seatbelt, and keep your vehicle in good condition. Additionally, it is useful to attend safe driving courses and stay updated on regulations.
What should I do in case of a road accident with injuries?
In case of a road accident with injuries, it is crucial to stay calm and immediately alert the emergency services. Secure the scene of the accident with emergency triangles and provide first aid to the victims, if possible. Gather information and cooperate with the authorities to facilitate the reconstruction of the accident.
