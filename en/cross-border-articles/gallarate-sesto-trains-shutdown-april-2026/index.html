Frequently Asked Questions
How can I book replacement buses for cancelled Eurocity trains?
You need to book through Trenitalia’s website or app, selecting the Milano Centrale – Domodossola route. Booking is mandatory, and seats are limited. Detailed schedules are not yet published but will be progressively updated on the official website.
Will tickets for cancelled trains be automatically refunded?
No. Passengers with already purchased tickets must request a full refund or a change to a later train via Trenitalia’s customer service or the official app. Automatic refunds are not provided.
Can I bring my bike on the replacement buses?
No. Replacement buses do not allow the transport of bicycles. Consider alternatives such as bike rental in Domodossola or using regional trains to transport your bike.
What are the most affordable alternatives to reach Ticino from Milano during the stoppage?
The most budget-friendly options include booked replacement buses (ticket plus booking fee), regional trains from Domodossola to Brig and then to Lugano/Bellinzona, or carpooling via services like BlaBlaCar. Shared taxis are more expensive but offer greater flexibility.
Can guide dogs board replacement buses?
Yes. Guide dogs are the only exception allowed for transporting large animals on replacement buses. Other animals are not permitted.
