Mother's Day at 161m: Palazzo Lombardia opens Belvedere (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 7 May 2026

On Sunday, May 10th, the 39th floor of Palazzo Lombardia will open for Mother's Day. Free visits and family activities.

Contesto

In brief - Extraordinary opening of the Belvedere at Palazzo Lombardia for Mother's Day - Free visits with mandatory online booking - Free make-up sessions and panoramic photo booth ## Key facts - What: Extraordinary opening of the Belvedere at Palazzo Lombardia - When: Sunday, May 10, 2026, from 10:00 to 18:00 - Where: Palazzo Lombardia, Milan - Who: Lombardy Region - Cost: Free entry On Sunday, May 10, 2026, for Mother's Day, the Lombardy Region opens the doors of Palazzo Lombardia, offering the chance to go up to the Berlusconi Belvedere to admire the Milan skyline from one of the highest and most suggestive points in the metropolis. This initiative is part of the cycle of extraordinary openings of the regional headquarters, aimed at making the institution's places more accessible and closer to citizens on the most felt occasions. Free visits and souvenir photos Despite uncertain weather forecasts, the day's program promises to offer a special experience. The 39th floor will be accessible from 10:00 to 18:00. For the occasion, an exclusive photo booth has been set up where families can take a souvenir photo with the backdrop of Milan's skyscrapers. As usual for these institutional openings, entry is free, but online booking is mandatory. A moment of relaxation: personalized make-up In addition to the panoramic view, the Region has thought of a treat dedicated to the mothers. In the foyer on the ground floor (accessible from the NP Space), ten stations will be set up where professionals will offer short sessions of personalized make-up. A way to combine the cultural visit with a moment of well-being and self-care. What to Do and What to Expect For cross-border workers who wish to participate in this initiative, it is important to plan the day in advance. Online booking is mandatory and must be done through the Experience portal of Regione Lombardia. It is advisable to book a few days in advance to secure a spot, especially considering that the event is free and may attract many families. Once arrived at Palazzo Lombardia, families can access the 39th floor and enjoy a breathtaking view of the city of Milan. The panoramic photo booth offers the opportunity to take memorable photos with a unique backdrop, perfect for immortalizing the special day. ### Free Make-Up Sessions Mothers can enjoy a moment of relaxation and self-care thanks to the personalized make-up sessions offered in the ground floor foyer. This service is an added value that makes the event even more special, offering a complete experience that combines culture, entertainment, and well-being. ### Practical Tips For those who decide to participate, it is advisable to arrive a bit early to avoid queues and ensure enough time to fully enjoy the experience. It is also important to check the weather conditions and dress accordingly, especially if you plan to spend time outdoors. ### Accessibility and Services The event has been designed to be inclusive and accessible to everyone. Priority access for pregnant women and people with disabilities ensures that no one is left out. Frequently Asked Questions
How can I book my visit to the Belvedere of Palazzo Lombardia?
To book your visit, access the Experience portal of Regione Lombardia and select the desired date and time slot. Booking is mandatory and must be done online.
Are there priority access services for people with disabilities?
Yes, priority access is provided for pregnant women and people with disabilities. Make sure to select the appropriate option during booking.
Can I bring my dog to the event?
Entry is not allowed for animals, except for guide dogs. If you need assistance with your guide dog, make sure to inform the staff during booking.
