In brief - Nighttime closures on A9 from Monday 11 to Friday 15 May 2026 - Green maintenance work along the stretch - Fino Mornasco and Como Centro interchanges involved ## Key facts - What: Nighttime closures on motorway A9 - When: 11-15 May 2026, 22:00-05:00 - Where: Between Lainate, Como, and Chiasso - Who: Autostrade per l'Italia - Amount: Not specified A new week of disruptions and traffic restrictions is expected on the A9 Lainate-Como-Chiasso motorway. Autostrade per l'Italia has announced a busy schedule of nighttime closures to allow urgent maintenance of tree vegetation along the stretch. The measures will affect several sections and interchanges between the state border and the Como area. ### First intervention: Lake Como - Como Centro The first intervention will involve the closure of the motorway section between the Lake Como junction and Como Centro, in the direction of Lainate. The block will be operational from 22:00 on Monday, 11 May, until 05:00 on Tuesday, 12 May. During the same time slots on the nights of Monday 11 and Tuesday 12 May, the Fino Mornasco interchange will also remain closed, both for entry and exit, in both directions of travel (Chiasso and Lainate). ### Second intervention: Como Centro - Chiasso Midweek, the construction site will move to the northbound carriageway. From 22:00 on Wednesday 13 May to 05:00 on Thursday 14 May, the section between Como Centro and Chiasso in the direction of Switzerland will be closed. During this phase, the Como Centro interchange will be completely closed for entry for vehicles heading both towards Chiasso and Lainate. ### Third intervention: Fino Mornasco The green maintenance program will conclude with an additional restriction over the weekend. Which sections of the A9 motorway are affected by nighttime closures?
The nighttime closures will affect the sections between Lago di Como and Como Centro, between Como Centro and Chiasso, and the Fino Mornasco exit. The closures are scheduled for the nights of May 11-12, 13-14, and 15-16, 2026.
