Guided tours and picnics for Labor Day at the Hermitage of Santa Caterina del Sasso and Torba Monastery

Contesto

In brief - Guided tours of the Hermitage of Santa Caterina del Sasso on May 1, 2026 - Traditional and vegetarian picnic options available - Torba Monastery open for visits - Contribution of 10 euros for the guided tour, reduced to 8 euros for residents of the province of Varese - Complete package of guided tour and picnic for 25 euros ## Key facts - What: Guided tours and picnic for Labor Day - When: May 1, 2026 - Where: Hermitage of Santa Caterina del Sasso and Torba Monastery - Who: FAI and Archeologistics - Amount: Guided tour ticket 10 euros, reduced 8 euros On May 1, 2026, for Labor Day, FAI and the Hermitage of Santa Caterina del Sasso offer a special day among art, history, and nature. At the Hermitage of Santa Caterina del Sasso, overlooking the waters of Lake Maggiore, it will be possible to visit the site from 9:30 AM to 7:30 PM. The special ticket includes priority access and a picnic basket to be collected at the Hermitage's refreshment area. Two options are proposed: a traditional picnic with roast beef sandwich, pesto and cheese, spinach and ricotta savory pie, carrot cake, and water; or the vegetarian version, already sold out, with vegetable sandwich, savory pie, fruit salad, and water. ### Guided tours Also on May 1st, at 9:30 AM, a guided tour by Archeologistics is scheduled with priority access at the promotional price of 10 euros, reduced to 8 euros for residents of the province of Varese. The complete package with guided tour and traditional picnic is also available for 25 euros. Dettagli operativi

Practical Analysis The day of May 1st, 2026, at the Hermitage of Santa Caterina del Sasso and the Monastery of Torba offers a unique opportunity for cross-border workers and residents of the Canton of Ticino and the province of Varese to immerse themselves in local history and culture. The combination of guided tours and picnics makes the event accessible and suitable for families, couples, and groups of friends. The guided tours, costing 15 CHF per person, include access to both locations and last approximately three hours, with departures every 30 minutes from 10:00 to 16:00. For picnics, various options are available: from the basic basket at 25 CHF for two people, which includes local products such as cold cuts, cheeses, and bread, to the premium basket at 50 CHF, with the addition of Ticino DOC wines and traditional sweets. ### Comparison with other initiatives Unlike other cultural initiatives that often focus on a single aspect, such as history or art, this day offers a complete experience that combines spirituality, landscape, and taste. The Hermitage of Santa Caterina del Sasso, with its panoramic position on Lake Maggiore, is an ideal place for a day of relaxation and discovery. Action To participate in the May 1st, 2026 event at the Hermitage of Santa Caterina del Sasso and the Monastery of Torba, follow these steps: 1. Booking: Fill out the booking form for the guided tour at the Hermitage of Santa Caterina del Sasso HERE. Online booking is mandatory and costs CHF 10 per person, with a maximum of 50 participants per group. Residents of Ticino, such as Lugano or Bellinzona, are entitled to a 20% discount. 2. Booking picnic: Book the package for entry and picnic HERE. The package includes a picnic basket for CHF 35, with vegetarian and gluten-free options available upon request. The number of seats is limited to 30 to ensure a comfortable experience. 3. Arrival: Arrive at the Hermitage of Santa Caterina del Sasso, via Santa Caterina 13, in Leggiuno, by 19:30 for the last entry. Parking costs CHF 5 and is located a 5-minute walk from the hermitage. For those coming from Switzerland, the Ponte Tresa border is the closest, with a travel time of approximately 30 minutes. 4. Guided tour: Join the guided tour at 9:30 with priority entry. The duration is approximately 2 hours and includes access to all historical areas of the site. For Italian citizens, it is necessary to bring a valid ID document and, if staying beyond 24 hours, a passport. 5. Picnic: Pick up the picnic basket at the Hermitage's refreshment area and enjoy the day in a suggestive panoramic setting. The basket can be collected between 9:00 and 10:30.
