Report the accident immediately to your employer and the relevant authorities, both in Switzerland and Italy, depending on the workplace location and residence. 2. What are the first steps to take in case of a work-related accident at the Italian-Swiss border?
It is essential to immediately report the accident to your employer and activate medical assistance by calling 112. Subsequently, you must document the incident and contact INPS or the LAMal insurance for reimbursement procedures.
How does the insurance coverage work for cross-border workers in case of a work-related accident?
Cross-border workers are covered by the employer's accident insurance if they work in the canton of Ticino. In Italy, INPS manages certain benefits. The LAMal guarantees health coverage for medical expenses.
