Helga Martinelli, founder of the Austria-Club Ticino, and Giuseppe Perale, Honorary Consul of Austria in Lugano, were awarded for their efforts in promoting dialogue and collaboration between Austria and Switzerland. The ceremony was attended by Lugano Mayor Michele Foletti, Austrian Ambassador to Switzerland Markus Josef Wuketich, and numerous representatives from the institutional and civil worlds. Mrs. Helga Martinelli was decorated with the Medal of Honor for her commitment to cultural and institutional relations between Austria and Ticino. The Honorary Consul of Austria in Lugano, Giuseppe Perale, was awarded the Silver Medal of Honor and the Knight's Cross of Merit II Class for scientific and diplomatic merits. ### Implications for cross-border workers The recognition of these contributions is a positive signal for cross-border workers who work in Ticino and reside in Italy.
