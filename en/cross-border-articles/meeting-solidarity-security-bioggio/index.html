Meeting between solidarity and security in Bioggio

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

Volunteers from City Angels Switzerland share direct experiences on May 16th at the Faressere Center in Bioggio

Contesto

In brief - Meeting with City Angels Svizzera on May 16 in Bioggio - Volunteers share experiences of solidarity and safety - Event at Centro Faressere, via Campagna 32 ## Key facts - What: Meeting with volunteers of City Angels Svizzera - When: May 16, 2026, from 17.30 to 19.00 - Where: Centro Faressere, via Campagna 32, Bioggio (Hangar 51, Box 34) - Who: Volunteers of City Angels Svizzera and president Antonio Chiarella - Cost: Not specified On Saturday, May 16, from 17.30 to 19.00, at Centro Faressere, via Campagna 32 in Bioggio (Hangar 51, Box 34), a meeting with City Angels Svizzera will take place. During the evening, volunteers will share stories and direct experiences, offering the public a concrete insight into their daily commitment to solidarity and urban safety promotion. A moment of listening and reflection on what it means, concretely, to 'be there' for others. President Antonio Chiarella will intervene. The meeting represents an opportunity to get to know those who operate daily in the territory in support of people in difficulty. This event is particularly relevant for the community of Bioggio and surrounding areas, where solidarity and safety are fundamental values. The volunteers of City Angels Svizzera, with their constant presence, contribute to creating a safer and more welcoming environment for all residents. ### The importance of solidarity and safety Solidarity and urban safety are highly topical issues, especially in a context like that of Bioggio, where the community is strongly linked to values of mutual aid and reciprocal protection. What to do to participate To participate in the meeting between solidarity and security in Bioggio, simply show up at the Centro Faressere, via Campagna 32, Hangar 51, Box 34, on Saturday, May 16th, from 5:30 PM to 7:00 PM. No registration is required, the event is open to everyone. ### How to get to Centro Faressere Centro Faressere is located in Bioggio, via Campagna 32. To reach the center, you can use public transportation or a car. If you use a car, there is parking available near the center. If you use public transportation, the closest stop is Bioggio, a few minutes' walk from the center. ### What to bring It is not necessary to bring anything special to participate in the meeting. However, it is advisable to bring a notebook and a pen to take notes during the volunteers' testimonies. Additionally, it is important to arrive a few minutes early to find a seat and prepare for listening. ### What to expect During the meeting, the volunteers of City Angels Svizzera will share their direct experiences of solidarity and urban security. It will be an opportunity to hear concrete stories and reflect on how to promote a more solidarity and secure community. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can participate in the meeting between solidarity and security in Bioggio?
The meeting is open to all residents of Bioggio and the surrounding area. No registration is required, just show up at the Centro Faressere at via Campagna 32, Hangar 51, Box 34, on Saturday, May 16th from 17:30 to 19:00.
What happens during the meeting?
During the meeting, volunteers from City Angels Switzerland will share their direct experiences of solidarity and urban security. It will be an opportunity to hear concrete stories and reflect on how to promote a more supportive and secure community. President Antonio Chiarella will introduce the topics of the meeting and moderate the discussion.
How can I stay in touch with the volunteers of City Angels Switzerland?
After the meeting, it will be possible to stay in touch with the volunteers of City Angels Switzerland and participate in other solidarity and urban security initiatives. Additionally, you can subscribe to the City Angels Switzerland newsletter to receive updates on events and the association's activities.
