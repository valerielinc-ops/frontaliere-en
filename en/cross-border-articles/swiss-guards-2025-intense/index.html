Frequently Asked Questions
What were the main events of 2025 for the Pontifical Swiss Guard?
The main events of 2025 for the Pontifical Swiss Guard were the death of Pope Francis, the conclave, and the jubilee. These events required extraordinary commitment from the corps, with the need to recall all guards from their leaves and ensure 24-hour service during the funeral vigil.
How many new recruits joined the Pontifical Swiss Guard in 2025?
Throughout 2025, 32 new recruits joined the Pontifical Swiss Guard, while 29 left the service. This maintained a relatively stable balance in the number of personnel.
What are the next steps for the Pontifical Swiss Guard?
The next steps for the Pontifical Swiss Guard include the swearing-in of new recruits on May 6, the meeting of Swiss Confederation President Guy Parmelin with Pope Leo XIV, submitting the plans for the new barracks to UNESCO for approval, and managing additional costs arising from increased prices in Rome's construction sector.
