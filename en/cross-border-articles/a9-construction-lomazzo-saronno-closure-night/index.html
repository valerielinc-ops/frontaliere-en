In brief - A9 closed Lomazzo Nord-Saronno 6-7 May - Green maintenance, nighttime closure - Alternative route on regular roads ## Key facts - What: Closure of A9 motorway section - When: 6 May 22:00-7 May 5:00 - Where: Lomazzo Nord-Saronno, direction Lainate - Who: Autostrade per l’Italia - Cost: Not specified The nighttime closure of the Lomazzo Nord-Saronno section on the A9 motorway, announced by Autostrade per l’Italia, will have a significant impact on cross-border workers who use this artery for travel between Canton Ticino and Lombardy. The closure, scheduled for the night between Wednesday 6 and Thursday 7 May, is necessary for maintenance of the tree lines along the motorway. This type of work is crucial to ensure the safety and functionality of the road infrastructure, but it inevitably causes inconvenience for users. For cross-border workers who daily cross the Italian-Swiss border, the nighttime closure might mean having to plan their route in advance. The affected section is between Lomazzo Nord and Saronno, exclusively in the direction of Lainate. The closure will be in effect from 22:00 on Wednesday 6 May until 5:00 on Thursday 7 May. This time slot has been chosen to minimize disruptions during peak hours, but it might still affect those working night shifts or needing to travel in the evening. ### Alternative route To limit disruptions to traffic, an alternative route on regular roads has been arranged. After the mandatory exit at the Lomazzo Nord interchange, drivers will need to follow a specific route: take the SP23 and SP30 towards Lomazzo, merge onto the SP33 and continue on via Como towards Turate.
