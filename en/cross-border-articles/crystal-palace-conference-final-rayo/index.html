Crystal Palace close to Conference final, dreams of Rayo

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

Crystal Palace beats Shakhtar Donetsk 3-1 and dreams of the Conference League final. Rayo Vallecano defeats Strasbourg 1-0.

Contesto

In brief - Crystal Palace wins 3-1 against Shakhtar Donetsk. - Rayo Vallecano beats Strasbourg 1-0. - Conference League final in sight. ## Key facts - What: Conference League semi-final. - When: April 30, 2026. - Where: Cracow and Vallecas. - Who: Crystal Palace, Shakhtar Donetsk, Rayo Vallecano, Strasbourg. - Score: Crystal Palace 3-1 Shakhtar Donetsk, Rayo Vallecano 1-0 Strasbourg. Crystal Palace has put a serious stake on the Conference League final, while Rayo Vallecano has taken the first act of the double header against Strasbourg. The English team won the first leg of the semi-final against Shakhtar Donetsk, imposing a 3-1 victory on the neutral ground of Cracow. Palace unlocked the score after barely a minute of play, with Mateta's assist to Sarr who crossed with his right foot beating Riznyk. Glasner's team immediately left their mark on a balanced first half. At the start of the second half, however, Shakhtar equalized: corner by Pedrinho and ball headed by Elias for Ocheretko's tap-in. After the 1-1 draw, the Londoners returned to the lead and, after a post and two saves by Riznyk, they regained the advantage at the 57th minute with Kamada's left-footed shot from the center of the area. It was then the former Lazio player himself who provided the assist for the final 3-1 goal scored in the 84th minute on the counter-attack by substitute Strand Larsen.
