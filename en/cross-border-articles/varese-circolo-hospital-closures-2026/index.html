Varese Hospital: Closures for May 1st and San Vittore

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

Changes to healthcare service hours for the May 1st bank holiday and San Vittore's patronal feast in Varese.

Contesto

In brief - Closure of the Ospedale di Circolo Collection Point on May 2 - General closure on May 8 for San Vittore - Suspended services: CUP, collections, retrieval of reports, administrative offices ## Key facts - What: Changes to healthcare service hours - When: May 2 and 8, 2026 - Where: Ospedale di Circolo di Varese and ASST Sette Laghi territorial sites - Who: ASST Sette Laghi - Amount: Not specified On the occasion of the upcoming spring bank holiday and the feast of San Vittore, the patron saint of Varese, ASST Sette Laghi has announced some changes to the opening hours of collection points and administrative offices. The week of changes to healthcare service hours in the area will begin on Saturday, May 2, with the closure of the Collection Point at Varese's Ospedale di Circolo, located in the Santa Maria Pavilion. The service will resume regularly the following week. ### General closure for San Vittore The most significant closure will concern Friday, May 8, the day on which San Vittore, the patron saint of Varese, is celebrated. On this date, the closure will be general and will affect all company sites, from Luino to Tradate. The services suspended for the entire Friday include: - CUP and Outpatient Clinic Secretariats - Collection Points and report retrieval desks - Administrative Reception and URP - District Offices and territorial sites The company specifies that the shutdown on May 8 involves the entire territorial network of ASST and not just the facilities in the provincial capital. As always in these cases, emergencies and ward activities remain guaranteed. ### Implications for patients The closures scheduled for May 2 and 8 may cause inconvenience to patients who need blood tests or to collect reports. Analysis of Closures and Alternative Services The closures scheduled for the May 1st bank holiday and for the San Vittore holiday could have a significant impact on patients who need healthcare services. In particular, the closure of the Blood Drawing Center at the Circolo Hospital in Varese on May 2nd could cause inconvenience to those who need blood tests or to deliver samples. It is important to plan ahead and, if possible, book the tests on the days before or after. ### Alternative Services For those who need to pick up test results or access administrative services, it is possible to contact their doctor or the clinic's office to check the availability of alternative services. Additionally, you can use the online services of ASST Sette Laghi to book tests or access test results. ### Comparisons with Other Holidays The closures scheduled for May 2nd and 8th follow a similar pattern to other national and local holidays. Generally, during these days, non-essential healthcare services are suspended to ensure the rest of the staff and the management of emergencies. However, it is important to note that the closures on May 2nd only affect the Blood Drawing Center of the Circolo Hospital, while those on May 8th are generalized and affect all company locations. ### Practical Scenarios For patients who need blood tests on May 2nd, it is advisable to book the tests on the days before or after.
