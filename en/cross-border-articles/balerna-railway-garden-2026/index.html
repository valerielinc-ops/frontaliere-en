The Municipality of Balerna and the SBB inaugurate the Railway Garden, a unique area in Switzerland with urban gardens and community spaces

Contesto

In brief - Railway Garden inaugurated in Balerna - Unique of its kind in Switzerland - Open to all with gardens for rent ## Key facts - What: Inauguration of the Railway Garden - When: Saturday, May 9, 2026, at 10:00 AM - Where: Via Stazione, Balerna - Who: Municipality of Balerna and SBB - Amount: Area of over 3,000 square meters The Railway Garden of Balerna is finally a reality. A project that sees the collaboration between the Municipality of Balerna and the Swiss Federal Railways (SBB) has led to the creation of a sustainable area unique throughout Switzerland. The official inauguration will take place on Saturday, May 9, 2026, at 10:00 AM and will be an opportunity to discover and experience this new green space dedicated to the community. The garden, located in Via Stazione, has been described as 'unique of its kind' thanks to the skillful reuse of SBB materials for the furnishings. The area of over 3,000 square meters includes not only green spaces, but also a communal orchard, relaxation and aggregation areas with tables and benches. The urban gardens, on the other hand, are reserved for those who have taken the space on rent. The inauguration event will see the participation of various authorities, including Adv. Luca Pagani, mayor of Balerna, Roberta Cattaneo, director Region South SBB, Ermes Fiori, SBB project manager, and Enrico Sassi, landscape architect. This will be followed by an aperitif offered. ### A sustainable and inclusive project The Railway Garden represents an example of sustainability and inclusivity. The furnishings, made with recycled materials from SBB, demonstrate how it is possible to create functional and welcoming green spaces without waste.
