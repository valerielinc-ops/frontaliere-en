Contesto

In brief - Austrian honors for two Ticino personalities - Promotion of cultural and diplomatic relations - Ceremony at Palazzo Civico in Lugano ## Key facts - What: Awarding of Austrian honors - When: May 8, 2026 - Where: Palazzo Civico, Lugano - Who: Helga Martinelli and Giuseppe Perale - Award: Medal of Honor and Silver Medal Hosted at Palazzo Civico in Lugano, on Friday, May 8, 2026, the Austrian Embassy in Switzerland conferred important honors on two Ticino personalities. Helga Martinelli, founder of the Austria-Club Ticino, and Giuseppe Perale, Honorary Consul of Austria in Lugano, were awarded for their significant contribution to promoting relations between Austria and Ticino. The ceremony was attended by the Mayor of Lugano, Michele Foletti, the Austrian Ambassador to Switzerland, Markus Josef Wuketich, and numerous representatives from the institutional and civil world. ### Helga Martinelli's contribution Helga Martinelli was decorated with the Medal of Honor for her commitment to cultural and institutional relations between Austria and Ticino. Her foundation, Austria-Club Ticino, played a crucial role in strengthening the ties between the two territories, promoting cultural exchanges and institutional collaborations. Martinelli dedicated years to intercultural dialogue, organizing events and initiatives that brought the two communities closer together. ### Giuseppe Perale's role Giuseppe Perale, Honorary Consul of Austria in Lugano, was awarded the Silver Medal of Honor and the Knight's Cross of Merit of the 2nd Class for scientific and diplomatic merits. Perale played a fundamental role in facilitating diplomatic and scientific relations between Austria and Ticino, contributing to the promotion of research and innovation.
