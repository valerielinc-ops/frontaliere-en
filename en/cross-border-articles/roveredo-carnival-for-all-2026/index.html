Roveredo, initiative for an inclusive Carnival (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 6 May 2026

A group of citizens from Roveredo launches an initiative to make the Lingera Carnival more inclusive and transparent

Contesto

In brief - Popular initiative for an accessible Carnevale Lingera for everyone - Signature collection by 28 July 2026 - Goal: fair and transparent public spaces ## Key facts - What: Municipal popular initiative 'Carnevaa per tucc' - When: Launched on 23 April 2026 - Where: Roveredo, Grisons - Who: Group of citizens - Amount: 150 signatures required During the Roveredo Carnival, public spaces should be accessible to all interested parties, under fair and non-discriminatory conditions. This is the heart of the municipal popular initiative 'Carnevaa per tucc', launched last April 23 by a group of citizens. The aim is to avoid situations where the use of certain areas leads to the general exclusion of other people, ensuring that those who are interested – citizens, groups or operators – can access them under correct conditions. The Municipality would be tasked with defining clear rules, reducing grey areas and those decisions that today can be perceived as somewhat non-transparent. The initiative arises following dissatisfaction that has developed in recent years around the Lingera. More and more people, in different contexts and of different ages, have begun to express doubts, perplexities and even a certain disappointment about how the festival is experienced. At the heart of the complaints is particularly the issue of public spaces, which during the carnival events should become the heart of the village life. Dettagli operativi

Analysis of Practical Implications The popular initiative 'Carnevaa for everyone' could have a significant impact on the management of public events in Roveredo. Currently, the perception of inequity in accessing public spaces during the Lingera Carnival has created a sense of exclusion among some residents. The introduction of clear and transparent rules could mitigate this situation, ensuring that all participants have the same opportunities to enjoy the festivities. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation Before the initiative, the management of public spaces during the Lingera Carnival was based on criteria that were not always clear and transparent. This led to situations where certain groups or individuals had privileged access to specific areas, while others felt excluded. The initiative aims to change this dynamic by introducing objective and proportional criteria for the occupation of public spaces. ### Future Scenarios If the initiative is successful, the Municipality of Roveredo will need to define new rules for managing public spaces during the Carnival. This could include the creation of a management committee that oversees the allocation of spaces, ensuring that all participants have equal opportunities. Additionally, a transparent booking system for public spaces could be introduced, thus avoiding situations of exclusion. ### Impact on the Community A more inclusive and transparent Carnival could have a positive impact on the community of Roveredo. Fair management of public spaces could increase participation and the sense of belonging among residents, making the Carnival an event even more united and celebrated by all. Furthermore, this initiative could serve as a model for other communities that wish to improve the management of their public event...

Punti chiave

What to do to support the initiative For supporting the 'Carnevaa per tucc' initiative, citizens of Roveredo can sign the petition by July 28, 2026. This is a crucial step to bring the proposal to the attention of the Municipality and start a public debate on the management of public spaces during the Carnival. Additionally, citizens can actively participate in the discussion, sharing their experiences and suggestions on how to improve the management of public spaces. ### Signing procedure The signature collection can be done at various meeting points in the community of Roveredo. Citizens can also contact the initiators directly to get more information on how to sign the petition. It is important that everyone interested in a more inclusive and transparent Carnival participates in this process. ### Useful tools To deepen the practical implications of this initiative, cross-border workers can use the event participation calculator to plan their presence at the Lingera Carnival. They can also consult the community participation guide to better understand how to actively contribute to the life of the Roveredo community. ### Conclusion The 'Carnevaa per tucc' initiative represents an important step towards a more inclusive and transparent Lingera Carnival. With the support of the community, this initiative can lead to a fairer management of public spaces, ensuring that all participants have the same opportunities to enjoy the festivities. Cross-border workers residing in Ticino can play a crucial role in this process by actively participating in the signature collection and public discussion. Source: laregione.ch

Frequently Asked Questions
What is the main objective of the 'Carnevaa per tucc' initiative?
The main objective of the initiative is to make public spaces accessible to everyone during the Lingera Carnival, ensuring fair and non-discriminatory conditions. This aims to avoid situations of exclusion and to promote a sense of belonging and inclusivity among participants.
How can I support the initiative?
You can support the initiative by signing the petition by July 28, 2026. You can find signature collection points at various locations in the Roveredo community or contact the initiators directly for more information.
What are the practical implications of this initiative for cross-border workers?
For cross-border workers residing in Ticino, this initiative could mean greater inclusivity and transparency in the management of public spaces during the Carnival. This could translate into greater participation and a sense of belonging to the local community, making the Carnival an even more significant and shared event.
