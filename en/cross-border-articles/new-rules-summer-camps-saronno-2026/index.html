Saronno: new rules for summer centres

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

From 2026, Saronno introduces a single management for summer centres, discounts for siblings and contributions up to 400 euros for families with ISEE up to 15,000 euros.

Contesto

In brief - Single management for summer centers ages 3-14 - Discounts for siblings even in different age groups - Contributions up to 400 euros for ISEE up to 15,000 euros ## Key facts - What: New rules for municipal summer centers - When: Starting July 2026 - Where: Don Marzorati, Monte Santo, and Damiano Chiesa schools in Saronno - Who: Municipality of Saronno - Amount: Contributions up to 400 euros per child The Municipality of Saronno has introduced new rules for municipal summer centers starting in 2026. The main novelty is the introduction of a single management system for all age groups, from 3 to 14 years old. This model was developed in collaboration with the Monsignor Zerbi Municipal Institution, with the aim of ensuring educational continuity and simplifying management for families. The organization will be homogeneous, with the extension of pre and post-center services to all ages and greater flexibility in attendance. Among the new measures introduced, there is also a much-anticipated measure for many families: the 'siblings' discount will also apply in cases where children attend summer centers of different age groups, such as early childhood and primary. The Municipality has allocated dedicated funds to support access to services. In particular, contributions of up to 400 euros per child are provided for families with an ISEE of up to 15,000 euros. This is in addition to an allocation of 14,000 euros destined for inclusion, to cover the costs of specialized educational assistance. The contribution will also be valid for participation in summer centers throughout the national territory. Dettagli operativi

The new rules for the municipal summer centers in Saronno introduce a series of significant changes that will have a positive impact on families. The unified management for all age groups, from 3 to 14 years old, ensures educational continuity and simplifies management for families. This model, developed in collaboration with the Monsignor Zerbi municipal institution, represents a step forward towards a more homogeneous and inclusive educational offer. ### Implications for Families The new rules introduce significant discounts for families. The 'siblings' discount has been extended to cases where children attend summer centers for different age groups. This is a great advantage for families with more than one child, who can benefit from reductions even if the children are in different age groups. Additionally, the Municipality has provided contributions of up to 400 euros per child for families with an ISEE of up to 15,000 euros. This contribution is also valid for participation in summer centers throughout the national territory, making the offer even more inclusive. ### Inclusion and Specialized Assistance Another important aspect of the new rules is the attention to inclusion. The Municipality has allocated 14,000 euros for specialized educational assistance. This contribution is intended to cover the costs of assistance for children who need additional support during summer centers. This is an important step towards a more inclusive and accessible educational offer for everyone. ### Enrollment Procedures The enrollment procedures and the request for contributions will be available shortly on the Municipality's website.
