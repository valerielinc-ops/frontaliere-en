In May 2025 alone, 80 reports were registered, the highest monthly figure collected by the platform in the period analyzed. June follows, with 42 reports, and July, with 29. How to use the Amico Fido app To use the Amico Fido app, you need to download it from your preferred app store and register. Once registered, you can report any dangers for dogs in your area. To report a danger, simply open the app, select the 'Report a danger' option, and follow the instructions. You can add a photo and a brief description of the reported danger. ### Danger notifications When a user is about 200 meters away from a point where a danger has been reported, they receive a push notification on their phone informing them of the potential threat. This helps to avoid dangerous situations and ensures the safety of their pet. ### Additional features The Amico Fido app also offers other useful features, such as the ability to find the nearest veterinary clinic in case of emergency. You can also interact with the artificial intelligence to receive general information on dog care, prevention, and well-being. The app also offers a 'Dog Academy' option for dog training and an interactive 'What would you do if...' section with practical scenarios to help owners think better about daily or emergency situations. ### Toxicity control The Amico Fido app allows you to check toxicity to help owners understand if a food, plant, or substance can be dangerous for the dog. This is a useful feature to avoid dangerous situations and ensure the safety of your pet. ### Conclusion The Amico Fido app is a valuable tool for dog owners, allowing them to report and prevent dangerous situations.
