In brief - Human-to-human transmission of hantavirus on the Hondius ship - Three deaths and five suspected cases - Ship blocked in Cape Verde ## Key facts - What: Human-to-human transmission of hantavirus - When: May 5, 2026 - Where: Cruise ship Hondius - Who: World Health Organization (WHO) - Amount: Three confirmed deaths The situation on the cruise ship Hondius has worsened with the confirmation by the World Health Organization (WHO) that hantavirus can spread from person to person. This has increased alarm among passengers and crew, with the ship still blocked at the port of Praia, in Cape Verde. The WHO's director for epidemic and pandemic prevention, Maria Van Kerkhove, revealed that the outbreak may have been triggered by transmission among people in close contact. ### Origin of the outbreak The WHO also provided updates on the evolution of the outbreak, revealing that it did not originate on the ship, as initially hypothesized, but in South America. The two Dutch spouses who died would have been infected in South America and then spread the virus to other passengers. This has led the WHO to initiate procedures to trace passengers on the Sant'Elena-Johannesburg flight, on which the Dutch woman had boarded on April 25, to prevent further spread of the infection. ### Current situation In addition to the confirmed cases, including a British man hospitalized in South Africa and improving, there are five suspected infections, not yet confirmed. Among these, two crew members, one English and the other Dutch, who will be evacuated to the port of Praia before being transferred to the airport for air evacuation. The Dutch cruise company Oceanwide Expeditions has communicated that two sick passengers will be evacuated to the Netherlands on a date not yet specified. ##...

Frequently Asked Questions
What are the symptoms of hantavirus?
Symptoms of hantavirus may include fever, headache, nausea, vomiting, muscle pain, and difficulty breathing. In some cases, it can lead to hantavirus pulmonary syndrome, which can be fatal. It is important to seek medical attention immediately if you experience suspicious symptoms.
How can I protect myself from hantavirus?
To protect yourself from hantavirus, it is important to take preventive measures such as frequent hand washing, using masks, and maintaining a safe distance from others. Additionally, it is crucial to avoid contact with rodents and their excrement, as they can carry the virus.
What should I do if I suspect I have been exposed to hantavirus?
If you suspect you have been exposed to hantavirus, it is important to seek medical attention immediately and follow quarantine procedures if necessary. Health authorities can provide further guidance on how to manage the situation and prevent the spread of the virus.
