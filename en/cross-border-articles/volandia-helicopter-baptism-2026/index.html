Volandia: helicopter flight baptism returns in 2026

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

From May 1 to 3, 2026, Volandia offers helicopter flights for adults and children, with prices set at 65 euros for adults and 45 euros for children.

Contesto

In brief - Volandia offers helicopter flight baptism from May 1 to 3, 2026 - Panoramic flight over Malpensa and Ticino Park - Prices set at 65 euros for adults and 45 euros for children ## Key facts - What: Helicopter flight baptism - When: May 1, 2, and 3, 2026 - Where: Volandia, Somma Lombardo (VA) - Who: Volandia in collaboration with Eliossola - Cost: 65 euros for adults, 45 euros for children up to 11 years old From May 1 to 3, 2026, Volandia, the park and museum of flight in Somma Lombardo, once again offers the eagerly awaited initiative of helicopter flight baptism. This unique experience, realized in collaboration with Eliossola, is open to both adults and children. Visitors to the museum will be able to experience the thrill of takeoff and observe from above the Malpensa area and Ticino Park. After a brief initial briefing, participants will board for a panoramic flight that will depart directly from the museum area. During the flight, lasting approximately five minutes, it will be possible to admire the airport runways, aircraft in motion, the control tower, and Ticino Park. The experience is designed for everyone, from aviation enthusiasts to those who simply want to live a different emotion. The activity is reserved for museum visitors and it will be possible to purchase the experience directly on-site during the event days. Prices for 2026 are set at 65 euros for adults and 45 euros for children up to 11 years old. Minors must be accompanied by an adult on board. The initiative is subject to weather conditions and authorization from Malpensa's control tower. Implications for Visitors The helicopter flight baptism initiative at Volandia represents a unique opportunity for visitors to experience an exciting and educational adventure. The panoramic flight over Malpensa and the Ticino Park offers a breathtaking view that few have the chance to admire. This type of experience is particularly suitable for families, as it allows children to approach the world of aviation in a fun and engaging way. ### Comparison with Other Experiences Compared to other tourist attractions in the area, the helicopter flight baptism stands out for its unique experience. While other activities may offer panoramic views, few can boast the opportunity to fly over one of Italy's most important airports. Additionally, the fact that the activity is reserved for museum visitors adds an educational value, allowing participants to deepen their knowledge of the world of flight. ### Practical Scenarios For those interested in participating, it is important to consider some practical aspects. First of all, it is necessary to purchase the entrance ticket to the Volandia museum to participate in the flight baptism. Furthermore, it is advisable to book in advance, especially on days of high attendance, to secure a spot on the helicopter. How to Participate To participate in the helicopter flight baptism at Volandia, you need to follow a few simple steps. First, it is advisable to visit the official Volandia website to get all the necessary information and book your spot. During the event days, you can purchase the experience directly on-site at the museum. It is important to remember that minors must be accompanied by an adult on the helicopter. ### What to Bring Along To make the most of the experience, it is advisable to bring a camera to capture the most beautiful moments of the flight. Additionally, it is important to wear comfortable and suitable clothing for the helicopter flight. Finally, it is advisable to bring a valid ID document, especially for minors. ### Monitor the Weather Conditions Since the activity is subject to weather conditions, it is important to monitor the weather forecasts in the days leading up to the event. In case of bad weather or lack of authorization from Malpensa control tower, the activity may be suspended. In such a case, unused tickets will be refunded. ### Conclusion The helicopter flight baptism at Volandia represents a unique opportunity to experience an unforgettable adventure. Thanks to the collaboration with Eliossola, museum visitors will be able to experience the thrill of takeoff and admire breathtaking views over Malpensa and the Ticino Park.
