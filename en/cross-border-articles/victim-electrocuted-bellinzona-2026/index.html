This was communicated by the Canton Police, specifying that the formal identification has been completed, but the investigations to establish the exact dynamics of the events have not yet been concluded and are ongoing. At the moment, the involvement of third parties is excluded, and it is also excluded that the victim was a worker or an employee of the FFS carrying out some operation. It was around 9:45 PM when the man came into contact with or approached the electric power line for the trains. The testimonies collected so far are contradictory: some say he was on the roof of a train, while others claim he had descended from the walkway leading to Daro.
