In brief - Bellinzona Public Baths reopens on May 9 - Online payment available - New 'Cielo Aperto' dining concept ## Key facts - What: Reopening of Bellinzona Public Baths - When: Saturday, May 9, 2026 - Where: Bellinzona, Canton Ticino - Who: City of Bellinzona - Amount: CHF 3.1 million for renovation The Bellinzona Public Baths are ready to reopen on Saturday, May 9, marking the start of the 2026 summer season. Among the most anticipated novelties, the possibility to pay for entry online via the sport.bellinzona.ch website, thus reducing queues at the ticket offices during peak times. This service is accessible directly via smartphone or other devices, offering a more fluid and convenient experience for visitors. In addition to online payment, the Public Baths introduce a new dining and entertainment concept, called 'Cielo Aperto'. This project includes the Cielo Aperto Lounge Terrace, where you can sip drinks accompanied by Thai specialties and good music, the Snack Bar Self Service restaurant enhanced by a pizzeria, and the Ciccio Burger outlet, specializing in artisanal burgers. The goal is to transform the Public Baths into a reference destination for residents and visitors, offering a complete experience that goes beyond simple swimming. ### Opening Hours and Tariffs The access hours for the new summer season have been divided into three periods: - Until June 12: every day from 9 am to 7:30 pm - From June 13 to August 16: every day from 9 am to 8 pm - From August 17 to September 13: every day from 9 am to 7:30 pm The tariffs and the agenda of sporting events, with the relevant dates on which the availability of some pools will be limited, are available on the sport.bellinzona.ch/bagno-pubblico page. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the opening hours of the Bellinzona Public Baths?
The opening hours vary depending on the period. Until June 12th, the Public Baths are open every day from 9 AM to 7:30 PM. From June 13th to August 16th, the hours are extended until 8 PM. From August 17th to September 13th, the hours return to 7:30 PM.
Is it possible to pay for entry online?
Yes, it is possible to pay for entry online through the [sport.bellinzona.ch](nav:azione) website. This service allows you to avoid queues at the ticket offices and access the facilities more quickly.
What are the new attractions at the Bellinzona Public Baths?
Among the new attractions are the online payment for entry, the new 'Cielo Aperto' concept for catering and entertainment, which includes the Cielo Aperto Lounge Terrace, the Snack Bar Self Service restaurant, and the Ciccio Burger sales point.
