Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main novelties of the 2026 pre-filled declaration?
The main novelties of the 2026 pre-filled declaration concern the new data entered by the Revenue Agency, the automatic limit on deductions for incomes over 75,000 euros, the extension of authorizations to the trusted person of the heir, and the differentiated calendar for 730 and Personal Income Tax.
How can I access the 2026 pre-filled declaration?
To access the 2026 pre-filled declaration, you need to log in to the restricted area of the Revenue Agency's website, select the option 'Pre-filled Declaration,' and choose between the 730 model or Personal Income Tax.
What are the important deadlines for the 2026 pre-filled declaration?
The important deadlines for the 2026 pre-filled declaration are April 30, 2026, for consulting the pre-filled 730, May 14, 2026, for modifying and submitting the 730, May 20, 2026, for consulting and modifying the Personal Income Tax, and May 27, 2026, for submitting the Personal Income Tax.
