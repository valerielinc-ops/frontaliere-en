Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

The airline celebrates six decades of connections with over 2.2 million passengers transported

Contesto

In brief - LOT Polish Airlines celebrates 60 years of flights between Milan Malpensa and Warsaw - Over 2.2 million passengers transported in the last 25 years - Two daily frequencies with Embraer aircraft ## Key facts - What: Celebration of 60 years of air connections - When: Since May 2, 1966 - Where: Between Milan Malpensa and Warsaw - Who: LOT Polish Airlines - Amount: Over 2.2 million passengers transported LOT Polish Airlines, a member of Star Alliance and partner of Miles & More, celebrates 60 years of connections between Milan Malpensa and Warsaw. The first flight between the two cities took off on May 2, 1966, and today the airline operates this route with two daily frequencies. In the last 25 years, LOT Polish Airlines has carried out over 44,000 flights on this route, covering nearly 50.6 million kilometers in over 80,000 hours of flight. ### A historic milestone "Flights to and from Milan are of great importance for LOT Polish Airlines and represent a key component of our growing presence in Italy, the domestic market of Star Alliance. Since the inaugural flight in 1966, we have connected businesses, people, and cultures on this route," states Amit Ray, Director for Italy, Dach markets, Malta, and India and Head of Global Corporate and Strategic Sales of LOT Polish Airlines. "Even today, these flights remain fundamental for both business travelers and leisure travelers, for those visiting friends and relatives, and for those traveling for cultural reasons." ### The strategic partnership "Celebrating 60 years of the connection between Milan and Warsaw together with LOT Polish Airlines represents a milestone of great importance, a historic route, today operated from Malpensa, which has significantly contributed to strengthening the economic, cultural, and...
