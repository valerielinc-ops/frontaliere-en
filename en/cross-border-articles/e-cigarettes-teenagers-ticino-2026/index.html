E-cigarettes: Ticino teens' favorite vice

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

E-cigarette use among 15- to 17-year-olds in Ticino is almost equal to that of traditional cigarettes.

Contesto

In brief - E-cigarettes as widespread as traditional ones among 15-17-year-olds - 22.5% of the Swiss population consumes nicotine monthly - 15.8% of consumers are daily users ## Key facts - What: Consumption of e-cigarettes and traditional cigarettes among adolescents - When: Survey conducted between April and June 2025 - Where: Switzerland, with a focus on Ticino - Who: Addiction Switzerland, Tobacco Prevention Fund (TPF), Federal Office of Public Health (FOPH) - Amount: 8.4% monthly consumption of e-cigarettes vs. 6.8% traditional cigarettes Among young people aged 15 to 17 in Ticino, e-cigarettes are almost as widespread as traditional ones. According to a new survey, the monthly consumption of e-cigarettes in the 15-17 age group is 8.4%, a figure very close to that of traditional cigarettes, which stands at 6.8%. This significant change emerged from a representative study conducted on behalf of the Tobacco Prevention Fund (TPF) and in collaboration with the Federal Office of Public Health (FOPH). The survey, carried out between April and June 2025 on a sample of 5,818 people over the age of 15, revealed that nicotine consumption in Switzerland has remained stable compared to the previous year. In 2025, 22.5% of the population consumed a tobacco or nicotine-based product at least once a month, a percentage stable since 2023. Furthermore, 15.8% of consumers reported daily use of these products. ### A growing phenomenon The number of cigarettes smoked per day increases with age, but what is most concerning is the increase in multiple use among young people. More than a quarter (25.2%) of monthly consumers use several products, a percentage up from 22% in 2023. Analysis of the Phenomenon The increase in the consumption of electronic cigarettes among adolescents in Ticino requires an in-depth analysis of the causes and consequences of this phenomenon. Electronic cigarettes, often perceived as less harmful than traditional ones, are gaining popularity among young people due to their accessibility and the variety of flavors available. ### Attraction Factors One of the main factors contributing to the popularity of electronic cigarettes among adolescents is the perception of lower risk. Many young people mistakenly believe that electronic cigarettes are a safer choice compared to traditional ones. However, recent studies have shown that electronic cigarettes can also have negative effects on health, especially if used improperly. Another important factor is the ease of access. Electronic cigarettes are often sold in specialized stores and online, making them easily accessible to young people. Furthermore, aggressive advertising and marketing can positively influence the perception of the products, making them more attractive to younger consumers. ### Health Consequences The use of electronic cigarettes can have significant consequences on the health of adolescents. In addition to the risks associated with nicotine, which can cause addiction and problems with brain development, electronic cigarettes can also contain harmful chemicals that can damage the lungs and the respiratory system. ### Prevention Strategies To counter this phenomenon, it is essential to implement effective prevention strategies. Punti chiave

What to do to prevent the use of electronic cigarettes For parents and educators, it is crucial to adopt concrete measures to prevent the use of electronic cigarettes among adolescents. Here are some steps that can be helpful: ### 1. Information and awareness Informing young people about the risks associated with the use of electronic cigarettes is the first step in preventing consumption. Schools can organize seminars and workshops to raise awareness among students about the dangers of nicotine and the chemicals present in electronic cigarettes. ### 2. Open dialogue with young people Maintaining an open dialogue with your children is essential to understand their habits and behaviors. Parents should be available to discuss the risks associated with the use of electronic cigarettes and encourage young people to make informed choices. ### 3. Monitoring and control Monitoring the use of electronic cigarettes among young people can help prevent abuse. Parents can control access to products and limit the purchase of electronic cigarettes by minors. ### 4. Promotion of healthy lifestyles Promoting physical activities and healthy lifestyles can help prevent the use of electronic cigarettes. Schools and communities can organize sports events and recreational activities to encourage young people to adopt positive habits. ### 5. Collaboration with health authorities Collaborating with health authorities and educational institutions can help implement effective prevention strategies. Information campaigns and educational initiatives must be supported by public policies aimed at protecting the health of young people. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the risks associated with the use of electronic cigarettes?
The use of electronic cigarettes can cause nicotine addiction and brain development issues, especially in adolescents. Additionally, they may contain harmful chemicals that can damage the lungs and respiratory system.
How can I prevent the use of electronic cigarettes among my children?
Educating young people about the risks, maintaining open dialogue, monitoring usage, promoting healthy lifestyles, and collaborating with health authorities are fundamental steps to prevent the use of electronic cigarettes.
What are the current regulations regarding the sale of electronic cigarettes?
Regulations vary between Switzerland and Italy. In Switzerland, the sale of electronic cigarettes is regulated, but it is important to check local laws for specific details.
