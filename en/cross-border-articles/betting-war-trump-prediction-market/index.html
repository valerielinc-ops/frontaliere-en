Users risk becoming desensitized to reality, as if a person chooses to bet on war dynamics, they are clearly not reflecting on what is behind it. Frequently Asked Questions
What are prediction markets?
Prediction markets are online platforms that allow betting on future events, such as political speeches or international conflicts. These markets position themselves as a middle ground between investment and gambling, but they raise ethical and moral questions.
Are they legal in Switzerland?
At the moment, platforms like Polymarket are not authorized in Switzerland. However, they can be accessed via VPN, although this involves significant risks, such as losing the deposited money and lack of legal protection.
What are the risks associated with prediction markets?
Risks include desensitization from reality, losing significant amounts of money without any protection, and the ethical and moral implications of betting on serious events like wars or political crises.
