What are the procedures for reporting a case of domestic violence in Ticino?

What are the legal consequences for perpetrators of domestic violence in Switzerland?
In Switzerland, attempts of murder and other acts of domestic violence are severely punished. Penalties can vary depending on the specific circumstances of the case, but they may include long prison sentences. Judicial authorities must carefully evaluate the evidence and testimonies to determine the appropriate penalty.
