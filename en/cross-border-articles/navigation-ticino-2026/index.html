With the arrival of spring, SNL has announced plans to expand services, improve passenger experience, and promote sustainable tourism. SNL aims to establish itself not just as a means of transport, but as a true tourist carrier that highlights the natural and cultural beauty of Ticino. During a presentation event, the director of SNL emphasized that > "the goal is to make navigation a unique experience, where passengers can enjoy the beauty of our lake and the surrounding mountains." With a planned investment of around 5 million Swiss francs, SNL intends to renew its fleet by purchasing two new eco-friendly boats, which will reduce CO2 emissions by 30% compared to older models. Dettagli operativi

SNL is not only enhancing passenger offerings but is also investing in infrastructure and technology to ensure greater operational efficiency. With the expiration of the federal concession in 2025, SNL has the opportunity to review and improve service terms, adapting to current customer needs. Among the expected innovations, there will be new routes connecting municipalities like Lugano, Locarno, and Bellinzona, with the introduction of at least 5 new lines linking tourist locations and points of interest, such as the Locarno National Park. To promote tourism, informative brochures and promotional packages are planned, such as a “Lake Pass” offering a 20% discount on ferries for tourists booking stays of at least 3 nights in nearby municipalities. Additionally, there will be an increase in ferry frequency during weekends and summer months when demand is higher. According to estimates, SNL anticipates a 30% increase in passenger numbers during the summer of 2026, with a schedule of 10 additional daily trips. SNL has also announced significant investments in sustainability, aiming to reduce the energy consumption of its vessels. This aligns with Swiss energy-saving regulations, such as the Federal Energy Act (LEne) of 2018, which mandates a 20% reduction in energy consumption by 2025. The new vessels, set to enter service in 2026, will be designed to use renewable energy, targeting a reduction of CO2 emissions by 50 tons per year. With growing attention to ecological issues, SNL aims to become an example of how public transport can contribute to a greener future. In fact, by 2026, SNL plans to operate 70% of its fleet with electric engines, further reducing environmental impact. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the new services that the Navigazione Lago di Lugano will present in 2026?
The Navigazione Lago di Lugano will present new services, including thematic itineraries, special events and a more effective navigation service, synchronized with the times of TILO trains.
What is the expected impact of the new SNL initiatives on sustainable tourism in Ticino?
SNL aims to become a model of sustainable tourism, with a reduction in CO2 emissions of 50 tons per year thanks to the new eco-friendly boats, and a 30% increase in passengers during the summer of 2026.
How does the 'Lake Pass' work for tourists staying in Ticino?
The 'Lake Pass' offers a 20% discount on SNL ferries for those who book at least 3 nights in neighboring municipalities. It is valid for the entire stay and includes access to thematic itineraries such as 'Lugano and its treasures'. Available from 2026.
Is there any news for cross-border commuters who use SNL boats to get to work?
Yes, from 2026 the ecological boats will be synchronised with the timetables of the TILO trains to Bellinzona and Chiasso. Rides will increase to 15 minutes during peak hours, reducing waiting times.
What are the new routes planned for SNL boats in 2026?
SNL will introduce at least 5 new lines that will connect Lugano, Locarno, Bellinzona and the Locarno National Park. The routes will also include points of interest such as Monte Brè and Parco Ciani.
