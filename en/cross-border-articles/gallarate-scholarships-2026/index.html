87 students awarded scholarships in Gallarate

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

The ceremony took place at Maga-Hic in Gallarate, where 87 students were awarded for their academic and extracurricular achievements.

Contesto

In brief - 87 students awarded scholarships in Gallarate - Ceremony at Maga-Hic, a symbolic place of culture - Recognition for academic and extracurricular merit ## Key facts - What: Scholarship awards - When: 29 April 2026 - Where: Maga-Hic, Gallarate - Who: Gallarate municipal administration - Amount: 87 scholarships An afternoon dedicated to merit and commitment, that of Wednesday, 29 April at Maga-Hic in Gallarate, where the municipal administration awarded scholarships to 87 students who distinguished themselves for academic and extracurricular results. The ceremony took place in the spaces of the museum in via De Magri, chosen not only as a symbolic place of the city's culture, but also as an environment increasingly frequented by young people for study. A recognition that looks at the present of the young people, but also at their future. 'Often we take it out on young people who don't have their heads on their shoulders – commented the mayor Andrea Cassani – today we have given the right value to 87 young people who have distinguished themselves at an academic and extracurricular level.' On the same line the councilor for Culture Claudia Mazzetti, who highlighted the meaning of the choice of the place: 'We want to reward the Gallarate excellences, we must recognize the commitment of these young people. We wanted to do it here at the Hic, where culture is at home, also because for many of them it has become a place of study.' During the ceremony, the president of the Culture Commission, Rossella Glorioso, also intervened, who addressed the awarded students directly: 'Three things that really count.
