A8 Milan-Varese, night closures between Solbiate and Cavaria for work

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

From May 4 to 8, night closures between Solbiate Arno and Cavaria for anti-noise barrier work

Contesto

In brief - Night closures on A8 Milano-Varese from May 4 to 8 - Noise barrier works between Solbiate Arno and Cavaria - Diversions on state and provincial roads ## Key facts - What: Noise barrier reinforcement and modernization works - When: Nights from May 4 to 8, 21:00-05:00 - Where: A8 stretch between Solbiate Arno and Cavaria - Who: Autostrade per l'Italia - Cost: Not specified A series of nighttime closures are scheduled on the A8 Milano-Varese to allow for reinforcement and modernization of noise barriers. The works will affect several stretches between Solbiate Arno, Albizzate, Gallarate, and Cavaria, with changes to traffic from Monday, May 4, to Friday, May 8. On the nights of Monday, May 4, and Tuesday, May 5, from 21:00 to 05:00, the stretch between Solbiate Arno Albizzate and the connection with the D08 Gallarate-Gattico branch, towards Milan, will be closed. Motorists heading to the Lombard capital will have to exit obligatorily at Solbiate Arno Albizzate and continue on the ordinary roads along the SS341, the SS33 del Sempione, and the SS336 della Malpensa, then re-enter the A8 at Busto Arsizio. The entrance of Gallarate towards Milan remains continuously closed. On the nights of Wednesday, May 6, Thursday, May 7, and Friday, May 8, always in the 21:00-05:00 time slot, the stretch between the connection with the D08 and Cavaria, towards Varese, will instead be closed. Concurrently, it will not be possible to enter the A8 towards Varese from the Gallarate-Gattico branch. In this case, it is advisable to exit early at Gallarate and follow the SS341 to re-enter the highway at Solbiate Arno Albizzate. ### Implications for cross-border workers The highway works could cause delays and inconveniences, especially during the evening and night hours.
