The design recalls the lines of the Ducati Panigale (front LED lights) and the Lamborghini Huracán (side profile), with an aerospace aluminum alloy frame (Al7075). Frequently Asked Questions
What is the Genny Zero wheelchair?
The Genny Zero is a self-balancing electric wheelchair produced by the Ticino-based company Genny Factory SA. It has been awarded the Compasso d'Oro for its innovative design and advanced functionality.
Where is the Genny Zero produced?
The Genny Zero is produced in Sant'Antonino, Ticino, by the company Genny Factory SA, which is part of the Wullschleger Group.
Who designed the Genny Zero?
The design of the Genny Zero was created by the Italian designer Enrico Pagano, drawing inspiration from motorcycles and sports cars to convey safety and agility.
What is the impact of the Genny Zero on the wheelchair market?
The Genny Zero represents a step forward in mobility for people with disabilities, combining design, technology, and functionality. It could have a significant impact on the wheelchair market, offering a more advanced and technological solution compared to traditional models.
How can I apply for a job at Genny Factory SA?
To apply, visit the company's website and check the available job offers. Make sure you have an updated resume and a cover letter that highlights your skills and experience in the technological sector.
