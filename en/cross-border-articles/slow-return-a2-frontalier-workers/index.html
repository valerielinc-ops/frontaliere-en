Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main causes of slowdowns on the A2?
Slowdowns on the A2 heading south are caused by a combination of factors, including the presence of tourists coming from the north, a storm in Mendrisiotto, and the traffic lights for flow control being on. These elements contribute to creating a situation of heavy traffic, especially during peak hours.
How can I monitor traffic in real-time?
You can monitor traffic in real-time using navigation apps like Google Maps or Waze, or traffic information services such as the one provided by TCS. These tools will provide you with updated information on the traffic situation and help you plan your route more effectively.
What alternative routes can I consider to avoid slowdowns on the A2?
If the A2 is particularly congested, you can consider alternative routes such as the state road that passes through the side valleys. However, keep in mind that these routes might be longer and less efficient compared to the highway. Using navigation tools can help you find the route most suitable for your needs.
