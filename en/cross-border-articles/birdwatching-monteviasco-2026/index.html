Birdwatching in Monteviasco: a nature discovery hike

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

Sunday hike through trails and woods to discover local birdlife. Colombo (LIPU): 'An experience to observe, listen and learn to protect the environment'.

Contesto

In brief - Birdwatching excursion to Monteviasco organized by Gruppo Amici Monteviasco and LIPU Varese. - Participants accompanied by expert guides to observe local bird species. - Initiative designed for enthusiasts, families, and curious individuals, with the reopening of the cable car. ## Key facts - What: Birdwatching excursion to Monteviasco. - When: Sunday. - Where: Monteviasco, Ticino. - Who: Gruppo Amici Monteviasco and LIPU Varese. - Cost: Not yet specified. The birdwatching initiative in Monteviasco, organized by Gruppo Amici Monteviasco and LIPU Varese, represents a unique opportunity to rediscover nature and local birdlife. On Sunday, participants will be able to immerse themselves in an immersive experience through trails and forests, accompanied by expert guides. The excursion will start after a welcome moment and a welcome coffee, then venture into the trails surrounding the village. Along the way, it will be possible to listen to the songs of the birds, observe the raptors in flight with binoculars, and discover the traces left by woodpeckers on the tree trunks. The initiative is made even more accessible by the reopening of the cable car that leads to the village, allowing more people to participate. The excursion is not just an opportunity for leisure but also a way to bring people closer to the protection of biodiversity. Learning to know and respect the natural heritage is fundamental to protecting the environment. We discuss this with Giordano Colombo of LIPU Varese, who explains the importance of these initiatives for the conservation of local fauna. ### Species to observe Among the species that will be possible to admire in Monteviasco are the common swift, the mountain swallow, the chimney swift, and the chough. Dettagli operativi

The birdwatching excursion to Monteviasco is not just an opportunity to observe birds, but also a way to learn how to protect the environment. During the excursion, participants will be accompanied by expert guides who will provide information and curiosities about the local avifauna. This type of initiative is fundamental to bring people closer to the protection of biodiversity and to promote the conservation of local wildlife. ### The importance of conservation The conservation of biodiversity is an increasingly important issue, especially in an era when the environment is increasingly threatened. Initiatives like the one in Monteviasco represent an important step in raising awareness and promoting the protection of ecosystems. Learning to know and respect the natural heritage is fundamental to ensuring a sustainable future. ### Species at risk Among the species that can be observed during the excursion, some are at risk or protected. For example, the golden eagle is a protected species in many regions and its presence is an indicator of the health of the ecosystem. Woodpeckers, such as the great spotted woodpecker and the black woodpecker, are also important for biodiversity and their conservation is fundamental. ### The impact of tourism Tourism can have a significant impact on the environment, but it can also be an opportunity to promote conservation. Initiatives like the one in Monteviasco represent an example of sustainable tourism, which combines nature observation with environmental awareness. This type of tourism can contribute to funding conservation and promoting the protection of ecosystems. ### Future initiatives The initiative in Monteviasco is just one of the many proposals by the Amici di Monteviasco association. Punti chiave

To participate in the birdwatching excursion to Monteviasco, you can register through the website of the Gruppo Amici Monteviasco. The excursion is open to everyone, enthusiasts, families, and curious individuals, and represents a unique opportunity to discover the local nature and avifauna. During the excursion, participants will be accompanied by expert guides who will provide information and interesting facts about the local avifauna. ### What to Bring For the excursion, it is advisable to bring binoculars, comfortable and suitable clothing for trekking, and a camera to capture the most beautiful moments. It is also important to bring water and snacks to sustain yourself during the journey. ### Where to Stay For those who wish to spend more time in Monteviasco, there are various accommodation options available, including hotels, bed & breakfasts, and apartments. It is advisable to book in advance, especially during peak seasons. ### How to Get There Monteviasco can be reached by car or public transport. The cable car, recently reopened, represents an easy way to reach the village and participate in the excursion. It is advisable to check the cable car's schedules and costs before departing. ### Conclusions The birdwatching excursion to Monteviasco represents a unique opportunity to discover the local nature and avifauna. Participating in this initiative means not only living an unforgettable experience but also contributing to the protection of the environment and the conservation of biodiversity. For more information and to register, visit the website of the Gruppo Amici Monteviasco and discover all the initiatives in progress. Frequently Asked Questions
Which bird species will be possible to observe during the excursion?
During the excursion, it will be possible to observe species such as the common swift, the alpine swift, the chimney swallow, the common chaffinch, various species of tits, blackcap, treecreeper, great spotted woodpecker, greater spotted woodpecker, green woodpecker, black woodpecker, common buzzard, European hobby, Eurasian sparrowhawk, and, with a bit of luck, even the golden eagle.
How can I sign up for the excursion?
To sign up for the excursion, visit the website of the Gruppo Amici Monteviasco and follow the instructions for registration. The excursion is open to everyone, enthusiasts, families, and curious individuals.
What should I bring with me for the excursion?
It is advisable to bring with you a pair of binoculars, comfortable and suitable clothing for trekking, a camera, water, and snacks to sustain you during the route.
