Fitness in Ticino: 1 in 5 adults signed up by 2026 (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 5 May 2026

In 2025, 1.45 million Swiss people will be members of fitness centers. Ticino ranks 6th for gym density. The 20-29 age group is the most active.

In brief - 1.45 million members in 2025, +5.8% vs 2024 - 1,441 fitness centers in Switzerland, +3.3% - Ticino 6th for gym density (17.0/100k inhabitants) ## Key facts - What: Record number of fitness center members in Switzerland - When: 2025 - Where: Switzerland, Ticino 6th for gym density - Who: Swiss Active - Amount: 1.45 million members, +5.8% vs 2024 The fitness sector in Switzerland set a new record in 2025, with a significant increase in the number of people signing up, particularly among young people, leading to a growth in the number of centers and overall revenue. According to Swiss Active, there were 1.45 million fitness memberships in 2025, a 5.8% increase compared to the previous year. This represents a new record for the sector. Ticino ranks sixth for gym density with 17.0 centers per 100,000 inhabitants, slightly above the national average of 15.9. The most active age group is now between 20 and 29 years old, overtaking the previous top group of 30 to 39-year-olds. According to Jochen Müller, vice president of Swiss Active, young people increasingly associate fitness with a healthy and conscious lifestyle. This trend is also reflected in a decrease in alcohol consumption among young people. The economic climate of the sector remains extremely positive, with nine out of ten managers rating their financial situation as good or very good. The sector's revenues grew by 4.4%, reaching a volume of 1.36 billion francs. The number of employees in the sector increased by 3.8%, reaching 27,667. ### Implications for cross-border workers Cross-border workers who work in Ticino and reside in Italy can benefit from this growth in the fitness sector.
