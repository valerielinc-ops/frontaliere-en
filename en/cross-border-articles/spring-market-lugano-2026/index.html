Spring Market in Lugano: lakefront closed to traffic

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

On Friday, May 1st, Lugano's lakefront transforms into a pedestrian island with craft stalls and local products.

Contesto

In brief - Spring Market in Lugano on Friday, May 1st - Lakeside closed to traffic from 9 AM to 8 PM - 50 stalls between Riva Giocondo Albertolli and Riva Vincenzo Vela ## Key facts - What: Spring Market with artisan stalls and local products - When: Friday, May 1st 2026, from 10 AM to 7 PM - Where: Lugano Lakeside, between Riva Giocondo Albertolli and Riva Vincenzo Vela - Who: Organized by the Events and Congress Division – City of Lugano - Amount: 50 selected stalls In Lugano, on Friday, May 1st, the lakeside will transform into a splendid pedestrian island to host the Spring Market. The closure to traffic will allow citizens and tourists to make the most of the walk, enjoying the panorama in total tranquility. From 10 AM to 7 PM, around fifty selected stalls will animate the stretch from Riva Giocondo Albertolli to Riva Vincenzo Vela. It will be the perfect moment to find unique objects, local crafts, original gift ideas, or clothing items and to visit the city in the full splendor of its springtime. ### Useful information On the occasion of the market, the lakeside will remain closed to traffic (between the LAC roundabout and Piazza Castello) from 9 AM to 8 PM. "We also remind you of the regulations of the municipal police regarding the event organized by the Swiss Trade Union, which will result in the temporary interruption of road traffic (especially during the procession scheduled between approximately 4 PM and 5 PM), on the following road sections: Via Capelli - Viale Castagnola - Viale Cassarate - Viale Cattaneo - last stretch of Corso Elvezia from Via Balestra - Via Magatti".
