Varese: CSR and Social Innovation Fair at Ville Ponti Congress Centre

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

On May 6, 2026, Varese will host the CSR Fair, an event dedicated to sustainability and social innovation with businesses, institutions, and the Third Sector.

Contesto

In brief - CSR Fair in Varese on May 6, 2026 - Sustainability and social innovation at the center - Free participation with online registration ## Key facts - What: CSR and Social Innovation Fair - When: Wednesday, May 6, 2026 - Where: Ville Ponti Convention Center, Varese - Who: Businesses, institutions, academia, and the third sector - Cost: Free participation The CSR and Social Innovation Fair will take place in Varese on Wednesday, May 6, 2026, at the Ville Ponti Convention Center. The event, organized by the Varese Chamber of Commerce, brings together businesses, institutions, academia, and the third sector to discuss sustainability and social innovation. The event will be opened by Rossella Sobrero of the Fair's promotional group and Mauro Vitiello, president of the Varese Chamber of Commerce. The program includes speeches and panels dedicated to the main challenges of sustainability: from ESG to bioeconomy, to social inclusion and collaboration in supply chains. Among the speakers will also be Professor Matteo Pedrini of the Catholic University. Various panels will bring together businesses, research, and local realities to discuss innovation, circular economy, and social responsibility. ### Implications for the territory Sustainability is today an indispensable horizon for the development of the local business system. The CSR Fair represents an important opportunity to raise awareness among businesses and citizens about the importance of choices capable of generating long-term value. Participation in the event is free with online registration and is open to businesses, students, and interested citizens. ### Detailed program The day's program includes: 1.
