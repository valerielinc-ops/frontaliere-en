Solidarity Mother's Day: restaurant vouchers for single-parent families (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

Soccorso d'Inverno and GastroTicino gift Mother's Day vouchers to 26 struggling single-parent families

Contesto

In brief - 26 single-parent families receive restaurant vouchers - Value between 100 and 300 francs - 74 beneficiaries, including 50 children ## Key facts - What: Restaurant vouchers - When: May 10, 2026 - Where: Ticino - Who: Soccorso d'Inverno Ticino and GastroTicino - Amount: 100-300 francs LUGANO - Solidarity Mother's Day for Soccorso d'Inverno Ticino and GastroTicino. The two associations have joined forces to celebrate May 10 by bringing a smile. In concrete terms, 26 single-parent families in economic fragility have received vouchers offered by GastroTicino worth between 100 and 300 francs, proportional to the family composition. A simple but significant gesture that will allow 74 people – including 50 children – to experience a moment of normality and sharing within the restaurants affiliated with the association chaired by Massimo Suter. ### Concrete help The initiative is particularly aimed at single mothers, often facing complex personal situations, such as difficult separations and lack of financial support. Women who, despite everything, carry out their parental role with determination, daily facing sacrifices and difficulties. In Ticino, single-parent families represent an increasingly relevant reality: about 20% of families with children under 25 are composed of a single parent and, in the vast majority of cases (over 80%), it is the mother. ### The economic situation These are nuclei particularly exposed to the risk of poverty: already in 2020, almost a third were in this condition and almost half were unable to face an unexpected expense. Data that, in recent years, has only worsened. Implications for cross-border workers This solidarity initiative not only helps struggling single-parent families but also demonstrates the importance of collaboration between the economic sector and volunteer associations. For cross-border workers living in Italy and working in Ticino, it is an example of how the social and economic fabric of the Canton of Ticino can offer concrete support in times of need. ### How to participate If you are a cross-border worker and want to contribute to similar initiatives, you can donate to Soccorso d'Inverno Ticino or participate in events organized by GastroTicino. Additionally, you can report families in need who could benefit from this type of support. Donations can be made online or at the local offices of Soccorso d'Inverno. ### The value of solidarity Solidarity is a fundamental value in an inclusive society. Initiatives like this show how small gestures can make a big difference in people's lives. For cross-border workers, who often find themselves navigating between two different systems, it is important to know that there are support networks ready to help in times of difficulty. ### Conclusion The initiative by Soccorso d'Inverno and GastroTicino for Mother's Day is an example of how solidarity can create a positive and lasting impact. For cross-border workers, it is an opportunity to see how the social and economic fabric of Ticino can offer concrete and inclusive support. ## Recommended tools For an updated estimate, use the net salary calculator and the CHF-EUR exchange comparator.

What to do if you're in difficulty If you find yourself in an economic difficulty and you're a single parent, you can contact Soccorso d'Inverno Ticino to request support. The organization offers a series of services, including the payment of urgent bills, the distribution of essential goods, and support for children's extracurricular activities. ### How to donate If you want to contribute to solidarity initiatives like this one, you can make a donation to Soccorso d'Inverno Ticino. Donations can be made online or at local offices. Every contribution, big or small, makes a difference. ### Report cases of need If you know of single-parent families in difficulty, you can report their case to Soccorso d'Inverno Ticino. The organization will assess the situation and, if necessary, provide the appropriate support. ### Use the salary calculator For cross-border workers who want to better plan their finances, it's useful to use the salary calculator available on our website.
