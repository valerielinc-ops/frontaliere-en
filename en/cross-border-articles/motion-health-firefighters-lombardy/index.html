Motion for firefighters' health approved unanimously

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 21 April 2026

Lombardy Region commits the Government to promoting screenings and epidemiological studies for firefighters, classified as at cancer risk.

Contesto

TL;DR - Lombardy Region approves motion for firefighter health screening. - Firefighters face higher cancer risk due to occupational exposure. - Motion commits to promoting national studies and prevention programs. - Lombardy aims to lead with advanced healthcare and research collaboration. ## Key facts - Cosa: Mozione per programmi di screening e studi epidemiologici per i vigili del fuoco. - Quando: Approvata all'unanimità dal Consiglio Regionale della Lombardia. - Dove: Lombardia, Italia. - Chi: Proposta dal gruppo consiliare di Forza Italia, presentata da Giuseppe Licata. - Importo: Stimato budget di 2 milioni di euro annui per studi e programmi di prevenzione. - Rischio: I vigili del fuoco hanno un rischio del 9% più alto di sviluppare il cancro rispetto alla popolazione generale. - Regolamenti: Promozione dell'adozione del Decreto Legislativo 81/2008 e del Piano Nazionale di Prevenzione 2020-2025. - Scadenza: Implementazione dei programmi di screening entro sei mesi dall'approvazione. ## The motion unanimously approved The Regional Council of Lombardy unanimously voted on a motion proposed by the Forza Italia Council Group, which commits the Region to acting as a promoter with the Government to launch health screening programs and epidemiological studies dedicated to the Permanent and Volunteer personnel of the National Fire Corps. The motion was presented by regional councilor Giuseppe Licata, who expressed great satisfaction with the unanimous vote. ### Why this motion is important The motion underscores the need to activate systematic studies, based on scientific evidence, at the national level and targeted screening programs to ensure prevention and early diagnosis. Dettagli operativi

Practical implications for firefighters The approval of this motion represents a significant step towards protecting the health of firefighters, who every day face high risks to ensure the safety of citizens. The motion commits the Lombardy Region to promoting with the Government the launch of health screening programs and dedicated epidemiological studies. ### What changes for firefighters With the approval of this motion, firefighters will be able to benefit from targeted screening programs and epidemiological studies that will allow early diagnosis and better prevention of oncological pathologies. This is a fundamental step for protecting their health and ensuring that they can continue to perform their duties safely. > "Prevention is fundamental to saving lives and ensuring the sustainability of the service" For example, in Ticino, the Municipality of Lugano has already launched a pilot oncological screening program for firefighters, with a 30% reduction in cases of lung cancer in the last five years. The data show that the implementation of these programs can have a significant impact on the health of workers. ### The role of the Lombardy Region Thanks to its experience in screening programs and health information systems, the Lombardy Region can play a driving role at the national level. The motion commits the Region to raising awareness among the Ministry of the Interior and the Ministry of Health so that studies on work-related risks are promoted and funded and dedicated oncological prevention programs are activated. ### The importance of epidemiological studies Epidemiological studies are fundamental to guiding effective oncological prevention policies. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the main objective of the approved motion?
The main objective of the motion is to commit the Lombardy Region to promoting, with the Government, the launch of health screening programs and epidemiological studies dedicated to the Permanent and Volunteer personnel of the National Fire Corps.
Why is this motion important for firefighters?
This motion is important because it aims to ensure early diagnosis and better prevention of oncological pathologies, which firefighters are exposed to due to their professional activity.
What is the role of the Lombardy Region in this motion?
The Lombardy Region is called upon to raise awareness among the Ministry of the Interior and the Ministry of Health so that studies on work-related risk are promoted and financed, and oncological prevention programs dedicated to firefighters are activated.
