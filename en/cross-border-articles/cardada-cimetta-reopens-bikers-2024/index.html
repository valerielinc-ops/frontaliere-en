Cardada Cimetta reopens to bikers: here's what changes (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 8 May 2026

After the closure for extraordinary maintenance, trail 397 at Cardada Cimetta is once again rideable. Here are the new features for mountain bike enthusiasts

In brief - Trail 397 reopened after maintenance closure - Fallen trees and rocks caused by exceptional weather conditions - Bike transport to the summit back in service ## Key facts - What: Reopening of trail 397 for mountain biking - When: After extraordinary maintenance work - Where: Cardada Cimetta, Locarno - Who: Trail Crew Ascona-Locarno and Cardada Tourist Facilities - Amount: Not specified The exceptional weather conditions at the start of the season caused numerous trees to fall and rocks to shift at Cardada Cimetta, necessitating a prolonged closure of trail 397. The extraordinary maintenance work, carried out by the Trail Crew of Ascona-Locarno with the support of Cardada Tourist Facilities, allowed the trail to be restored to a safe condition. 'Thanks to the great restoration and securing work, we are happy today to welcome back all mountain biking enthusiasts and bring them back to the summit to enjoy the outdoor season to the fullest,' reads the official statement. ### What changes for bikers With the reopening of the trail, bikers can once again use the bike transport to the summit, which was previously suspended for safety reasons. The route has been fully verified and secured to ensure maximum protection for all users. Mountain bike enthusiasts can now once again enjoy the splendid panoramic views and well-maintained trails of Cardada Cimetta. ### Practical implications For cross-border workers who love outdoor sports, the reopening of the trail represents an opportunity to spend their free time in a safe and well-organized environment. Practical analysis: what changes for cross-border workers The reopening of trail 397 at Cardada Cimetta represents an important opportunity for cross-border workers who love practicing outdoor sports. The possibility of transporting bicycles to higher altitudes facilitates access to more challenging routes, making the experience even more enjoyable. Additionally, the extraordinary maintenance has improved the quality of the trails, offering a smoother and safer riding experience. ### Tax implications For cross-border workers residing in Italy and working in Switzerland, practicing outdoor sports can influence their tax residence. It is important to remember that tax residence is determined by the duration of the stay and the frequency of movements. The possibility of spending leisure time in Switzerland can influence the determination of tax residence, so it is advisable to consult a tax expert to avoid problems with the authorities. ### Comparison with other destinations Cardada Cimetta is one of the most appreciated destinations for mountain biking in the Canton Ticino. However, there are other locations that offer similar routes. For example, Monte San Salvatore and Monte Brè are two other popular destinations for mountain bike enthusiasts. Each location has its unique characteristics, but all offer an unforgettable riding experience. ### Future scenarios As the summer season approaches, it is likely that the number of visitors will increase significantly. Local operators are already preparing to welcome a larger number of tourists, offering additional services and further improving the infrastructure.
