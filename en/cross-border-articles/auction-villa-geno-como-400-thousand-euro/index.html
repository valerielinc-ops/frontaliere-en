Villa Geno in Como up for auction: nearly 400k euros needed (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

The municipality of Como is auctioning off Villa Geno and the adjacent green area. The concession has an annual rent of nearly 400,000 euros.

Contesto

In brief - Villa Geno in Como up for auction for nearly 400,000 euros - 6-year concession for tourist/accommodation activities - Participation applications by July 6, 2026 ## Key facts - What: Public auction for the concession of Villa Geno and green area - When: Applications by July 6, 2026, opening of bids on July 9, 2026 - Where: City of Como - Who: City of Como - Amount: 345,950 euros annually for the villa, 30,118 euros annually for the green area The City of Como has announced a public auction for the concession of Villa Geno and the adjacent green area. The villa, located between numbers 12 and 13 of the homonymous avenue, was managed until 2024 by the company Sunlake, but the renewal of the concession was blocked. Now, the City is looking for a new manager for tourist/accommodation activities with high-level catering and event organization. The green area, on the other hand, must be maintained as such. The concession will last 6 years, with an annual fee of 345,950 euros for the villa and 30,118 euros for the green area. The provisional award will go to the competitor who offers the highest annual consideration compared to the auction base. Applications to participate must be received by 12 PM on July 6, 2026 at the City Hall. The opening of the bids is scheduled for July 9. ### Implications for cross-border workers The auction of Villa Geno represents an opportunity for entrepreneurs interested in managing a prestigious tourist facility in the heart of Como. For cross-border workers in the tourism or catering sector, this could be a chance to expand their activities or invest in a high-level project.
