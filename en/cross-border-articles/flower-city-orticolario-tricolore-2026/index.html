Blooming cities: balconies in Como and Cernobbio honor the Tricolor

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

Orticolario launches the 'Cities in Bloom' initiative to celebrate the 80th anniversary of the Italian Republic, transforming balconies and shop windows into Tricolor-themed artworks.

Contesto

In brief - Orticolario launches 'Città in fiore' for the 80th anniversary of the Republic - Balconies and shop windows in Como and Cernobbio will celebrate the Tricolore - Participation open to residents, businesses, and schools ## Key facts - What: 'Città in fiore' initiative to celebrate the 80th anniversary of the Italian Republic - When: Deadline for applications May 24, 2026 - Where: Como and Cernobbio - Who: Orticolario, Grandi Giardini Italiani, Fondazione Minoprio, Prefettura di Como - Amount: Not specified On the occasion of the eightieth anniversary of the Italian Republic, Orticolario has launched the 'Città in fiore' initiative, an invitation to transform the city into a dispersed garden. Shop windows, balconies, and windowsills overlooking the streets of Como and Cernobbio become places of creative expression and community value. The initiative is open to everyone: residents, businesses, schools, and associations. Each composition, simple or elaborate, will contribute to creating a great collective story spread through the streets, neighborhoods, and shores of the lake. Each participant is asked to interpret, with a creative eye, the Tricolore: green, white, and red. To participate, simply fill out an online form with the materials of the realized setup, by Sunday, May 24, 2026. A jury will select the most original balcony, which will be featured on Orticolario's and Grandi Giardini Italiani's official channels on Tuesday, June 2, Republic Day. ### Participation and Selection To make the initiative even more alive and shared, the project also involves a selection of florists and local realities, daily custodians of beauty and expertise. Dettagli operativi

Implications for the Community The 'Città in fiore' initiative not only celebrates an important anniversary but also strengthens the sense of community among the residents of Como and Cernobbio. The participation of commercial activities, schools, and associations creates a closer bond among the different actors in the community, promoting collaboration and the sharing of common values. The transformation of private spaces into collective works of art represents a tangible way to express pride in one's identity and history. ### Cultural and Touristic Impact The initiative also has a significant impact on the cultural and touristic levels. The creation of a diffused garden along the streets and shores of the lake attracts the attention of visitors and tourists, contributing to the enhancement of the territory and the promotion of local tourism. Moreover, the participation of florists and local entities offers an opportunity to showcase local skills and creativity, reinforcing the image of Como and Cernobbio as cities at the forefront in the field of horticulture and floral design. ### Involvement of Schools and Associations Schools and associations play a crucial role in this initiative. By involving the younger generations in the creation of floral compositions, 'Città in fiore' promotes environmental education and awareness of the importance of urban greenery. Associations, on the other hand, can use this occasion to organize events and activities that engage community members, creating a sense of belonging and active participation. ### Sustainability and Educational Value The initiative also promotes sustainability and the educational value of urban greenery. How to Participate To participate in 'Città in fiore', you need to fill out an online form with the materials of the setup created by Sunday, May 24, 2026. The form is available on the Orticolario website, where you can also find the complete rules and application procedures. The jury will select the most original balcony, which will be awarded with a spot on the official channels of Orticolario and Grandi Giardini Italiani. ### Tips for Creating Compositions To create floral compositions that reflect the Tricolore, you can use local plants and materials. Florists and local entities involved in the initiative offer suggestions and materials to inspire participants. It is important to choose plants that are suitable for the local climate and require little maintenance to ensure the longevity of the compositions over time. ### Related Events and Activities During the initiative period, related events and activities are planned to involve the community. Schools and associations can organize workshops and workshops to teach participants how to create floral compositions. Additionally, guided tours of the gardens and participating storefronts are planned to allow citizens to appreciate the beauty of the collective creations. ### Conclusion 'Città in fiore' represents a unique opportunity to celebrate the 80th anniversary of the Italian Republic and to promote green culture and sustainability. The active participation of the community, schools, and associations makes this initiative an example of how art and nature can come together to create a positive impact on the territory and society.
