Ticino Highway Works: Nighttime Closures in Besnate, Turate, and on the A9

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 20 April 2026

Autostrade per l'Italia has scheduled nighttime interventions from April 20 to 24, 2026. Closures will affect Besnate, Turate, and the A9 between Lainate, Como, and Chiasso.

Contesto

TL;DR - Autostrade works April 20-24, 2026 - Night closures on A9 and D08 - Alternatives for cross-border workers - Plan ahead to avoid delays ## Key facts - Intervention Period: April 20 to April 24, 2026 - Lario Ovest Closure: Night between April 20 and 21, 9 PM to 5 AM - Besnate Station Closure: Night between April 21 and 22, 9 PM to 5 AM - Turate Exit Closure: Night between April 23 and 24, 9 PM to 5 AM - Impacted Motorways: A9 Lainate-Como-Chiasso and D08 Gallarate-Gattico - Alternative Exits: Sesto Calende Vergiate, Solbiate Arno, Busto Arsizio - Purpose of Works: Maintenance of protective barriers for safety standards Autostrade per l'Italia has scheduled a series of maintenance interventions that will affect the junctions between the A8 and A26 and the main exits of the Lainate-Como-Chiasso section. The works will begin on Monday, April 20, and continue until Friday, April 24, with various methods and diversions planned. ## First intervention: closure of the Lario Ovest service area The first intervention is scheduled for the night between Monday, April 20, and Tuesday, April 21. On the A9 Lainate-Como-Chiasso, the 'Lario Ovest' service area, located between Fino Mornasco and Lomazzo Nord in the direction of Lainate, will be closed. The closure will be effective from 9:00 PM to 5:00 AM the following morning. ## Works on the Gallarate-Gattico On Tuesday and Wednesday, the works will move to the D08 Gallarate-Gattico. The Besnate station will be closed in both directions from 9:00 PM on Tuesday, April 21, to 5:00 AM on Wednesday, April 22. For those heading towards the A26, it is recommended to enter at Sesto Calende Vergiate, while for those heading towards Varese or Milan, the alternatives are Solbiate Arno or Busto Arsizio on the A8. The entrance to Sesto Calende Vergiate for those heading towards the A26 and the exits of Solbiate Arno or Busto Arsizio for those heading towards Varese or Milan are the recommended solutions.
