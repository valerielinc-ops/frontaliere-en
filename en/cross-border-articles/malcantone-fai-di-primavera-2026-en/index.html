Dettagli operativi

Discover Malcantone with the 2026 Spring FAI Days The Spring FAI Days are organised by FAI Swiss and focus on promoting the culture and history of the Swiss territory. This year the event highlights Malcantone, a region offering a unique blend of nature, art and architecture. Visitors can explore natural sites such as the Malcantone Nature Park and discover the secrets of historic homes like Castelgrande in Bellinzona. During the Spring FAI Days the cultural and historic wealth of Malcantone can be experienced by visiting landmarks such as Sonvico Castle in Giubiasco, which offers panoramic views over the Leventina Valley, or the Lugano Civic Museum, home to a collection of artworks and archaeological finds from the region. Guided tours and cultural activities also provide an opportunity to deepen knowledge of the area. One of the most interesting activities scheduled is a guided visit to the Malcantone Nature Park, covering about 20 hectares and hosting a unique variety of flora and fauna. The tour will be led by an expert naturalist who will present the park's most interesting aspects, such as spontaneous vegetation and local wildlife. A guided walk through the park will also be available, offering the chance to uncover Malcantone's natural secrets. Other activities include a visit to Castelgrande in Bellinzona, which offers sweeping views over the city and the Basso Valley, and a conference on conservation strategies for Malcantone's cultural heritage, presenting the most effective initiatives to preserve the region's history and culture. To take part, register on the FAI Swiss website, which offers online booking. The registration fee for the Spring FAI Days is CHF 50, while guided activities cost CHF 20.
