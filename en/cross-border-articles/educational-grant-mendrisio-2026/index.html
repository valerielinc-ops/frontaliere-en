Practical Implications and Concrete Scenarios The municipal social benefits offered by Mendrisio represent an important support for families and people in need. The Educational Allowance, for example, can make a difference for families with children, allowing them to cope with the expenses related to education and the growth of young people. For the 2025-2026 school year, the allowance has been set at 500 francs per child, with a maximum limit of 1,500 francs per family. This contribution can be used for the purchase of school materials, extracurricular activities, or language courses. The Contribution for stays, colonies, summer camps, and courses offers an opportunity for children and young people to participate in summer activities, improving their quality of life and giving parents a moment of respite. For 2026, the contribution is 300 francs per child, with a maximum of 900 francs per family. In Lugano, for example, the contribution is 250 francs per child, with a maximum limit of 750 francs per family. The Housing Subsidy is particularly relevant in a context where rental costs are constantly increasing. This contribution can help families maintain their home, avoiding housing discomfort situations. In Mendrisio, the subsidy is calculated based on taxable income and family composition. For a family with two children, the maximum contribution is 1,200 francs per month. Punti chiave

What to do to request the benefits To request the Educational Allowance, the Contribution for stays, colonies, summer camps and courses, or the Housing Subsidy in the Municipality of Mendrisio, it is necessary to follow a specific procedure. Here are the detailed steps to follow: 1. Check the requirements: Verify the taxable income and asset limits established by the municipal regulations. For example, for the 2026 Educational Allowance, the maximum taxable income for a family of 4 is set at CHF 120,000. For the Housing Subsidy, the taxable income limit is CHF 80,000 for a single person. If you have doubts, contact the nearest municipal office or consult the official website of the Municipality of Mendrisio. 2. Gather the documentation: Prepare all the necessary documents, such as the residence certificate, the income certificate (model D), any expense certificates for summer courses or stays, and the completed application form. Make sure you also have a copy of your passport or ID card and, if applicable, the rental contract for the Housing Subsidy. 3. Fill out the form: Download and fill out the appropriate application form available on the website of the Municipality of Mendrisio. Make sure to fill out all the required fields and attach the necessary documentation. For the Contribution for summer stays, for example, you need to attach a copy of the confirmation of enrollment in the camp or course. 4. Submit the application: Send the completed application and the required documentation by June 30, 2026. You can submit the application in person at the municipal office of Mendrisio, located at Via G. Motta 2, or send it by mail to Comune di Mendrisio, Servizio Sociale, Via G. Motta 2, 6850 Mendrisio.
