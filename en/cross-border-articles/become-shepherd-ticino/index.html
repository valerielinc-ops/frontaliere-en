The Canton of Ticino offers a training program to become a pastor, open to anyone interested.

Frequently Asked Questions
How can I enroll in the training course for shepherds in Ticino?
To register, go to https://pastore a. eventbrite. com, click on 'Register' and fill out the form with name, email address and tax code from Ticino. The deadline for registration is 30 March.
What is the economic advantage of the training of 5 weeks compared to the traditional approach to becoming a pastor in Ticino?
The training of 5 weeks offers a faster economic return with an initial investment of 650 CHF + 260 CHF of subsidy and an estimated annual income of 5,500 CHF, against 10,000 CHF of initial investment and 4,000 CHF of annual income of the traditional approach.
What are the safety regulations to be respected for the management of livestock in the mountains?
The safety regulations include the obligation to have safety equipment, such as ski lifts and zavorres, and the use of a GPS system for tourist trails, according to the Ticino Ordinance 2021. In addition, regular check-in with park office and GPS system verification is required.
What incentives are available to install environmentally friendly fences?
The incentive to install environmentally friendly fences is a bonus of CHF 1,200. This incentive is part of the policy to promote sustainable pasture practices and reduce environmental impact.
How can I use the training certificate to improve job opportunities?
The training certificate is recognised at regional level and opens up new employment opportunities in the Alpine tourism sector. It increases the chances of obtaining seasonal employment contracts and improves the recognized professionalism on the territory, essential to access regional contributions.
