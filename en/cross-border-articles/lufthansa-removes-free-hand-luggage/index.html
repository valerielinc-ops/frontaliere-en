Dettagli operativi

• ## Implications for passengers and cross-border workers The new 'Economy Basic' fare will have a significant impact on passengers, particularly cross-border workers who frequently travel between Switzerland and Italy. With the introduction of this fare, passengers will have to choose between a more economical fare with fewer baggage services and a more expensive fare that includes standard hand luggage. ### What changes for cross-border workers? Cross-border workers who frequently travel between Switzerland and Italy will have to adapt to the new 'Economy Basic' fare. If they choose this fare, they will be limited to one personal item, such as a laptop bag or a small backpack. This could be a problem for those who need to carry more luggage, for example for work reasons or for longer trips. ### Comparison with the previous situation Before the introduction of the new 'Economy Basic' fare, the most economical fare of Swiss was the 'Light', which included hand luggage up to eight kilograms and the accumulation of miles with Miles & More. With the new fare, passengers will have four fares available in Economy class: Basic, Light, Classic and Flex. The 'Light' fare will be updated and booking changes will only be possible by paying a commission. ### What to do if you need more luggage? If you need to carry more luggage, you can choose one of the more expensive fares, such as 'Light', 'Classic' or 'Flex'. These fares include standard hand luggage and, in the case of 'Flex', also the possibility to change the booking without paying a commission. However, these fares are more expensive than 'Economy Basic'. ## Useful tools to protect your net income To reduce FX leakage, compare CHF-EUR exchange options and banks for cross-border workers.
