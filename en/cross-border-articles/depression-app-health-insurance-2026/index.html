Frequently Asked Questions
What is Deprexis?
Deprexis is a mobile or laptop app that offers personalized therapeutic support to help manage depression. It was developed by the German company GAIA and approved by the Swiss Federal Office of Public Health (FOPH).
How does Deprexis work?
Deprexis provides immediate and location-independent access to personalized therapy. The app is based on clinical studies that have demonstrated its effectiveness. However, it does not replace traditional therapy and should be integrated with it.
How much does Deprexis cost?
The cost of Deprexis is 300 euros for a three-month subscription, totaling approximately 1,200 euros per year. The rates in Swiss francs have not yet been determined, but they are expected to be similar.
