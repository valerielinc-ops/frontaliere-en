Frequently Asked Questions
What should I do if I suspect I have been hacked?
If you suspect you have been hacked, it's important to act quickly. Block access to the account using the secret code or by immediately contacting the bank. Change all passwords of the compromised accounts and ensure they are complex and unique. Contact the relevant authorities to report the incident and carefully monitor your account movements to identify any suspicious transactions.
What are the main differences between banking security systems in Italy and Switzerland?
Banking security systems in Italy and Switzerland can vary significantly. In Italy, authentication is often based on passwords, while in Switzerland it is often based on SMS codes. Monitoring in Switzerland is advanced and timely, while in Italy it can be limited. Customer service in Switzerland is generally efficient and timely, while in Italy it can be variable.
How can I protect myself from online scams?
To protect yourself from online scams, it's important to take some preventive measures. Set complex and unique passwords for each account, enable two-factor authentication, monitor suspicious activities, regularly update security software, and use secure networks. Use security tools such as security calculators, password generators, and updated security software.
