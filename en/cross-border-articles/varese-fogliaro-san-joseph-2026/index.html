Fogliaro celebrates St. Joseph the Worker with mass, food and market

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

The Fogliaro district in Varese celebrates St. Joseph the Worker with a day of events including solemn mass, stalls and local gastronomy

Contesto

In brief - Fogliaro celebrates St. Joseph the Worker on May 1, 2026 - Solemn Mass at 11:00 and Holy Rosary at 18:00 - Craft market and gastronomic stall for the entire day ## Key facts - What: Feast of St. Joseph the Worker - When: May 1, 2026, from 11:00 to 18:00 - Where: Streets adjacent to the parish church of San Giuseppe, Via Santa Caterina, 15, Varese - Who: Parish of San Giuseppe in Fogliaro, with the patronage of the Municipality of Varese - Cost: Not specified The Fogliaro district in Varese celebrates St. Joseph the Worker with a day rich in events that combines religious devotion and community involvement. The feast, which will take place on May 1, 2026, begins at 11:00 with the solemn Mass at the parish church of San Giuseppe. In the afternoon, at 18:00, the recitation of the Holy Rosary is scheduled. Throughout the day, in the streets adjacent to the church, a craft market will be set up, appreciated for its variety of handicraft products. Additionally, at noon, the gastronomic stall will open, offering participants the opportunity to taste local specialties. The event is organized by the Pastoral Community Santi Gottardo and Giovanni Paolo II, which groups the parishes of Bregazzana, Fogliaro, Velate, Rasa, and Sant'Ambrogio, with the patronage of the Municipality of Varese. ### Detailed program The day begins with the solemn Mass at 11:00, a moment of prayer and reflection dedicated to St. Joseph the Worker. Subsequently, the gastronomic stall will offer traditional dishes and local delicacies, allowing participants to taste the cuisine of the territory. The craft market, which will take place throughout the day, will present a wide range of handicraft products, from local crafts to unique items. Frequently Asked Questions
Where is the parish church of San Giuseppe in Fogliaro located?
The parish church of San Giuseppe is located at Via Santa Caterina, 15, Varese. It is easily accessible by public transport or by car, with parking available nearby.
What are the event schedules during the festival?
The festival begins with a solemn mass at 11:00 AM, followed by the opening of the gastronomic stall at noon. The artisan market is open all day, and the recitation of the Holy Rosary is scheduled for 6:00 PM.
Is it necessary to book to participate in the festival?
It is not necessary to book to participate in the festival. However, for information and bookings, you can contact the number 333 208 7165.
