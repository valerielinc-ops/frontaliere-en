Cardiocentro Lugano: three more floors and a panoramic bar

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

The Cardiocentro in Lugano unveils three additional floors and a panoramic bar after three years of work. Comfort and breathtaking views for patients and staff.

Contesto

In brief - Cardiocentro Lugano: three more floors and a panoramic bar - New bright and comfortable spaces for patients and staff - 28 rooms, including 3 private ones per department ## Key facts - What: Renovation of Cardiocentro Lugano - When: Works completed in 2026 - Where: Lugano, Canton Ticino - Who: Cardiocentro Ticino Institute of EOC - Amount: Three more floors and a panoramic bar Lugano, April 28, 2026 - After three years of work, Cardiocentro Lugano unveils its new face, characterized by brightness, comfort, and functionality. The structure, which never interrupted its activity during the works, has been enriched with three new floors and a panoramic bar called Baricentro. The director of Cardiocentro, Massimo Manserra, explained that the new spaces include two floors dedicated to patient care and administrative areas, while the third floor hosts the panoramic bar. 'The construction site, although significant, never stopped the activity of the structure,' Manserra emphasized. From an assistential point of view, the renovation has brought a concrete improvement in the quality of the environments. 'The new spaces have certainly brought more light and more comfort to the nurses and, in particular, to our patients,' declared Stefano Bernasconi, head of the nursing service. Currently, there are 28 rooms, including 11 shared and semi-private, and three private ones per department. ### Patients' perception The change has also been perceived by the patients. 'Patients are certainly quite amazed because it doesn't seem like being inside a hospital,' added Bernasconi. For the staff, the new context also represents a stimulus to do better. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the new features of the Cardiocentro in Lugano?
The Cardiocentro in Lugano has been enriched with three new floors and a panoramic bar called Baricentro. The new spaces include two floors dedicated to patient accommodation and administrative areas, while the third floor houses the panoramic bar.
How have the environments of the Cardiocentro been improved?
The new spaces have brought more light and comfort to nurses and patients. Currently, there are 28 rooms, of which 11 are common and semi-private, and three are private for each department.
What is the role of Baricentro within the Cardiocentro?
Baricentro is a meeting and break area located on the roof of the building. It is open not only to staff but also to patients and their families, offering a panoramic view of the mountains of Lugano, the lake, and the entire landscape.
