What are the rescue procedures in case of a road accident?
In case of a road accident, it is important to stop in a safe place, turn on the emergency lights, and call for help at the number 112. Provide accurate information about the location and the condition of the injured parties, do not move the injured unless there is an immediate danger, and wait for the arrival of the rescue team.
What should I do to document a road accident?
To document a road accident, it is important to take photographs of the scene, gather information with the other drivers involved, and draw up a detailed report of the accident, including dates, times, and circumstances.
