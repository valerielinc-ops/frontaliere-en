In brief - Book presentation on pacifist feminism in Varese - Thursday, May 7th, Morselli Hall, Civic Library - Authors: Francesca Lacaita and Maria Grazia Suriano ## Key facts - What: Book presentation 'Above hate and massacre' - When: Thursday, May 7th 2026, 6:00 PM - Where: Morselli Hall, Varese Civic Library (via Sacco 9) - Who: Authors Francesca Lacaita and Maria Grazia Suriano, with professor Alberto Castelli - Cost: Free admission, booking recommended On Thursday, May 7th 2026, at 6:00 PM, in Morselli Hall of the Varese Civic Library, the presentation of the book 'Above hate and massacre' by Francesca Lacaita and Maria Grazia Suriano will take place. The peace strategies developed during that historical period were fundamental for building a more just and peaceful world, and their analysis offers insights for the present. ### The role of the history of political thought Professor Alberto Castelli, with his experience in the field of the history of political thought, will enrich the debate with critical analysis and reflections.
