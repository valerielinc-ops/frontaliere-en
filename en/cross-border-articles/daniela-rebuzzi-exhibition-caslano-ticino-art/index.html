Ticino's art beyond the border: Rebuzzi's exhibition (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 17 February 2026

From January 16 to April 26, 2026, in Caslano, the 'TRA-S-PARENZE' exhibition by Daniela Rebuzzi. An opportunity for cross-border workers to discover a locally-based, internationally renowned artist.

TL;DR - Daniela Rebuzzi's solo exhibition in Caslano, Ticino. - Exhibition titled 'TRA-S-PARENZE – Portals to the essence' - Free admission, open Jan 16 - Apr 26, 2026. - Artist known for awards, Venice Biennale 2024. ## Key facts - Artista: Daniela Rebuzzi, nata a Sorengo, Ticino. - Titolo mostra: TRA-S-PARENZE – Portals to the essence. - Luogo: La Galerie-Caslano, Casa Anziani Malcantonese, Caslano. - Data inizio: 16 gennaio 2026. - Data fine: 26 aprile 2026. - Orario: 10:00-11:00 e 13:30-18:00, tutti i giorni. - Biglietto: Ingresso gratuito. - Premi: Premio Lugano Art Award 2023, partecipazione Biennale Venezia 2024. Ticino is not just the daily destination for our work, but also a territory rich in cultural ferment, often just a few minutes from the Gaggiolo or Ponte Tresa border crossings. An example of excellence is the new solo exhibition by Ticino artist Daniela Rebuzzi, a name that has successfully captivated the national and international scene. The exhibition, titled “TRA-S-PARENZE – Portals to the essence,” will be hosted at La Galerie-Caslano, within the Casa Anziani Malcantonese, from January 16 to April 26, 2026. For thousands of cross-border workers, the weekend represents an opportunity to explore the canton beyond the office or construction site. This exhibition offers a highly valuable suggestion. Daniela Rebuzzi, born in Sorengo and active in the Malcantone region, is no ordinary artist. Her career is studded with prestigious awards: from her participation in the 60th Venice Biennale in 2024 to being awarded the Lugano Art Award in 2023. Her works, featured in collections across Europe, America, and the Middle East, are in constant dialogue with Nature, understood as a symbolic and spiritual space. Dettagli operativi

Portals to the essence: what to expect The exhibition's title, “TRA-S-PARENZE,” is a manifesto in itself. Rebuzzi's works do not stop at the surface but invite a deeper exploration. As described in the presentation, the exhibition "investigates the boundary between the visible and the invisible, matter and light, appearance and depth." The viewer is called upon to suspend immediate judgment and open up to a more conscious perception of reality. Through a language that the artist has refined over more than twenty years of research, the layered surfaces of her creations become symbolic portals. Form transforms into an inner experience, a perceptual journey. It is interesting to note that this search for depth is not limited to painting. Daniela Rebuzzi is also the author of the historical novel “Solstizio d’amore” (Solstice of Love), set during Napoleon's Russian campaign. A narrative sensibility that, according to the curators, subtly dialogues with her visual production, combining history, passion, and mystery. A multifaceted artist, therefore, capable of expressing her worldview through different mediums united by the same search for essence. 💡 Opening Reception: The vernissage will be held on Sunday, February 8, 2026, from 3:00 PM to 5:30 PM. An excellent opportunity to meet the artist and immerse yourself in her world.

Frequently Asked Questions
When will the vernissage of the Daniela Rebuzzi exhibition be held in Caslano?
The inauguration will be held on Sunday 8 February 2026, from 15:00 to 17:30.
Can I visit the exhibition 'TRA-S-PARENZE' by Daniela Rebuzzi during the working week?
Yes, the exhibition is open every day, including working weeks, with schedules from 10:00-11:00 and 13:30-18:00.
What are the awards received by Daniela Rebuzzi in her artistic career?
Daniela Rebuzzi has received numerous awards during his career. Among these is the participation in the 60th Venice Biennale in 2024 and the award of the prestigious Lugano Art Award in 2023. His works are present in art collections in Europe, America and the Middle East.
What is the main theme explored in the works of Daniela Rebuzzi?
The main theme of Daniela Rebuzzi's works is the exploration of the boundary between visible and invisible, matter and light, appearance and depth. Through his creations, the artist invites the viewer to a more conscious perception of reality, transforming the layers of his works into symbolic portals.
Did Daniela Rebuzzi only work in painting?
No, Daniela Rebuzzi is not limited to painting. She is also author of the historical novel 'Solstizio d'amore', set during the campaign of Napoleon's Russia. This highlights its artistic versatility, since it combines its narrative sensibilities with its visual production, creating a subtle dialogue between history, passion and mystery.
