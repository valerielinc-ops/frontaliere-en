Procedure for Free Access To benefit from free entry to the Verbania Landscape Museum on May 8, 2026, residents must follow a simple but well-defined procedure. They need to present themselves at the museum's ticket office with a valid ID that proves residency in Verbania or neighboring towns such as Cannobio, Cannero Riviera, and Maccagno with Pino and Veddasca. This document will be verified by the museum staff to confirm free access. > 'The initiative is a way to thank the local community for their continuous support,' said the museum director. ### Operational Checklist: - Valid ID (identity card, passport, or driver's license) - Proof of residence (recent bill, rental contract, residence certificate) - Electronic ticket (optional, but recommended to avoid queues) ### Practical Scenarios Comparison: - Resident of Verbania: Immediate access with ID. - Resident in neighboring towns: Additional residence verification with bill or contract. - Visitors from other regions: Paid access, with tickets at €10 for adults and €5 for youths under 18. ## Exhibited Works The initiative represents a unique opportunity to discover the works of Paolo Troubetzkoy, an artist who left a significant mark in the art world. The exhibited sculptures, coming from the museum's deposits, offer a rare and precious glimpse into the artist's work. Who can access the Museo del Paesaggio in Verbania for free on May 8, 2026?
