Fire in Chiasso: 30 residents evacuated from a building (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

A fire on the roof of a building in Chiasso led to the evacuation of 30 people. Two workers were slightly injured.

Contesto

In brief - Roof fire in a residential building in Chiasso - 30 residents evacuated - Two workers slightly injured ## Key facts - What: Roof fire in a residential building - When: May 4, 2026, just before 1:30 PM - Where: Piazza Indipendenza, Chiasso - Who: Cantonal police, Chiasso municipal police, Mendrisiotto firefighters - Impact: 30 people evacuated, 2 workers injured A fire broke out late this morning, just before 1:30 PM, on the roof of a residential building in Piazza Indipendenza, Chiasso. The incident required the intervention of numerous rescuers and the evacuation of residents. According to the Cantonal Police, the flames developed while four workers were carrying out work on the roof using a gas torch. The exact causes of the fire are still under investigation. As a precaution, the temporary evacuation of about thirty people present in the building was ordered. On the scene were the agents of the Cantonal Police, supported by the Municipal Police of Chiasso. Also present were the rescuers of the Mendrisiotto Autoambulance Service and the firefighters of the Cantonal Fire Rescue Center of Mendrisiotto, engaged in extinguishing operations. The flames remained confined to the outside of the building. Two workers were slightly injured: one suffered burns to the hands, while another showed symptoms of mild intoxication. Both were transported by ambulance to the hospital for examination. ### Impact on traffic To allow rescue operations and secure the area, Via Livio and Corso San Gottardo were temporarily closed to traffic, with inevitable repercussions on the area's traffic.
