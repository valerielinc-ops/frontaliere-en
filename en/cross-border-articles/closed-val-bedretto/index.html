Closed until further notice: Val Bedretto Road (cross-border guide)

The Val Bedretto is closed due to a high risk of landslides. Here are the measures taken and instructions for residents.

Contesto

TL;DR - Val Bedretto closed due to landslide risk - Closure starts from Airolo, effective immediately - Residents advised to stay indoors ## Key facts - Cosa: Chiusura della Val Bedretto - Quando: Da oggi, dalle 18:00 - Dove: Da Airolo in poi - Chi: Comune di Bedretto - Motivo: Alto rischio di frane - Impatto: Mobilità locale compromessa The Val Bedretto has been closed until further notice, starting from Airolo. The population remaining in the valley is advised to stay indoors as there is a high risk of landslides. This notice was issued by the Bedretto municipality, which specified that the measure is in effect from 18:00 of this evening. The Val Bedretto is an important route connecting Ticino to Switzerland, and its closure has a significant impact on local mobility.

Dettagli operativi

The Val Bedretto, which crosses the Ticino region, is an important road connecting Ticino to Switzerland. Its closure, announced by the Bedretto municipality, has an immediate impact on the daily lives of residents and visitors. The road, which has been closed from Airolo, is a fundamental route for the transport of goods and people between the two regions.
