FNMA seeks drivers and mechanics: meet on May 12 in Saronno (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 6 May 2026

FNMA organizes a Recruiting Day in Saronno on May 12 to hire drivers and mechanics. Direct interviews and company presentation.

Contesto

In brief - FNMA is looking for drivers and mechanics for public transport - Recruiting Day in Saronno on May 12 - Direct interviews and company presentation ## Key facts - What: Recruiting Day for drivers and mechanics - When: May 12, 2026 - Where: Villa Gianetti, Saronno - Who: FNMA Autoservizi - Perks: Benefits including free licenses and company welfare On Tuesday, May 12, Villa Gianetti in Saronno will host a Recruiting Day for selections at Ferrovie Nord Milano Autoservizi (FNMA). The event, promoted by the Employment Centers of the Province of Varese with the patronage of the Municipality of Saronno, will take place from 9 AM to 5:30 PM. Throughout the day, candidates can attend a company presentation and participate in individual interviews with recruiters. The search is particularly aimed at two professional profiles: bus drivers and mechanical maintainers. For selected candidates, various benefits are planned, including the free acquisition of D and CQC licenses through dedicated training paths, as well as a company welfare package and opportunities for internal professional growth. The Recruiting Day represents a collaboration between public institutions and businesses, with the aim of facilitating employment and supporting the local job market. To participate in the interviews, candidates must apply by Friday, May 8, by filling out the form available online or by scanning the QR code on the poster. ### Application procedure To participate in the Recruiting Day, candidates must follow a few simple but crucial steps. First, they need to fill out the application form available on the Saronno Employment Center website or by scanning the QR code on the event poster. Dettagli operativi

Implications for cross-border workers The FNMA Recruiting Day represents a concrete opportunity for cross-border workers who wish to work in the public transport sector. The proximity of Saronno to the Swiss border makes the event particularly interesting for those residing in Ticino and seeking job opportunities in Italy. ### Comparison with other job opportunities Compared to other job opportunities in the transport sector, the FNMA Recruiting Day offers some unique advantages. Firstly, the possibility of participating in direct interviews with recruiters allows for accelerating the selection process and obtaining a quick response on one's application. Furthermore, the company presentation provides a clear overview of the required tasks and career prospects, facilitating the candidates' choice. ### Practical scenarios for cross-border workers For a cross-border worker residing in Ticino and working in Italy, participating in the FNMA Recruiting Day could mean the opportunity to find stable and well-paid employment in the public transport sector. The proximity of Saronno to the border allows for reducing travel times and more easily reconciling work life with family life. ### Comparison with other companies in the sector Compared to other companies in the transport sector, FNMA offers a series of benefits that make it an interesting choice for cross-border workers. Among these, free training courses for obtaining D and CQC licenses stand out, which represent a significant investment for those intending to work in the sector. Furthermore, the company welfare package offers health assistance and mobility benefits, improving the quality of working life. ## Recommended tools For an updated estimate, use the net salary calculator and the CHF-EUR exchange comparator.

Punti chiave

How to Prepare for the Recruiting Day To maximize your chances of success at the Recruiting Day, candidates should prepare adequately. First, it is advisable to update your curriculum vitae and highlight any relevant experiences in the transport or mechanical maintenance sector. Additionally, it is useful to inform yourself about the required tasks and the skills necessary for the roles offered by FNMA. ### Required Documents During the Recruiting Day, candidates should bring with them some fundamental documents. Among these, the updated curriculum vitae stands out, any relevant certifications for the role you are applying for, and a valid ID document. Additionally, it is advisable to bring a copy of the application form completed and submitted online. ### Interview Tips During individual interviews with recruiters, it is important to present yourself in a professional and clear manner. Candidates should be prepared to answer questions about their work experience and the specific skills required for the roles offered. Additionally, it is useful to show interest in the company and the professional growth opportunities it offers. ### Next Steps After the Recruiting Day, selected candidates will receive an official communication from FNMA with details about the hiring process. It is important to follow the instructions provided and arrive punctually for any subsequent interviews or trial days. For those who are not selected, it is possible to continue monitoring FNMA's job offers and participate in future recruitment events. For more information and to apply, visit the Saronno Employment Center website or contact the number 02-9602166.
