Frequently Asked Questions
Which fireworks are subject to the ban?
The ban applies to firecrackers and fireworks designed solely to produce a bang without any light effects. Sparklers and volcanoes, which do not cause noise, remain permitted.
How can I obtain authorization to use fireworks?
To obtain cantonal authorization for the use of fireworks that present a medium or high risk, you need to submit a specific application to the competent authorities. The procedure may vary depending on your canton of residence and the type of event planned.
What are the alternatives to firecrackers and bang fireworks?
Less noisy and safer alternatives include sparklers and volcanoes, which are not subject to the ban and can be used without particular restrictions, unless they are subject to local regulations.
