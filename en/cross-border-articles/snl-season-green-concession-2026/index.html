Dettagli operativi

The federal concession is crucial for SNL, as it guarantees the right to operate on Lake Lugano and Lake Maggiore. The current license, which expires in 2026, was granted by the Swiss Confederation and allows SNL to offer public and tourist transport services on the lakes. The renewal of the concession is a complex process that requires the approval of the Swiss federal authorities. SNL has submitted its application and is awaiting the final decision. Director Bianchi expressed confidence but also emphasized the importance of this step for the company's future. "The concession is fundamental for our business," Bianchi stated. "Without it, we would not be able to offer our services and ensure mobility on the lake. We are optimistic, but we are aware that it is a long and complex process." SNL has invested a lot in sustainability and fleet electrification, and the renewal of the concession is crucial to continuing on this path. The company has already started collaborating with Swiss and Italian authorities to ensure efficient and environmentally friendly public transport. "We are working closely with the authorities to demonstrate our commitment to sustainability and reducing emissions," Bianchi added. "Our goal is to offer a modern and cutting-edge public transport service that is accessible to all and respects the environment." The federal concession is not the only aspect to consider. SNL must also comply with labor contracts and public transport regulations, ensuring the safety and comfort of passengers. The company plays an important role in promoting sustainable mobility and reducing traffic on the roads, especially for cross-border commuters who travel between Switzerland and Italy daily. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the new SNL service for cross-border commuters between Lugano and Porlezza in 2026?
A fully electrified fast boat, with a journey in 15-20 minutes, available early in the morning and at the end of working hours.
What is the expected impact of the electrified boat service on reducing emissions for cross-border commuters?
The electrified boat service is expected to significantly reduce greenhouse gas emissions for cross-border commuters, offering a sustainable alternative to road transport. SNL aims to electrify the entire fleet within the 'Venti35' project, with a positive impact on the environment.
How does the purchase of tickets for the new SNL electric boats between Lugano and Porlezza work in 2026?
Tickets can be purchased online via the SNL app or at the ticket machines at the departure piers. They will be valid for unlimited rides throughout the day with discounted rates for monthly passes, designed specifically for cross-border commuters.
What are the tax breaks for cross-border commuters using SNL electric boats in 2026?
Cross-border commuters using SNL electric boats will be able to benefit from a 15% tax deduction on the cost of annual passes, as provided for in the new agreement between Switzerland and Italy for sustainable cross-border mobility.
Are there free parking spaces near the piers in Lugano for those who leave their cars to take the electric boat to Porlezza?
Yes, SNL has entered into an agreement with the Municipality of Lugano to reserve 200 free parking spaces in the P1 and P2 car parks near the Lugano pier, accessible with an electronic card dedicated to boat passengers.
