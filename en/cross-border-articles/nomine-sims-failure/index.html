The Failure of SIMS Appointments: Lega's Stance

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

The SIMS appointment process ends in failure according to the Ticino Lega, while the government seeks to find a solution.

Contesto

In brief - The cantonal court has annulled the 2024 and 2025 appointments of the SIMS heads of section. - The Lega dei Ticinesi defines the management a failure. - The Cantonal Court declared the appointment of the two officials illegitimate. - The Government decided to publish a new tender. ## Key facts - What: Annulment of the appointments of Désirée Mallè and Mattia Pini - When: February 3, 2024, second ruling - Where: Cantonal Administrative Court, Canton Ticino - Who: Cantonal Court, Cantonal Government, Deputy Raffaella Zucchetti - Amount: not yet specified The case of the SIMS appointments, the Section for Upper Secondary Education, has dragged on over time, involving multiple appeals and judgments. After the Cantonal Administrative Court declared the appointment of the two heads of section, Désirée Mallè and Mattia Pini, in job sharing at 50%, illegitimate on February 3, 2024, the Court issued a second annulment ruling. The State Council had decided not to appeal to the Federal Court, confirming the annulment and announcing the publication of a new competition. The affair has sparked controversy and criticism, even in the Grand Council, where the State Councillor Marina Carobbio-Guscetti expressed regret for the officials involved. ### Details on the ruling The Court annulled the appointments of Mallè and Pini, decided in 2024 and renewed in 2025, as they were deemed illegitimate. The Government decided not to appeal this ruling, accepting the annulment and announcing that it would proceed with a new competition tender. Dettagli operativi

The analysis of this case highlights how the management of public competitions in Ticino can be subject to critical issues that undermine the credibility of institutions. Before the Tribunal's intervention, the appointments had been deemed regular, but the decision to annul them has raised doubts about the transparency and correctness of the selection processes. The fact that the Government has decided not to appeal against the Tribunal's ruling and to proceed with a new tender indicates a willingness to correct the course, but also raises questions about the overall management of appointments and the ability to prevent errors or favoritism. For cross-border workers and citizens of the Canton of Ticino, this case has practical repercussions. The credibility of public appointments influences the quality of educational policies and the transparency of recruitment procedures, which in turn can affect the quality of the school environment, tax returns, and any refunds of withholding tax. Frequently Asked Questions
Why have the SIMS appointments been canceled?
The appointments of Désirée Mallè and Mattia Pini were canceled by the Administrative Court of the canton because they were deemed unlawful. The decision is based on an appeal that highlighted irregularities in the selection process, leading to the annulment of the appointments for 2024 and 2025. The government has decided not to appeal this ruling, announcing the publication of a new competition announcement.
What is the stance of the Lega on these events?
The Ticino League describes the entire management of the SIMS appointments as a failure, accusing Emanuele Berger of managerial incompetence and claiming that the incident has undermined the credibility of the public selection system. Deputy Raffaella Zucchetti has submitted an inquiry seeking clarification on the management and credibility of the appointments.
How can one participate in a new competition?
To take part in a new competition, it is necessary to follow the official notices issued by the Department of Education, Culture, and Sports (DECS). It is important to prepare the required documents, such as management experience certificates and a detailed curriculum vitae. Regularly checking the official website and verifying the requirements published each time is highly recommended.
