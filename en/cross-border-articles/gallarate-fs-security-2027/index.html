Frequently Asked Questions
When will the new Fs Security outpost in Gallarate open?
The new Fs Security outpost in Gallarate will open during the course of 2027. The goal is to bring forward the date as much as possible, but the exact date has not yet been specified.
How many operators will be present at the Gallarate outpost?
The Gallarate outpost will initially consist of 12-15 operators, with the goal of achieving 24/7 coverage for 365 days a year.
What activities are planned for the Fs Security operators?
Fs Security operators will perform ticket inspections, sanction irregular passengers, and provide support in managing railway security. They will also be equipped with bodycams for high-risk situations.
