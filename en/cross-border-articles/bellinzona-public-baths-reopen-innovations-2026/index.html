Among the main ones, the possibility to purchase the entrance online to reduce queues at the ticket offices during peak hours. The structure, which has been operating for nearly sixty years, has undergone renovation work, including the renovation of the changing rooms on level +1 with new showers, replacement of the glazing, and refurbishment of lockers and benches. Opening hours will vary during the season: until June 12 from 9 am to 7:30 pm, from June 13 to August 16 from 9 am to 8 pm, and from August 17 to September 13 from 9 am to 7:30 pm. ### Dining novelties The catering service has been completely revised with the new 'Cielo Aperto' concept, which includes the Lounge Terrace with drinks and Thai specialties, a Self-Service Snack Bar with pizzeria, and the Ciccio Burger outlet dedicated to artisan burgers. The aim is to make the facility a reference point for residents and visitors. ### Renovations The renovation work, funded with a credit of 3.1 million CHF, mainly concerned the changing rooms on level +1. The new showers will not be immediately available, but the refurbishment of lockers and benches has already been completed.
