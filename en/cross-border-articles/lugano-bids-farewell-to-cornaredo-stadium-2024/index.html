Lugano bids farewell to Cornaredo Stadium: event on June 4 (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 7 May 2026

Public celebration for the stadium's 75th anniversary before its replacement with the new AIL Arena in 2026

Contesto

In brief - Farewell party on June 4 for Cornaredo Stadium - Picnic, circus, and concert for the entire population - New AIL Arena will replace the stadium from 2026-2027 ## Key facts - What: Farewell party for Cornaredo Stadium - When: Thursday, June 4, 2024, from 17.00 to 22.00 - Where: Cornaredo Stadium, Lugano - Who: City of Lugano - Cost: Free with registration for the picnic Cornaredo Stadium, a sporting symbol of Lugano, is ready to host its last major event before being replaced by the new AIL Arena. On Thursday, June 4, from 17.00 to 22.00, the city is organizing a party open to everyone to celebrate 75 years of history. The facility, inaugurated on November 25, 1951, has hosted memorable events such as the visit of Pope John Paul II in 1984 and the Cycling World Championships in 1996. ### Party program From 17.00 to 18.00, children aged 5 to 14 will be able to train with the local team players: Mattia Bottani, Mijat Maric, and Jonathan Sabbatini. Subsequently, from 18.30, the field will transform into a large meadow for a picnic with entertainment provided by the City of Lugano. The first 300 registrants via the MyLugano app will receive a free picnic basket. Even without registration, it will be possible to access the stadium for the entire evening, with bar points and a free buffet until it runs out. The atmosphere will be animated by the artists of Circo Fortuna and, from 20.30, by a concert of the United Soloists Orchestra. In case of bad weather, the event will take place on Sunday, June 7 with the same times. ### The future of the stadium After many years of service, Cornaredo Stadium will give way to the new AIL Arena. Dettagli operativi

Implications for cross-border workers The event on June 4th represents a unique opportunity for cross-border workers living and working in Ticino to participate in a historic moment for the city of Lugano. The celebration not only honors the past of the Cornaredo stadium but also looks towards the future with the construction of the new AIL Arena and the sports and events hub. ### What to expect Cross-border workers who decide to participate in the festival can enjoy an afternoon and evening filled with fun and socializing. The picnic on the grass, the circus, and the concert offer a varied program that can interest both families and young people. Additionally, the opportunity to train with local players is a unique experience for children and teenagers. ### Comparison with the previous situation Before the inauguration of the Cornaredo stadium in 1908, a group of young people had created a football field on the Campo Marzio grounds. The construction of the stadium represented a significant step for the city, offering an adequate sports facility for national and international events. The new AIL Arena will continue this tradition, offering modern and functional spaces for sports and cultural events. ### Future scenarios With the construction of the new sports and events hub, Lugano aims to become a reference point for sports and culture not only in Switzerland but also internationally. Cross-border workers will benefit from new entertainment and cultural event opportunities, contributing to improving the quality of life in the region. ### Procedure to participate To participate in the picnic, you need to register via the MyLugano app. The first 300 registrants will receive a free picnic basket.
