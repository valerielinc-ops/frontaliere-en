New schools for Vernate and Neggio: an investment for the future

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

The communities of Vernate and Neggio have inaugurated the new intermunicipal schools, a strategic project for education and community building

Contesto

In brief - Vernate and Neggio inaugurate new intermunicipal schools - Ceremony with cantonal and municipal authorities - Significant investment for education and community gathering ## Key facts - What: Inauguration of new intermunicipal schools - When: April 26, 2026 - Where: Vernate and Neggio, Canton Ticino - Who: Communities of Vernate and Neggio, cantonal and municipal authorities - Amount: Significant investment for the area The communities of Vernate and Neggio have a new intermunicipal school. The schools were inaugurated last Saturday, April 26, 2026, with a ceremony attended by the mayors and vice-mayors of the two towns, State Councillor Claudio Zali, president of the government, as well as a large participation of families, citizens, and representatives of the institutions. For the area, local authorities underline, this is 'a strategic project, born from the collaboration between the two municipalities to offer students a modern, functional, and future-oriented environment.' A shared operation resulting from 'a significant investment for the territory, designed to meet contemporary educational needs and to promote an innovative and welcoming learning environment.' ### A strategic project The new schools represent an important step for the communities of Vernate and Neggio. 'This is a strategic project that arises from the collaboration between the two municipalities,' emphasized State Councillor Claudio Zali during the ceremony. Local authorities highlighted how this investment is designed to meet contemporary educational needs, offering an innovative and welcoming learning environment.
