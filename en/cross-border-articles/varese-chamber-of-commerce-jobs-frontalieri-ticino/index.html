Varese: new job and trade opportunities for cross-border workers (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 26 May 2026

Over 5,000 expressions of interest for jobs from cross-border workers in Ticino. Varese Chamber of Commerce signs agreement with employment agencies.

Contesto

In Brief - Over 5,000 expressions of interest in employment from cross-border workers in Ticino. - Varese Chamber of Commerce signs agreement with leading employment agencies. - New agreement to facilitate employment of cross-border workers in the province of Varese. - Most in-demand sectors: manufacturing, services, welfare, and logistics. ## Key Facts - What: Agreement between Varese Chamber of Commerce and employment agencies. - When: Date not yet specified. - Where: Province of Varese, Italy. - Who: Varese Chamber of Commerce, Adecco, E-Work, Manpower, Openjobmetis, Randstad. - Amount: Over 5,000 expressions of interest for work. The Varese Chamber of Commerce has launched a new step in the “Come Live in Varese” project. Following agreements with real estate agents and a credit institution to facilitate home purchases, a new agreement with employment agencies has been established. The agreement aims to ease entry into the labor market for those wishing to move to the province of Varese. The agencies involved are Adecco, E-Work, Manpower, Openjobmetis, and Randstad. This initiative was driven by data emerging from the calls for applications: of the over 5,000 expressions of interest received, as many as 62% were from people primarily seeking employment. ### Project Numbers Among the new profiles are applicants from abroad (France, Spain, Austria, Switzerland, and Brazil) as well as from a broad range of Italian regions, including Sicily, Calabria, Puglia, Campania, Umbria, Lazio, Liguria, Emilia-Romagna, Marche, Veneto, Piedmont, and Lombardy itself. Dettagli operativi

New opportunities for cross-border workers The agreement signed between the Varese Chamber of Commerce and the main labor agencies represents a concrete opportunity for cross-border workers who wish to relocate and work in the province of Varese. In particular, the sectors where the mismatch is most felt are manufacturing and high-tech, the services and innovation sector, welfare and territory, as well as consumption channels and logistics. The province of Varese offers various job opportunities in these sectors, and the agreement with the labor agencies facilitates the meeting of supply and demand. «Our goal – explained President Vitiello – is to match this demand with the local business realities that you agencies know well, trying to solve the territorial mismatch problem that still shows very high rates.» ### Sectors with highest demand for personnel The sectors where the mismatch is most evident are: - Manufacturing and high-tech sector (mechanical engineering, aerospace and manufacturing industry) - Services and innovation (technology, IT, professional consulting, financial services) - Welfare and territory (social-health and assistance services) - Consumption channels and network logic (retail, catering, GDO, event organization, logistics and transport) ### Concrete examples of job opportunities A cross-border worker in a similar situation could find opportunities in various sectors. For example, an engineer from Canton Ticino could find work in manufacturing and high-tech companies in the province of Varese. Who can access the job opportunities offered by the Varese Chamber of Commerce?
