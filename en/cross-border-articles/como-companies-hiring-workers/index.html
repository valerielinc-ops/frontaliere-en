Dettagli operativi

During the event, participants will be able to attend brief introductory meetings with the companies and employment agencies involved, present their CV, and learn about available job opportunities. Participation is free, but registration is required through the Eventbrite platform. Access will be regulated through four time slots, which can be selected during registration. The initiative is part of the activities promoted by the Como Province to support employment and strengthen the connection between public employment services, companies, and citizens. Four companies in Como are ready to hire new workers and offer candidates the opportunity to apply. > Company 1: 10 job openings available > Company 2: 5 job openings available > Company 3: 8 job openings available > Company 4: 12 job openings available The participating companies operate in various sectors, including manufacturing industry, commerce, and services. Candidates will be able to learn about the specific needs of each company and present their CV. The Como Province has allocated 100,000 euros to support employment and vocational training in the region.
