MomòRide: carpooling challenge for Chiasso, Balerna and Novazzano

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

From May 1st, MomòRide, a biennial carpooling initiative for companies and commuters in Chiasso, Balerna and Novazzano, kicks off. Goal: 40,000 points in a month to donate CHF 1,000 to the Provvida Madre Foundation in Balerna.

Contesto

In brief - MomòRide starts on May 1st for companies and commuters from Chiasso, Balerna, and Novazzano - Goal: 40,000 points in a month to donate CHF 1,000 to the Fondazione Provvida Madre - Banca dello Stato del Canton Ticino sponsors the challenge ## Key facts - What: Biennial charitable carpooling initiative - When: From May 1st, 2024 - Where: Chiasso, Balerna, and Novazzano - Who: Companies and commuters from the three municipalities - Amount: CHF 1,000 for the Fondazione Provvida Madre On May 1st, the first collective challenge MomòRide kicks off, a biennial carpooling initiative designed for companies and commuters from Balerna, Chiasso, and Novazzano. The goal is to reach 40,000 points in a month, which will allow donating CHF 1,000 to the Fondazione Provvida Madre in Balerna. Participants will need to track all shared car commutes with the Mobalt app, accumulating points with each trip. Upon reaching the target, Banca dello Stato del Canton Ticino, the challenge's sponsor, will materialize the charitable act. ### A virtuous circle The collective challenge model aims to create a virtuous circle, where, in addition to the benefits of promoting carpooling for mobility and the environment, positive social impacts are also generated. Further challenges will be launched in the autumn and will target other public utility entities in the area. Organizations wishing to propose themselves as sponsors of collective challenges can announce themselves at info@momoride.ch to receive more information. ### Prizes and participation With April, the fourth month ends in which the prize pool made available by the promoting municipalities, with a total value of CHF 500, was awarded to the crews of companies based in the three municipalities. Dettagli operativi

MomòRide: the beneficial carpooling challenge for Chiasso, Balerna and Novazzano The MomòRide challenge offers a concrete opportunity for cross-border workers in the municipalities of Chiasso, Balerna and Novazzano to actively contribute to the well-being of the local community. By participating in carpooling, cross-border workers can not only reduce transportation costs and environmental impact but also earn individual rewards and contribute to a charitable cause. ### How to participate To participate in the MomòRide challenge, cross-border workers must download the Mobalt app and register as users. Once registered, they can start tracking their commuting trips by sharing a car. Each tracked journey accumulates points that contribute to the collective goal of 40,000 points in a month. Upon reaching the target, Banca dello Stato del Canton Ticino will donate CHF 1,000 to the Fondazione Provvida Madre di Balerna. 📊 Operational checklist: - Download the Mobalt app - Register as users - Start tracking carpooling trips - Accumulate points to reach the goal ### Benefits for the community The MomòRide challenge not only promotes carpooling as a sustainable means of transport but also helps to create a sense of community among participants. Through car sharing, cross-border workers can meet new colleagues and create networks of mutual support. Additionally, the project offers the opportunity to actively contribute to the well-being of the local community by supporting public utility entities such as the Fondazione Provvida Madre. 💡 Concrete examples: - Cost reduction: A cross-border worker using carpooling can save up to CHF 200 per month on transportation costs. - Environmental impact: Each carpooling trip can reduce CO2 emissions by approximately 50 grams per kilomete...

Concrete actions for cross-border workers To participate in the MomòRide challenge, cross-border workers in Ticino, particularly those residing in Chiasso, Balerna, and Novazzano, should follow these steps: 1. Download the Mobalt app on your smartphone, available for both iOS and Android. 2. Register as app users, providing the required personal data and verifying your account via email. 3. Start tracking your commuting trips by carpooling, specifying the number of passengers and the distance traveled. 4. Accumulate points for each tracked trip. For example, a 20 km trip with 3 passengers can earn up to 10 points. 5. Contribute to reaching the collective goal of 40,000 points in a month. With around 200 active participants, each user should accumulate approximately 200 points to reach the target. ### Deadlines and prizes The MomòRide challenge started on May 1st and will last for a month. Participants can earn individual prizes each month, such as fuel vouchers or discounts on local services. Additionally, they will contribute to a donation of CHF 1,000 to the Provvida Madre Foundation in Balerna, which supports social projects in the municipality. > "Sustainable mobility is a goal we can only achieve together." Organizations interested in sponsoring further challenges can contact info@momoride.ch for more information. For example, a local company could sponsor a challenge for its employees, offering additional prizes such as extra vacation days or meal vouchers. ### Useful tools For more information and to participate in the MomòRide challenge, cross-border workers can visit the official website of the initiative or contact the organization directly via the email address info@momoride.ch. Frequently Asked Questions
How can I participate in the MomòRide challenge?
To participate in the MomòRide challenge, you need to download the Mobalt app, register as a user, and start tracking your commute by carpooling. Each tracked trip earns points that contribute to the collective goal of 40,000 points in a month.
What are the benefits of participating in MomòRide?
By participating in MomòRide, you can reduce transportation costs and environmental impact, earn individual prizes every month, and contribute to a charitable cause. Additionally, you can meet new colleagues and create mutual support networks.
Who can participate in the MomòRide challenge?
The MomòRide challenge is open to all workers in the three municipalities of Balerna, Chiasso, and Novazzano. You can participate as an individual or as a group of colleagues.
