Drug dealing in Busto: 18,617.60 euros seized in cash (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 7 May 2026

The State Police of Busto Arsizio arrested two foreign citizens with 18,617.60 euros in cash and drugs hidden in their home.

In brief - Two arrested for drug dealing in Busto Arsizio - 18,617.60 euros in cash seized - Drugs hidden in the ceiling and in the shutter boxes - Comparison with similar cases in Ticino: seizures in Chiasso and Lugano ## Key facts - What: Drug dealing and resistance to public official - When: May 6, 2026 - Where: Busto Arsizio, Italy - Who: State Police of Busto Arsizio - Amount: 18,617.60 euros in cash - Drugs: 27 doses of cocaine and 92 grams of hashish The State Police of Busto Arsizio arrested two foreign citizens, a 34-year-old and a 28-year-old, responsible for possessing narcotic substances for dealing and resisting a public official. The operation stemmed from a territorial control in the early hours of the morning when a patrol of the Flying Squad of the police station stopped a vehicle with the 34-year-old on board, already known for previous police records. The man, in an attempt to evade the control, tried to hide a bunch of keys inside the passenger compartment, providing false information about his address at the same time. > "We noticed suspicious behaviors and decided to deepen the control," stated an agent of the State Police. Thanks to the checks carried out by the police, it was possible to identify the man's real residence in the municipality of Busto Arsizio, where the agents went to proceed with a house search. During the phases of access to the property, the man put up violent resistance, striking blows with his head, engaging in a scuffle with the agents and deliberately damaging the lock in an attempt to prevent entry, making it necessary to intervene with the Fire Brigade to force the door. Dettagli operativi

The operation carried out by the State Police of Busto Arsizio, in collaboration with Swiss authorities, has uncovered a well-organized drug trafficking operation with a sophisticated system for hiding narcotics. The discovery of 27 doses of cocaine and a block of hashish weighing over 92 grams, hidden in the ceiling and in the roller shutter boxes, demonstrates the complexity of the criminal organization. Additionally, the presence of packaging material and four mobile phones suggests that the drug trafficking activity had been ongoing for some time and that the two arrested individuals had established contacts with other subjects involved in the drug trade. The sum of 18,617.60 euros in cash, seized during the search, represents further evidence of the scale of the illegal activity. This amount, together with the drugs and materials found, confirms that the two arrested individuals were involved in large-scale drug trafficking. The resistance put up during the access to the property and the attempt to dispose of the drug doses by flushing them down the toilet demonstrate the awareness of the two individuals of the seriousness of their actions and the risk of being caught. ### Operational Checklist - Sophisticated concealment: Use of hidden spaces such as ceilings and roller shutter boxes. - Packaging material: Presence of tools for preparing and packaging the doses. - Communication: Four mobile phones used to coordinate illegal activities. - Resistance during access: Attempt to obstruct the police operation. - Attempt to destroy evidence: Throwing drug doses into the toilet. ### Comparison of Practical Scenarios - Busto Arsizio vs. Lugano: In Lugano, in 2022, 500 grams of cocaine were seized, hidden in a car parked in a private garage. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the penalties for drug dealing in Italy and Switzerland?
In Italy, the law provides for penalties from 6 to 20 years of imprisonment and fines of up to 260,000 euros for drug dealing. In Switzerland, the drugs law provides for prison sentences of up to 3 years and fines of up to 10,000 Swiss francs for possession and dealing.
Where was the arrest for drug dealing made and how much money was seized?
The arrest took place in Busto Arsizio, Italy. During the operation, the State Police seized a total of 18,617.60 euros in cash, believed to be proceeds from the sale activity.
How was the drug hidden during the Busto Arsizio operation?
The drugs were hidden in a sophisticated way inside the house. Specifically, 27 doses of cocaine and 92 grams of hashish were found in cavities in the false ceiling and shutter bins, sealed with silicone.
What were the precedents in Ticino similar to this case?
In Ticino, similar cases were registered in Chiasso in 2025, with a drug seizure of 50,000 Swiss francs, and in Lugano in 2024, where a group of drug dealers was arrested with 30,000 Swiss francs and narcotics.
What happened during the check that led to the arrests at Busto Arsizio?
Police noticed a vehicle with a man already known to law enforcement. The man attempted to conceal the keys and provided false home directions. Subsequently, he put up violent resistance during the house search.
