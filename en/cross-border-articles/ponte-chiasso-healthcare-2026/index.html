Ponte Chiasso: blood draws at the parish thanks to a nurse

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 22 April 2026

In Ponte Chiasso, a volunteer nurse offers blood draw services at the local parish, while the Obiettivo Salute Committee calls for a permanent healthcare facility.

Contesto

TL;DR - Blood collection service in Ponte Chiasso parish - Petition for nearby healthcare point in Via Napoleona - 16,000 inhabitants, many elderly, need healthcare - Volunteers can't replace public health facilities ## Key facts - Iniziativa: Servizio di raccolta sangue nella parrocchia di Ponte Chiasso - Responsabile: Infermiere volontario, Signor Crippa - Centro di raccolta: Maslianico, collegato all'Ospedale S. Anna - Popolazione servita: 16.000 abitanti, molti anziani - Petizione: Promossa dal Comitato Obiettivo Salute di Como Nord - Supporto politico: Azione Como - Proposta: Creazione di una sede staccata della Casa di Comunità di Sagnino in via Napoleona - Come sostenere: Firmare la petizione online o alla Farmacia di Tavernola ## The blood collection service at the parish In Ponte Chiasso, a voluntary initiative has established a blood collection service at the local parish. The service was set up thanks to the volunteer work of a nurse, Mr. Crippa, who goes to the parish to perform the blood draws and then transports the samples to the collection center in Maslianico, connected to S. Anna Hospital. This initiative was born in response to the complaints of residents and volunteers about the complete state of 'health' abandonment of the population of Ponte Chiasso, particularly the most vulnerable and fragile individuals, such as lonely people and the elderly. The Comitato Obiettivo Salute di Como Nord has launched a petition to ensure a nearby healthcare point in Via Napoleona, serving the neighborhoods of Monte Olimpino, Sagnino, Tavernola, and Ponte Chiasso. In this area, about 16,000 inhabitants live, a significant part of whom are elderly. Dettagli operativi

The implications for residents and the future of the service The initiative of the parish blood collection service represents an important first step in responding to the health needs of Ponte Chiasso residents. However, as Azione Como has pointed out, volunteering cannot replace a permanent public health facility. The petition promoted by the Comitato Obiettivo Salute is a strong signal of the demand for local healthcare, which can no longer be ignored. ### What happens if the health facility is not established? If a permanent health facility is not established, Ponte Chiasso residents will continue to rely on volunteers for essential services such as blood tests. This could lead to delays and difficulties in accessing care, especially for the elderly and more vulnerable. Additionally, the lack of a public health facility could worsen the sense of abandonment and isolation that many residents already feel. ### How to support the initiative To support the initiative of the Comitato Obiettivo Salute of Como Nord, you can sign the petition online or in paper form at the Farmacia di Tavernola. The petition is a concrete way to make your voice heard and ask institutions to intervene with structured solutions. ### The proposal of Azione Como Azione Como has proposed the creation of a detached seat of the Casa di Comunità di Sagnino in via Napoleona. However, Carlo Bordoni has emphasized that 'a real service is needed for the citizens of neighborhoods a bit abandoned by politics and which need attention, starting from health.'
