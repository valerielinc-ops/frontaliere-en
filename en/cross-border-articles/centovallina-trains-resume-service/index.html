Dettagli operativi

The reopening of the Centovallina between Camedo and Domodossola is an important event for transborder mobility in the region. Trains that will circulate on this route will be subject to rigorous technical controls to guarantee passenger safety. Ferrovie Autolinee Regionali Ticinesi SA has worked to reactivate the service as soon as possible, after the technical checks had a positive outcome. Passengers can therefore return to using this railway connection for their daily journeys or for tourist purposes. The Centovallina is a narrow-gauge railway line that connects Locarno to Domodossola, passing through the Vigezzo Valley and the Ossola Valley. The section in question, approximately 10 kilometers long, connects Camedo, a municipality located in the southernmost part of the Canton of Ticino, to Domodossola, a Piedmontese city located on the border with Switzerland. ## Details on the reopening The reopening of the Centovallina between Camedo and Domodossola was made possible thanks to maintenance and technical verification work carried out by Ferrovie Autolinee Regionali Ticinesi SA. The work began in January 2023 and was successfully completed at the end of June of the same year. - > 3 million Swiss francs: the amount allocated for extraordinary maintenance and technological updating of the section. - 25 km/h: the maximum speed allowed for trains on this section. ## Benefits for the region The reopening of the Centovallina between Camedo and Domodossola offers several benefits for the region: - to facilitate daily journeys of residents between Ticino and Piedmont; - to incentivize tourism in the Vigezzo Valley and the Ossola Valley; - to improve connections between the Ticino municipalities facing the Vigezzo Valley (including Brissago, Ronco Briantino). ## Reg...
