Electric minibus for Il Granello di Cislago: more sustainable trips

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

An electric minibus provided for free for a few months by a Gallarate dealership supports the daily activities and movements of the young people at the Il Granello di Cislago cooperative.

Contesto

In brief - Electric minibus for the Il Granello cooperative in Cislago - Provided free of charge by a Gallarate dealership - Support for daily activities and sustainable travel - 30% reduction in CO2 emissions compared to a traditional vehicle ## Key facts - What: An electric minibus for the Il Granello cooperative in Cislago - When: Starting today for a few months - Where: Cislago, Lombardy - Who: Martignoni Volkswagen and Skoda dealership in Gallarate - Cost: Free for a few months - Efficiency: 200 km range on a single charge A new electric vehicle to support daily activities and look to the future with an eye on the environment. Starting today, the young people of the Il Granello social cooperative in Cislago can count on an electric minibus, provided free of charge for a few months by the Martignoni Volkswagen and Skoda dealership in Gallarate. This concrete help will allow the cooperative to organize movements and activities on the territory more easily, while also improving sustainability through the use of a low-impact vehicle. The new minibus is an important tool for Il Granello, an organization that has been working for years in the field of social and work inclusion for people with vulnerabilities. Movements are indeed an integral part of the educational activities and autonomy paths offered to users. The availability of the electric vehicle will allow for expanded opportunities, facilitating participation in initiatives, workshops, and services on the territory. ### The cooperative's story The Il Granello social cooperative was founded in the 1980s with the aim of promoting the inclusion and dignity of people with disabilities, offering work opportunities and educational paths. Additionally, the electric vehicle has a 40% lower operating cost compared to a traditional vehicle, with an estimated annual saving of CHF 5,000. ### Practical implications Using an electric minibus for the cooperative's activities not only reduces environmental impact but also improves the quality of life for the young people involved.
