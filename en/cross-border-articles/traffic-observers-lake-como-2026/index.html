Traffic wardens on duty Sunday for record Lake Como traffic

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

Extra service Sunday 3 May along the SS 340 Regina to manage the expected tourist influx

Contesto

In brief - Movieri in action on Sunday, May 3rd along the SS 340 Regina - Huge influx of tourists expected during the first weekend of May - Decision made by the President of the Province and the cooperative in charge ## Key facts - What: Extraordinary service by traffic observers - When: Sunday, May 3rd, 2026 - Where: Section of the SS 340 Regina between Colonno and Ossuccio - Who: President of the Province Fiorenzo Bogniasca and the cooperative in charge - Cost: Not specified In an extraordinary measure, the service of traffic observers will also be guaranteed on Sunday, May 3rd along the SS 340 Regina, in the section between Colonno and Ossuccio. This decision was made following an agreement between the President of the Province and the cooperative in charge of the service, considering the favorable weather forecasts and the expected increase in tourist flows during the first weekend of May. The extraordinary extension to Sunday further strengthens the measures implemented for managing traffic along one of the most critical sections of the Regina, which has already seen an increase in traffic in recent weeks. ### President Bogniasca's statements 'We deemed it necessary to take an additional step, ensuring the service is also provided on Sunday, given the forecasted conditions and the high expected influx,' explained President Fiorenzo Bogniasca. 'This is a responsible choice aimed at safeguarding safety and improving traffic management along the Regina, during a particularly delicate time for our territory.' ### Implications for cross-border workers For cross-border workers using the SS 340 Regina for travel between Italy and Switzerland, the extraordinary service by traffic observers represents an additional measure to ensure safety and traffic fluidity. Dettagli operativi

Procedures for cross-border workers For cross-border workers using the SS 340 Regina, it is important to be aware of the measures adopted to manage traffic. Traffic observers will be positioned along the critical stretch between Colonno and Ossuccio, ready to intervene if necessary. It is advisable to plan your movements taking into account the possible presence of slowdowns and any diversions. ### Comparison with other critical areas The SS 340 Regina is not the only artery affected by an increase in traffic. Other roads around Lake Como, such as the SS 340 dir and the SS 583, also record a constant increase in vehicular flow. However, the stretch between Colonno and Ossuccio is considered one of the most critical, especially due to the presence of tight bends and particularly winding sections. ### Implications for tourism The increase in traffic along the SS 340 Regina can have a significant impact on the tourism sector. On the one hand, a greater influx of tourists can represent an opportunity for local businesses. On the other hand, heavy traffic can compromise the quality of the visit, making it more difficult to access the main tourist attractions. ### Measures to improve traffic flow In addition to the extraordinary service of traffic observers, other measures are being evaluated to improve traffic flow along the SS 340 Regina. These include the introduction of real-time traffic monitoring systems and the optimization of traffic lights to reduce waiting times. These measures could help make the road safer and smoother, especially during peak periods. ## Recommended tools For an updated estimate, use the net salary calculator and the CHF-EUR exchange comparator.

What to do in case of heavy traffic In case of heavy traffic along the SS 340 Regina, it is important to stay calm and follow the instructions of traffic observers. It is advisable to avoid using the phone while driving and to maintain a safe distance from the vehicles in front. Additionally, it is important to be ready to modify your route if necessary, using the alternative roads indicated. ### Deadlines and procedures At the moment, no specific deadlines have been communicated for the extraordinary service of traffic observers. However, it is likely that this service will be extended to other weekends, especially during the summer season. To stay updated on the latest news, it is advisable to regularly consult the websites of local authorities and the cooperatives in charge of the service. ### Useful tools for cross-border workers To plan their movements as best as possible, cross-border workers can use tools such as the traffic calculator and the route comparator. These tools can help identify the least congested roads and plan your trip more efficiently. ### Conclusion The extraordinary service of traffic observers along the SS 340 Regina represents an important step to ensure the safety and fluidity of traffic in a period of increasing tourist influx. For cross-border workers, it is fundamental to be aware of the measures adopted and to plan their movements adequately. By using the available tools and following the instructions of the authorities, it is possible to face heavy traffic in a safe and efficient manner. Source: comozero.it
