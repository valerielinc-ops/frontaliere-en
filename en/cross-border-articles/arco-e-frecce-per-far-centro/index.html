Contesto

In brief - A new archery society has been founded in Bellinzona. - President Rita De Franco announced that the society hopes to find a permanent field. - The society's presentation is scheduled for Sunday, May 3rd. ## Key facts - What: Foundation of a new archery society. - When: April 30, 2026 - Where: Bellinzona - Who: Rita De Franco - Amount: Not specified President Rita De Franco announced the birth of a new archery society in Bellinzona. The society, named 'Arco e Frecce', aims to hit the bullseye in the hearts of archery enthusiasts. The presentation of the new society is scheduled for Sunday, May 3rd, with President Rita De Franco stating that the society hopes to find a permanent field for its activities. The foundation of this new society represents a significant step forward for the world of archery in Bellinzona and the Canton of Ticino. ### Context and additional details The foundation of 'Arco e Frecce' is a significant event for the world of archery in Bellinzona and the Canton of Ticino. The city of Bellinzona, with its rich history of sports and cultural events, is an ideal location for a society of this kind. The city already hosts various sports competitions and events, such as the Singing Festival and the Corsa di San Giovanni, which attract thousands of spectators every year. The society 'Arco e Frecce' aims to create a hub for archery enthusiasts, offering training, competitions, and special events. President Rita De Franco emphasized that finding a permanent field is a priority for the society, as a stable field is essential to ensure the quality and safety of practical activities. 📊 To find a permanent field, the society is considering various options in Bellinzona and nearby municipalities, such as Locarno and Lugano. 📍 ### Operational C...
