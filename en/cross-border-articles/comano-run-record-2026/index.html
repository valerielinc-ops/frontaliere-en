Comano Run 2026: record participants and new route (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 5 May 2026

Over 800 participants in the sixth edition of the Comano Run, with new records in the route and youth categories.

Contesto

In brief - 801 participants in the Comano Run 2026 - New record in the men's 7 km race - High turnout in youth categories and walking ## Key facts - What: Sixth edition of the Comano Run - When: May 4, 2026 - Where: Comano, Canton Ticino - Who: Organized by BancaStato - Number: 801 participants The sixth edition of the Comano Run set new records both in terms of participant numbers and sporting performances. With 801 registrations, the event saw a high turnout across all categories, particularly among young people and in walking and nordic walking disciplines. The main race, valid for the ASTi BancaStato Athletics Cup and the ASTi BancaStato Youth Cup, saw Tommaso Marani and Francesca Sabena emerge victorious. Tommaso Marani, of SAL Lugano, won the M20 category with a time of 23 minutes and 30 seconds, setting a new record for the 7-kilometer course. Behind him, Andrea Alagona of Triathlon Team Ticino and Evan Dingle of CPTC – Tracksmith completed the podium. In the women's category, Francesca Sabena of OCR Ticino dominated the F20 category with a time of 29 minutes and 48 seconds, followed by Cristina Maggetti and Sara Solari. The sunny day favored mass participation, with a large crowd contributing to the event's success. Among the special guests, HC Lugano players Ramon Tanner and Joren Van Pottelberghe met fans for autographs and photos. Complete rankings are available on the site www.comanorun.ch. ### Implications for cross-border workers The Comano Run represents an opportunity for cross-border workers to participate in high-level sporting events in Canton Ticino.
