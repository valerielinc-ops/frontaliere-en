Frequently Asked Questions
What happens if I don't pay my health insurance premiums on time?
If you don't pay your health insurance premiums on time, the health insurance company can send a reminder letter and, in case of non-payment, a second notice with a fine or late interest. If the debt is not settled, you might be included in the list of defaulters.
How can I avoid being put on the defaulters' list?
To avoid being put on the defaulters' list, it is crucial to pay your health insurance premiums on time. If you encounter difficulties, immediately contact the health insurance company to explain the situation and find a solution.
What should I do if I receive a reminder letter from the health insurance company?
If you receive a reminder letter from the health insurance company, it is important to act promptly. Verify the debt and, if necessary, contact the health insurance company to regularize your position. You can pay the premium on time or agree on an installment payment plan.
