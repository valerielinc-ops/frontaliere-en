Varese Solidale: conference on health and food poverty (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 6 May 2026

Varese Solidale presents the conference on health and food poverty on May 15, 2026. Experts and institutions discuss strategies and tools to address the phenomenon.

Contesto

In brief - Conference on health and food poverty in Varese on May 15, 2026 - Presentation of tools and strategies to address the phenomenon - Thematic exhibition and solidarity dinner on May 16 ## Key facts - What: Conference on health and food poverty - When: May 15, 2026, 6:00 PM - Where: Conference Hall of Villa Recalcati, Varese - Who: Experts, healthcare operators, representatives of institutions and the third sector - Cost: Not specified The conference 'Health Poverty: The Right to Care' will be held on Friday, May 15 at 6:00 PM in the Conference Hall of Villa Recalcati in Varese. The event, promoted by Varese Solidale, will feature the participation of experts, healthcare operators, representatives of institutions and the third sector. The speeches will offer a concrete analysis of the phenomenon of health and food poverty, proposing tools and strategies to address it in a coordinated and effective manner. Thematic exhibition panels will be presented, illustrating data, experiences and best practices related to health and food poverty. The exhibition will accompany the proceedings, offering participants an additional tool for in-depth analysis and awareness. The initiative is part of the broader program of Varese Solidale, which will continue on Saturday, May 16 with a day dedicated to citizen participation, between moments of meeting, awareness and sharing. ### The day of May 16 The day of May 16 will be dedicated to citizen participation, with moments of meeting, awareness and sharing. The day will conclude with the solidarity dinner, scheduled for 8:00 PM at the Church of Brunella. Participation in the dinner is by registration at the Evenbrite link. Dettagli operativi

Analysis of the presented strategies During the conference, various strategies to tackle health and food poverty will be presented. Among these, the adoption of integrated healthcare models, which combine medical, social, and psychological services, could offer more effective solutions for people in need. Furthermore, promoting nutritional education programs and distributing nutritious meals could contribute to improving access to adequate nutrition. ### The role of institutions Local and national institutions play a crucial role in combating health and food poverty. Through collaboration with the third sector and healthcare providers, it is possible to develop policies and programs aimed at supporting people in difficulty. During the conference, representatives of the institutions will discuss the ongoing initiatives and future strategies to address the phenomenon. ### The importance of awareness Raising awareness among citizens is fundamental to promoting awareness and support for initiatives against health and food poverty. Through moments of meeting, awareness, and sharing, it is possible to actively involve the community and promote a sense of solidarity and social responsibility. ### The exhibition The thematic exhibition represents an additional tool for in-depth analysis and awareness-raising. Through exhibition panels that illustrate data, experiences, and best practices, participants will be able to better understand the phenomenon of health and food poverty and the strategies to address it. What to do to participate To participate in the conference on health and food poverty, you need to follow a few simple steps. First, it is advisable to register for the solidarity dinner through the Eventbrite link, available on the Varese Solidale website. Additionally, it is useful to consult the detailed program of the event to know the schedules and speakers. ### Where to go The conference will take place in the Conference Hall of Villa Recalcati in Varese. The location is easily reachable by public transport or by car. For those coming from Switzerland, it is possible to use the Brogeda or Gaggiolo border crossings to reach Varese. ### Required documents To participate in the conference and the solidarity dinner, no specific documents are required. However, it is advisable to bring an identity document and, if necessary, the confirmation of registration for the solidarity dinner. ### Deadlines Participation in the solidarity dinner is by registration via the Eventbrite link. It is important to register by the indicated date to ensure the availability of seats. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the goal of the conference on health and food poverty?
The goal of the conference is to shed light on an increasingly widespread form of vulnerability that limits access to healthcare and adequate nutrition. Through a concrete analysis of the phenomenon and the presentation of effective tools and strategies, the event aims to strengthen the dialogue between institutions, operators, and citizens, promoting the quality of life and community cohesion.
How can I participate in the solidarity dinner?
To participate in the solidarity dinner, you need to register via the Evenbrite link available on the Varese Solidale website. The dinner will take place at the Chiesa della Brunella and represents a convivial occasion that also translates into a concrete gesture of support for the solidarity activities of the territory.
What are the implications for cross-border workers who work in Switzerland and reside in Italy?
For cross-border workers, participation in the conference and solidarity dinner represents an opportunity to better understand the challenges related to health and food poverty and to actively contribute to the well-being of the community. The strategies and tools presented during the event could offer practical solutions to address these issues, improving the quality of life and social cohesion.
