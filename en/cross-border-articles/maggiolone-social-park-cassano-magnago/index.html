Maggiolone Social Park: a month of parties in Cassano Magnago

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

From May 1st, Cassano Magnago hosts the new Maggiolone Social Park with free events for all ages.

Contesto

In brief - New event space in Cassano Magnago - Free events from May to September - Over 100 concerts on schedule ## Key facts - What: Maggiolone Social Park - When: 1st May - 30th September 2026 - Where: Feste Area, via Primo Maggio, Cassano Magnago - Who: Le Officine Association - Cost: Free entry Cassano Magnago is preparing to welcome a new season of outdoor events with the launch of the Maggiolone Social Park, a new multifunctional space within the Feste Area on via Primo Maggio. The project, presented at a press conference by councillor Alessandro Passuello and the Le Officine association, aims to be a social and cultural hub for the entire area. "Maggiolone because it evokes the month of May, Maggiolone because the tent structure resembles a beetle. Maggiolone because it smells of summer, of carefreeness," declared Matia Campanoni of the Le Officine association. The Maggiolone Social Park will host fairs, festivals, markets, concerts, and outdoor activities every weekend until the end of September, with free and accessible events for all, from children to the elderly. Among the main attractions, a large stage for live music with over a hundred concerts scheduled, areas dedicated to relaxation, sports, and games, free inflatables for the little ones, creative spaces, trampolines, and sports activities such as table tennis, five-a-side football, shooting, and much more. The official debut is set for the weekend of 1st, 2nd, and 3rd May, with a grand opening party open to all and free entry. Three days of music, shows, activities, and good food to present to the community the new face of the Feste Area. ### Inaugural weekend programme The gastronomic part will be led by the Costina Fair, an unmissable event for all lovers of convivial cuisine and intense outd...

Dettagli operativi

The Maggiolone Social Park represents a unique opportunity for cross-border workers who commute to Switzerland and reside in Italy, offering a place for leisure and entertainment just a few kilometers from the border. Its proximity to the Canton of Ticino makes this space easily accessible for those who wish to spend their free time in a lively and inclusive environment. ### Implications for cross-border workers For cross-border workers residing in the Cassano Magnago area and surroundings, the Maggiolone Social Park offers a valid alternative for leisure time, with free events accessible to everyone. The possibility of attending concerts, sports activities, and cultural events without having to cross the Swiss border represents a significant advantage. ### Comparison with other local initiatives Compared to other similar initiatives in the area, the Maggiolone Social Park stands out for its continuous and varied programming, ranging from live music to sports activities, passing through gastronomic events and moments of relaxation. The presence of a large stage and areas dedicated to different age groups makes it a unique point of reference. ### Future scenarios The project, destined to grow in the coming years, maintains a strong connection to the territory and conviviality. The goal is to create a lively, inclusive, and participatory place that can become a point of reference not only for Cassano Magnago but for the entire surrounding area. The collaboration between the municipality and the Le Officine association represents a virtuous model of synergy between public and private entities for the promotion of the territory. ## Recommended tools For an updated estimate, use the net salary calculator and the CHF-EUR exchange comparator.

To participate in the Maggiolone Social Park events, no special permit or particular document is required. Entry is free and open to everyone. For activities that require booking, such as the Costina Festival, you can book online through the link provided on the official event website. ### Booking Procedure 1. Access the official Maggiolone Social Park website. 2. Select the event you wish to book. 3. Fill out the booking form with your details. 4. Confirm the booking and wait for the email confirmation. ### What to Bring To participate in the events, it is advisable to bring: - Identification document - Cash or credit card for any purchases - Comfortable clothing suitable for the planned activities - Sunscreen and hat for outdoor events ### Useful Tools To best plan your participation in the events, you can use the event calculator available on the Frontaliere Ticino website. This tool allows you to view the event dates and organize your free time effectively. ### Conclusion The Maggiolone Social Park represents a unique opportunity to spend moments of leisure and entertainment in a lively and inclusive environment. For cross-border workers who work in Switzerland and reside in Italy, this space offers a valid alternative for free time, with free and accessible events for everyone. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the program for the inaugural weekend of Maggiolone Social Park?
The inaugural weekend of Maggiolone Social Park will take place from May 1st to 3rd. On Friday, May 1st, the event will open at 12:00 PM with kitchens, play area, and activities for all ages. On Saturday, May 2nd, the opening will be at 6:00 PM with the Bisboccia Fest preview. On Sunday, May 3rd, there will be a double appointment: opening at 12:00 PM with a juggling show and evening reopening at 6:00 PM with the live performance by Luciano and Paola dedicated to liscio music.
Is a special permit required to participate in the events of Maggiolone Social Park?
No, no special permit or particular document is required to participate in the events of Maggiolone Social Park. Entry is free and open to everyone.
How can I book for the Sagra della Costina?
To book for the Sagra della Costina, you can access the official website of Maggiolone Social Park, select the event, fill out the booking form with your details, and confirm the booking. The confirmation will be sent via email.
