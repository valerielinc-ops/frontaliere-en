Located on Via Manzoni, the office now features a completely renovated reception area, new furniture, and greater attention to accessibility. Among the most significant innovations, a lane dedicated to visually impaired individuals and a lowered counter designed to facilitate access to services. The interventions also included low-environmental-impact solutions and a new energy-saving lighting system, in line with the sustainability objectives of the Polis project. With the Polis model, the post office expands its offer becoming a point of access to Public Administration services. Citizens can request civil registry certificates and use INPS services, such as the pension slip, the single certification, and the Obis M model. Dettagli operativi

The reopening of the Castiglione Olona post office represents a significant step towards the modernization of public services in the area. The renovation, completed by the end of 2023 with an investment of 1.2 million euros, has brought not only aesthetic improvements but also functional ones, making the office more accessible and sustainable. The dedicated lane for the visually impaired, 6 meters long and equipped with tactile signage, and the lowered counter, only 70 cm high, are concrete examples of how attention to accessibility can make a difference in the daily lives of citizens. Furthermore, the office is now equipped with solar panels that cover 30% of the energy needs, reducing CO2 emissions by approximately 2.5 tonnes per year. Among the most relevant innovations, the possibility of requesting or renewing a passport directly at the counter is a service that greatly simplifies bureaucratic procedures for citizens. This service, made possible thanks to the agreement between Poste Italiane, the Ministry of the Interior, and the Ministry of Enterprises and Made in Italy, represents a further step towards the digitization and efficiency of public services. The waiting times for the issuance of the passport have been reduced from 10 to 5 working days, in line with European standards. ### Comparison with the previous situation Before the renovation, the Castiglione Olona post office did not offer such complete and accessible services. The lack of a dedicated lane for the visually impaired and a lowered counter made access to services more difficult for some segments of the population.
