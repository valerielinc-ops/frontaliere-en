The Income Support (IS) is a financial aid measure introduced in 2018 to combat poverty and promote social integration of the most vulnerable citizens. The payment of IS is made through a debit card, the Inclusion Card, which can be used to withdraw cash and make purchases at affiliated commercial establishments. The processing of the ordinary recharge of the Income Support is expected to start on Monday, May 25, 2026, although as always, INPS timelines may vary from case to case. In recent months, the Institute has managed the processing progressively, distributing the cases over several days. This means that some beneficiaries may see the processing on the first available day, while others may only see it later. The official INPS date for the end-of-month ordinary recharge remains Wednesday, May 27, 2026. However, as has happened in the past, credits may start as early as the previous day, Tuesday, May 26, for at least some beneficiaries. For this reason, many users await the start of processing on May 25, which usually anticipates the payment on the Inclusion Card by about 24 hours. For beneficiaries residing in Ticino, the recharge of the Income Support can be made at the INPS offices in Bellinzona, Lugano and Mendrisio. It is important to remember that to withdraw the money on the Inclusion Card, a valid identity document and the card itself are required. Moreover, cash can only be withdrawn at automated teller machines affiliated with the PostePay circuit, such as those present in the branches of the Cantonal Bank of Ticino or Raiffeisen Switzerland. Frequently Asked Questions
How do I know if my recharge has been completed?
You can check the reserved area dedicated to the Integration Allowance on the INPS website or through the INPS Mobile app. If the operations have been completed, you should see the credit on your Integration Card.
What happens if I have problems with my Integration Card?
If you have problems with the Integration Card, we recommend contacting the INPS customer service or going to an INPS office to resolve the problem.
How do I know if I am eligible for the Integration Allowance?
To know if you are eligible for the Integration Allowance, you need to check the requirements provided by the law and submit your application to INPS. We recommend consulting the INPS website or going to an INPS office for further information.
