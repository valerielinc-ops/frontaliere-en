BFS data shows 78,561 cross-border workers in Ticino, a -0.16% change from the previous quarter.

For example, a cross-border worker employed in Lugano must register with the AVS Office of the Canton of Ticino within 30 days of starting work. AVS contributions are calculated at 10.25% of the salary, with a maximum of CHF 28,710 per year for 2023. 2. Tax declaration: File a tax declaration in Italy and Switzerland to avoid double taxation. Health insurance: Choose a health insurance compliant with LAMal (Health Insurance Law) to cover medical expenses in Switzerland.
