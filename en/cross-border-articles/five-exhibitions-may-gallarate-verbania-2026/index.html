From Gallarate to Verbania, here are the most anticipated exhibitions of May 2026. Discover dates, venues, and details.

In brief - Five exhibitions open in May in Ticino and surroundings - Exhibitions in Gallarate, Sesto Calende, Como and Verbania - Exhibitions from May 16 to November 29, 2026 ## Key facts - What: Art exhibitions - When: From May 16 to November 29, 2026 - Where: Gallarate, Sesto Calende, Como, Verbania - Who: Fondazione Giancarlo Sangregorio, Comune di Verbania, Museo Maga - Amount: Not yet specified In May 2026, Ticino and its surroundings are set to welcome a series of art exhibitions that promise to capture the attention of enthusiasts. Between Gallarate, Sesto Calende, Como, and Verbania, there will be several exhibitions ranging from the second half of the 20th century to the most iconic works of contemporary artists. In Sesto Calende, the Fondazione Giancarlo Sangregorio will inaugurate on May 16, 2026, at 11:30 AM, the Spazio Luce, a new exhibition area born from the restoration of an old rural building thanks to a Fondazione Cariplo tender. The inaugural exhibition, curated by Lorella Giudici, is titled 'Incontri. Da Fontana a Baj, da Rotella a Mondino. Una collezione svelata' and will be visible until November 29, 2026. The review will present 34 works from the Sangregorio collection, including works by Lucio Fontana, Enrico Baj, Mimmo Rotella, and Aldo Mondino. In Verbania, Villa Giulia will host from May 16 to September 27, 2026, a rich anthology dedicated to Alessandro Mendini. The exhibition, curated by Loredana Parmesani, will present 130 works that trace the artist's career, covering a chronological span from the 1970s to postmodern theories. The exhibition is organized by the Comune di Verbania in collaboration with the Archivio Alessandro Mendini. In Gallarate, the Museo Maga will host two exhibitions from May 23 to October 11, 2026. What are the dates for the May 2026 exhibitions?
The May 2026 exhibitions will be held from May 16 to November 29, 2026. Specific exhibitions include 'Incontri. Da Fontana a Baj, da Rotella a Mondino. Where will the May 2026 exhibitions be held?
The May 2026 exhibitions will be held in various locations, including Sesto Calende, Verbania, and Gallarate. The exhibition 'Incontri. Da Fontana a Baj, da Rotella a Mondino. Una collezione svelata' will be held in Sesto Calende, the exhibition dedicated to Alessandro Mendini will be held in Verbania, and two exhibitions will be held at the Museo Maga in Gallarate.
