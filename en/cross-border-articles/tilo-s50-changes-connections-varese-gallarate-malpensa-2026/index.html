TILO S50: bus replacement for stops between Varese and Malpensa on 11–12 April (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 8 April 2026

On Saturday 11 and Sunday 12 April 2026, TILO S50 services between Varese, Gallarate and Malpensa Airport will be replaced by buses. Travellers should expect longer journey times, cancellations and earlier departures for the replacement buses.

Frequently Asked Questions
What are the exact dates when the TILO S50 services between Varese and Malpensa change?
The changes take effect on Saturday 11 April 2026 and Sunday 12 April 2026. On Saturday evening, two late-night services are cancelled, while on Sunday all trains are replaced by direct buses with no intermediate stops.
I have a flight from Malpensa on Sunday morning. How can I plan my journey from Mendrisio?
On Sunday 12 April the only option is the direct replacement bus from Varese to Malpensa. The first bus departs at 05:00 from Varese and arrives at Malpensa T2 at 05:45. We recommend leaving Mendrisio no later than 04:30 to reach Varese in time.
Can I request a refund if I miss a service due to the works?
Yes, you can request a partial refund by keeping the replacement bus receipt and submitting a request through TILO online form. Specify the number of the cancelled service and the reason for the disruption.
Are there alternatives to the TILO S50 to reach Malpensa during these days?
You can consider the regional train from Lugano to Milano Centrale and then the Malpensa Express. Alternatively, rent a car or book a taxi via apps like Uber or Taxi Mendrisio.
When will the TILO S50 line return to normal service?
The infrastructure works are temporary. The railway service will return to normal on Monday 13 April 2026. TILO has not provided further details on the duration of the works.
