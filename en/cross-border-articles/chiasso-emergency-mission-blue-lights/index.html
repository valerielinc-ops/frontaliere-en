Dettagli operativi

The Emergency Mission in Chiasso is scheduled for April 26, 2026. This date was chosen to take advantage of favorable weather conditions, including mild temperatures and a low probability of precipitation, ensuring maximum safety for participants. The ten first response entities involved will be selected based on their specific skills and ability to intervene in emergency situations. Among these entities, there will be police officers, firefighters, medical personnel, and specialized technicians in various fields, such as risk management and civil security. The day will be structured into different sessions, each dedicated to a specific aspect of emergency operations. Agents and operators can participate in practical simulations, which include scenarios such as flood interventions, forest fires, and health emergencies. For example, during a flood simulation, entities might have to manage the distribution of first aid kits to 500 residents of the municipality of Biasca, which was recently hit by severe floods. The simulation will include the distribution of first aid kits, traffic management, and coordination with local law enforcement. Agents and operators can also receive updates on the latest technologies and procedures, such as the use of drones to monitor at-risk areas and the adoption of emergency management software. There will also be moments of reflection and experience sharing among the different entities involved, which can discuss intervention strategies with sector experts. For example, an expert from the Civil Protection of the Canton Ticino might share lessons learned from the 2021 flood management, which affected numerous Ticino municipalities, including Lugano and Mendrisio. Chiasso in Emergency Mission: first response entities in contact with the population The Emergency Mission in Chiasso offers a concrete opportunity to strengthen the preparation and efficiency of first response entities in the Canton Ticino. This event, organized with the support of the Municipality of Chiasso, will be crucial for testing operational capabilities, improving service coordination, and raising public awareness on how to handle emergencies. The day, to be held at the Ovale Center Plaza in Chiasso, will feature interactive activities and practical demonstrations. ### Objectives and main activities The event focuses on real emergency simulations, including interventions in case of fires, road rescues, and evacuations. For example, during an exercise conducted last year in Bellinzona, the Fire Department took 12 minutes to extinguish a simulated car fire, highlighting the speed required in critical situations. Similar activities will be repeated in Chiasso, with the addition of first aid simulations organized by the Red Cross of Lugano. Police officers will demonstrate their skills in managing public order situations and communicating with citizens during crises. Operators from the Mendrisiotto Civil Protection will also illustrate procedures for evacuating public buildings, with an operational checklist that includes: - Identification of emergency exits - Coordination of evacuation groups - Timely communication with competent authorities ### Participation and practical details Registration is mandatory and can be done by contacting the Municipality of Chiasso at 091 970 81 11.
