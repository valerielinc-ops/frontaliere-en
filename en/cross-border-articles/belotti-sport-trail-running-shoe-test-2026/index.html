Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need a specific level of preparation to participate in the test?
No, the test is open to everyone, from beginners to experts. The important thing is to wear comfortable sports clothing and bring a bottle of water with you.
Can I buy the shoes I tried during the event?
Yes, at the end of the event there will be the possibility to buy the shoes you tried. The experts at Belotti Sport will be available to advise you on the best choice.
How can I reach Parco Parsifal del Monte Verità?
Parco Parsifal del Monte Verità is easily accessible by car or public transport. For those arriving by car, there are parking spaces available nearby. For those who prefer public transport, you can take a bus or train to Ascona and then walk to the park.
