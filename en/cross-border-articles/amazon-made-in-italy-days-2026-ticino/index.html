Frequently Asked Questions
How can I register my company to participate in the Made in Italy Days?
To register your company, access the Amazon website and follow the instructions to become a seller. Once registered, search for the Made in Italy program and follow the instructions to join. You may need to provide additional information about your company and the products you wish to sell.
What are the costs associated with participating in the Made in Italy Days?
Costs vary depending on the sales plan chosen on Amazon. You can choose from different plans, depending on your needs. Additionally, you might need to invest in marketing and logistics to promote your products during the event.
What are the requirements to participate in the Made in Italy Days?
Requirements include registration as a seller on Amazon and joining the Made in Italy program. You need to provide accurate information about your company and the products you wish to sell. Additionally, it's important to comply with local regulations of the destination countries, especially regarding taxes and customs.
