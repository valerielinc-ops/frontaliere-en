From May 14 to 17, the Canton of Ticino will host the Alge Alp Sport football tournament, an international event dedicated to youth sports and inclusion. The event will take place in Bellinzona, Tenero, and Gambarogno, involving 120 young athletes from Alpine regions. The initiative promotes sports and cultural exchange between territories that share traditions, values, and a passion for sports. The tournament will be divided into three categories: U15 male, U15 female, and an inclusive tournament for people with and without disabilities. This event aims to highlight sports as a tool for meeting, integration, and personal growth. Alongside the sports competitions, the event will offer moments of sharing among the participating delegations, fostering the spirit of friendship and collaboration that has always characterized the Arge Alp project. The playing fields distributed between Bellinzona, Tenero, and Gambarogno will host matches and activities during four days of sports and celebration, with the participation of teams from various Alpine regions....
