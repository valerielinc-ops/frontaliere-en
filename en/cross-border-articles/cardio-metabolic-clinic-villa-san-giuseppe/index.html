Villa San Giuseppe KOS: new cardio-metabolic clinic

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

In Anzano del Parco, at the Villa San Giuseppe KOS Clinic, a new outpatient clinic dedicated to cardiovascular and metabolic health is now active.

This structured and continuous clinical pathway is aimed at those who wish to address issues such as overweight, high blood pressure, glycemic alterations, and sedentary lifestyle in a conscious and personalized manner. This integrated approach aims to provide comprehensive and personalized support for each patient. ### Clinical pathway The pathway develops month by month. The initial phase includes a cardiological visit with electrocardiogram and color Doppler echocardiogram, nutritional counseling, and bioimpedance analysis, followed by the start of the personalized therapeutic plan. This service offers an integrated and personalized approach to managing cardiovascular and metabolic health, which can be particularly useful for those living a hectic and stressful lifestyle. ### Advantages for cross-border workers Cross-border workers can benefit from a structured clinical pathway that includes cardiological assessment, metabolic analysis and body composition, personalized nutritional plan, and constant follow-up. This multidisciplinary approach can help better manage weight and cardiometabolic risk factors, thus improving quality of life and reducing the risk of chronic diseases. ### Comparison with the previous situation Before the activation of this service, cross-border workers often had to turn to different healthcare facilities to obtain a cardiological assessment and a personalized nutritional plan. With the cardio-metabolic outpatient clinic, everything is available in one place, making the process more convenient and accessible. Furthermore, constant follow-up ensures continuous health monitoring, which is fundamental for the prevention and management of chronic diseases. ### Practical scenarios Imagine a cross-border worker who works in Lugano and lives in Como. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can access the new cardio-metabolic outpatient clinic?
The new cardio-metabolic outpatient clinic at Clinica Villa San Giuseppe KOS is for anyone who wants to improve their weight with clinical support, manage cardiometabolic risk factors, work on their nutrition continuously, and be followed by a specialized healthcare team. The service is open to everyone, including cross-border workers who work in Ticino and live in the Como, Lecco, and Merate areas.
What are the advantages of a multidisciplinary approach?
A multidisciplinary approach offers comprehensive and personalized support for each patient. It includes cardiological evaluation, metabolic and body composition analysis, personalized nutritional plan, ongoing nutritional education with constant follow-up, psychological counseling, physiotherapy, and pharmacological support. This integrated approach aims to provide comprehensive and personalized support for each patient.
How can I book the first visit?
To book the first visit, you can use the [health calculator](nav:health) available on the Frontaliere Ticino website or call the number 031 6340534. The booking can be made online or by phone.
