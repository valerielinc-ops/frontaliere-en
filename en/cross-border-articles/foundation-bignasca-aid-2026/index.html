Bignasca Foundation: over 20,000 francs for those in need (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 6 May 2026

The Giuliano Bignasca Foundation has granted over 20,000 francs in 2025 to support families and individuals in financial difficulty in the Canton of Ticino.

Contesto

In brief - Over 20,000 francs disbursed in 2025 - 9,600 francs in shopping vouchers - 10,860 francs for rent and health insurance premiums ## Key facts - What: Financial aid for people in need - When: 2025 - Where: Canton Ticino - Who: Giuliano Bignasca Foundation - Amount: Over 20,000 francs The Giuliano Bignasca Foundation has renewed its commitment to supporting people and families in need in Canton Ticino. Throughout 2025, the organization disbursed a total of over 20,000 francs in direct aid. Specifically, 9,600 francs were allocated for shopping vouchers for families and individuals in need, while 10,860 francs covered essential bills, rent, and health insurance premiums. The Foundation primarily operates in the areas of sustenance, housing payment, support for education costs, and professional integration assistance, with particular attention to elderly individuals facing economic difficulties. ### Listening and support activities In addition to financial support, the Foundation has carried out extensive listening and support activities: during the year, over 60 personal meetings were held with users and families, with the aim of understanding individual situations in detail and providing rapid, concrete, and targeted support. The Giuliano Bignasca Foundation expressed heartfelt thanks to donors, volunteers, curators, and all those who, with their support and trust, made the work carried out throughout the year possible. ### Collaboration with TicinoSociale In 2025, a new collaboration was launched with the association TicinoSociale, which further strengthened support for people in need through territorial synergy and the association's direct contribution to the disbursement of some financial aid. Analysis of Economic Needs The economic needs of families and individuals in difficulty in the Canton of Ticino are manifold and varied. The Giuliano Bignasca Foundation has identified several key areas where financial aid can make a difference. Among these, daily sustenance, housing payments, support for education costs, and professional integration support are particularly important. Elderly individuals in economic difficulty represent a particularly vulnerable group that requires specific attention. ### Impact of Financial Aid The financial aid provided by the Giuliano Bignasca Foundation has a significant impact on the lives of people in difficulty. For example, food vouchers allow the purchase of essential goods such as food, clothing, and household products, contributing to an improved quality of life. Support for rent and health insurance premiums is equally crucial, as it ensures that people can maintain a roof over their heads and access necessary medical care. ### Collaboration with Other Organizations Collaboration with the TicinoSociale association has enabled the Giuliano Bignasca Foundation to expand its support network and reach a greater number of people in need. This territorial synergy is fundamental to ensuring that financial aid reaches those who need it most. The TicinoSociale association directly contributes to the provision of some aid, increasing the effectiveness and efficiency of the support offered. ### Success Stories Throughout 2025, the Giuliano Bignasca Foundation has received numerous success stories from individuals who have benefited from its aid. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can apply for assistance from the Giuliano Bignasca Foundation?
Anyone and families experiencing financial hardship in the Canton of Ticino can apply for assistance from the Giuliano Bignasca Foundation. The Foundation evaluates each request on a case-by-case basis, providing personalized and targeted support.
What documents are required to submit an assistance request?
To submit an assistance request to the Giuliano Bignasca Foundation, you need to provide a valid ID, documents proving financial hardship such as bills, rental contracts, and medical receipts, and information about your current income.
How long does it take to receive a response from the Giuliano Bignasca Foundation?
The time it takes to receive a response from the Giuliano Bignasca Foundation can vary depending on the complexity of the situation. However, the Foundation is committed to evaluating requests as quickly as possible.
