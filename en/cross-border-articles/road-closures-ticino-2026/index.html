Frequently Asked Questions
What are the recommended alternative routes for those traveling to Milan?
For those traveling to Milan, the mandatory exit is at the Solbiate Arno interchange. From here, it is advisable to continue on the SS341 towards Gallarate, take the SS33 of the Simplon and the SS336 of Malpensa, and then re-enter the highway at Busto Arsizio.
What are the alternatives for those coming from Varese?
For those coming from Varese, access to the Fiera Milano Ring Road (R37) towards Rho and Monza will be prohibited. The suggested alternative route involves exiting at Cascina Merlata and passing through the ordinary roads towards Pero and the SS33 of the Simplon.
What are the planned closures on the Gallarate-Gattico Branch (D08)?
On the D08, the work will mainly concern the maintenance of tree species, with total closures of the section between Castelletto Ticino and Besnate. On the night between April 28 and 29, the direction towards A8 Milan-Varese will be closed, while on the night between April 29 and 30, the section towards the A26 will be closed.
