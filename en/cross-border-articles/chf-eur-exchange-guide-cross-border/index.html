Frequently Asked Questions
How can I avoid paying high fees for currency exchange?
Choose an exchange service with a low spread and fixed commissions of less than 5 CHF per transaction. Some examples are Wise, Revolut, and Neon.
What is the best time of year to change your salary to euros considering the stability of the CHF-EUR exchange rate?
The months of January-February and June-July tend to offer more stable rates due to decisions by the Swiss Central Bank (SNB) and lower pre-holiday volatility.
What are the tax differences between the CHF-EUR exchange rate during the year and the average annual rate of the Revenue Agency for tax returns?
The Italian Revenue Agency uses a fixed average annual rate (e.g. 1 CHF = 1.0540 EUR in 2025) to convert Swiss income, which may not coincide with the actual rate used to change salary. This generates a difference between declared and received income, influencing IRPEF.
Can I use a SEPA transfer in CHF to avoid exchange fees?
Yes, but some Italian banks charge reception fees (10-15 EUR) for foreign transfers in CHF. It is advisable to check with your bank before proceeding, especially for amounts over CHF 10,000.
Which exchange services allow you to convert your salary without fees up to CHF 2,000 per month?
Neon, a Swiss digital bank, offers commission-free exchange up to CHF 2,000 per month. It is ideal for those with moderate salaries and want to maximize savings on foreign exchange transactions.
