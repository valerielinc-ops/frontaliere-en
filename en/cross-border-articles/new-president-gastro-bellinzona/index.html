Change at the top of Gastro Bellinzona and Alto Ticino: Andrea Giuliani replaces Luca Merlo. Concerns for the current season.

Contesto

In brief - Andrea Giuliani new president of Gastro Bellinzona and Alto Ticino - Luca Merlo steps down after years of service - Concerns over strong franc and Middle East war ## Key facts - What: Change at the top of Gastro Bellinzona and Alto Ticino - When: 27 April 2026 - Where: Airolo - Who: Andrea Giuliani, Luca Merlo, Stefano Bernasconi, Salvatore Squillante, Massimo Gandola, Davide Della Vecchia, Giuseppe Luciano, Mattia Manzocchi - Amount: Not yet specified Andrea Giuliani of Trattoria Er Pipa in Monte Carasso was appointed the new president of Gastro Bellinzona and Alto Ticino during the association's assembly held on Monday, 27 April, in Airolo. Giuliani succeeds Luca Merlo, who has stepped down after many years of service. During the assembly, the section expressed heartfelt thanks to Merlo for his commitment to the association and the cantonal federation GastroTicino. On the occasion, Stefano Bernasconi of Grotto Sbardella in Giubiasco, Salvatore Squillante of Antica Osteria del Carlin in Claro, and Massimo Gandola of Locanda Marco in Bellinzona were elected to the committee. Davide Della Vecchia of Hotel Des Alpes in Airolo was confirmed in his role as treasurer, Giuseppe Luciano of Ristorante Red in Biasca, and secretary Mattia Manzocchi were also confirmed. In his report, Andrea Giuliani drew a positive balance of the past year, especially in comparison to 2024, a particularly difficult year. However, he expressed concern for the current season, highlighting some uncertainties related to the strong franc, increases in health insurance premiums, and reduced purchasing power.
