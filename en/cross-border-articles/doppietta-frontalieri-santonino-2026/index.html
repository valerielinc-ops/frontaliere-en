This permit allows for daily border crossing for work purposes. To obtain it, a series of documents must be presented, including the employment contract, proof of residence, and the completed application form. It is important to follow the procedures carefully and ensure that all necessary documents are available. ### Managing Health Insurance Managing health insurance is another crucial aspect for cross-border workers who choose the double residency. In Switzerland, health insurance is mandatory, and cross-border workers must adhere to the LAMal system. In Italy, health coverage is provided by the National Health Service (SSN). What documents are required to obtain the G permit?
