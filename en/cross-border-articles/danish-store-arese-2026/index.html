The opening follows the success of the 14 stores already active in Lombardy and the rest of Italy, consolidating the brand's presence in the national territory. The inauguration was conceived as a real party for visitors, with free products, gifts for the first customers, games and surprises for adults and children, and live DJ sets to accompany shopping. The enthusiasm for the brand was already evident last weekend, when Normal organized an 'event spot' itinerant within Il Centro, distributing prizes and anticipating the arrival of the store. 'We can't wait to bring the unique and innovative atmosphere of Normal to Arese as well,' said Jesper Brask Laumann, Country Manager of Normal for Italy. 'Our stores in Milan, Rome, Venice, and Genoa have recorded great appreciation. This gives us confidence for future expansion in Italy.' The new store in Arese enhances the offer in Milan, where the brand is already present in Via Torino, Milanofiori, and Merlata Bloom. The network also extends throughout Italy, with stores in Rome, Udine, Venice, Limbiate, Vicenza, Genoa, Savignano sul Rubicone, and Casalecchio di Reno. ### The philosophy of Normal The philosophy of Normal is simple: fixed low prices all year round, without having to wait for discounts or sales.
