In Fagnano Olona, the naming of a street after Giannino Landoni, a mechanotextile entrepreneur who passed away in 2023, is approaching. The administrative process has been concluded, and the prefectural decree has already been issued in the past months. The proposal was presented in September 2025 by the Enrico Dell'Acqua Association, which asked the Municipality of Fagnano Olona to name the private street between Busto Arsizio and Cassano Magnago after their 'honorary member.' The request was unanimously accepted by the municipal council. Subsequently, on January 7th, the prefect of Varese, Salvatore Pasquariello, signed the necessary decree to proceed with the naming. Now, the final formal step is awaited. Mayor Marco Baroffio communicated to the association that the council will examine the final deliberation and that a meeting will be scheduled to define the date and modalities of the inauguration ceremony.
