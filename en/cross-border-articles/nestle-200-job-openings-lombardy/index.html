Nestle's Lombardy headquarters will be open to new talents in Italy, with a particular emphasis on the municipalities of Como (22,000 inhabitants), Varese (79,000 inhabitants), Lecco (48,000 inhabitants), and Lugano (70,000 inhabitants), which boast a strong presence of innovative and dynamic enterprises. What do Nestle's Recruiters Look For? - Management Engineer: An expert in process optimization and productivity improvement is sought, with at least 3 years of experience in a similar role. - HR Business Partner: A professional with experience in human resource management is sought, with particular attention to data management and result analysis. - Boutique Vice Manager: A manager with experience in project and team management is sought, with particular attention to risk management and result analysis. How Can I Apply? To apply, visit Nestle's website and download the application form. Note that Nestle requires basic knowledge of Italian and English, as well as at least 1 year of experience in a similar role.
