Castellanza: €1.8m investments for 2025 (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 7 May 2026

The municipality of Castellanza has approved a 2025 budget with €1.8 million in investments for roads, schools, and sports facilities

Contesto

In brief - 1.8 million euros invested in 2025 in Castellanza - 127.92 euros per capita for public works - Priority to roads, sports and municipal buildings ## Key facts - What: Investments for public works - When: 2025 - Where: Castellanza, Italy - Who: Municipality of Castellanza - Amount: 1,810,456.54 euros The Municipality of Castellanza closed 2025 with a positive balance and an investment plan that involved every corner of the city. The municipal council approved the management report, a document that photographs the state of municipal finances and the administration's ability to transform public resources into concrete works for citizens. The investments made or started during 2025 amount to 1,810,456.54 euros, with an expenditure of 127.92 euros per inhabitant. Leading the ranking for investment volume are roads and lighting, which together absorb 987,320.65 euros. Of these, 899,841.66 euros were allocated to the redevelopment of roads and sidewalks, while 87,478.99 euros financed interventions on public lighting. Following are sports and youth policies, with 751,099.18 euros mainly used for the Sports Palace (674,243.36 euros) and for sports and recreational facilities (76,855.82 euros). ### Key investments The third sector for resources mobilized is that of municipal buildings, with 566,602.76 euros divided among the Town Hall (318,411.99 euros), ERP Houses (243,782.87 euros) and the barracks (4,407.90 euros). Following are investments in public green spaces and the environment, totaling 434,440.43 euros, divided between public green spaces (325,631.28 euros) and the waste collection center (108,809.15 euros). What are the main investments made in Castellanza in 2025?
The main investments made in Castellanza in 2025 include 987,320.65 euros for roads and lighting, 751,099.18 euros for sports and youth policies, and 566,602.76 euros for municipal buildings. Other significant investments concern public green spaces and the environment (434,440.43 euros), schools (480,000 euros), video surveillance (101,261.46 euros), the cemetery (56,042.48 euros), and cultural sites (29,478.92 euros).
How were the 2025 investments in Castellanza financed?
The 2025 investments in Castellanza were financed through a combination of sources, including 250,000 euros from new loans, 200,000 euros from regional contributions, 173,451.96 euros from building permit revenues, 63,150.69 euros from asset sales, 47,302.82 euros from state transfers, and 1,569,919.65 euros from the multi-year restricted fund for capital expenditure.
