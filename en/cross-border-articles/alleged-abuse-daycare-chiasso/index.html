Investigations by the police and the Public Prosecutor's Office are ongoing, but no official statements have been released at this time. The facility, which benefits from public support from the Canton, has been rooted in the Ticino reality for decades and welcomes children aged between zero and three years, many of whom come from families in difficulty. The family filed a formal complaint with the cantonal police on May 15, 2023. ### Investigation procedure The investigations are being conducted by the cantonal police and the Public Prosecutor's Office. The investigations are still in the initial phase and no specific details have been released about the facts under investigation. It is crucial that the competent authorities conduct thorough investigations to verify the truth of the accusations and ensure that similar episodes do not recur.
