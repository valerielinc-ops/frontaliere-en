TL;DR - Ammonia leak at Rapelli facility in Stabio, Switzerland. - 100 people evacuated; 5 hospitalized, all recovered quickly. - No contamination; production resumed with full safety. ## Key facts - Incident: Ammonia leak at Rapelli facility in Stabio. - Date: Thursday evening, around 9:00 PM. - Cause: Rupture of a pipe in a compressor of the refrigeration system. - Evacuated: 100 people evacuated as a precaution. - Hospitalized: 5 individuals transported to hospitals for medical assessments. - Recovery: All hospitalized individuals were discharged within 24 hours. - Safety: No contamination; production resumed with full safety. - Alert System: Alert Swiss system notified local population and authorities. On Thursday evening, around 9:00 PM, an ammonia leak occurred at the Rapelli facility in Stabio. The incident was caused by the sudden rupture of a pipe located inside a compressor that is part of the company's refrigeration system. Following the malfunction, an alert was immediately activated through the Alert Swiss system, a standard procedure designed to notify the local population and relevant authorities in case of environmental or technical risks. ### Emergency management and rescue operations Rapelli's press office emphasized that the situation was managed quickly and efficiently, thanks to the perfect coordination between the company's internal emergency plan and the prompt response of external authorities. Police officers, rescue teams, and firefighters arrived on-site in a very short time, working together to contain the leak and secure the entire affected area. Dettagli operativi

Rapelli has clarified the technical nature of the incident in an unequivocal manner, explaining that the ammonia used in refrigeration circuits is a highly volatile gas. This substance, which is essential for maintaining the temperatures required in preservation processes, circulates in closed circuits dedicated exclusively to cooling systems. It is important to emphasize that, at no stage of operations, does ammonia come into contact with raw materials, finished products, or food production lines. ## Food safety analysis The company sought to reassure consumers and business partners by reiterating that the total safety of its products is fully guaranteed, regardless of the technical failure that occurred. There has been no contamination, and no interruptions or changes to the food safety chain are expected. Production was restarted after verifying the integrity of all systems and the effectiveness of the safety measures adopted.
