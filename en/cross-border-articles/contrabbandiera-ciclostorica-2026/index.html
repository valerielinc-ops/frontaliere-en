In brief - La Contrabbandiera 2026 will take place on Sunday, May 24, in Uggiate con Ronago. - A 45 km route with 500 meters of positive elevation gain. - Event coinciding with the 12th gathering of vintage vehicles. ## Key facts - What: Ciclostorica La Contrabbandiera 2026. - When: Sunday, May 24, 2026, at 9:00 AM. - Where: Departure from Piazzale Europa, Uggiate con Ronago. - Who: Organized by BMX Ciclistica Olgiatese ASD. - Cost: Not specified. Get ready with wool jerseys and shoulder holsters: the vintage cycling event that touches the border area between Como, Varese, and Ticino is back. The 2026 edition will start on Sunday, May 24, at 9:00 AM from Piazzale Europa in Uggiate con Ronago, coinciding with the 12th gathering of vintage vehicles organized by the association 'I röd vècc de fruntiera'. The route will cover 45 km with 500 meters of positive elevation gain, passing through places of great historical and landscape charm: from Venegono Superiore to the ancient village of Castiglione Olona, from the suggestive Molera quarries to the Trotto Mill, and then returning to Uggiate con Ronago along the old Valmorea railway track. As per tradition, several refreshment points will be set up along the route. At the end of the ride, participants can join the friends of the automotive gathering for a convivial lunch. ### Route and Activities The route of the Ciclostorica La Contrabbandiera 2026 has been designed to offer participants a unique experience, combining the passion for vintage cycling with the discovery of historically and scenically charming places. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the route of the vintage bike ride La Contrabbandiera 2026?
The route of the vintage bike ride La Contrabbandiera 2026 covers 45 km with 500 meters of positive elevation gain, starting from Piazzale Europa in Uggiate with Ronago and passing through places of great historical and scenic charm such as Venegono Superiore, the ancient village of Castiglione Olona, the Molera Quarries, and the Molino del Trotto, before returning to Uggiate with Ronago along the old Valmorea railway track.
What is required to participate in the vintage bike ride?
To participate in the vintage bike ride La Contrabbandiera 2026, the use of vintage bicycles and vintage-style clothing is required. You can contact the BMX Ciclistica Olgiatese ASD in Olgiate Comasco for information and registrations.
Will there be refreshment points along the route?
Yes, several refreshment points will be set up along the route where participants can rest and refuel. At the end of the bike ride, a convivial lunch is planned in collaboration with the friends of the automotive rally.
