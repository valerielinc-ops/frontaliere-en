In brief - New single by Freddie & The Cannonballs with the Cinelli Brothers - Inspiration from 'Scarpette Ricamate' - Collaboration born in London - Concerts planned in Switzerland and the United Kingdom ## Key facts - What: New single 'Dancing Shoes' - When: Released on April 15, 2024 - Where: London and Bellinzona - Who: Freddie Albertoni, Mattia Mantello, Cinelli Brothers - Amount: Not specified, but production cost around CHF 20,000, funded through crowdfunding - Events: Concerts in Bellinzona (May 23, 2024) and London (June 30, 2024) BELLINZONA/LONDON - Some people relax during their sabbatical year, while others... end up working anyway. In the case of Federico “Freddie” Albertoni, rest assured, it’s a sweet labor in a funky style. Indeed, 'Dancing Shoes,' the new single by the Ticinese band, was recently released in collaboration with the British Cinelli Brothers, young stars of London’s soul scene whom Albertoni met, along with his trusted friend Mattia 'Mad' Mantello, right in the city. 'Mattia already had an embryonic idea for the piece; Marco Cinelli then shuffled the cards by deciding to sing it in falsetto,' and the track was immediately swept by a purple wave in the style of... Prince: 'It wasn’t supposed to be like this, but in the end, it became a modern but not too modern blues funk,' explains the bassist and singer. ### 'Scarpette Ricamate' in a modern key And then it turns out that these dancing shoes are none other than... the legendary 'Scarpette' embroidered: 'Yes, exactly those from the folk song, which I used to sing to my son Filippo to put him to sleep,' confirms Albertoni. The nuances of the song, however, are definitely... more adult. ### Collaboration and future The collaboration between the Bellinzona band and the Cinelli duo doesn’t...

Frequently Asked Questions
Who are the Cinelli Brothers?
The Cinelli Brothers are a British duo of young talents in the London soul scene, highly sought after in the London music scene. They collaborated with Freddie Albertoni and Mattia Mantello for the single 'Dancing Shoes'.
Where can I listen to the new single 'Dancing Shoes'?
