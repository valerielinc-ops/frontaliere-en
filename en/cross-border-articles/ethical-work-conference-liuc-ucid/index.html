LIUC Conference: Ethics and Responsibility at Work

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

On Friday, May 8th, LIUC will host a conference on ethics and responsibility in the workplace with UCID. Speakers include Cardinal Angelo Bagnasco and the Bishop of Lodi.

Contesto

In brief - Conference at LIUC on ethics and responsibility at work - Speakers include Cardinal Angelo Bagnasco and the Bishop of Lodi - Focus on the quality of work relationships ## Key facts - What: Conference on ethics and responsibility at work - When: Friday, May 8, 2026, at 5 PM - Where: LIUC – Cattaneo University, Castronno - Who: LIUC, UCID, Cardinal Angelo Bagnasco, Monsignor Maurizio Malvestiti - Cost: Not specified On Friday, May 8, 2026, at 5 PM, a conference titled 'How should work be to be as it should? Justice in relationships: ethics and responsibility in organizational action' will be held at LIUC – Cattaneo University in Castronno. The event, organized by the Pier Giorgio Frassati Pastoral Center together with the Christian Union of Entrepreneurs and Managers (UCID), aims to provide a moment of reflection and discussion on the topics of ethics, responsibility, and the quality of relationships in work and institutional contexts. Confirmed speakers include Cardinal Angelo Bagnasco, ecclesiastical advisor to UCID Nazionale, and His Excellency Monsignor Maurizio Malvestiti, Bishop of Lodi and ecclesiastical advisor to UCID Lombardo Group. Other speakers will include Dr. Fabio Storchi, president of the UCID section of Reggio Emilia and former president of Federmeccanica, and Eng. Aldo Fumagalli, president of UCID Lombardo Group. ### In-depth analysis and testimonials The conference will also feature the participation of Don Walter Magnoni, who will provide a theological approach, and Eliana Minelli, who will address the organizational reflection of work. Additionally, there will be testimonials from Luisa Bove, Elena Salda, and Fabio Storchi, contributing to an authoritative and comprehensive debate on the topic. Analysis of Work Relationships The conference at LIUC addresses crucial themes for the modern world of work, particularly for cross-border workers operating in transnational contexts. The quality of work relationships is a determining factor for the well-being of employees and the efficiency of organizations. Ethics and responsibility are key elements for building a positive and productive work environment. ### Comparison of Work Contexts In Ticino, cross-border workers often have to adapt to different corporate cultures and labor regulations. The conference offers the opportunity to engage with industry experts and to acquire useful tools for managing work relationships effectively. The presence of representatives from the ecclesiastical world underscores the importance of values such as justice and responsibility in the work context. ### Future Scenarios The conference aims to explore future scenarios for the world of work, with a particular focus on how ethics and responsibility can positively influence organizational dynamics. Participants will have the opportunity to discuss with experts and share their experiences, thus contributing to a constructive and stimulating debate. ### Practical Implications For cross-border workers, participating in events like this can be an opportunity to improve their skills and better understand the dynamics of the labor market.
