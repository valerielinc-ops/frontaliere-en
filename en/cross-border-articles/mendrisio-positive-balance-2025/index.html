Mendrisio's municipal council announces a fourth consecutive positive financial result, despite geopolitical uncertainties.

Municipal finances remain 'comfortable', despite uncertainties related to reforms and the geopolitical landscape. The Municipality of Mendrisio announced today the financial results for the year 2025, highlighting fiscal stability that has rewarded the city. This positive result is part of a series of four consecutive years of surplus budgets, a sign of prudent and careful financial management. The mayor of Mendrisio, Samuele Cavadini, commented on the results emphasizing the importance of maintaining a solid financial balance in a globally uncertain economic context. 'We are satisfied with the results achieved, but we remain vigilant in the face of future challenges,' declared Cavadini.
