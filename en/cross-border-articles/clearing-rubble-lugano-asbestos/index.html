Frequently Asked Questions
What are the risks associated with the presence of asbestos?
The presence of asbestos can cause the dispersion of dust in the air, which can be harmful to health. Prolonged exposure to these dust particles can increase the risk of developing respiratory diseases and other health issues. It is important to take the necessary precautions to reduce exposure.
How can I protect myself from asbestos dust?
To protect yourself from asbestos dust, close windows and doors during working hours, avoid passing near the construction site, and follow the instructions provided by the authorities. If you experience symptoms related to exposure, consult a doctor immediately.
What are the regulations in place for the management of asbestos?
The regulations in place for the management of asbestos are established by the Section for the Protection of Air, Water, and Soil of the Department of Territory and by the Suva. These regulations require the use of specific equipment and the adoption of preventive measures to ensure the safety of workers and residents.
