Personalized license plates approved in Switzerland

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

The National Council has approved Lorenzo Quadri's motion for personalized license plates, paving the way for 'vanity plates'

In brief - National Council approves Quadri motion - License plates with letters in addition to numbers - Vanity plates in Switzerland ## Key facts - What: Motion for customizable license plates - When: April 30, 2026 - Where: Bern - Who: National Council - Vote: 95 in favor, 91 against, 4 abstentions The Swiss National Council has approved, with 95 votes in favor, 91 against, and 4 abstentions, a motion presented by Ticino National Councilor Lorenzo Quadri, which proposes the introduction of customizable license plates. The motion, discussed and approved on April 30, 2026, aims to solve the problem of the exhaustion of available numeric combinations for license plates, paving the way for so-called 'vanity plates'. Quadri explained that the cantons of Bern and Zurich are approaching the exhaustion of numeric combinations, making it necessary to use letters to create new license plates. This system would allow citizens to customize their license plates with names, words, or abbreviations. The passion for special license plates is already widespread in Switzerland, as demonstrated by the case of the license plate 'SO 1', sold for 390,000 francs. Quadri also proposed that the cantons could control the combinations, with a federal ban on content contrary to good morals or discriminatory. The parliamentary act will now be examined by the Council of States. ### Practical implications The approval of Quadri's motion could have several implications for Swiss citizens and cross-border workers in Ticino. Frequently Asked Questions
What are 'vanity plates'?
'Vanity plates' are customizable license plates that contain names, words, or abbreviations chosen by the vehicle owner. These plates are already common in many countries, such as the United States and the United Kingdom, and could be introduced in Switzerland thanks to the approval of Quadri's motion.
What are the advantages of customizable license plates?
Customizable license plates offer several advantages, including the ability to express your personality or interests through the combination of letters and numbers. Additionally, customizable license plates can make it easier to identify vehicles, especially in areas with high traffic density.
What are the potential risks of customizable license plates?
The potential risks of customizable license plates include increased costs for purchasing special plates and the possibility of abuse with inappropriate content. To avoid these risks, it is important that the new plates are managed securely and in accordance with federal regulations.
