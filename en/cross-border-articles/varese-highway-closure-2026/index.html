Partial closure of Varese-A8 connector on Monday evening

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

From 22:00 to 24:00 on Monday, April 27, 2026, the on-ramp to the A8 Milan-Varese, towards Varese, will be closed.

Contesto

In brief - Partial closure of the Varese-A8 connector on Monday, April 27, 2026 - From 22:00 to 24:00 - Maintenance work on horizontal road markings ## Key facts - What: Partial closure of the Varese-A8 connector - When: Monday, April 27, 2026, from 22:00 to 24:00 - Where: On the SC5 Varese-Gazzada Road (Varese-A8 motorway connector) - Who: Autostrade per l'Italia - Cost: Not specified On Monday evening, from 22:00 to 24:00, the entrance ramp to the A8 Milan-Varese, towards Varese, will be closed for those coming from Buguggiate. The closure is necessary for maintenance work on the horizontal road markings on the SC5 Varese-Gazzada Road. Horizontal road markings are essential for road safety, and these works are crucial to ensure the visibility and clarity of road signs. ### Alternative routes For those who need to reach Varese by car, it is recommended to take the A60 Varese Ring Road, exit at the first interchange towards the SP57 Gazzada/Morazzone and re-enter the A60 towards Buguggiate, then merge onto the A8 towards Varese. This alternative route allows you to avoid the closed section and still reach your destination without too many inconveniences. It is important to plan your route in advance to avoid delays. 📊 Concrete example: - Additional time: The alternative route adds about 10-15 minutes to the journey, depending on traffic. - Distance: Approximately 5 km more than the direct route. ### Impact on traffic The partial closure of the Varese-A8 connector could cause an increase in traffic on the A60 Varese Ring Road and the SP57 Gazzada/Morazzone. Motorists are invited to pay attention and respect speed limits and road signs.
