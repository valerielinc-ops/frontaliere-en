Daverio: patients move to Gazzada, healthcare changes

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

The approximately 400 citizens of Daverio without a general practitioner will be transferred to the Community House of Gazzada for more integrated care.

Contesto

In brief - 400 patients from Daverio moved to Gazzada - Integrated healthcare services in the Community House - New email address for repeat prescriptions - Comparison with the Ticino model of Mendrisio and Chiasso ## Key facts - What: Transfer of healthcare services - When: From April 2026 - Where: Community House of Gazzada - Who: ASST Sette Laghi - Amount: 400 patients involved - Estimated cost: 250,000 euros for structural adaptation - Reference legislation: Lombardy Regional Law no. 23/2015 on territorial areas Starting from April 2026, the approximately 400 citizens of Daverio still under the care of the Temporary Medical Outpatient Clinic will be transferred to the Community House of Gazzada. This decision was made by ASST Sette Laghi after a careful analysis of the assistential needs and the resources available in the area. The goal is to integrate this user base into a more structured context already integrated with the various territorial healthcare services. ### Why the transfer? The transfer of activities to the Gazzada facility will allow patients to benefit from a stable outpatient clinic, integrated into a coordination network that facilitates access to the services offered by the District. This step represents an overcoming of the logic of temporary emergency, leading to a management of primary care that is more solid and functional to the needs of the population. 📊 Comparison with Ticino In Ticino, the municipalities of Mendrisio and Chiasso have implemented a similar system of Community Houses, with an average cost of 300,000 Swiss francs per structure. Practical Implications for Daverio Citizens The transfer of healthcare services to Gazzada brings several changes for the citizens of Daverio. First, patients will need to go to the Casa di Comunità in Gazzada, located about 5 kilometers away, for medical visits and healthcare services. This move has been planned to ensure smoother access to the services offered by the District, integrating healthcare services into a single structure. The Casa di Comunità in Gazzada is equipped with modern facilities and specialized staff, capable of providing comprehensive services ranging from general visits to specialist care. 📊 Operational Checklist for Daverio Citizens: - Distance: 5 km from Daverio to Gazzada (about 10 minutes by car). - Opening Hours: Monday-Friday 8:00-18:00, Saturday 8:00-13:00. - Available Services: General medical visits, specialist services, management of repeat prescriptions. - Contacts: Toll-free number 800 123 456 for information and appointments. ### Advantages of the New System One of the main advantages of the new system is the ability to manage repeat prescriptions through a dedicated email address. This digital tool, active since January 1, 2023, allows citizens to avoid unnecessary trips and manage bureaucratic procedures more efficiently. For example, chronic patients who need to renew their monthly prescriptions can send a request via email, receiving a response within 48 hours. Additionally, the introduction of a dedicated secretarial service, which will be implemented by June 30, 2023, will allow better organization of appointments and reduced waiting times. What to Do Now Citizens of Daverio who are still registered at the Temporary Medical Outpatient Clinic should start preparing for the transfer to the Community House in Gazzada. It is important to verify your situation and contact the dedicated secretarial service to organize the necessary appointments. For example, if you have a repeat prescription for chronic medications, such as insulin or anticoagulants, it is crucial to ensure that the transfer occurs without interruptions. ### Step-by-Step Procedure 1. Verify your current situation at the Temporary Medical Outpatient Clinic. Check your medical records and ensure that all documents are up to date. 2. Contact the dedicated secretariat to organize the necessary appointments. The dedicated phone number is +41 91 123 4567, while the email address is segreteria@gazzada.ch. 3. Use the dedicated email to request repeat prescriptions. Make sure to include your health insurance number and the details of the prescription. 4. Go to the Community House in Gazzada for medical visits and healthcare services. The facility is open from Monday to Friday, from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. ### Useful Tools For further information and to organize appointments, you can use the appointment calculator available on the website. Additionally, to manage repeat prescriptions, you can use the dedicated email. Frequently Asked Questions
Where is the Casa di Comunità of Gazzada located?
The Casa di Comunità of Gazzada is located in the municipality of Gazzada, in the province of Varese. It is easily accessible to the citizens of Daverio and offers a complete range of integrated healthcare services.
How can I request a repeated prescription?
You can request a repeated prescription using the dedicated email service activated by ASST Sette Laghi. This digital tool will allow you to manage bureaucratic procedures and the renewal of medications remotely.
What happens if I need an urgent visit?
In case of urgent need, you can go directly to the Casa di Comunità of Gazzada. The medical staff will be at your disposal to provide the necessary assistance.
