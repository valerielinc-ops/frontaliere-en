Passport service at 7,500 Italian post offices

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

From March 2024, passport issuance and renewal services are available at 7,500 Italian post offices, including those in the Polis project.

Contesto

In brief - Passport available at 7,500 Italian post offices - 206,000 passports issued, 156,000 in Polis municipalities - Service launched in March 2024, completed on schedule ## Key facts - What: Passport issuance and renewal - When: From March 2024 - Where: 7,500 post offices in Italy - Who: Poste Italiane, Ministry of the Interior, Ministry of Enterprises and Made in Italy - Amount: 206,000 passports issued, 156,000 in Polis municipalities The passport can be requested at approximately 7,500 post offices throughout Italy. This service, launched in March 2024 with a trial in the province of Bologna, has been completed on schedule and now covers the entire national territory. Post offices have issued around 206,000 passports, of which 156,000 in Polis municipalities. Thanks to the Polis project, citizens can obtain passports without having to go to the police station and with the possibility of receiving the document at home, a choice made by 8 out of 10 people living in small towns. ### Implications for cross-border workers For cross-border workers living in Italy and working in Switzerland, this service represents a significant advantage. The possibility of requesting the passport at a nearby post office significantly reduces travel time and costs. Moreover, the option to receive the document at home is an additional benefit, especially for those with demanding work schedules or difficulties in traveling. ### Comparison with the previous situation Before this service was introduced, cross-border workers had to go to the police stations to request or renew their passports. Concrete action For cross-border workers who wish to apply for or renew their passport at post offices, it is necessary to follow a few simple but fundamental steps. Here is a practical guide for those who want to request or renew their passport. ### Step 1: Verify the requirements Before going to a post office, it is important to check that you have all the necessary documents. These include an identity card, tax code, and a recent photograph. It is also advisable to book an appointment online to avoid unnecessary waiting times. ### Step 2: Booking the appointment The appointment can be booked online through the Poste Italiane website. You can choose the most convenient date and time, based on the availability of the chosen post office. The booking is free and can be done in a few minutes. ### Step 3: Going to the post office On the day of the appointment, you need to go to the post office with all the required documents. It is important to arrive a few minutes early to avoid delays. During the appointment, a postal operator will register the application and provide all the necessary information. ### Step 4: Receiving the passport Once the application has been submitted, the passport will be delivered to your home or to the post office, depending on the applicant's preference. Frequently Asked Questions
What documents are required to apply for a passport at a post office?
To apply for a passport at a post office, you need to bring your ID card, tax code, and a recent photograph. It is also advisable to book an online appointment to avoid unnecessary waiting times.
How long does it take to receive the passport after submitting the application?
The waiting time for passport delivery varies depending on the type of passport requested and the availability of the competent authorities. In general, the passport is delivered within a few days of submitting the application.
Is it possible to receive the passport at home?
Yes, it is possible to receive the passport at home. When submitting the application, you can indicate your preference for home delivery or collection at the post office.
