Sintab Project: Rio dei Gioghi Trail Upgrade (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 May 2026

Work begins on securing the CAI Rio dei Gioghi trail in Ligurno, Cantello, as part of the Sintab project.

Contesto

In brief - Work has begun on the requalification of the Rio dei Gioghi trail in Ligurno - Sintab project for safety and natural enhancement - Interventions following storm damage ## Key facts - What: Requalification of the Rio dei Gioghi trail - When: Work recently begun - Where: Ligurno, a hamlet of Cantello - Who: Sintab project and contracted companies - Amount: Not yet specified Work on the requalification of the CAI "Rio dei Gioghi" trail in the hamlet of Ligurno, Cantello, has finally begun. This intervention is part of the Sintab project (Integrated Development for Cross-border Nature and Biodiversity), which aims to return to the community a safe, accessible trail enriched with educational and environmental content. In recent days, an operational site visit was held with the presence of the companies and professionals in charge. The meeting served to define the final technical details and to verify the condition of the trail after the heavy rains of the past few weeks. The bad weather has caused several issues, including fallen trees that made passage difficult. ### Planned interventions The work plan includes a series of actions aimed at consolidating the trail. Initially, a general cleanup and removal of unstable materials will be carried out, followed by securing the slopes and boulders. Particular attention will be given to the regulation of rainwater and the consolidation of the trail bed, essential interventions to ensure the trail's durability over time and prevent new landslides. ### Educational enhancement In addition to safety, the project focuses on natural enhancement. Impact on the Territory and Cross-Border Commuters The Sintab project represents an important opportunity for the Cantello area and for cross-border commuters who live and work in the region. The redevelopment of the Rio dei Gioghi trail will not only improve the safety and accessibility of the route, but will also offer new educational and recreational opportunities for the local community and for cross-border workers who wish to enjoy nature during their free time. ### Benefits for the Community The natural enhancement of the trail is a valuable addition to the territory. The wooden information structures and rest stops will allow hikers and school groups to deepen their knowledge of local biodiversity. This project, therefore, will not only improve the safety of the trail, but will also contribute to promoting environmental awareness and raising awareness of nature. 📊💡 ### Implications for Cross-Border Commuters For cross-border commuters who live in Italy and work in Switzerland, the Sintab project offers a unique opportunity to enjoy a safe and well-equipped natural environment. Once redeveloped, the Rio dei Gioghi trail could become an ideal destination for hikes and walks, allowing cross-border workers to relax and rejuvenate after a long day's work. ⚠️ ### Comparison with the Previous Situation Before the intervention, the Rio dei Gioghi trail presented several critical issues, especially due to the damage caused by bad weather. The presence of fallen trees and unstable materials made passage difficult, limiting the accessibility of the route.
