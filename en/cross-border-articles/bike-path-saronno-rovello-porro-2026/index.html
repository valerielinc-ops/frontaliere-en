Saronno-Rovello Porro cycle path: when will it be ready?

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

Three years after the ghost bike was installed, Fiab Saronno Ciclocittà urges the municipalities to build the protected cycle path between Saronno and Rovello Porro.

In brief - Three years after the ghost bike, Fiab Saronno urges municipalities for the cycle path. - The cycle path between Saronno and Rovello Porro is still incomplete. - Concrete updates on timelines and progress status requested. ## Key facts - What: Protected cycle path between Saronno and Rovello Porro - When: Promised three years ago, still incomplete - Where: Along the provincial road SP31 - Who: Fiab Saronno Ciclocittà, Municipalities of Saronno and Rovello Porro - Amount: Not yet specified On April 22, 2023, Fiab Saronno Ciclocittà organized a ceremony on SP31 in Rovello Porro to remember Paola Greselin and Simone Vavassori, who died while cycling on the provincial road. The commemoration was attended by the mayors of both municipalities, who publicly committed to building a protected cycle path. However, three years after that commitment, the cycle path between Saronno and Rovello Porro remains unfinished. Fiab Saronno is once again urging the involved administrations, asking for concrete updates on the timelines and progress status of the project. The implementation of a protected cycle path along SP31 is seen as a necessary intervention to prevent similar tragedies from recurring. The association reminds the institutions of the responsibilities they publicly assumed, asking that the municipal administrations officially inform citizens about the progress status and the implementation timelines of the work. ### The safety issue The implementation of a protected cycle path along SP31 is not only about sustainable mobility but also about road safety. The provincial road has been the scene of tragedies involving cyclists, and securing the stretch is seen as a duty towards the victims and their families. ### Remembering the victims During the 2023 ceremony...

Practical Analysis The creation of a protected cycle path between Saronno and Rovello Porro represents a fundamental step for road safety and sustainable mobility in the area. The provincial road SP31 is one of the main connections between the two municipalities and sees intense traffic of vehicles and cyclists. The presence of a protected cycle path could significantly reduce the risk of accidents, improving safety for all road users. ### Comparison with the Current Situation Currently, SP31 does not have a protected cycle path, making bicycle trips dangerous. The realization of such infrastructure could encourage the use of bicycles as a daily means of transport, reducing traffic and improving air quality. Furthermore, a protected cycle path could promote cycling tourism, attracting visitors to the area. ### Future Scenarios If the cycle path were realized, there could be an increase in the number of cyclists using the road for daily commutes. This could lead to a reduction in vehicular traffic and greater livability of the area. Additionally, the presence of a protected cycle path could encourage the organization of cycling events and sports competitions, contributing to the promotion of the territory. ### Concrete Procedures For the realization of the cycle path, the municipalities of Saronno and Rovello Porro should follow a series of specific steps. First, it is necessary to conduct a feasibility study to evaluate the costs and implementation times of the work. Subsequently, detailed design of the cycle path should be carried out, taking into account the needs of cyclists and the characteristics of the territory.
