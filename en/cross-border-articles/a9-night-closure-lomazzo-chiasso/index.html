A9 night closure: changes for cross-border workers

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

From 10:00 PM on May 4th to 5:00 AM on May 5th, nighttime closure between Lomazzo Sud and Como Centro towards Chiasso. Mandatory detours for cross-border workers.

Frequently Asked Questions
What are the dates and times for the nighttime closure of the A9 between Lomazzo sud and Como Centro?
The nighttime closure of the A9 between Lomazzo sud and Como Centro is scheduled from 10:00 PM on Monday, May 4th, to 5:00 AM on Tuesday, May 5th. The Lario est service area will be closed from 9:00 PM to 5:00 AM.
What are the mandatory detours for cross-border workers heading towards Chiasso?
For cross-border workers heading towards Chiasso, the mandatory exit is at the Lomazzo sud interchange.
