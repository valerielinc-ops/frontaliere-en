A9 motorway towards Chiasso closed at night: here’s when and why (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 11 April 2026

Como Centro-Chiasso section of the A9 motorway will be blocked from 10 PM to 5 AM due to road resurfacing, high-voltage cable replacement, and the transit of special vehicles from April 13 to 15, 2026

Contesto

TL;DR - A9 motorway Como Centro-Chiasso section closed at night for works. - Closures from 10 PM to 5 AM on April 13-15, 2026. - Drivers must exit at Como Centro and use ordinary roads. ## Key facts - Cosa: Closure of A9 motorway section for paving and cable replacement. - Quando: From 10:00 PM on April 13 to 5:00 AM on April 15, 2026. - Dove: Como Centro-Chiasso section of the A9 motorway. - Chi: Autostrade per l’Italia and Terna are responsible for the works. - Motivo: Paving works and replacement of a high-voltage cable. - Impatto: Delays expected for cross-border commuters and trade. - Alternative: Drivers must exit at Como Centro and use ordinary roads. - Fonti: Autostrade per l’Italia and Terna communications. From 10:00 PM on Monday, April 13 to 5:00 AM on Tuesday, April 14, and then from 10:00 PM on Tuesday, April 14 to 5:00 AM on Wednesday, April 15, the Como Centro-Chiasso section of the A9 motorway will be closed to night-time traffic. This measure, announced by Autostrade per l’Italia, has been implemented to allow paving works and the replacement of a high-voltage cable managed by the company Terna. On the night between Tuesday and Wednesday, the closure will instead be necessary to facilitate the transit of special vehicles, as confirmed by motorway authorities. Drivers intending to use this section must exit at the Como Centro junction and continue along the ordinary road network towards Chiasso, until reaching the customs checkpoint. ### Scheduled works The night-time construction activities are part of a broader plan for extraordinary maintenance of the motorway infrastructure, which has already seen partial closures in the past. Frequently Asked Questions
What happens if I arrive in Como after 10 PM and need to cross into Switzerland?
You will have to exit at the Como Centro interchange and follow standard road signs towards Chiasso. Customs checks at Chiasso remain active even at night, so make sure you have your Swiss motorway vignette (mandatory for Italian vehicles) and valid ID with you. Wait times may increase due to additional traffic.
Can I use the A9 motorway during the day despite the construction work?
Yes, closures are only scheduled **from 10 PM to 5 AM** on the specified days. During the day, the Como Centro to Chiasso stretch remains accessible, though you may encounter delays due to ongoing roadworks.
Are there alternatives to private vehicles for crossing the border?
At this time, it hasn’t been confirmed whether similar closures will be repeated in the future. Autostrade per l’Italia and Terna have not yet provided details about any additional nighttime interventions. We recommend monitoring official announcements on [autostrade.it](nav:traffic-history) or Ticino authorities’ social media channels.
What are the risks if I ignore the A9 closure?
Italian and Swiss traffic police conduct constant checks during nighttime closures. If caught driving on a closed motorway, you may face **administrative fines** or, in case of repeat offenses, **license suspension**. Additionally, special vehicles in transit may not spot your vehicle in time, increasing the risk of accidents.
Will the A9 construction work also affect daytime traffic in the coming months?
This has not yet been specified. Nighttime work has been planned to minimize daytime traffic disruption, but extended closures may still be necessary. Authorities urge commuters to follow official updates to avoid unexpected delays.
