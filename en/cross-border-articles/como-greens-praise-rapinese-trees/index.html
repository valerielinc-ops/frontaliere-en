Greens praise Rapinese for tree care

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

Green Party leader Elisabetta Patelli praises Rapinese administration for tree care in Como, ahead of the no-confidence vote against the Green Councillor.

Contesto

In brief - Elisabetta Patelli praises the Rapinese administration for tree care in Como. - High-quality technical intervention on Viale Varese with four new trees. - Tomorrow, the municipal council will discuss the motion of no confidence against the Green Spaces Councillor Chiara Bodero Maccabeo. ## Key facts - What: Praise from Green Party leader Elisabetta Patelli to the Rapinese administration. - When: 28 April 2026. - Where: Viale Varese, Como. - Who: Elisabetta Patelli, leader of the Como Greens and representative of Europa Verde. - Amount: Four new trees planted (three maples and one birch). The leader of the Como Greens, Elisabetta Patelli, expressed an unexpected praise for the administration of Mayor Alessandro Rapinese. In a note released in the last few hours, Patelli commended the way specialized technicians intervened on the trees in Viale Varese. This gesture of sportsmanship comes after months of controversy over other trees, such as the cherry trees. Tonight, the municipal council will also discuss the motion of no confidence against the Green Spaces Councillor Chiara Bodero Maccabeo, postponed from yesterday's assembly, 27 April. The motion was presented by the Pd, an ally of Alleanza Verdi e Sinistra, of which Europa Verde is a part. ### High-quality intervention on Viale Varese Today in Viale Varese, Patelli witnessed an intervention on the tree heritage that can be defined as a model of good practice for the city. The planting of four new species – three maples and one birch – to replace missing or dead trees was not just a simple replacement, but a high-quality technical and agronomic intervention. The choice was made for specimens grown in pots, a more expensive option but definitely more reliable in terms of establishment and overall quality....

Dettagli operativi

Elisabetta Patelli's praise for the Rapinese administration is a gesture of great sportsmanship, especially in a tense political context. The no-confidence motion against Green Councilor Chiara Bodero Maccabeo, presented by the Pd, could put the administration in difficulty. However, Patelli's intervention could offer unexpected support to Mayor Rapinese, especially in view of tomorrow evening's debate. The intervention on Viale Varese is an example of how urban green management can be improved, with an approach that combines aesthetics, functionality, and sustainability. ### Implications for Urban Green Management The intervention on Viale Varese demonstrates that urban green spaces can be managed in a more environmentally friendly and plant-respectful manner. The use of already formed trees, grown in pots with adequate manure, ensures better rooting and healthier growth. This approach could become a model for other cities, not just Como. The two-year rooting guarantee is a further indicator of the seriousness of the intervention and the willingness to ensure quality urban greenery. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation Before this intervention, urban green management in Como had been the subject of controversy, especially regarding the cherry trees. The intervention on Viale Varese represents a significant change, with a more technical and plant-respectful approach. This could be a positive sign for the future management of urban greenery, not only in Como but also in other cities. ### Future Scenarios If this approach becomes a consolidated practice, it could significantly improve the quality of urban greenery in Como.
