Tourism in Varesotto: 2026 figures and opportunities for cross-border workers (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 7 May 2026

The 2026 data confirms the growth of tourism in Varesotto, with 3.2 million overnight stays. Opportunities for cross-border workers between lake and city.

Contesto

In brief - 3.2 million overnight stays in Varesotto in 2026 - Somma Lombardo tops the chart with 483,251 arrivals - Varese and Saronno among the main tourist hubs ## Key facts - What: 2026 tourism data in Varesotto - When: January-December 2026 - Where: Province of Varese - Who: Lombardy Region - Amount: 3,272,972 total tourist arrivals Tourism in the province of Varese continues to show signs of growth and consolidation, with numbers that tell of an attractive territory both for international tourism, thanks to its proximity to Malpensa and Milan, and for local tourism. Presented during the meeting organized by Councillor Caruso in Gallarate, the data from the regional DAI Destinations platform, referring to the period January-December 2026 and focused on overnight stays, depict a province capable of combining different forms of tourism, with established hubs but also emerging destinations. Somma Lombardo tops the chart of municipalities with the highest number of arrivals, recording 483,251 overnight stays, equal to 15% of the provincial total. A figure closely linked to its proximity to Malpensa and the presence of accommodation facilities dedicated to airport flows, but which also testifies to the strategic role of the area as a gateway to the territory. Immediately after comes Varese with 360,585 arrivals, equal to 11%, confirming the provincial capital as a reference point for cultural, congress, and naturalistic tourism, as well as sports tourism, both in terms of practice on vacation and in terms of hosting competitive events. ### Emerging destinations Among the emerging destinations, Maccagno with Pino and Veddasca ranks eighth overall with over 103,000 arrivals, confirming the growing interest in tourism related to lakes and outdoor activities. Analysis of Opportunities The Varesotto, with its dual tourist vocation, offers numerous opportunities for cross-border workers who wish to explore the region during their free time. The proximity to Malpensa and Milan makes the area easily accessible, while the presence of accommodation facilities dedicated to airport flows guarantees a wide choice of accommodation. ### Cultural and Naturalistic Tourism Varese, with its 360,585 visitors, is a significant cultural and naturalistic hub. Cross-border workers can take advantage of this destination to participate in cultural events, visit museums, and enjoy the surrounding nature. The city also offers numerous sports activities, making it an ideal destination for those who love physical activity. ### Lake Tourism Lakeside resorts such as Maccagno with Pino and Veddasca and Angera are perfect for those seeking a bit of tranquility and relaxation. These destinations offer a wide range of outdoor activities, such as hiking, lakeside walks, and water sports. Cross-border workers can easily reach these locations for a day trip or a long weekend. ### Proximity Tourism Saronno, with its 286,674 visitors, is an example of proximity tourism. Thanks to its proximity to Milan and the presence of frequent rail connections, Saronno almost serves as a peripheral district of the big city. Cross-border workers who work in Milan can easily reach Saronno for a relaxing break or to participate in local events. ### Conclusion The Varesotto offers a wide range of tourist opportunities for cross-border workers. Whether you are passionate about culture, nature, or simply looking for a place to relax, the province of Varese has something to offer. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main tourist destinations in the Varesotto area?
The main tourist destinations in the Varesotto area include Somma Lombardo, Varese, Saronno, Maccagno with Pino and Veddasca, Angera, Vergiate, and Gallarate. Each location offers different opportunities, from cultural and natural tourism to lake tourism and proximity tourism.
How can I easily reach the Varesotto area?
The Varesotto area is easily accessible due to its proximity to Malpensa and Milan. You can reach the area by car, train, or bus. Many locations are well connected by public transport, making access easy even without a car.
What activities can I do in the Varesotto area?
In the Varesotto area, you can engage in a wide range of activities, including cultural visits, natural excursions, water sports, lakeside walks, and participation in local events. The choice depends on your preferences and the type of experience you wish to have.
