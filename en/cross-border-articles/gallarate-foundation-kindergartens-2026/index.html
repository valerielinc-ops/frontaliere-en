Gallarate: Foundation for Nursery Schools, council clash

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

Gallarate city council heats up again over the Foundation for Nursery Schools, with president's resignation and disputes over minutes.

Contesto

In brief - Foundation for Nursery Schools at the center of political debate in Gallarate - Resignation of President Marco Castoldi - Controversy over the minutes of January 15 ## Key facts - What: Clash in the municipal council regarding the Foundation for Nursery Schools - When: Monday evening, April 2026 - Where: Gallarate, Italy - Who: Councilor Massimo Gnocchi, Mayor Andrea Cassani - Amount: 7,500,000 euros of annual support over the last ten years Monday evening, the municipal council of Gallarate experienced a particularly lively phase during the “Question Time”, with a heated exchange between the opposition and the majority regarding the request for answers from Massimo Gnocchi, councilor of the civic list Obiettivo Comune Gallarate. The central theme of the debate is – once again – the Foundation for Nursery Schools and the issue of the non-admission of the urgent interrogation presented by Gnocchi. Gnocchi opened the debate expressing his displeasure for the 'non-admission' of the interrogation in the question time decided by the presidency office. According to the councilor, the decision to exclude his immediate response interrogation was unjustified and inappropriate, especially considering that the Foundation for Nursery Schools carries out an activity of great relevance for the community. Gnocchi argued that the Foundation, defined in 2004 as the 'Foundation of the Municipality of Gallarate', although of a private nature, is of public interest, with 'bodies appointed directly by the mayor and an annual support that has reached 7,500,000 euros in the last ten years'. ### The resignation of the president Gnocchi urged an urgent update on the situation, following the sudden resignation of President Marco Castoldi, made public last Thursday afternoon by Va...

Implications for Families The political clash over the Gallarate Nursery Schools Foundation has significant implications for families using the entity's services. The Foundation manages four nursery schools and kindergartens, and any instability or lack of transparency can affect the quality of services provided and the trust of families. ### The Current Situation The current situation of the Foundation is complex, with the resignation of President Marco Castoldi adding further uncertainty. Families find themselves having to face a transitional period, with the need for clear and timely answers. Gnocchi's request for an urgent update is therefore understandable, especially in light of the disruptions already caused by the Foundation's management. ### The Case of Primavera One of the critical points is the case of Primavera, which has caused significant disruptions to the families involved. The loss of a teacher at the Ronchi location is just the latest chapter in a series of problems that have put families in difficulty. The lack of a clear relaunch plan and the lack of transparency in the Foundation's management have contributed to creating a climate of uncertainty and mistrust. ### Requests for Transparency The requests for transparency from the opposition are fundamental to ensuring that the Foundation can operate efficiently and transparently.
