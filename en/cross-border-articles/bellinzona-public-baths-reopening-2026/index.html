Frequently Asked Questions
Can tickets and subscriptions be purchased online?
Yes, tickets and subscriptions can be purchased online via the [sport.bellinzona.ch](nav:azione) website. Registration via smartphone is required to use this feature.
What are the price differences between residents and non-residents?
Prices differ between residents and non-residents. For instance, a single entry for children aged 2 to 6 costs 1 franc for residents and 2 francs for non-residents. For youths aged 7 to 18, the price is 5 francs for residents and 7 francs for non-residents. Adults pay 7 francs (6 for AVS/AI) if resident and 11 francs (9 for AVS/AI) if non-resident.
What are the opening hours of the Bellinzona Public Baths?
Opening hours vary depending on the period. Until June 12th, the Public Baths are open daily from 9 AM to 7:30 PM. From June 13th to August 16th, the hours are from 9 AM to 8 PM. From August 17th to September 13th, the Public Baths are open from 9 AM to 7:30 PM.
