Ethical and safe work truck in Varese with companies, students and 3D viewers (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 5 May 2026

On Monday, May 11th in Piazza Monte Grappa in Varese, the Ethical and Safe Work Truck offers guidance and opportunities for students and the unemployed.

Contesto

In brief - Ethical and safe work truck in Varese on May 11th - Career guidance and matching with local companies - 3D visors for immersive safety experiences ## Key facts - What: Ethical and safe work truck - When: Monday, May 11th, from 9 AM to 6 PM - Where: Piazza Monte Grappa, Varese - Who: Fondazione Studi dei Consulenti del Lavoro, Ordine dei Consulenti del Lavoro di Varese, Ufficio scolastico territoriale di Varese, Comune e Provincia di Varese, Regione Lombardia, Università dell’Insubria, Camera di Commercio di Varese - Cost: Not yet specified On Monday, May 11th, in Piazza Monte Grappa in Varese, the Ethical and Safe Work Truck will make a stop. This initiative is promoted by the Fondazione Studi dei Consulenti del Lavoro in collaboration with the Provincial Council of the Ordine dei Consulenti del Lavoro di Varese. The event, primarily aimed at young people and the unemployed, will run from 9 AM to 6 PM and will offer career guidance, seminars, and matching with important local companies. ### Career guidance and opportunities On the truck, a small room with a capacity of 40 people is set up, where every 20 minutes approximately, career guidance seminars and meetings with local companies operating in the sectors of robotics, engineering services, agriculture, well-being, and web marketing will take place. This event represents a concrete opportunity for students to explore future career prospects and delve into topics related to safety and legality in the world of work. ### Immersive experiences with 3D visors In the square, two monitors will be installed for playful and educational activities, including CruciLavoro and the video game GenL, which promotes ethical work through seven different missions. What to do to participate To participate in the Ethical and Safe Work Truck event in Varese, simply go to Piazza Monte Grappa on Monday, May 11th, from 9 AM to 6 PM. No registration is required, but it is advisable to arrive a bit early to secure a spot in the seminars and meetings with companies. ### Required documents No specific documents are required to participate in the event. However, it is advisable to bring an updated CV to hand to the work consultants or the companies present. ### Step-by-step procedure 1. Go to Piazza Monte Grappa in Varese on Monday, May 11th. 2. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can participate in the Ethical and Safe Work Truck event in Varese?
The event is primarily aimed at young people and the unemployed, but it is open to anyone interested in reassessing future job prospects or exploring topics related to safety and legality in the world of work.
What can I do during the event?
During the event, you can attend orientation seminars, meet with local companies, use 3D viewers for immersive safety experiences, and consult professional career counselors to draft your resume and get career guidance.
Is it necessary to book to participate in the event?
No booking is required to participate in the event. However, it is advisable to arrive a little early to secure a place in the seminars and meetings with companies.
