Dettagli operativi

The event 'Trame in Trasformazione', held in Como, represented a unique opportunity for young artists to showcase their talent and connect with professionals in the textile sector. Over 150 participants, including students from the Liceo Artistico Paolo Carcano in Como and industry professionals, animated the event. The students worked under the guidance of their teachers to create high-quality fabric designs, demonstrating how art and textile tradition can merge innovatively. Such initiatives are fundamental to promoting textiles as an art form and supporting emerging talents in the sector. ### Sustainability and Innovation Another significant aspect of the event was the exhibition of Giada Mieli's creations, owner of J.and.C Cosmetics. Her creations, made from silk waste, represent a concrete example of how sustainability and research can dialogue with the textile world. This innovative approach not only reduces waste but also opens new creative and commercial possibilities for the sector. For example, Mieli revealed that 60% of her products are made from recycled materials, a figure that testifies to the positive impact of these practices. ### Opportunities for Young People For young artists, participating in events like 'Trame in Trasformazione' can open new doors and offer opportunities for collaboration with companies and professionals in the sector. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the goal of the ComON Creativity Sharing project?
The ComON Creativity Sharing project, launched in the early 2000s by Seterie Argenti, aims to support emerging creativity in all its forms, offering opportunities for young talents to express themselves freely and develop their skills in the textile sector.
Who participated in the 'Trame in Trasformazione' event?
The 'Trame in Trasformazione' event was attended by over 150 guests including textile sector professionals, enthusiasts, and representatives from the creative world. Among the participants were students from the Liceo Artistico Paolo Carcano and Giada Mieli, owner of J.and.C Cosmetici.
What is the importance of events like 'Trame in Trasformazione' for young artists?
Events like 'Trame in Trasformazione' offer young artists the opportunity to showcase their talent, receive valuable feedback, and create new connections in the textile sector. These events are crucial for promoting innovation and sustainability in the industry, while also providing new growth opportunities for young talents.
