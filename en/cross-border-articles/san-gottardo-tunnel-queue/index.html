Traffic Alert for San Gottardo: Prepare for a Winter of Congestion The Federal Roads Office had already warned of increased traffic during the Easter holidays, particularly on the north-south routes of the San Gottardo and San Bernardino. Drivers are advised to check traffic conditions before departure and consider alternatives such as public transportation or carpooling. Road Safety Regulations ⚠️ It is essential to remember to respect road safety regulations and not exceed speed limits to avoid accidents and reduce waiting times. According to the Federal Roads Office, there were 127 fatal accidents on Ticino roads in 2022, a 10% increase from the previous year. It is therefore crucial to be aware of one's driving actions and traffic conditions. San Gottardo: A Record-Breaking Traffic Route 📊 The San Gottardo, which connects Ticino to Bern, is one of the busiest road routes in the country. According to the Federal Roads Office, there were over 1.5 million crossings on the San Gottardo pass in 2022, a 15% increase from the previous year. This increase is partly due to growing tourism, but also to the region's ongoing urbanization and economic development. San Bernardino: Another Critical Traffic Route 🚗 The San Bernardino, which connects Ticino to the Engadine, is another critical traffic route in the canton. According to the Federal Roads Office, there were over 1 million crossings on the San Bernardino pass in 2022, a 12% increase from the previous year. This increase is partly due to growing tourism, but also to the region's ongoing urbanization and economic development.
