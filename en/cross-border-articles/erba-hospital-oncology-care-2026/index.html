Erba Hospital: massages and make-up for cancer patients (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

New project for the psycho-physical well-being of cancer patients with massages and make-up consultations. Initiative starting from September 2026.

Contesto

In brief - New project for oncological patients at Erba Hospital - Massages and make-up consultations from September 2026 - Initiative supported by Confartigianato Como and CNA Lombardia ## Key facts - What: Wellness treatments for oncological patients - When: From September 2026, Monday-Friday 9:30-11:00 - Where: Erba Hospital, Italy - Who: Professional volunteer aestheticians - Cost: Not yet specified A new project dedicated to the well-being of oncological patients, with particular attention to female patients, is launching at Erba Hospital. The initiative aims to improve the quality of time spent in the hospital, transforming waiting times between exams and therapies into moments of care, relief, and serenity. Starting in September, thanks to the collaboration with specialized professional aestheticians, targeted treatments such as facial, hand, and foot massages, as well as corrective make-up consultations, will be offered between approximately 9:30 and 11:00. The activities are carried out by specially trained volunteers and always under the supervision and coordination of medical and nursing staff. The project represents a concrete step in addressing the psycho-physical well-being of oncological patients, helping to reduce stress and anxiety related to treatment paths and offering moments of pampering and carefreeness in a particularly delicate context. The initiative is made possible thanks to the contribution and collaboration of Confartigianato Como and CNA Lombardia, which support the training of the volunteers involved in the project. ### Objectives and Benefits The initiative aims to improve the quality of life of oncological patients during their treatment journey. Dettagli operativi

Implications for Cross-Border Workers The new project at Erba Hospital represents a significant opportunity for cross-border workers who commute to Switzerland and reside in Italy, particularly for those who have family members or friends who could benefit from these services. The geographical proximity and ease of access to the hospital make this initiative particularly relevant for the cross-border community. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation Before this project, oncological patients at Erba Hospital did not have access to wellness treatments such as massages and makeup consultations. The introduction of these services represents a significant improvement in the quality of care offered, providing emotional and psychological support that can make a difference in the healing journey. ### Practical Scenarios Imagine an oncological patient visiting Erba Hospital for a series of therapies. Before starting the therapies, she can enjoy a relaxing massage or a makeup consultation to improve her appearance and mood. These moments of wellness can help her better manage the stress and anxiety related to the disease, improving her overall quality of life. ### Comparison with Other Hospitals While some hospitals in Switzerland offer psychological support and wellness services, Erba Hospital stands out for its holistic approach to the care of oncological patients. The integration of beauty and wellness treatments with traditional medical therapies represents an innovative model that could be adopted by other hospitals as well. ### Long-Term Benefits The benefits of this project are not limited to the period of hospital care. Punti chiave

What to Do to Access the Services To access the treatments offered by Erba Hospital, oncological patients must follow a simple procedure. First, it is necessary to contact the hospital to schedule an appointment. During the visit, the medical staff will assess whether the patient is suitable to participate in the wellness treatments. Once approved, the patient can book the desired treatments within the indicated time slot. ### Required Documents To participate in the treatments, patients must present a series of documents, including: - Identity card or passport - Health insurance document - Medical reports related to the oncological diagnosis - Any other documentation requested by the medical staff ### Deadlines and Timelines Treatments are available from September 2026 and are held Monday to Friday between 9:30 AM and 11:00 AM. Patients are invited to book in advance to ensure the availability of the services. ### Useful Tools For further information and to book the treatments, patients can visit the Erba Hospital website or contact the hospital directly. Additionally, it is possible to use the health cost calculator to estimate the costs associated with the treatments and plan accordingly. ### Conclusion The new project of Erba Hospital represents an important step towards a more holistic care of oncological patients. Thanks to the collaboration with Confartigianato Como and CNA Lombardia, the initiative offers wellness treatments that improve the quality of life of patients during their care journey. For cross-border workers who work in Switzerland and reside in Italy, this initiative represents a valuable opportunity to access high-quality services near home. Source: comozero.it

Frequently Asked Questions
Who can participate in the treatments offered by the Erba Hospital?
Treatments are reserved for cancer patients who attend the Erba Hospital for therapies and care. A medical report and approval from the medical staff are required to access the services.
What treatments are offered?
Treatments include facial, hand, and foot massages, as well as corrective makeup consultations. These services are designed to improve the psycho-physical well-being of patients during their treatment journey.
How can I book a treatment?
To book a treatment, you need to contact the Erba Hospital and schedule an appointment. During the visit, the medical staff will assess the patient's suitability for the well-being treatments.
