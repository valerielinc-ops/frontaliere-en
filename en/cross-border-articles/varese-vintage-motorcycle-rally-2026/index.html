Vintage motorbike rally in Varese: a leap back to the '80s and '90s

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

On Sunday, April 26, 2026, a vintage motorbike rally between Varese and Lake Maggiore. Around 100 iconic motorcycles on display.

Contesto

In brief - Vintage motorcycle rally in Varese on April 26, 2026 - Around 100 motorcycles from the '80s and '90s - Departure from Iper shopping center in Varese ## Key facts - What: Vintage motorcycle rally - When: Sunday, April 26, 2026 - Where: Varese and Lake Maggiore - Who: Motoclub dei Nostalgici - Number: Around 100 motorcycles On Sunday, April 26, 2026, a major rally dedicated to vintage motorcycles from the '80s and '90s will take place between Varese and Lake Maggiore. The event, organized by Motoclub dei Nostalgici, will attract around a hundred motorcycles that marked an era. From the legendary 125 two-strokes to the 'over' bikes that made motorcycle history, this is the perfect opportunity for enthusiasts and curious onlookers to admire models that have become true symbols up close. The meeting point is set for 9 AM at the Iper shopping center on Viale Belforte in Varese, from where the tour will depart, taking participants through Valganna. The convoy will then continue to Angera, where lunch will be served at Bar Fornace. It is in Angera that a space dedicated to the exhibition of motorcycles will be set up, offering the public the chance to observe these historic vehicles up close. The initiative aims to share a passion while contributing to the enhancement and preservation of the motor heritage. For more information, you can contact President Aldo Marcomini, Vice President Massimiliano Celsi, and Councilor and Organizer Antonio Capalbio. ### A trip down memory lane The vintage motorcycle rally in Varese is a unique opportunity to relive the excitement of a bygone era.
