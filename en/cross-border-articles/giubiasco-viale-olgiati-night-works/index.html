Frequently Asked Questions
What are the closing hours of Olgiati Avenue?
The stretch of C. Olgiati Avenue affected by the work will be closed every night from 8 PM to 6 AM the following morning, for the entire duration of the planned works for the next week.
Which road section is affected by the work?
The closure concerns the stretch that begins shortly after the intersection with Zorzi Street and extends almost to Caratti Street, in Giubiasco.
Will access to private properties be blocked?
Yes, during the closing hours (from 8 PM to 6 AM), vehicular access to private properties adjacent to the affected stretch will not be usable.
Can the work schedule change?
Yes, in case of unfavorable weather conditions, the paving work schedule may change from what was initially planned.
