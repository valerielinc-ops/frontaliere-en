2026 Ticino contractors' directory: safety & transparency

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

The 2026 directory of Ticino contractors has been published, an essential tool for clients and designers. Find out how to navigate among qualified construction companies.

Contesto

In brief - 2026 Annuaire des entrepreneurs tessinois published - Tool for choosing qualified construction companies - Cantonal regulations for work exceeding 30,000 francs ## Key facts - What: 2026 Annuaire des entrepreneurs tessinois - When: April 29, 2026 - Where: Canton Ticino - Who: SSIC Ticino Section - Amount: 30,000 francs (threshold for construction work) The 2026 edition of the Annuaire des entrepreneurs tessinois has been published, a tool designed to support clients and designers in choosing companies listed in the cantonal register. Cantonal regulations require that for public and private construction work exceeding 30,000 francs - a threshold that drops to 10,000 francs for specialist work such as reinforcing steel installation, formwork for concrete, masonry construction, and subbase laying - only companies listed in the register can be used. Otherwise, sanctions and site closures are possible. The annuaire lists construction companies and specialist operators registered in the cantonal register, accompanied by informative contributions about the sector. Companies are organized by district and municipality, facilitating the identification of local partners. To be admitted to the register, available online at www.ti.ch/albo, companies must meet precise professional and personal requirements. ### Companies and requirements The publication also includes a list of companies associated with the SSIC Ticino Section, whose admission criteria are even more restrictive than those required by the cantonal register, 'thus representing an additional guarantee of quality for clients.' Dettagli operativi

The 2026 Directory of Ticino contractors represents a fundamental step to ensure safety and transparency in the construction sector of the Canton Ticino. For clients and designers, this tool offers a comprehensive overview of qualified construction companies, facilitating the choice of reliable partners for their projects. ### Practical Implications The cantonal regulation, which requires registration in the Register for works exceeding 30,000 francs, underscores the importance of relying on companies that meet high standards of quality and safety. This not only protects clients from potential risks but also ensures that construction sites comply with current regulations, avoiding sanctions and blockages. Companies registered in the Register must meet rigorous professional and personal requirements, which include demonstrating technical competence and adherence to ethical standards. This selection process ensures that only the most qualified companies can operate in the Ticino construction sector, offering clients the certainty of working with reliable professionals. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation Before the publication of this directory, clients and designers had to face a more complex search to find qualified construction companies. The lack of a centralized list made it difficult to identify reliable partners, increasing the risk of encountering companies non-compliant with regulations. With the introduction of the 2026 Directory, this process has become much simpler and more transparent. ### Concrete Scenarios Imagine a client who needs to carry out a major construction project in Bellinzona. Punti chiave

For clients and designers who wish to use the information contained in the 2026 Yearbook of Ticino contractors, it is possible to request a free copy by contacting the SSIC Ticino Section. The procedure is simple and quick, ensuring access to an essential tool for choosing qualified construction companies. ### Procedure for the request 1. Contact the SSIC Ticino Section: Send a request via email to info@ssic-ti.ch or call 091 825 54 23. 2. Specify your needs: Indicate whether you want a printed or digital copy of the yearbook. 3. Receive the copy: The SSIC will send the requested copy within a few days. ### Using the yearbook Once you receive a copy of the yearbook, you can consult the list of qualified construction companies, organized by district and municipality. This facilitates the search for local partners, ensuring a more targeted choice in line with the project's needs. ### Checking requirements Before choosing a company, it is important to verify that it meets the professional and personal requirements of the Cantonal Register. The yearbook provides detailed information about each company, including admission criteria and specific qualifications. ### Reporting irregularities In case of suspected irregularities on construction sites, you can use the practical guide included in the yearbook to report the situation to the competent control authorities. This helps maintain high standards of safety and quality in the Ticino construction sector. ### Useful tools To further explore information about the yearbook and qualified construction companies, you can consult the official website of the Canton of Ticino at www.ti.ch/albo. Frequently Asked Questions
How can I obtain a copy of the 2026 Directory of Ticino entrepreneurs?
To obtain a copy of the directory, you can contact the SSIC Ticino Section via email at info@ssic-ti.ch or call the number 091 825 54 23. The copy will be sent free of charge within a few days.
What are the requirements for companies listed in the Cantonal Register?
Companies must meet specific professional and personal requirements, which include demonstrating technical competence and adhering to ethical standards. These requirements are detailed in the directory.
What should I do in case of irregularities on construction sites?
The directory includes a practical guide for reporting any suspicious situations to the competent control authorities. Follow the instructions provided to ensure that the work is carried out in compliance with regulations.
