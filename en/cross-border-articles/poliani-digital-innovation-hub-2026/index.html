Poliani reappointed at Digital Innovation Hub (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 6 May 2026

The entrepreneur will lead the body for the 2026-2028 term with the goal of guiding SMEs towards artificial intelligence

Contesto

In brief - Stefano Poliani confirmed as president of the DIH of Confindustria Lombardia - 2026-2028 term to guide SMEs towards AI - 2 million euros of services provided and 350 companies involved ## Key facts - What: Confirmation of Stefano Poliani as president of the DIH - When: 2026-2028 term - Where: Lombardy - Who: Confindustria Lombardia - Amount: 2 million euros of services provided Stefano Poliani has been confirmed as president of the Digital Innovation Hub (DIH) of Confindustria Lombardia for the 2026-2028 term. The decision was made by the Executive Council following the renewal of the leadership by the General Assembly of Members on April 14. Poliani will lead the organization with the aim of guiding small and medium-sized enterprises towards the new frontiers of artificial intelligence. The balance of the recently concluded term is positive: the DIH has provided services worth approximately 2 million euros, involving over 350 companies. In total, more than 480 interventions have been carried out, with the distribution of over 500,000 euros of public funds in the territory to support local realities in the complex path of digitalization. This result was made possible also thanks to the active participation in projects related to the PNRR, such as M.I.A. Lombardia and DAMAS. ### The new challenges The program for the next term focuses on cutting-edge technologies. At the center of Poliani's strategy are three macro-areas: cybersecurity, the adoption of artificial intelligence, and the new opportunities of European programs that will follow the PNRR season. Dettagli operativi

Future Goals and Territorial Presence Beyond the technological challenges, the new mandate will focus on strengthening governance through the Technical and Scientific Committee and on the development of the 'AI Adoption' path, designed to concretely integrate artificial intelligence into the processes of SMEs. Fundamental will remain the physical presence in the territory, which in the last period has seen the enhancement of the 9 'Territorial Antennas' and the organization of almost a hundred meetings and events to bring digital services directly to the doorstep of Lombard companies. ### Implications for Cross-Border Workers The reconfirmation of Poliani and the DIH's plan for the next triennium could have important implications for cross-border workers employed in Lombardy. The adoption of advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence and cybersecurity could open new job and training opportunities for cross-border workers. Furthermore, collaboration with regional institutions could facilitate access to funds and resources for companies, improving working conditions and career prospects for cross-border workers. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation In the last three years, the DIH has radically transformed its role, shifting from a mere cultural facilitator to a real operational arm of regional and national industrial policies. This change has allowed the provision of services worth approximately 2 million euros and the involvement of over 350 companies. Poliani's new mandate aims to consolidate these results and further expand the range of services for Lombard SMEs. ### Future Scenarios If the DIH manages to achieve its goals, Lombard manufacturing could become a national reference point for the adoption of advanced technologies. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main objectives of the DIH for the 2026-2028 triennium?
The main objectives of the DIH for the 2026-2028 triennium are the promotion of cybersecurity, the adoption of artificial intelligence, and access to new opportunities from European programs that will follow the PNRR season. The goal is to make the DIH a reference point for Lombardy's manufacturing sector.
How can I participate in the events and workshops organized by the DIH?
You can participate in the events and workshops organized by the DIH by visiting the official website and subscribing to the newsletter to receive periodic updates. You can find the event calendar on the DIH's website.
What are the advantages for cross-border workers who work in Lombardy?
Cross-border workers who work in Lombardy can take advantage of the opportunities offered by the DIH, such as training on advanced technologies, access to funds and resources, and participation in events and workshops. These advantages can improve working conditions and career prospects.
