Wind Power at the Gotthard Pass: The Canton Launches Public Consultation for Expansion (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 20 March 2026

The Canton of Ticino has initiated a public consultation for the expansion of the wind farm at the Gotthard Pass.

Contesto

TL;DR - Ticino expands Gotthard wind farm with 2 new turbines. - Public consultation runs March 25-May 8, 2026. - Goal: increase renewable energy, reduce non-renewable dependence. ## Key facts - Cosa: Expansion of Gotthard wind farm with 2 new turbines. - Quando: Public consultation: March 25, 2026 - May 8, 2026. - Dove: Gotthard Pass, Canton of Ticino, Switzerland. - Chi: Canton of Ticino and Ticino Electricity Company (AET). - Produzione attuale: Up to approximately 13 GWh per year. - Obiettivo: Increase wind energy production and reduce dependence on non-renewable energy. - Piattaforma: consultazionipd.ti.ch. The Canton of Ticino has decided to expand the wind farm at the Gotthard Pass. The public consultation was launched on March 25, 2026, and will conclude on May 8, 2026. The project involves the construction of two new wind turbines similar to those already in place. The procedure will be entirely digital and will take place through the online platform dedicated to the consultation at consultazionipd.ti.ch. The expansion of the wind farm is necessary to meet growing demands for renewable energy and to ensure the security of the electricity supply. The wind farm at the Gotthard Pass was inaugurated five years ago and currently produces up to approximately 13 GWh per year. The new expansion will be an important step forward in the realization of the Confederation's 2050 Energy Strategy and the Canton's Energy and Climate Plan. The environmental impact assessment has analyzed the possible impacts on the territory and has indicated the possibility of coexisting with the protection of the landscape, monumental and natural components of the pass, as well as the protection of birds and bats. Dettagli operativi

The expansion project of the wind farm at the Gotthard Pass involves the construction of two new wind turbines similar to those already in place. The expansion of the wind farm is necessary to meet growing demands for renewable energy and to ensure the security of the electricity supply. The wind farm at the Gotthard Pass was inaugurated five years ago and currently produces up to approximately 13 GWh per year. The new expansion will be an important step forward in the realization of the Confederation's 2050 Energy Strategy and the Canton's Energy and Climate Plan. The expansion of the wind farm at the Gotthard Pass has been financed by the Canton of Ticino and the Ticino Electricity Company (AET). The goal is to increase wind energy production and reduce dependence on non-renewable energy. The public consultation is an opportunity to involve citizens and associations in the decision-making process and to collect observations and proposals to improve the design and implementation of the project. The State Council of the Canton of Ticino has decided to launch the public consultation for the expansion of the wind farm at the Gotthard Pass after evaluating the need to increase wind energy production and reduce dependence on non-renewable energy. Frequently Asked Questions
When does the public consultation end for the extension of the wind farm to Passo del San Gottardo?
The consultation will end on 8 May 2026.
How much will wind energy production increase with new turbines at the San Gottardo Pass?
The current production is about 13 GWh per year. Although the exact value of the increase is not specified, the new turbines will be similar for production and technical characteristics to the existing ones, therefore an increase proportional to their quantity, i.e. two new turbines.
How can I participate in the public consultation for the extension of the wind farm to Passo del San Gottardo?
Citizens can participate in public consultation through the online platform dedicated to consultationspd.ti.ch or by writing directly to the authorities. The consultation is open from 25 March 2026 to 8 May 2026.
What are the environmental protection measures envisaged for the new wind farm at Passo del San Gottardo?
The preliminary environmental evaluation indicated the possibility of a cohabitation with the protection of the landscape, the monumental and natural components of the step, as well as the protection of avifauna and chiroptera. The protection, mitigation and compensation measures must be integrated into the subsequent design phases.
Who finances the expansion of the wind farm at Passo del San Gottardo?
The extension of the wind farm is funded by the Canton of Ticino and the Ticinese Electric Company (AET). The aim is to increase wind energy production and reduce dependence on non-renewable energy.
