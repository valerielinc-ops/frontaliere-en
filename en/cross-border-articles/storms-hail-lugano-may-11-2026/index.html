In brief - Level 3 weather alert for Luganese and Bellinzonese - Hail in Capriasca, roads whitened - Risk of landslides, flooding, and uprooted trees ## Key facts - What: Level 3 weather alert - When: 11 May 2026 - Where: Luganese and Bellinzonese - Who: MeteoSwiss - Amount: Not specified MeteoSwiss has issued a level 3 alert for the Luganese and Bellinzonese regions. The federal agency warns of the risk of landslides on steep slopes, sudden flash floods in torrents, flooding, hail damage, broken branches, uprooted trees, and lightning. Precipitation intensified within minutes, turning into hailstones that whitened roads and surfaces in Capriasca. The bad weather is affecting the entire canton, with particular criticalities in the Luganese and Bellinzonese areas. Hail has caused damage to crops and local infrastructure, while heavy rains have increased the risk of landslides and flooding. ### Implications for cross-border workers Cross-border workers commuting to Luganese and Bellinzonese should pay particular attention to adverse weather conditions.
