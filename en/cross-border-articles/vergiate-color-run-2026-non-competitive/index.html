Vergiate turns colorful: the non-competitive Color Run returns for all ages

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

On Sunday, May 24, 2026, Vergiate hosts the Color Run, a 4 km race open to all ages. Registrations open until May 10.

Contesto

In brief - Non-competitive 4 km run in Vergiate - Open to all ages - Registrations until May 10th - Cost: 10 euros ## Key facts - What: Color Run, non-competitive run - When: Sunday, May 24, 2026 - Where: Vergiate, Italy - Who: Organized by Oratorio San Giovanni Bosco with the patronage of the Municipality - Fee: Registration cost 10 euros Vergiate is preparing for an explosion of joy with the new edition of the Color Run, a non-competitive 4 km run that will cross the city streets. The event, organized by Oratorio San Giovanni Bosco with the patronage of the Municipality, is open to all age groups: a perfect opportunity for families, young people, and sports enthusiasts to enjoy a carefree day. Last year, the event saw the participation of over 300 people, including many residents from neighboring towns such as Besnate and Somma Lombardo. ### Day Program The event will take place in the afternoon of Sunday, May 24, according to the following schedule: - 4:00 PM: Pick up race kit at the Oratory of Vergiate. - 5:00 PM: Official start from the chapel of Sant'Eurosia. - 6:00 PM: Expected arrival at the Oratory (via Enrico Locatelli). - 6:30 PM: Opening of food stands for a moment of refreshment and final celebration. ### Registrations and Race Kit To participate, you must register by Sunday, May 10, by scanning the QR Code on the official poster. The registration fee is 10 euros, to be paid directly when picking up the kit on the day of the event. Each participant will receive a special race kit that includes: - A personalized bag. - Colored powders to throw during the race. - A water bottle. ### Operational Checklist - Documents: Bring a valid ID. - Clothing: Comfortable clothes and sneakers. - Payment: 10 euros in cash or card when picking up the kit. - Health: Chec...

Implications for cross-border workers The Color Run in Vergiate represents a unique opportunity for cross-border workers who commute to Switzerland and reside in the border area. This non-competitive event offers a moment of relaxation and socialization, allowing them to better know the local community and spend a day filled with fun. Participating in local events like the Color Run can help integrate these workers better into their communities of residence, improving their quality of life and creating stronger social bonds. > "Events like these are fundamental to creating a sense of belonging among cross-border workers and local communities" — declared a representative of the Municipality of Vergiate. ### Comparisons with similar events Events like the Color Run are becoming increasingly popular across Europe, but the one in Vergiate stands out for its openness to all ages and its family-friendly atmosphere. Frequently Asked Questions
When is Color Run in Vergiate and how can I enroll?
The Color Run in Vergiate will be held on Sunday 24 May 2026. To sign up, you need to frame the QR Code on the official poster by May 10th. The cost is 10 euros, to be paid to the collection of the kit on the day of the event.
What does the Color Run race kit include in Vergiate?
The race kit includes a custom bag, colored powders to be launched during the course and a bottle of water. You can pick it up at 16.00 at the Oratory of Vergiate on the day of the event.
What are the differences between the Color Run in Vergiate and the Lugano Color?
The Color Run in Vergiate costs 10 euros, while the one in Lugano costs 15 euros. Both are open to all ages, but Vergiate offers a more community feel and a lower cost of membership.
What should I bring to the Color Run in Vergiate?
You must bring a valid ID, comfortable clothes, and sneakers. The payment of 10 euros can be made in cash or by card when collecting the kit. Check for any allergies to colored powders.
What happens if it rains on the day of the Color Run in Vergiate?
In case of bad weather, the event will be postponed to a later date. It is advisable to check the official cpvergiate.it website for any updates.
