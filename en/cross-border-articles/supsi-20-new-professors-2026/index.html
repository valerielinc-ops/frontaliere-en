Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

The conferment ceremony took place in Mendrisio on April 28, 2026. 12 senior appointments were also awarded.

The positions, assigned between December 2024 and February 2026, concern the four departments of SUPSI and two affiliated schools: the Fernfachhochschule Schweiz and the Scuola universitaria di Musica del Conservatorio della Svizzera italiana. The ceremony, presided over by the Council President Giovanni Merlini and the Director General Franco Gervasoni, saw the participation of the directors of the departments and affiliated schools involved. This initiative is part of the academic career review launched in 2021, with the aim of enhancing professional paths and strengthening academic recognition. ### Implications for cross-border workers The new appointments represent a significant step towards strengthening the skills and project capabilities of SUPSI. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the requirements to work as a professor at SUPSI?
The requirements to work as a professor at SUPSI include a master's degree or a PhD in a relevant field, relevant professional experience, and a strong ability in research and teaching. SUPSI also evaluates language skills and the ability to work in a multicultural environment.
How can I apply for a professor position at SUPSI?
To apply for a professor position at SUPSI, you need to visit the official SUPSI website and consult the available job offers. Follow the application instructions and submit your curriculum vitae and a cover letter.
