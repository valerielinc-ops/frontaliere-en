The alarm was immediately raised to the Mendrisiotto firefighters, who intervened on site with their special chemical defense unit. The area was quickly isolated, and the leak was identified and repaired. Three people, all maintenance workers, were attended by SAM rescuers and the Lugano Red Cross, who promptly arrived at the scene. However, none of them required hospital admission. ### Rescuers' intervention The SAM rescuers and the Lugano Red Cross intervened promptly at the scene. Despite the seriousness of the situation, none of the maintenance workers needed hospital admission. The ammonia leak was quickly identified and repaired, thus avoiding further complications. ### Safety measures The area affected by the ammonia leak was immediately isolated by the Mendrisiotto firefighters, who used their special chemical defense unit. This allowed the situation to be contained and ensured the safety of all those present. ### Implications for cross-border workers For cross-border workers who work at or frequent the Chiasso ice rink, it is important to be aware of the safety procedures in case of similar emergencies.
