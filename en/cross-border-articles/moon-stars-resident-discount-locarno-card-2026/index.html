Frequently Asked Questions
Who can purchase discounted tickets for the Moon&Stars Festival 2026?
Only residents of Locarno holding the Locarno Card. Each cardholder may purchase a maximum of two tickets for personal use. Sales are exclusively reserved for residents and are not open to cross-border workers who do not reside in Locarno.
What is the price of discounted tickets with the Locarno Card?
Tickets for evening concerts cost 75 francs (plus service fees), while the Opening Night on July 9 is priced at 45 francs (plus service fees). These prices are reserved for Locarno Card holders and are only valid for online purchases within the indicated deadlines.
Until when can I purchase discounted tickets with the Locarno Card?
Discounted ticket sales will open on April 22, 2026, at 12:00 and close on May 29, 2026, at 23:59, or until stocks run out. It is advisable to purchase tickets as soon as possible to avoid missing out.
Can I resell or transfer tickets purchased with the Locarno Card?
The source does not specify whether tickets are transferable or resellable. However, since each ticket is linked to the personal code of the Locarno Card, it is likely that they cannot be transferred to other individuals. It is recommended to check the festival’s official sales terms before proceeding.
What happens if discounted tickets sell out before May 29?
If discounted tickets run out early, sales will automatically close and it will no longer be possible to purchase discounted tickets with the Locarno Card. Residents may still buy tickets at full price during the general sale, though it is not specified when this will begin.
