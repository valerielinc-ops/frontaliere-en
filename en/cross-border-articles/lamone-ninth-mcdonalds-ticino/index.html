Lamone's ninth McDonald's in Ticino (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 5 May 2026

New opening in Lamone, 35 jobs and 85% local ingredients. Here's what changes for cross-border workers

Contesto

In brief - New McDonald's in Lamone, ninth in Ticino - 35 jobs and 85% Swiss ingredients - First opening in 15 years ## Key facts - What: Opening of new McDonald's - When: Not specified - Where: Lamone, Via Serta - Who: Licensee Luca Sergi - Amount: 35 jobs On December 27, 1993, the first Big Mac menu was served in Ticino at the McDonald's restaurant in Coldrerio. Today, after 30 years, the American fast food chain opens its ninth outlet in the Canton, in Lamone, on the cantonal road Via Serta. This is the first new opening of a McDonald's restaurant in Ticino in the last 15 years. The local licensee is Luca Sergi, who manages a total of six McDonald's restaurants in the Sottoceneri. The three restaurants in the Sopraceneri are managed by the second Ticino licensee, Trevor Meier. The new restaurant with McDrive creates 35 jobs in the region and generates orders for the local economy, as over 85% of the ingredients come from Swiss partners. With the new outlet in Lamone, the company now manages 191 restaurants in Switzerland – 9 of which in Ticino. ### Implications for cross-border workers The opening of a new McDonald's in Lamone represents an opportunity for cross-border workers in the area. With 35 new jobs, the restaurant offers new employment opportunities for those who live in Italy and work in Switzerland. Moreover, the use of local ingredients for over 85% of the menu means that cross-border workers can enjoy meals prepared with Swiss quality products, often at competitive prices. For cross-border workers in the catering sector, the opening of a new McDonald's can also represent a new career opportunity. With the possibility of working in an international and dynamic environment, cross-border workers can acquire new skills and improve their career prospects...
