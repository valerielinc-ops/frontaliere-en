In brief - New healthcare facility in Bellinzona - Procedures and same-day return home - Open to the public on May 9, 2026 ## Key facts - What: New Bellinzona Castello Medical Center - When: Open to the public on May 9, 2026 - Where: Bellinzona, Canton Ticino - Who: Management added by Jacopo Dandrea - Amount: 2,500 square meters of surface area BELLINZONA - 'You shouldn’t think of it as being in a hospital. You arrive, have the procedure, and then go home.' These are the words of Manuela Androtti, a patient recently operated on her right wrist and elbow, describing her experience at the new Bellinzona Castello Medical Center. The healthcare facility, opened in September 2025, will welcome the public on Saturday, May 9, with a day dedicated to visits and exploring the spaces. Located in the heart of the city, just steps away from the historic center, the new Center occupies a 2,500 square meter building completely renovated and modernized over the past two years. A reality aiming to become a reference point for the Sopraceneri, mainly thanks to a feature considered practically unique in the region. 'The particularity of this Medical Center is certainly the presence of an operating block with two rooms dedicated to outpatient surgery,' explains Jacopo Dandrea, deputy director of the Bellinzona Castello Medical Center. 'Currently, the rooms are mainly used for procedures related to hand, wrist, and elbow surgery, as well as everything concerning ophthalmology. However, the structure is already prepared to host other surgical specialties. This is a rather unique reality in the Sopraceneri, especially because it is not directly connected to a hospital.' Advantages for cross-border workers The new Centromedico Bellinzona Castello represents a significant opportunity for cross-border workers in Ticino. The possibility of undergoing outpatient surgical procedures without having to stay in the hospital offers a considerable practical advantage. Cross-border workers can return home on the same day, minimizing recovery times and absences from work. ### Comparison with traditional facilities Unlike traditional hospital facilities, Centromedico Bellinzona Castello offers a lighter and less traumatic experience. Patients do not have to face long hospital stays but can quickly return to their daily activities. This is particularly advantageous for cross-border workers, who often have to manage work and family commitments on both sides of the border. ### Procedures and services offered Centromedico Bellinzona Castello offers a wide range of services, including specialist consultations in ophthalmology, orthopedics, and multispecialty care, as well as radiology, ergotherapy, and physiotherapy services. The facility is equipped with two operating rooms dedicated to outpatient surgery, with a particular focus on hand, wrist, elbow surgery, and ophthalmology. ### Customization of the experience One of the most innovative aspects of Centromedico Bellinzona Castello is the ability to customize the patient's experience. Patients can choose the music, images, and colors of the environment during the procedure, making the day of the operation less traumatic.
