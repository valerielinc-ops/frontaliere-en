Locarno 2026 Food Truck Festival: 5,000 visitors in Piazza Grande (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 6 May 2026

Over 5,000 visitors attended the 8th edition of the Locarno Food Truck Festival, featuring street food, free concerts, and plenty of sunshine.

In brief - 8th edition of Food Truck Festival Locarno - Over 5,000 visitors in Piazza Grande - 22 food trucks from Ticino and beyond the Gotthard ## Key facts - What: 8th edition of Food Truck Festival Locarno - When: April 30 - May 3, 2026 - Where: Piazza Grande, Locarno - Who: Organized by local committee - Attendance: Over 5,000 visitors The 8th edition of the Food Truck Festival Locarno concluded with great participation, animating the beautiful Piazza Grande from Thursday, April 30, to Sunday, May 3, 2026. The square was transformed into a 'village of taste on the road' with free concerts and entertainment for children. For four days, 22 food trucks from all over Ticino and beyond the Gotthard offered gastronomic specialties for all tastes, and according to organizers' estimates, over 5,000 visitors reached the city center. ### Musical and drink novelties On the stage at the center of Piazza Grande, the Ticinese band 'Hippie Fish' performed on Thursday evening and 'Dan e i suoi Fratelli' on Saturday evening; on Friday, Claire McDermott Imesch, with her Irish sounds, and DJ Magenta alternated to make the guests sing and dance. The 'buvette degli Amis,' sensitive to local novelties, this year launched the 'Mezz & Mezz.' The Personico Fizzy company, in fact, bottled the traditional mixture of wine and grotto soda and presented it to the public during the event in Piazza Grande. ### Implications for cross-border workers The event attracted visitors not only from the canton of Ticino but also from nearby Lombardy, offering an opportunity for cross-border workers to enjoy a weekend of entertainment and gastronomy.
