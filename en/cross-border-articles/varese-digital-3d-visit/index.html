Varese goes digital: explore the city in 3D with a click

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

You can explore Varese in 3D thanks to the new portal Varesedigitale.it. Find out how to visit the city virtually.

Contesto

In brief - Varese is now explorable in 3D online - New portal Varesedigitale.it - Immersive experience with virtual reality ## Key facts - What: New digital portal to explore Varese in 3D - When: Official launch on April 29, 2026 - Where: Varese, Italy - Who: Coordinated by Mauro Turci and Giacomo Lenoci - Amount: Not yet specified Varese has taken a significant step towards the digital future with the launch of the portal Varesedigitale.it. This innovative project allows you to explore the city in 3D, offering an immersive experience that combines culture and technology. The portal was officially presented on April 29, 2026, by local coordinator Mauro Turci together with Giacomo Lenoci, promoter of the initiative born in Puglia. Varese is the first city to be included in this national circuit dedicated to the enhancement of the territory through 3D technology. At the presentation in Sala Montanari were also present the vice-mayor of Varese Ivana Perusin and the regional councilor Giuseppe Licata. The site Varesedigitale.it offers an advanced Virtual Tour that allows a 360-degree exploration of the city's symbolic places. It is not just a simple photo gallery, but an interactive experience where the user can 'walk' virtually among the symbolic places of Varese. You can admire the details of the child mummy at Villa Mirabello, explore the parks of the historic villas or immerse yourself in the silence of the chapels of the Sacro Monte. ### Immersive experience Using virtual reality viewers, the experience becomes total, giving the visitor the exact feeling of being inside the monuments, being able to observe paintings and finds with surprising definition. Dettagli operativi

The virtual 3D tour experience of Varese offers numerous advantages for tourists and residents. Firstly, it allows you to explore the city in an interactive and immersive way, without having to physically move. This is particularly useful for those who want to plan a visit or for those who wish to rediscover the symbolic places of Varese comfortably from home. ### Advantages for tourists Tourists can use the portal to get a taste of what Varese has to offer before planning a visit. They can explore the details of the child mummy at Villa Mirabello, take a virtual stroll through the parks of the historic villas, or immerse themselves in the silence of the chapels of the Sacro Monte. This helps create a more engaging and informed experience, making the real visit even more appreciated. ### Advantages for local businesses For local businesses, the portal represents a unique opportunity to increase visibility and attract new customers. Thanks to geolocation and dynamic buttons, businesses can directly contact potential customers, display photo galleries of their products, and promote their services. This facilitates the meeting between demand and supply, creating a digitally advanced environment that fosters neighborhood commerce and local industry. ### Implications for tourism The Varese Digitale project has important implications for the tourism sector. By offering an advanced digital platform, Varese can attract a wider and more diverse audience, increasing the flow of tourists and improving the overall experience. Furthermore, the portal can be used as a territorial marketing tool, promoting not only cultural sites but also commercial and industrial activities in the city. ### Future of the project After the Varese launch, the industrial plan envisages the digitaliz...

Punti chiave

To make the most of the Varesedigitale.it portal, it's important to follow a few simple steps. First, access the Varesedigitale.it website and explore the various sections dedicated to the symbolic places of Varese. Use the virtual map to navigate through the different points of interest and discover the commercial and industrial activities of the city. ### How to use the portal 1. Explore the symbolic places: Use the virtual map to visit the symbolic places of Varese, such as Villa Mirabello, the Sacro Monte, and the parks of the historic villas. 2. Discover local activities: Browse through the commercial and industrial activities present on the map and use the dynamic buttons to get in direct contact with the companies. 3. Book services: Use the dynamic buttons to book services such as tennis courts or tables at the restaurant. 4. Share your experience: Share your virtual experience on social networks and invite friends and family to explore Varese in 3D. ### Useful tools To delve deeper, you can use the travel cost calculator to plan your visit to Varese. Additionally, consult the event calendar to discover the events and events scheduled in the city. ### Conclusion The Varesedigitale.it portal represents an innovative opportunity to explore Varese in an interactive and immersive way. Whether you are a tourist or a resident, this digital tool allows you to discover the city in a completely new way. Take advantage of this technology to plan your visit, discover local activities, and live a unique experience.
