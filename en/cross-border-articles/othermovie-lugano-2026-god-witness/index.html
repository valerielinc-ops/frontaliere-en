'God as my Witness' wins OtherMovie Lugano 2026

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

The winning film of the OtherMovie Lugano 2026 festival tackles the delicate issue of sexual abuse with over 1,200 attendees and three sold-out screenings

Contesto

In brief - OtherMovie Lugano 2026 registers over 1,200 attendees - 'God as my Witness' wins the festival - Three sold-out events confirm the festival's strength ## Key facts - What: OtherMovie Lugano 2026 Winner - When: 17-25 April 2026 - Where: Lugano - Who: Director Lindsay Q. Pitre - Attendance: 1,200 The 2026 edition of OtherMovie Lugano concluded with great success, registering over 1,200 attendees at the events and three sold-out screenings. The winning film, 'God as my Witness – Lo giuro a Dio', tackled the delicate issue of sexual abuse, one of the central themes of the event. The fifteenth edition of the festival, held from 17 to 25 April, focused on the theme of change. The film by American director Lindsay Q. Pitre was awarded for its incisive investigation into the testimonies of victims and the institutional responsibilities within the Catholic Archdiocese of New Orleans. The jury, chaired by Matteo Balsamo, praised the film for its stylistic solidity, effective editing, and careful photography, calling it a universal appeal for justice. In the L’Incontro section, dedicated to international feature films, 'Mamma Yamuna' by Vito Robbiani and 'Brides' by Nadia Fall were also awarded. The former addressed the issue of environmental pollution, while the latter told the story of two Muslim teenagers fleeing difficult family contexts. Among the short films, 'Tenére, tenére, tenére… fino alla fine' by Edoardo Nerboni won in the OtherSwiss Short category for the depth of its narrative and emotional intensity. The award for the best international short film went to the Spanish 'Tribù' by Carlos Gomez Trigo, while the award for best direction was given to 'Phosgene Tears to Old Kassandra' by Alessandra Pescetta, made with the help of artificial intelligence. ###...
