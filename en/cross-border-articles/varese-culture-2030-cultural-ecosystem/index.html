Varese Culture 2030: building a cultural ecosystem

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

Varese Culture 2030 is a project to reshape the cultural future of Varese province, with a shared strategic plan and a digital platform for mapping local heritage.

Contesto

In brief - Varese Cultura 2030 redesigns the cultural future of the province of Varese - Digital platform operational by December 2026 - Enhancement of Santa Caterina del Sasso as a gateway to the territory ## Key facts - What: Varese Cultura 2030 project - When: Ongoing, digital platform operational by December 2026 - Where: Province of Varese - Who: Province of Varese and local partnership - Amount: Not yet specified Varese Cultura 2030 is a complex and shared project aimed at redesigning the cultural future of the province of Varese. Supported by Fondazione Cariplo and led by the Province of Varese together with a wide local partnership, the project aims to build a common strategy to overcome the fragmentation and isolation of the local cultural system. Paolo Landini of the Province of Varese introduced the work by emphasizing the importance of abandoning localism and contributing with systemic ideas. Among the main directions of the project are the construction of a shared strategic plan for provincial culture, a digital platform for mapping local cultural heritage operational by December 2026, the enhancement of main cultural hubs, the strengthening of Santa Caterina del Sasso as a gateway to the territory, and the development of cultural itineraries integrated with landscape, environment, and sustainable mobility. ### Ongoing actions Among the ongoing actions, there is also an experimental shuttle bus service to connect the cultural sites of Lake Maggiore. Varese is aiming for the Capital of Culture, with a challenge that starts from Villa Andrea for the provincial cultural system. Dettagli operativi

After the framing phase, the participants divided into eight thematic tables: communication and cultural storytelling, accessible and inclusive culture, skills development and capacity building, cultural demand development, coordination of supply, economic sustainability, connections with tourism and landscape and mobility, and environmental sustainability. Each table was entrusted to an expert facilitator, tasked with translating the critical issues that emerged in March into concrete objectives and practical actions. The next appointment is scheduled for May 18, online, with a focus on the economic, environmental, and social sustainability of culture. On May 25, there will be a meeting dedicated to presenting local realities and networking. ### Practical Implications The Varese Cultura 2030 project has significant implications for the territory and its residents. The creation of an integrated cultural ecosystem can attract more tourists and improve the quality of life for residents, offering new opportunities for participation and engagement. The digital platform for mapping local cultural heritage will be a fundamental tool for enhancing the cultural resources of the territory and making them accessible to a wider audience. Furthermore, the enhancement of Santa Caterina del Sasso as a gateway to the territory can promote the development of sustainable and high-quality tourism, integrating culture, landscape, and mobility. ### Future Scenarios With the implementation of the Varese Cultura 2030 project, an increase in cultural tourism in the province of Varese is expected, with economic benefits for local businesses and an improvement in the quality of life for residents.
