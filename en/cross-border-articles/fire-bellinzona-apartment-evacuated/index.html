According to initial reports, the flames originated from the kitchen of a ground-floor apartment. The cause of the fire is still under investigation. As a precaution, around ten people present in the building were temporarily evacuated, while emergency services quickly intervened to contain the situation. On the scene were agents from the Cantonal Police, supported by the Bellinzona City Police, rescuers from Croce Verde, and firefighters, who managed to extinguish the fire before it spread beyond the affected area. A woman experienced mild symptoms of intoxication, but her condition is not concerning. ### Rapid response from emergency services The emergency services responded promptly and were well-coordinated. Firefighters worked tirelessly to extinguish the flames and prevent further damage. The police managed the evacuation of the building's residents, ensuring everyone's safety.
