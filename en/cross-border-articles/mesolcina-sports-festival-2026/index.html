Outdoor sports festival returns to Mesolcina (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 6 May 2026

From May 9 to 17, 2026, Mesolcina hosts the sixth edition of MisoXperience with kayaking, bouldering, and paragliding.

Contesto

In brief - Outdoor sports festival from May 9 to 17, 2026 in Mesolcina - Kayaking, bouldering, and paragliding among the disciplines - Events for families and sports enthusiasts ## Key facts - What: MisoXperience outdoor sports festival - When: May 9 to 17, 2026 - Where: Piani di Verdabbio, Cama, Mesolcina - Who: Festival organizers - Cost: Not yet specified The sixth edition of MisoXperience, the outdoor sports festival in Mesolcina, will take place from May 9 to 17, 2026, at Piani di Verdabbio, a hamlet of Cama. The valley will transform into a lively meeting point for outdoor sports enthusiasts from all over Switzerland. The festival brings together a community of athletes who share a passion for nature and movement. With disciplines such as kayaking, bouldering, and paragliding, as well as a varied multisport offer, a unique experience is created for everyone, from beginners to more ambitious athletes. ### Program and Activities The festival is completed by a suggestive program with music, entertainment, and a convivial atmosphere. Accommodation is in tents, vans, or camper vans, in the middle of nature next to the Moesa River. The highlight is the national final of Boater-X on Saturday, May 16, from 9 to 11 am, where four boats start simultaneously from a ramp and make their way through a challenging course. The two fastest advance to the next round, offering an action-packed spectacle that can be followed live from the riverbank. ### Main Events The bouldering sites in the area offer climbing experiences in a suggestive nature, suitable for beginners and experts. What makes the event even more exciting is the bouldering competition in parallel on Saturday, May 16, on the main stage from 2:30 pm. This type of event promotes integration and family well-being, offering a shared experience that can strengthen bonds between family members. ### Networking Opportunities For sports enthusiasts among cross-border workers, the festival also represents an opportunity to network with other enthusiasts and professionals in the field.
