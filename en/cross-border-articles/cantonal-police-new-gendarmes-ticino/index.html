New Officers: Ticino Strengthens Cantonal Security (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 March 2026

In Giubiasco, the Cantonal Police School launches the SCP 2026 training program, aiming to integrate 10 new officers and enhance safety in the Canton.

Contesto

TL;DR - New SCP 2026 training program launched in Giubiasco. - 10 new officers recruited for Cantonal Police in Ticino. - Two-year training includes classroom and practical experience. - Program aims to enhance security for cross-border workers. ## Key facts - Cosa: Launch of SCP 2026 training program for Cantonal Police. - Quando: Inaugurated on morning of 8:00, date not specified. - Dove: Giubiasco, Canton of Ticino. - Chi: Key figures: Claudio Zali, Captain Christophe Cerinotti, Andrea Pronzini. - Importo: 10 new aspiring officers recruited (7 men, 3 women). - Durata: Two-year training program. - Esame: Federal professional examination at the end of the program. - Impatto: Enhanced security for cross-border workers and increased law enforcement presence. The launch of the SCP 2026 training program in Giubiasco marks a significant step forward for security in the Canton of Ticino. This morning at 8:00, the Cantonal Police School of the 5th Examination District officially inaugurated its program. The event was attended by key figures, including Claudio Zali, Director of the Department of Territory, Captain Christophe Cerinotti, Head of the Training Section, and Andrea Pronzini, Director of the Police Training Center (CFP), who delivered official welcome speeches to the new recruits. This initiative is particularly relevant for a border region like Ticino, where the constant flow of people and goods, including a considerable number of cross-border workers, necessitates a robust and well-prepared law enforcement presence. The Cantonal Police announced the recruitment of 10 new aspiring officers, consisting of 7 men and 3 women, who will undergo a rigorous two-year training program combining classroom instruction and hands-on experience.

Dettagli operativi

The comprehensive training path for these aspiring officers is designed to culminate in a federal professional examination, leading to the acquisition of the Federal Professional Certificate for the Cantonal Police. This two-year program is meticulously structured, blending theoretical knowledge taught in classrooms with intensive practical experience gained within their respective police forces. Such a dual approach ensures that the new officers are not only well-versed in legal frameworks and police procedures but also skilled in handling real-life situations, from routine patrols in Lugano and Bellinzona to complex interventions near the border in Chiasso or Mendrisio.
