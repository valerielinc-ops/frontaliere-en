Russian Ark in Chiasso: Culture Beyond the Border (cross-border guide)

TL;DR - Russian Ark screening in Chiasso on Feb 18, 2026 - 99-minute single-take film shot in Hermitage Museum - Event for cross-border workers to explore Ticino culture - Film in Russian with Italian subtitles at Cinema Teatro ## Key facts - Film Title: Russian Ark (Russkij kovcheg) - Director: Aleksandr Sokurov - Release Year: 2002 - Filming Location: Hermitage Museum, St. Petersburg - Event Date: Wednesday, February 18, 2026 - Event Time: 8:30 PM - Event Location: Cinema Teatro, Via Dante Alighieri 3, Chiasso - Language: Russian with Italian subtitles Chiasso is not just the Brogeda border crossing or an economic hub. It is also a center of vibrant, accessible, and high-quality cultural life, just minutes from the border. A striking example is coming on Wednesday, February 18, 2026, when at 8:30 PM the Cinema Teatro in Chiasso will screen "Russian Ark" (Russkij kovcheg), the masterpiece by director Aleksandr Sokurov. For the thousands of cross-border workers who cross the border every day to work in the Mendrisiotto region, this event represents a unique opportunity to enrich their experience in Ticino, going beyond the work routine. ## A Journey Through Time and Art The film, shot in 2002, is an unprecedented cinematic work. Its most extraordinary feature is being filmed in a single, uninterrupted 99-minute tracking shot, a technical feat accomplished with a special Steadicam inside the Hermitage Museum in St. Petersburg. The plot follows a contemporary filmmaker and a 19th-century French diplomat on a dreamlike journey through three centuries of Russian history. The viewer witnesses surreal and powerful scenes: from Peter the Great whipping his generals to Catherine the Great searching for a restroom, to the last supper of the Tsar's family. The event, hosted in the historic theater at Via Dante Alighieri 3 in Chiasso, holds particular value for the cross-border community. The Mendrisiotto region, with its numerous companies and industries, is the daily destination for a huge portion of cross-border workers. Often, however, knowledge of the area is limited to the commute. Initiatives like this, promoted by the Centro Culturale Chiasso, offer a chance to discover another side of the region—one of culture, social life, and integration. ## Beyond Work: Living in Ticino Participating in such an event means transforming a weekday evening into a moment of personal growth. The proximity to the border makes logistics extremely simple for those living in the provinces of Como or Varese. Instead of facing the return traffic, one can choose to stay and enjoy a cultural offering that is on par with that of major cities. This helps to create a deeper connection with a territory that is not just a workplace, but an environment to be lived in and appreciated in all its facets. It's a fundamental piece of the work-life balance everyone seeks. ⚠️ Event Details: - What: Screening of "Russian Ark" (Russkij kovcheg) - When: Wednesday, February 18, 2026, 8:30 PM - Where: Cinema teatro, Via Dante Alighieri 3, 6830 Chiasso - Language: Russian, with Italian subtitles

Frequently Asked Questions
When is the screening of the film 'Russian Ark' taking place in Chiasso?
The screening will take place on Wednesday, February 18, 2026 at 8:30 pm.
Where is the Cinema Teatro in Chiasso located?
The Cinema Teatro is located in Via Dante Alighieri 3, 6830 Chiasso. This historic hall is easily accessible and represents a cultural reference point for cross-border commuters working in the Mendrisiotto, offering an opportunity for evening recreation after work.
In what language is the film 'Russian Ark' shown and are there subtitles?
The film 'Russian Ark' is screened in the original Russian language for a total immersion in the atmosphere desired by the director. To facilitate the understanding of the public, there will be subtitles in Italian. This allows cross-border commuters to fully enjoy the cinematographic work, appreciating both its technique and the historical and artistic narration.
Why is the screening of 'Russian Ark' an opportunity for cross-border commuters?
The screening of 'Arca Russa' offers cross-border commuters the opportunity to enrich their experience in Ticino, going beyond the work-home routine. It allows you to discover the lively cultural offer of Chiasso, promoting sociality and integration. It is an opportunity to improve the work-life balance, transforming a midweek evening into a moment of personal growth and leisure.
Where can I find more information or buy tickets for the event?
For more information on the screening of 'Russian Ark', including details on ticket pre-sale and other events, please visit the official website of the Chiasso Cultural Center at www.centroculturalechiasso.ch. Here cross-border commuters can plan their evening and discover the entire cultural offer of the region.
