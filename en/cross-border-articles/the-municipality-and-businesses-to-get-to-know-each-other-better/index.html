Vezia Municipality hosts an event to strengthen dialogue with local businesses

Contesto

In brief The municipality of Vezia organized an event to strengthen dialogue with local businesses, aiming to create a constructive exchange and provide useful information to local companies. The meeting, held on Thursday evening, saw the participation of around thirty local businesses, representing various economic sectors. ## Key facts - What: Meeting between the municipality of Vezia and local businesses - When: Thursday evening - Where: Vezia - Who: The municipality of Vezia and local businesses - Amount: Not specified The executive of Vezia organized an event to strengthen dialogue with local businesses. The meeting, held on Thursday evening, saw the participation of around thirty local businesses, representing various economic sectors. During the evening, current topics related to building procedures, fire safety, and energy strategy were discussed. Interventions by the head of the Technical Office, Fabio Manetti, and the head of the consulting area of the Ticino Energy Association, Luca Pampuri, provided concrete insights and useful information for the attending businesses. In particular, regulations on building procedures and fire safety were explained, with reference to the Ticino law of December 17, 2008, and the application regulation of May 22, 2012. Furthermore, opportunities offered by new technologies for reducing energy consumption and optimizing costs were presented. The evening concluded with a convivial aperitif, which offered the opportunity for an informal exchange between businesses and municipal representatives. This event represented an important opportunity for dialogue and collaboration between the municipality and local businesses, with the aim of creating a favorable environment for the economic development of the territory.
