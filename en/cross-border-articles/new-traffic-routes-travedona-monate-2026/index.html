New heavy vehicle traffic in Travedona Monate

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

From May 7, 2026, trucks will no longer be allowed to circulate in the center of Travedona Monate. Here's what changes.

Contesto

In brief - New experimental traffic layout in Travedona Monate starting May 7, 2026 - Via Trieste becomes one-way for increased safety - Interchange on state road 629 in funding phase ## Key facts - What: New experimental traffic layout for heavy vehicles - When: Starting May 7, 2026 - Where: Travedona Monate, Varese - Who: Municipality of Travedona Monate and Province of Varese - Amount: Not specified A new experimental traffic layout for heavy vehicles will be introduced in Travedona Monate, effective from May 7, 2026. This change is part of a broader project promoted by the Province of Varese in collaboration with local municipalities, aimed at facilitating and securing the flow of trucks. Mayor Angelo Fiombo confirmed the previously outlined plans and introduced new details about the project for the new interchange on state road 629 Besozzo-Vergiate. The traffic modifications in the center of Travedona Monate remain unchanged from those presented during the project's launch. The most significant change involves opening Via Roma, Via Colombo, and Via Veneto to heavy traffic, allowing trucks to travel towards Cassinetta. Simultaneously, a ban on vehicles over 3.5 tonnes entering from Cassinetta towards the town center will be implemented. ### Traffic modifications Mayor Fiombo specified that trucks will not enter the piazza or pass through Via Cavour. Among the planned interventions is the modification of traffic flow in Via Tamborini, where the one-way street will be reversed. This street will become a passage for heavy vehicles coming from Brebbia and heading towards state road 629, thus avoiding traffic through the piazza. When will the new experimental traffic regulations come into effect?
The new experimental traffic regulations will come into effect from May 7, 2026.
