Detailed cost analysis Living in Lugano as a cross-border worker involves a series of costs that are important to know in detail. In addition to the main costs such as rent, transportation, and health insurance, there are other expenses that can influence the monthly budget. ### Rent and accommodation Rent is one of the most significant costs for those who decide to live in Lugano. A two-room apartment in the city center can cost between 1,500 and 2,000 CHF per month, while a larger apartment or one in a more peripheral area can be slightly more affordable. It is also important to consider additional costs such as utilities, which can add another 150-200 CHF per month. ### Public transportation Public transportation in Lugano is very efficient and covers the entire city and its surroundings. A monthly subscription for public transportation costs approximately 70 CHF, but there are also weekly or daily options if the service is used less frequently. For those who work in Switzerland, it might be convenient to consider a combined subscription that includes public transportation in Italy. ### Health insurance Health insurance is mandatory in Switzerland and must be subscribed within three months of arrival in the country. The average cost of a basic insurance according to LAMal is approximately 300 CHF per month, but it can vary depending on the provider and the chosen plan. It is possible to choose between different levels of coverage, depending on individual needs and available budget. ### Groceries Groceries in Lugano can be more expensive compared to Italy, but the quality of the products is generally very high.
