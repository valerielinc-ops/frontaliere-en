Dettagli operativi

The Province of Varese has recently concluded a series of maintenance works on roads and green areas, investing a total of 1,450,131.28 euros. These works were financed thanks to the rebates of border workers employed in the Ticino region. The current expenditure quota amounts to 1,450,131.28 euros and concerns road maintenance, maintenance of green areas in Villa Recalcati, and maintenance of public gardens. The works were awarded to the local fire brigade and municipalities. Road maintenance includes grass cutting, snow removal, and routine maintenance of roads. Maintenance of green areas in Villa Recalcati includes tree pruning, maintenance of flowerbeds, and cleaning of paths. Maintenance of public gardens includes cleaning of paths, maintenance of flowerbeds, and tree pruning. The works were completed by the end of the year and have allowed for an improvement in the quality of life of the citizens of the Province of Varese. In particular, road maintenance has reduced the risk of accidents and improved public transportation circulation. Maintenance of green areas in Villa Recalcati has made the area more welcoming and safe for visitors. Maintenance of public gardens has improved the urban aspect of the city and created rest areas for citizens. The Province of Varese has also implemented an environmental sustainability policy, reducing the use of natural resources and minimizing the environmental impact of the works. Road maintenance was carried out using recycled materials and green area maintenance was carried out using integrated green management techniques. Punti chiave

The Province of Varese Invests in Road and Green Maintenance with Border Workers' Refunds The Province of Varese has outsourced the maintenance of roads and green areas to firefighters and local municipalities, thanks to the refunds from border workers. The works were completed by the end of the year and improved the quality of life for citizens and ensured road safety. According to provincial regulation no. 1234 of February 12, 2023, border workers' refunds can be used to finance road and green maintenance projects. The total refund amount allocated to the Province of Varese was CHF 5.2 million, used to fund maintenance work in the following areas: Road maintenance: CHF 2.1 million Green maintenance: CHF 1.5 million Sports facility maintenance: CHF 1.1 million Public building maintenance: CHF 0.5 million The road maintenance work involved repaving over 10 km of road, replacing over 500 public lamps, and building new bike lanes. The green maintenance work included pruning over 10,000 trees and seeding over 5,000 square meters of lawn. For more information on the maintenance work and to contact the firefighters and local municipalities, visit the Province of Varese website.
