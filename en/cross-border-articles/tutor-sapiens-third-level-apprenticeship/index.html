Tutor Sapiens: tools for third-level apprenticeships

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

Chamber of Commerce and Province of Varese present the results of the Tutor Sapiens training path to promote the integration of young talents.

Contesto

In brief - Chamber of Commerce and Province of Varese present the results of Tutor Sapiens - The project aims to facilitate the insertion of young talents - BotSapiens and Carte in Azione simplify bureaucracy ## Key facts - What: Presentation of the results of the training path Tutor Sapiens - When: April 29, 2026 - Where: Centro Congressi Ville Ponti, Varese - Who: Chamber of Commerce and Province of Varese - Amount: 2.7% of job placements in the province of Varese in 2025 The Chamber of Commerce and Province of Varese presented the final results of the training path Tutor Sapiens with a workshop at the Centro Congressi Ville Ponti. The initiative aims to leave the territory with a concrete legacy, made of innovative tools designed to facilitate the insertion of young talents through the third-level apprenticeship contract. This innovative contractual formula allows companies to hire young students while they complete their training path (ITS Academy or University), benefiting from important contributory and regulatory advantages. The Tutor Sapiens project saw the active participation of local companies, ITS foundations, trade associations, and labor consultants. Together, these actors collaborated to enhance the fundamental figure of the company tutor, who is responsible for transmitting vision and skills to new generations, ensuring an effective handover within production organizations. ### Data analysis From the data presented during the conference, processed by the Labor Market Observatory of the Province of Varese from the Sistal 2.0 source, it clearly emerges that the contractual tool is advantageous but underutilized. Practical Analysis The Tutor Sapiens project offers various opportunities for young talents and for companies wishing to invest in the training of their employees. The simplification of bureaucracy and the introduction of innovative tools such as BotSapiens and Carte in Azione represent a significant step forward in the field of third-level apprenticeships. ### Advantages for Companies For companies, the third-level apprenticeship contract offers important contributory and regulatory advantages. This type of contract allows hiring young students while they complete their educational path, ensuring an effective handover within productive organizations. Moreover, the active participation of local companies, ITS foundations, industry associations, and labor consultants has strengthened the role of the company tutor, making them a fundamental resource for transmitting vision and skills to new generations. ### Opportunities for Young People For young people, the Tutor Sapiens project represents an opportunity to access high-quality training paths and benefit from contributory and regulatory advantages. The third-level apprenticeship contract allows completing their educational path (ITS Academy or University) while working, ensuring easier job placement and greater economic stability. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation Before the introduction of the Tutor Sapiens project, third-level apprenticeships were underutilized, and bureaucracy represented a significant obstacle for companies and young people. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the advantages of the third-level apprenticeship contract?
The third-level apprenticeship contract offers significant contributory and regulatory advantages for companies and young people. Companies can hire young students while they complete their educational path, benefiting from tax advantages and greater economic stability. Young people, on the other hand, can complete their educational path (ITS Academy or University) while working, ensuring easier job placement and greater economic stability.
How can I access the training courses offered by the Tutor Sapiens project?
To access the training courses offered by the Tutor Sapiens project, you need to submit an application through the official channels indicated by the Chamber of Commerce and the Province of Varese. It is important to verify the requirements for access to the third-level apprenticeship contract and ensure that you meet the criteria provided. Additionally, you can obtain information about the available training courses through the ITS foundations and industry associations.
What innovative tools have been introduced by the Tutor Sapiens project?
The Tutor Sapiens project has introduced two innovative tools to simplify bureaucracy and facilitate job placement: BotSapiens, an artificial intelligence agent specifically developed for bureaucratic simplification, and Carte in Azione, a physical toolkit that guides the tutor through the crucial phases of corporate onboarding. These tools help manage apprenticeship contracts and ensure an effective handover within production organizations.
