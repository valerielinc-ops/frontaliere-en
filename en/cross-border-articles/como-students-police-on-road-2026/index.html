Como: 10 students become local police officers with On the Road project

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

A three-year agreement between the City of Como, the Local Police, and the On the Road association to train young officers

Contesto

In brief - 10 students from 5 schools become local police officers in Como - Three-year project with the association Ragazzi On the Road - Practical experience with the Local Police of Como ## Key facts - What: On the Road educational project - When: Three-year agreement, starts April 28, 2026 - Where: Local Police Command of Como, viale Innocenzo XI - Who: Municipality of Como, Local Police, Ragazzi On the Road Association - Amount: 10 students involved Como chooses to transform prevention from an isolated initiative into a pillar of civic growth. This is the value of the three-year agreement signed between the Municipality of Como, the Local Police Command, and the association Ragazzi On the Road, which brings to the city 'On the Road', a consolidated educational model at the national level, which is revolutionizing the relationship between institutions and new generations. A project that has already received significant recognition even at the institutional level: during the last municipal council, the mayor Alessandro Rapinese, the President of the Municipal Council Fulvio Anzaldo, and the entire Council expressed their praise to the young participants, underlining the educational value of the initiative and its impact on the territory. An important institutional moment, shared in the presence of the development manager of the association Ragazzi On the Road Marco Zanchi and the staff manager Riccardo Bonzi. ### The inaugural day The inaugural day, held at the Local Police Command of viale Innocenzo XI on Tuesday, April 28, is the first application: a group of 10 students has started a path that in the coming years will be progressively expanded, consolidating the bond between young people and institutions. Implications for Youth and the Community The On the Road project represents a unique opportunity for young people in Como to approach the world of safety and prevention. Through this experience, students can acquire practical skills and develop a sense of civic responsibility. ### Comparison with Similar Initiatives Unlike other educational initiatives, On the Road offers a direct and concrete experience, allowing young people to work closely with law enforcement. This practical approach is fundamental for the formation of aware and responsible citizens. ### Future Scenarios With the three-year agreement, the On the Road project has the potential to become a reference model for other cities. The goal is to increase the number of students involved and to extend the project at the regional level. ### Impact on the Territory The initiative has a positive impact not only on the participating youth but also on the local community. The presence of young local police officers contributes to strengthening the sense of security and belonging to the community. ### School Involvement The schools involved in the project represent an important part of Como's educational fabric. The participation of students from different schools fosters collaboration between educational institutions and the Local Police. ### Skill Development Through the On the Road project, students have the opportunity to develop cross-cutting skills, such as teamwork, emergency management, and effective communication. These skills are fundamental for their personal and professional growth. ### Collaboration with Institutions The collaboration between the Municipality of Como, the Local Police, and the Ragazzi On the Road association represents a virtuous example of synergy between institutions. What to do to participate If you are a student interested in participating in the On the Road project, you can contact your school or the Ragazzi On the Road association for more information. The schools involved in the project are I.I.S. “Leonardo da Vinci – Ripamonti” in Como, I.T.I.S. “Magistri Cumacini” in Como, I.I.S. “G. Bertacchi” in Lecco, CFP “Giuseppe Terragni” and the “Matilde di Canossa” Institute. ### Enrollment procedure To enroll in the project, you need to fill out a registration form available at the participating schools or on the Ragazzi On the Road association’s website. The form must be submitted to the school’s secretariat or sent via email to the address indicated on the website. ### Participation requirements The requirements to participate in the On the Road project include a minimum age of 16 years and attendance at one of the participating schools. Students must also be available to participate in the activities planned by the project, which will take place during the indicated period. ### Planned activities The activities planned for the On the Road project include road checks, evening services, canine units, and checks on heavy vehicles. Students will have the opportunity to work closely with the Local Police, acquiring practical skills and developing a sense of civic responsibility. ### Deadlines and important dates Important dates for the On the Road project include the start of activities on April 28, 2026, and the end of activities on May 4, 2026. Interested students must enroll by April 20, 2026, to participate in the project. ### Useful contacts For more information about the On the Road project, you can contact the Ragazzi On the Road association at the phone number or email address indicated on the website. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can participate in the On the Road project?
Students aged 16 or older attending one of the participating schools can join the On the Road project: I.I.S. 'Leonardo da Vinci – Ripamonti' in Como, I.T.I.S. 'Magistri Cumacini' in Como, I.I.S. 'G. Bertacchi' in Lecco, CFP 'Giuseppe Terragni', and the 'Matilde di Canossa' Institute.
What activities are included in the On the Road project?
The On the Road project includes road checks, evening services, canine units, and inspections of heavy vehicles. Students will have the opportunity to work closely with the Local Police, gaining practical skills and developing a sense of civic responsibility.
How can I sign up for the On the Road project?
To sign up for the On the Road project, you need to fill out a registration form available at the participating schools or on the Ragazzi On the Road association's website. The form must be submitted to the school's office or sent via email to the address provided on the website.
