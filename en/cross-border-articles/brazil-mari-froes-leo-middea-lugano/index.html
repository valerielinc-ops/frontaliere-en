On Tuesday, July 14, at Boschetto del Parco Ciani, the Lugano LongLake Festival presents an event that combines tradition and contemporary music, with Mari Froes, one of the most promising faces of the new Brazilian music scene, as the star. Opening the concert will be Leo Middea, already appreciated by the Lugano audience, while the evening will start at 18:00 with the tropical DJ set by Carla from the duo Batukizer. Born in 2003, Mari Froes is establishing herself internationally as one of the artists capable of renewing the language of Brazilian music. Her stylistic signature blends MPB, bossa nova, samba, and jazz, creating a personal and recognizable sound identity. Her journey began on YouTube, where she shared reinterpretations of famous songs, surpassing 100 million views. In 2019, she released the singles 'Moça' and 'Rosa e Laranja,' followed in 2020 by the EP 'Nebulosa,' which surpassed 20 million streams. In July 2024, she was invited by the COLORS format to perform 'Gabriela,' while in the same year, an excerpt from 'Figa de Guiné' went viral on TikTok, reaching over 72 million views. The track was then released as a single on April 11, distributed by Sony Germany.
