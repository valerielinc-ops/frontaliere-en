Frequently Asked Questions
How many post offices could close in Canton Ticino by 2025?
The post of Switzerland provides for the closure of about 10 post offices by 2025 due to the reorganization of the service network.
What impact could the post office transfer on 30,000 inhabitants and 15,000 frontier workers in Chiasso?
The transfer could reduce local services and jobs, but also offer the opportunity to redesign the center, improving the quality of services for residents and visitors, as demonstrated by the case of Mendrisio with a 15% increase in service quality.
How can I check if the new post office in Via Bossi in Chiasso will be accessible by public transport for Italian frontier workers?
The new post office of Via Bossi will be accessible by line 1 of the STA (Ticinese Society of Autotrasporti) that connects Chiasso to Como every 15 minutes. The 'Via Bossi' stop is 200 metres from the office, with direct connections from Balerna and Mendrisio.
What digital alternatives does Post Switzerland offer to replace the services of Chiasso Post Office?
Post Switzerland allows you to purchase stamps, send parcels and manage postal accounts via the 'Mobile Tax' app or website. In addition, 120 PickPost points are available in Ticino, including supermarkets and shops, for shipments and retreats.
What happens to the parcels already in transit towards the post office of Piazza Indipendenza in Chiasso after the transfer?
The packages already addressed to Independence Square will be automatically redirected to the new office of Via Bossi. No intervention is required by the sender or recipient. The Post Switzerland guarantees delivery at no extra cost.
