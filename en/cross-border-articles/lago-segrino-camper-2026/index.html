Lake Segrino: No to 105 camper pitches, asphalt area

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

The 'Ilaria Alpi' Environment Circle opposes the project of 105 camper pitches in Canzo, near Lake Segrino, a community site of importance.

Contesto

In brief - Project for 105 camper pitches in Canzo near Lake Segrino - Circolo Ambiente 'Ilaria Alpi' criticizes the choice to asphalt the area - Water and sewer management issues highlighted ## Key facts - What: Project for 105 camper pitches - When: 02/05/2026 - Where: Canzo, Lake Segrino, Como province - Who: Circolo Ambiente 'Ilaria Alpi' - Amount: 105 camper pitches Lake Segrino, a natural gem in Como province, is at the center of a new controversy. The project to create a camper area with 105 pitches in Canzo has sparked protests from Circolo Ambiente 'Ilaria Alpi'. According to the group, the choice to asphalt a large area near the lake is unacceptable, especially considering that the zone is a Site of Community Importance (SCI) and should be preserved. The group's alternative proposal is to restore the area to green space, creating an ecological connection with Parco Lago del Segrino. ### Water management issues Circolo Ambiente 'Ilaria Alpi' has also highlighted the problems of water and sewer management. According to the environmental opinion of Parco Lago Segrino, Canzo's sewers are still 66% of the mixed type, which could cause problems with the increase in clear water due to the impermeabilization of the area. Furthermore, the Parco's opinion highlights the need to upgrade the water and sewer networks, avoiding the contribution of meteorological waters in the mixed and black sewer network. ### Criticisms of the project Roberto Fumagalli, president of Circolo Ambiente 'Ilaria Alpi', stated: 'We are against the spread of asphalt in an area that should be preserved from a naturalistic point of view. Our proposal is to create a green and wooded area, to maintain a more natural environment, given the proximity to the beautiful lake.' ### New parking lot nea...
