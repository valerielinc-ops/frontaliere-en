How can I help the police prevent supermarket theft?
You can help by reporting any suspicious activity to local police officers. Observe your surroundings carefully while shopping and report individuals who are behaving suspiciously or who appear to be hiding goods.
What is the procedure to follow for a cross-border commuter if he witnesses a theft in a supermarket in Lavena Ponte Tresa?
The cross-border commuter must immediately contact the local police of Lavena Ponte Tresa on the emergency number 113 or 115, providing a detailed description of the event, the suspicious individuals and any vehicles involved. It is important to stay safe and wait for instructions from law enforcement.
What happens if a cross-border commuter sees someone stealing from a supermarket near the border?
If you are a cross-border commuter and witness a theft at a supermarket near the border, it is important to report it to your local law enforcement immediately.
