Equans confirms 19 job cuts

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

After failed mediation, Equans will proceed with 19 layoffs in Monteceneri. Affected employees are free immediately.

Contesto

In brief - Equans lays off 19 employees in Monteceneri - Affected workers are free immediately - Unions failed to mediate ## Key facts - What: Layoffs at Equans - When: Announced at the end of March 2026, effective immediately - Where: Equans' headquarters in Monteceneri - Who: Equans, unions - Amount: 19 full-time positions Equans will proceed with 19 layoffs at its Monteceneri headquarters. The announcement was confirmed by Corriere del Ticino after social partners' mediation failed to prevent the cuts. Affected employees will be considered free immediately, with the possibility to leave their job before the notice period if they find another position. The layoffs were announced at the end of March, initially with a prospect of 22 layoffs. The reduction to 19 positions is the result of negotiations between the company and the unions, which, however, failed to prevent the cuts. ### Implications for workers Workers laid off by Equans now face a complex situation. The possibility to leave their job before the notice period could be an opportunity for those who have already found another job, but for those who don't have an alternative yet, the situation might be more difficult. Affected workers will have to go through unemployment procedures and, if necessary, look for a new job. It's important that they inform themselves about their rights and the available assistance options, such as training courses and professional reintegration programs. ### Comparison with the previous situation Before the layoffs announcement, Equans had initially planned 22 layoffs.
