Dettagli operativi

Federica Giudici, a graduate in social work and holder of an executive master in business administration, has been officially appointed as the director of the Social Services of Bellinzona in July 2024. Her appointment represents a crucial moment for the city, as it comes at a time when pressure on social services is continuously growing, due to an increase in the number of needy individuals and challenges related to inclusion. ## Responsibilities and strategic objectives As director, Federica Giudici will be responsible for managing the personnel of the Social Services, which currently consists of around 35 staff members, including social workers, educators, and administrators. Her primary mission will be to ensure effective coordination of initiatives and projects in favor of citizen sociality, ensuring that no vulnerable group is overlooked. A concrete example of the work carried out is the 'Active Integration Bellinzona' project, which in 2023 supported over 120 people in conditions of unemployment, providing professional training and facilitating their reintegration into the labor market. 📊 Key numbers In 2023, the Social Services of Bellinzona managed around 1,250 requests for economic assistance, with a dedicated budget of 3.8 million Swiss francs. This figure reflects the importance of stable and competent leadership in managing public resources and ensuring fair and transparent use of available funds. ## Projects of inclusion and integration Among Giudici's primary objectives is the development of targeted social inclusion projects. In detail, there are plans to expand collaborations between neighboring municipalities, such as Giubiasco and Arbedo-Castione, to promote synergies in supporting migrant families.
