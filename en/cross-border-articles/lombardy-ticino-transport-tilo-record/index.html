Operational details

Tilo's railway service offers a range of advantages for commuters and occasional travellers. Firstly, Tilo trains are equipped with modern comforts such as power outlets, Wi-Fi connection, and dedicated spaces for bicycles. Additionally, the company offers convenient fares, with monthly and annual subscriptions that allow passengers to save on transportation costs. In terms of frequency, Tilo trains run on regular and scheduled timetables, ensuring a reliable and punctual service. The company has also implemented a real-time information system, allowing passengers to stay updated on any delays or schedule changes. Regarding accessibility, Tilo trains are designed to be inclusive and accessible to all. Railway stations are equipped with dedicated paths for people with disabilities, and the trains are fitted with reserved spaces for wheelchairs and strollers. "Tilo's service is an example of how public transport can be efficient and accessible to all," stated the Director of the Swiss Federal Office of Transport, Martin Meyer.
