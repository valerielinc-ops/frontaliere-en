New life for the former Corecco Hotel in Quinto (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 6 May 2026

Restoration and enhancement of the former Corecco Hotel in Lurengo, Quinto, with the creation of a wellness-focused accommodation facility.

Contesto

In brief - Restoration of the former Albergo Corecco in Lurengo, Quinto - 22 rooms and 4 apartments - Wellness area with panoramic pool ## Key facts - What: Restoration and enhancement of the former Albergo Corecco - When: Project in the implementation phase - Where: Lurengo, Municipality of Quinto - Who: Promoter Daniele Pronzini, Municipality of Quinto, Regional Tourism Organization Bellinzona and Valli - Amount: 22 rooms and 4 apartments In Lurengo, in the Municipality of Quinto, a new hotel project is taking shape that involves the restoration and enhancement of a historic Leventine building. The initiative includes the redevelopment of the former Albergo Corecco with a contemporary expansion and the creation of a reception structure with a component dedicated to well-being. The project, named 'SORA', has obtained the construction permit from the Municipality of Quinto and will be developed with the support of the Regional Tourism Organization Bellinzona and Valli. The historical memory of the valley The intervention concerns, as mentioned, the historic Albergo Pensione Corecco, a building that belongs to the tourist memory of the valley. The structure probably dates back to the late 19th century, between 1880 and 1895, in a phase of growth of alpine tourism following the opening of the Gotthard line and tunnel in 1882. In those years, Leventina became one of the most accessible mountain destinations from Milan and Northern Italy, and places like Faido experienced significant hotel development linked to the presence of a bourgeois and aristocratic clientele. Slow enjoyment of the territory The new structure will feature 22 rooms and 4 apartments for secondary residence use connected to hotel management. Dettagli operativi

Impact on Local Tourism The restoration project of the former Albergo Corecco in Lurengo represents a significant opportunity for local tourism. The new structure, with its 22 rooms and 4 apartments, will offer a quality accommodation solution for visitors wishing to explore Val Leventina. The wellness area with a panoramic pool, sauna, and treatment cabins is an added value that can attract a broader and more diverse audience. ### Benefits for the Community The realization of the 'SORA' project not only enhances a historic building but also strengthens the local economy. The creation of new job opportunities and the increase in tourist flow can have a positive impact on the community of Quinto and on Val Leventina as a whole. Furthermore, the presence of a quality accommodation facility can encourage the development of other tourist and commercial activities in the area. ### Sustainability and Innovation The 'SORA' project stands out for its sustainable and innovative approach. The integration of modern technologies, such as electric charging stations and spaces for bicycles and e-bikes, reflects a sensitivity towards the environment and a willingness to promote an active and sustainable lifestyle. This approach can attract an increasingly environmentally conscious audience. ### Future Prospects With the completion of the 'SORA' project, Val Leventina will be able to offer a more complete and diversified tourist experience. The new structure, with its focus on well-being and quality of stay, can become a reference point for visitors seeking an authentic and relaxing experience. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main features of the «SORA» project?
The «SORA» project involves the restoration and enhancement of the former Albergo Corecco in Lurengo, Quinto, with the creation of a reception facility that includes 22 rooms and 4 apartments. The facility will offer a wellness area with a panoramic pool, sauna, room for physical activities and relaxation, as well as a restaurant and a bar with a panoramic terrace.
What are the benefits for the local community?
The «SORA» project helps to strengthen the local economy by creating new job opportunities and increasing tourist traffic. Additionally, it enhances a historic building and promotes a sustainable lifestyle, attracting an audience mindful of environmental issues.
