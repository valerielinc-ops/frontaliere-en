TL;DR - Piano genitorialita - 10M euro - 1 mese leave - 3250 euro bonus ## Key facts - Azienda: Acinque - Regione: Lombardia - Provincie: Varese, Como, Lecco, Sondrio - Importo: 10 million euros - Durata: 10 anni - Bonus: up to 3250 euros per year - Leave: 1 mese extra paid leave Acinque launches the 'GenWe - welfare generation' plan: an innovative approach to support parenthood and counteract the demographic winter. The Lombardy multi-utility company has allocated 10 million euros over ten years to support its employees in the provinces of Varese, Como, Lecco and Sondrio. The 'GenWe' plan provides a series of concrete measures to help parents balance work and family life. These include a month of extra paid leave, economic bonuses of up to 3,250 euros per year and hours of leave to accompany children on their first day of school. This initiative is aimed at employees in the provinces of Varese, Como, Lecco and Sondrio. According to ISTAT data, the Lombardy region is facing an important demographic challenge, with a declining birth rate and an ageing population. Acinque's 'GenWe' plan represents a concrete example of how a company can contribute to mitigating this phenomenon.
