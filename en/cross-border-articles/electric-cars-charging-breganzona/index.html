Electric cars: charging is the most critical moment

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

A fire in a Breganzona garage has refocused attention on the risks associated with charging electric cars.

The fire likely started from an electric car during battery charging. According to the Swiss Firefighters Federation, electric cars are not more flammable than gasoline or diesel ones. However, these vehicles are often charged in garages, which could be the most critical moment. Claudio Dick, an expert in electric motors and batteries, explains that "in 90% of cases, the most delicate moment is always charging. We input current and create a chemical process that, together with voltage, charges the battery. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the risks associated with charging electric cars?
The risks associated with charging electric cars include fires caused by malfunctions during the charging process. According to Claudio Dick, an expert in the field, the charging moment is the most delicate, as it involves a chemical process that can lead to fires. It is important to adopt adequate preventive measures, such as the installation of specific extinguishers for lithium batteries, to ensure safety.
What are the tax incentives available for purchasing electric cars in Ticino?
The Canton of Ticino offers various tax incentives for the purchase of electric cars, which may include reductions in circulation taxes and subsidies for the installation of domestic charging points. It is important to verify the specific requirements and conditions to access these incentives, as well as to submit the necessary documentation within the established deadlines.
