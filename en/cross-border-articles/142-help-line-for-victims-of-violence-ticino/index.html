142: new helpline for violence victims in Ticino

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

The number 142 is now active in Ticino to support victims of physical, psychological, or sexual violence. Free and anonymous, it offers 24/7 counseling.

Contesto

In brief - Number 142 active in Ticino for victims of violence - Free, anonymous and available 24/7 - Trained operators from FCTSA ## Key facts - What: New helpline for victims of violence - When: Active as of today - Where: Canton Ticino - Who: Federazione Cantonale Ticinese dei Servizi Autoambulanze (FCTSA) - Cost: Free and anonymous The number 142, a direct helpline for victims of violence, is finally operational in Ticino. This long-awaited service offers a response on three levels: crisis interventions, basic counseling, and triage. The line is managed by the Federazione Cantonale Ticinese dei Servizi Autoambulanze (FCTSA), with specially trained operators. 'The Federation of ambulances is already very accustomed to listening,' declared President Luciano Anselmi. 'We have professionals who have obtained a federal diploma after a training path in which listening played a fundamental role.' The number 142 is completely free and anonymous. 'We do not see the number of the caller and we do not know who the person is on the other end,' Anselmi reassured. The line is active 24 hours a day and offers a direct access point for victims of physical, psychological, or sexual violence. During office hours, the counseling is specialized, while outside office hours it is basic counseling. ### Implications for cross-border workers The new helpline represents a significant step for supporting victims of violence, including cross-border workers who live and work in Canton Ticino. Practical Analysis The new helpline 142 represents a significant step forward in supporting victims of violence in Ticino. This service offers a response on three levels: crisis interventions, basic counseling, and triage. The operators of the FCTSA have been specifically trained to handle calls of this type, ensuring professional and compassionate support. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation Before the activation of the number 142, victims of violence in Ticino had to rely on emergency services such as the police or the ambulance. Although these services are essential in case of imminent danger, they do not offer the same level of continuous and specialized support. The new helpline 142 fills this gap, offering a service dedicated to victims of violence. ### Implications for Cross-Border Workers For cross-border workers who work and live in Ticino, the new helpline represents an important resource. Being free and anonymous, the line 142 offers a service accessible to everyone, regardless of their work or residential situation. This is particularly important for cross-border workers who might hesitate to seek help due to language or bureaucratic barriers. ### Practical Scenarios Imagine being a cross-border worker living in Ticino and experiencing domestic violence. You can call 142 to receive immediate and anonymous support. The operators can help you assess the situation and decide the next steps, such as filing a report or seeking refuge. The line is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week, offering an essential service for anyone in a crisis situation. ### Concrete Procedures If you find yourself in a situation of violence or know someone who is a victim, you can contact the number 142 at any time. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can use the number 142?
The number 142 is available to all victims of physical, psychological, or sexual violence, as well as their family members, friends, and witnesses. It is a free and anonymous service, accessible to everyone, regardless of their employment or residential status.
What happens if I call 142?
When you call 142, you will be connected with a trained operator who can provide immediate and anonymous support. The operators can help you assess the situation and decide on the next steps, such as filing a report or seeking shelter. If necessary, they can refer you to competent services for more in-depth support.
Is the 142 service available 24/7?
Yes, the number 142 is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. The operators are always ready to provide immediate and anonymous support at any time of the day.
