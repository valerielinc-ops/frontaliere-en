The Smile Gala enchants: magic and solidarity for Del Ponte Hospital

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

Great success for the charity event in Varese, with magic shows and fundraisers for the hospital's delivery rooms

Contesto

In brief - High turnout for the Galà del Sorriso in Varese - Funds raised for the labor wards of Del Ponte Hospital - Magic show with top-level artists ## Key facts - What: Galà del Sorriso – La Magia esiste - When: May 1, 2026 - Where: Varese - Who: Organized by Il Ponte del Sorriso - Amount: Funds raised for the labor wards The Galà del Sorriso – La Magia esiste enchanted the audience of Varese with an evening dedicated to magic and solidarity. The event, organized by Il Ponte del Sorriso, saw the participation of a large cross-section of the public, offering moments of amazement, fun, and generosity. The artistic direction was entrusted to Walter Maffei, one of the most appreciated Italian illusionists, with the hosting by Thomas Incontri. On stage, artists of great talent alternated, including Gigi Rock with his irresistible jokes, Zio Potter with his incredible performance, and Raffaele Scircoli with his mentalism. Applause also for Mirco Menegatti, master of classical magic, Mattia Santangelo who involved the children, and the duo Ale Bellotto and Cristina Valenti. The evening was closed by the comedian Ricky Bokor and the suggestive number of Leonardo Butera. Among the most emotional moments, Walter Maffei's story recalling his encounter with a child with a hand deformity, turning a magic trick into a gesture of delicacy and inclusion. The evening also featured the participation of singer Faluem and the models of Nazionale Modelle, engaged in charitable initiatives. ### The magic of solidarity The true magic of the evening was the generosity of the audience. The funds raised will contribute to creating more welcoming and colorful environments in the labor wards of Del Ponte Hospital, making the moment of birth even more serene.
