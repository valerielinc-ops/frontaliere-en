The exercise closed with a net profit of 14.4 million euros, in line with the set objectives and an increase compared to the 12.8 million of 2024. A significant part of this profit, amounting to 2 million euros, will be allocated to the territory between charity and mutuality. This decision was underlined by President Roberto Scazzosi, who highlighted how the bank does not retain value but puts it back into circulation in favor of associations, families, and local realities. For example, 500,000 euros will be allocated to educational projects in the schools of Busto Garolfo and Buguggiate, while 300,000 euros will go to support cultural initiatives in Lugano and Locarno. On the patrimonial front, the bank confirms a position of absolute solidity.
