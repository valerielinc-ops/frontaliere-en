Autostrada A8: nighttime closure for noise barrier work (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

The A8 Milan-Varese motorway will be closed for two nights between Busto Arsizio and Gallarate towards Varese for noise barrier work.

Contesto

In brief - Nighttime closure of Autolaghi between Busto and Gallarate - Work on noise barriers - Alternative routes indicated ## Key facts - What: Nighttime closure for work on noise barriers - When: May 4 and 5, 2026, from 21:00 to 5:00 - Where: Section between Busto Arsizio and Gallarate towards Varese - Who: Autostrade per l'Italia - Cost: Not specified Work on the noise barriers on the Autolaghi continues to cause disruptions to traffic. On the nights of May 4 and 5, 2026, from 21:00 to 5:00, the section between Busto Arsizio and Gallarate towards Varese will be closed. The closure is necessary to allow for the enhancement and modernization of the noise barriers. For those traveling towards Varese, after the mandatory exit at the Busto Arsizio interchange, it is recommended to take the regular routes: SS336 towards Malpensa, SS33 del Sempione towards Gallarate, via Mornera, via Pier Capponi, via Lombardia, via Vespucci, via Sanzio, via Vittorio Veneto, and re-enter the A8 towards Varese at Gallarate. ### Impact on cross-border workers Cross-border workers using the Autolaghi to reach the Canton Ticino should pay attention to the nighttime closures. The diversions could cause delays, especially for those working night shifts or needing to reach their workplace in Switzerland in the early morning hours. It is advisable to plan alternative routes and check the work schedules to avoid disruptions. ### Alternative routes Autostrade per l'Italia has indicated alternative routes for those heading to Varese. In addition to the route described, it is possible to use the SS341 towards Cavaria/Solbiate Arno/Albizzate and re-enter the A8 at the Solbiate Arno Albizzate interchange.
