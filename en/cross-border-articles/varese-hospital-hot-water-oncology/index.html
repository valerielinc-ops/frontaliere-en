Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

According to testimonies, the problem has been reported several times, but without a definitive solution. Sette Laghi, the responsible hospital company, has downsized the timing of the failure, stating that it has persisted since Friday, April 25th, and not for a week as reported by family members. The repair has been entrusted to a specialized company, which started the work on Friday and should complete it by Monday, April 27th, once the necessary spare parts arrive. The disruption has caused discomfort to patients, already undergoing oncological therapies, and their relatives. The lack of hot water is a significant problem for those who have to face demanding medical treatments.
