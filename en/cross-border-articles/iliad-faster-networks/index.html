Iliad unveils 'Faster', new-gen mobile network plan

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

Iliad's 'Faster' plan aims to accelerate mobile network development in Italy, impacting citizens, businesses, and operators.

Contesto

In brief - Iliad presents 'Più Veloci' for next-generation mobile networks - Frequency licenses expire in 2029 - Plan based on four fundamental pillars ## Key facts - What: Presentation of the 'Più Veloci' plan for mobile networks - When: April 28, 2026 - Where: Italy - Who: Iliad - Amount: Not yet specified Iliad, the operator that has put trust, innovation, and offers accessible to everyone at the center, presents today 'Più Veloci', a proposal to accelerate the development of next-generation mobile networks. A plan that aims to generate more growth for the entire country: citizens, businesses, and operators. The idea was born within the reflections that the sector has been carrying out for more than a year, in view of the 2029 expiry of the licenses for the use of 73% of the frequencies overall used in Italy for mobile telephony. The modalities of reallocation of such frequencies will be decisive in creating the necessary conditions to ensure a competitive market structure and that allows Italian operators to invest in the realization of the mobile networks of the future. The decision that will be made in these months, therefore, will influence the logic of competition, investment, and development over the next decade with an impact on the growth, competition, and innovation of the country. ### Impact on Canton Ticino The 'Più Veloci' plan by Iliad could have a significant impact on Canton Ticino, where mobile connectivity is fundamental for cross-border workers who work in Italy and reside in Switzerland.
