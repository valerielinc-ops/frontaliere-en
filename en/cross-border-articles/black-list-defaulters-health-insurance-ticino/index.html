Frequently Asked Questions
What happens if I end up on the bad debtors' blacklist of the health insurance fund?
If you end up on the bad debtors' blacklist of the health insurance fund, you might face several negative consequences, including the suspension of healthcare services and the inability to access new insurance coverage. Additionally, it might be more difficult to find a new job in Ticino, as employers may check your debt situation.
How can I avoid ending up on the blacklist?
To avoid ending up on the blacklist, it is crucial to pay the health insurance fund's fees promptly. It is advisable to set up automatic payments or remember to make the payment by the established deadlines. In case of financial difficulties, you can contact your health insurance fund to request a payment plan for the debt.
What should I do if I already have debts with the health insurance fund?
If you already have debts with the health insurance fund, it is important to contact your health insurance fund to verify the amount due and regularize your position as soon as possible. You might request a payment plan for the debt to avoid ending up on the blacklist.
