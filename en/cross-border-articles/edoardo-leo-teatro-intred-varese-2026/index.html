Edoardo Leo filled the Teatro Intred in Varese with laughter and applause on May 6th, 2026. The show 'Ti racconto una storia' saw a packed house, with the audience engaged from the start. The show, already seen in December 2023, featured some variations compared to the first performance. Leo, born in 1972, entertained the audience with anecdotes about his theatrical beginnings, his resume, and the films 'Smetto quando voglio' and 'Noi e la Giulia'. ### A journey through words and laughter The actor's starting point is that we spend half our lives living and the other half telling stories. To tell stories well, according to Leo, you need to start with the truth but with the license to exaggerate, appeal to memories, and dive into the content of great authors. Among those cited are Gabriel García Márquez, Italo Calvino, Umberto Eco, and Alessandro Baricco. Very funny were the anecdotes about his theatrical beginnings, his resume, and the films 'Smetto quando voglio' and 'Noi e la Giulia'. ### Narration at the heart of the monologue The center of the monologue always remains narration: after personal memories, Leo also analyzes another form of orality, that of the joke. He cites Achille Campanile and Freud and especially Gigi Proietti, dwelling on the figure of Pierino and the Italian dealing with the comic triad...
