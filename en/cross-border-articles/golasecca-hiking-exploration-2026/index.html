Golasecca: 140 hikers explore the most beautiful spots

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

A 13 km walking day through the most suggestive places of Golasecca, with aperitif, lunch, and snack. Participants receive gadgets and a donated defibrillator.

In brief - 140 people explore Golasecca on foot - 13 km route with aperitif, lunch, and snack - Defibrillator donated to Pro Loco ## Key facts - What: Quatar pass in cumpagnia - When: 1st May 2026 - Where: Golasecca - Who: Pro Loco Golasecca and Parole per Terra Golasecca - Distance: 13 kilometers On 1st May 2026, 140 people participated in the Quatar pass in cumpagnia, a walking day organized by Pro Loco Golasecca in collaboration with Parole per Terra Golasecca. The group covered a route of almost 13 kilometers, exploring the most picturesque corners of the town, including the Ipposidra site, the mysterious Torrazza di Sesona, the Mira Bonomi villa, and the Sass di Biss. During the walk, participants enjoyed a good aperitif, lunch, and snack. Among the curiosities encountered was gold prospector Giorgio Bogni. At the end of the walk, participants received commemorative gadgets. Furthermore, on the occasion of the Quatar pass, the Parole per Terra association donated a defibrillator to Pro Loco Golasecchese. ### Places of interest The route included some of the most fascinating places in Golasecca, such as the Ipposidra, an archaeological site of great importance, and the Torrazza di Sesona, a mysterious medieval building. The Mira Bonomi villa and the Sass di Biss added further points of landscape and cultural interest. The presence of gold prospector Giorgio Bogni offered a touch of curiosity and entertainment during the walk. ### Activities and donations In addition to the walk, participants were able to enjoy an aperitif, lunch, and snack, making the experience even more pleasant.
