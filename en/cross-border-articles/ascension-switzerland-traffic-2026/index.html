Ascension Day: Brogeda border closed (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 13 May 2026

Changes to traffic on the A9 and closure of the Brogeda border crossing for Ascension Day in Switzerland. Here's what changes for cross-border workers

Contesto

In brief - Brogeda customs closed on May 14-15, 2026 - Traffic changes on the A9 Lainate-Como-Chiasso - Ramps closed to limit disruptions ## Key facts - What: Customs closure and traffic changes - When: May 14-15, 2026 - Where: A9 Lainate-Como-Chiasso, Brogeda customs - Who: Autostrade per l'Italia, Como Police - Amount: Not specified On the occasion of the Ascension Day bank holiday in Switzerland, scheduled for May 14-15, 2026, some traffic changes are planned on the A9 Lainate-Como-Chiasso. The Brogeda commercial customs office in Como will remain closed all day on Thursday, May 14, with reopening scheduled for the morning of Friday, May 15. This closure could cause long queues of heavy vehicles waiting for the resumption of customs activities. To avoid traffic paralysis between the Como Centro ramp and the Swiss border, Autostrade per l'Italia has announced the installation of a lane diversion, agreed with the Lombardy Road Police Department and with the support of the territorial forces of the Como Police. The aim is to facilitate the passage of cars, often penalized by the presence of heavy vehicles queuing near the customs office. ### Ramps closed on May 15 Meanwhile, to limit the formation of further queues, from 4 am to 2 pm on Friday, May 15 – and in any case until the end of the traffic regulation measures – some ramps on the A9 will be closed. In detail: - Closed the Lomazzo north ramp heading towards Chiasso - Closed the Fino Mornasco ramp heading both towards Lainate and Chiasso - Also closed the Fino Mornasco exit for those coming from Lainate For those heading towards Chiasso, Autostrade recommends using the ordinary traffic until the city customs office or entering the A9 at the Turate ramp. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the opening and closing hours of the Brogeda customs office during the Ascension Day holiday?
The Brogeda customs office will be closed all day on Thursday, May 14, 2026, with reopening scheduled for the morning of Friday, May 15, 2026.
What are the suggested alternatives to avoid queues and delays?
For those heading to Chiasso, Autostrade recommends using the regular roads up to the city customs office or entering the A9 at the Turate exit. For those traveling towards Lainate, the suggested alternative is the entrance at Lomazzo nord.
What are the restrictions on the A59 Como Ring Road?
The connecting ramps between A9 and A59 Como Ring Road will be closed for vehicles coming from Lainate in both directions. As an alternative, it is recommended to continue on the A9 towards Chiasso and exit at the Como Centro interchange.
