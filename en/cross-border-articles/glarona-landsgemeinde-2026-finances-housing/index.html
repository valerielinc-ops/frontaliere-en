Among the main topics addressed, the confirmation of the 58% tax rate for the coming year stands out, despite the difficult situation of the cantonal coffers. This decision was made to ensure financial stability in a period of economic uncertainty. This is an important factor to consider in financial planning, especially for those living in Italy and working in Switzerland. ### New deductions for commuters The possibility of deducting up to 6,000 francs for travel expenses represents a significant advantage for commuters. This change allows partially offsetting the costs incurred for daily travel between the place of residence and the workplace. For cross-border workers who regularly cross the Italian-Swiss border, this deduction can make a noticeable difference in the family budget. ### Health insurance subsidies The introduction of automatic subsidies for health insurance premiums is another important novelty. Cross-border workers residing in Italy and working in Switzerland often face high healthcare costs. What is the confirmed tax rate for 2027 in the canton of Glarus?
The confirmed tax rate for 2027 in the canton of Glarus is 58%. This decision was made during the Landsgemeinde on May 3, 2026, despite the difficult situation of the cantonal finances.
What is the new limit for deductions of travel expenses for commuters?
The new limit for deductions of travel expenses for commuters is 6000 francs. This measure was introduced as part of a savings package approved during the Landsgemeinde of Glarus.
What does the new regulation on subsidies for health insurance premiums provide?
The new regulation provides that in Glarus no one will have to spend more than 15% of their available income on health insurance premiums. Furthermore, those entitled to the reduction will receive it automatically, without having to apply.
