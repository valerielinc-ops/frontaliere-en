Car overturned at Fornasette border crossing: police and rescuers intervene (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 5 May 2026

A road accident involving a car with Italian plates occurred at the Fornasette border crossing, in the municipality of Tresa. Police, firefighters, and rescuers were on the scene.
