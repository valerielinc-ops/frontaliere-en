Dettagli operativi

The Proximity Project in Locarno not only aims to strengthen social cohesion but also offers concrete opportunities for cross-border workers living and working in Ticino. The initiatives planned by the project include cultural, sports, and educational activities, which can be accessible to all residents, regardless of their nationality. ### Cultural and Sports Activities One of the main areas of intervention of the Proximity Project is the promotion of cultural and sports activities. This includes events such as concerts, exhibitions, sports tournaments, and workshops, which can be an opportunity for cross-border workers to integrate into the local community and to better understand Ticinese culture. Participating in these activities can also help improve language skills and make new friendships. ### Training and Inclusion The project also includes training and inclusion initiatives, such as language courses, creative workshops, and mentoring programs. These programs can be particularly useful for cross-border workers who want to improve their professional skills or adapt better to life in Switzerland. Additionally, the project promotes the active participation of young people, offering opportunities for volunteering and civic engagement. ### Access to Public Services Another important aspect of the Proximity Project is facilitating access to public services for all residents, including cross-border workers. This includes promoting social, health, and educational assistance services, as well as simplifying bureaucratic procedures. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main activities promoted by the Proximity Project in Locarno?
The main activities promoted by the Proximity Project in Locarno include cultural, sporting, and educational events, such as concerts, exhibitions, sports tournaments, language courses, creative workshops, and mentoring programs. These activities are open to all residents, regardless of their nationality.
How can I sign up for the activities promoted by the Proximity Project?
To sign up for the activities promoted by the Proximity Project, you can consult the calendar of events and activities on the official website of the municipality of Locarno or through local communication channels. Registrations can be made online or at the municipal offices.
What documents are required to participate in the activities of the Proximity Project?
To participate in the activities promoted by the Proximity Project, you need to have some documents with you, such as the residence permit, identity document, certificate of residence, and any certificates of participation in previous courses.
