A2 Melide: night closures for road resurfacing

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

From Sunday 3 May to Friday 8 May 2026, the Melide exit will be closed every night from 9:30 PM to 5:00 AM for maintenance work.

Contesto

In brief - Nighttime closure of Melide A2 exit from 3 to 8 May 2026 - Road surface renewal work - Closure from 21.30 to 05.00 - Traffic diverted to the south-north tube of the tunnel - Delays of up to 20 minutes expected during peak hours ## Key facts - What: Renewal of sound-absorbing road surface - When: From 3 to 8 May 2026, nights from 21.30 to 05.00 - Where: Melide motorway exit (nr. 51) heading south - Who: Federal Roads Office USTRA - Cost: CHF 1.2 million (estimate) - Regulation: Compliance with federal road safety directives (DSS 2023) The Melide motorway exit on the A2 heading south will be closed from Sunday 3 May to Friday 8 May 2026, every night from 21.30 to 05.00. The closure is necessary for the renewal of the sound-absorbing road surface of the exit ramp, from the Melide-Grancia Tunnel portal to the roundabout in front of Melide station. This is a routine maintenance intervention aimed at restoring sound-absorbing performance and ensuring the safety of a ramp particularly stressed by traffic, with an average of 12,000 vehicles passing through per day. During the nighttime hours, to allow the work and related logistics to be carried out, traffic in the tunnel will be reduced to one lane per direction and traffic will be diverted to the south-north tube of the tunnel. The roundabout on the cantonal road will not be directly affected and will remain passable in all directions. ### Implications for cross-border workers Cross-border workers who regularly use the Melide exit to reach the Canton of Ticino will need to plan alternative routes during the nights of the closure.
