Agesp: Waste collection schedule for May 1st 2026

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

Changes to urban waste collection services in Busto Arsizio, Fagnano Olona and Venegono Superiore for Labor Day.

Contesto

In brief - Agesp administrative offices are closed on May 1st. - Waste collection in Busto Arsizio will take place as scheduled. - The service is suspended in Fagnano Olona and Venegono Superiore. - Waste collection will be recovered on May 2nd in the affected areas. ## Key facts - What: Reorganization of urban hygiene services - When: Friday, May 1st, 2026 - Where: Busto Arsizio, Fagnano Olona, Venegono Superiore - Who: Agesp Spa Group and Agesp Ambiente - Status: Administrative offices closed In view of the Labor Day holiday on May 1st, 2026, the Agesp Spa and Agesp Ambiente group has officially announced the operational plan for urban hygiene services. The schedule provides for a clear differentiation between the various municipalities managed, affecting the daily waste management for citizens and cross-border commuters who transit through or reside in these areas. In Busto Arsizio, the municipality will maintain the continuity of the door-to-door collection service. Despite the holiday, sanitation workers will operate according to standard procedures without any interruption. This decision aims to ensure urban decorum in the most populous town, avoiding waste accumulation that could result from a forced day of suspension. Conversely, the situation appears different for the neighboring towns of Fagnano Olona and Venegono Superiore. In these two municipalities, the door-to-door collection scheduled for Friday, May 1st, has been officially suspended. The calendar will therefore undergo a planned shift: waste collection operations will be recovered on Saturday, May 2nd, 2026, following the usual timings provided by standard municipal calendars. Dettagli operativi

Operational Analysis of Closures In addition to home collection, the entire administrative and public service apparatus of the Agesp group will remain inactive on the public holiday of May 1st. This total closure affects not only the management offices but also direct points of contact with the public that are fundamental for resolving bureaucratic procedures related to waste management and urban mobility. Specifically, the Tarip Office on via Pepe, the Parking Sector offices located on via Mazzini, the Territory Services at the Marzoli Mills, and the Environmental Hygiene Office on via Canale will be inaccessible. The closure also extends to crucial infrastructures for the territory. The four municipal pharmacies managed by the Agesp group, the Crematorium Temple on via per Samarate, and the Multiraccolta Center on via Tosi in Busto Arsizio will close their shutters for the entire duration of the holiday. A similar closure scenario applies to the Multiraccolta Centers in Fagnano Olona on via Nobile and in Venegono Superiore on via Ferrarin. For a cross-border worker or a citizen who had planned to dispose of bulky waste or special waste at the collection centers, it is essential to anticipate or postpone the operation. Going to the facilities on May 1st would result in a wasted trip, as access will not be allowed at any of the mentioned locations. This reorganization, although limited to the specific day, highlights the importance of consulting the municipal services to avoid logistical complications. There are no exceptions communicated for the mentioned facilities; therefore, the resumption of all administrative activities and collection centers will take place regularly on the following working day, i.e., Saturday, May 2, 2026. For residents of Busto Arsizio, no changes are required in their usual behavior: bins and bags must be displayed at the times specified by the standard calendar of their neighborhood. The regularity of the service ensures that there are no anomalies in the disposal. For those residing or working in Fagnano Olona and Venegono Superiore, the correct procedure is not to display waste on Friday, May 1st. The suspension of the service requires that the deposit be made directly in the morning or evening of Saturday, May 2nd, in accordance with the type of waste provided for that specific day of the calendar. Failure to comply with these instructions may result in waste remaining in the street, with potential aesthetic or hygienic issues. Frequently Asked Questions
Is waste collection suspended in Busto Arsizio on May 1st?
No, in Busto Arsizio, the door-to-door waste collection service is not suspended. Operators will carry out the scheduled rounds as usual, even on May 1st.
What happens with waste collection in Fagnano Olona and Venegono Superiore?
In these two municipalities, the door-to-door collection scheduled for Friday, May 1st, is suspended. The service will be resumed the following day, Saturday, May 2nd, following the normal collection schedule for that day.
Will the Multiraccolta Centers be open during Labor Day?
No, all Multiraccolta Centers managed by the Agesp group, including those in Busto Arsizio, Fagnano Olona, and Venegono Superiore, will remain closed for the entire day of May 1st, 2026.
Are the Agesp administrative offices accessible?
No, all offices and public counters of the Agesp group, including the Tarip Counter, the Parking Sector offices, and the Environmental Hygiene Counter, will remain closed during the May 1st holiday.
