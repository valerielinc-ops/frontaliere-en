Elmec Innovation Summit 2026: AI, Cybersecurity & IT in Brunello

On May 14, 2026, the Elmec Innovation Summit returns to Brunello, an event dedicated to IT innovation with workshops, tech experiences, and discussions on AI, cybersecurity, and IT infrastructure.

A day dedicated to innovation and the evolution of IT infrastructures, aimed at professionals, companies, and enthusiasts in the sector. The event will focus on the main drivers redefining the technological world: infrastructures, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence. The goal is to provide an updated overview of the ongoing transformations and opportunities related to digitalization. The Elmec Innovation Summit 2026 represents a unique opportunity for IT professionals to update themselves on the latest technological innovations and to engage with industry experts. The 20 thematic workshops will cover a wide range of topics, including artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and IT infrastructures, offering participants the chance to deepen their knowledge and discover new technological solutions. ### Workshops and Tech Experience The workshops, each lasting 90 minutes, will be led by international and local experts, with a focus on practical cases. For example, the workshop on artificial intelligence will explore the implementation of AI solutions in Ticino companies such as Dufry and Banca Stato, with a case study that led to a 30% increase in operational efficiency. The 18 tech experiences will allow participants to get hands-on with the most innovative solutions and tools in the industry, offering a practical and interactive experience. Interactive sessions and live demonstrations will enable participants to see the most advanced technologies in action and to engage directly with industry experts. ### Networking Opportunities The summit will also offer numerous networking opportunities, with sessions dedicated to direct meetings with industry experts and informal socialization moments.
