Caslano defies forecasts with 311k CHF surplus

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

The municipality of Caslano closes 2025 with a surplus of 311,000 CHF, exceeding the forecasted deficit of 79,000 CHF.

Contesto

This result, described as 'significantly better' by the local Municipality, allowed for the consolidation of own capital, which reached 5.2 million francs, confirming an economically and financially balanced situation. The municipal budget benefited from significant fiscal windfalls and revenues from supplementary and special taxes. The Executive acted decisively to contain current expenditure where possible, adopting careful and targeted management measures. This prudent approach was considered fundamental in a context characterized by a generalized increase in consumer prices and costs related to social services. The Municipality emphasized the importance of maintaining responsible and sustainable investment planning, thus ensuring the long-term financial stability of the Municipality and the quality of services offered to the population.
