Frequently Asked Questions
What to do in case of an accident in a wooded area?
In case of an accident in a wooded area, try to stay calm and assess the situation. If possible, try to reach a safe area and call for help. If you can't move, try to attract attention with visual or auditory signals. Remember that rescuers are trained to intervene in emergency situations and can provide immediate assistance.
What precautions should be taken before a hike?
Before a hike, plan your route carefully and familiarize yourself with the area. Bring a map, a GPS, a charged cell phone, a flashlight, and a basic first aid kit. Inform a friend or family member of your route and expected return time. Respect warning signs and stay on marked trails.
How to contact emergency services in case of an emergency?
In case of an emergency, call the local emergency number. Make sure you have a charged cell phone and know the emergency number before you leave. If possible, provide your location and a detailed description of the situation to the rescuers.
