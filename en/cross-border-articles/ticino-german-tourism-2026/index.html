In brief - Ticino among the protagonists of Bahnsinnige Schweiz 2026 - 450,000 German overnight stays in 2025 - 8.2% growth compared to 2024 ## Key facts - What: Biennial event Bahnsinnige Schweiz - When: 23-25 April 2026 - Where: Ticino and Lugano region - Who: Ticino Tourism Agency (ATT), Lugano Tourism Board (ETL), Switzerland Tourism Germany, Ameropa Reisen - Amount: 450,000 overnight stays in 2025 The 2026 edition of 'Bahnsinnige Schweiz,' a biennial event promoting Switzerland in the German market, took place from April 23 to 25. Ticino and the Lugano region were among the main protagonists of this event, which has been a reference for promoting Switzerland in Germany since 1998. Organized by the Ticino Tourism Agency (ATT) in collaboration with the Lugano Tourism Board (ETL), Switzerland Tourism Germany, and Ameropa Reisen, the event involved 82 selected travel agents. ### Discovering the territory Participants had the opportunity to directly experience the regional tourist offer through workshops and experiential tours. After the initial stop in Chur, participants reached Lugano aboard the Bernina Express, enjoying a travel experience focused on sustainable mobility. The program in the Lugano region alternated between networking moments and workshops, held at Asilo Ciani, and numerous activities in the area. Highlights included the welcome evening at the top of Monte San Salvatore, a visit to the Hermann Hesse Museum in Montagnola, excursions to Monte Tamaro, Splash and Spa, Monte Brè, and the discovery of authentic places like Gandria and the Olive Tree Path. ### Importance of the German market The German market remains one of the pillars of Ticino tourism. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the importance of the German market for Ticino tourism?
The German market represents one of the pillars of Ticino tourism, with over 450,000 overnight stays in 2025, accounting for 10% of the total hotel industry. This market remains the second most important among the countries of origin of visitors, highlighting an 8.2% growth compared to 2024.
What are the job opportunities for cross-border workers in the tourism sector?
The hospitality, catering, and tourism sectors offer numerous employment opportunities for cross-border workers. It is important to stay informed about local regulations and take advantage of the services offered by the Ticino Tourism Agency (ATT) and the Lugano Tourism Board (ETL) to make the most of these opportunities.
