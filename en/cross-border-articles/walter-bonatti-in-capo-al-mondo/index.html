The theater's capacity of 500 seats has been chosen for its ability to offer an immersive experience to the audience. Frequently Asked Questions
Where and when is the show on Walter Bonatti?
The show 'In the head of the world. On a journey with Walter Bonatti' will be held on 21 March at 21.00 at the Teatro Sociale di Montegrino Valtravaglia. This historic place was chosen to celebrate the life and works of the explorer.
How much are the tickets for Walter Bonatti's show in Montegrino?
Tickets for the show range from 20 to 30 francs, depending on the location chosen in the theatre. It is advisable to book in advance, as entrances are limited to 150 people.
How can I book tickets for the world's leading show?
You can book your tickets for the show on Walter Bonatti both online, visiting the website of the Montegrino Social Theatre, and directly at the theatre ticket office. The availability is limited.
Who are the artists involved in the show on Walter Bonatti?
The show features actor Luca Radaelli, who plays Walter Bonatti, accompanied by musician Maurizio Aliffi. The director is curated by Federico Bario and Luca Radaelli, with sets by Marco Rossi.
