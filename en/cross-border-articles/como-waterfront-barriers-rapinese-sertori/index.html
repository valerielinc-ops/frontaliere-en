Sertori to Rapinese: 'I act, not TV appearances'

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

Regional councillor Massimo Sertori announces a meeting with Como mayor Alessandro Rapinese to discuss the lakefront parapets.

Contesto

In brief - Sertori announces meeting with Rapinese for lakeside parapets - Resources available, but evaluation underway - Goal: to return the lakeside to the people of Como ## Key facts - What: Meeting between Sertori and Rapinese - When: In the coming weeks - Where: Como - Who: Regional councillor Massimo Sertori and mayor Alessandro Rapinese - Amount: Resources available, but amount not specified At the end, after being called into question several times in the last few hours on the parapets issue, comes the intervention of the competent regional councillor Massimo Sertori. His words were clear: 'In the coming weeks, we will organize a meeting with the dynamic mayor of Como, Alessandro Rapinese. The main issue will be the handover of the construction site to the municipality, and therefore the details of future management. It will also be an opportunity to define the parapets issue once and for all.' Parapets that have sparked numerous controversies. 'I reiterate that, in my opinion, they can also not be built. In any case, having taken note of the mayor's will, we will evaluate the matter. As already known, I underline that the resources are available. In general, as done in the realization of the construction site, replacing the Administration, I am used to doing things, rather than debating in newspapers or on TV. I am happy that we have returned the lakeside of Como to the people of Como and to citizens of the world, this was the goal,' Sertori concluded. ### Controversies over the parapets The issue of parapets on the lakeside of Como has sparked numerous controversies. Dettagli operativi

Practical implications for citizens The decision regarding the parapets on Como's lakeside will have a direct impact on the daily lives of citizens and visitors. If the parapets are installed, it will be necessary to consider possible temporary restrictions on traffic and potential inconveniences during the works. Moreover, the presence of the parapets could alter the aesthetic appearance of the lakeside, influencing the experience of those who frequent it. ### Comparison with the previous situation Before the start of the works, Como's lakeside was one of the city's main tourist attractions. Its management was relatively simple, with few restrictions on traffic and particular attention to maintenance. The installation of the parapets represents a significant change from this situation, with possible both positive and negative implications. ### Possible scenarios If the parapets are implemented, it is expected that the works will begin by the end of the year. This could involve the temporary closure of some parts of the lakeside and the need to find alternative solutions for pedestrians and cyclists. Furthermore, the presence of the parapets could improve safety, reducing the risk of accidents. If, on the other hand, the decision is not to implement the parapets, the lakeside will return to its current configuration. This could mean fewer inconveniences in the short term, but also the lack of improvements in terms of safety. ### Comparisons with other cities The situation of Como's lakeside can be compared with that of other Italian cities that have faced similar problems.
