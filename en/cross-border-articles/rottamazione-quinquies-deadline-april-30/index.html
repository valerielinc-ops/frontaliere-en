Rottamazione-quinquies: apply by April 30

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

The deadline to join the rottamazione-quinquies is April 30. Here's what you need to know and how to apply.

In brief - Rottamazione-quinquies applications due by April 30 - Payments without penalties or interest - Debts from 2000 to 2023 ## Key facts - What: Rottamazione-quinquies - When: April 30, 2026 - Where: Italy - Who: Agenzia delle Entrate-Riscossione - Amount: Not specified The deadline to apply for Rottamazione-quinquies is set for April 30, 2026. The facilitated settlement of tax bills, introduced by the 2026 Budget Law, allows paying the due amount without incurring penalties, interest, or fees. The application can only be submitted electronically on the Agenzia delle Entrate-Riscossione website. This measure is particularly relevant for cross-border workers who commute to Ticino and reside in Italy, as it allows them to regularize their tax position without incurring additional costs. ### Scope of application The Rottamazione-quinquies applies to debts assigned for collection from January 1, 2000, to December 31, 2023. These include declared but unpaid taxes, omitted payment of INPS contributions (other than those requested following an assessment), and administrative penalties for road code violations assigned by the prefectures. Taxpayers can submit their application in the 'Facilitated settlement (Rottamazione-quinquies)' section, available in both the reserved and public areas of the Agenzia delle Entrate-Riscossione website. ### Application procedure To access the service, taxpayers must use SPID, CIE, CNS credentials, or, for professionals and businesses, the Agenzia delle Entrate credentials. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the deadline to join the rottamazione-quinquies?
The deadline to join the rottamazione-quinquies is set for April 30, 2026. It is important to respect this date to not miss the opportunity to join the procedure.
Who can join the rottamazione-quinquies?
Taxpayers with debts assigned for collection from January 1, 2000, to December 31, 2023, can join the rottamazione-quinquies. These debts must be derived from declared but unpaid taxes, omitted payment of INPS contributions (other than those requested following an assessment), and administrative penalties for violations of the road code assigned by the prefectures.
How do you submit the application to join the rottamazione-quinquies?
The application to join the rottamazione-quinquies can only be submitted electronically on the Agenzia delle Entrate-Riscossione website. You can access the service using SPID, CIE, CNS credentials, or, for professionals and businesses, the credentials of the Agenzia delle Entrate.
