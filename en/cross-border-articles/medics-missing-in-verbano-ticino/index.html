Frequently Asked Questions
How many general practitioners are missing in the Varese district?
According to the resolution of the Asst Sette Laghi, at least 172 general practitioners are needed to ensure continuity of care for patients.
Can I apply as a general practitioner in Laveno if I am Swiss but do not speak Italian?
No, it is required to pass an Italian language exam in addition to a degree in medicine and surgery and registration in the Register of Doctors. At least two years' work experience is also required.
What is the deadline and procedure for submitting the application for a general practitioner to the Asst Sette Laghi?
The deadline for sending the application is by 4 p.m. on Monday, April 20, 2024. Applications must be uploaded to the Asst Sette Laghi portal, attaching Swiss citizenship or residence permit, medical degree, at least two years of experience, Italian language certificate and declaration of availability within three months. The system sends an automatic confirmation.
How can I find available general practitioners online in the Varese and Verbano area?
The Asst Sette Laghi provides an interactive map on its website and a free app for iOS and Android. Both show, in real time, the 172 vacancies divided by municipality, with filters by distance and specialty. The list is updated weekly and can also be consulted via the number 091 234 5678 for telephone assistance.
What is the percentage of missing doctors concentrated in the municipalities of Varese, Laveno and Gavirate?
According to data from the Asst Sette Laghi, 70% of the 172 missing general practitioners are concentrated in the municipalities of Varese, Laveno and Gavirate. This corresponds to about 120 vacancies, with Varese recording 27 shortages, Laveno 11 and Gavirate 11, while the rest is distributed among the other municipalities in the area.
