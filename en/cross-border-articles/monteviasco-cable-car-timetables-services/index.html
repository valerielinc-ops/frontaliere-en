Frequently Asked Questions
When will the Monteviasco cable car be operational with summer hours?
The Monteviasco cable car will be operational with summer hours from June, from 10:00 to 18:00, Monday to Friday, and from 10:00 to 20:00 on weekends and holidays.
How many closing days will there be in the week for the winter time of the Monteviasco cable car?
In winter, the cable car will be closed only on Tuesday.
What documents do you need to use the Monteviasco cable car as a frontier between Italy and Switzerland?
For Italian residents using the cable car to move to Switzerland, the valid identity card for expatriation or passport is sufficient. Visa is not required for short stays up to 90 days in the Schengen area.
Is the Monteviasco cable car accessible to disabled people or to those with reduced mobility?
Yes, the cable car is equipped with cabins accessible to disabled people and to those with motor difficulties. It is advisable to book in advance by calling the dedicated number to ensure availability of assistance.
Are there tariff concessions for residents of Val Veddasca or for those who travel frequently?
Yes, residents of Val Veddasca can take advantage of discounted rates by submitting a document that you expect the residence. In addition, monthly or annual subscriptions are available for travellers often.
