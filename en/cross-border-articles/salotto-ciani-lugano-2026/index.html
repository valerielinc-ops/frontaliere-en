In brief - Event 'Il Salotto' on May 9, 2026 in Lugano - Art and music with local young artists - After party at Auberge de Lugano ## Key facts - What: Cultural event 'Il Salotto' - When: Saturday, May 9, 2026, from 17.00 to 23.00 - Where: Asilo Ciani, Lugano - Who: Organized by Art Lab Events - Cost: Not specified The event 'Il Salotto' will light up Asilo Ciani in Lugano on Saturday, May 9, 2026, from 17.00 to 23.00. This meeting of different languages, visions, and sensitivities is organized by the non-profit association Art Lab Events and sponsored by the City of Lugano. The event celebrates the union between art and music, transforming the dialogue between disciplines into an immersive experience. The protagonists are young local artists, called to share their creative path in a dynamic and contaminated context. The main guest of this edition is Mattia Labadessa, who presents himself to the public in his dual role as a cartoonist and musician. In both expressions, his stylistic signature emerges, a cynical irony embodied by the bird-man with red plumage, a symbol capable of transforming anxiety into creative resistance. Next to him, Fenice's musical proposal stands out for a never monotonous balance between rebellious texts and more intimate moments, through a fusion of pop, urban, and electronic music. The dj Kitsune, on the other hand, builds a sound path that combines house, funk, and groove in a curated and engaging selection. Space is also given to visual arts and design. Chiara Vancheri, class of 2001, uses the tufting technique to shape textile compositions that transform the childish imagination into tactile experiences suspended between dream and reality.
