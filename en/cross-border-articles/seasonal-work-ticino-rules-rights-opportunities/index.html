Frequently Asked Questions
What is the maximum duration of a seasonal contract in Ticino?
The maximum duration of a seasonal contract in Ticino is 9 months per year. This allows Italian cross-border workers to work seasonally for an extended period without needing an indefinite work permit.
What are the most common sectors for seasonal work in Ticino?
The most common sectors for seasonal work in Ticino are agriculture, tourism, and catering. These sectors offer numerous seasonal job opportunities, especially during peak seasons such as summer and the Christmas holidays.
How can I obtain the G permit to work seasonally in Ticino?
To obtain the G permit, you need to submit an application to the competent cantonal authorities, providing documents such as the employment contract and passport. The cantonal authorities review the application and, if everything is in order, issue the G permit.
