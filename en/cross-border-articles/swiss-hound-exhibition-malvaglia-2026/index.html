Swiss Hound National Show in Malvaglia: Tradition and Competition

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

On Sunday, May 3, 2026, the National Show of Swiss Hounds will take place in Malvaglia, featuring competitions and celebrations of native breeds.

Contesto

In brief - National Exhibition of Swiss Hounds in Malvaglia on May 3, 2026 - Competitions and awards for Swiss hound breeds - Participation of prestigious associations such as the Swiss Hound Club ## Key facts - What: National Exhibition of Swiss Hounds - When: Sunday, May 3, 2026 - Where: Boschetto locality in Malvaglia, Serravalle municipality (TI) - Who: Swiss Hound Club, Schweizer Niederlaufhund- und Dachsbrackenclub, Beagle Club Schweiz - Fee: Not yet specified A major event for dog lovers and hunting tradition enthusiasts is coming to Malvaglia. On Sunday, May 3, 2026, the Boschetto locality in Malvaglia, in the municipality of Serravalle, will host the National Exhibition of Swiss Hounds. This event, recognized both in Switzerland and internationally, celebrates Swiss hound breeds and hunting tradition. The day will begin at 7:30 AM with a welcome to participants, accompanied by traditional music from the Alpine horn group 'I corni da Corzönas'. Starting at 9:00 AM, canine competitions will begin, culminating in the afternoon with awards for the best dogs and breeders. Among the most anticipated awards: best male and female of each breed, best Swiss hound of the day, and best hunting dog of SNLC and Beagle breeds. But not just competitions: the event also offers moments of fun for all ages, with large green spaces, a walk along the Brenno river, a playground for children, refreshment points offering typical dishes and local products, convenient parking, and areas dedicated to dog welfare. The event will conclude at 3:30 PM. ### A significant moment for Ticino hunting dog lovers The initiative takes place at a significant time for Ticino hunting dog lovers. Frequently Asked Questions
Which breeds of Swiss hounds will be celebrated during the exhibition?
During the National Exhibition of Swiss Hounds in Malvaglia, various breeds of Swiss hounds will be celebrated, including the Swiss Hound, the Schweizer Niederlaufhund, and the Beagle. These dogs represent an important hunting tradition and are at the center of the competitions and awards planned during the event.
How can I participate in the competitions with my dog?
To participate in the competitions with your dog, you need to contact the organizing associations, such as the Swiss Hound Club, the Schweizer Niederlaufhund- und Dachsbrackenclub, or the Beagle Club Schweiz, and follow the registration procedures provided. It is important to check the requirements and deadlines to ensure participation.
What leisure moments are planned during the exhibition?
In addition to the competitions and awards, the National Exhibition of Swiss Hounds in Malvaglia offers leisure moments for all ages, such as walks along the Brenno river, playgrounds for children, refreshment points with typical dishes and local products, convenient parking, and areas dedicated to the well-being of dogs. These moments allow you to spend a day filled with fun and socialization.
