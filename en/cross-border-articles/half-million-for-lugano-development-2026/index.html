Half a million for Lugano's 2026 development (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

ERSL is providing 500,000 francs for local projects, with an 8-to-1 leverage effect for every franc invested.

Contesto

In brief - 500,000 francs for local projects in the Luganese area - Leverage effect of 8 francs for every franc invested - Informative webinar on May 20, 2026 ## Key facts - What: Luganese Regional Promotion Fund (FPRL) - When: 2026 - Where: Luganese - Who: Luganese Regional Development Authority (ERSL) - Amount: 500,000 francs The Luganese Regional Development Authority (ERSL) has announced the availability of a 500,000-franc fund for 2026, aimed at supporting local projects. This fund, known as the Luganese Regional Promotion Fund (FPRL), is a key tool to encourage small and medium-sized initiatives and to strengthen the economic development of the region. The main objective is to promote entrepreneurial projects, enhance local potential, and improve basic services, particularly in the most peripheral areas. ### 2025 Results The results of 2025 demonstrated the effectiveness of the fund: 27 projects were supported for a total of 655,291 francs in contributions. The financed initiatives generated total investments of approximately 5.5 million francs, with a significant leverage effect. For every franc allocated, about eight francs of investment were activated. This shows how the fund not only supports specific projects but also contributes to the overall socio-economic growth of the Luganese area. ### Informative Webinar To illustrate in detail the opportunities offered by the fund, ERSL is organizing a webinar titled "Do you have a project in the Luganese area? Discover how the Regional Development Authority can support you." The meeting is scheduled for May 20, 2026, at 6 PM and will be free of charge upon registration through the website www.ersl.ch. Dettagli operativi

Implications for cross-border workers Cross-border workers living in Italy and working in Lugano can benefit from this fund in several ways. Firstly, the fund can support projects that improve infrastructure and basic services, making the area more attractive for cross-border workers. Additionally, the funded projects can create new job opportunities and improve the quality of life, making Lugano a more livable and dynamic place. ### Comparison with the previous situation Before the introduction of the fund, many local projects struggled to find adequate financing. This led to slower economic growth and a lack of essential services in some areas. With the FPRL fund, the situation has improved significantly. The supported projects have not only created jobs but have also improved local infrastructure, making Lugano more attractive for cross-border workers. ### Concrete scenarios Imagine a cross-border worker has an idea for a new restaurant or a local transport service. Thanks to the FPRL fund, they could obtain a non-repayable contribution or an interest-free loan to start the project. This would not only help the cross-border worker realize their entrepreneurial dream but also improve the range of services in the area, benefiting the entire community. ### Concrete procedures To apply for funding, cross-border workers and other interested parties must visit the website www.ersl.ch and fill out the available form. It is important to provide all the requested information and demonstrate how the project will contribute to the economic and social growth of Lugano. Punti chiave

What to do to participate To participate in the FPRL fund, cross-border workers and other interested parties must follow some concrete steps. First, it is necessary to visit the website www.ersl.ch and download the application form. Subsequently, the form must be completed with all the required information, including project details, expected costs, and anticipated benefits. It is important to provide a clear and detailed description of the project, highlighting how it will contribute to the economic and social growth of Lugano. ### Deadlines and required documents Funding applications must be submitted by the deadlines indicated on the website www.ersl.ch. It is important to check the deadlines and ensure that the application is submitted in a timely manner. In addition to the completed form, further documents may be required, such as a detailed business plan, a forecast budget, and a description of the project's costs and benefits. ### Useful tools To help cross-border workers and other interested parties submit a funding application, ERSL has made several useful tools available. On the website www.ersl.ch, you can find detailed guides, examples of funded projects, and form templates. In addition, ERSL regularly organizes webinars and information meetings to provide further clarification and support. ### Final CTA If you are a cross-border worker with a project idea for Lugano, do not miss the opportunity to participate in the FPRL fund. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can participate in the FPRL fund?
The fund is aimed at individuals, legal entities, associations, foundations, patriziates, and public bodies active in the Luganese area. Contributions can be granted as non-repayable grants or as interest-free loans, up to a maximum of 50% of the investment and with a limit of 60,000 francs per project.
What are the criteria for evaluating projects?
Evaluation criteria include economic, environmental, and social sustainability, the solidity and feasibility of the initiative, the degree of innovation, the medium-to-long-term impact, and the ability to generate synergies and collaborations in the area.
How can I submit a funding application?
To submit a funding application, you need to visit the website www.ersl.ch and fill out the available form. It is important to provide all the requested information and demonstrate how the project will contribute to the economic and social growth of the Luganese area.
