Süssli also has experience in the financial sector, as well as in strategy, digitalization, and cybersecurity. Süssli, 59, was the chief of the Swiss army from 2020 to 2025. Recently, he announced his intention to run for the National Council with the PLR, representing the canton of Lucerne.
