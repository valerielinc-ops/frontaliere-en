Frequently Asked Questions
What are the requirements to apply for the FNM Autoservizi Academy?
To apply, you must be at least 21 years old, have a lower secondary school diploma, a category B driver's license, physical and psycho-attitudinal fitness for the job, a clean criminal record, and no pending charges. Additionally, you must be available to work shifts, have a passion for driving, and a good aptitude for interpersonal relationships.
How is the 3000 euro entry bonus paid?
The 3000 euro entry bonus is paid in three installments: 1000 euros upon hiring after obtaining the driver's license, 1000 euros at the 18th month after hiring, and the final installment of 1000 euros at the 36th month after hiring.
What are the additional benefits offered by FNM Autoservizi?
FNM Autoservizi offers a significant welfare package that includes meal vouchers, health services, child education, training, and leisure time. These benefits complement the salary and make the offer even more attractive for new drivers.
