Among the topics discussed were parking, traffic, and neighborhood involvement. The village square, which overlooks the ancient sacred building and the former school renovated two years ago, will be transformed into a green area. The church will also undergo improvements, although specific details have not yet been disclosed. The new green square will have an area of approximately 1,200 square meters and will be equipped with green areas, benches, and spaces for community events. The project also includes the creation of new parking spaces, with an increase of approximately 30 parking spots compared to the current situation.
