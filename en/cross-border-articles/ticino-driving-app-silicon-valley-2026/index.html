Dettagli operativi

Implications for cross-border workers The SteerBudd app could have a significant impact on cross-border workers practicing driving in Ticino. Being an app based on learning data and GPS, it can help learners improve their driving skills more efficiently, reducing the time needed to obtain a driver's license. This is particularly useful for cross-border workers who work in Switzerland and must comply with local driving regulations. Currently, a cross-border worker working in Ticino must pass a practical exam costing CHF 75 and a theoretical exam costing CHF 35. With SteerBudd, the average time to obtain a driver's license could be reduced by 20%, from 6 to 5 practical lessons, saving approximately CHF 300. For example, a cross-border worker from Chiasso practicing in Lugano could benefit from optimized routes that avoid heavy traffic, such as the main road to the city center. ### Comparison with other solutions Compared to other driving apps, SteerBudd stands out for its use of personalized learning data. While other apps offer predefined routes, SteerBudd adapts the routes based on the individual skills of the learner and the available time. This personalized approach can make driving lessons more effective and less stressful. For example, an app like Driving Test Success offers fixed routes and exam simulations, but it does not adapt the lessons to the specific needs of the learner. SteerBudd, on the other hand, uses machine learning algorithms to analyze the learner's performance and suggest personalized routes. This can be particularly useful for a cross-border worker from Mendrisio who needs to practice in areas with complex traffic, such as the Bizzarone roundabout. ### Practical scenarios Imagine being a cross-border worker practicing driving in Ticino. Frequently Asked Questions
How does SteerBudd work?
SteerBudd uses GPS and learning data to create personalized driving routes. Choose your destination and the app will suggest a route based on your skills and available time. The accompanying driver receives real-time tips and instructions.
Who can use SteerBudd?
SteerBudd is designed for learner drivers practicing their driving skills, both with an instructor and at home. It is particularly useful for cross-border workers who need to comply with local driving regulations.
What are the benefits of SteerBudd for accompanying drivers?
Accompanying drivers receive real-time tips and instructions, making driving lessons more structured and less chaotic. This improves the effectiveness of the lessons for both the learner and the accompanying driver.
