Eggs take center stage, but each village celebrates this custom linked to fertility in its own way. This rite, which takes place in spring, is a way to drive away winter and welcome the new season with joy and hope. Eggs, a symbol of rebirth and fertility, are at the heart of this celebration that brings local communities together in a moment of sharing and festivity. ### Switzerland, the country of four seas What may at first seem like a paradox is actually a unique geographical feature in Europe: Switzerland is connected to as many as four seas through its rivers.
