Frequently Asked Questions
What documents are required to cross the Chiasso customs?
To cross the Chiasso customs, you need to have valid identity documents, a G permit if you are a cross-border worker, and vehicle documents. Make sure you also have any documents related to transported goods, such as invoices or customs declarations.
What are the opening hours of the Chiasso customs?
The Chiasso customs is open 24 hours a day, but waiting times can vary significantly depending on the time. During the night and on weekends, waiting times are generally lower than during peak working hours.
