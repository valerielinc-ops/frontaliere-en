On Wednesday, May 27, 2026, an informative evening on commercial safety will be held in Locarno, with interventions from the Police and Firefighters.

In brief - Informative evening on commercial security in Locarno on May 27, 2026 - Interventions by Local Police, Cantonal Police, and Firefighters - Mandatory registration by May 22 ## Key facts - What: Informative evening on security in commercial activities - When: Wednesday, May 27, 2026 - Where: Emergency Response Center, Via Morettina 9, Locarno - Who: Society of Industrial and Artisan Traders of Locarno (Scia) - Cost: Free (mandatory registration) Following a series of events where malicious individuals or people in distress threatened shop staff, the Society of Industrial and Artisan Traders of Locarno (Scia) is organizing an informative evening dedicated to security in commercial activities. These protocols are fundamental to ensuring the safety of employees and customers. Participants will have the opportunity to learn how to react in critical situations and how to coordinate with law enforcement and firefighters. ### Interaction with Law Enforcement One of the most important aspects of the evening will be the direct interaction with representatives from the Locarno Municipal Police, the Cantonal Police, and the Locarno Fire Department. Participants will be able to ask specific questions and receive detailed answers on how to manage emergency situations. This interaction is fundamental to acquiring practical skills and better understanding safety procedures. ### Regulatory Context Safety in commercial activities is regulated by various regulations at the cantonal and federal levels. During the evening, the main regulatory aspects and the responsibilities of employers and employees will be illustrated.
