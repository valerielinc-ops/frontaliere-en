Unemployment rate set to fall in Ticino: USI forecast

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

The University of Lugano's Institute of Economic Research predicts a drop in the unemployment rate in Ticino in the coming quarters.

Contesto

In brief - Unemployment rate drops in Ticino according to USI - Forecasted at 2.62% in the II quarter 2026 - Data differentiated by gender and nationality ## Key facts - What: Forecast of the unemployment rate in Ticino - When: II and III quarter 2026 - Where: Canton Ticino - Who: Institute of Economic Research (IRE) of USI - Amount: 2.62% (II quarter 2026), 2.54% (III quarter 2026) The Institute of Economic Research (IRE) of the University of Italian Switzerland (USI) has forecast a progressive decrease in the unemployment rate in Ticino in the coming quarters. According to estimates, the SECO unemployment rate for the second quarter of 2026 should stand at 2.62%, while for the third quarter a further decrease to 2.54% is expected. These data represent a significant reduction compared to the first quarter of 2026, when the unemployment rate in Ticino rose to 3.20%, with an increase of 0.1 percentage points on an annual basis. ### Data analysis The unemployment rate for Swiss residents stood at 2.37%, while for foreign residents it was 5.00%. The variation in unemployment was around 0.1 percentage points for both Swiss and foreign residents. Analyzing the numbers by gender characteristics, the female unemployment rate was 3.07%, while the male unemployment rate recorded a value of 3.27%. The share of women in the total number of unemployed fell compared to the previous period to 43.6%, with a value significantly higher (+1.4%) than that reported last year. ### Youth unemployment The youth unemployment rate also increased compared to the previous year's value, with a figure of 5.10% (+0.2%).
