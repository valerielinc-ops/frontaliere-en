Noise control checks in Lugano from 28 to 30 April

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

Lugano's municipal police will extend noise checks from 28 to 30 April. Focus on noisy vehicles and construction sites

Contesto

In brief - Noise checks in Lugano from April 28 to 30 - Focus on noisy vehicles and construction sites - National awareness campaign ## Key facts - What: Noise checks - When: From April 28 to 30, 2024 - Where: Lugano - Who: Lugano municipal police - Amount: Not specified The municipal police of Lugano has launched a targeted prevention and control action on the territory on the occasion of the Day against Noise, scheduled for Wednesday, April 29. The initiative, extended from Tuesday, April 28 to Thursday, April 30, fits within the framework of the national awareness campaign on the effects of noise on health and quality of life. During the three days, operational activities over the 24-hour period will focus on road traffic, with particular attention to vehicles with modified or non-homologated exhausts, noisy motorcycles, unnecessary accelerations in the urban area, and improper use of the horn. On April 29, a dedicated control post will also be activated, signaled with the official poster of the campaign 'You can also die from traffic noise'. ### Targeted checks In parallel, for a few days, a noise measurement device (noise meter) will be placed in another sensitive location. Targeted checks will also be carried out at construction sites to verify compliance with the municipal ordinance on the repression of annoying and unnecessary noises. The action combines prevention and intervention: informing and raising awareness of the population remains central; in case of infringement, the measures provided for will be adopted. 'With this initiative, we want to intervene in a targeted manner on behaviors that directly affect the quality of life in the neighborhoods. Concrete actions for cross-border workers Cross-border workers residing in or working in Lugano can adopt various measures to avoid fines and contribute to the quality of life in the city. Here is a step-by-step procedure to ensure compliance with noise regulations: ### Vehicle verification 1. Exhaust check: Ensure that your vehicle's exhaust system complies with current regulations. If in doubt, consult a trusted mechanic or an authorized inspection center. 2. Noise measurement: Use a noise emission measurement device (sound level meter) to check your vehicle's noise levels. This tool can be found in many inspection centers or purchased online. 3. Documentation: Keep all documentation related to the checks carried out and any modifications made to the vehicle. ### Road behavior 1. Avoid unnecessary acceleration: Observe speed limits and avoid sudden acceleration, especially in urban areas. 2. Horn use: Use the horn only when necessary, avoiding improper use that can disturb the public peace. 3. Respect rules: Follow the instructions of the Lugano municipal police and participate in awareness initiatives promoted by the administration. ### Checks at construction sites Cross-border workers working at construction sites must ensure that activities comply with the municipal ordinance on the repression of annoying and unnecessary noise. Here are some concrete measures: 1. Working hours: Respect the established working hours and limit noisy activities during nighttime hours. 2. Equipment: Use equipment compliant with noise regulations and maintain regular maintenance to minimize noise emissions. 3.
