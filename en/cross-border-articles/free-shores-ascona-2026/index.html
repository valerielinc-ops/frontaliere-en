Free riverbanks: Ascona opens to public in winter

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

The Rive pubbliche association of Italian-speaking Switzerland celebrates two successes: inclusion in the working group for the Riverbanks Path and the winter opening of the Lido patriziale riverbank in Ascona.

Contesto

Firstly, the Ticino section of the organization has been included in the working group 'Enhancement of the Lake Shores between Minusio and Tenero-Contra', known as the Shores Path. Two representatives of Rpsi will participate in the search for a shared and innovative solution for the fruition and landscape enhancement of the lake shore between the port of Minuso-Mappo and the Verzasca river, following the intentions of the P7-Lakes and lake shores sheet of the cantonal master plan. Dettagli operativi

The inclusion of Rive Pubbliche in the working group for the Sentiero delle Rive represents a significant step towards enhancing the lakeside areas in Ticino. The participation of two representatives from Rpsi, along with representatives from the Canton, the municipalities of Minusio and Tenero, the National Sports Centre of Tenero, and the six campsites, will allow for a shared and innovative solution for the management of the shores. This project aligns with the P7-Lakes and Lakeshore Sheet of the Cantonal Director Plan, which aims to balance public and private interests. ### 📊 Data and Prospects The Canton of Ticino has allocated a budget of 500,000 Swiss francs for 2023 for the maintenance and enhancement of the lakeside areas. This fund is destined for projects like the Sentiero delle Rive, which extends for over 10 kilometres along Lake Maggiore, involving the municipalities of Locarno, Ascona, Brissago, Ronco sopra Ascona, Gerra (Gambarogno), and Vira (Gambarogno). The winter opening of the Lido patriziale shore in Ascona is another victory for the association. This initiative not only offers residents and visitors the opportunity to enjoy the natural beauty of the lake even during the colder months but could also encourage other locations to follow suit. The lakeside walk, which starts at the public bath and continues along the Lido shore, has been highly appreciated and could become a winter tradition. ### 💡 Operational Checklist - Monitoring lake levels: Installation of sensors for real-time monitoring of Lake Maggiore's levels. - Maintenance of infrastructure: Planning periodic interventions for the maintenance of walkways and green areas. - Collaboration with municipalities: Active involvement of coastal municipalities for joint management of the shores...

What are the benefits of access to free shores for cross-border workers?
