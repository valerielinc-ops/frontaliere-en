Fabio Giorgianni, Italy's most loved driver: wins Autista Stellato award (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 7 May 2026

The Varesine driver for Autoservizi Morandi wins the Autista Stellato award for the highest passenger ratings on FlixBus

Contesto

In brief - Fabio Giorgianni wins the Star Driver Award - Highest average passenger reviews on FlixBus - Awarded during the FlixStars Day in Milan ## Key facts - What: Star Driver Award - When: May 7, 2026 - Where: Milan, during the FlixStars Day - Who: Fabio Giorgianni, driver for Autoservizi Morandi - Amount: Highest average passenger reviews on FlixBus Fabio Giorgianni, a driver from Varese working for Autoservizi Morandi, has been named Italy's most beloved driver. He won the Star Driver Award for achieving the highest average passenger reviews on the FlixBus network. The award ceremony took place on May 7, 2026, in Milan, during the third edition of the FlixStars Day, an event dedicated to long-distance transport professionals. The award was given in recognition of his dedication and professionalism. Giorgianni was chosen by the passengers themselves, who evaluated the service quality through Post-Ride Surveys, questionnaires sent at the end of each trip. The reviews highlighted his empathy and professionalism, qualities that set him apart among thousands of feedback. During the ceremony, Giorgianni shared his passion for interacting with people and the importance of small courtesies, such as helping with luggage or providing useful information. According to him, these gestures can make a difference in the passengers' travel experience. ### The importance of human service The FlixStars Day emphasized the evolution of the driver's role, who is now seen not only as a technical operator but also as the human face of the service. In addition to the award given to Giorgianni, four other awards were presented for training, mentoring young drivers, on-board sales, and new beginnings. Frequently Asked Questions
How can I report a particularly kind or professional driver?
You can report a driver through the FlixBus Post-Ride Survey, which you will receive via email at the end of your trip. Complete the survey and submit your feedback within 24 hours of the trip's end.
What are the criteria for winning the Star Driver award?
The Star Driver award is based on passenger reviews collected through the Post-Ride Survey. Punctuality, cleanliness, and the quality of service provided by the driver are evaluated.
What are the benefits for cross-border workers using services with awarded drivers?
Cross-border workers can benefit from a more pleasant and less stressful travel experience, thanks to the professionalism and empathy of the awarded drivers. Small gestures of courtesy can make a difference, making the journey more relaxing and better preparing them for the workday.
