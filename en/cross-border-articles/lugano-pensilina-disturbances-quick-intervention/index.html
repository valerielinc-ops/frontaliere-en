Contesto

In brief - Heavy police intervention at Lugano Pensilina - Two lightly injured people rescued - Use of urticating spray to contain the situation ## Key facts - What: Disturbances at Lugano Pensilina - When: Shortly after 10:30 PM last night - Where: Lugano Pensilina - Who: Cantonal Police, City of Lugano Police, Ceresio Nord Police - Amount: Two lightly injured The Cantonal Police and the City of Lugano Police have clarified the details of last night's massive intervention shortly before midnight at Pensilina in Lugano. The images, which circulated widely on social media, showed several police officers forced to intervene harshly to contain a group of agitated people. These individuals threw objects at the officers. The images also revealed that the authorities used urticating spray. Contacted while the intervention was still ongoing yesterday, the Cantonal Police and the City of Lugano Police explained this morning what had happened. According to the joint statement, the disturbances were reported shortly after 10:30 PM. Based on an initial reconstruction and for reasons that the investigation will need to determine, the situation escalated, resulting in two people being lightly injured and promptly rescued. On the scene, officers from the City of Lugano Police, later joined by the Cantonal Police and the Ceresio Nord Police, as well as the rescuers from the Lugano Croce Verde, intervened. During the intervention, some individuals obstructed the work of the officers, making it necessary to use the aforementioned urticating spray. The situation normalized shortly before 11:00 PM.
