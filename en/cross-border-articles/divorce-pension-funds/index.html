Divorce: What Happens to the Pension Fund? - UBS (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 9 June 2026

The UBS announces a new rule for the pension fund in case of divorce. The new regulation aims to provide clarity and stability for cross-border workers and their spouses. According to the UBS, the new rule will ensure that the pension fund is divided fairly and in accordance with the applicable laws. The UBS emphasizes that the new regulation is designed to protect the interests of both the cross-border worker and their spouse. The exact details of the new rule are still unclear, but the UBS promises to provide more information in the coming weeks. 💡

For example, if one spouse has a pension fund of 100,000 Swiss francs, the other spouse will receive 50,000 Swiss francs. It is essential to note that this rule applies only to pension funds opened after January 1, 2020. Pension funds opened before this date will be managed in accordance with the existing rules.
