Como-Napoli at Sinigaglia: restrictions and parking

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

Contesto

In brief - Big match Como-Napoli tomorrow at 18:00 - Parking and traffic restrictions from 6:30 - Anti-alcohol ordinance from 15:00 to 21:00 ## Key facts - What: Big match Como-Napoli - When: May 2, 2026, at 18:00 - Where: Stadio Sinigaglia, Como - Who: Como municipal administration - Amount: 18 parking spots already blocked since this morning The Stadio Sinigaglia is preparing to host the last big match of the season: the challenge against Napoli, scheduled for tomorrow at 18:00, which has already sold out. To ensure public order and safety, Palazzo Cernezzi has officially announced the plan of restrictions and prohibitions that will affect the entire area surrounding the sports facility. The first restrictions – already in effect – involve the 18 parking spots located on the left side of viale Vittorio Veneto starting from 8:00. On Saturday, the day of the match, the entire stadium area will become off-limits for vehicles starting at 6:30 in the morning. Residents accustomed to parking on the street are advised to pay maximum attention to avoid the forced removal of the vehicle. ### Streets affected by restrictions Specifically, the parking and traffic ban will be active in the following streets: - Via Sinigaglia and the right side of viale Vittorio Veneto - Viale Puecher, largo Borgonovo, and piazzale Somaini - The stretch of viale Masia between piazzale Somaini and viale Rosselli - Via Pietro da Breggia, via Martinelli, via Vacchi, and via Campo Garibaldi - The parking areas of via Recchi and viale Rosselli Residents and private parking spot holders will be able to access their homes up to three hours before the start of the match, at which point the temporary gates will be closed.
