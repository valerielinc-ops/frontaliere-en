Free consultation and meetings in Cunardo and Marchirolo with the ME-TE project

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

Starting in May, a cycle of three free meetings for parents on how to deal with the various stages of their children's development, promoted by the ME-TE project in collaboration with the Family Consulting Center of the Valleys of Cadegliano Viconago.

Contesto

In brief - Free meetings for parents in Cunardo and Marchirolo - ME-TE project for family support - Monthly listening office in Cunardo ## Key facts - What: Meetings and office for parents - When: May 2026 - Where: Cunardo and Marchirolo - Who: ME-TE project and Consultorio Familiare delle Valli di Cadegliano Viconago - Cost: Free Starting in May, parents in Cunardo and Marchirolo will have the opportunity to participate in a series of free meetings dedicated to parenthood. The initiative is promoted by the ME-TE project – Center for the evolving family, in collaboration with the Consultorio Familiare delle Valli di Cadegliano Viconago. The project is funded by the Family Policies Fund 2024 of the Presidency of the Council of Ministers through Regione Lombardia and in collaboration with ATS Insubria. The meetings, led by two psychologists and psychotherapists, will offer concrete tools and moments of discussion on the delicate topic of parenthood. The first appointment will take place on May 6 in Marchirolo and on May 13 in Cunardo, with a repeat to facilitate participation. The subsequent meetings will take place on May 20 and 27, in Cunardo and Marchirolo respectively. In addition to the meetings, a reception and guidance office is active in Cunardo, in collaboration with the Municipality, open to parents and young people who wish to have a listening moment or who are experiencing educational and relational difficulties. The office is monthly and represents a simple and accessible point of reference for those who need a discussion, advice or indications on the services available in the area. ### Meeting calendar 1° May 6, 2026 20:00 – 21:30 Marchirolo Middle School 1° (repeat) May 13, 2026 20:00 – 21:30 Cunardo Middle School 2° May 20, 2026 20:00 – 21:30 Cunardo...

Dettagli operativi

The meetings offered by the ME-TE project represent a unique opportunity for parents in Cunardo and Marchirolo to discuss and find support during the various stages of their children's growth. These meetings are structured to offer not only moments of discussion but also practical tools to tackle the daily challenges of parenthood. ### Tools and Resources During the meetings, parents will have access to a series of useful tools and resources to recognize and address warning signs that may cause concern. The psychologists and psychotherapists will guide participants through practical exercises and discussions aimed at transforming the exchange between parents into a true resource. ### Community Exchange The ME-TE project also facilitates the exchange between parents from different communities, creating an opportunity for people facing similar challenges to meet. This community approach is fundamental to promoting family well-being and preventing discomfort. ### Listening Office The reception and orientation office active in Cunardo represents a crucial reference point for families. Open on a monthly basis, the office offers a moment of listening and support for those experiencing educational and relational difficulties. Access is free and open both during office hours and by appointment. ### Practical Implications For parents attending the meetings, it is important to consider how these resources can be integrated into their daily routine. For example, the practical tools offered during the meetings can be used to improve communication with children and address educational challenges more effectively.
