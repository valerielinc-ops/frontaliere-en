Swiss Market Index up 2.3% in Zurich (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 6 May 2026

The Swiss Market Index soars to around 13,352 points, with a 2.30% increase. The podium is occupied by Richemont, Amrize, and Holcim.

Contesto

In brief - Swiss Market Index at 13'352 points (+2.30%) - Richemont +6.10%, Amrize +5.13%, Holcim +4.19% - Logitech -1.90%, Alcon -5.19% (dividend) ## Key facts - What: Swiss Market Index - When: 6 May 2026 - Where: Zurich Stock Exchange - Who: Investors and listed companies - Amount: +2.30% The Swiss Market Index recorded a significant increase, reaching approximately 13'352 points, with a progress of 2.30%. This rise was supported by better-than-expected quarterly and annual results. In particular, the companies that recorded the best performances were Compagnie financière Richemont Sa (+6.10%), Amrize Ag (+5.13%) and Holcim Limited (+4.19%). On the other hand, Logitech International Sa (-1.90%) and Alcon Incorporated (-5.19%) were lagging behind, the latter with a decline to be relativized due to the detachment of a substantial dividend. ### Performance of other exchanges Other European exchanges also recorded a significant increase. These data indicate a positive trend that involves the whole of Europe. ### Exchange rates and currencies Exchange rates recorded the following variations: 91.62 cents of francs for one euro and 77.89 cents of francs for one US dollar.
