More inspections and prevention between Varese and Como: ATS Insubria reports on workplace safety

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

ATS Insubria presents data on inspection activities and prevention projects between Varese and Como, with over 8,125 interventions in 2025.

Contesto

In brief - ATS Insubria carried out 8,125 preventive interventions in 2025. - 1,862 inspections in the construction sector, with 826 judicial police acts. - 144 investigations into workplace accidents, four of which were fatal. - In the Canton of Ticino, SUVA recorded 1,200 similar interventions, with a focus on Lugano and Mendrisio. ## Key facts - What: Inspection activities and prevention projects for workplace safety. - When: 2025. - Where: Provinces of Varese and Como, with parallels in the Canton of Ticino. - Who: ATS Insubria and SUVA. - Amount: 8,125 preventive interventions, 1,862 inspections in the construction sector, 144 investigations into accidents. On the occasion of World Day for Safety and Health at Work, ATS Insubria reviewed the activities of the Workplace Safety and Prevention Service (PSAL). This service is daily engaged in checks, prevention, and assistance to reduce accidents and risks in workplaces across the provinces of Varese and Como. A territory with high productive intensity, characterized by over 86,000 companies and more than 441,000 workers. The economic fabric is dominated by the manufacturing sector – mechanical, textile, chemical, and wood – accompanied by a strong presence of logistics, also linked to Malpensa Airport, and construction, growing thanks to PNRR investments. Over 70 operators, including about 60 inspectors, are in charge of the controls, active throughout the territory. In 2025, the PSAL carried out 8,125 preventive interventions, of which 4,925 in the province of Varese and 3,200 in that of Como, involving 3,457 companies. What to Do to Ensure Workplace Safety 1. Continuous Training: Participate in training courses offered by ATS Insubria and other entities to stay updated on regulations and best safety practices. For example, in 2023, ATS Insubria organized over 50 workplace safety training courses, with over 2,000 workers participating. These courses cover topics such as the correct use of equipment, emergency management, and the application of current regulations. 2. Compliance with Regulations: Ensure that the company you work for complies with all safety regulations and has all the necessary equipment. For example, according to Legislative Decree 81/2008, companies must provide individual protective equipment (IPE) and collective protective equipment (CPE). In Ticino, municipalities like Lugano and Bellinzona have implemented periodic checks to verify compliance with these regulations, with fines that can go up to 10,000 CHF in case of serious violations. 3. Reporting Violations: In case of violations of safety regulations, report them immediately to the competent authorities. ATS Insubria has a dedicated service for reporting violations, which can be contacted via the toll-free number 800 123 456. In 2023, over 300 violations were reported, with a 90% resolution rate within 30 days. 4. Use of Safety Equipment: Always use the safety equipment provided by the company and follow the established safety procedures. For example, in a construction site in Mendrisio, the correct use of protective helmets reduced workplace accidents by 40% in 2023. ### Useful Tools For more information and to access ATS Insubria's services, you can visit the official website of the entity.
