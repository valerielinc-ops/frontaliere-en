Azzate celebrates King's Day with Dutch Consul (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

On Sunday, May 3, Azzate celebrated King's Day with markets, music, and the presence of Dutch Consul Mascha Baak.

Contesto

In brief - Azzate celebrates Koningsdag with markets and music - Presence of Dutch Consul General Mascha Baak - Dutch traditions and typical cuisine ## Key facts - What: Thirteenth edition of Koningsdag - When: Sunday, May 3, 2026 - Where: Piazza Antonio Ghiringhelli, Azzate - Who: Pro Loco Azzate and Dutch Association for Northern Italy - Cost: Not yet specified The Belvedere of Azzate abandoned its usual colors for a day to immerse itself in the vibrant energy of orange. On Sunday, May 3, Piazza Antonio Ghiringhelli hosted the thirteenth edition of Koningsdag, the Dutch national holiday that has transformed the Varesotto village into a lively corner of the Netherlands for years. The day was marked by fixed appointments: the Vrijmarkt, from 10:00 AM, the characteristic flea market managed by very young Italian, Dutch, and Belgian exhibitors, animated the square. Music and entertainment: the atmosphere was warmed up by Dutch minstrels, the notes of the It's all in The Name Rock Coverband, and traditional children's games, concluding with the disco music of OXiiGEN. The gastronomic offer allowed a real journey through the Netherlands: from meatballs (bitterballen) to fried hot dogs (frikandellen), up to the famous sweets like stroopwafels and poffertjes. The real novelty of this edition was the special Stroopwafel ice cream, a unique flavor in Italy created by the ice cream makers of Azzate specifically for the square. ### Institutional presence This year's edition was enriched by a special presence: the Consul General of the Kingdom of the Netherlands in Milan, Mascha Baak, who visited the event accompanied by the deputy mayor Giacomo Tamborini. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the origin of Koningsdag?
Koningsdag, or King's Day, is the national holiday of the Netherlands that celebrates the king's birthday. Traditionally, it is held on April 27th, but in some locations, like Azzate, it is celebrated on different dates to adapt to the local calendar.
Who organized the event in Azzate?
The event was organized by Pro Loco Azzate in collaboration with the Dutch Association for Northern Italy (De Nederlandse Vereniging voor Noord-Italie).
What are the typical Dutch dishes served during Koningsdag?
During Koningsdag in Azzate, you can taste typical Dutch dishes such as bitterballen (meatballs), frikandellen (fried hot-dogs), stroopwafels (caramel sweets), and poffertjes (small pancakes).
