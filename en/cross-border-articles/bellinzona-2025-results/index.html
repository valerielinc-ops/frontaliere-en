Bellinzona ends 2025 with smaller deficit

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

The 2025 financial report of the City of Bellinzona shows a deficit of 2.7 million francs, much lower than the 13.4 million francs forecasted

Contesto

In brief - 2025 deficit: 2.7 million CHF - Initial forecast: 13.4 million CHF - Net investments: 25 million CHF ## Key facts - What: 2025 Financial Statement of the City of Bellinzona - When: 2025 - Where: Bellinzona, Canton Ticino - Who: Municipality of Bellinzona - Amount: Deficit of 2.7 million CHF - Amount: Net investments of 25 million CHF - Amount: Residents increase: over 47,000 - Amount: Revenues above forecasts: +9.7 million CHF - Amount: Controlled expenses: -1 million CHF - Amount: Self-financing: 12.72 million CHF The 2025 Financial Statement of the City of Bellinzona showed a smaller deficit than initially forecasted. The red figure stood at 2.7 million francs, compared to the 13.4 million initially expected. The message on the 2025 Financial Statement was approved by the Municipality during the meeting on April 29 and transmitted to the Municipal Council for examination. According to the Municipality, the result is positive and allows maintaining an own capital of over 60 million francs. Despite a still difficult overall economic situation, the City continued with investments, which reached 25 million francs net. This happened against the backdrop of demographic growth, with the capital exceeding 47,000 residents in 2025. The Municipality emphasized that the municipality has now reached a cruising speed with the consolidation of structures. Among the factors that contributed to the improvement compared to the budget, the Municipality highlighted controlled expenses and revenues above expectations, including tax revenue. In particular, personnel expenses increased only in cases bound, such as for the Elderly Homes and the Somen Center.
