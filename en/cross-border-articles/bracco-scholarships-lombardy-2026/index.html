Bracco Scholarships: 11 opportunities for students from six Lombard municipalities

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

The Bracco Foundation has opened the call for 11 scholarships, with contributions up to 4,000 euros, for deserving students from Bovisio Masciago, Ceriano Laghetto, Cesano Maderno, Cogliate, Limbiate, and Solaro.

Contesto

In brief - Open call for 11 Bracco Foundation scholarships - Contributions up to 4,000 euros for deserving students - Residency required in six Lombard communes ## Key facts - What: Scholarship call - When: Applications by October 15, 2026 - Where: Communes of Bovisio Masciago, Ceriano Laghetto, Cesano Maderno, Cogliate, Limbiate, Solaro - Who: Bracco Foundation, Bracco Imaging Spa, municipal administrations - Amount: 6 scholarships of 3,000 euros, 1 scholarship of 3,000 euros in memory of Emilio Giudici, 4 scholarships of 4,000 euros The call for scholarships from the Bracco Foundation is back, an initiative involving six Lombard communes aimed at supporting the university path of deserving students. The new edition is open to young residents of Bovisio Masciago, Ceriano Laghetto, Cesano Maderno, Cogliate, Limbiate, and Solaro. The competition, promoted by the Bracco Foundation in collaboration with Bracco Imaging Spa and the involved municipal administrations, offers a total of 11 scholarships. In detail, the following are provided: 6 scholarships of 3,000 euros each reserved for students in their first three years of university, both in humanities and scientific disciplines; 1 scholarship of 3,000 euros dedicated to first-year students, established in memory of Emilio Giudici, mayor of Ceriano Laghetto from 1980 to 1990; 4 scholarships of 4,000 euros each destined for students in their second two years. The initiative is part of the "I Will Become" project by the Bracco Foundation, which for years has accompanied students in their educational path, valuing merit and promoting the link between territory, education, and the world of work. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the requirements to participate in the Fondazione Bracco scholarship program?
To participate in the program, you must reside in one of the indicated municipalities (Bovisio Masciago, Ceriano Laghetto, Cesano Maderno, Cogliate, Limbiate, Solaro), be enrolled in a degree course, and have an Isee value within the limits set by the program.
What is the deadline for submitting applications?
Applications must be submitted by October 15, 2026, through the online platform indicated in the program.
How can I verify if my application has been received correctly?
After submitting your application, you will receive a confirmation receipt. Keep this receipt for any future checks. Also, monitor the official communications from Fondazione Bracco for any updates.
