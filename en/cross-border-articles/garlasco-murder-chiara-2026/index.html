After more than a year of investigations, consultations, and an expert opinion ordered with a probatory incident, the Prosecutor's Office of Pavia has closed the investigation into the murder of Chiara Poggi, hypothesizing a reconstruction far from that set out in the sentence with which Alberto Stasi, the former boyfriend of the victim, is finishing serving 16 years in prison. The deputy prosecutor Stefano Civardi, with the public prosecutors Valentina De Stefano and Giuliana Rizza, has notified the conclusion of the investigation notice against Andrea Sempio, the longtime friend of the victim's brother, Marco, who has been defined as 'hostile' and dealing with a 'constant defense of office' of the current clerk in a telecommunications center. ### New Scientific Evidence Based on the results of the 'Bpa', the analysis of blood traces, entrusted to Lieutenant Colonel Andrea Berti of the Scientific Investigations Department of Cagliari, and the reading of the anatomopathologist Cristina Cattaneo, the indictment redraws the escalation of violence, which began no earlier than 9:45 am (half an hour after the time emerged in the first investigation) that led to Chiara's death.
