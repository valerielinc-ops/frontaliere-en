New echocardiography clinic opens in Busto Arsizio on May 7

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

From May 7, a new echocardiography service will be available at the Casa di Comunità in Busto Arsizio, by appointment with a medical prescription.

Contesto

In brief - New echocardiography clinic in Busto Arsizio - Opening on May 7, 2026 - Service by appointment with medical referral ## Key facts - What: New echocardiography clinic - When: From May 7, 2026 - Where: Casa di Comunità in Busto Arsizio - Who: ASST Valle Olona - Cost: Not yet specified Starting May 7, 2026, residents of Busto Arsizio will have access to a new echocardiography service at the local Casa di Comunità. This new clinic was announced by the Socio-Sanitary Director of ASST Valle Olona, John Tremamondo, who emphasized the importance of this service for proximity medicine. The clinic will operate every Thursday, from 8:30 AM to 1:30 PM, and can be accessed with a referral from the primary care physician, booking through CUP or at the PUA of the Case di Comunità. ### A step forward for local healthcare The activation of this service represents an important step forward for the local healthcare system in Busto Arsizio. The new echocardiography clinic complements the existing clinic dedicated to heart failure already operating in the Casa di Comunità of Cassano Magnago. This service was made possible thanks to the commitment of the Director of the Complex Structure of Internal Medicine with a cardiological focus, Nicola Mumoli, and his team. ### Access procedure To access the new echocardiography clinic, citizens will need to follow a simple procedure. First, they must obtain a referral from their primary care physician. Then, they can book the exam through the CUP (Single Booking Center) or at the PUA (Single Access Point) of the Case di Comunità.
