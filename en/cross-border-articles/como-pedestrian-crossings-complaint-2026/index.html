Report: dangerous pedestrian crossings in Como

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

A reader reports the critical condition of pedestrian crossings across almost the entire city of Como, making street crossings dangerous.

Contesto

According to the report, pedestrian crossings have literally disappeared in almost the entire city, making it dangerous to cross the streets. The reader waited until last October, hoping the crossings would be repainted, but nothing has been done. The situation is critical and requires urgent intervention by the competent authorities. The reader sent a signed message to the ComoZero editorial team, expressing concern about the city's road signage. She described how pedestrian crossings have literally disappeared and how crossing the street has become dangerous. This is not just one area, but the entire city. The reader also included photos from different neighborhoods in the city to demonstrate the seriousness of the situation. ### Urgency of intervention The situation described by the reader is alarming and requires immediate action.
