Jacky Marti leaves Estival Jazz after 47 years

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

The founder and artistic director of the Lugano music festival retires. The program of the 46th edition has been presented with two paid evenings.

Contesto

In brief - Jacky Marti leaves Estival Jazz after 47 years - The program of the 46th edition has been presented - Two paid evenings due to financial difficulties ## Key facts - What: Jacky Marti's retirement from Estival Jazz - When: April 28, 2026 - Where: Lugano - Who: Jacky Marti, founder and artistic director - Amount: Two paid evenings in the 46th edition The founder and artistic director of Estival Jazz, Jacky Marti, has announced his retirement after 47 years at the helm of the Lugano music festival. The decision comes following the introduction of paid evenings, a choice that left a bitter taste for Marti but which he considers legitimate. 'It has been a wonderful adventure. It is right now to leave space for others, also because the City now has other orientations,' Marti declared to the microphones of RSI. The city of Lugano has had to introduce paid evenings this year and three next year due to financial difficulties and changes in the world of music, the public, and sponsors. Marti will continue to follow the festival 'standing among the public' and cares about the name of the festival, which he considers a 'piece of history' and a 'bit of his son.' The first evening of the 46th edition, Thursday, July 9, will be dedicated to contemporary Brazilian instrumental music with the Hamilton de Holanda Trio feat. Salomão Soares & Thiago 'Big' Rabello and the Orchestra della Svizzera italiana. They will be followed by Antonello Venditti on Friday, July 10, and Ernia on Saturday, July 11, with the opening by Oddisee and Good Company. ### Implications for cross-border workers The introduction of paid evenings could influence the participation of cross-border workers, who traditionally have enjoyed free access to the festival. Frequently Asked Questions
Why did Jacky Marti decide to leave Estival Jazz?
Jacky Marti decided to leave Estival Jazz after 47 years due to changes in the music world, the audience, and sponsors, as well as financial difficulties that led to the introduction of paid evenings. Marti stated that it is right to make room for others and that the city of Lugano has new orientations.
What are the dates of the paid evenings for the 46th edition of Estival Jazz?
The 46th edition of Estival Jazz includes two paid evenings. The first evening is on Thursday, July 9th with the Hamilton de Holanda Trio feat. Salomão Soares & Thiago 'Big' Rabello and the Orchestra della Svizzera italiana. The second evening is on Friday, July 10th with Antonello Venditti. On Saturday, July 11th, the evening will be opened by Oddisee and Good Company, followed by Ernia.
