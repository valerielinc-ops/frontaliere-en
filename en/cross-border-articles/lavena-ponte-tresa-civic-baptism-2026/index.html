Who can participate in the Civic Baptism of Lavena Ponte Tresa?

When does the Civic Baptism ceremony take place?
The Civic Baptism ceremony takes place annually on April 25th, on the occasion of Liberation Day. The event begins at 11:00 AM in Piazza don Attilio Sangiorgio.
What happens during the Civic Baptism ceremony?
