Health tax for cross-border workers: repeal rejected, but cuts are coming

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 21 April 2026

The Lombardy Region has rejected the Pd's motion to abolish the health tax for cross-border workers, but is aiming for deductions and minimum rates.

Contesto

Frequently Asked Questions
What is the health tax for cross-border workers?
The health tax for cross-border workers is a fiscal levy that affects workers who reside in Switzerland and work in Lombardy. The tax was introduced to finance the national healthcare system and increase the salaries of the staff of the border Asst.
Who is subject to the health tax for cross-border workers?
The health tax for cross-border workers applies to so-called 'old cross-border workers', i.e., workers who reside in Switzerland and work in Lombardy before the new fiscal agreement between Italy and Switzerland came into force.
How can I benefit from tax deductions and discounts?
The Lombardy Region is negotiating with Rome to obtain tax deductions and discounts for cross-border workers. The details of the deductions and discounts have not yet been specified, but it is important that you stay updated on these developments.
