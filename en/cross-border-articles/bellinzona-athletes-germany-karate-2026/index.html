Young athletes Noemi Willi, Ginevra Fazioli, Delia Ignorato, and Nicolas Albanese participated in the tournament, accompanied by Salvatore Guzzetta, an international referee, and Pepo Vaghi, coach of the Swiss national team. The tournament saw the participation of 200 athletes from Germany, Austria, Italy, Czechia, and Switzerland, with about twenty members of the Swiss national team. ### Results The Bellinzona team achieved excellent results. The Swiss team, composed of Delia Ignorato, Ginevra Fazioli, and Angela Romero of Höri, won first place in the Kumite Team. Additionally, the Swiss Kata Team, consisting of Noemi Willi, Ginevra Fazioli, and Delia Ignorato, secured second place. Nicolas Albanese also contributed to the success of the Swiss team, obtaining second place in the Kumite Team alongside Lukas Kunzli from Aarau and Roman Ruppen from Visp. ### Operational checklist 1.
