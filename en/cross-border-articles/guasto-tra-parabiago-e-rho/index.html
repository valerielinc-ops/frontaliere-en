Frequently Asked Questions
How many minutes are there delays on the lines to Milan due to the breakdown between Parabiago and Rho?
Delays can be up to 30 minutes.
What are the alternatives to reach Milan if the railway line is interrupted?
In case of interruption, you can use buses or shuttles that connect the municipalities of Lake Maggiore with Milan. The journey time ranges from 1 to over 2 hours, depending on traffic.
How can I keep an eye on real-time updates on delays or cancellations?
For real-time updates, you can consult the Trenord app or website, in the 'Real Time | Lines and timetables – train search'.
What is the Italian legislation for delays in public transport and what are the consequences for operators?
Italian legislation requires public transporters to guarantee a timely service. Delays of more than 10 minutes are considered serious and must be resolved within 24 hours. Non-compliant operators may incur penalties.

Yes, you may be eligible for compensation. For example, Trenord may offer up to €50 for significant delays, depending on current legislation and the conditions of your ticket.
