Scarves waving, stadium chants, and a few tears of joy: for Gallarate fans, the night of May 3, 2026, will long remain in their memory. In Lugano, Piazza della Riforma saw a significant turnout of fans, with around 500 people gathering to celebrate the victory. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the symbolic places for football celebrations in the Varesotto area?
The symbolic places for football celebrations in the Varesotto area are Piazza Montegrappa in Varese and the fountain in Piazza San Lorenzo in Gallarate. These places are traditional stages for Inter's triumphs and host hundreds of fans during the big nights of football.
Who led Inter to victory in the 21st scudetto?
Inter was led by Cristian Chivu, in his first season on the nerazzurri bench. Chivu thus became the second in the club's history to win the title both as a player and as a coach, a feat achieved before him only by Armando Castellazzi in 1937-38.
What was the result of the match that delivered the scudetto to Inter?
Inter won the 21st scudetto with a 2-0 victory against Parma. The goals were scored by Thuram, assisted by Zielinski, and by Mkhitaryan, perfectly served by Lautaro Martinez ten minutes from the end.
