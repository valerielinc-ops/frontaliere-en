Openjobmetis in Materia: job guidance every Thursday

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

Every Thursday afternoon, the Openjobmetis office in Materia provides support and guidance for those seeking work in the province of Varese.

Contesto

In brief - Openjobmetis office every Thursday at Materia - Hours: 14:30-18:00 - Free support for job search ## Key facts - What: Listening and guidance office for job search - When: Every Thursday from 14:30 to 18:00 - Where: Via Confalonieri 5, Sant’Alessandro di Castronno - Who: Openjobmetis S.p.A. - Cost: Free service The Openjobmetis office at Materia has been active for nearly a year, a space designed for all citizens looking for employment. Every Thursday afternoon from 14:30 to 18:00, candidates will have a concrete point of reference to find new professional opportunities in the province of Varese. Those who attend can submit their resume, receive targeted information on active positions, and discuss with experienced operators in an initial interview. The goal is to break down the barriers between job seekers and employers, providing a free and regular information office designed to facilitate access to the world of employment. Materia is located at Via Confalonieri 5, Sant’Alessandro di Castronno. No appointment is required, and the office is open every Thursday afternoon from 14:30 to 18:00. ### Who can use the office? The Openjobmetis office at Materia is open to all citizens seeking employment. It is not necessary to book an appointment; just show up during opening hours to receive support and guidance.
