Guido and Bruno Tardivo, the company's owners, shared their experiences from the first four months of this new venture. The company, which until a few months ago had left the Italian market unexplored, decided to fill this gap, and the results are already speaking for themselves. We meet Guido and Bruno Tardivo in their offices at Via Genova 15 in Cuneo. Two men who know every gram of carbon that leaves their company, every bolt, every setup choice. And who have a lot to say about the Italian market. Four months ago, you decided to seriously target the Italian public as well. How has it gone? Guido and Bruno Tardivo: The feedback we've received has been definitely above our rosiest expectations. The products in the current CBT ITALIA range have sparked high, concrete interest that has surprised us with its intensity and quality. We're not talking about superficial curiosity but about people who want to understand, who ask precise questions, who focus on the details. What strikes you about this return of interest? G. and B. Tardivo: Firstly, we register daily from private individuals an increasingly meticulous attention to understanding the type of product they intend to purchase, relating it to its real production cost.
