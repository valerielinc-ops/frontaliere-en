Disruptions on the Tilo line between Mendrisio and Malpensa: what changes (cross-border guide)

Dettagli operativi

The ongoing infrastructure works on the Tilo S50 railway line between Mendrisio and Malpensa are essential for strengthening connections between Switzerland and Italy, but in the short term they are causing significant disruptions for cross-border commuters and regular travelers. Those using this route to get to work or reach Malpensa Airport must carefully plan their journeys, accounting for longer travel times and schedule changes. According to Tilo estimates, the works will last until October 31, 2024, with possible extensions depending on construction progress. ## Comparison with previous situation Before the works, the journey from Mendrisio to Malpensa took about 50 minutes by direct train, with a 30-minute frequency during peak hours. During the disruption period, travel times may increase to 70–80 minutes due to stops by replacement buses and alternative road routes. For example, a passenger departing from Chiasso (10 minutes from Mendrisio) will see their journey extend to 90 minutes, compared to the previous 60 minutes. This forces cross-border workers to recalculate travel times, especially those living in municipalities like Stabio, Balerna, or Morbio Inferiore, where the train was previously the fastest option. ## Practical scenarios for cross-border commuters A commuter who usually takes the 6:00 a.m. train from Mendrisio to arrive at Malpensa at 6:50 a.m. will now need to consider that the replacement bus could take up to 80 minutes. Leaving at 5:30 a.m. would get them to their destination at 6:50 a.m., but with little margin for unforeseen delays. It is therefore advisable to leave even earlier, at 5:15 a.m., particularly for those working in areas such as Malpensa Customs, where checks may require an additional 15 minutes. Frequently Asked Questions
Which trains are cancelled on Saturday, April 11?
On Saturday, April 11, the Tilo S50 train departing Mendrisio at 21:33 is cancelled between Gallarate and Malpensa Airport. The train departing Malpensa T2 at 22:13 is also cancelled between Malpensa T2 and Gallarate.
How does the replacement bus service work on Sunday, April 12?
On Sunday, April 12, all Tilo S50 connections between Varese and Malpensa Airport will be replaced by shuttle buses with no intermediate stops. Passengers must use the buses for the entire route.
Can I take my bicycle on the replacement buses?
No, bicycles are not allowed on the replacement buses. Passengers with bicycles must arrange alternative transportation.
How can I request assistance if I have a disability?
In Ticino, call the toll-free number 0800 007 102 at least one hour before departure. In Italy, communicate your needs by calling 800 210 955 at least 48 hours in advance.
Where can I find the updated schedules for the replacement buses?
Check Tilo's online timetable for train changes and replacement bus schedules. You can also use our [route planner](nav:transport) to plan your journey.
