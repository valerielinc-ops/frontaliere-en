Medical guard active for the 1st May bank holiday: what to know

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

Continuity of Care active until 8 AM on Monday 4th May. Saturday Paediatric Clinic active on 2nd May. Emergency number 116 117.

Contesto

In brief - Guardian medical service active until 8 AM on Monday, May 4th - Pediatric Clinic active on May 2nd - Green number 116 117 for both services ## Key facts - What: Continuity of Care and Pediatric Clinic - When: From May 1st to May 4th - Where: Como and province - Who: ASST Lariana - Cost: Free services On the occasion of the May 1st bank holiday, the Continuity of Care service in Como will be active until 8 AM on Monday, May 4th. This free service can be accessed by calling the green number 116 117. Additionally, the Continuity of Care service is enhanced by the Saturday Pediatric Clinic, active on May 2nd. For this free service as well, it is necessary to contact the number 116 117. ### What is Continuity of Care The Continuity of Care service is activated by calling the number 116 117 every day from 7 PM to 8 AM and 24/7 on holidays and eve of holidays. The doctors of the Continuity of Care provide a service for situations of illness that cannot be postponed to your own family doctor. They offer telephone advice or a teleconsultation if the problem can be resolved in this way, or they invite the patient to reach the nearest Continuity of Care location for an outpatient evaluation. If they deem it necessary, the doctors can carry out a home visit or activate the Emergency Service. ### What is the pediatric clinic The pediatric clinic is a service active every Saturday (non-holiday) dedicated to minors residing/domiciled in the territory of competence of ASST Lariana. It is indicated for all those cases that require non-urgent clinical advice and evaluation. On Saturday, from 8 AM to 6 PM, call the number 116 117 and a first information sheet is drawn up regarding the problem. Implications for cross-border workers For cross-border workers residing in Ticino and working in Como, it's important to know that the Continuity of Care service is free and accessible via the toll-free number 116 117. This service is particularly useful for those who cannot reach their primary care physician during holidays or bank holidays. Additionally, the Saturday Pediatric Clinic offers a valuable service for parents who need non-urgent pediatric advice. ### Comparison with the previous situation Before this initiative, cross-border workers often faced difficulties in finding medical assistance during holidays. With the activation of Continuity of Care and the Saturday Pediatric Clinic, cross-border workers can now rely on a more accessible and free service. This represents a significant improvement in the quality of healthcare offered to residents and cross-border workers. ### Practical scenarios Imagine a scenario where a cross-border worker residing in Ticino and working in Como needs medical assistance during the 1st May bank holiday. Thanks to Continuity of Care, they can call the toll-free number 116 117 and receive immediate assistance. If they have a child who needs non-urgent pediatric advice, they can use the Saturday Pediatric Clinic, calling the same toll-free number and following the described procedure. ### Concrete procedures To access the Continuity of Care and Pediatric Clinic services, follow these steps: 1. Frequently Asked Questions
How can I access Continuity of Care during the 1st May bank holiday?
To access Continuity of Care during the 1st May bank holiday, you need to call the green number 116 117. This service is active until 8 AM on Monday, 4th May, and offers free assistance for medical conditions that cannot be postponed to your regular doctor.
What does the Saturday Paediatric Clinic offer?
The Saturday Paediatric Clinic offers non-urgent clinical advice and evaluation for minors residing or living in the area served by ASST Lariana. You can schedule an appointment by calling the number 116 117 and following the described procedure.
Which are the active locations of Continuity of Care?
The active locations of Continuity of Care are in Cantù, via Domea, 4, in building P, the building that houses the Community House (it's the first one on the left when entering) and in Fino Mornasco, via Trieste 5, in the Continuity of Care premises.
