Dettagli operativi

The main driver of this increase is the value of homes, which has risen due to those working in Switzerland and benefiting from higher salaries and a strong Swiss franc. A view of the lake is one of the main attractions for buyers, but the price increases due to the ever-higher demand. According to the Region, 1,200 homes were sold on Verbano and Ceresio lakes in 2025. In 2024, 900 homes were sold. The increase is mainly due to demand from Italian and neighboring country buyers. The Region has also noted that demand for homes has increased due to the request for houses and apartments for families. The average price of homes on Verbano and Ceresio lakes has increased by 10% in 2025 compared to 2024. The highest price was recorded in the Verbano area, where buyers are willing to pay up to 1.2 million euros for a home with a view of the lake. According to the Region, demand for homes has also increased due to the request for houses and apartments for foreign investors. Frequently Asked Questions
Why are Ticino residents and cross-border commuters buying more and more houses on lakes Verbano and Ceresio?
The main reason for this is the value of the houses, which has risen due to higher wages in Switzerland and a strong Swiss franc, as well as the view of the lake.
What is the average price of real estate increased in 2025 compared to 2024 on lakes Verbano and Ceresio?
The average price has increased by 10%.
What are the tax advantages for an Italian cross-border commuter who buys a house in Ticino in 2025?
Buying in Ticino offers advantages such as inheritance tax exemption for properties under CHF 1.5 million and reduced VAT rates of 2.5% for residential properties. However, the cantonal and municipal wealth tax applies, which varies depending on the municipality.
How does the G work permit affect the financing of a property for cross-border commuters in Ticino?
The G permit allows you to apply for a mortgage loan in Switzerland up to 80% of the value of the property, but the rates are higher than in Italy. Swiss banks require stable income and residence in Ticino for at least 6 months.
What documents does an Italian cross-border commuter need to buy a house in Ticino in 2025?
You will need: ID, Swiss tax number, bank statement in CHF, proof of residence, employment contract in Switzerland and income declaration. A residence permit (G or B) is also required for non-residents.
