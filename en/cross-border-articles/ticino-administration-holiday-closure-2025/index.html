Administrative Services Closure in Ticino: 31 December 2025 (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 18 December 2025 · Updated on 27 May 2026

Afternoon of 31 December 2025, the administrative services of the Canton will be closed.

Brief notice

Dettagli operativi

The closure of cantonal administrative services on the afternoon of December 31, 2025, has practical implications for cross-border workers employed in Ticino and residing in Italy. Specifically, those requiring bureaucratic procedures related to G permits, AVS, LPP, LAMal, tax refunds, or withholding tax must plan ahead. The year-end period, traditionally busy for tax and administrative tasks, risks causing delays if the service suspension is not taken into account. For cross-border workers, the closure means that requests for documents, permit renewal applications, consultations on tax refunds, or clarifications on withholding tax will not be processed on the afternoon of December 31. It is therefore necessary to anticipate administrative procedures, especially those requiring physical presence at cantonal offices in Bellinzona, Lugano, Mendrisio, or Chiasso. Online or postal requests can be submitted, but responses will only occur after the offices reopen. ### Concrete scenarios for cross-border workers A cross-border worker needing to renew a G permit, submit an AVS application, or clarify double taxation issues must schedule their appointment in advance. Those working in the public or private sector in Ticino and planning to handle LAMal or tax refund matters should avoid visiting offices on the afternoon of December 31. The risk is having to wait until after New Year's Day for offices to reopen, with potential delays in procedures. The official communication from the Division of Resources and the Statistical Office aims to prevent misunderstandings and unnecessary waits by raising awareness among cross-border workers about the importance of planning. Punti chiave

To ensure smooth handling of administrative procedures ahead of the afternoon closure on December 31, 2025, cross-border workers should follow several practical steps: 1. Check the schedule for cantonal offices and mark the suspension of administrative services. 2. Submit requests, applications, and documents related to G permits, AVS, LPP, LAMal, refunds, and withholding tax by the morning of December 31. 3. Use the Canton Ticino's online services for non-urgent matters, keeping in mind that responses will only be provided after reopening. 4. Keep receipts and records of any documents sent, so you can prove the timeliness of your requests. 5. Schedule appointments at physical counters in the days leading up to the closure, avoiding the afternoon of December 31. The cantonal administration has not planned any exceptions or special openings: the closure applies to all services and counters. Anyone needing to manage double taxation, refunds, or withholding tax matters should act well in advance. Frequently Asked Questions
When will the cantonal administration's services be closed?
According to the official announcement, the cantonal administration's services will be closed in the afternoon of December 31, 2025. This suspension affects all public counters and offices.
Will the closure also affect AVS, LAMal, refunds, and G permit procedures?
Yes, the afternoon closure of December 31, 2025, involves all administrative procedures, including G permits, AVS, LAMal, refunds, withholding tax, and double taxation practices.
What should cross-border workers do to avoid delays in their procedures?
Cross-border workers should submit documents and requests in advance by December 31, 2025, or on the preceding days. Procedures sent afterwards will only be processed after the offices reopen.
