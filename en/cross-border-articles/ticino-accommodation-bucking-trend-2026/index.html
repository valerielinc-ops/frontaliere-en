What to do for cross-border workers For cross-border workers interested in working in the hotel or catering sector in Ticino, it is important to follow some specific procedures. First of all, it is necessary to obtain a G permit, which allows you to work in Switzerland for a determined period. This permit can be requested at the competent cantonal office or through the Ticino Canton online portal. ### Step-by-step to obtain the G permit 1. Verify the specific requirements for the hotel or catering sector. 2. Contact the competent cantonal office to obtain the G permit application form. 3. Fill out the form and attach the required documents, such as the employment contract and the certificate of employment. 4. Submit the application to the cantonal office and wait for the response. 5. Once you have obtained the permit, you can start working in Switzerland. ### Deadlines and required documents It is important to respect the deadlines for the G permit application and to ensure that you have all the necessary documents.
