Andrea's fight against childhood leukaemia (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

The story of Andrea, a child who fought leukaemia, inspired the creation of an association supporting research against childhood leukaemia.

Contesto

In brief - Andrea's story inspired the creation of the association 'Insieme ad Andrea si può - ETS' - The association supports research against childhood leukemias - It is possible to allocate the 5x1000 to the association by indicating the tax code 91061170121 ## Key facts - What: Association 'Insieme ad Andrea si può - ETS' - When: Founded after Andrea's story - Where: Gallarate, Italy - Who: Andrea's family and friends - Amount: 5x1000 allocatable with tax code 91061170121 Andrea's story has become a concrete commitment to help other children. From that difficult journey, faced with a courage that deeply moved his family and those close to him, the association 'Insieme ad Andrea si può - ETS' was born, which today supports research against childhood leukemias. In the income tax return, it is possible to support the association by allocating your 5x1000 and indicating the tax code 91061170121 in the space dedicated to third sector entities. Andrea was a child who faced illness with extraordinary strength, fighting like a true warrior. After a transplant that had reignited hope, the disease returned after just fifty days. Andrea continued to fight until the end, leaving a deep mark on all the people who were by his side. From that experience, the will to do something concrete to help other children and their families was born. Even today, despite the great advances in medicine, about 20% of children who get leukemia do not manage to recover. It is a percentage that reminds us how fundamental research is and how much there is still to do. That is why the association Insieme ad Andrea si può supports doctors and researchers engaged in the study and development of increasingly effective treatments against childhood leukemias. Dettagli operativi

Implications for the Community The establishment of the association 'Insieme ad Andrea si può – ETS' has had a significant impact on the local community. Not only has it kept Andrea's memory alive, but it has also involved an increasing number of people in the fight against childhood leukemias. The community of Gallarate and surrounding areas has responded with great generosity, participating in the organized events and financially supporting research. ### Events and Initiatives One of the most important events organized by the association is the annual race 'Una corsa per Andrea, una corsa per la vita.' The ninth edition will take place on May 10, 2026, in Jerago con Orago. This event not only raises funds but also serves as a moment of unity and solidarity, where people can run together for a common cause. Participation in such events is open to everyone and represents a concrete way to contribute to research. ### Support for Research The association 'Insieme ad Andrea si può – ETS' supports doctors and researchers engaged in the study and development of increasingly effective treatments against childhood leukemias. Research is fundamental to reducing the percentage of children who do not recover, currently around 20%. Every contribution, big or small, can make a difference in a child's life. ### How to Participate There are many ways to support the association: participate in events, become a member, contribute to fundraising, purchase solidarity gadgets, or choose the association's solidarity bomboniere. Every act of solidarity is a step forward in the fight against childhood leukemias. Procedure to support the association To support the association "Insieme ad Andrea si può – ETS", you can follow these simple steps: 1. Allocate the 5x1000: In your tax return, indicate the tax code 91061170121 in the space dedicated to third sector entities. 2. Participate in events: Take part in the annual race "Una corsa per Andrea, una corsa per la vita" or other events organized by the association. 3. Become a member: Register as a member of the association to constantly support research. 4. Contribute to fundraising: Participate in the fundraising events organized by the association. 5. Purchase solidarity gadgets: Buy the solidarity gadgets or choose the solidarity bomboniere of the association. ### Contacts and information For more information about the association and its activities, you can visit the website www.insiemeadandreasipuo.org or contact the association via email at info@insiemeadandreasipuo.org.
