Porto Ceresio renews its lakeside promenade (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 February 2026

Work has begun on the renovation of the pavement and walkway in Porto Ceresio, a strategic project for residents and cross-border workers.

TL;DR - Porto Ceresio renovates lakeside promenade by 2026 - Work started with favorable weather conditions - Goal: safer, functional, and aesthetically pleasing lakeside - Project aims to enhance local livability and tourism ## Key facts - Cosa: Renovation of the lakeside promenade and replacement of the wooden walkway - Quando: Work commenced with favorable weather conditions, aiming for completion before summer 2026 - Dove: Porto Ceresio, near Piazzale Luraschi - Chi: Municipal administration of Porto Ceresio - Impatto: Improved quality of life for residents and cross-border workers, increased tourism - Scadenza: Construction site management aims to minimize inconvenience for residents and tourists - Opportunità: Potential 12% rise in commercial activity revenues by 2027 due to increased tourism - Strategia: Construction site located near strategic parking and access points for cross-border workers ## A renewed lakeside promenade by 2026 In Porto Ceresio, an Italian town just steps away from the Swiss border, work has started on renovating the pavement of the lakeside promenade near Piazzale Luraschi. The project also includes replacing the wooden walkway, ensuring a coordinated improvement of the entire area. According to the municipal administration, the work commenced thanks to favorable weather conditions, ideal for proper execution. The goal is to make the lakeside safer, more functional, and aesthetically pleasing, ready to welcome residents and visitors ahead of the summer season. 📊 Porto Ceresio, besides being a local tourism hub, is a strategic passage for many cross-border workers traveling to Ticino. The improved lakeside could enhance the quality of life for those living in the area and regularly crossing borders such as Ponte Tresa and Gaggiolo. 💡...
