Lugano strengthens appeal for high-net-worth individuals

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

The Municipality of Lugano confirms an action plan to attract wealthy taxpayers and international investors

Contesto

In brief - Lugano strengthens its appeal for high-net-worth individuals - Action plan with international marketing - Dedicated services for relocations and settlements ## Key facts - What: Action plan to attract wealthy taxpayers - When: Throughout 2026 - Where: Lugano, Canton Ticino - Who: Lugano Municipality - Amount: Not specified The Municipality of Lugano has confirmed a coordinated action plan to enhance the city's visibility as a prime destination for wealthy taxpayers and international investors. In response to an inquiry by Christian Tresoldi, the Municipality detailed the ongoing and planned initiatives for 2026. Among these, collaborations with international agencies specializing in the relocation of high-net-worth individuals stand out. These collaborations aim to expand the network of qualified contacts and offer dedicated support services to facilitate the transfer and settlement in Lugano. The action plan also includes targeted marketing initiatives, both locally and internationally, and the systematic use of available data to support strategic decisions. The Municipality emphasizes the importance of continuing to strengthen Lugano's positioning as an attractive city for wealthy taxpayers, investors, entrepreneurs, and high-potential families. ### Benefits for the local community Among the points raised by the Lega municipal councilor is also that of the impact on the local community. The Municipality confirmed that increased attractiveness of the city, accompanied by adequate promotional and support capacity, can translate into concrete benefits for the entire territory. In addition to direct tax revenues, the settlement of new taxpayers and new activities can generate positive effects for the real estate market, the banking sector, local businesses...

Dettagli operativi

Analysis of Practical Implications The arrival of new wealthy taxpayers in Lugano could have a significant impact on the local economic fabric. Among the expected benefits are an increase in tax revenues, greater dynamism in the real estate market, and new job opportunities, especially in high-value-added service sectors. ### Impact on the Real Estate Market The settlement of new taxpayers and economic activities could increase the demand for luxury properties and related services. This could lead to an increase in prices and a greater offer of high-end services. However, it is important to monitor how these dynamics may affect the accessibility of the city for local residents. ### Job Opportunities The presence of wealthy taxpayers and entrepreneurs could create new job opportunities, especially in the financial, legal, and consulting service sectors. Furthermore, the increased attractiveness of the city could stimulate the opening of new commercial activities and services, indirectly benefiting the local economy. ### International Collaborations Collaborations with international agencies specializing in the relocation of high-net-worth individuals represent an opportunity for Lugano to expand its network of contacts and attract foreign investments. This could lead to an increase in commercial exchanges and business opportunities for local companies. ### Monitoring and Evaluation It is essential that the Municipality carefully monitors the impact of these initiatives on the local economic fabric.
