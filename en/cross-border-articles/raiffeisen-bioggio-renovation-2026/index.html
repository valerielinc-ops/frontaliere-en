Raiffeisen Bioggio: grand opening on May 9th

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

The Bioggio branch of Raiffeisen Bank Malcantone is being renovated and invites the public to the grand opening on May 9, 2026.

Contesto

In brief - Inauguration of Raiffeisen Bioggio branch on May 9, 2026 - Public event with guided tours - Renovation of spaces for greater functionality ## Key facts - What: Renovation of Raiffeisen Bank branch in Bioggio - When: May 9, 2026 - Where: Bioggio, Canton Ticino - Who: Raiffeisen Bank of Malcantone - Amount: Not yet specified In Bioggio, the Raiffeisen Bank of Malcantone branch is being renovated and invites the local community, clients, and partners to participate in a morning dedicated to discovering the new spaces. The official ceremony will begin at 10:00 AM on May 9, 2026. During the event, until 2:00 PM, the agency will be open to the public for tours of the completely renovated environments, designed to offer a modern and functional welcome. Breakfast and refreshments will be served during the event. The renovation required particular design attention, as the Bioggio agency is located in the heart of the municipality, a context that imposes strict building and architectural protection regulations. Constraints that the bank has chosen to address consciously, confirming its willingness to remain present in the heart of the country. ### The choice to stay in the heart of Bioggio 'Remaining in the heart of Bioggio, despite the construction constraints, was a deliberate choice,' emphasizes David Giamboni, President of the Management Board of Raiffeisen Bank of Malcantone. 'At a time when many realities are leaving the centers of our countries, we have decided to invest and stay. It is a concrete sign of our commitment as a cooperative bank towards the community and the territory.'
