Inarzo: Nature, Tales & Drag Queens at Oasi Festival (cross-border guide)

On Sunday, May 10, 2026, the LIPU Reserve in Inarzo opens its doors with workshops, guided tours, and shows for all ages.

In brief - Oasi della Palude Brabbia Festival on May 10, 2026 - Children's workshops and activities for adults - Queer botany show with drag queen ## Key facts - What: Oasi della Palude Brabbia Festival - When: Sunday, May 10, 2026 - Where: Riserva Naturale e Oasi LIPU Palude Brabbia, Inarzo - Who: Organized by LIPU - Cost: Free or paid activities (not specified) - Activities: Workshops, guided tours, shows Nature awakening, the call of raptors, and a touch of magic along the trails. On Sunday, May 10, 2026, the Riserva Naturale e Oasi LIPU Palude Brabbia will celebrate its eagerly awaited Oasi Festival 2026, offering a schedule of events designed to engage all age groups, from birdwatching enthusiasts to families. Morning in the skies The day will begin at 9:30 AM with the event 'Tra fischi e nitriti.' Accompanied by experts, participants will be able to observe kites, buzzards, and falcons flying over the reserve, discovering the unique characteristics of these majestic raptors through their aerial maneuvers and calls. An afternoon of discoveries Starting at 2:30 PM, the program will come alive with various simultaneous activities: Guided tours: A walk with binoculars in hand to spot flora and fauna at their peak splendor. Wild cooking: With 'Erbacce in padella,' participants will learn to recognize edible wild herbs from the Oasis' meadows, concluding with a botanical treasure hunt. For the little ones: Children aged 4 to 7 will be the protagonists of an itinerant fairy tale to restore order in the marsh, while the older ones (8-12 years) will take on the roles of naturalists and paleontologists, digging for bone artifacts and studying aquatic life with nets and microscopes. Dettagli operativi

The Festa Oasi della Palude Brabbia represents a unique opportunity to discover the biodiversity of the reserve through interactive and engaging activities. Birdwatching enthusiasts can enjoy a privileged experience thanks to the morning event dedicated to birds of prey, while families can spend a day in the sign of nature and fun. ### Activities for Families Activities for children are designed to engage the little ones in an educational and fun way. The itinerant fairy tale for children aged 4 to 7 promises to transform the swamp into a magical kingdom where the young protagonists will have to solve riddles and adventures. For older children, the opportunity to become little paleontologists and naturalists is an experience that combines learning and play. ### Botanica Queer: a unique experience The appointment with 'Botanica Queer' is undoubtedly one of the most original of the day. The drag queen Demetra will guide the public on a journey to discover plants, offering a unique and inclusive perspective on biodiversity. This type of initiative is increasingly common in nature reserves, with the aim of making the nature experience accessible and engaging for everyone. ### Practical Tips For those who decide to participate in the Festa Oasi, it is advisable to book in advance, especially for activities that involve a limited number of participants. It is also important to check the weather conditions and dress accordingly, as some activities take place outdoors. Don't forget to bring water and snacks to sustain your energy throughout the day. ### Environmental Impact The Festa Oasi della Palude Brabbia is an opportunity to raise public awareness about the importance of conserving biodiversity. Frequently Asked Questions
What activities are planned for children?
Children aged 4 to 7 can participate in an itinerant fairy tale, while children aged 8 to 12 will have the opportunity to become little paleontologists and naturalists, digging for bone remains and studying aquatic life.
How can I sign up for the activities?
To sign up for the activities, visit the official website [www.lipupaludebrabbia.it](nav:calculator) or contact the organizers at 0332.964028 and 393.9153897. Fill out the online registration form or contact the organizers directly to book.
What should I bring to participate in the Oasi Festival?
It is advisable to bring binoculars for guided tours, comfortable clothing for walking outdoors, and any specific equipment for the workshops. Don't forget to bring water and snacks to keep your energy up during the day.
