Festival of Wonder: Art, Music & Meetings in Laveno and Luino (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 5 May 2026

From May 8 to 15, five evenings of art, music, and philosophy with international guests. Discover the full program.

Contesto

In brief - Five evenings of art, music, and philosophy between Laveno and Luino - International guests and free entry for some events - From Shakespeare's theatre to mountaineering, through urban art ## Key facts - What: Festival della Meraviglia 2026 - When: From 8 to 15 May 2026 - Where: Laveno Mombello and Luino - Who: Organized by VareseNews - Cost: Free entry for some events, booking required for others The Festival della Meraviglia returns to amaze the shores of Lake Maggiore with five evenings dedicated to art, music, and philosophy. From Shakespeare's theatre to Ravo Mattoni's murals, to Lama Michel Rinpoche's philosophy, the festival explores different stories and languages together with international guests. The first evening will take place on Friday, 8 May at 21:00 at Villa Frua in Laveno Mombello with the theatrical performance 'The Tempest'. Actor Davide Lorenzo Palla, accompanied by pianist Tiziano Cannas Aghedu, will transform Shakespeare's masterpiece into an engaging show between storytelling, music, and historical curiosities. The event is by reservation only. On Saturday, 9 May, at 21:00, the former Ceramica area in Laveno will host Andrea Ravo Mattoni, the Varese artist famous for his murals. During the evening, some of Ravo's works will be projected onto the facades of the former industrial complex. The event is free entry. Laveno's program concludes on Sunday, 10 May at 21:00 with the Concert of Hope. On the stage of Villa Frua, the GAMA Quartet, a clarinet formation composed of young talents from the Novara Conservatory and multiple award-winners in international competitions, will perform. During the week, the festival will move to Luino with an evening dedicated to the mountains scheduled at Palazzo Verbania on Thursday, 14 May at 20:30....

Dettagli operativi

The Festival of Wonder 2026 is a unique opportunity to explore various forms of art and culture. The events range from theatre to music, from urban art to philosophy, offering a complete experience for all tastes. For theatre enthusiasts, the show 'The Tempest' by Shakespeare is a must-see. Actor Davide Lorenzo Palla, known for his ability to make great classics accessible, will transform the Shakespearean masterpiece into an engaging show. Live music by pianist Tiziano Cannas Aghedu will add another level of engagement, making the performance a multisensory experience. Urban art is another highlight of the festival. Andrea Ravo Mattoni, an internationally renowned artist from Varese, will bring his works to the streets of Laveno. The projection of his murals on the facades of the former industrial complex of ex-Ceramica di Laveno will be an event not to be missed. Free entry makes this event accessible to everyone, offering the opportunity to appreciate art in a unique context. Music will take center stage with the Concerto for Hope by the GAMA Quartet. This group of young talents from the Novara Conservatory has won numerous international competitions and promises to deliver an unforgettable concert. The location of Villa Frua will add a touch of elegance to the event, making it a complete cultural experience. For mountain lovers, the meeting with writer and mountaineer Enrico Camanni will be a unique opportunity to delve into the history and culture of mountaineering. Camanni, known for his books and lectures, will share his experiences and knowledge with the audience, offering a unique insight into the world of peaks.
