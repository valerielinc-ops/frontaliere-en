Born and raised in Lodano with her two sisters and three brothers, Teresina Cerini lived a simple and hardworking life. For years, she worked as a farmer, tending to the cattle in the Alzasca and Fiumina alpine pastures and producing cheese, an activity that deeply marked her life. In 1949, she married Olivo Cerini, with whom she built a family, having three children: Alma, Ezio, and Sergio.
