Frequently Asked Questions
Who can nominate a candidate for the Lumen Claro award?
Anyone can nominate a person worthy of the Lumen Claro award. It is sufficient to contact the Lions Club Varese Prealpi and send a nomination with the candidate's data and a brief description of their achievements.
How is the winner of the Lumen Claro award selected?
The winner of the Lumen Claro award is selected by a jury composed of members of the Lions Club Varese Prealpi and personalities from the cultural and business world. The jury evaluates the nominations received and selects the annual winner.
Which sectors are awarded by the Lumen Claro?
The Lumen Claro award covers a wide range of sectors, including economics, art, science, fashion, design, aerospace engineering, literature, music, and much more. The recognition highlights local excellence in a cross-cutting way.
