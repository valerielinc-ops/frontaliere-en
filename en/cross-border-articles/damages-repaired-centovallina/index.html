The reopening of the Centovallina-Vigezzina is extremely positive news for the local economy and for the tourism sector in Ticino. Commuters and tourism enthusiasts will once again be able to use this railway line to travel between Ticino and the Vigezzina Valley, enjoying a direct and efficient connection between the two regions. The Centovallina-Vigezzina line, which connects the Ticino capital Lugano to the Vigezzina Valley, was closed for several months due to damages to the line itself. The reopening was made possible thanks to the repair works carried out by the Swiss Federal Railways (SBB) and local authorities. ## Details on the Reopening - The official reopening is scheduled for June 1, 2024. - The repair works had an estimated cost of around 5 million Swiss francs. - The line will be operational with a frequency of 4 trains per day per direction. For further information on the Centovallina-Vigezzina railway line and train schedules, please consult the Swiss Federal Railways (SBB) website.
