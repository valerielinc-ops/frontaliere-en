Di Valerie Linc · Published on 5 May 2026

From May 8 to 24, Laveno Mombello and Luino host the Festival of Wonder with cultural events and meetings on history, science, and art.

In brief - Festival of Wonder returns to Laveno Mombello and Luino from May 8 to 24, 2026 - Theme of the edition: “History, stories, and little stories” - Events between philosophy, science, art, and journalism ## Key facts - What: IV edition of the Festival of Wonder - When: May 8-10 (Laveno Mombello), until May 24 (Luino) - Where: Laveno Mombello and Luino, Lake Maggiore - Who: Organized with national and international guests - Amount: Not yet specified From May 8 to 10, 2026, Laveno Mombello and Luino will transform into a crossroads of ideas and visions with the return of the Festival of Wonder. The fourth edition of the festival, dedicated to the theme “History, stories, and little stories,” weaves together philosophy, science, art, and journalism to create an open and contemporary dialogue. The festival has established itself as a cultural laboratory spread across the territory of Lake Maggiore, involving young people, institutions, and local communities. ### Program and participants The festival opens on Friday, May 8, with an introductory dialogue on journalism between “History, stories, and little stories” with Marco Giovannelli, director of VareseNews. On Saturday, May 9, the program includes workshops, exhibitions, and performances, with the participation of experts such as Stefano Caserini of the University of Parma and Ilja Leonard Pfeijffer, a multi-award-winning Dutch writer. On Sunday, May 10, the festival continues with debates and exhibitions, including “Stories make worlds” and “Where do we come from, who are we, where are we going?” ### Implications for cross-border workers For cross-border workers who work in Ticino and reside in Lombardy, the festival represents an opportunity to participate in high-level cultural events without having to trave...

Frequently Asked Questions
What is the theme of the Festival of Wonder 2026?
The theme of the Festival of Wonder 2026 is 'History, stories, and little stories.' The festival weaves together philosophy, science, art, and journalism to create an open and contemporary dialogue.
Where does the Festival of Wonder 2026 take place?
The Festival of Wonder 2026 takes place in Laveno Mombello and Luino, on Lake Maggiore. The main events are held from May 8 to 10 in Laveno Mombello, while some events continue until May 24 in Luino.
Who are some of the guests at the Festival of Wonder 2026?
Among the guests at the Festival of Wonder 2026 are Stefano Caserini from the University of Parma, Ilja Leonard Pfeijffer, an award-winning Dutch writer, and Angela Guimarães Pereira from the Joint Research Centre, CE.
