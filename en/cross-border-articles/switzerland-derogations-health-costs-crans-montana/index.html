In Switzerland, the healthcare system is based on mandatory insurance, while in Italy the system is public and universal. This difference can influence how costs are covered and reimbursed. ### Possible Scenarios 1. Scenario 1: Full exemption from healthcare costs - In this case, healthcare costs would be fully covered by Swiss or Italian authorities, with no burden on patients or their families. - Implications: This scenario could set a dangerous precedent, as it could be interpreted as an exception to the general rule. 2.
