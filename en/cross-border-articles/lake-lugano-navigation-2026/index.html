Dettagli operativi

From a technical perspective, Lugano Lake Navigation is investing in electrification projects to make lake transport more sustainable. This includes the anticipated arrival of a new electric boat, the 'Lugano Elettrico,' expected by the end of the year, which will connect Porlezza and Lugano in just 20 minutes. This will not only improve transport efficiency, reducing waiting times by 30%, but also enhance the region's attractiveness, which is crucial for commuters and tourists. The establishment of new navigation companies, such as 'Navigazione del Lago di Lugano SA,' represents a significant innovation in the region's transport landscape. Details regarding governance and the participation of lakeside municipalities, including Lugano, Melide, and Gandria, will be crucial for ensuring the future sustainability of these projects. Another innovative element is the introduction of direct weekly connections between Locarno and Cannobio, planned for 2024, which will not only facilitate travel between Italy and Switzerland but also contribute to greater economic and tourist integration between the two shores of the lake. Local authorities and the navigation company are actively addressing issues that have arisen in recent months, such as the need to coordinate schedules with public transport services, and the openness to closer collaboration with municipalities is seen as a fundamental step to gain the necessary consensus for these developments.
