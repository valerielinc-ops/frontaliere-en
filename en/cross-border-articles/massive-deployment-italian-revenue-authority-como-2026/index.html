Massive deployment of the Guardia di Finanza in Como: 36 patrols and 77 officers

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

Intense checks in Como: undocumented workers and seized drugs. Here are the details of the operation.

Contesto

In brief - 36 patrols and 77 Guardia di Finanza officers in Como - Undeclared workers found in the catering sector - Over 2 grams of hashish seized in Alto Lario ## Key facts - What: Guardia di Finanza checks - When: Weekend of April 27, 2026 - Where: Province of Como - Who: Guardia di Finanza of Como - Amount: 3 undeclared workers found A weekend of intense checks in the provincial area of Como saw the deployment of 36 patrols and 77 Guardia di Finanza officers. During the operation, 150 vehicles, 226 people, and 7 commercial establishments were checked. In Alto Lario, the Menaggio Company and the Oria Tenancy carried out targeted interventions against drug trafficking, reporting 2 individuals to the Prefecture and seizing over 2 grams of hashish. In Olgiatese, an individual was stopped with 2 seized joints. These checks follow similar operations in Erbese, where an individual was reported to the judicial authorities for possession of 12.60 grams of hashish and 997 CHF in cash. ### Discoveries in the catering sector Officers from the Como Group and the Erba Company checked 4 commercial establishments, identifying 3 undeclared workers in the catering sector. This led to the request for suspension of activity by the Territorial Labor Inspectorate. Additionally, 1 irregular worker was discovered, and 2 fines were issued for irregularities in telematic transactions. ### Implications for cross-border workers These intensified checks can have a significant impact on cross-border workers in the catering sector.
