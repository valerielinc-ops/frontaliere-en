Record passenger numbers on Swiss trains in 2026

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

Major Swiss rail companies report a 5% increase in the first quarter of 2026, while freight transport drops by 4%.

Contesto

In brief - Record number of passengers on Swiss trains in 2026 - 5% increase in the first quarter - 4% drop in freight transport ## Key facts - What: Record number of passengers on Swiss trains - When: First quarter 2026 - Where: Switzerland - Who: Swiss railway companies - Amount: 5.79 billion passenger-kilometers Major Swiss railway companies recorded a record number of passengers in the first quarter of 2026. According to the Public Transport Information Service (LITRA), passenger transport reached 5.79 billion passenger-kilometers, a 5.0% increase compared to the same period the previous year. However, compared to the previous quarter (October-December 2025), the figure is down 5.2%, reflecting a seasonal trend. ### Data analysis The 5.2% drop compared to the previous quarter is due to lower demand in the early months of the year, when leisure time, shopping, and commuting are less frequent than at the end of the year. This phenomenon is normal and is confirmed by LITRA. ### Impact on freight transport For its part, rail freight transport remains in difficulty even at the start of 2026. With 2.74 billion tonne-kilometers, the volume is down 4.0% year-on-year and 0.4% compared to the last quarter of 2025. This is the worst result for a first quarter since records began in 2014, a sign that the recovery is slow to come.
