In brief - Nearly 2000 euros raised for a playground in memory of Matteo - The funds will be used for a new playground in the oratory - Don David Maria Riboldi thanked the donors ## Key facts - What: Fundraising for a playground - When: Announced on May 4, 2026 - Where: Church of Sant'Anna, Busto Arsizio - Who: Don David Maria Riboldi - Amount: 1,935.11 euros At the Church of Sant'Anna in Busto Arsizio, nearly 2000 euros have been raised in memory of little Matteo. Don David Maria Riboldi announced that the funds will be used to create a new playground in the oratory, dedicated to Matteo's memory. The money was collected in the church's donation box over the past few days and will help keep Matteo's memory alive, as wished by his parents. Don David expressed gratitude to everyone who contributed to the fundraising, emphasizing the importance of honoring Matteo's joyful legacy by bringing smiles to the children of the oratory. ### Community implications The fundraising for the playground in memory of Matteo is an example of how the community can come together to honor a child's memory and create a space of joy for the youngest members. This type of initiative not only honors Matteo's memory but also improves the recreational infrastructure for the children of the oratory, providing them with a safe and fun place to play. ### Fundraising procedure The fundraising was organized through the donation box at the Church of Sant'Anna. Donors could contribute freely, and the total amount raised was announced by Don David Maria Riboldi. This method of collection is simple and accessible, allowing anyone to participate with a small contribution. Transparency in the management of the funds is crucial to ensure that the money raised is used for the intended purpose. ### Impact...

Frequently Asked Questions
How can I contribute to the realization of the playground?
You can contribute to the realization of the playground by donating funds through the collection box at Sant'Anna church or by participating in the design and construction phase. Contact don David Maria Riboldi for further information.
Who can use the new playground?
The new playground will be available to all children of the Sant'Anna oratory in Busto Arsizio. It will be a place of fun and entertainment for the little ones, honoring the memory of Matteo.
How will the maintenance of the playground be managed?
The maintenance of the playground will be managed through a maintenance plan created by the project committee. The community can be involved in maintenance through volunteering or local sponsorships.
