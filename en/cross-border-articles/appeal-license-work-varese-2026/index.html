Frequently Asked Questions
What should I do if my appeal for a driving license is pending for months?
If your appeal for a driving license has been pending for months, it is crucial to maintain constant communication with the court and your lawyer. Additionally, consider requesting a temporary driving permit and document every step of the process.
How can I work if I can't drive?
If you can't drive, consider working from home or finding temporary solutions that don't require using a car. Talk to your employer to find an agreement that allows you to continue working while waiting for the court's decision.
