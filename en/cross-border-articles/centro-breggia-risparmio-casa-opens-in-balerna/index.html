The Breggia Centre in Balerna is getting a makeover with the arrival of Savings House in the former Manor department. Convenience and everyday solutions tailored for cross-border workers in the Mendrisiotto region.

The arrival of Risparmio Casa at Centro Breggia in Balerna is a tangible new development for cross-border workers from the Mendrisiotto region. This change goes beyond mere product selection, directly impacting the spending habits of commuters who cross the border daily to work in Ticino. ## Before and after: what changes for cross-border workers Before the opening of Risparmio Casa, cross-border workers looking for household and cleaning products could turn to stores like Manor (now in the process of closing) or shopping centres in Como. With the new addition of Risparmio Casa, commuters now have a closer and more accessible option. Located just a few minutes from the Brogeda border crossing and the Swiss border, Centro Breggia becomes an appealing alternative for those wanting to avoid trips to Como or Varese. ### Impact on logistics and shopping time For cross-border workers who return to Italy after work each day, the option to shop at Centro Breggia can save time and fuel. According to the centre’s management, Risparmio Casa’s offerings are designed to meet the needs of those seeking everyday essentials at competitive prices. For cross-border workers, this means less traffic heading to Como and more shopping opportunities closer to the border. Punti chiave

For cross-border workers looking to take advantage of the new opportunity offered by Risparmio Casa at the Centro Breggia in Balerna, here’s what you need to know and how to plan your visit. ### How to reach Centro Breggia Centro Breggia is located at Via Breggia 10 in Balerna, just a few minutes from the Brogeda border crossing (Chiasso) and the Swiss border. For cross-border workers coming from Como or Varese, access is straightforward: - From Como: take SS35 to Valmorea, then follow signs for Chiasso/Balerna. - From Varese: take the A8 towards Milan, exit at Lomazzo, and follow SS35 towards Balerna. - Parking: the shopping centre offers ample free parking for customers. ### Opening hours and additional services Risparmio Casa will operate during the same hours as Centro Breggia. In line with Ticino regulations and the shopping centre’s policies, typical opening hours for this type of store are: - Monday-Saturday: 9:00-20:00 (hours confirmed upon opening) - Sunday: closed (except on special occasions, such as bank holidays) The centre also provides additional services for cross-border workers, including basic food products and everyday essentials. ### Practical tips for cross-border workers - Credit/debit cards: Centro Breggia accepts international credit and debit cards. However, it’s advisable to check card network coverage (Visa, Mastercard, etc.) before visiting. - Receipts: For those needing tax documentation for refunds or declarations, Centro Breggia’s stores issue receipts with Ticino VAT (7.7%). - Transport: The centre is well-connected by public transport. Frequently Asked Questions
Is 'Risparmio Casa' already operational at Centro Breggia in Balerna?
As of 16 April 2026, the opening of 'Risparmio Casa' has been officially announced but has not yet taken place. The exact opening date has not been specified in the source. The inauguration is being finalised and will be announced through the official channels of Centro Breggia.
What products can I find at 'Risparmio Casa' in Centro Breggia?
'Risparmio Casa' specialises in detergents, perfumery, cleaning products and household care items. According to the source, these include household detergents, shampoos, soaps, perfumes and personal and domestic hygiene products.
Does Centro Breggia accept foreign credit cards such as Visa or Mastercard?
Yes, Centro Breggia accepts payments with international credit and debit cards, including Visa and Mastercard. However, it is advisable to verify card coverage before visiting the centre, especially for those using cards with high spending limits.
Can I obtain a receipt with Ticino VAT for potential refunds or tax declarations?
Yes, the retail outlets at Centro Breggia regularly issue receipts with Ticino VAT at 7.7%. This document can be useful for potential refunds or tax declarations, particularly for cross-border workers employed in Ticino.
What are the opening hours of Centro Breggia after the arrival of 'Risparmio Casa'?
The opening hours of Centro Breggia remain unchanged following the arrival of 'Risparmio Casa'. According to typical shopping centre policies, the hours are: Monday-Saturday 9:00-20:00, Sunday closed (except on special occasions). The source does not specify any exceptions for 'Risparmio Casa'.
