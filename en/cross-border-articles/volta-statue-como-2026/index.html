Here's what he writes: 'Three years after my denunciation in the Municipal Council, we are back to square one: the statue of Alessandro Volta is once again overrun by weeds. Cantaluppi criticizes the administration for the lack of concrete interventions and asks for an immediate cleaning of the monument in view of next year's celebrations. ### The current situation The statue of Alessandro Volta, located in Como, is an important monument dedicated to the famous scientist and inventor. However, in recent years, the monument has been neglected, with weeds growing around and on the statue itself. This state of abandonment has been reported several times, but it seems that no adequate measures have been taken.
