What is the average actual working time in Switzerland in 2025 and how is it calculated?
In 2025, the average actual working time in Switzerland was 40 hours and 3 minutes per week. This figure is obtained by adding the contractual weekly duration (41h42) to the overtime (0h39) and subtracting the absences (2h18), as indicated by the Federal Statistical Office (FSO).
How have sickness or accident absences changed for Swiss workers from 2020 to 2025?
Between 2020 and 2025, average annual absences for health reasons decreased by 155 minutes, dropping to 8.2 days in 2025. This reduction is a post-pandemic effect, with differences between managers (5.1 days) and unskilled workers (11.8 days).
