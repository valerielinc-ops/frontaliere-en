2026 Master Workers: Leonardo leads in Varese province

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

The new Master Workers 2026 were celebrated in Milan. Leonardo S.p.A. is the company with the most awarded in the province of Varese.

Contesto

In brief - New 2026 Masters of Labour awarded in Milan - Leonardo S.p.A. with the highest number of recipients in Varese province - Recognition for at least 25 years of service and over 50 years of age ## Key facts - What: Star of Labour Merit - When: 1st May 2026 - Where: Giuseppe Verdi Conservatory, Milan - Who: Lombard civil and military authorities - Amount: Not specified On the upcoming 1st May, at the Giuseppe Verdi Conservatory in Milan, the new 2026 Masters of Labour will be celebrated, awarded with the prestigious Star of Labour Merit. This recognition rewards workers who distinguish themselves for professionalism, continuity, and concrete contribution to the country's development. The ceremony, in the presence of Lombard civil and military authorities, represents one of the most significant symbolic moments of Labour Day. Among the new awardees of the Varese provincial consulate are numerous employees of Leonardo S.p.A., headquartered in Cascina Costa di Samarate. Among them are Massimo Casolo Ginelli, Attilio Carlo Colombo, Daniele Consonni, Agostino Crespi, Eugenio Crespi, Gianluca Frattini, Alessandro Guglielmo, Marco Marzetta, Giuseppina Giovanna Panosetti, and Alessandro Tommasino. Other local companies, such as MV Agusta Motor S.p.A., Fercam S.p.A., Sistemi Quemme S.r.l., Lati Industria Termoplastici S.p.A., Quanta System S.p.A., and S.E.A. Società Esercizi Aeroportuali S.p.A., have also seen their employees receive the prestigious recognition. ### The value of the recognition The Star of Labour Merit confirms itself not only as an honor but as a tribute to the social value of work. Dettagli operativi

Implications for Workers and Companies The recognition of the Maestri del Lavoro 2026 not only rewards individual workers but also the companies that employ them. For Leonardo S.p.A., for example, this recognition represents an important milestone that underscores the constant commitment to quality and innovation. The presence of numerous awarded employees demonstrates the company's attention to the training and development of the skills of its collaborators. ### Transmission of Skills The Provincial Consul of Varese, Emilio Frascoli, highlighted the importance of transmitting skills and work culture to new generations. This recognition represents an opportunity for young workers to learn from the masters of work, acquiring valuable experience and skills for their professional future. ### Social Value of Work The Stella al Merito del Lavoro is a recognition that goes beyond a simple award. It is a tribute to the social value of work, which rewards those who, with constancy and passion, have contributed to the economic and civil growth of the country. This recognition is an example of how work can be a driving force for development and progress, not only for individuals but for the entire community. ### Comparison with Previous Years Compared to previous years, the number of recipients from the Provincial Consulate of Varese has increased significantly. This positive trend reflects the constant commitment of local companies to the quality of work and the training of their employees. Punti chiave

What to do to be considered for recognition To be considered for the recognition of Maestri del Lavoro, workers must have at least 25 years of service and be over 50 years old. Companies can propose their employees who meet these requirements, highlighting their significant contribution to the development of the company and the territory. ### Procedure for nomination The nomination procedure involves the submission of an application by the company, accompanied by documentation that certifies the worker's experience and skills. The examining commission will evaluate the nominations based on criteria of professionalism, continuity, and concrete contribution to the development of the country. ### Deadlines and timelines The deadlines and timelines for submitting nominations vary from year to year. It is important that companies inform themselves promptly to avoid missing the opportunity to propose their employees for recognition. ### Useful tools For further information and to access the nomination forms, you can visit the official website of the Provincial Consulate of Varese or contact the competent office directly. Additionally, you can consult the salary calculator to get an idea of the average wages in the sector and compare them with your expectations. ### Conclusions The recognition of Maestri del Lavoro 2026 represents an important milestone for the workers and companies of the province of Varese. This award not only rewards the commitment and professionalism of individuals but also the attention of companies towards the training and development of the skills of their employees. For young workers, this recognition represents an opportunity to learn from the masters of work and acquire valuable experiences for their professional future. Source: varesenews.it

Frequently Asked Questions
Who can be awarded the Star of Labour Merit?
Workers with at least 25 years of service and over 50 years of age, who distinguish themselves for professionalism, continuity, and concrete contribution to the country's development, can be awarded the Star of Labour Merit.
How can I nominate an employee for the recognition?
Companies can nominate their employees by submitting an application accompanied by documentation that certifies the worker's experience and skills. The examining commission will evaluate the nominations based on criteria of professionalism, continuity, and concrete contribution to the country's development.
What are the evaluation criteria for the recognition?
The evaluation criteria for the recognition include professionalism, continuity, and concrete contribution to the country's development. The examining commission will evaluate the nominations based on these criteria.
