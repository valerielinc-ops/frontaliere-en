BPER-BCC branch transfer in Varese province

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

BPER Banca is transferring six branches to the BCC Iccrea Group in the provinces of Varese and Como. Here's what changes for customers and employees.

Contesto

In brief - BPER Banca transfers six branches to the BCC Iccrea Group - The branches concerned are in the provinces of Varese and Como - Customers in distress and private banking are excluded from the transfer ## Key facts - What: Transfer of six branches from BPER Banca to the BCC Iccrea Group - When: April 30, 2026 - Where: Provinces of Varese and Como - Who: BPER Banca and BCC Iccrea Group - Amount: Not specified - Details: The branches involved are Cantello, Malnate, Biumo Inferiore in Varese, Lavena Ponte Tresa, Cernobbio, and Luino BPER Banca has signed two contracts for the overall transfer of six branches to the BCC Iccrea Group in the provinces of Varese and Como. The operation is part of the implementation of the authorizing measure of the Italian Competition Authority related to the public offer of purchase and exchange promoted by BPER on Banca Popolare di Sondrio. The branches subject to the transfer include five branches belonging to BPER – Cantello, Malnate, Biumo Inferiore in Varese, Lavena Ponte Tresa, and Cernobbio – and the branch of Luino of Banca Popolare di Sondrio. The agreement provides for the transfer of legal relationships, activities, and liabilities related to the branches, including contracts with customers and employees of the branches concerned, with the exclusion of customers classified as 'in distress' and private banking customers. The branches will be acquired by BCC Busto Garolfo and Buguggiate and by BCC Cantù, both belonging to the BCC Iccrea Group. ### Implications for Customers Customers of the branches concerned should pay attention to the official communications that will be sent by the banks involved.
