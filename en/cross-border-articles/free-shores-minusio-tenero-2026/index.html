RPSI pleased with riverbanks working group

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

RPSI joins the working group for the enhancement of the riverbanks between Minusio and Tenero-Contra. Petition for year-round free access.

Contesto

In brief - RPSI joins the working group for the shores between Minusio and Tenero-Contra - Petition for year-round free access - Winter opening of Ascona Lido shore ## Key facts - What: Inclusion of RPSI in the working group - When: 1st May 2026 - Where: Minusio and Tenero-Contra - Who: RPSI, Canton, Municipalities of Minusio and Tenero-Contra, CST, six campsites - Amount: Not yet specified RPSI (Public Shores of Italian Switzerland) announced with satisfaction its inclusion in the Working Group 'Enhancement of Lake Shores between Minusio and Tenero-Contra'. Two representatives of the association will participate in the work together with representatives of the Canton, the Municipalities of Minusio and Tenero-Contra, CST, and the six campsites involved. The aim is to identify a shared and innovative solution for the enhancement of the use and landscape of the shore stretch between the port of Minusio-Mappo and the Verzasca river. This project is in line with the guidelines of Sheet P7 - Lakes and lake shores of the cantonal directive plan and takes into account both public and private interests. Meanwhile, RPSI invites citizens to support the online petition promoted by the Giovani Verdi del Locarnese to guarantee free access to the lake shores throughout the year, not only on Lake Maggiore. The signature collection is available by clicking here. A second reason for satisfaction for the association is represented by the winter opening of the shore of the Lido patriziale of Ascona, following the example of the Sentiero delle Rive between Minusio and Tenero. The lakeside walk starts from the public bath, continues along the lido shore and reconnects to the 'golf course' route. Dettagli operativi

Analysis of Initiatives The inclusion of RPSI in the working group for the enhancement of the shores between Minusio and Tenero-Contra is an important step to ensure that the needs of citizens are taken into account. The participation of representatives from the Canton, the municipalities, and the involved campsites ensures a comprehensive and shared vision of the project. This collaborative approach is essential to find innovative solutions that satisfy both public and private interests. ### Petition for year-round free access The petition promoted by the Giovani Verdi del Locarnese aims to guarantee free access to the lake shores throughout the year. This is an ambitious goal that, if achieved, could have a significant impact on the quality of life of residents and cross-border commuters. Year-round free access to the shores would not only improve the use of public spaces but also contribute to promoting a healthier and more active lifestyle. ### Winter opening of the shores The winter opening of the Lido patriziale shore in Ascona has been an initiative highly appreciated by the population. This example demonstrates that it is possible to extend access to the shores even during the winter season, offering new opportunities for leisure and relaxation. Extending this initiative to other lakeside areas could have a positive impact on the local community, promoting greater use of public spaces. ### Implications for cross-border commuters For cross-border commuters residing in Ticino, these initiatives offer new opportunities for leisure and relaxation throughout the year. Free access to the lake shores and the winter opening of the shores represent a significant step forward towards greater use of public spaces. This could have significant consequences for both public and private properties. Frequently Asked Questions
Who participates in the working group for the enhancement of the shores between Minusio and Tenero-Contra?
The working group includes representatives from RPSI, the Canton, the municipalities of Minusio and Tenero-Contra, the CST, and the six campsites involved. The goal is to find innovative solutions for the enhancement of the shores.
How can I support the petition for free access to the lake shores all year round?
You can support the petition by signing the online petition. The petition is promoted by the Giovani Verdi del Locarnese and aims to guarantee free access to the lake shores all year round.
What are the implications of the increase in the lake's regulation level decided by Italy?
If confirmed, the increase in the lake's regulation level could require the adjustment of the reference level of Lago Maggiore, currently set at 194.50 meters above sea level. This could have significant consequences for both public and private properties.
