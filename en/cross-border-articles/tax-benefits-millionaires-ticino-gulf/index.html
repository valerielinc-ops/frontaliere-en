Dettagli operativi

Why is Ticino so attractive to millionaires fleeing the Gulf? The phenomenon is not solely linked to the war in Iran. Ticino offers a unique combination of advantages that makes it a key destination for those seeking stability and security. Housing flexibility is one of the main drivers: purchasing a property in Ticino allows individuals to establish themselves without immediately needing a Swiss residence permit. This is possible thanks to the availability of holiday homes that can be bought without specific restrictions. ### The mechanism of lump-sum tax benefits The lump-sum tax regime for foreign residents is one of the primary reasons for attraction. Under Ticino’s regulations, individuals who transfer their tax residency to Ticino and earn income abroad can opt for a taxed at a fixed rate, often lower than traditional systems, without having to declare each individual source of income. A significant advantage for those looking to protect their wealth. ### Proximity to Milan: a plus for families For families with children, Ticino offers another strategic advantage: its proximity to the Milan metropolitan area. Malpensa Airport, less than an hour away, provides direct international connections, facilitating travel for work or leisure. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the tax benefits for millionaires moving to Ticino?
Ticino offers an attractive lump-sum tax regime for foreign residents earning income abroad. This system allows individuals to pay a fixed tax amount, often lower than traditional tax structures, without declaring each income source separately. Applicants must prove that their income originates from abroad and meet all cantonal requirements.
Can I buy a house in Ticino without a residence permit?
Yes, you can purchase property in Ticino as a second home without needing a residence permit, provided it is not your primary residence. This applies especially to holiday homes, which face no specific restrictions on acquisition.
Which areas in Ticino are most sought-after for luxury property purchases?
The most desirable areas for luxury real estate in Ticino are Lugano and Ascona. According to Engel & Völkers Ticino’s database, these two locations boast 261 available properties, including lakeside villas with private pools and exclusive amenities.
How much has demand for Ticino properties increased since the war in Iran?
Following the outbreak of war in Iran, property purchase requests in Ticino have surged by 15%, as reported by Simon Incir, owner of Engel & Völkers Ticino. While no transactions have been finalised yet, initial viewings have already been scheduled.
What documents are required for a permanent move to Ticino?
To relocate permanently to Ticino, you must register with the local municipality within 14 days of arrival, obtain a residence permit (B or C, depending on the length of stay), and update your tax residency with the relevant authorities. Required documents include a passport, employment contract (if applicable), proof of residence, and tax documentation.
