Dettagli operativi

The interpellation by Quadranti raises important issues regarding the safety of cross-border workers and residents in the Canton of Ticino, particularly in areas near the border. The growing concern for safety in public transport and residential areas requires a concrete and coordinated response. ### Safety Measures in Transport Quadranti has asked the State Council whether the measures currently adopted by the FFS and other competent actors guarantee an adequate level of safety in stations and on trains. Furthermore, he asked what concrete improvements have been requested or are in the process of being implemented. Safety in public transport is crucial for cross-border workers who use public transport daily to travel between Switzerland and Italy. ### Cross-Border Cooperation Another crucial point concerns the effectiveness of cross-border cooperation with Italian authorities in preventing and combating mobile crime. Quadranti has asked whether operational tools or specific agreements are planned to strengthen such cooperation. Collaboration between law enforcement agencies of the two countries is essential to address the challenges of cross-border crime. ### Patrols by the Cantonal Police Quadranti has also requested updated data on the evolution of patrolling activities by the cantonal police in Mendrisiotto and other sensitive areas, as well as indications on the current distribution of resources. The visible presence of law enforcement is fundamental to ensuring a sense of security among citizens and cross-border workers. ### Operational Priorities of Patrols Finally, Quadranti has asked how the operational priorities of the cantonal police patrols are defined, monitored, and, if necessary, adjusted. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main concerns regarding security in the Canton of Ticino?
The main concerns involve security in public transport, particularly in stations and on trains, and the occurrence of burglaries and robberies in homes, especially in the Mendrisiotto area. Additionally, there is a widespread perception of a less visible presence of law enforcement.
What is the State Council doing to address these concerns?
The State Council is tasked with responding to the questions raised by Matteo Quadranti and presenting a concrete action plan to strengthen both the actual security and the population's perception of security. The measures adopted must be targeted and results-oriented.
How can citizens and cross-border workers contribute to improving security?
Citizens and cross-border workers can contribute by reporting incidents of insecurity to the relevant authorities, taking precautions when using public transportation, and participating in neighborhood initiatives to improve local security.
