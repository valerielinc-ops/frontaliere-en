Frequently Asked Questions
What are the new energy regulations that will come into force in Ticino from 2026?
The new cantonal energy regulations (RUEn) will be more stringent and will require the updating of many existing heating systems.
What is the tax impact in Switzerland for a cross-border commuter who receives annual savings from the new energy regulations?
Annual savings are not subject to taxes in Switzerland, as they are a reduction in expenses and not income. However, any interest earned on a high-yield account, where savings are deposited, may be subject to taxation.
What are the funding opportunities or incentives available in Ticino for the modernization of heating systems?
From 2026, the Canton of Ticino offers incentives and concessions for the replacement of obsolete heating systems, such as non-repayable grants and tax deductions, promoting sustainable investments and reducing initial costs for property owners.
