May Day concert in Rome: artists on the bill

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

The traditional event for Labour Day in Rome with Arisa, Pierpaolo Spollon and BigMama. All the artists on the bill

Contesto

In brief - Concertone del Primo Maggio in Rome with Arisa and Pierpaolo Spollon - BigMama at her third appearance as host - Paolo Belli opens the event, La Notte della Taranta closes ## Key facts - What: Concertone del Primo Maggio - When: May 1st, 2026 - Where: Piazza San Giovanni in Laterano, Rome - Who: Arisa, Pierpaolo Spollon, BigMama, Paolo Belli, La Notte della Taranta - Cost: Not specified The Concertone del Primo Maggio, a traditional event for Labor Day, will take place today in Rome at Piazza San Giovanni in Laterano. For the first time, the concert will be hosted by Arisa and Pierpaolo Spollon, joined by 'veteran' BigMama, at her third appearance. The trio announced that the event will be marked by 'lightness', spontaneity, and an authentic dialogue with young people, with music at the center to convey important messages 'without boring' them. Paolo Belli will open the TV broadcast from the square, while the Orchestra Popolare La Notte della Taranta will close the event. According to artistic director Massimo Bonelli, the presence of international guests is excluded. 'At this historical moment, Italian music is much more central to the narrative of the nation and more representative of what the country listens to', explained Bonelli. ### Artists on the bill On stage will perform, in alphabetical order, Angelica Bove, Bambole di Pezza, Birthh, Casadilego, Chiello, Delia, Ditonellapiaga, Dolcenera, Dutch Nazari, Eddie Brock, Emma Nolde, Ermal Meta, Frah Quintale, Francamente, Francesca Michielin, Fulminacci, Geolier, I Ministri, Irama, La Niña, Lea Gavino, Levante, Liftiba, Maria Antonietta e Colombre, Mobrici, Niccolò Fabi, Nico Arezzo, Okgiorgio, Paolo Belli, Primogenito, Riccardo Cocciante, Rkomi, Rob, Rocco Hunt, Roshelle, Santamarea, Sayf, Senza Cri,...
