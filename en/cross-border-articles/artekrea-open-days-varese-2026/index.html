In brief - ArteKrea Open Days from May 15 to 17 in Varese - Free activities upon reservation - Yoga, tai chi, holistic treatments, and creative workshops ## Key facts - What: ArteKrea Open Days - When: From May 15 to 17, 2026 - Where: Via Dalmazia 78, Varese - Who: ArteKrea Courses & Events - Cost: Free (upon reservation) A weekend dedicated to well-being, creativity, and personal growth. From Friday, May 15 to Sunday, May 17, ArteKrea Courses & Events organizes three days of open days at the venue in Via Dalmazia 78 in Varese, with free activities (upon reservation) open to all ages. The initiative represents an opportunity to discover the ArteKrea project up close, which was born as a stationery shop in 2024 and expanded in 2025 with a space dedicated to courses and events. A place designed to offer experiences that combine well-being, training, and creativity, thanks to the collaboration of various professionals in the field. ### Full program During the three days, you can participate in yoga activities, holistic treatments, creative workshops, and personal growth meetings, with free entry and mandatory reservation. These activities offer a moment of relaxation and well-being, which is fundamental for those who face daily traffic and the stress of commuting. Participating in yoga, tai chi, and meditation sessions can help improve the quality of life and better manage the stress accumulated during the week. ### Creative activities and socialization Creative activities such as scrapbooking workshops and book clubs also offer an opportunity for socialization and sharing, allowing cross-border workers to make new friends and feel part of a community. This is particularly important for those who live far from family and friends and are looking for a welcoming and stimulating environment. ### Practical benefits Participating in holistic treatments and creative workshops can have immediate practical benefits. For example, acupressure and reiki treatments can help reduce stress and improve concentration, while creative workshops can stimulate the mind and improve mood. These benefits can translate into greater productivity and overall well-being, both at work and in private life. ### Special event for Anime, Manga, and Card Games enthusiasts The special event dedicated to lovers of Anime, Manga, and Card Games is another opportunity for cross-border workers to have fun and socialize. Frequently Asked Questions
How can I book activities for ArteKrea's open days?
To book activities for ArteKrea's open days, you need to contact ArteKrea Corsi & Eventi by phone at 333 806 26 79 or through social media (Instagram or Facebook). Specify the activity or activities you wish to participate in and provide your personal details. You will receive confirmation of your booking via email or message.
What are the benefits of participating in these activities?
Participating in these activities can have numerous benefits, including stress reduction, improved concentration, creativity stimulation, and socialization. These activities offer a moment of relaxation and well-being, which is fundamental for those who daily face traffic and the stress of commuting.
What should I bring to participate in the creative workshops?
To participate in the creative workshops, it is recommended to bring comfortable clothing, a towel and a change of clothes for holistic treatments, material for the scrapbooking workshop (if available), and a notebook and pen for personal growth meetings.
