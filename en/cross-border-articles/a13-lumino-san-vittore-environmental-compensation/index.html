Dettagli operativi

Analysis of environmental compensation works The environmental compensation works carried out along the A13 between Lumino and San Vittore represent a significant example of how infrastructure can be sustainably integrated into the territory. These interventions not only mitigate the impact of road works but also contribute to improving the biodiversity and hydrological stability of the area. ### Floodplain renaturation One of the most important aspects of the project is the renaturation of nationally significant floodplains. These interventions have allowed space to be returned to the river, raising the water table and creating diverse aquatic environments. The presence of floodplain forests and wet meadows is fundamental for biodiversity and the hydrological stability of the entire area. These environments offer ideal habitats for numerous animal and plant species, contributing to maintaining the ecological balance of the area. ### Wildlife crossing The 42-meter-wide wildlife crossing is an innovative element that allows wild animals to safely cross the A13. This intervention is particularly important for reconnecting an ecological corridor that has been interrupted for decades and for reducing the risk of road accidents. The presence of a safe crossing for wildlife not only improves road safety but also contributes to maintaining the ecological connectivity of the territory, allowing animals to move freely between different natural areas. ### Educational trail The educational trail along the A13 offers a unique opportunity to delve into the themes related to the enhancement of the area.
