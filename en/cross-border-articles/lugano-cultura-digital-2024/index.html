The new platform includes a section dedicated to cultural operators, both professionals and non-professionals, operating in the area. ### Accessible services The new platform has been designed to make the cultural services offered by the Culture Division of the City of Lugano more accessible. In addition to the existing sections dedicated to the historical, artistic, and intangible heritage of the city, new pages have been added designed to support and promote cultural operators active in the area. ### Cultural development One of the most important innovations is the new Cultural Development section, which serves as a tool for local cultural industries. The section dedicated to cultural operators can be particularly useful for those who wish to collaborate with local associations or participate in cultural projects. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation Before the launch of the new portal, information about the cultural activities promoted by the City of Lugano was scattered across various platforms and websites.
