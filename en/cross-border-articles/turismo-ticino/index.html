Frequently Asked Questions
How much did the number of Italian tourists in Ticino increase in 2025?
According to ISTAT data, the number of Italian tourists increased by 15.6% in 2025 compared to 2024.
What was the impact of the legislation on vaccination and quarantine on foreign tourists in Ticino in 2025?
Vaccination and quarantine legislation has affected tourism, but the region has managed the situation successfully, recording a 10.3% increase in foreign tourists compared to 2024.
What is the fine for disturbing the peace after 22:00 in Ticino?
In Ticino, anyone who disturbs the peace after 22:00 is subject to an administrative fine of CHF 50. The fine is applied by the cantonal or municipal authorities and can also be accepted in the event of a repeat offense, with possible increases in the amount.
How many tourists did Locarno welcome in 2025 and what percentage of the regional flow does it represent?
In 2025, the city of Locarno recorded over 250,000 visitors, corresponding to 21% of the total of 1.2 million tourists who crossed Ticino. This makes Locarno one of the most popular destinations, especially for cultural and sporting events.
How much did the number of foreign tourists in Ticino increase in 2025 compared to 2024?
Foreign tourists increased by 10.3% in 2025 compared to 2024. Considering the total flow of 1.2 million visitors, this means about 110,000 more arrivals from outside Italy, contributing significantly to the recovery of the Ticino tourism sector.
