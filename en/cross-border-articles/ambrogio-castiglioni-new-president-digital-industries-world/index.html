Ambrogio Castiglioni new president of Digital Industries World

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

Ambrogio Castiglioni from Varese has been appointed as the new president of Digital Industries World, aiming to create an integrated ecosystem for industrial innovation.

Ambrogio Castiglioni, originally from Carnago in the province of Varese, has been officially appointed as the new president of Digital Industries World (DIW). The members' assembly confirmed Castiglioni's appointment, a prominent figure in the industrial automation landscape. With over 37 years of experience in the sector, Castiglioni brings international expertise gained in leadership roles both in Italy and Germany. Currently, he is the Sales Head EMEA Domain Motion Control for Siemens' Digital Industries, where he oversees the development and promotion of hardware and software solutions for machine builders across the EMEA region. The appointment of Castiglioni represents a bridge between technical expertise and a global strategic vision for industrial digitalization. His experience includes leading the Business Unit Motion Control at Siemens S.p.A. and serving as Head of Global Sales, Marketing, and Vertical Markets at Siemens AG. ### The vision of the new president Castiglioni stated: 'I am deeply honored to take on the presidency of this Association, and I do so with a profound sense of responsibility and a clear vision: in the era of complexity, no one can win alone. Frequently Asked Questions
Who is Ambrogio Castiglioni?
Ambrogio Castiglioni is a manager from Carnago, in the province of Varese, with over 37 years of experience in the industrial automation sector. He is currently the Sales Head EMEA Domain Motion Control for Digital Industries at Siemens.
What does Digital Industries World do?
Digital Industries World is a multidisciplinary community that works to accelerate innovation in the industrial sector. Companies, research institutions, and industry associations come together to promote digital transformation and the exchange of skills.
How can I become a member of Digital Industries World?
To become a member of Digital Industries World, you need to visit the association's website, fill out the online registration form, and provide detailed information about your professional experience. After acceptance, you can participate in the events and workshops organized by the association.
