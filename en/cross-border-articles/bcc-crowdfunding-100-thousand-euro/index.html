BCC crowdfunding: nearly 100,000 euros raised

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

BCC Busto Garolfo and Buguggiate raised nearly 100,000 euros through crowdfunding, with over 1,200 donors involved and a 141% overfunding.

Contesto

In brief - Nearly 100,000 euros raised - 1,200 donors involved - 141% overfunding ## Key facts - What: Fundraising for third-sector projects - When: First phase of the project - Where: Busto Garolfo and Buguggiate - Who: BCC Busto Garolfo and Buguggiate - Amount: Nearly 100,000 euros BCC Busto Garolfo and Buguggiate has announced the success of the first phase of the project 'Local Projects, Cooperative Heart,' raising nearly 100,000 euros with the help of over 1,200 donors and achieving 141% overfunding. The project aims to support solidarity initiatives in the area through crowdfunding. The bank has launched a new call dedicated to crowdfunding, with the goal of turning solidarity ideas into concrete actions for the territory. The event is scheduled for Wednesday, May 6th at 5 PM at the bank's headquarters on via Manzoni 50 in Busto Garolfo. The meeting is open to associations, foundations, social cooperatives, religious entities, and volunteer organizations interested in understanding how to build, finance, and promote a project through online fundraising on the Ginger platform. The bank offers a co-financing mechanism, providing a contribution equal to 30% of each selected campaign's economic goal, up to a maximum of 5,000 euros, and covering registration and training costs. ### Implications for the territory The 'Local Projects, Cooperative Heart' project has already supported eight projects, demonstrating the ability of local entities to mobilize the community and turn widespread support into concrete resources. The latest active campaign reached 40% of its goal in just twenty days, showcasing the enthusiasm and commitment of the local community. Analysis of crowdfunding opportunities The project "Local Projects, Cooperative Heart" represents a unique opportunity for third-sector organizations to fund their projects through crowdfunding. The bank offers a significant contribution, equal to 30% of each campaign's financial goal, up to a maximum of 5,000 CHF. This contribution, along with the coverage of registration and training costs, provides a decisive boost towards achieving fundraising goals. ### Comparison with other crowdfunding initiatives Compared to other crowdfunding initiatives, "Local Projects, Cooperative Heart" stands out for the co-financing mechanism offered by the bank. This mechanism not only reduces the risk for donors but also increases trust in the campaign, encouraging greater participation. Furthermore, collaboration with Ginger Crowdfunding ensures high-quality operational and strategic support, increasing the chances of campaign success. ### Success scenarios The last active campaign reached 40% of its goal in just twenty days, demonstrating the effectiveness of the adopted crowdfunding model. This success can be attributed to the strong mobilization of the local community and the concrete support provided by the bank. Organizations participating in the project can expect a high level of engagement and support from donors, thanks to the trust that the bank instills in the fundraising process. ### Implications for third-sector organizations For third-sector organizations, participating in "Local Projects, Cooperative Heart" represents an opportunity to fund projects that might otherwise not find financing.
