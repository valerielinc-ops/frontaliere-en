Cantello Asparagus Fair 2026: two weekends of celebration and flavors

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

From May 15 to 24, Cantello celebrates the white De.Co. asparagus with food, music, and markets. Here is the complete program.

Contesto

In brief - Cantello Asparagus Fair from May 15 to 24 - Two weekends of gastronomic and musical events - Markets, guided tours, and children's workshops ## Key facts - What: Cantello Asparagus Fair - When: May 15-17 and 22-24, 2026 - Where: Cantello, festival area - Who: Organized by the Municipality of Cantello - Cost: Not specified The Cantello Asparagus Fair, now in its 84th edition, returns to fill the town for two consecutive weekends, from May 15 to 24, 2026. The event, dedicated to the white De.Co. asparagus, a symbol of the area, is one of the most anticipated of the year in the province of Varese. The first weekend of the event opens on Friday, May 15, at 7 PM with an aperitif, followed at 8 PM by the opening of the restaurant. On the menu: risotto with asparagus, lasagna with asparagus, chicken in a basket, and salamelle. The evening continues with live music: from 10 PM, dancing with the group Effetto Vip, for a disco revival evening of the Seventies, Eighties, and Nineties. On Saturday, May 16, the restaurant offers two dinner shifts at 7 PM and 8:30 PM, with a menu featuring Cantello asparagus: risotto with asparagus tips, asparagus à la Bismarck, and mixed grill. The evening ends with music with 'Asparaghouse,' a tech house DJ set by Vela & Val.N. On Sunday, May 17, from 8:30 AM, the craft and flavor market opens in via Madonna di Campagna. Visitors can visit the 11th-century Romanesque church or book a 75-minute guided tour. At 10 AM, exhibition of the best asparagus bunches and awarding of the prize for the best drawing. Dettagli operativi

The Asparagus Fair in Cantello is an event that attracts numerous visitors not only from the province of Varese but also from the Canton of Ticino, thanks to its geographical proximity and the fame of the De.Co product. The white asparagus of Cantello, recognized as a typical product of the area, is at the center of this event that celebrates local gastronomic tradition. For cross-border workers who commute to Switzerland and reside in Italy, the Asparagus Fair represents an opportunity to spend a weekend focused on relaxation and good food, without having to travel too far. The proximity to the Swiss border makes Cantello an easily reachable destination, even for those who work in the Canton of Ticino. ### Practical Implications The event offers a varied program that includes not only gastronomic tastings but also musical entertainment and activities for children. This makes the event suitable for families and groups of friends, who can spend a day enjoying themselves and discovering local flavors. For those interested in participating, it is important to book in advance, especially for large groups. To participate in the Asparagus Fair in Cantello, you need to follow a few simple steps. First, it's advisable to book in advance, especially for large groups. Reservations open on Sunday, May 3rd, and are necessary for groups of more than 15 people who want the first dinner shift on Saturday or lunch on Sunday. ### Booking Procedure 1. Contact the organization: To book, you can send a WhatsApp message to the number 334 3432003, indicating your name and the number of people. 2. Confirmation of booking: Once you receive confirmation, you can proceed with payment and finalize the booking details. 3. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the days and times of the Cantello Asparagus Fair 2026?
The Cantello Asparagus Fair takes place from May 15 to 24, 2026. The program includes events every day, with evening and afternoon openings. For specific details on times and programming, consult the official event website.
Is it necessary to book to participate in the fair?
Bookings are only required for groups of more than 15 people who want the first dinner shift on Saturday or the Sunday lunch. Bookings open on Sunday, May 3, and can be made by sending a WhatsApp message to the number 334 3432003.
What activities are planned for children during the fair?
During the fair, children's workshops are planned, such as 'Riciclattoli', organized by Legambiente volunteers, where children can rebuild traditional games using recycled materials. Additionally, there are performances of artistic and rhythmic gymnastics and hip hop.
