Fair Trade Day in Ticino: breakfast events

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

On May 9, 2026, Ticino celebrates World Fair Trade Day with breakfasts, tastings, and promotions at various World Shops.

Contesto

In brief - Fair Trade Breakfast in Ticino on May 9, 2026 - Events at various World Shops - Promotions and tastings of fair trade products ## Key facts - What: World Fair Trade Day - When: May 9, 2026 - Where: Ticino, Switzerland - Who: Fair Trade Town, World Shops - Amount: Up to 20% off on fair trade products The 'fair trade breakfast' is also in Ticino. On the occasion of 'World Fair Trade Day', celebrated on May 9, 2026, various activities are planned in the canton. From coffee to tea, from bananas to orange juice to flowers: even at breakfast, products from all over the world are consumed, often made under conditions that would not be accepted in Switzerland. Their real cost is not indicated on the label, but falls on people and the environment. This is reported in a note by Fair Trade Town. In Ticino, Capriasca, Bellinzona and Lugano promote sustainable consumption by involving businesses, catering, schools, institutions and administrations in a local change process. The Fair Trade Town movement counts over 2000 cities in 32 countries; in Switzerland there are 24, three of which are in Ticino. ### Events planned for Saturday, May 9 • Fair Trade Breakfast at the Bellinzona World Shop. Dettagli operativi

Fair trade proposes a different model, based on long-term partnerships, greater transparency, fair wages, and protection of people and the environment. An approach that connects daily choices to global justice, reaffirming that exploitation should not end up on the table. Organizations in the sector, including members of Swiss Fair Trade, draw attention to these critical issues and the possible alternatives. In Ticino, the Botteghe del Mondo in Bellinzona, Cavergno, Faido, Giubiasco, Locarno, Lugano, Mendrisio, Tesserete, and Poschiavo are ready to welcome visitors with a variety of fair and sustainable products. ### Why participate in fair trade events? Participating in fair trade events not only allows you to enjoy quality products but also to support a fairer and more sustainable consumption model. By purchasing fair trade products, you contribute to improving the living conditions of producers in developing countries, guaranteeing them fair wages and dignified working conditions. Moreover, participating in these events is a way to raise awareness among yourself and others about the importance of fair trade and sustainable consumption. The Botteghe del Mondo offer a unique opportunity to discover new products and meet people who share the same values and interests. ### How to participate in fair trade events? To participate in fair trade events in Ticino, simply visit one of the Botteghe del Mondo listed in the program. It is advisable to check the specific times and participation methods for each event, so you can plan your day better. Additionally, you can follow the social media pages of the Botteghe del Mondo and Fair Trade Town to stay updated on all the latest news and events. Frequently Asked Questions
What is fair trade?
Fair trade is a trading model that ensures fair working conditions and a fair wage for producers in developing countries. It promotes environmental and social sustainability, offering consumers quality products that are respectful of the environment.
Where can I find fair trade products in Ticino?
In Ticino, you can find fair trade products at the Botteghe del Mondo in Bellinzona, Cavergno, Faido, Giubiasco, Locarno, Lugano, Mendrisio, Tesserete, and Poschiavo. These shops offer a wide range of fair and sustainable products.
