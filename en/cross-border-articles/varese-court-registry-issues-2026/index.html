Computer issues at Varese's criminal record office, only paper requests

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

The website to book the pickup of the criminal record is down. Only paper requests with ID are accepted.

Contesto

By typing the address https://certificaticasellario.giustizia.it/web/guest/servizi-al-cittadino1, the message 'The page is not working' appears. The reason is a computer problem, perhaps a hacker attack, as stated on a sign that appeared this morning, Wednesday, April 29, also in the Varese offices, on the ground floor of the courthouse. The advice is to access the service by filling out paper forms. The request must be accompanied by a copy of the ID document and the stamp duty. ### Implications for cross-border workers For cross-border workers who need the judicial record for administrative or work procedures, this situation represents a significant obstacle.
