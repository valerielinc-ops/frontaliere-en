Frequently Asked Questions
What are the opening hours of the new stores at Fashion Outlet Landquart?
The opening hours for the 15 new stores have not yet been officially announced. It is recommended to check the official website [foxtown.ch](https://www.foxtown.ch) or contact the shopping centre directly for the most up-to-date information. Typically, Swiss shopping centres open at 10:00 on weekdays and at 9:00 on weekends.
Is Fashion Outlet Landquart accessible by public transport from the Grisons and Ticino?
Yes, the centre is easily reachable by train thanks to Landquart station, which is just a 5-minute walk away. However, there are currently no direct bus connections from major Ticino cities like Lugano or Bellinzona. For those coming from Ticino, the train remains the most convenient option.
Which international brands have opened in the 15 new stores?
The names of the 15 new brands have not yet been made public. The shopping centre already features international brands such as Tommy Hilfiger, Michael Kors, and Hugo Boss, but it has not been confirmed whether these or other stores have been added with the expansion.
Are there any tax benefits for Ticino residents working in Landquart?
The municipality of Zizers benefits from tax revenues thanks to the presence of Fashion Outlet Landquart, but no specific tax relief has been introduced for Ticino cross-border workers at this time. For details on potential local tax reductions, you can contact the municipal office in Zizers.
How will traffic to Landquart change after the opening of the new stores?
Mayor Daniel Freund has stated that he does not anticipate a significant increase in traffic, thanks to the proximity of the railway station. However, no specific measures have been implemented to regulate vehicle flow, such as differentiated parking fees or additional shuttle services.
