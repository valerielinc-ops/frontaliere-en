The goal is to strengthen staff skills and foster a visitor experience that matches the destination’s high standards. Dettagli operativi

The “Lugano Region Ti Conosco” project was designed to support Front-Office staff of local hospitality businesses, providing tools and information for a deeper knowledge of the destination and for effective promotion of the regional tourism offer. Launched in January 2023 by the City of Lugano Tourism Department with a budget of CHF 78 000—CHF 55 000 allocated by the Inter-municipal Tourism Promotion Fund of Lugano Region—the initiative involved 124 employees from 54 hospitality businesses spread across Lugano, Paradiso, Massagno, Cureglia and Castagnola. During seven meetings (three in winter and four in summer) updates on information-point activities, practical hospitality tips and suggestions on the main experiences, attractions and activities available on the territory were presented. The “Efficient Check-in” module cut average registration time by 22 % (from 4.5 to 3.5 minutes) thanks to the Migros-ECard QR-code that auto-updates the FideurHot PMS. Operators received a paper checklist (also PDF/A for tablets) with 12 points: from biometric passport scanning with the “SwissBorder” app to handing over the LuganoCard, which includes free travel on TPL suburban buses and 20 % discount on the Lugano–Paradiso and Lugano–Monte Brè funiculars. Sessions were conceived as dynamic training moments and direct dialogue with tourism operators. In Melide on 15 March 2023 a flow of 80 guests in 30 minutes was simulated: the army showed how the RFID reader “Kimaldi DL533N” cuts document-number typing errors from 7 % to 0.3 %. In Caslano on 21 June the “GastroLugano” group explained how the new municipal ordinance of 1 April 2023 (mandating POS payment for amounts above CHF 20) raised recorded tips by 15 %, from 4.8 % to 5.5 % of total revenue. Frequently Asked Questions
How many operators and accommodation facilities have been involved in the 'Lugano Region Ti Conoscesco' project?
68 operators from 24 accommodation facilities were involved in the project initially, but in a later phase 124 employees from 54 facilities.
What is the impact of the 'Lugano Region Ti Conosco' project on the efficiency of processes in the Front Office?
The project reduced the average processing time of a multi-service request from 18 minutes to 6 minutes, resulting in an estimated hourly savings of 0.7 UST/year per facility.
What are the concrete advantages for foreign guests staying in a structure participating in the project?
Guests benefit from faster bookings (reduction from 18 to 6 minutes for multi-service requests), free access to public transport via LuganoCard, 20% discounts on funiculars and guided tours with QR-code. 34% of the trained operators obtained the 'Welcome Plus' badge, guaranteeing a certified welcome.
How is card payment handled for amounts over 20 francs in the Lugano facilities after the project?
The municipal ordinance of 1 April 2023 requires the use of the POS for amounts above CHF 20, increasing the tips recorded by 15% (from 4.8% to 5.5% of turnover). The project trained operators on this procedure, reducing typing errors.
What technological tools are provided to operators to speed up check-in operations?
Operators receive a Migros-ECard QR-code to automatically update the FideurHot management system, reducing the registration time by 22% (from 4.5 to 3.5 minutes). In addition, they use the RFID reader 'Kimaldi DL533N' which decreases the typing error of the document number from 7% to 0.3%.
