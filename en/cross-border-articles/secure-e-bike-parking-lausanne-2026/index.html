Lausanne introduces secure e-bike parking (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 6 May 2026

The city of Lausanne installs Vélobox to counter the rise in electric bike thefts. Pro Vélo Lausanne welcomes the initiative.

Contesto

In brief - E-bike thefts in Switzerland increased from 6,000 in 2020 to 26,000 in 2025 - Lausanne installs Vélobox for secure paid parking - Pro Vélo Lausanne: "Secure parking is lacking, especially in older buildings" ## Key facts - What: Installation of Vélobox for secure e-bike parking - When: Ongoing in 2026 - Where: Lausanne, Switzerland - Who: City of Lausanne, Pro Vélo Lausanne - Amount: 1,000 new parking spots planned E-bikes are becoming increasingly popular, but unfortunately also more appealing to thieves. In Switzerland, e-bike thefts have risen significantly, from around 6,000 in 2020 to 26,000 in 2025. Faced with this situation, the city of Lausanne has decided to take action by installing special containers called Vélobox, which allow for secure and paid parking of bicycles. The initiative has been positively received by Pro Vélo Lausanne, the association that promotes cycling in the city. "We really like it, especially because the city lacks secure parking, particularly in older buildings," said Yann Roth, president of Pro Vélo Lausanne, to the Telegiornale of the RSI. Indeed, parking a bike, especially an electric one, has become risky throughout Switzerland. ### Lausanne's initiative The City of Lausanne intends to expand the offer of Vélobox, with the aim of creating a total of one thousand new parking spots throughout the year. These containers offer a secure place to leave bicycles, thus reducing the risk of theft. Pro Vélo Lausanne emphasizes the importance of attaching bicycles to fixed points, but acknowledges that this is not always possible. Therefore, the association advocates for the creation of adequate and secure parking, systematically reporting those that do not allow the bike to be tied. ### The rise of thefts in Switzerland Accor...

Analysis of Practical Implications The installation of Véloboxes in Lausanne has significant implications for residents and cross-border workers who use e-bikes. These containers offer a safe place to park bicycles, reducing the risk of theft. However, it is important to consider some practical aspects to make the most of this initiative. ### Comparisons Before and After the Installation of Véloboxes Before the installation of Véloboxes, e-bike owners in Lausanne had to rely on often unsafe public parking. This entailed a high risk of theft, especially for electric bicycles, which are more expensive and appealing to thieves. With the introduction of Véloboxes, citizens can now park their bicycles in a controlled and protected environment, significantly reducing the risk of theft. ### Concrete Scenarios for Cross-Border Workers For cross-border workers who work in Switzerland and use e-bikes for daily commutes, Véloboxes represent a practical and secure solution. For example, a cross-border worker residing in Italy and working in Lausanne can park their e-bike in one of the Véloboxes during the workday, knowing that their vehicle is safe. This is particularly useful for those who do not have access to secure parking at their workplace or residence. ### Procedure for Using Véloboxes To use the Véloboxes, a simple procedure must be followed. First, it is possible to purchase a subscription or pay per use for the parking. Once payment is made, access to the container can be gained and the bicycle can be parked in one of the available spaces. What to Do Concretely To make the most of the Vélobox initiative in Lausanne, it's important to follow some concrete steps. First, you need to identify the available parking points in the city. You can consult the website of the municipality of Lausanne or contact Pro Vélo Lausanne directly to get updated information on the locations where the containers have been installed. ### Step-by-Step Procedure 1. Identify parking points: Consult the website of the municipality of Lausanne or contact Pro Vélo Lausanne to know the locations where Véloboxes are available. 2. Purchase a subscription or pay as you go: You can purchase a subscription for using the Véloboxes or pay as you go. Check the available options and choose the one that best suits your needs. 3. Park your bicycle: Once you have made the payment, access the container and park your bicycle in one of the available spaces. Make sure to securely lock your bicycle inside the container. 4. Check security: Before leaving your bicycle, check that the lock is properly closed and that the bicycle is attached to a fixed point inside the container. 5. Retrieve your bicycle: When you go to pick up your bicycle, make sure you have the ticket or subscription used to access the container. Check that the bicycle is in good condition before leaving. ### Useful Tools To facilitate the use of Véloboxes, you can use some useful tools. Frequently Asked Questions
How can I find the Vélobox parking spots in Lausanne?
You can consult the website of the municipality of Lausanne or contact Pro Vélo Lausanne to get updated information on where the Vélobox containers are available.
What are the costs for using the Vélobox?
The costs for using the Vélobox depend on the type of subscription or pay-as-you-go option chosen. You can check the available options on the website of the municipality of Lausanne or by contacting Pro Vélo Lausanne.
What should I do in case of theft of my e-bike?
In case of theft of your e-bike, it is important to report the incident to the competent authorities immediately. Provide as much information as possible, such as the bicycle's serial number and any identifying details. Also, check if your insurance covers damages resulting from theft.
