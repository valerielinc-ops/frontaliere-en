Contesto

In brief - Lonza sells Micro Macinazione SA to Schedio Group Holding and Microsize - New synergies for the Ticino pharmaceutical hub - Strategic know-how and high-specialization jobs in Ticino ## Key facts - What: Sale of Micro Macinazione SA - When: Operation completed - Where: Monteggio, Canton Ticino - Who: Lonza, Schedio Group Holding, Microsize - Amount: Not specified Lonza has sold the activities of Micro Macinazione SA, a historic Ticino company based in Monteggio, to the Schedio Group Holding of Stabio and the American company Microsize of Quakertown, Pennsylvania. The operation, announced in a press release today, aims to strengthen the cantonal pharmaceutical hub and create new industrial synergies in the region. Before joining the Lonza group, Micro Macinazione had become a leader in the field of pharmaceutical active ingredient micronization for third parties. The integration of Micro Macinazione's industrial activities with Schedio's engineering know-how will consolidate in Ticino a center of excellence in the field of particle engineering and pharmaceutical formulation. The synergy between the two entities will allow offering the industry integrated solutions, from particle size control to the development of complex formulations, to the benefit of international clients and the local supply chain. Mattia Wiedemeier, new CEO of Micro Macinazione, explained that this operation brings back to Ticino strategic know-how and high-specialization jobs. It is an investment in the future of the Canton as a reference hub for life sciences.
