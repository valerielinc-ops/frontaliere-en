The ceremony took place on April 24, 2026, on the eve of Liberation Day, at the Customs Building of Ponte Chiasso, a symbol of the border with Switzerland. The plaque, measuring 75x40 cm and 2 cm thick, is dedicated to Giuseppina Panzica, the financier Gavino Tolis, and Marshal Paolo Boetti, who in 1943 helped Jews and persecuted individuals escape to Switzerland through an opening in Giuseppina's garden fence. Frequently Asked Questions
Who were the Angels of Ponte Chiasso?
The Angels of Ponte Chiasso were Giuseppina Panzica, the financial officer Gavino Tolis, and the marshal Paolo Boetti, who in 1943 helped Jews and persecuted people escape to Switzerland through an opening in Giuseppina's garden fence.
Where is the plaque dedicated to the Angels of Ponte Chiasso located?
The plaque is located at the Ponte Chiasso Customs House building, on the border between Italy and Switzerland.
What changes to traffic were made for the plaque installation ceremony?
Restrictions on parking and vehicle circulation were established in Piazza XXIV Maggio and via Bellinzona, with vehicles heading to Switzerland diverted towards the Maslianico border crossing.
