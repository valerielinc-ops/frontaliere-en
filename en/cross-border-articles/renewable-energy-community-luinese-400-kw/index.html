Germignaga: Luinese Renewable Energy Community reaches 400 kW

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 26 April 2026

The Luinese Renewable Energy Community reaches 400 kW of power and aims for 1000 kW by the end of 2026. 17 municipalities and 287 members involved.

Practical Implications for Cross-Border Workers The growth of the Luinese Renewable Energy Community represents a concrete opportunity for cross-border workers who live or work in the area. The increase in renewable energy production and the reduction in energy bills can have a significant impact on the purchasing power and quality of life of cross-border workers. Additionally, active participation in the CER can offer tax benefits and social contributions, making daily life more sustainable. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation Before the establishment of the Luinese CER, the involved municipalities primarily relied on the traditional electrical grid, with high costs and less energy independence. The creation of the CER has allowed for a reduction in energy costs thanks to local renewable energy production and the sharing of incentives. This model represents a virtuous example of how local communities can come together to address energy and environmental challenges. ### Future Scenarios With the goal of reaching 1000 kW of installed capacity by the end of 2026, the Luinese CER aims to become one of the largest and most advanced energy communities in Italy. This objective will require further commitment from the municipalities, members, and installers, but the benefits for the community will be significant. The rapid approval of the European Directive RED III could further accelerate this process, leading to even more substantial reductions in energy bills for CER members. ### Collaboration with the Installers' Network Close collaboration with the CER Alto Verbano Installers' Network 'Energia Vicina' is fundamental to ensuring the quality and efficiency of photovoltaic systems. Frequently Asked Questions
How can I become a member of the CER del Luinese?
To become a member of the CER del Luinese, you can contact your municipality of residence or visit the official CER website to obtain information on the requirements and fill out the application form.
What are the benefits of participating in the CER del Luinese?
The benefits of participating in the CER del Luinese include reduced energy costs, greater energy independence, environmental protection, and the opportunity to contribute to social projects.
What does the European Directive RED III provide?
The European Directive RED III provides for the unbundling of shared energy among CER members in the bill, leading to significant reductions in energy bills for community members.
