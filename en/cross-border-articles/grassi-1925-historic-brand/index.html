Grassi 1925 enters the Special Register of Historical Brands

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

The Varese-based company, founded in 1925, receives recognition from the Ministry of Enterprises and Made in Italy for the continuous use of its brand.

Contesto

In brief - Grassi 1925 enters the Special Register of Historic Brands - Recognition by the Ministry of Enterprises and Made in Italy - Continuous use of the brand for over 50 years ## Key facts - What: Registration in the Special Register of Historic Brands - When: May 1, 2026 - Where: Lonate Pozzolo, Varese - Who: Ministry of Enterprises and Made in Italy - Amount: Not specified Grassi 1925, a Varese-based company founded in 1925, has officially entered the Special Register of Historic Brands of National Interest. This recognition was established by the Ministry of Enterprises and Made in Italy to enhance entrepreneurial realities that represent a significant part of Italian productive history. The registration was accepted by the Italian Patent and Trademark Office following the verification of the required criteria, including the continuous use of the brand for over fifty years. A milestone that certifies not only the longevity of the company but also the continuity and coherence of its positioning over time. Founded in 1925, Grassi has gone through a century of industrial transformations while maintaining a clear identity: designing and manufacturing technical garments destined for complex operational contexts, from public administration to the industrial world, up to the most specialized areas of personal protection. ### A century of technical manufacturing The recognition of Historic Brand fits into this path as an institutional confirmation of the company's role in the national manufacturing system. The registration concerns a wide range of products, from technical and protective clothing to safety devices, up to fabrics and materials used in production.
