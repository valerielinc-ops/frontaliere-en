Traffic Police: Over 1,600 Licenses Revoked in a Year

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

In 2025, the Traffic Police of Varese revoked 1,600 licenses, with a 42% decrease for speeding and a 25% decrease for drunk driving.

The data, released on the occasion of the 174th anniversary of the founding of the State Police, shows a significant decrease compared to the previous year. In 2024, in fact, the licenses revoked were 1,645. Among the main causes of revocation, 946 licenses were revoked for speeding, while 699 for driving under the influence. Compared to 2024, there is a 42% decrease in licenses revoked for speeding and a 25% decrease for those revoked for driving under the influence. ### Fines on the decline The number of fines has also decreased. In 2025, the Polstrada officers issued 13,185 fines, 30% less than the previous year, when there were 18,742. The amount of the fines in 2025 totals just over 4.5 million euros, compared to the over 5.5 million of the previous twelve months. ### Arrests and reports However, there has been an increase in arrests carried out by the traffic police officers: 79 compared to 42 of the previous year, with an increase of 88%.
