In brief - Trade union parade in Lugano on May 1st - Road closures from 16:00 to 17:00 - Markets along the lake from 09:00 to 20:00 ## Key facts - What: Trade union parade and markets - When: May 1st, 2024 - Where: Lugano, city center - Who: Swiss Trade Union (USS) Ticino and Moesa - Amount: Not specified On May 1st, Lugano will host the traditional trade union parade organized by the Swiss Trade Union (USS) Ticino and Moesa. The event, authorized by the city authorities, will result in significant restrictions on traffic in the city center. Road closures are expected between 16:00 and 17:00, during which the parade will take place. ### Affected streets The streets that will be temporarily closed to allow the parade to take place include: - Via Capelli - Viale Castagnola - Viale Cassarate - Viale Cattaneo - Last stretch of Corso Elvezia from Via Balestra - Lungolago from the LAC Roundabout (Via Adamini) to Piazza Castello - Via Magatti The city police have already announced that the necessary traffic diversions will be implemented and suggest that drivers use alternative routes to avoid inconvenience. Additionally, the lakeside will be closed to vehicular traffic and will host markets, with the affected stretch running from the LAC roundabout to Piazza Castello, from 09:00 to 20:00. ### Implications for cross-border workers Cross-border workers who work in Lugano and reside in Italy should plan their route in advance to reach their workplace, considering the road closures and possible traffic jams. However, this year, the extent of the closures is wider, also involving the lakeside promenade, one of the city's main arteries. For example, in 2022, the closures only affected Via Nassa and Via San Salvatore, while this year the affected area extends from Piazza Riforma to the lakeside promenade. ### Possible Scenarios 1. Frequently Asked Questions
Which streets will be closed during the 1st May procession in Lugano?
The streets that will be closed during the procession include Via Capelli, Viale Castagnola, Viale Cassarate, Viale Cattaneo, the last section of Corso Elvezia from Via Balestra, the lakeside from the LAC roundabout to Piazza Castello, and Via Magatti. The closures are scheduled from 16:00 to 17:00.
Will there be markets during the 1st May in Lugano?
Yes, the lakeside will be closed to vehicular traffic and will host markets. The affected section runs from the LAC roundabout to Piazza Castello, from 09:00 to 20:00.
How can I avoid the inconveniences caused by the road closures?
To avoid inconveniences, it is advisable to check the road diversions in advance through the ViaSuisse platform, use public transport, and plan alternative routes. Additionally, if possible, agree on flexible working hours to avoid going out during the road closures.
