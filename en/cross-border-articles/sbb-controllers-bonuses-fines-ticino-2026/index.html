Frequently Asked Questions
What happens if I forget to validate my ticket?
If you forget to validate your ticket, you might be fined. With the new bonus system for SBB inspectors, the risk of fines could increase. Always make sure to validate your ticket before boarding the train or bus.
How can I avoid fines?
To avoid fines, always make sure you have a valid ticket, validate it before boarding the train or bus, and follow the rules of public transport. Keep an eye on the official communications from SBB to stay updated on any changes in rules or procedures.
Which documents should I have with me?
Make sure you always have the necessary documents with you to travel safely and without problems. These include a valid ticket, an ID document, a G permit (if applicable), and health insurance.
