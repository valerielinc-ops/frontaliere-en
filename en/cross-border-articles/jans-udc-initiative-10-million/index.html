Jans criticizes UDC initiative: 'Risk for Switzerland'

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

Federal Councillor Jans criticizes the UDC initiative 'No to a Switzerland of 10 million', warning of serious consequences for the country.

Contesto

According to Jans, the acceptance of this initiative could have serious consequences for Switzerland. The head of the Federal Department of Justice and Police (DFJP) spoke in Bienne on the occasion of a speech delivered for May Day. The text of the initiative promises solutions to problems deeply felt by the population, such as overcrowded trains, traffic jams, rents, crime, and environmental difficulties. However, Jans argues that these are 'empty promises'. According to him, if approved, the initiative would oblige Switzerland to denounce the agreement on the free movement of persons, thus endangering the bilateral path with the European Union. ### Implications for cross-border workers The UDC initiative could have a significant impact on cross-border workers employed in Switzerland.
