Summer camp in Malnate: nature, farm and English for kids

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

At Tenuta La Novella in Malnate, a summer among animals, outdoor activities and English for children aged 6 to 10.

Contesto

In brief - Summer camp in Malnate for children aged 6-10 - Nature, farm, and English with IngleseBimbi® method - Active from July 6 to September 11, 2026 - Maximum 25 participants per week - Weekly cost: 250 CHF (approx. 230 EUR) including meals and activities ## Key facts - What: Happy Farm Summer Camp - When: July 6 to September 11, 2026 - Where: Tenuta La Novella, Malnate (VA), 10 minutes from the Swiss border, 30 minutes from Lugano - Who: Tenuta La Novella managed by Domitilla Lenzi and Marco Visconti - Regulations: Compliance with Italian laws on safety and insurance, with liability coverage up to 5 million EUR In Malnate, at Tenuta La Novella, the Happy Farm Summer Camp is born, an initiative designed to let children experience a summer in contact with nature, among animals, outdoor activities, and English learning. The estate, managed by Domitilla Lenzi and Marco Visconti, offers large green spaces (about 5 hectares), stables, vegetable garden, and orchard, where children can move safely and explore farm life. Its proximity to Ticino (30 minutes from Lugano, 20 minutes from Mendrisio) makes it ideal for cross-border families. ### Activities and methodology The summer camp is designed for children aged 6 to 10 and will be active from July 6 to September 11, 2026. Each week will accommodate a maximum of 25 participants to ensure a complete and safe experience. The days will alternate between quiet moments, such as the Plant & Care Lab (plant and vegetable garden care) and farm routine activities (Farm Helpers), to dynamic games like obstacle courses and water games. A distinctive element is the presence of English, introduced through the IngleseBimbi® method by Sara Nicoliello Ellis, which accompanies children in daily activities in a natural and playful way....

Dettagli operativi

Practical Implications for Parents The Happy Farm Summer Camp, located in Malnate, represents a unique opportunity for parents seeking an educational and fun experience for their children. The combination of outdoor activities, contact with nature, and English learning offers a stimulating and safe environment. Parents can be certain that their children will be engaged in activities that promote creativity, curiosity, and respect for the environment. The summer camp welcomes children aged 5 to 11 and offers a 1:5 ratio between educators and participants, ensuring personalized attention. Costs range from 200 to 250 CHF per week, depending on the package chosen, and include meals, teaching materials, and insurance. ### Comparison with Other Summer Offers Compared to other summer camps, the Happy Farm Summer Camp stands out for its focus on nature and farm life. The presence of animals such as rabbits, goats, and chickens, along with the opportunity to participate in practical workshops, offers a more authentic and engaging experience. Additionally, the introduction of English through the IngleseBimbi® method adds value, allowing children to learn the language naturally and playfully. Unlike other summer camps that focus primarily on sports or artistic activities, Happy Farm integrates language learning with practical farm experience. ### Scenarios and Opportunities Parents can take advantage of this opportunity to give their children an unforgettable summer, combining fun and learning. The estate offers a safe and welcoming environment, with qualified staff taking care of the children. How to Participate To participate in the Happy Farm Summer Camp, parents can follow these steps: 1. Contact the estate via WhatsApp at +39 351 551 1852 or email info@tenutalanovella.it. The estate is located in Malnate, just a few kilometers from the border with Ticino, in an area easily accessible from towns like Mendrisio, Chiasso, and Lugano. 2. Attend the open days on May 9 and 23, from 14:00 to 17:00, to visit the facility and meet the organizers. During the open days, parents will also have the opportunity to meet some of the tutors who will accompany the children during the summer camp. 3. Fill out the participation form available on the estate's website, specifying any allergies, dietary needs, or other relevant information for the safety of the children. 4. Pay the participation fee of 450 CHF per week, which includes meals, accommodation, educational activities, and materials. It is possible to pay in monthly installments, with a 30% deposit at the time of registration and the balance due by June 15. ### Costs and Details Costs and specific details are now available on the estate's website. The summer camp is open to children aged 6 to 12 and offers a weekly program from Monday to Friday, from 9:00 to 17:00. For parents working in Switzerland, it is possible to use the pre and post-camp service, from 8:00 to 9:00 and from 17:00 to 18:00, for an additional cost of 50 CHF per week. 📊 Cost Comparison: - Happy Farm Summer Camp: 450 CHF per week - Summer Camps in Ticino: on average 500-600 CHF per week - Summer Camps in Italy: on average 300-400 EUR per week ### Useful Tools For further information and to plan the participation in the summer camp, parents can use the cost calculator available on the Frontaliere Ticino website. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the age requirements to participate in the summer camp?
The summer camp is designed for children aged 6 to 10. Parents can contact the estate for more details.
How can I enroll my child in the summer camp?
To enroll your child in the summer camp, you can contact the estate via WhatsApp at +41 79 551 1852 or email info@tenutalanovella.it. You can also attend the open days on May 9th and 23rd to visit the facility and meet the organizers.
What activities are planned during the summer camp?
The summer camp includes a variety of activities, such as practical workshops, outdoor games, visits to the stables and paddocks, and picking fresh produce from the garden for snacks. Additionally, children will learn English through the IngleseBimbi® method.
