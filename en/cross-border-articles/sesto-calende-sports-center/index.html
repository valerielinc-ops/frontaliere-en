Sesto Calende's Sports Center Receives 380,000 Euros from Lombardy Region (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 24 March 2026

The town of Sesto Calende has received 380,000 euros from the Lombardy Region's 'Impianti Sportivi 2025' program to improve its sports center.

Contesto

TL;DR - Sesto Calende receives 380,000 euros for sports center upgrade. - Funding covers safety, accessibility, and energy efficiency improvements. - Project part of Lombardy Region's 5 million euro sports development plan. ## Key facts - Grant Amount: 380,000 euros - Program: Impianti Sportivi 2025 - Funding Source: Lombardy Region - Purpose: Safety, accessibility, and energy upgrades - Total Program Budget: 5 million euros - Annual Users: 30,000 - Energy Savings Goal: 30% reduction in energy consumption - Photovoltaic Coverage: 20% of energy needs The town of Sesto Calende has received a grant of 380,000 euros from the Lombardy Region's 'Impianti Sportivi 2025' program to improve its sports center. This funding is intended for the safety of the sports center, compliance with fire, seismic, and accessibility regulations, as well as CONI (Italian National Olympic Committee) standards, and the renovation and expansion of locker rooms and a significant energy and plant upgrade of the buildings. The Lombardy Region has chosen to fund the Sesto Calende sports center project as part of the region's sports development plan. The local administration commented, saying: 'A concrete and far from obvious political result that brings direct resources to the territory and allows for the reduction of the loan taken with the Sports Credit, both capital and interest.' This funding is an important step forward for the Sesto Calende sports center and the region's sports development. According to the town's estimates, the Sesto Calende sports center receives around 30,000 users per year, including athletes and spectators, who use the sports facilities for training and competitions.

Dettagli operativi

The Lombardy Region has allocated a budget of 5 million euros for the 'Impianti Sportivi 2025' program, which will fund 10 projects to improve sports facilities throughout the region. The Sesto Calende sports center was selected among the 10 funded projects, along with four other sports centers in Lombardy. The 380,000 euro funding will be used for various purposes. Specifically, 120,000 euros will be allocated for the renovation of the locker rooms, while 80,000 euros will be used for the installation of new energy-efficient lighting and ventilation systems. Another 30,000 euros will be allocated for the safety of the sports center, with the installation of new accessibility features and alarm systems. In addition, the funding will be used for the energy upgrade of the sports center buildings. According to the town's estimates, the goal is to reduce energy consumption by at least 30% compared to current standards. Frequently Asked Questions
How does the Lombardy Region's 2025 Sports Facilities call work?
The Lombardy Region's 2025 Sports Facilities call is a funding program for the improvement of sports facilities throughout the region. The Region has allocated a budget of 5 million euros to finance 10 projects to improve sports facilities. The Sesto Calende sports center was selected among the 10 projects funded.
What is the total budget allocated by the Lombardy Region for the 2025 Sports Facilities call?
The total budget allocated is 5 million euros to finance 10 projects to improve sports facilities throughout the region.
What are the selection criteria for obtaining funding for the 2025 Sports Facilities call?
For the 2025 Sports Facilities call, the Lombardy Region evaluates the projects on the basis of four main criteria: safety (fire and seismic regulations), the elimination of architectural barriers, energy redevelopment (installation of low-consumption systems) and the ability to increase the sports offer for the local population. Only projects that meet all points receive funding.
How long does it take for the municipality of Sesto Calende to receive the funds after the approval of the project?
The Municipality of Sesto Calende received the funding notice in April 2024; The disbursement procedure provides for a period of 60-90 days for the stipulation of the contract and the transfer of the 380,000 euros. On average, Lombardy municipalities take about eight weeks from signing to the availability of funds.
What reduction in energy consumption is expected thanks to the new photovoltaic system of the sports center?
The new photovoltaic system installed at the Sesto Calende sports center will produce enough energy to cover 20% of the annual electricity needs and will help reduce overall consumption by at least 30% compared to current standards, according to municipal estimates. This leads to an estimated saving of about 15,000 euros per year.
