Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

Three people are under investigation for the illegal disposal of special waste in an industrial warehouse in Arcisate and in an industrial area within the municipal territory of Varese. The structures, along with two vehicles used to transport the materials, have been seized.
