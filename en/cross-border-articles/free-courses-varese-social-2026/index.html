Do you like helping others? Free social courses in Varese

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

Your Self Company offers free courses for ASA, OSS, and ASACOM, funded by Regione Lombardia. Limited spots, registrations open.

Contesto

In brief - Free courses for ASA, OSS, and ASACOM in Varese - Funded by Regione Lombardia with a budget of 500,000 euros - Limited to 100 spots, registrations open until September 30, 2024 ## Key facts - What: Courses for ASA, OSS, and ASACOM - When: Registrations open until September 30, 2024, courses start in October 2024 - Where: Varese, with guaranteed internships in affiliated facilities such as the Varese Nursing Home and Varese Circolo Hospital - Who: Your Self Company, an entity accredited by Regione Lombardia - Cost: Free courses for the unemployed, subsidized fee of 300 euros for the 600-hour ASACOM course Do you enjoy taking care of people and want to turn this passion into a profession? In Varese, Your Self Company has opened registrations for excellence training courses in the social sector. The available paths are for ASA (Social and Health Care Assistant), OSS (Social and Health Care Operator), and ASACOM (Assistant for Autonomy and Communication). These courses are funded by Regione Lombardia with a budget of 500,000 euros and offer a unique opportunity to enter a job market that increasingly requires specialized figures, especially in border areas like Ticino, where the demand for social and health care operators is constantly growing. Dr. Maria Rosaria Infante, psychologist-psychotherapist and educational coordinator of Your Self Company, emphasizes the nobility of these professions: “Choosing to become an ASA, OSS, or ASACOM operator is not just a career choice, but a true life mission.” ### Why choose these profiles? #### ASA and OSS: The heart of assistance If you are an empathetic person and want to make a difference in the daily lives of the most vulnerable, the courses for ASA and OSS are perfect for you. Practical Implications for Cross-Border Workers The courses offered by Your Self Company represent a significant opportunity for cross-border workers who live in Lombardy and work in Ticino. These training programs, funded by the Lombardy Region, can open up new career prospects in the social sector, a constantly growing field. For example, the course for Socio-Health Operator (OSS) has a 92% employment rate within six months of completing the course, according to 2023 data. Additionally, the possibility of attending free courses or with subsidized contributions is a concrete advantage for those who wish to requalify without incurring high costs. Cross-border workers who work in Ticinese municipalities such as Lugano, Mendrisio, or Chiasso can benefit from these courses to improve their skills and find job opportunities closer to home. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation Before these courses, opportunities for training in the social sector were limited and often expensive. For example, a course for Community Assistant (ASACOM) could cost up to 3,000 euros, a prohibitive sum for many. The Lombardy Region's initiative to fund these paths represents a significant step forward for access to professional training. This not only facilitates entry into the job market but also helps to meet the demand for specialized figures in the socio-health sector. According to 2022 data, Lombardy needs approximately 15,000 new socio-health operators by 2025 to meet the needs of healthcare and socio-assistance facilities. ### Concrete Scenarios Imagine being a cross-border worker in Ticino who wants to change careers. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can participate in the free ASA and OSS courses?
The free courses for ASA and OSS are aimed at the unemployed. For the ASACOM course, there are two options: free for those with previous experience in the educational field and a subsidized contribution of 300 euros for those without prior experience.
How can I enroll in the courses?
You can contact the secretariat of Your Self Company by calling the number 03321760720 or by sending an email to info@centroyourself.com. Alternatively, you can go to the secretariat during opening hours to pre-register.
What are the job opportunities after completing the courses?
There are numerous job opportunities in the Varese area. The courses prepare you to work as an ASA, OSS, or ASACOM, roles that are highly sought after in the socio-health sector and in the field of school inclusion.
