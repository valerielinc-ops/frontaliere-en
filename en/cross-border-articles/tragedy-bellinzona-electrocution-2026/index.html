Di Valerie Linc · Published on 4 May 2026

A man has died after being electrocuted by the railway line at Bellinzona station. Investigations are ongoing.

The alarm was raised just before 21:45, immediately mobilizing a significant deployment of rescue teams and law enforcement. Unfortunately, there was nothing that could be done to save the victim. According to the Cantonal Police, the man was electrocuted by a discharge from the railway's overhead line. The exact circumstances that led to contact with the high-voltage cables are not yet known. It will be up to the ongoing investigation, already launched by the competent authorities, to establish the dynamics of the event and the reason why the person was in a position to trigger the electric arc. Rescue teams from Croce Verde Bellinzona and firefighters from the Ticinese capital arrived promptly at the scene. Despite the swift response, medical personnel could only confirm the man's death, which occurred instantly due to the severe injuries sustained during the electrocution. In addition to the medical teams, agents from the Cantonal Police, supported by the Bellinzona City Police and the Transport Police, arrived at the station to conduct the necessary investigations and manage the railway area.
