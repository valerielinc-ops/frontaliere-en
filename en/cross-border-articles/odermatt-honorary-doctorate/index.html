Marco Odermatt awarded honorary doctorate by EPFL (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 7 May 2026

Swiss alpine skiing champion Marco Odermatt honored with an honorary doctorate by the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne.

Contesto

In brief - Marco Odermatt receives honorary doctorate from EPFL - Five World Cup titles and 54 victories on the World Cup circuit - Second Swiss athlete after Roger Federer to receive this honor ## Key facts - What: Honorary doctorate - When: May 6, 2026 - Where: EPFL - Who: Marco Odermatt - Achievements: 5 World Cup titles, 11 specialty cups, 3 world championships, 54 victories Marco Odermatt, the Swiss phenomenon of alpine skiing, has added a new title to his already extraordinary career. On Monday, May 6, 2026, the 28-year-old was awarded an honorary doctorate by the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne. This recognition rewards an exceptional career and his extraordinary contribution to the world of sports. Odermatt's record speaks for itself: five overall World Cup titles, eleven specialty cups, a complete set of Olympic medals, three world championships, and 54 victories on the World Cup circuit. Numbers that, for EPFL, are more than enough to confer one of the highest academic honors. For Odermatt, the surprise was enormous. 'During my career, I never thought that one day I would receive a doctorate,' he told SRF microphones with a smile. 'I graduated from high school, then I entered the World Cup early and thought that would be the highest title I would achieve.' With this recognition, Odermatt joins an exclusive club. Before him, only one other Swiss athlete had received an honorary doctorate from a Swiss university: Roger Federer, awarded in 2017 by the Faculty of Medicine of the University of Basel. 'Being the second Swiss athlete after Roger Federer to receive this title is a huge honor,' concluded Odermatt. ### Implications for the world of sports Odermatt's recognition represents an important milestone not only for his career but also for...
