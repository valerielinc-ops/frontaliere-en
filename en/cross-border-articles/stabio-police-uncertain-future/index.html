The constant presence of law enforcement is crucial to ensuring security and public order, essential elements for the quality of life of residents, including cross-border workers. Currently, approximately 12,000 cross-border workers work in Ticino, with about 1,500 in the Mendrisio district alone, which includes Stabio.
