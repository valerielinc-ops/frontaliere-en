Let's examine the implications and possible solutions in detail. ### Seasonal Balance Sheet A detailed seasonal balance sheet is essential to understand the performance of ski resorts. This document should include data on attendance, revenue, costs, and marketing initiatives. In this way, resort managers could identify areas for improvement and plan future activities more effectively. ### New Evaluation Criteria Parliamentarians propose new criteria for credit allocation, such as environmental sustainability and technological innovation. These criteria could help promote more responsible and innovative management of ski resorts. For example, ski resorts could adopt more advanced technologies for snow management and skier safety. ### Impact on Cross-Border Workers Ticino's ski resorts are an important resource for cross-border workers employed in the canton. Improving infrastructure and services could attract more tourists and, consequently, create new job opportunities. Furthermore, a transparent seasonal balance sheet could help cross-border workers better understand the sector's dynamics and plan their activities. ### Future Scenarios If the parliamentary inquiry is accepted, Ticino's ski resorts could benefit from more targeted credit and a more promising future.
