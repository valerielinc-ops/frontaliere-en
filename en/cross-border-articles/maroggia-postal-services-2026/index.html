Maroggia, Posta to reorganize services from 2026

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

From September 2026, Maroggia will have a new home postal service while awaiting collaboration with a local business.

Contesto

In brief - Home delivery postal service starting September 2026 - Collaboration with a local business in the works - 500,000 Swiss households already use home delivery service ## Key facts - What: Restructuring of postal services in Maroggia - When: Starting September 2026 - Where: Maroggia, Canton Ticino - Who: Swiss Post and the Municipality of Val Mara - Amount: 500,000 Swiss households already use home delivery service Starting in early 2026, postal services in Maroggia will be available thanks to a collaboration with a local business. While waiting for the new model to kick off, a home delivery service will be launched in the autumn, temporarily replacing the current branch. This change was announced by Swiss Post and the Municipality of Val Mara, which have evaluated various options to ensure service in the area. In recent years, Maroggia has seen a significant increase in digital operations, particularly in payment traffic, leading to a decrease in demand at the counter. Swiss Post has developed solutions to maintain quality and proximity services, adapting to the new needs of the population. ### Home delivery service The home delivery service, already used by around 500,000 households in Switzerland, will allow customers to perform various operations right at their doorstep, from Monday to Friday. It will be possible to send letters and parcels, order stamps, make deposits, and handle cash transactions. Requests can be made online via the Post-App, with an order pen, or by contacting customer service. The postal worker will receive instructions on their scanner and will pass by the home during the next round. ### Collaboration with a local business The adopted solution is the result of a discussion between Swiss Post and the Municipality of Val Mara. Dettagli operativi

Implications for cross-border workers The new home delivery service represents an opportunity for cross-border workers residing in Maroggia and working in Switzerland. Since the service is available from Monday to Friday, cross-border workers will be able to manage their postal operations without having to physically go to a branch. This is particularly useful for those with intense work schedules or who prefer to manage their postal operations flexibly. ### Comparison with the previous situation Before the introduction of the home delivery service, residents of Maroggia had to go to the local branch to carry out postal operations. With the new service, they can avoid unnecessary trips and manage their operations directly from home. This change is in line with the general trend towards digitalization and the convenience of services. ### Concrete scenarios Let's imagine a cross-border worker who works in Lugano and resides in Maroggia. Before the new service, they had to go to the post office branch during working hours to send an urgent parcel. Now, thanks to the home delivery service, they can order the parcel pickup via the Post-App and the postman will pick it up during his rounds. This saves time and simplifies the management of postal operations. ### Promotion of the use of the Post-App Swiss Post is actively promoting the use of the Post-App, which offers numerous useful features for cross-border workers. With the Post-App, you can track shipments in real time, manage deliveries flexibly, frank mailings and request home pickup. Procedure to use the home delivery service 1. Request the service: Customers can request the home delivery service via the Post-App, with an order pen, or by contacting customer service. 2. Receive instructions: The postman will receive the instructions on their scanner and will pass by the home during the next round. 3. Perform operations: It will be possible to send letters and parcels, order stamps, make deposits, and manage cash operations directly at home. 4. Collect shipments: Missed deliveries can be redelivered upon request. ### Deadlines and timelines The home delivery service will start in September 2026. Collaboration with a local business is being finalized and will be activated in the first months of 2026. Customers can already start using the Post-App to manage their postal operations flexibly. ### Useful tools For more information and to use the home delivery service, cross-border workers can visit the Swiss Post website or download the Post-App. Additionally, they can use the postal cost calculator to estimate the costs of their shipments. ### Conclusion The new home delivery service represents a significant step forward for the residents of Maroggia, offering greater convenience and flexibility in managing postal operations. Cross-border workers can benefit from this service to simplify their daily lives and manage their postal operations more efficiently. Frequently Asked Questions
When will the home delivery service start in Maroggia?
The home delivery service will start from September 2026. This temporary service will replace the current branch until the start of the collaboration with a local business, scheduled for the first months of 2026.
What operations can I perform with the home delivery service?
With the home delivery service, you will be able to send letters and parcels, order stamps, make deposits, and manage cash transactions. Requests can be submitted online, via Post-App, with an order pen, or by contacting customer service.
How can I request the home delivery service?
To request the home delivery service, you can use the Post-App, an order pen, or contact the customer service of La Posta Svizzera. The mail carrier will receive the instructions on their scanner and will pass by your home during the next round.
