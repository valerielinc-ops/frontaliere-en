Free breast cancer prevention visits in Gallarate with LILT

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 27 April 2026

Two days of free senological visits with ultrasound in Gallarate at Piazza Libertà during the month of May.

In brief - Free visits for breast cancer prevention in Gallarate - Two days of free ultrasounds in Piazza Libertà - Mandatory booking at 380 8644677 ## Key facts - What: Free breast examinations with ultrasound - When: May - Where: Piazza Libertà, Gallarate - Who: Municipal administration, Lilt Varese, Salve - Cost: Free In May, on the occasion of Mother's Day, Gallarate hosts an initiative dedicated to female oncological prevention. The municipal administration, together with Lilt – Italian League for the fight against cancer, provincial association of Varese, and with the contribution of Salve, promotes two days of free breast examinations with ultrasound, aimed at the early diagnosis of breast cancer. The initiative will take place in Piazza Libertà, where the "Prevention Camper" of Lilt will be set up. The goal is to offer women a concrete opportunity to take care of their health, especially in a context where, between work and daily commitments, they often tend to postpone checks. "I welcome with great enthusiasm the initiative promoted by Lilt in Gallarate" said the mayor Andrea Cassani. "It will offer our city two important days dedicated to breast cancer prevention. The possibility of accessing free visits and ultrasounds represents a concrete opportunity to spread the culture of prevention, which is fundamental." On the same line, the president of the provincial Lilt of Varese, Ivanoe Pellerin, who underlines how "cancer is today a chronic pathology that is increasingly preventable and, in many cases, also curable". However, the data remains significant: in 2025, approximately 390,700 new cancer cases are estimated in Italy, a number that requires strengthening prevention and early diagnosis actions. Practical Implications for Women in Gallarate The initiative promoted by Lilt in Gallarate represents a concrete opportunity for women to take care of their health in a free and accessible way. The possibility of accessing free breast examinations with ultrasound is a fundamental step for the prevention of breast cancer, one of the most common forms of cancer among women. > "Early diagnosis saves lives" - Lilt Campaign 2023 Early diagnosis is crucial to increasing the chances of recovery and reducing the impact of the disease. According to data provided by Lilt, approximately 390,700 new cases of cancer are estimated in Italy in 2025. This number underscores the importance of strengthening prevention and early diagnosis actions, especially in a context where cancer is a chronic pathology that is increasingly preventable and, in many cases, curable. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation In recent years, positive signs have been recorded in the fight against cancer. Cancer survivors have increased from 2.5 million in 2006 to the current 3.6 million, equal to 5.7% of the Italian population. This data confirms the importance of investing in prevention, research, and early diagnosis. However, it remains fundamental to maintain high attention and spread the culture of prevention, especially among young people. ### Future Scenarios The Lilt initiative in Gallarate could be a model to replicate in other cities, offering free and accessible prevention opportunities to an increasing number of women. How to Participate To participate in the free breast cancer prevention visits in Gallarate, follow these simple steps: 1. Booking: Contact the number 380 8644677 (Lilt) from Monday to Friday, 9 am to 12 pm, starting from Tuesday, April 28. Lilt, the Italian League for the Fight Against Cancer, offers these free visits thanks to the support of volunteers and donations. For example, in 2022, Lilt organized over 5,000 free visits in Lombardy, with a 15% increase compared to the previous year. 2. Documentation: Bring a valid ID and your health card. Ensure your health card is up-to-date and valid. In Ticino, for example, the health card must be renewed every five years, costing 20 Swiss francs. 3. Location: Present yourself in Piazza Libertà, Gallarate, on the indicated dates. For residents in Ticino, such as Lugano or Bellinzona, you can plan a day trip, considering the border is just a few kilometers away. A trip from Lugano to Gallarate can take approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes by car. 4. Visit: Follow the medical staff's instructions for the free breast examination with ultrasound. The visit includes a clinical evaluation and a mammary ultrasound, lasting about 20-30 minutes. In Switzerland, a private breast examination can cost between 150 and 300 Swiss francs, while the ultrasound can add another 100-200 francs. ### Useful Tools For more information and to stay updated on prevention initiatives, you can visit the official Lilt website or contact the municipal administration of Gallarate. In Ticino, the Department of Health and Social Affairs also provides useful information on similar prevention programs.
