In view of Coop's Good Deed Day, we spent some time with her to learn more about her experience. Dolores, affectionately called 'Dolly,' is a woman who has faced many challenges in her life but who today finds 'something beautiful' in every moment. Widowed too soon, Dolores had to raise her daughter alone after her husband's death in a plane crash. Despite the difficulties, she found the strength to move forward and build a meaningful life. 'I was a seamstress, and ladies would come to us to have their clothes made to measure. They all dressed so chic,' Dolores recalls with a smile. 'They were a bit heavy... they were never satisfied,' she adds with a quick wit that reveals her indomitable spirit. Today, Dolores lives at the Polis elderly home in Pregassona, where she feels pampered and appreciated. 'I feel good, I'm very pampered, and I lack nothing,' she says proudly. 'I am one of the favorites...' she adds with a mischievous smile. 'They don't say it, but...' ### The company of a volunteer A fundamental element in Dolores' life is the company of a Red Cross volunteer who spends time with her every Tuesday.
