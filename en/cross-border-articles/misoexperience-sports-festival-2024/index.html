Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main sports disciplines of the MisoXperience Festival?
The main sports disciplines of the MisoXperience Festival are whitewater kayaking, bouldering, and paragliding. Each discipline offers competitions and activities for all levels of experience, from beginners to ambitious athletes.
Where does the MisoXperience Festival take place?
The festival takes place in the Mesolcina valley, at Stradon 5, Piani di Verdabbio a Cama. The location is surrounded by nature, next to the Moesa river, and offers a picturesque setting for the sports activities.
What are the main days of the festival?
The main days of the festival are Saturday 16 May, with the national final of Boater-X, the bouldering competition, and the Hike & Fly competition, and Sunday 10 May, with the Family Day dedicated to activities for the whole family.
