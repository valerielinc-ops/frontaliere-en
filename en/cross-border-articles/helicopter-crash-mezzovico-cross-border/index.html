Helicopter crashes in Mezzovico: six injured, one serious (cross-border guide)

A helicopter crashed during landing in Mezzovico-Vira, six people injured, one in serious condition.

Contesto

In brief - Helicopter crashes during landing in Mezzovico - Six injured, one seriously - Investigations ongoing ## Key facts - What: Helicopter crash - When: May 4, 2026, 4:30 PM - Where: Mezzovico-Vira - Who: Cantonal police, Rega, Croce Verde Lugano - Amount: Not specified Moments of fear on the afternoon of Monday, May 4, in the territory of Mezzovico-Vira. Shortly after 4:30 PM, a private company helicopter crashed to the ground while preparing to complete the landing phase at a work site. According to a preliminary reconstruction by the Cantonal Police, the aircraft fell from a height of about twenty meters. The causes that led to the loss of control of the aircraft are not yet known and remain under investigation by the authorities. There were six people on board the helicopter, including the pilot. Impressive was the deployment of rescue forces that arrived at the scene: the crews of Croce Verde di Lugano and Rega intervened, supported by the firefighters of Lugano and Rivera. The medical balance is serious: one passenger suffered very serious injuries, putting his life in danger. Two occupants suffered injuries of medium severity. The remaining three people involved suffered less serious injuries. To allow investigations and safety operations, Via Vira was closed to traffic and will remain closed to circulation for several hours. At the scene of the accident also arrived the agents of the Polizia del Vedeggio, the investigators of fedpol and the Swiss Investigation Bureau for Safety (SISI), which has already opened a preliminary investigation to determine the technical causes of the crash.
