In Viggiù, a grant for young people: 1000 euros for community projects

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

An initiative for the young people of Viggiù: 1000 euros to realize projects for the community. Deadline May 10, 2026.

Contesto

In brief - Call for young people aged 14 to 20 in Viggiù - 1000 euro contribution for community projects - Submission deadline: May 10, 2026 ## Key facts - What: Call for community projects - When: Submission by May 10, 2026 - Where: Viggiù - Who: Cooperativa Lotta Contro l’Emarginazione and the Municipality of Viggiù - Amount: 1000 euros A call has been launched in Viggiù for young people aged 14 to 20 to carry out projects that can improve the local community. The initiative, promoted by the street education service of the Cooperativa Lotta Contro l’Emarginazione in collaboration with the Municipality of Viggiù, offers a contribution of 1000 euros for one or more projects related to areas such as events, sports, culture, and social gatherings. The aim is to stimulate active participation and youth leadership, giving space to the new generations and enhancing their ability to imagine initiatives for the town. To participate, it is sufficient to present a project, even a simple one, with a clear idea, defined objectives, and an indication of the target audience. The groups must consist of at least three people, at least one of whom must be a resident of Viggiù. The proposals must be sent to the email address giulia.ossola@cooplotta.org by May 10, 2026. The participants will be contacted and will be able to take part in a series of support meetings, during which they will be guided by educators in the realization of their ideas. A path that aims not only to reward the best projects but also to build skills, relationships, and new opportunities. ### How to participate To participate in the call, young people from Viggiù must present a project that meets the established criteria. Procedure to participate in the call To participate in the call, young people from Viggiù must follow a series of simple but important steps. First, it is necessary to form a group of at least three people, at least one of whom must be a resident of Viggiù. Subsequently, the participants must develop a clear and defined idea, with specific objectives and well-identified recipients. The project must have a concrete impact on the territory and can concern various areas such as events, sports, culture, and moments of aggregation. ### Project presentation Once the idea is developed, the participants must prepare a presentation of the project. The presentation must include a detailed description of the project, the objectives, the recipients, and the implementation methods. It is important that the presentation is clear and well-structured, in order to facilitate the evaluation by the commission. ### Submission of the proposal The proposal must be sent to the email address giulia.ossola@cooplotta.org by May 10, 2026. It is important to respect the deadline and ensure that the proposal is complete and well-presented. Once the proposal is received, the participants will be contacted again to confirm receipt and provide further information on the next steps. ### Accompaniment meetings The selected participants will have the opportunity to participate in a series of accompaniment meetings.
