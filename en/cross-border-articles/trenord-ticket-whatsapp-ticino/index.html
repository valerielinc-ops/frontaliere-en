Trenord tickets on WhatsApp: how it works

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

Trenord introduces tickets purchasable via WhatsApp, available at self-service and Digital Gate in Milan and Malpensa T1

Contesto

In brief - Trenord tickets purchasable via WhatsApp - Available at self-service machines and Digital Gates - Valid for regional, suburban, and cross-border trains ## Key facts - What: Trenord tickets purchasable via WhatsApp - When: Available from April 2026 - Where: Self-service machines and Digital Gates at Milano Centrale, Milano Cadorna, Milano Porta Garibaldi, and Malpensa T1 - Who: Trenord - Amount: Not specified Trenord has introduced a new service that allows you to purchase train tickets directly via WhatsApp. This service is available at the self-service machines and Digital Gates located at the stations of Milano Centrale, Milano Cadorna, Milano Porta Garibaldi, and Malpensa T1. Tickets purchased via WhatsApp are valid for regional and suburban services, the Malpensa Express airport link, and cross-border lines. Additionally, you can purchase integrated tickets for travel in the STIBM area and tickets for 'Train Trips', train+experience packages for leisure time. ### How it works Tickets on WhatsApp have all the features of a digital ticket: convenient, practical, and purchasable with just a few clicks. The ticket arrives directly via chat: just open the conversation to view all the travel ticket information and show it during the inspection. Railway, airport, and cross-border tickets do not require validation but are activated at the time indicated at purchase. If you depart early, you can advance the time using a dedicated function. For STIBM tickets, activation is required with a 'tap' before departure. ### Purchase method To receive tickets via WhatsApp, you need to select the WhatsApp option and scan the QR Code on the self-service machine or Digital Gate screen. Frequently Asked Questions
How can I purchase a Trenord ticket on WhatsApp?
To purchase a Trenord ticket on WhatsApp, you need to go to the self-service machines or Digital Gates located at Milano Centrale, Milano Cadorna, Milano Porta Garibaldi, and Malpensa T1 stations. Select the WhatsApp option on the screen of the distributor, scan the QR Code, and confirm the last digits of your phone number to receive the ticket link.
Are WhatsApp tickets valid for all Trenord trains?
Yes, WhatsApp tickets are valid for regional and suburban service trains, the Malpensa Express airport link, and cross-border lines. Additionally, you can purchase integrated tickets to travel in the STIBM area and tickets for the 'Train Excursions'.
How can I receive updates about my train via WhatsApp?
To receive updates about your train via WhatsApp, you can use the 'Il mio treno' (My Train) channel of Trenord. Accessible from a specific link, it allows you to search for your train of interest and receive push notifications with updates on scheduled transit times and tracks.
