Somma Lombardo medical service moves to Lonate Pozzolo

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

The emergency medical service temporarily moves to Lonate Pozzolo until July for renovations.

Contesto

In brief - Medical guard service moves from Somma Lombardo to Lonate Pozzolo - Service active until July - Transfer for works at the new Community House ## Key facts - What: Transfer of medical guard service - When: From May 4th to July 31st, 2026 - Where: Lonate Pozzolo - Who: ASST Valle Olona - Amount: Not specified Starting Monday, May 4th, the continuity of care service, known as medical guard, of Somma Lombardo will temporarily move to Lonate Pozzolo. The decision was communicated by ASST Valle Olona with a protocol note on April 30th. The transfer is necessary to allow the completion of works at the new Community House of Somma Lombardo, which will open by the summer. The service, fundamental for nighttime, holiday, and eve-of-holiday healthcare, will continue to be guaranteed but in a different location. Citizens who need the medical guard service will have to go to the Community House of Lonate Pozzolo for the entire duration of the transfer. This move is part of a broader plan to improve local healthcare services, aiming to ensure greater efficiency and quality of the services offered. ### Implications for cross-border workers The transfer of the medical guard service could have a significant impact on cross-border workers residing in the territory of Somma Lombardo and surrounding areas. They will need to adapt to going to Lonate Pozzolo to use the service, which may involve longer travel times and more organization. However, the service remains essential to ensure continuous healthcare, especially during nighttime hours and on holidays. ### Comparison with the previous situation Before the transfer, the medical guard service was easily accessible at the Somma Lombardo location.
