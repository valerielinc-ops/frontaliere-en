Frequently Asked Questions
What are the most common causes of car fires?
The most common causes of car fires include electrical malfunctions, fuel leaks, short circuits, and road accidents. It is important for drivers to adopt preventive measures, such as regular vehicle maintenance and the use of adequate safety equipment.
What should I do in case of a car fire?
In case of a car fire, it is crucial to stop in a safe place, turn on the hazard lights, and call for help. Do not attempt to extinguish the fire yourself and wait for the arrival of the firefighters. If possible, move away from the vehicle and wait for instructions from the competent authorities.
How can I best plan my trips in case of road emergencies?
To best plan your trips in case of road emergencies, it is advisable to constantly monitor traffic conditions through dedicated apps or local information websites. Additionally, it is useful to have alternative routes available and to know secondary routes that can be used if necessary. Finally, it is essential to always keep your phone charged and to have water and snacks with you in case of prolonged delays.
