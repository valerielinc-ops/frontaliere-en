Gottardo - Who: Lugano City Police - Impact: Waiting times between 15 and 30 minutes The match between FC Lugano and FC St. Gallen scheduled for Sunday, May 10, 2026, at 14:00 at the Cornaredo Stadium will cause traffic disruptions in the Cornaredo/Molino Nuovo area. The Lugano City Police have announced that the parade of the visiting fans, coming from St. Gallen, will pass through the streets of Lugano's city center. The square in front of Lugano Station will be closed to public transport from 12:00. ### Parade route The parade will follow this route: - Lugano SBB Station - Via S. Gottardo (South-North section from Besso tunnel to Via Tesserete) - Via Tesserete - Via Torricelli - Via Trevano - Cornaredo Stadium South Parking On the mentioned streets, there will be a total road closure, with waiting times that may vary from 15 to 30 minutes. At the end of the match, around 15:50, the parade will depart from the Stadium towards the SBB Station, following the same route as the outward journey. ### Available parking For the football match, the 'Gerra' gravel area will be available for the cars of local fans, while the visiting fans will have the Cornaredo Stadium South parking lot, Via Trevano side, at their disposal. Frequently Asked Questions
Which streets will be blocked during the procession?
The streets that will be blocked during the procession are: Lugano FFS Station, via S. Gottardo (South-North section from the Besso tunnel to via Tesserete), via Tesserete, via Torricelli, via Trevano, and the Cornaredo South Stadium parking lot.
What are the expected waiting times for the roadblocks?
The expected waiting times for the roadblocks will vary from 15 to 30 minutes. The procession will move on foot from 12:00 to approximately 13:15 and will depart from the Stadium around 15:50.

For updated information on roadblocks and diversions, consult the website of the [Lugano City Police](https://www.lugano.ch/polizia).
