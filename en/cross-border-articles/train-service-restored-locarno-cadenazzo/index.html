This issue created significant inconvenience for passengers, despite the introduction of replacement buses from the early hours of the morning. The fault was resolved at 11:45 a.m., allowing train services to resume from noon. ### The situation The fault affected the core of a switch between Tenero and Gordola, blocking train traffic for the entire morning. Passengers faced significant inconvenience, despite the replacement buses provided. The situation returned to normal only after 12 p.m., when trains resumed regular service. ### Impact on cross-border workers Cross-border workers using the Locarno-Cadenazzo line to reach their workplaces in Ticino experienced delays and inconvenience.
