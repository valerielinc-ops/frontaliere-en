The course, which will start on Monday, May 18, 2026, will run for four consecutive lessons, every Monday from 20:30 to 22:30 at the Palestra Scuola El. Morandi. Through practical exercises and moments of discussion, the course guides participants in refining their style, managing the psychological dynamics of the classroom with greater awareness, and using advanced storytelling and engagement techniques. ### Course objectives The goal of the course is to learn not just to speak, but to capture attention, convey confidence, and build a message that lasts. The course is aimed at those who have already attended a beginner's course or have gained some experience in public speaking and feel the need to raise the bar of their performances. No specific materials are required, but comfortable clothing is recommended to facilitate practical activities. ### Target audience and requirements The course is ideal for those who have already acquired the basics of public speaking and want to take it a step further. It is aimed at professionals, students, and anyone who wants to improve their public communication skills.
