In brief - Conference on public venue safety at Ville Ponti on June 29, 2026 - Organized by Varese Prefecture with local institutions - Focus on fire prevention and regulations ## Key facts - What: Conference on safety in public venues - When: June 29, 2026 - Where: Ville Ponti Convention Center, Varese - Who: Varese Prefecture with local institutions and entities - Cost: Not yet specified On June 29, 2026, the Ville Ponti Convention Center will host a conference dedicated to safety in public performance venues and public establishments. The initiative is promoted by the Varese Prefecture together with local institutions and entities. During an organizational meeting held in the Motta Room of the Prefecture, representatives from the Province, Police Forces, Fire Department, Labor Inspectorate, ATS Insubria, Chamber of Commerce, UPEL, and major industry associations, as well as the involved professional orders, participated. ### Conference objectives The objective of the conference is to define content and operational aspects to strengthen the culture of prevention and greater knowledge of the current regulations regarding safety in public places. During the event, crucial topics such as fire prevention, structural and plant safety, worker protection, and organizational aspects related to the management of events with high attendance will be addressed. ### Participants and continuing education credits The conference will be accredited for the recognition of professional continuing education credits, involving engineers, architects, surveyors, and industrial experts. This confirms the technical and multidisciplinary nature of the event. Dettagli operativi

In-depth look at fire prevention One of the central themes of the conference will be fire prevention. This is a crucial aspect for safety in public places, especially in contexts with high attendance. Italian legislation provides specific requirements for fire safety, which must be respected by all public venues. During the conference, the main regulatory obligations and best practices to ensure safety will be illustrated. ### Structural and plant safety Another important topic will be structural and plant safety. This includes verifying the stability of structures, installing electrical and hydraulic systems compliant with regulations, and the presence of alarm and evacuation systems. Knowledge of these regulations is essential to guarantee the safety of workers and the public. ### Worker protection Worker protection is another fundamental aspect that will be addressed during the conference. This includes staff training, the use of personal protective equipment, and emergency management. Workplace safety is a priority for all companies, and participation in events like this can help improve safety practices in the workplace. ### Managing events with high attendance Managing events with a high attendance requires careful planning and in-depth knowledge of safety regulations. During the conference, the main organizational aspects to ensure safety during events will be discussed. This includes managing queues, the arrangement of seats, and the presence of security personnel. ### Comparison with the previous situation The conference is part of the broader coordination work promoted by the provincial institutions, with the aim of guaranteeing increasingly high safety standards in entertainment venues. Participation is open to all operators, professionals, and entities interested. Once the registration is complete, a confirmation email will be sent with all the necessary information to participate in the conference. ### Deadlines and important dates The deadline for registering for the conference is June 20, 2026. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can participate in the conference on public venue safety?
The conference is open to all operators, professionals, and entities interested in public venue safety. Participation is free and open to anyone who wants to update their knowledge on regulations and improve safety practices.
What are the main topics of the conference?
The main topics of the conference include fire prevention, structural and system safety, worker protection, and organizational aspects related to managing events with high attendance. These topics are crucial for ensuring safety in public venues.

Once registered, you will receive a confirmation email with all the necessary information to participate in the event.
