Di Valerie Linc · Published on 7 May 2026

The PLR of Morbio Inferiore reports a less frequent and more expensive green waste collection service compared to neighboring municipalities

Contesto

In brief - Less frequent collection of plant waste in Morbio Inferiore - Similar or higher costs compared to neighboring municipalities - Residents report practical inconveniences ## Key facts - What: Interpellation by the PLR of Morbio Inferiore on the plant waste collection service - When: May 7, 2026 - Where: Morbio Inferiore, Canton Ticino - Who: PLR Group of Morbio Inferiore - Amount: Costs of the tags similar or less advantageous compared to neighboring municipalities The PLR group of Morbio Inferiore has submitted an interpellation to the Municipality to request clarifications on the plant waste collection service, deemed inadequate compared to that offered in neighboring municipalities. The document highlights how the current frequency of collection is lower than similar realities such as Vacallo and Balerna, despite the costs of the tags being substantially similar for the smaller containers and even less advantageous for those of 240 liters. A situation that, according to the signatories, penalizes the citizens of Morbio. This is added to the reports of some residents, who complain about a deterioration of the service and the need to keep the green waste for longer periods, with consequent practical inconveniences. The interpellation also highlights the system adopted in neighboring municipalities, where a simpler method based on a single-colored tag has been introduced: one tag for small volumes, two for larger quantities. A solution considered clearer and more functional. In light of these considerations, the PLR submits some questions to the Municipality. Analysis of Critical Issues The PLR's interpellation from Morbio Inferiore highlights several critical issues with the green waste collection service. In particular, the reduced frequency of collection compared to neighboring municipalities and the higher costs for larger containers represent a significant problem for residents. These practical inconveniences, such as the need to store green waste for longer periods, have a negative impact on the quality of life of citizens. ### Comparison with Neighboring Municipalities A comparison with neighboring municipalities such as Vacallo and Balerna shows that the green waste collection service in Morbio Inferiore is less efficient and more expensive. In neighboring municipalities, a system based on a single-colored band has been introduced, making the service simpler and more functional. This system could also be adopted in Morbio Inferiore to improve service efficiency and reduce costs for residents. ### Proposals for Improvement The PLR's interpellation proposes several solutions to improve the green waste collection service in Morbio Inferiore. In particular, it suggests introducing a simpler and more functional collection system based on a single-colored band. Additionally, it proposes reviewing the service schedule immediately or starting next year, restoring a more adequate frequency. These solutions could improve service efficiency and reduce inconveniences for residents. ### Impact on Residents Residents of Morbio Inferiore have reported a deterioration in the green waste collection service, resulting in practical inconveniences.
