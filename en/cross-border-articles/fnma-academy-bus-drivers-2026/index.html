Become a bus driver at no cost: FNM Autoservizi's Academy is back

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 22 April 2026

Open selections for aspiring drivers in Lombardy. FNMA covers the costs of the D license and CQC, offers permanent employment and a 3,000 euro bonus.

TL;DR - Free bus driver training for Como and Varese residents - FNMA covers D license and CQC certification costs - Includes 3,000 euro sign-on bonus and welfare package - Leads to full-time permanent employment contracts ## Key facts - Program Name: FNM Autoservizi (FNMA) Academy - Target Areas: Provinces of Como and Varese - Minimum Age: 21 years old - Required License: B license - Application Deadline: June 30, 2026 - Training Start: September 2026 - Sign-on Bonus: 3,000 euros paid in three installments - Training Locations: Saronno or Tradate The FNM Autoservizi (FNMA) Academy returns with a new edition dedicated to those who want to become bus drivers without incurring training costs. The program, aimed at residents of the provinces of Como and Varese, fully covers the expenses for obtaining the D license and the CQC (Professional Competence Card). ### Requirements and Procedures To participate, you must be at least 21 years old, have a secondary school diploma, and hold a B license. Selected candidates will be hired with a part-time fixed-term contract, with a monthly salary of approximately 800 euros. During this phase, FNMA will train the new drivers and cover the costs of the necessary certifications. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the requirements to participate in FNMA's Academy?
To participate in FNMA's Academy, you must be at least 21 years old, have a secondary school diploma, and hold a category B driver's license. Additionally, you need to be physically fit, have a clean criminal record, and be willing to work shifts.
How long does the training last and when does it start?
The training will last several months and will start by September 2026. During this period, selected candidates will be hired with a part-time fixed-term contract and will receive a monthly salary of approximately 800 euros.
What benefits does FNMA offer to new drivers?
FNMA offers a 3,000 euro sign-on bonus, paid in three installments of 1,000 euros over the first 36 months of service. Additionally, new hires will be entitled to a welfare package that includes meal vouchers, health services, child education reimbursements, and leisure time benefits.
