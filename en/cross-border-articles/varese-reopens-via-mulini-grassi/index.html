Varese, Mulini Grassi road reopens after safety works (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 7 May 2026

The road connecting Sant'Ambrogio with Valle Olona is now passable again after structural reinforcement works.

The works were necessary to carry out consolidation and securing of the road surface. The intervention allowed stabilizing the section affected by subsidence phenomena, once again ensuring the full safety of vehicles in transit.
