Olgiate Olona Solidarity Emporium Opens

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

Inauguration on Saturday, May 9th with info stands and community aperitif. Local groups like Mensa del Padre Nostro and CAV of Castellanza will participate.

Contesto

In brief - Inauguration of Emporio Solidarietà Olgiate Olona - Participation of local entities - Concluding community aperitif ## Key facts - What: Inauguration of the Solidarity Emporium - When: Saturday, May 9, 2026 - Where: Parish of San Giuseppe al Buon Gesù, Olgiate Olona - Who: Decanato Valle Olona, Monsignor Franco Agnesi, volunteers - Amount: Not specified On Saturday, May 9, 2026, at 9:30 AM, the inauguration of the Solidarity Emporium will take place at the Parish of San Giuseppe al Buon Gesù in Olgiate Olona. The event, organized by the Decanato Valle Olona, will see the participation of numerous local entities engaged in supporting vulnerabilities. Among these are the Mensa del Padre Nostro and the CAV of Castellanza, the Amicizia Group of Gorla Minore, Icore of Marnate, Il pane di San Martino of Fagnano and Gorla Maggiore. For Olgiate Olona, the communities Sichem and Pachamama with the cooperative Promozione e Lavoro will be present, supported by Solidarietà Famigliare, In cammino per la Pace, and the Ambrosian Caritas decanale. The morning will begin with informational stands, followed at 10 AM by the presentation of the project together with the volunteers of the Emporium of Saronno and the intervention of Monsignor Franco Agnesi, Vicar General of the Diocese. At 12 PM, the official ribbon-cutting ceremony is scheduled, concluded by a community aperitif. The initiative, accompanied by the motto 'solidarity well spent,' aims to become a reference point for supporting the vulnerabilities of the area. ### Detailed program The program for the day has been structured to actively involve the community. Dettagli operativi

The Solidarity Emporium in Olgiate Olona is not just a food distribution point, but a true hub of aggregation and support for the community. Its inauguration represents an opportunity to strengthen social bonds and offer concrete help to those in need. The initiative is the result of collaboration between various local entities, which have decided to join forces to create a solidarity project that can have a lasting impact. ### Local Collaborations The participation of numerous local entities is a sign of the strong social cohesion that characterizes Olgiate Olona and the surrounding municipalities. The Mensa del Padre Nostro and the CAV of Castellanza, the Amicizia Group of Gorla Minore, Icore of Marnate, Il pane di san Martino of Fagnano and Gorla Maggiore, together with the Sichem and Pachamama communities and the Promozione e Lavoro cooperative, represent an example of how collaboration can lead to significant results. These organizations have long experience in supporting vulnerabilities and will be fundamental to the success of the Emporium. ### Services Offered The Solidarity Emporium will not limit itself to food distribution. Among the services offered will also be social counseling, psychological support, and aggregation activities. This holistic approach aims to provide comprehensive help to those in difficulty, addressing not only immediate needs but also the root causes of vulnerabilities. The goal is to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where people can feel supported and valued. ### Project Sustainability To ensure the sustainability of the project, the Solidarity Emporium relies on voluntary contributions and donations. Punti chiave

To participate in the inauguration of the Solidarity Emporium in Olgiate Olona, you can simply show up at the parish of San Giuseppe al Buon Gesù on the day of the event. No reservation is required, but it is advisable to arrive a bit early to have access to all the information stands and to participate in the project presentation. ### How to Contribute If you wish to contribute to the project, there are several ways to do so. You can donate food items or other essential products directly to the Emporium. Additionally, you can offer your time as a volunteer, participating in distribution and support activities. Every contribution is valuable and helps to keep this solidarity project alive. ### Useful Contacts For further information about the Solidarity Emporium and the ways to participate, you can contact the Decanato Valle Olona or the local entities involved in the project. Contacts will be available during the event and on the official channels of the participating organizations. ### Conclusion The inauguration of the Solidarity Emporium in Olgiate Olona is an important step towards a more supportive and inclusive community. Participating in this event means supporting a project aimed at making a difference in the lives of many people. Do not miss the opportunity to be part of this initiative and to contribute to creating a better future for everyone. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can access the services of the Solidarity Emporium?
The Solidarity Emporium is open to all residents of Olgiate Olona and neighboring municipalities who need food and social support. There are no specific requirements, but it is advisable to come during opening hours for more information.
How can I become a volunteer at the Emporium?
To become a volunteer, you can go to the parish of San Giuseppe al Buon Gesù or contact the local realities involved in the project directly. You will be welcomed and guided through the integration process.
What items can I donate to the Emporium?
You can donate non-perishable food items, personal hygiene products, and other essential items. Every contribution is valuable and helps support the project.
