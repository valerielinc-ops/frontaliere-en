Frequently Asked Questions
How much does a visit to the Sessa Gold Mine in Switzerland cost?
The ticket price is 15 Swiss francs for adults and 10 Swiss francs for children.
Can I visit the Sessa Gold Mine with a small child?
Yes, the visit is suitable for all ages. However, it is advisable to contact Swissminiatur for specific information on safety and accessibility conditions for very young children.
What are the opening hours of the Sessa Gold Mine in 2026?
The Sessa Gold Mine will be open from March 1 to October 31, 2026, with hours that vary according to the season. From March to May and from September to October, the hours are from 10:00 to 17:00, and from June to August from 9:00 to 18:00. It is advisable to check the updated schedules on the official website before visiting.
Is the Sessa Gold Mine accessible with a stroller or pram?
Yes, the Sessa Gold Mine is also accessible to people with disabilities or with strollers. There are adequate routes and services to ensure accessibility, but it is recommended to book in advance to check the availability of specific assistance.
Can I buy Sessa Gold Mine tickets online or only on-site?
Tickets for the Sessa Gold Mine can be purchased both online on the official Swissminiatur website and on-site at the ticket office. It is advisable to book online to avoid queues and ensure availability, especially during busy periods.
