Delegates from entities affiliated with the Ticino Farmers' Union have raised concerns about the need to promote local products more during events. The current situation, characterized by international conflicts, high production costs, and the presence of large predators such as wolves, is putting the agricultural sector to the test. ### Current concerns Delegates have expressed particular concern about the impact of large predators, such as wolves, on livestock and crops. This problem, combined with international conflicts and rising production costs, is making it increasingly difficult for Ticino farmers to maintain their activities. The request for greater presence of local products at events is seen as a way to support the agricultural sector and promote local production. Events, in fact, represent an important opportunity to showcase Ticino products and increase their visibility in the market. ### Impact on cross-border workers For cross-border workers who work in the agricultural sector or consume local products, this request could mean greater availability of Ticino products at the events they attend.
