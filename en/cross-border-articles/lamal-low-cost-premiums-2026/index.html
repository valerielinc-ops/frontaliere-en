In an interview with TVS, Baume-Schneider emphasized that a reduction in premiums could compromise the quality and scope of the services offered. 'A low-cost LAMal is not the solution,' she stated, defending the current system that ensures adequate health coverage for all residents, including cross-border workers. ### Impact on cross-border workers Cross-border workers who work in Ticino and reside in Italy are particularly interested in this discussion. Currently, cross-border workers are subject to withholding tax and must comply with Swiss regulations regarding health insurance. A low-cost LAMal could significantly influence their finances and the quality of medical care they have access to. Baume-Schneider emphasized that the current system, although it may seem expensive, offers comprehensive protection that a low-cost version could not guarantee. ### Comparison with the current system The current LAMal system involves premiums that vary depending on the canton and the chosen health insurance company.
