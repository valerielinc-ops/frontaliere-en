Military on Trenord trains: 20 euros per year for enhanced security

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 20 April 2026

From 1st May 2026, military personnel will be able to travel on Trenord trains in Lombardy with a discounted annual subscription of 20 euros to increase security on board.

Contesto

TL;DR - Military personnel can travel on Trenord trains in Lombardy for 20 euros/year starting May 1, 2026. - The initiative aims to increase security perception among commuters with uniformed personnel on trains. - The Region allocated 1.6 million euros for the biennium 2026-2027 to finance the measure. - Military personnel must register online and pay 20 euros to activate the subscription. ## Key facts - Effective date: May 1, 2026 - Expiry date: December 31, 2026 - Subscription fee: 20 euros per year - Regional funding: 1.6 million euros for the biennium 2026-2027 - Eligible personnel: Military personnel operating in commands located in Lombardy - Online application: IVOLA – I travel everywhere in Lombardy at a discount - Annual savings: Approximately 1,180 euros per military personnel - Cross-border workers: Approximately 60,000 daily between Lombardy and Ticino ## A symbolic subscription for security From May 1, 2026, military personnel from the Army, Navy, and Air Force will be able to travel on all Trenord trains in Lombardy with a subsidized subscription of just 20 euros per year. This measure was approved by the Regional Council on the proposal of the Transport and Sustainable Mobility Councillor, Franco Lucente, through a protocol of understanding involving the Region, military commands, and the railway company. The initiative, valid until December 31, 2026, aims to increase the perception of security among commuters thanks to the presence of uniformed personnel on the trains. To finance the measure, the Region has allocated a total of 1.6 million euros for the biennium 2026-2027. ### How the subsidy works The service is aimed at military personnel operating in commands located in the Lombardy region. The introduction of this symbolic subscription represents a significant step forward, not only for security but also for supporting the Armed Forces. Cross-border workers employed in Lombardy and residing in Ticino could indirectly benefit from this measure, thanks to a safer and more controlled travel environment. ### Practical Scenarios Imagine a cross-border worker who takes the train from Varese to reach their workplace in Lugano every day. What to do to benefit from the facilitation To benefit from this facilitation, military personnel must follow a simple but precise procedure. Here are the detailed steps to follow: 1. Access the IVOLA platform: Military personnel must access the "IVOLA – I travel everywhere in Lombardy facilitated" application via their mobile device or computer. The platform is available for both iOS and Android and can be downloaded for free from official stores. 2. Registration and verification: Once on the platform, military personnel will need to register by entering their personal data and verify their affiliation with the Armed Forces. This step is fundamental to access the facilitated subscription. Verification can be done by uploading a valid identity document and a declaration issued by their military command. For example, a soldier stationed in Chiasso who travels daily to Milan can verify their affiliation with the Armed Forces through a declaration from their command. 3. Payment of the fee: After verification, military personnel will need to pay the annual contribution fee of 20 euros. This payment can be made by credit card, PayPal, or other payment methods available on the platform. It is important to note that the fee is valid for the entire solar year, regardless of the activation date. 4. Activation of the subscription: Once the payment is completed, the subscription will be activated immediately and will be valid for the entire regional railway network covered by Trenord. The subscription allows unlimited travel on all Trenord regional trains, including those connecting Ticino to Lombardy, such as the Chiasso-Milan line. ### Operational checklist - Required documents: - Valid identity document - Declaration from the military command - Credit card or PayPa...

Frequently Asked Questions
Who can benefit from this discount?
The discounted subscription is reserved for military personnel of the Army, Navy, and Air Force operating in commands located in the Lombardy region. Cross-border workers who are not part of the Armed Forces cannot benefit from this discounted rate.
How much does the discounted subscription cost?
The discounted subscription costs only 20 euros per year. This amount is the responsibility of the military personnel and must be paid through the IVOLA platform.
How can I verify my membership in the Armed Forces on the IVOLA platform?
To verify your membership in the Armed Forces, you will need to provide the required documents during registration on the IVOLA platform. These documents may include the military identification card or other proof of membership.
