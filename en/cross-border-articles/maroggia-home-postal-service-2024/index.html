Maroggia, home mail delivery from 2024

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

From September 2024, Maroggia residents will be able to use home mail delivery while waiting for the new branch scheduled for 2027.

Contesto

In brief - Home postal service in Maroggia starting September 2024 - New branch in partnership planned for 2027 - Transitional service to ensure essential postal operations ## Key facts - What: Home postal service - When: Starting September 2024 - Where: Maroggia, Canton Ticino - Who: Swiss Post in collaboration with a local business - Cost: Not yet specified Starting September 2024, residents of Maroggia will be able to use a home postal service, a transitional solution that will allow them to perform postal operations, payments, and cash withdrawals directly at their doorstep. This service has been implemented while waiting for the new branch in partnership, which will open in the first months of 2027. Until September, the current branch will remain active. ### Municipality's Goal The Municipality of Val Mara, represented by Mayor Jgor Zocchetti, supports the temporary solution. According to Zocchetti, it is crucial to ensure accessibility to essential services for the population. The home service represents a valid alternative to ensure good postal service during the transitional phase, while awaiting the adaptation of the offer through collaboration with a local business. ### Implications for Cross-Border Workers For cross-border workers residing in Maroggia, this service represents a significant convenience. They will be able to perform postal operations without having to physically go to the branch, saving time and simplifying procedures. Procedure to Access the Home Delivery Service To access the home postal service, residents of Maroggia will need to follow a simple procedure. First, it is necessary to contact Swiss Post to request the service. Once activated, the delivery staff will carry out postal operations directly at your home. ### Deadlines and Required Documents There are no specific deadlines for activating the service. However, it is important to ensure you have all the necessary documents to carry out postal operations, such as your ID card and tax documents. ### Useful Tools For further information and to activate the service, you can consult the official Swiss Post website or contact the Maroggia post office directly.
