Giro-E 2026: Stage from Angera to Verbania on Lake Maggiore

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 28 April 2026

A Giro-E stage combines sports, sustainability, and tourism promotion along Lake Maggiore, starting from Angera on May 22, 2026.

Contesto

In brief - Giro-E stage Angera-Verbania on May 22, 2026 - 50 km on e-bikes before the Giro d'Italia - Tourism promotion and sustainable mobility ## Key facts - What: Giro-E stage - When: May 22, 2026 - Where: Angera to Verbania, Lake Maggiore - Who: Municipality of Angera, former cycling champions, sports figures, journalists, politicians - Distance: 50 km route On May 22, 2026, Angera will host the start of a Giro-E stage, a side event of the Giro d'Italia. Numerous teams, composed of former cycling champions, sports figures, journalists, and politicians, will cover the last stretch of the route, approximately 50 km, on electric-assisted bicycles before the passage of the Giro d'Italia riders. This initiative combines the beauty of Lake Maggiore, the environment, and technology, offering an international showcase for the territory. The Culture and Tourism Councillor of Angera, Cristian Marzetta, underlines the importance of the event: 'Our participation in this event stems from the desire to showcase, on a national and international scale, all the beauties and excellences of our City. This is a unique opportunity that will allow participants to live an engaging and unforgettable experience.' ### The Green Fun Village The logistical heart of the event will be the Green Fun Village, set up along the lakeside of Piazza Garibaldi. This area, built with sustainable materials, will function as an entertainment and information center where stands and games will raise public awareness about soft mobility and environmental protection. The village will also feature a stage for local realities, giving schools, associations, and local projects the opportunity to introduce themselves to visitors and residents.
