What to do in case of a check In case of a police check, cross-border workers must follow these procedures: 1. Stop immediately and turn off the engine. 2. Show identity documents (valid passport or ID card) and residence permit (for non-EU citizens, the B or L permit). For EU citizens, an ID card is sufficient. 3. Provide vehicle documents if requested (registration certificate, car insurance, driving license). In Switzerland, the driving license must always be carried, even when driving a rental vehicle. 4. Follow the instructions of the officers and stay calm. It is important to avoid discussions and cooperate with the authorities. 5. In case of an accident, call emergency services immediately (112 in Switzerland, 118 for the ambulance) and cooperate with the authorities.
