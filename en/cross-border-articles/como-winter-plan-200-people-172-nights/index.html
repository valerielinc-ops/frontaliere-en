Como Cold Weather Plan: 200 people hosted for 172 nights

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 1 May 2026

Como's Cold Weather Plan has hosted 200 homeless people for 172 nights, thanks to the collaboration of volunteers and donors.

Contesto

In brief - Piano Freddo Como: 200 people welcomed - 172 nights of winter accommodation - 550 volunteers and 20,000 euros raised ## Key facts - What: Winter accommodation for homeless people - When: From 1st December 2025 to 1st May 2026 - Where: Como and surroundings - Who: Vicini di Strada, Como Municipality, Como Province - Amount: 200 people welcomed, 550 volunteers, 20,000 euros raised The Piano Freddo of Como concluded with a positive balance: 200 homeless people found shelter for 172 nights thanks to the initiative that started on 1st December 2025. This project, carried out thanks to the collaboration between Vicini di Strada, the Planning Office of the Como Territorial Area, the Municipality of Como and the Province of Como, offered vital support during the winter months. The protection network was articulated in various structures and projects, including the Dormitory of via Borgovico, the Betlemme Project of the Caritas Foundation, the Dormitory of via Napoleona and the Piccola Casa Federico Ozanam. Each structure welcomed a significant number of people, with a total of 96 unique presences at the Dormitory of via Borgovico, 27 people at the Betlemme Project, 69 people at the Dormitory of via Napoleona and 2 admissions to the Piccola Casa Federico Ozanam. ### The heart of the project The implementation of the Piano Freddo was made possible thanks to the indispensable contribution of about 550 volunteers, 400 in the parishes of the Betlemme Project and 150 at the Winter Dormitory of Via Borgovico. Extraordinary also the response of the citizens: the fundraising campaign reached almost 20,000 euros. The Cold Plan has bridged this gap, providing vital support during the winter months. Frequently Asked Questions
How can I make a donation to the Fondo Vicini di Strada?
You can make a donation to the Fondo Vicini di Strada through the crowdfunding platform of the Fondazione Provinciale della Comunità Comasca. Visit the official website for more information and to make your donation.
Who can participate as a volunteer in the Piano Freddo?
Anyone can participate as a volunteer in the Piano Freddo. Contact the associations or cooperatives that are part of Vicini di Strada for information on the requirements and how to participate.
Which facilities are involved in the Piano Freddo?
The facilities involved in the Piano Freddo include the Dormitorio di via Borgovico, the Progetto Betlemme of the Fondazione Caritas, the Dormitorio di via Napoleona, and the Piccola Casa Federico Ozanam.
