In brief - Sunday, May 31, 2026, from 14:30 to 18:00 - Free and paid activities at the Scientific Educational Center in Tradate - Guided tours, solar system exploration, and thematic trails ## Key facts - What: Scientific and naturalistic event - When: May 31, 2026, from 14:30 to 18:00 - Where: Scientific Educational Center – EcoPlanetarium, via ai Ronchi, Tradate (VA) - Who: Organized by Parco Pineta Appiano Gentile - Cost: Free entry, paid activities only for the EcoPlanetarium On Sunday, May 31, 2026, from 14:30 to 18:00, the Scientific Educational Center of Tradate opens its doors for a day dedicated to the discovery of nature and space. Located in the heart of Parco Pineta, the center offers a variety of activities for all ages, allowing visitors to explore the solar system, participate in guided tours, and walk along thematic trails. Among the proposed activities, the journey in the EcoPlanetarium stands out, a unique experience that allows you to explore the solar system. This activity is the only one that requires payment and booking. Additionally, visitors can participate in guided tours of the Astronomical Observatory, bookable on-site, and the explorer's trials, a path of experiences and games for the youngest. The Magic of the Forest Trail and the Nature Trail offer instead an immersive experience in nature, with guided visits or independently. To participate, visitors can choose between two options: free entry to the center, which includes all activities except the Planetarium, or the complete package for the EcoPlanetarium. Booking is simple: just select the desired type on the official website and add the seats to the cart. Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main activities offered at the Centro Didattico Scientifico di Tradate?
The main activities include the journey in the EcoPlanetarium, the visit to the Astronomical Observatory, the explorer tests for the youngest, the Magic Forest Trail, the Solar System, and the Nature Trail.
How can I book activities at the Centro Didattico Scientifico di Tradate?
You can book activities online on the center's official website. Select the desired option, add the seats to the cart, and communicate your last name to the staff at the entrance.
What is the cost to participate in the event?
Entry to the center is free, while the activity in the EcoPlanetarium is paid. The other activities are free.
