Laurent Morel new EF Switzerland CEO

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

EF Education First appoints Laurent Morel as new CEO to strengthen its presence in the Swiss market.

Contesto

In brief - EF appoints Laurent Morel as General Manager Switzerland - New national structure unifies linguistic regions - Morel at EF since 2011 with international experience ## Key facts - What: Laurent Morel appointed General Manager EF Switzerland - When: April 29, 2026 - Where: Lugano and Lausanne - Who: EF Education First - Amount: 20 years international experience Laurent Morel has been appointed General Manager of EF Switzerland, a significant step for the company in strengthening its presence in the Swiss market. Concurrently, EF introduces a new national organizational structure that unifies the country's three linguistic regions under a single responsibility. Morel, who has been part of EF since 2011, boasts nearly 20 years of international experience in leadership roles in Europe and Asia, more than 15 of which in the education sector. At EF, Morel began his career in Hong Kong as Asia Sales Director, then took over general management in Taiwan. He later returned to Switzerland, where since 2023 he has been responsible for activities in French-speaking Switzerland as Country Manager. His international perspective and long career at EF characterize his leadership style. The new structure aims to strengthen strategic orientation, create further synergies between linguistic regions, and further consolidate EF Switzerland's market position. ### Implications for cross-border workers The new national structure of EF Switzerland could have a significant impact on cross-border workers in the education sector. With unified management, the company aims to ensure a more consistent and integrated approach in the development of educational activities and international mobility programs. This could translate into more opportunities for training and cultural exchange for cross-border workers, who represent an important part of the Swiss market. ### Procedure to access EF programs To access EF programs, cross-border workers can follow a simple and direct procedure. First, they need to visit the EF Switzerland website and select the program of interest. Then, they can fill out the online registration form and provide the required information. Once the registration is complete, cross-border workers will receive a confirmation and can start planning their study program. ### Comparison with other educational options Compared to other educational options available in Switzerland, EF offers a series of unique advantages. Additionally, EF offers customized courses that can be adapted to the specific needs of students, ensuring a tailored learning experience. ### Success scenarios With the new national structure, EF Switzerland could create new opportunities for collaboration with local and international educational institutions.
