Among the most significant works is the new Genzana Tunnel in Lugano, which is causing traffic disruptions. Drivers and local residents need to be patient, but not for much longer. The construction site on Via San Gottardo is paused during the day, and work only proceeds at night. A road section that has been one-way for about a year should have its traffic restored by June 2026. ### The Lugano Station Renovation Project The Genzana Tunnel construction site is part of the Lugano station renovation project, specifically the phase that includes the creation of a new underpass. The works, including the renovation of Via Basilea, are expected to be completed in 2027. 'We are in good shape, they are respecting the deadlines,' says Lugano Municipal Councilor Filippo Lombardi. Practical implications for cross-border workers The ongoing and planned construction works in the Sottoceneri area will significantly impact the mobility of cross-border workers crossing the Italian-Swiss border. In particular, the works on the Genzana Tunnel and the reconstruction of Via Basilea in Lugano will result in changes to traffic until 2027. Drivers will need to adapt to alternative routes and possible delays, especially during peak hours. ### Alternative routes and travel times For cross-border workers who use their car to commute to work, it is essential to plan alternative routes. For example, during night works on Via San Gottardo, it might be useful to consider secondary roads or alternative public transport. It is advisable to constantly monitor updates on construction sites and expected travel times. ### Impact on public transport The works planned for 2027 related to the tram-train will have a significant impact on public transport. The mobility in the city center will be rethought, with possible changes to bus and tram lines. Cross-border workers who use public transport will need to be prepared for changes in routes and waiting times. ## Comparison with the previous situation Before the works, traffic in the Sottoceneri area was more fluid, with fewer inconveniences for drivers and commuters. With the start of the construction sites, the situation has changed, requiring greater attention and patience from everyone. However, the ongoing works are necessary to improve mobility in the long term and reduce traffic pressure on the lakeside. ### Long-term benefits Despite the temporary inconveniences, the ongoing and planned works will bring long-term benefits. What are the most relevant construction sites currently underway in the Sottoceneri area?
Among the most relevant construction sites, the new Genzana Tunnel in Lugano stands out, which is causing traffic disruptions. Drivers and local residents need to be patient, but not for much longer. What are the planned works for 2027?
2027 will see the start of works for the tram-train. 'For the city, next year will be a year of challenges as the operation related to the ex-schools square, the tram-train network tunnel, the Sant'Anna exit, and Corso Pestalozzi will begin,' continues Lombardi. 'This means that the mobility of the city center will need to be rethought for a couple of years, both private and public. The solutions are already in place, they have been approved, now it's a matter of moving to the execution phase.'
