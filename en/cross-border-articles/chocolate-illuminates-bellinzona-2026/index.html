Chocolat Stella opens new store in Bellinzona

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 2 May 2026

The famous Chocolat Stella returns to the center of Bellinzona with a new store in Piazza Nosetto, Palazzo Civico.

The choice to open a new store in Bellinzona represents an important opportunity for the city and for chocolate lovers. The opening of the new sales point in Piazza Nosetto, Palazzo civico, is a significant event for Bellinzona. The strategic position of the store will allow Chocolat Stella to reach a wider audience and further promote its range of products.
