Lombard cuisine bill advances in region (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 6 May 2026

The bill to promote Lombard cuisine advances in Lombardy region with a first allocation of 600,000 euros.

Contesto

In brief - Bill for Lombard typical cuisine advances in Region - Initial allocation of 600,000 euros - Creation of the 'Lombard typical cuisine' brand - Hearings with experts and representatives of the gastronomic sector ## Key facts - What: Bill for the enhancement of Lombard typical cuisine - When: Under examination in Commission, final vote expected for June 23, 2026 - Where: Lombardy Region - Who: Promoted by councilor Alessandro Corbetta (Lega) - Amount: 600,000 euros initially allocated The process of the bill dedicated to the enhancement of Lombard typical cuisine continues within the Productive Activities Commission of the Lombardy Region. The text, promoted by councilor Alessandro Corbetta (Lega) and supported by an initial allocation of 600,000 euros, aims to officially recognize traditional dishes as cultural heritage and a strategic lever for the economy and tourism. Meanwhile, the cycle of hearings has concluded, involving numerous experts and representatives of the gastronomic sector. A step considered fundamental by the vice-president and rapporteur Silvia Scurati, who underlined how the contributions collected will serve to refine the measure to make it even more effective in enhancing a heritage considered 'inestimable', not only from a cultural point of view but also economic and tourist. On the same line, the president of the Commission Marcello Ventura, who highlighted the importance of building legislation capable of concretely protecting and promoting the Lombard cuisine supply chain, from raw materials to transformation. Dettagli operativi

Practical Analysis The bill for the enhancement of typical Lombard cuisine represents an important step for the promotion of the regional gastronomic heritage. The creation of the 'Typical Lombard Cuisine' brand and the codification of traditional recipes could have a significant impact on the wine and food sector, with benefits for both local producers and consumers. ### Tourism Promotion The promotion of typical Lombard dishes could attract a greater number of tourists, with indirect benefits for the catering and hospitality sectors. This could translate into an increased demand for local products and greater visibility for restaurants offering traditional Lombard cuisine. For cross-border workers employed in this sector, this could mean new job opportunities and collaboration with companies and restaurants aiming to enhance Lombard cuisine. ### Enhancement of Certified Products The bill also provides for the enhancement of DOP, IGP, PAT, and De.Co certified products. This could create new job opportunities and collaboration between Lombard producers and Ticinese restaurateurs. The promotion of these products could also contribute to preserving local culinary traditions and ensuring the quality and healthiness of food. ### Generational Transition Measures to facilitate generational transition are another important aspect of the bill. Ensuring the transmission of culinary traditions is fundamental for the conservation of Lombard gastronomic heritage. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the main objective of the bill for the enhancement of typical Lombard cuisine?
The main objective of the bill is to officially recognize traditional Lombard dishes as cultural heritage and a strategic lever for the economy and tourism. The project aims to protect and promote the Lombard cuisine supply chain, from raw materials to processing, ensuring quality, healthiness, and continuity of local traditions.
What measures does the bill provide to facilitate generational change?
The bill provides specific measures to facilitate generational change, ensuring the transmission of culinary traditions. This includes promoting initiatives for training and collaboration between new generations of chefs and producers, offering new career and collaboration opportunities for cross-border workers operating in the catering and gastronomy sectors.
How can I take advantage of the opportunities offered by the bill for the enhancement of typical Lombard cuisine?
To take advantage of the opportunities offered by the bill, it is important to stay updated on the news and initiatives promoted by the Lombardy Region. Participating in gastronomic events and fairs, collaborating with local producers and restaurateurs, and deepening the knowledge of Lombard culinary traditions are some of the concrete actions that cross-border workers can undertake.
