Roberto Grassi is the new president of LIUC University

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 30 April 2026

The Board of Directors of LIUC has appointed Roberto Grassi as the new president of the university in Castellanza.

Contesto

In brief - Roberto Grassi is the new president of LIUC in Castellanza - He led Confindustria Varese for six years - He replaces Riccardo Comerio, president since 2019 ## Key facts - What: Appointment of the new president of LIUC - When: April 28, 2026 - Where: Castellanza - Who: Roberto Grassi - Amount: Not specified The Board of Directors of LIUC – Università Cattaneo has appointed Roberto Grassi as the new president of the university in Castellanza. The appointment took place during the meeting on Tuesday, April 28, 2026. Grassi, who led Confindustria Varese for six years before stepping down in May 2025, takes over from Riccardo Comerio, who has been president since 2019 and recently became president of ITS INCOM Academy. Comerio will remain on the Board of Directors as a counselor: the board thanked him for his contribution to strengthening the university's brand in the national and international academic landscape, and for affirming its role as a competitive lever for local businesses. For Confindustria Varese, the reference shareholder and historical soul of LIUC, this transition is part of a process of alternating leadership aimed at ensuring a continuous influx of new ideas and perspectives, while maintaining a strong link between the university and the business community that originated it. The Board of Directors wished the new president, Roberto Grassi, to lead LIUC towards a future of quality and innovation, always at the service of education and the realization of young people. ### Roberto Grassi's background Roberto Grassi has a long entrepreneurial background. He led Confindustria Varese for six years, stepping down in May 2025. His appointment as president of LIUC is an important recognition of his commitment to the business and academic worlds.
