Frequently Asked Questions
What are the unique experiences offered by The Lake Como EDITION?

Who is chef Mauro Colagreco and what is his role at The Lake Como EDITION?
Mauro Colagreco is an award-winning chef who personally oversees the gastronomic offerings at The Lake Como EDITION. His cuisine represents a dialogue between culinary excellence and local roots, offering guests a unique and unforgettable gastronomic experience.
