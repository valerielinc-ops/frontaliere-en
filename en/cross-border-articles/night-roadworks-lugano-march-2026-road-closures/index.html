Night roadworks in Lugano: roads closed in March (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 23 February 2026

Construction sites in Viganello, Pregassona, Castagnola, and Ruvigliana. Here is the complete schedule of closures and detours for those traveling at night.

Contesto

TL;DR - Lugano roadworks - Night shifts - Traffic delays ## Key facts - Where: Lugano, Ticino - When: March 2026 - Time: 8:00 PM to 6:00 AM - District: Viganello - End date: April 30th - Impact: Traffic delays, road closures - Alternative: Use real-time navigation apps Cross-border workers on shifts or staying late in Ticino, take note. The Lugano City Police Command has announced a dense schedule of nighttime roadworks that will affect several areas of the city starting from the beginning of March. The most extensive intervention concerns the Viganello district, where work is underway to upgrade AIL infrastructures. Specifically, in via Pazzalino and via la Santa, the works will take place at night, from 8:00 PM to 6:00 AM, starting from Monday, March 2nd until Thursday, April 30th. For the entire duration of the construction, vehicular traffic will not be interrupted but will be regulated by an alternating one-way system, with traffic management entrusted to security agents. Therefore, possible slowdowns and longer travel times are expected for those passing through that area in the evening and at night. In addition to this long-term construction site, the City has planned a series of shorter, but high-impact, interventions for the laying of the final road pavement in other strategic areas. These works will in some cases involve the total closure of the affected street.

Dettagli operativi

To help commuters plan their journeys and avoid unpleasant surprises, here are the details of the short-term interventions announced by the Lugano Police. These are mainly asphalting works that require temporary closures. ## Nighttime Construction Schedule - RUVIGLIANA, VIA VALL'ORBA - When: Night of Sunday, March 1st, from 11:00 PM to 4:00 AM. - Where: Near civic number 15. - Impact: The road will be completely closed to vehicular traffic. - CASTAGNOLA, VIA CORTIVO - When: Night of Monday, March 2nd, from 3:00 AM to 6:00 AM. - Where: Near civic number 2. - Impact: Traffic managed by security agents, private access guaranteed. - VIGANELLO, VIA PEDEMONTE - When: Night of Monday, March 2nd, from 8:00 PM to 6:00 AM. - Where: Near civic number 20. - Impact: Road closed. However, private access will be guaranteed, with traffic management by agents. - PREGASSONA, VIA GIOVANNI MARAINI - When: Night of Tuesday, March 3rd, from 8:00 PM to 6:00 AM. - Where: Near civic number 2. - Impact: No total closure, traffic will be managed by security agents. - VIGANELLO, VIA MUGGINA - When: Night of Wednesday, March 4th, from 8:00 PM to 6:00 AM. - Where: At the intersection with via al Lido. - Impact: Road closed to traffic. In this case as well, private access is guaranteed and traffic is managed on-site.

Punti chiave

Although these interventions are concentrated during nighttime hours to minimize the impact on daytime commuter traffic, those who work evening hours or return late will need to be particularly careful. The advice is to check your route before setting off, perhaps using real-time navigation apps that report closures. 💡 Practical tips: - If your usual route includes one of the mentioned streets, consider an alternative route for the indicated nights. - Leave a few minutes early to compensate for any slowdowns, especially in areas with alternating one-way traffic. - Always follow the instructions of the security agents on site. These disruptions, although temporary, are the price for more modern and safer infrastructures.
