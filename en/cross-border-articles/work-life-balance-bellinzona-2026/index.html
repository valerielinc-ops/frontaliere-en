Bellinzona: Work-life balance in the Rod The motion presented in Bellinzona to include the principle of work-life balance in the Organic Regulation of Employees (Rod) represents a significant step towards recognizing the well-being of employees as a strategic element for the quality of public services. This principle, already adopted by the Canton of Ticino, is considered a structural condition for people's well-being, the quality of work, and social cohesion. - Work-life balance is fundamental to ensure that employees can balance their work and personal responsibilities, improving their job satisfaction and reducing stress. ## Comparison with the previous situation Before this motion, work-life balance was not explicitly recognized as a guiding principle in the organizational regulations of the Municipality of Bellinzona. The inclusion of this principle in the Rod represents a significant change, aligning the city with the standards already adopted by the Canton of Ticino. - In 2019, the Canton of Ticino introduced the Law on Work-Life Balance (LConvi), which provides measures to support employees in managing their work time and personal responsibilities. ## Future scenarios If the motion is approved, Bellinzona could become a model for other municipalities in the Canton of Ticino, promoting an organizational culture that values work-life balance. This could lead to greater attractiveness of the Municipality as an employer and a reduction in gender inequalities, as women often suffer the most consequences of poor work-life balance in terms of career, income, and pension. - According to data from the Statistical Office of the Canton of Ticino (USTAT), in 2020, 44.6% of women in Ticino worked part-time, compared to 22.1% of men. - The Municipality of Locarno, for...

For cross-border workers employed in Bellinzona, the adoption of the work-life balance principle in the Rod could mean concrete improvements in working conditions. Here are some steps that employees could take to benefit from these new policies: ## Get informed and plan 1. Find out about the new regulations: Check with your employer about the new measures adopted for work-life balance, such as the recently introduced flexible work law in Ticino. 2. Use available facilities: Take advantage of the childcare and micro-childcare facilities and after-school centers created by the Canton of Ticino, such as the 'Nidi e spazi gioco' project in Bellinzona, which offers 30 childcare places and 20 play spaces. ## Concrete examples For example, a parent with a 4-year-old child working in Bellinzona could benefit from a monthly contribution of 600 CHF for childcare, thanks to the new family support regulations. At the same time, they could enjoy a flexible working schedule to accompany their child to school. ## Operational checklist Here is an operational checklist for employees: - Check with your employer about flexible work possibilities - Find out about family support structures available in your municipality - Participate in initiatives promoted by the Municipality to promote an organizational culture that values work-life balance ## Comparisons between practical scenarios Let's consider two scenarios: - without the new policies: a parent works 40 hours a week and has to pay 1,200 CHF per month for their child's childcare. - with the new policies: the same parent works 30 hours a week with flexibility and receives a monthly contribution of 600 CHF for childcare. ## References and resources For further information and to calculate the impact of these new policies on yo...

Frequently Asked Questions
What does the motion presented in Bellinzona entail?
The motion proposes to include the principle of work-life balance in articles 3 and 4 of the Municipal Ordinance on Employees (Rod) of the City of Bellinzona, following the example of the Canton of Ticino.
What are the active policies of the Canton of Ticino for work-life balance?
The Canton of Ticino has developed active policies for work-life balance, recognizing it as a factor of well-being, equality, and economic development. Among the actions planned for the 2025-28 period are the creation of childcare structures and extracurricular centers.
What are the implications for cross-border workers who work in Bellinzona?
The adoption of the principle of work-life balance in the Rod could lead to concrete improvements in working conditions for employees of the City of Bellinzona, promoting an organizational culture that values worker well-being and reduces gender inequalities.
