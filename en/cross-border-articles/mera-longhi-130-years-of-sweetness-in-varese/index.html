The Bosina family awards the historic Varese company Mera & Longhi for 130 years of confectionery excellence.

In brief - Mera & Longhi awarded for 130 years of activity in Varese - Award given by the Bosina Family - Company founded in 1896 by Pietro Mera ## Key facts - What: Award for 130 years of activity - When: May 3, 2026 - Where: Varese - Who: Bosina Family - Amount: Not specified On Sunday, May 3, 2026, as part of the celebrations for the patron saint Victor, the city of Varese once again honors its deepest roots. Among the most anticipated moments of the official ceremony is the recognition of Mera & Longhi, a historic company that for almost thirteen decades has represented the excellence of Bosina confectionery art. The award, given by the Bosina Family, recognizes not only the entrepreneurial value of a business capable of surviving two world wars and the changes of the global market, but also the indissoluble link with the social fabric of Varese. A story that began in via San Martino It all started in 1896, when Pietro Mera opened a small biscuit factory with a craft workshop in the back of via San Martino. It was here that, handcrafted according to tradition, the first candies were born that would conquer generations of Varese residents. In the 1920s, with the move to via Maspero and the entry of partner Longhi, the company experienced a period of great expansion, reaching over a hundred employees and diversifying production into confetti and licorice. Pioneers of figurines and 'Rabarbaro' Few know that Mera & Longhi was among the first companies in Italy to realize the potential of footballer figurines. Between 1928 and 1929, long before it became a national trend, the company included images of the 'Sports Aces' in their packages, creating collections that are now true treasures for collectors. Frequently Asked Questions
What is the history of Mera & Longhi?
Mera & Longhi is a historic company from Varese, founded in 1896 by Pietro Mera. Initially a small biscuit factory, the company experienced significant growth in the 1920s with the entry of partner Longhi and the diversification of production into confetti and licorice. It is known for introducing footballer stickers and creating the famous Rabarbaro Chinese Melsa.
What are the most famous products of Mera & Longhi?
Among the most famous products of Mera & Longhi are the sage-lemon candies, juniper candies, and the famous Rabarbaro Chinese Melsa. The company has also developed sugar-free lines and innovative products like the Rabarbarone liqueur.
Where is the headquarters of Mera & Longhi located?
The headquarters of Mera & Longhi is located in Casale Litta, where the company moved its production in 1984. The headquarters is easily accessible for cross-border workers living in the Varese area and working in Switzerland.
