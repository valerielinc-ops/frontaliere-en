Adam Walder wins Under 14 national title (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 8 May 2026

The young athlete from Circolo Scherma Locarno wins the Under 14 national title at the Swiss Championships in Morges.

Contesto

In brief - Adam Walder wins the Under 14 national title - Medals also for Eric Walder and Giacomo Ambrogi - Locarno athletes also compete in Italy ## Key facts - What: Under 14 national fencing title - When: May 7, 2026 - Where: Morges, Switzerland - Who: Adam Walder, Circolo Scherma Locarno - Amount: Gold for Adam Walder, bronze for Eric Walder The 2026 competitive season for Circolo Scherma Locarno concluded with a significant success for Adam Walder, who won the Under 14 national title at the Swiss Championships in Morges. The young athlete demonstrated great skill and determination, securing the gold medal in the Under 14 men's category. His brother Eric Walder also achieved an excellent third place in the same category, while Giacomo Ambrogi won the bronze medal in the Under 12 men's category. Isabel Dellamora conquered silver in the Under 12 women's category. Circolo Scherma Locarno also participated in international competitions, thanks to the collaboration with A.S.D. Scherma Verbania. Some Locarno athletes competed in various regions of Italy, winning medals during the preliminary phases of the Italian national circuit. The Italian season will conclude with the national final in Riccione, where Locarno athletes will participate after winning several medals. After the social tournament and the usual closing party, the club will observe the summer break to resume activities in September.
