This could lead to uncertainties and instability in the real estate market. With the neutralization of valuations, greater stability and predictability are sought, thus protecting the lifelong sacrifices of owners. ### Concrete Scenarios If the initiative is approved, cross-border workers could benefit from greater stability in the real estate market, with more predictable prices and greater availability of housing.
