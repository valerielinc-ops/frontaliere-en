Single-parent families and the middle class Among those who most often forgo dental visits are single-parent families, notes Sandra Killer, president of the association representing them. "This happens because if money is tight, people postpone treatment until it becomes urgent. This means the situation worsens and costs increase." The initiative would benefit the middle class and the so-called "working poor," argues Nicole Rossi, political secretary of the Swiss Trade Union in Ticino. Although workers – along with employers and the Canton – are also called upon to contribute, it won't result in a burden for them. "Currently, the costs of dental health are entirely borne by workers," declares Rossi. Therefore, according to Rossi, in most cases, there would be a reduction in costs. ### What changes for cross-border commuters Cross-border commuters residing in Italy and working in Ticino could benefit from this new reimbursement system. However, it's important to note that the system will be funded through a proportional income tax, both for employers and employees. This means that cross-border commuters will need to contribute to the system but could also benefit from reimbursements for basic dental care. ### Procedure to access reimbursement To access reimbursement for basic dental care, Ticino residents will need to follow a specific procedure. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can benefit from the reimbursement of basic dental care in Ticino?
Every resident of the Canton of Ticino, including cross-border workers who work in Ticino and live in Italy, will be able to benefit from the reimbursement of basic dental care. The system will be funded through a proportional tax rate for both employers and employees.
Which dental treatments are covered by the reimbursement?
Basic dental care includes all treatments except cosmetic ones. A specific catalog of services has not yet been defined, but it is hoped that industry associations can establish a catalog suitable for modern dentistry.
How can I request the reimbursement of dental care?
To request the reimbursement, you will need to submit an application to the competent office of the Canton. It is important to keep all invoices and documents related to the dental care received to present them as proof. The specific details of the procedure will be officially communicated by the Canton.
