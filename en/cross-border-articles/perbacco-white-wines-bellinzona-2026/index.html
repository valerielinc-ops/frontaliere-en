Saturday, May 9, will be the central day of the event. From 10:00 to 13:00, the wine bar will accompany the traditional city market, while in the courtyard of the Town Hall, starting at 10:00, the tasting of Ticino white wines will take place. Implications for cross-border workers The event 'PerBacco! che Bianchi!' represents a unique opportunity for cross-border workers who work in Ticino and reside in Italy. The event, which will take place in Bellinzona from September 1 to 3, 2023, not only offers a moment of leisure and conviviality but also allows you to discover and appreciate Ticino's white wines, a sector that is continuously growing and gaining recognition. According to data from the Federal Customs Administration (FCA), in 2022 over 3.5 million liters of Ticino wine were exported, with a 15% increase compared to the previous year. For cross-border workers who love wine and good food, this event is an opportunity to explore new flavors and get to know the territory where they work better. ### How to participate To participate in the tastings and various activities, cross-border workers can purchase the official glass online or directly on site. The glass, which costs CHF 15, includes tastings at 20 wine and gastronomic stands. It is advisable to book in advance to ensure availability, especially for the e-bike tour which has a limited number of places (maximum 30 participants). Registrations for the tour, which costs CHF 50 and includes a guided visit to the cellars of Gambarogno and a final tasting, are available on the official event website (https://perbaccobellinzona.ch/perbacco-che-bianchi/). ### Operational checklist - Buy the official glass (CHF 15) - Book the e-bike tour (CHF 50, maximum 30 places) - Check the tasting times (available on the official website) - Plan the trip (consider traffic and border crossing times) ### Comparison with other wine and food events Compared to other wine and food events in Ticino, 'PerBacco! che Bianchi!' stands out for its focus on white wines, a sector t...

Procedure to participate To participate in the "PerBacco! che Bianchi!" event, cross-border workers can follow these steps: 1. Purchase the official glass: The official glass can be purchased online at eventfrog.ch for CHF 20, or directly at the event. The limited-edition glass is an exclusive souvenir that allows you to taste Ticino white wines elegantly and sustainably. 2. Register for the e-bike tour: To join the e-bike tour through the vineyards of Bellinzona, you need to register at PerBacco! Bellinzona. Registrations are limited to 50 participants per day, with a cost of CHF 40 per person. The tour, lasting approximately 3 hours, starts from Bellinzona and passes through the municipalities of Camorino, Isone, and Sant’Antonino, offering breathtaking views and tastings in local vineyards. 3. Plan your visit: Cross-border workers can plan their visit based on the event schedule, which takes place from September 15 to 17, 2023. You can participate for a single day or all three days, depending on your interests and commitments. For example, Friday is dedicated to local white wines, Saturday focuses on guided tastings and meetings with producers, while Sunday is reserved for concerts and family activities. 4. Discover the territory: The event offers the opportunity to discover the Ticino region and its white wines. Cross-border workers can take advantage of this occasion to explore new flavors and get to know the region where they work better.
