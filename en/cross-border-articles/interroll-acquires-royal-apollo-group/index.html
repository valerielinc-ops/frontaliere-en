This move represents a significant step for Interroll, which aims to integrate Royal Apollo Group's spiral conveyor technology into its portfolio of solutions. 'With this acquisition, we integrate Royal Apollo Group's spiral conveyor technology into Interroll's portfolio of solutions, filling a gap in our offering and strengthening our lifecycle support services,' states CEO Markus Asch. The activities of both companies are complementary, and this acquisition will enable the provision of more comprehensive services to customers. Royal Apollo Group, founded in 1847, operates three plants with over 200 employees. Claudia van den Pol, chair of the board and owner of the company, expressed satisfaction with the operation, emphasizing that access to Interroll's global network represents a unique opportunity. 'The operation allows us to access Interroll's much more extensive global network, an opportunity we would not have had if we had remained independent,' van den Pol declared.
