Frequently Asked Questions
What are the main events of the 1st May weekend in Varese?
The main events include the San Vittore Festival in Varese, the hot air balloon show in Angera, the Dutch festival in Azzate, the Cardinal's Fair in Castiglione Olona, the Art Photography Day in Gemonio, the special opening of the Monastery of Torba in Gornate Olona, and the visit to the Hermitage of Leggiuno.
How can I participate in the events of the 1st May weekend in Varese?
To participate in the events, it is advisable to plan the trip in advance, check the event schedules, book any tickets, bring appropriate clothing, and book a lunch or a picnic.
Are there events suitable for children?
Yes, there are several events suitable for children, such as the Dutch festival in Azzate, the Cardinal's Fair in Castiglione Olona with the activity 'Firefighter for a Day', and the Art Photography Day in Gemonio with workshops and meetings.
