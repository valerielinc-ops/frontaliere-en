Di Valerie Linc · Published on 5 May 2026

As of June 1, 2026, the service book will become digital. Here's what changes for military personnel and civil protection.

This transition concerns both military personnel and civil protection. The information contained in the paper booklet will be transferred to the digital version, allowing military personnel to consult their career information, services performed, and remaining days online. However, the physical booklet must be kept until discharge from service obligation.
