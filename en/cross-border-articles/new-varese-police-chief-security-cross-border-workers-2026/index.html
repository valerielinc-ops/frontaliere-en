In brief - Paolo Iodice is the new police chief of Varese - Focus on fraud, theft, and robberies - Continuity of work done by predecessors ## Key facts - What: Appointment of the new police chief of Varese - When: May 4, 2026 - Where: Varese, Italy - Who: Paolo Iodice - Amount: Not specified The new police chief of Varese, Paolo Iodice, officially introduced himself this morning. During the meeting with the press, Iodice emphasized the importance of continuing the work done by his predecessors, with a particular focus on the safety of citizens. 'We will uphold the continuity of the service provided so far to the community of Varese,' declared Iodice, thanking those who entrusted him with this role. The new police chief expressed great enthusiasm and dedication for his new position, highlighting his knowledge of the territory and its challenges. Iodice has worked in the past in the Varese police headquarters as a deputy, developing an in-depth knowledge of the territory and the local reality. This experience will be valuable in addressing future challenges. 'I am fortunate to know this territory with all its challenges and characteristics,' Iodice stated, praising the respect for rules, hard work, and generosity of the people of Varese. ### Safety and crime The safety of citizens is one of the main priorities of the new police chief. Iodice mentioned several challenges of the territory, including predatory crimes such as fraud, pickpocketing, theft, and robberies. In particular, he highlighted fraud targeting the elderly and drug trafficking managed by transnational organizations, especially in the woods.
