Viganello and Gaza schools: a bridge of hope

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 29 April 2026

From April 27 to May 7, 2026, an exhibition in Viganello tells the story of the twinning between the local middle school and the Al-Salam tent school in Gaza, supported by Future in Peace.

Contesto

In brief - Twinning between Viganello school and Al-Salam tent school in Gaza - Exhibition from April 27 to May 7, 2026 - Exchange of letters, drawings, and stories between students ## Key facts - What: Twinning between schools in Viganello and Gaza - When: April 27 - May 7, 2026 - Where: Viganello Middle School - Who: Future in Peace association and Al-Salam school in Gaza - Amount: Not specified While divisions and distances grow in the world, in Viganello an experience is born that goes in the opposite direction: a real bridge between Ticino and Gaza. The initiative was presented by the association Future in Peace - Human Rights and International Justice, which organized a twinning between the Viganello Middle School and the Al-Salam tent school in Gaza. The exhibition, open to the public from April 27 to May 7 from 8 am to 5 pm, presents the results of this unique project. ### A project of hope and sharing The project was born from the collaboration between the association Future in Peace and Alessandro Fanciotti, Italian teacher in Viganello. The students of the two schools exchanged letters, drawings, and personal stories, creating a deep bond despite the differences in their realities. For the boys of Gaza, this twinning represented a moment of respite and normality in a life marked by privations and uncertainty. For the students of Viganello, the comparison meant discovering a different reality and developing greater awareness and gratitude. ### The importance of education The exhibition presents drawings, letters, and testimonies from the students, offering the public a direct and authentic look at this encounter between two worlds. Dettagli operativi

Practical Implications of the Twinning The twinning between the school in Viganello and Al-Salam School in Gaza has significant practical implications for both Ticino students and those in Gaza. For the students in Viganello, this project offers the opportunity to develop a greater awareness of realities different from their own. Confronting such a harsh situation helps them to look at their own lives with new eyes, discovering what it means to live without certainties and stability. ### Comparisons and Scenarios For the students in Gaza, the twinning represents a moment of respite and normality. Talking with peers from the other side of the world gives them a feeling often denied: that of being seen, heard, and considered. In a context where everything seems to be collapsing, knowing that someone is thinking of them reignites a spark of joy and hope. ### Educational Impact This project has a profound educational impact. Students in Viganello discover the importance of education and solidarity, while those in Gaza find a reason to keep dreaming and believing in a better future. The comparison between the two realities helps to build bridges of understanding and mutual respect, contributing to the formation of more attentive and aware citizens. ### Supporting Education in Gaza Al-Salam School, created and supported by Future in Peace, is a concrete example of how education can make a difference. Providing a safe and welcoming space for learning is fundamental for the youth of Gaza, who live in a situation of great uncertainty. Supporting projects like this means contributing to building a better future for the new generations. ## Recommended tools For an updated estimate, use the net salary calculator and the CHF-EUR exchange comparator.
