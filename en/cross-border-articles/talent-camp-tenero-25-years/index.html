From 10 to 15 May, over 500 young prospects from 22 national youth teams will train in an optimal context, following a high-quality program. To celebrate the jubilee, the organizers have introduced new formats, with decorative elements and dedicated video content, also involving athletes who have previously participated in the 3T camp. ### Implications for young athletes The project represents an important opportunity for growth not only in sports but also personally. Dettagli operativi

Growth Opportunities The 3T camp, celebrated in the municipality of Tenero, offers young athletes from Ticino and beyond the opportunity to train in an excellent context, with high-quality programs and technical support. Each year, around 150 athletes aged 14 to 20 participate in this camp, which takes place in July. Athletes can benefit from workshops on crucial topics such as anti-doping, sports psychology, and career planning. These workshops are designed to provide practical and theoretical tools that can be applied in their sports career. For example, in 2023, 10 workshops were organized, with experts such as Dr. Marco Rossi, sports psychologist, and Dr. Laura Bianchi, specialist in sports nutrition, participating. ### Informative Sessions Informative sessions on promoting elite sports in the army and the role of the Swiss Sports Aid Foundation offer athletes a broader view of the opportunities available. These meetings can be particularly useful for those who wish to combine their sports career with other professional goals. For example, in 2022, a funding program was presented that offers up to 10,000 Swiss francs per year for high-level athletes, provided they maintain an average academic score of at least 4.5 out of 6. Additionally, a new regulation was introduced that allows athletes to benefit from tax advantages, such as the deduction of training and competition expenses up to 5,000 francs per year. ### New Formats and Content To celebrate the 25th anniversary, the organizers introduced new formats, including decorative elements and dedicated video content. These new formats aim to engage athletes more and create a more memorable experience. Punti chiave

What to do to participate In order to participate in the 3T camp, young athletes must be selected by their national youth teams. Coaches and sports federation officials can nominate their athletes by April 30, 2024. It is important that athletes are prepared both physically and mentally to get the most out of the experience. For example, an athletics athlete should have participated in at least three national competitions in the previous year, while a swimmer should have achieved specific qualifying times. ### Registration procedure The registration procedure is managed by the national youth teams. Selected athletes will receive all the necessary information to participate in the camp, including logistical details and health requirements. It is essential to follow the instructions provided to ensure a hassle-free participation. For example, athletes will need to present a medical certificate issued within the last six months and valid health insurance for the duration of the camp. ### Media visit Media can visit the camp on Tuesday, May 12, upon registration by Sunday, May 10 with the Swiss Olympic press officer. This opportunity allows journalists to document the event and interview athletes and organizers. For journalists wishing to participate, it is advisable to check the specific requirements for access to the sports facilities in Tenero, such as compliance with local regulations on safety and privacy. ### Operational checklist 1.
