The fire, which occurred on the night of April 29, 2026, caused severe damage to the service infrastructure and the facades of adjacent buildings. The investigations, conducted by the Anti-Crime Office of the Gallarate Commissariat, were based on municipal and private surveillance footage. The man was intercepted shortly after the fire with clothing and objects compatible with those captured by the cameras. The alarm was raised just after 3:30 AM through the single emergency number 112. The Fire Department and the State Police intervened at the scene, finding the flames already spread from the garbage bags exposed for collection to the nearby buildings.
