Andrea Arcidiacono, born in 1966 and raised in Bellinzona, is the new press officer for the Diocese of Lugano. The appointment was officialized by Apostolic Administrator Alain de Raemy. Arcidiacono, formerly a correspondent from Bern for CdT and Rsi, and later vice-chancellor and spokesperson for the Federal Council, succeeds Luca Montagner, who held the position from 2018 to 2025. The public competition organized by the Curia attracted around eighty candidates. Arcidiacono boasts extensive experience in institutional communication, public relations, and journalism. He held various roles in Bern, including spokesperson for Federal Councillors Ruth Dreifuss and Pascal Couchepin. Between 2024 and 2025, he served as vice-chancellor and spokesperson for the Federal Council, before resuming his role as head of his own public relations agency. To take on his main role as press officer for the Diocese, Arcidiacono will move from Bern to Lugano. ### Duties and responsibilities In his role as press officer, Arcidiacono will be responsible for media relations, managing the website and social media, creating content, and developing communication strategies in both ordinary and crisis situations.
