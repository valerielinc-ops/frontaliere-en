Former municipal house of Lopagno for sale: minimum price and deadlines (cross-border guide)

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 7 May 2026

The Municipality of Capriasca is selling the former municipal house of Lopagno for a minimum price of 315,000 francs. Bidding deadline: July 31.

Contesto

In brief - Former municipal building of Lopagno for sale - Minimum price: 315,000 francs - Deadline for offers: July 31 ## Key facts - What: Sale of former municipal building - When: Offers by July 31 - Where: Lopagno, Canton Ticino - Who: Municipality of Capriasca - Amount: Minimum price 315,000 francs The Municipality of Capriasca has decided to put up for sale, through a public tender, the former municipal building of Lopagno. The minimum price set is 315,000 francs. The sale price includes the building permit from last October 17 concerning the renovation of the building and its transformation from a public building to a single-family home. The permit will be transferred from the Executive to the buyer. All expenses related to the real estate transaction are entirely the responsibility of the buyer. ### How to participate Offers must be submitted by registered mail in a sealed envelope by 12 PM on Friday, July 31. The envelope must be marked 'Tender for the sale of the former municipal building of Lopagno – DO NOT OPEN' and sent to the address Municipality of Capriasca, Piazza Motta 1, 6950 Tesserete. The date of delivery of the registered mail is binding. An appraisal, as well as the building permit and the respective documentation (plans and attachments), are available upon request at the email address [email protected].
