Frequently Asked Questions
Can I buy an HC Lugano or HC Ambrì-Piotta season ticket as an Italian resident?
Yes. Both clubs sell season tickets to Italian residents without restrictions. Select 'abroad' as your residence at checkout. European credit cards are accepted. Season ticket campaigns typically open in May or June for the following season.
What time do games end and can I catch a train back to Italy?
Games generally start at 19:45 and finish by 22:30. From Lugano, trains run towards Como and Varese past midnight. Check sbb.ch for timetables as late evening frequency varies between weekdays and weekends.
HC Lugano or HC Ambrì-Piotta — which club matters more for workplace integration in Ticino?
It depends on where you work. In Lugano and the Lugano district, support is split between the two clubs but HC Lugano has the broader fan base. In the Sopraceneri region and around Ambrì, local support is near-universal. Following both clubs at least superficially is the most inclusive approach.
Is it possible to watch Swiss National League games online from Italy?
Yes. MySports broadcasts the National League and is accessible via streaming from abroad with a direct subscription. RSI (Swiss Italian television, available free via streaming) covers the later playoff rounds. Club apps also provide live audio commentary and highlight packages.
