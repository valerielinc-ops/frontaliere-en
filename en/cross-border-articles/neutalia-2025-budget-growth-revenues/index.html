In brief - Neutalia closes 2025 with growing revenues - EBITDA +17% and profit at 3.17 million euros - Investments of 12.2 million euros in plant revamping ## Key facts - What: 2025 annual report of Neutalia - When: 2025 - Where: Busto Arsizio, Italy - Who: Neutalia S.p.A. - Amount: Revenue 22.5 million euros, EBITDA 7.09 million (+17%), net profit 3.17 million - Investments: 12.2 million euros - Waste processed: 100,000 tonnes Neutalia, the benefit company managing the waste-to-energy plant in Busto Arsizio, closed 2025 with results exceeding expectations. The approved balance sheet highlights a phase of growth and consolidation of its industrial development. The economic data shows a total revenue of 22.5 million euros, in line with forecasts, and an EBITDA of 7.09 million, up 17% compared to the previous year. Net profit stands at 3.17 million euros, marking an increase over expectations and strengthening the company's economic solidity. Throughout 2025, modernization interventions of the plant continued as planned in the Industrial Plan, with total investments amounting to 12.2 million euros. The activities concerned in particular the production lines and the start of the works of the first phase of the plan, marking a concrete advancement in the renewal process. During the year, Neutalia processed approximately 100,000 tonnes of waste, confirming its strategic role in the integrated waste management system of the territory and consolidating the function of the plant as a central infrastructure for the area.
