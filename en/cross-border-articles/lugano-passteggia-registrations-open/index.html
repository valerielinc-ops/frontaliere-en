Registrations for the thirteenth edition of Lugano Passteggia are open. Find out how to participate and what to expect.

Contesto

In brief - Registrations open for Lugano Passteggia 2026 - 10 km enogastronomic walk - Participation limited to 450 people ## Key facts - What: Lugano Passteggia 2026 - When: 30 August 2026 - Where: Lugano, Sorengo, Collina d’Oro, Paradiso - Who: Local organizers - Cost: 50 CHF adults, 25 CHF children 6-13 years, free under 6 years Lugano Passteggia, the most anticipated late summer enogastronomic and walking event in Ticino, has officially opened registrations for its thirteenth edition. The event, scheduled for Sunday, 30 August 2026, promises to combine nature, movement, and local flavors along a route of over 10 kilometers that passes through the neighborhoods and landscapes of Lugano, Sorengo, Collina d’Oro, and Paradiso. Along the way, participants can enjoy local specialties accompanied by musical and convivial moments. Departures are scheduled in staggered groups between 08.30 and 10.30 from the LAC in Lugano. To ensure quality and safety, the number of participants is limited to 450 people. The registration fee remains unchanged from previous editions: 50 francs for adults, 25 francs for children aged 6 to 13, while children under 5 participate for free. The price also includes a ticket for public transport from your home to the starting point and return. Registrations are available exclusively online on the official website www.luganopassteggia.ch. Organizers recommend booking in advance, given the high demand recorded in past editions.
