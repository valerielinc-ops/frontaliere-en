Contesto

In brief - 500'000 francs for projects in Lugano - 27 projects funded in 2025 - 5.5 million francs in investments generated ## Key facts - What: Regional promotion fund for projects in Lugano - When: 2026 - Where: Lugano, Canton Ticino - Who: Ente regionale per lo sviluppo del luganese (Ersl) - Amount: 500'000 francs - Leverage effect: 8 francs of investment for every franc allocated The Ente regionale per lo sviluppo del luganese (Ersl) has made half a million francs available to support small and medium-sized projects in the Lugano area. The aim is to stimulate regional economic development, supporting entrepreneurial initiatives, projects to enhance local potential, and measures to maintain or strengthen basic services in the more peripheral areas. In 2025, the Ersl supported 27 projects for a total of 655'291 francs in contributions, generating total investments of around 5.5 million francs. This has made it possible to activate a significant leverage effect, capable of activating eight francs of investment for every franc allocated. ### How to participate To learn more about how the fund works, the Ersl is organizing a free webinar on May 20 at 6 PM. The event is free but registration is required on the Ersl website www.ersl.ch. The fund is aimed at individuals and legal entities, associations, foundations, and public bodies active in the Lugano area. Contributions can be granted as non-repayable grants or as interest-free loans, up to a maximum of 50% of the planned investment and with a limit of 60'000 francs per project. The fund particularly supports costs related to the start-up or development of an initiative, such as the purchase of machinery, equipment, or furniture. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can apply for the Regional Promotion Fund's contributions?
Individuals, legal entities, associations, foundations, and public bodies active in Lugano can apply for these contributions. Cross-border workers who live in Italy and work in Switzerland can also benefit from these opportunities to start or develop business activities in Lugano.
What are the criteria for evaluating funding applications?
Evaluation criteria include economic, environmental, and social sustainability, the solidity and feasibility of the initiative, the degree of innovation, the medium-to-long-term impact, and the ability to generate synergies and collaborations in the region.
What is the maximum contribution limit per project?
The maximum contribution per project is 60,000 francs. Contributions can be granted as non-repayable grants or as interest-free loans, up to a maximum of 50% of the planned investment.
