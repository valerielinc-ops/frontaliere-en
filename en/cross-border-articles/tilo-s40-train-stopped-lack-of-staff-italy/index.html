S40 line disrupted until 21:00 due to staff shortage in Italy

Di Valerie Linc · Published on 25 April 2026

The S40 line between Mendrisio and Como San Giovanni and Varese is limited until 21:00 due to a lack of staff in Italy.

The Swiss Federal Railways (SBB) have communicated on their portal that rail traffic is limited due to an 'event in Italy'. This limitation is expected to last at least until 9 PM tonight. The main reason for this interruption has been clarified by the former federal administration to Ticinonline: 'there is a lack of personnel in Italy'. ### Implications for cross-border commuters This situation creates considerable disruptions for cross-border commuters who use the S40 line daily to travel between the Canton of Ticino and neighboring Italian cities.
