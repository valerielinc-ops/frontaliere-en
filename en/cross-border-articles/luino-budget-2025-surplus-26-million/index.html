The overall administration result exceeds 12 million, growing compared to the previous year. These figures indicate an overall balanced management, although more than half of the surplus is set aside and an additional portion is earmarked by law or for specific purposes. ### Main expenses The most significant item on the expenditure side remains social spending, with around 5 million euros allocated to interventions for families, minors, and vulnerabilities. Alongside social spending, a significant portion of resources has been directed towards the protection of the territory and the environment, including maintenance, ordinary management, and works against hydrogeological instability. Investments have also been made in the education sector, including educational services and interventions on school buildings. ### Financial situation From a financial point of view, the Municipality has not resorted to new borrowing and closes with a cash availability exceeding 12 million.
