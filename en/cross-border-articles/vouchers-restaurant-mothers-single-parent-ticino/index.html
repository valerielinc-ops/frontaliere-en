Winter Aid Ticino and GastroTicino gift restaurant vouchers to 74 people, including 50 children, for Mother's Day 2026

Contesto

In brief - 74 people, including 50 children, will receive restaurant vouchers - Vouchers worth between 100 and 300 francs - Initiative for Mother's Day 2026 ## Key facts - What: Restaurant vouchers - When: Sunday, May 10, 2026 - Where: GastroTicino-affiliated restaurants in Ticino - Who: Winter Aid Ticino and GastroTicino - Amount: Between 100 and 300 francs - Who: Struggling single-parent families Winter Aid Ticino, a non-profit organization aimed at helping people affected by poverty, renews its support for struggling single-parent households with vouchers offered for amounts ranging from 100 to 300 francs. For the fourth consecutive year, on the occasion of Mother's Day, which falls on May 10, 2026, Winter Aid Ticino, in collaboration with GastroTicino, is repeating the solidarity initiative to support struggling single-parent families. Over 70 people, including 50 children, will be able to enjoy a moment of normality and sharing within the restaurants affiliated with the association chaired by Massimo Suter, GastroTicino, to have a completely free dinner together. The initiative is particularly aimed at single mothers, often faced with complex personal situations, such as difficult separations and lack of financial support. These account for about 80% of single-parent families, which currently represent around 20% of families with children under 25 years of age. These are the families most exposed to poverty cases, as nearly one-third were in this condition in 2020 and about half were unable to cope with an unplanned expense. ### Implications for cross-border workers This initiative represents important support for single-parent families residing in Ticino, including cross-border workers who work in Switzerland and live in Italy. Dettagli operativi

Practical Analysis The initiative by Soccorso d’inverno Ticino and GastroTicino represents significant support for economically struggling single-parent families. In Ticino, single-parent families make up about 20% of families with children under 25 years of age, and 80% of these families are single mothers. These families are particularly vulnerable to poverty, with nearly one-third of single-parent families in 2020 facing this condition. Additionally, about half of these families were unable to cope with an unplanned expense. ### Comparison with the Previous Situation In previous years, the initiative has allowed numerous families to experience a moment of normality and sharing during Mother's Day. This type of support is particularly important for single mothers, who often face complex personal situations, such as difficult separations and lack of financial support. The initiative not only provides economic aid but also a moment of leisure and sharing, which can make a difference in the lives of these families. ### Concrete Scenarios Imagine a single mother who works as a cross-border commuter in Ticino and lives in Italy. This mother might struggle to manage daily expenses and ensure a peaceful life for her children. Thanks to the vouchers offered by Soccorso d’inverno Ticino and GastroTicino, this mother could finally afford a dinner at a restaurant with her children, experiencing a moment of normality and sharing. This type of initiative can have a significant positive impact on the quality of life of these families, offering a moment of leisure and relaxation in a difficult economic context. ### Implications for Cross-Border Workers For cross-border workers who work in Ticino and live in Italy, this initiative represents an important opportunity to benefit...

Concrete actions To participate in the initiative, interested single-parent families must contact Soccorso d’inverno Ticino for more information on how to obtain the restaurant vouchers. It is important to verify the participation requirements and ensure that the necessary documentation is provided to access the vouchers. Families must be prepared to provide information about their economic and family situation to demonstrate the need for support. ### Step-by-step procedure 1. Contact Soccorso d’inverno Ticino: Interested single-parent families must contact Soccorso d’inverno Ticino to request information about the restaurant vouchers. The organization can be contacted through their website or by phone. 2. Verify participation requirements: Soccorso d’inverno Ticino will provide detailed information about the participation requirements and the necessary documentation to access the vouchers. Families must be prepared to provide information about their economic and family situation. 3. Submit the necessary documentation: Once the participation requirements have been verified, families must submit the necessary documentation to access the vouchers. This documentation may include documents that demonstrate the family's economic and family situation. 4. Receive the restaurant vouchers: After submitting the necessary documentation, families will receive the restaurant vouchers. These vouchers can be used in restaurants affiliated with GastroTicino to enjoy a free dinner together. ### Useful tools For more information and to calculate their income and expenses, families can use the income calculator. Frequently Asked Questions
Who can participate in the initiative?
The initiative is aimed at single-parent families in economic difficulty. Single mothers, often faced with complex personal situations such as difficult separations and lack of financial support, can benefit from this initiative. Interested families should contact Soccorso d’inverno Ticino for more information on participation requirements.
How can I obtain the restaurant vouchers?
To obtain the restaurant vouchers, interested single-parent families must contact Soccorso d’inverno Ticino. It is necessary to verify the participation requirements and submit the necessary documentation to access the vouchers. Families must be prepared to provide information about their economic and family situation.
What is the value of the restaurant vouchers?
The restaurant vouchers have a value between 100 and 300 francs. These vouchers can be used in restaurants affiliated with GastroTicino to enjoy a free dinner in good company.
