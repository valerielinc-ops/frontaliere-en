Role overview

As we continue to grow and innovate, we are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Group Financial Controller to join our dynamic team. This pivotal role will drive transformation across our financial control framework and lead strategic initiatives to optimize our Group’s financial operations.

  • Lead the ICS and control framework process for the Group
  • Lead the procedures and accounting policy transformation
  • Manage the Balance Sheet optimization of the Group as well as Capital and dividend flows globally from subsidiaries
  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance or similar
  • Banking experience will be considered as a plus
  • Qualified Charter accountant with experience outside of Audit practice

Main responsibilities

  • Lead the ICS and control framework process for the Group
  • Lead the procedures and accounting policy transformation
  • Manage the Balance Sheet optimization of the Group as well as Capital and dividend flows globally from subsidiaries
  • Own the chart of accounts evolution process, lead the discussions around Consolidation tool and general ledger / ERP.
  • Finance process an systems Optimisation
  • Coordinate with the Local CFOs and manage the Global CFO

Key requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance or similar
  • Banking experience will be considered as a plus
  • Qualified Charter accountant with experience outside of Audit practice
  • Minimum of 8 to 10 years of experience ina similar role
  • Proven track record of managing complex projects and delivering results.
  • Fluent in English both written and spoken, French, German and/or Italian are a plus;
  • Knowledge of regulatory requirements and Company Law.
  • Excellent project management skills, including planning, execution and monitoring.
  • Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills.
