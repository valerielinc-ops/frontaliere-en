Role overview

If you are looking for a dynamic environment where your care expertise will be used for the well-being of patients, join our team in the Central Long Term Pool (PCLD)!

As a member of the PCLD, you will be required to intervene throughout the Canton of Geneva for long-term replacements, ranging from 6 to 15 months, within our antennas.

Your main mission will be to ensure continuity in the follow-up of patients, acting as focal points in the management of their records and providing quality services.
