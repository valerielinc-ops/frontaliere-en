Main responsibilities

  • Cartier International Risk & Compliance Department reports to the Maison CFO and mainly covers the following scope of responsibilities: Regulatory compliance, related to the adherence to external Laws and Regulations relevant to the activities of the Maison.
  • Business compliance, encompasses Cartier internal rules, procedures, control framework and business processes.
  • Risk management, involves identifying the Maison's strategic risks through an Enterprise Risk Management approach and defining appropriate mitigation plans.
  • In a context of increasing volume, complexity and pace of regulatory change across Regions and Functions, and the deployment of Maison sustainability initiatives, we are seeking a dynamic and experienced Compliance Risk Manager.
  • This role will involve two key missions: Defining and leading the Maison’s Compliance Risk framework, contributing to the Group-wide Regulatory, Risk & Compliance Monitoring strategic initiative;
  • Supporting the Cartier Sustainability team as the Maison Finance Sustainability Lead.
  • Compliance Risk Management (80%) Creating a comprehensive Compliance Risk Management Governance structure for the Maison, engaging key stakeholders from all Functions and Regions.
  • Coordinating this Risk Network by establishing a communication, consultation and engagement strategy.
  • Delivering training to Compliance Risk Stakeholders to ensure a successful IRM tool adoption.
  • Conducting the assessment of the Maison's Compliance and Regulatory risks, in collaboration with subject matter experts.
