Application process

  • The CHAD brings together the teams from the application line (LAD), IMAD link to the HUGs, IMAD arcade to the HUGs, the Mental Health and Psychiatry Team (ESMP), Multi-purpose Home Hospitalization (HAD-poly), Interprofessional Oncology Team (EISO), Paediatric Care and Family Respite, Home Palliative Care Consultation (CospaDom), the Geriatric Coordination Team (COGERIA), the Night EMD, as well as POOL CHAD and the long-term centralized pool.
  • Connected to the team leader (RO) and integrated with the EMP, the nurse coordinator IC organises the support and care activities of an EISO team, ensures harmonised management of customer flows and acts as a contact person for case reference, on aspects of quality and adequacy of services.
  • It promotes customer orientation in all actions undertaken through a multidisciplinary and interprofessional approach.
  • Process new applications at the clinical level, assign references under the control of the ROE, and plan care and assessments in advance, taking into account available resources

Company and context
