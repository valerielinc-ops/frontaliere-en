Role overview

The nurse intervenes at home with clients, with the whole population, all age groups, requiring nursing care.

The specificity of home work is linked to the unpredictability of the context, the nature and variety of situations encountered, both socio-economic, environmental and psycho-physical.

The services are performed independently, respecting the customer's lifestyle, beliefs, values, culture, autonomy and integrity.
