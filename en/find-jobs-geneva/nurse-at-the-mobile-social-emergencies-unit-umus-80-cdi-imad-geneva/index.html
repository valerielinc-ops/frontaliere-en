Role overview

The Mobile Social Emergencies Unit (UMUS), composed of nurses and social workers, intervenes in situations mainly related to violence and insecurity in coordination with the Geneva social health network.

The team works in pairs and operates from 17:00 to 08:00 on weekdays and 24 hours on weekends and public holidays.
