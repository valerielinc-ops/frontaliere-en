Company and context

  • CONTEXT We are looking for a Finance Analytics Engineer to translate business needs into data requirements, validating outputs and enabling our FP&A organization to work effectively in the new data environment.
  • This is not a pure engineering role.
  • The ideal candidate combines analytical engineering capability, comfortable with SQL, BigQuery and Looker, with strong Finance domain fluency and the communication skills to bridge the technical and business worlds.
  • You will be embedded in a small, high-impact team and will work closely with the Group Data Office, Finance Process & Data, and FP&A leadership.
  • HOW WILL YOU MAKE AN IMPACT?
  • Own the FP&A requirements for the GCP/Looker migration, defining what data FP&A needs, in what form, and to what quality standard Act as the primary liaison between Group Business Analytics and the Finance Data domain lead, ensuring business priorities are represented and delivered Build, validate and maintain Looker dashboards and data models that serve the reporting and insight needs of the team Drive adoption of the new data platform within the Central Group Finance organization, providing training, documentation and ongoing support to non- technical stakeholders
  • Identify and close data gaps: working with upstream teams to ensure the right data assets exist, are accurate and are accessible Enable the Business Analytics team to operate effectively in the new stack, upskilling colleagues and embedding good data practices Contribute to the design and continuous improvement of the team's reporting and insight infrastructure HOW WILL YOU EXPERIENCE SUCCESS WITH US?
  • Education, Experience and Core Competencies Master’s degree in Data Analytics, Data Science, Engineering or a related field 5+ years of experience in an analytics, data or BI role, ideally within a Finance context Hands-on proficiency in SQL and BigQuery
  • experience with Looker (LookML) is strongly preferred Proven experience translating business requirements into data models, dashboards or analytical solutions Solid understanding of Finance processes, reporting cycles and KPIs
  • you can speak the language of FP&A Experience working alongside a data migration or platform transformation program Fluency in English is a must;
