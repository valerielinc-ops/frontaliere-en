Role overview

As part of the temporary replacement of one of the unit's senior doctors, you have a clinical and academic activity within the paediatric pneumonology team.

You ensure, autonomously and under the supervision of the medical hierarchy, the management of the hospitalized patientele and in outpatient paediatric pneumonology (a wide variety of pathologies: asthma, obstructive respiratory pathologies of the little child, acute or chronic respiratory infectious pathologies, fibrosis, ciliary dyskinesia, respiratory pathology related to prematurity, oncological follow-up, non-invasive ventilation, pulmonary interstitial pathologies, respiratory control disorders).

You participate and collaborate closely with other paediatric specialties and partner services (close collaboration with, inter alia, the department of general pediatrics, d-ORL, immunology, d-allergology, paediatric resuscitation and neonatology, surgery, paediatric oncology, general paediatrics).
