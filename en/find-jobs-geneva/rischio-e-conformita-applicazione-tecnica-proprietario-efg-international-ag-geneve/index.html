Role overview

Within Risk & Compliance IT, you are responsible for key applications (primarily from sas.com) and play a hands-on role on the technical solutions: support of the application, data models, reports, data flows and functional enhancements.

The role is evenly split between hands‑on technical activities (around 50%) and business analysis / project responsibilities (around 50%).

You combine a solid understanding of Risk & Compliance topics with strong technical skills (SQL / SAS / data modelling), while working closely with business stakeholders and external providers.

  • Act as IT Application Owner for Risk & Compliance solutions.
  • Analyse business needs and translate them into concrete technical solutions.
  • Design, adapt and optimise data models, extractions and reports (SQL, SAS).
  • <strong>Accountability</strong>: Taking ownership for tasks and challenges as well as seeking continuous improvement.
  • <strong>Hands-on</strong>: Being proactive to rapidly deliver high-quality results.

Main responsibilities

  • Act as IT Application Owner for Risk & Compliance solutions.

Profile

  • University degree in IT, Computer Science or a related field.

  • Strong hands‑on skills in SQL and relational databases (writing and optimising queries, designing data models).
  • Practical experience with SAS for reporting and/or data processing; knowledge of SAS Asset & Liability Risk Management is a strong plus.
  • Comfortable working with Windows Server and Linux environments.
