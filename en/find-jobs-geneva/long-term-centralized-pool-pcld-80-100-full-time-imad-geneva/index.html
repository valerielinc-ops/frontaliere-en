Role overview

The Central Long-Term Pool (CPLD) is composed of nurses, community health and care assistants (CHS) and home helpers.

They intervene throughout the canton of Geneva to carry out replacements, about ten to twelve months, in our branches.

The aim of this pool is to ensure continuity and the highest quality of services for our customers.
