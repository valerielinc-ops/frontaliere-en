## What are we looking for?
We are seeking a talented and motivated university student to join our finance team as a Finance Data Analytics & Power BI Intern for a 7-month contract. The successful candidate will play a critical role in leveraging Power BI to improve reporting, controls, and operational efficiency within the finance department. Responsibilities will be centred on utilizing Power BI to enhance the accuracy, transparency, and efficiency of financial data reconciliation, with a particular focus on complex revenue streams such as forex, derivatives, and structured products.

Key Responsibilities & Achievements

  • Assist in the reconciliation of net other income revenue, using Power BI to cross-validate accounting data from multiple sources (statutory and consolidation levels), ensuring data integrity and consistency.
  • Support the automation of reconciliation processes, reducing manual effort and minimizing errors in financial reporting.
  • Collaborate with technical Power BI specialists to design, develop, and maintain interactive dashboards and reports tailored for financial data reconciliation and control.
  • Enhance real-time visibility for finance teams, enabling faster identification and resolution of reconciliation discrepancies.
  • Conduct in-depth analysis of existing reconciliation workflows and lead discussions with key stakeholders to define and implement a target operating model, integrating Power BI as a core tool for reconciliation and reporting.
