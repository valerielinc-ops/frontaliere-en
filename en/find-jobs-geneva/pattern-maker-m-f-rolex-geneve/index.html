  • Introduction For our Design department, we are looking for a Model Maker (M/F) whose main mission will be to actively participate in the creation of the constituent elements of the watch's clothing, by combining artisanal know-how and modern technological tools, as part of the development of new models and the search for new aesthetics.

Main responsibilities

  • Create and model elements of the watch's clothing in line with the stylistic orientations defined by the design office
  • Actively participate in research and development projects aimed at exploring new aesthetics for future collections
  • Collaborate closely with the Design teams to ensure the aesthetic definition of the pieces designed
  • Use CAD/CAD tools in addition to traditional manual techniques
  • Contribute to the continuous improvement of processes, remaining attentive to technological developments in the sector Profile
  • CFC of Jeweler – Jeweler or equivalent training recognized in the field
  • Several years of experience in a jewelry workshop, with significant exposure to the world of watchmaking
  • Skills in setting for simple components desired
  • Good knowledge of materials, machining techniques and constraints specific to watches
  • Mastery of jewelry techniques and manufacturing processes applied to watchmaking clothing
