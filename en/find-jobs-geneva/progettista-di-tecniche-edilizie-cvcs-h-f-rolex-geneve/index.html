Role overview

For our Purchasing, Support & Infrastructure division, we are looking for a Building Techniques Designer CVCS whose main mission will be to develop plans and diagrams, size and coordinate heating, ventilation, air conditioning and sanitation installations.

  • For our Purchasing, Support & Infrastructure division, we are looking for a Building Techniques Designer CVCS whose main mission will be to develop plans and diagrams, size and coordinate heating, ventilation, air conditioning and sanitation installations.
  • Develop the technical projects entrusted to him in collaboration with the project manager
  • Participate in coordination meetings and synthesize information
  • Hold a CFC as a building techniques designer Heating / Ventilation or equivalent
  • Solid experience in various CVCS techniques in the industrial sector

Main responsibilities

  • Develop the technical projects entrusted to him in collaboration with the project manager
  • Participate in coordination meetings and synthesize information
  • Size hydraulic heating, ventilation, sanitation networks
  • Select materials according to technical guidelines
  • Coordinate installations on plan jointly with the electrical counterpart Take measurements on site in operation
  • Create plans, sections and model CVCS installations using Autocad / Revit
  • Integrate the BIM methodology implemented in the company Establish principle diagrams Control, verify and update revision plans

Key requirements

  • Hold a CFC as a building techniques designer Heating / Ventilation or equivalent
  • Solid experience in various CVCS techniques in the industrial sector
  • Good knowledge of current standards according to SIA Good mastery of BIM methodology / tool Rigor, attention to detail and precision Mastery of Autocad and Revit tools Mastery of main office software
