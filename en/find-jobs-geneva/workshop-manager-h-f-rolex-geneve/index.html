Role overview

Introduction For our Production division, we are looking for a Workshop Manager (M/F) whose main mission will be to manage the organization of resources in an assembly workshop by implementing actions to guarantee compliance with production objectives.

Responsibilities

  • Provide information and analyze production indicators, as well as monitoring the short-term objectives of the workshop
  • Adapt the organization of the workshop on a daily basis according to production hazards
  • Guarantee the link and cross-functional exchanges with the production support services
  • Participate in coordination sessions or lead them
  • Manage the organization of inventories
  • Carry out ad hoc and operational analyzes to ensure the proper functioning of the service.

Profile

  • Post-compulsory secondary training, CFC or equivalent, in production management and/or logistics
  • Significant industrial experience in a production workshop management function
  • Mastery of IT tools (MS-Office, SAP and production monitoring) and knowledge of an ERP
  • Strong analytical and synthesis skills
  • Rigorous and methodical, with good team spirit
  • Available and with very good listening and communication skills.

Conclusion We offer you a stimulating environment, excellent working conditions, flexible working time solutions and first-rate social benefits.
