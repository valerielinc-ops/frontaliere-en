Main responsibilities

  • Identify and assess safety risks in the industrial control systems environment (ICS/OT, IoT, IT)
  • Develop and implement safety policies and procedures for industrial control systems
  • Designing OT system architectures using "default security" and "zero trust" principles
  • Modernize current architectures to integrate cloud and artificial intelligence systems
  • Install and configure appropriate security measures to protect industrial control systems
  • Ensure secure integration of equipment into IT infrastructure
  • Conduct technical audits and intrusion tests on ICS/OT systems and their IT environment
  • To be the preferred point of contact for cybersecurity advice and topics in the fields of: Technical building management (GTC), industrial control systems (ICS), automatic storage systems, Research & Development (R&D) equipment
  • To carry out a technological watch on advances in security ICS/OT, IoT, IT and good practices
  • To participate in the monitoring of industrial control systems to detect suspicious activities and respond to security incidents Profile
