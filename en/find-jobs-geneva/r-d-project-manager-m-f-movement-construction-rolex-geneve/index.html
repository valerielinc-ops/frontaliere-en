Role overview

For our Research & Development division, we are looking for an R&D Engineer in watchmaking design (M/F) with varied experience in designing high-precision watch movements that integrate complex mechanical systems and advanced technologies.

Reporting to the 'Movement Construction' department, you will participate in the research phases of concepts, construction and development of our future calibers.

Involved in the phases of innovation and creativity, you will be at the heart of the development of new technical solutions that will enable us to respond to performance, reliability and exclusivity issues of our new products.
