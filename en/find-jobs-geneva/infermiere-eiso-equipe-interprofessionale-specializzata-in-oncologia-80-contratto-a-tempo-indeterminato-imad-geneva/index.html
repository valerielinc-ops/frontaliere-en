Role overview

In collaboration with the Department of Oncology of the HUGs and with a view to improving the outpatient and home care of cancer patients, the IMAD is creating a pilot project to develop a home care device specializing in adult oncology, based on a joint clinical route of the IMAD-HUG with a view to improving the continuity of care and home care of cancer patients in the course of treatment at the HUGs.

The team formed will carry out a very broad and complex activity in the clinical, social, psychological and environmental activities of the client.

The EISO team will also have the task of establishing close links with the specific Geneva oncological network (e.g. the Oncological Department of the HUG, the Cancer League, OTIUM).

Main responsibilities

  • Your missions and responsibilities
  • Effectively respond to EISO team-specific care by providing autonomous and medical delegation of technically complex home nursing care to adult clients with oncological pathology, usually provided in hospital
  • Provide comprehensive personalized care to adult clients with oncological pathology who are part of a global vision, taking into account their direct and social environment, their psychological constraints.
