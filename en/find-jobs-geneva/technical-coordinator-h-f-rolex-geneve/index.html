Role overview

Introduction For our Purchasing, Support and Infrastructure division, we are looking for a Technical Coordinator (M/F) whose main mission will be to act as an interface between the maintenance teams, internal and external partners as well as the various requesters.

He will analyze, guide and manage technical requests, while leading discussions, following action plans and guaranteeing the updating of associated data.

It will also contribute to the continuous improvement of equipment performance, budget control and anticipation of technical risks linked to installations.
