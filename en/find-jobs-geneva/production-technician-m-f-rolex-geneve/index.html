Role overview

Introduction For our Production division, we are seeking a Production Technician (M/F) whose main mission will be to coordinate and supervise the various activities of the production workshop - machining and finishing.

Associated with the entire production process, he/she is able to integrate a multidisciplinary specialized team and collaborate with support services.

He/she participates in a collegial manner in the management and animation of the performance of the flow on the quality, cost, and time axes.
