Role overview

Introduction For our Production Division , we are looking for a New Product Project Leader (H/F) whose main mission will be to pilot the industrialization and production of new products as part of the company's industrial strategy.

You will be at the heart of our manufacturing activities in a stimulating environment.

You will coordinate various topics within multidisciplinary teams, mainly to renew the offer of products in the areas of dial, ceramics and jewellery, while respecting the contractually established performance in terms of quality, costs and deadlines.
