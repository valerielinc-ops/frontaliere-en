Original description

OSCAM – Ospedale e Casa Anziani Malcantonese is publishing the following competition: General Pharmacy Assistant. GENERAL PHARMACY ASSISTANT The Giuseppe Rossi Foundation manages a 56-bed hospital in Castelrotto. Of these, 26 accommodate patients suffering from psychiatric disorders and 30 are destined for a medicine department (RAMI – acute minor intensity department). The hospital also has a first aid center and several outpatient clinics (cardiology, acupuncture, hepatology, radiology, ergotherapy, physiotherapy, laboratory, dietary service). In addition, a 105-bed home for the elderly and another 70-bed care institution in Caslano are managed under the same roof, as well as a day and night therapeutic center. The Malcantonese Hospital, to complete its staff, is looking for a Pharmacy Assistant with a 40% employment rate, with the possibility of adjusting the employment rate up to 50%. Profile The person we are looking for will be responsible, under the supervision of the responsible healthcare service manager, for the following tasks: - Organizing, managing and coordinating the optimal functioning of the pharmacy service at the Malcantonese Hospital, ensuring the correct storage and procurement of medicines and medical devices intended for departments and outpatient services. The person will monitor the stock, manage orders and receipt of goods, ensure the correct conservation of medicines in accordance with current regulations and collaborate with healthcare staff to ensure efficient and safe distribution of medicines to various hospital departments. They will also contribute to controlling deadlines, managing documentation and respecting internal procedures in the pharmaceutical field.
