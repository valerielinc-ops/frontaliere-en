Student assistant for support with website editing (Wordpress) - Sustainability & Ethics in Education Initiative 10%-30%, Zurich, fixed-term print Drucken The Strategy and Analytics Team supports the systematic integration of sustainability and ethics into teaching and learning. The team's goal is to enable students to address global challenges such as climate change, resource justice and ethical decision-making competently, and to act as 'change agents for a better tomorrow'. As a student assistant, you will contribute to embedding sustainability and ethics in teaching and learning at ETH Zurich by providing organisational and content-related support. Project background Within the S&E Initiative, we have collected teaching activities for sustainability across ETH departments and partner universities (ENHANCE+). We are now getting ready to publish such collection on the existing Competence View website and on similar catalogues. We aim to make such innovative teaching activities visible, accessible, and easy to adopt for instructors, thereby enabling knowledge exchange and supporting high‑quality teaching across the university and within the ENHANCE Alliance.
