Original description

This ESEDE pre-admission course (400 hours or 800 hours according to the written validation of the ESEDE) is aimed at persons who have passed the ESEDE entrance examination and obtained a pre-admission certificate, requiring practical experience according to the EEP. Applications that do not meet these criteria will not be considered. We are looking for an intern on the Cluse-Roseraie Site and an intern on the Belle-Idee site. This internship allows you to immerse yourself in the reality of the teaching profession or educator of childhood by participating in the educational activities of everyday life (accommodation, free play, awakening activities, meals, naps, transitions, exits) under the supervision of a reference EDE or EDE. You observe, question and develop a professional posture while participating in team meetings and institutional life. We also accompany you to complete the ESEDE portfolio according to the school's expectations. The 800 hours must be performed continuously in the same structure, and any absence must be compensated.
