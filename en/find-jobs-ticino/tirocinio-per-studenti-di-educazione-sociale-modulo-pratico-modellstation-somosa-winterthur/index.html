Role overview

The social-pedagogical-psychiatric model station SOMOSA offers a stationary 6- to 12-month intensive care program for 14- to 18-year-old female and male youths who are in a severe adolescence crisis.

The aim of the treatment is to stabilise clients in their personality so far that they can exist when leaving the existing supporting or replacing socialization systems.

The treatment team consists of social-pedagogical, psychiatric, psychotherapeutic and work-agogical professionals who implement the somosa-specific, therapeutic-pedagogical concept.
