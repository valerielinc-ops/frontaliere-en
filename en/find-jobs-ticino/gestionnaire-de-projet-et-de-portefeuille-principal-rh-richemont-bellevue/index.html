Reference code: JR127327 Richemont owns some of the world’s leading luxury goods Maisons, with particular strengths in jewellery, fine watches and premium accessories. Prepare project reporting incl. Steering Committee presentations around ongoing projects. Track and support the continuing roll-out of HR Digital solutions into new markets, new Maisons, and new functionalities. Work in strong partnership with Group HR, Group HRIS and Group Product Office to ensure timely delivery of enhancements to Workday and other tools. Escalate risk to Senior Management & propose mitigation actions to avoid deviation
