Role overview

The Damiani Group, among the leading international players in the high-end jewellery industry, is looking for an IT Business Intelligence Specialist that works as a bridge between business and technology.

The resource will become part of the IT team and will work closely with the function manager, senior colleagues and external consultants, contributing to the development and maintenance of reporting solutions and data analysis based on Microsoft technologies.

The role is ideal for a motivated person to grow professionally in the business intelligence and data analytics world.
