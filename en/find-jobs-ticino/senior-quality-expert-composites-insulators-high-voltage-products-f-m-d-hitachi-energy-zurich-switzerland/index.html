The opportunity In this role you drive excellence in quality, delivery, and compliance for the supply base. Furthermore, you ensure global processes are understood and implemented by the SCM network. How you'll make an impact Lead and execute PPAP qualification plans, supplier audits, surveillance programs, and process\/product verification activities. Collaborate closely with Category teams to support CIPS strategies, supplier classification, selection, onboarding, and performance improvement.
