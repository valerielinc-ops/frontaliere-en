Role overview

Mission We are offering a full-time (100%) PhD Student at the Laboratory for the Chemistry of Materials and Manufacturing (ALCHEMY) at EPFL Neuchatel.

At EPFL ALCHEMY, we are focused on developing accessible chemistries and processing strategies that can enable the fabrication of a wide variety of advanced functional materials and devices.

You will be working on a project whose goal is to develop new photocrosslinking chemistries for vat photopolymerization 3D printing technology.
