Please note we do not offer sponsorship This is a unique 12-month programme based on a permanent contract within our Wealth Management Team in Jersey. We are offering an exceptional opportunity for a recent master’s graduate to join our exclusive programme, designed to provide in-depth exposure to Wealth Management at a renowned Private Bank. The programme offers a comprehensive learning experience, enabling you to gain valuable insights into the finance industry and develop essential skills for a successful career in private banking. These rotations take place in different teams, all closely connected to Wealth Management and Treasury and Trading, providing an excellent opportunity to build a broad internal network of colleagues and experts. Furthermore, you will benefit from shadowing experiences, gaining valuable exposure to various aspects of the bank’s operation. This programme is specifically designed for young graduates holding a master’s degree, with a maximum of 12 months of professional experience since graduation. You will have the opportunity to transition into a permanent role within the team after completing the programme.
