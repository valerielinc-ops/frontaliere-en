Original description

Become part of something bigger. Work with more than 2000 specialists from more than 100 professions and enable paraplegic people to live a self-determined life. On the modern campus in Nottwil, an inspiring atmosphere and interdisciplinary cooperation at eye level awaits you. The service area Social and Peer Counselling provides comprehensive support to the paraplegic in all regions of the country. The consultations take place at our location in Nottwil, partly in clinics, at the clients’ home or by telephone. We advise on questions regarding social insurance, finances, housing situation, employment or personal topics. To support our Social Work team, we are looking for an empathetic and professionally experienced personality. These are your tasks • (psychosocial) counselling and support of people with paraplegia or cross-sectional diagnoses and their relatives • Clarification of entitlements in the field of social insurance and securing needs-based support • Assistance in the preparation and submission of applications to social insurance and foundations • Participation in securing the economic livelihood of clients • Accompaniment in challenging life situations and change processes (e.g. professional reorientation, separation, transition to AHV age) • Promoting the independence and personal responsibility of clients • Interdisciplinary cooperation with involved bodies and partner organisations • Writing specialist contributions for your own media This sets you apart • You have completed training in social work FH/BSC with appropriate professional experience, • You bring in-depth knowledge in the field of social insurance. • You design your work flexibly and independently – also in the private setting at your clients at home. • You like to bring your knowledge and experience into projects. • Your German is stylish and you appreciate the work in an interdisciplinary team.
