Role overview

As a leading Swiss company in the convenience, gas station and energy sectors, we operate a network of over 320 Coop Pronto Shops with or without gas stations in Switzerland and Liechtenstein.

The Coop Pronto locations are managed very successfully as part of our franchise concept.
