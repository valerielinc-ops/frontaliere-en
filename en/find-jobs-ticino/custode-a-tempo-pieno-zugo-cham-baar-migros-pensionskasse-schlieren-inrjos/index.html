Role overview

As a full-time caretaker at Migros-Pensionskasse, you will have a responsible task where you can demonstrate your strengths such as independence, organizational talent, and flexibility every day. You will be responsible for four properties (mixed portfolio 1/3 commercial, 2/3 residential) in the Canton of Zug.

Tasks

  • First point of contact for our tenants
  • Assistance with the re-letting of rental properties (showing apartments, etc.) as well as apartment inspections and handovers (electronically with iPad)
  • Carrying out minor repair work as well as issuing, coordinating, and monitoring orders to tradespeople
  • Maintenance of technical systems (heating/ventilation, etc.) and organization of tradespeople in case of damage
  • Maintenance of the surroundings (flower bed care, lawn mowing, hedge cutting, etc.)
  • Checking and signing off invoices for correctness and completeness (electronically)

Requirements

  • At least 5 years: in facility management/caretaking
  • Completed vocational apprenticeship (EFZ): facility maintenance or technical/manual profession
  • Driving license Cat. B
  • Experience as a caretaker for commercial and/or residential properties
  • Good manners, service-oriented and communicative personality with joy in contact with tenants and tradespeople
  • Experience in using iPad, IT user skills
  • Federal Diploma of Higher Education as a Caretaker is an advantage
  • German (native speaker)
  • English (an advantage)

What we offer

  • Social benefits: Excellent pension fund and generous sick pay and accident insurance
  • Flexible working hours: Liberal working time regulations with a lot of flexibility for employees
  • Individual further training: Promotion of professional development
  • Personal responsibility & freedom: Great scope for design at the workplace
  • "Du" culture: Uncomplicated and open interaction
