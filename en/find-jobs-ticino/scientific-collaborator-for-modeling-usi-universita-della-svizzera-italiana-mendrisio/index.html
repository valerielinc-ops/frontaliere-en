Accademia di architettura - Modellistica Coordinatore/Coordinatrice per la Modellistica (80-100%), Accademia di architettura, Università della Svizzera italiana (USI) The University of Italian Switzerland (USI) is a young and agile reality in continuous development, launched towards new challenges in the sign of its three guiding values: quality, openness and responsibility. Active in different areas of study and research, USI offers its students a training as protagonists, immersive and interdisciplinary, and its researchers and collaborators a privileged area of autonomy and freedom of initiative. The USI Academy of Architecture in Mendrisio (USI-ARC) aims to train architects who can participate in a critical, responsible and innovative way to transform the living space of contemporary man. The faculty offers the student a study and work environment centered on the complexity of the architectural project as a factor of integration and reference for all the historical-humanistic and technical-scientific teachings, which complement the interdisciplinary program of the studies. In addition to pursuing academic educational goals, the faculty pursues research objectives through affiliated institutions also in collaboration with national and international institutions. To complete its team, the USI opens a position of Coordinator/Coordinator for Modeller at the Academy of Architecture in Mendrisio. ## Position The ideal candidate for the position of Coordinator/Coordinator for the Modeller of the Academy of Architecture, with coordinating tasks of the USI-ARC modeling laboratories and the work team, is preferably an architect with a strong technical and organizational attitude, able to activate the realization of models and prototypes for architecture and design in general.
