Start-up: to be agreed Activity rate: 80% to 100% Join the French Rehabilitation Clinic (CRR) of the Suva in Sion, a reference centre specializing in the rehabilitation of injured persons, with locomotor disorders, neurological disorders or spinal cord injuries. If you are passionate about quality care and want to contribute actively to the well-being of patients, this position is for you! The RRC care department consists of four units, organized according to the following rehabilitation specialties: paraplegia, neuroreeducation, traumatology and mixed unit. In order to complement its teams, the care department is seeking, for a start to the activity to be agreed, a multi-purpose nurse (planned replacements) Are you motivated, flexible and looking for a variety of activities to develop skills in the different stationary units of our clinic? We have the perfect opportunity for you! To complete our team, we are looking for a graduate nurse who wants to work on all of our units as part of planned replacements. Placed under the direct responsibility of the care management, you will have a clear schedule, prepared and communicated in advance.
