Original description

Your • Tasks With your organizational design, you will ensure the smooth operation of our five psychiatry centers in St. Gallen, Rorschach, Wattwil, Uznach and Rapperswil and will be responsible for all infrastructural topics. You plan, tax and maintain the properties, including contracting, cleaning and catering, and actively develop the facility management. You will also conduct small to medium-sized construction projects and participate in location-related infrastructure and investment planning. The responsibility for operating and investment budgets is also in your hands and you are responsible for economic planning and control. You also ensure compliance with statutory requirements in the areas of occupational safety, fire protection and the environment, as well as contract management with owners, administrations and sub-parents. In close cooperation with internal stakeholders, you coordinate FM topics and promote cooperation with our Partner Stiftung Förderraum. In addition, you will take over the project management at the Wil site under the leadership of technology and construction. Your profile You have a Bachelor in Facility Management or comparable training as well as business know-how. You bring experience in professional practice, leadership and project management and work structured and organized. Expertise in area management in conceptual, processual and standard-oriented terms is what distinguishes you. Working with internal customers motivates you; you care for an open and value-adding approach to employees and stakeholders. You think networked, customer-oriented and process-oriented and keep an overview even in challenging situations. Social competency, team skills, conflictability, enforcement and loyalty complete your profile. With around 1'400 employees, we ensure well-accessible, personal and effective psychiatric treatments and the care of adult people in the cantons of St.Gallen and Appenzell Ausserrhoden and in the Principality of Liechtenste
