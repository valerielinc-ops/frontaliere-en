Description

PROFILE

  • You have completed training or studies as an automation engineer, technician in HF or a comparable field
  • You have very good knowledge and a confident handling of electrical circuit diagrams
  • You have already gained in-depth experience in dealing with electrical CAD tools (preferably E3 Series)
  • You already have industry knowledge in plant, mechanical or rail vehicle construction
  • You value an independent way of working and close cooperation with internal and external bodies
  • You communicate Fluent in German and English TASKS
  • With the help of your in-depth specialist knowledge, you create the electrical circuit diagrams and connection concepts (plug concept)
  • You develop and control the production documents (cable lists, terminal plans and resource list) and support the placement of components in the control cabinet, including digital cabling (routing)
  • You maintain close cooperation with the electrical engineers and the electrical installation planning and make the necessary decisions Material and component selection with the help of the PLM system
  • You incorporate the specified changes and support electrical engineering with assembly support
