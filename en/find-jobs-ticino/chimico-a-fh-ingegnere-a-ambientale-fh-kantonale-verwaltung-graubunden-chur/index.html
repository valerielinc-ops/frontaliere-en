Original description

The Office for Nature and Environment (ANU) is a department of the Department of Education, Culture and Environmental Protection. The ANU is responsible for the cantonal implementation of the Water Protection, Environmental Protection and Nature and Homeland Protection Act. We are looking for a chemist(in) FH / environmental engineer(in) FH tasks by 1 January 2027 or by agreement • Supervision of the operation of public and industrial wastewater treatment plants (ARA) in the canton of Graubünden. • They evaluate the operating data of the ARA and check whether the initiation conditions are met. • You evaluate the control reports for the comparative analyses and create the annual reports for all ARAs. • They check the self-analysis, determine the causes of deviations and train the operating staff in wastewater analysis. • They visit the ARAs and inform themselves about the operation. Requirements • Completed studies as a chemist FH or environmental engineer FH with in-depth knowledge and experience in process engineering and wastewater analysis. • Willingness to complete the VSA sewage treatment plant personnel training. • They perform safely, have negotiating skills, work independently, are communicative and capable of teamwork. A good verbal and written expressive ability in German is a matter of course, knowledge of Italian and Romance is an advantage. • Category B driving licence.
