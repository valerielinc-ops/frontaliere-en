Original description

Work in a challenging, international and fast paced SAP transformation programs focusing on design and implementation of Group Consolidation solutions for financial consolidation enabled by SAP and S/4HANA. Analyze our client's business finance and controlling processes, gather the requirements and provide best in class advice on design and implementation of consolidation solutions. Manage sub-projects and lead teams of functional consultants and client staff. Acuire a broad range of technical and soft skills through on the job and off the job training. Be part of a growing, dynamic team blending SAP innovation and industry expertise. A degree (bachelor / master / diploma) preferred in business, IT, engineering or business information systems or digital discipline. Preferably focused and specialized on finance/controlling during your primarily and further education. 5 years of experience in process consulting, design and implementation of SAP group consolidation solutions with focus on SAP S/4 HANA Group Reporting. Experience with other SAP consolidation solutions SEM-BCS, EC-CS or BPC is beneficial. Expert knowledge in group closing and financial consolidation topics and a very good understanding regarding the overall finance integration across finance and operations. Profound experience in setting up balance carry forward, currency translation, intercompany elimination, consolidation of investments and Validation processes. Strong analytical skills with a “can do” attitude, fluent in English and German. Any other languages are a plus. Flexible to work on engagements in Switzerland and/or abroad.
