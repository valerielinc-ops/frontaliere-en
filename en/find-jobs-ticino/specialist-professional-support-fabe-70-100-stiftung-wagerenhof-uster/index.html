Original description

Immediately or by agreement we are looking for you as Specialist / Professional Support / FaBe (70 - 100%) You have experience in caring for and caring for people with mental and physical impairments. With your committed, natural and human-oriented nature, you make an important contribution to making our residents feel at home here. What matters is you. Every single person is at the center with their individual quality of life. We do everything we can to ensure that our 250 residents, 246 daycareers and 52 employees at protected workplaces in the Wagerenhof can experience the highest possible quality of life. For employees of different backgrounds and professionalism, Wagerenhof is an attractive employer and a competent training partner for learners of all levels. For this we give you the responsibility: • Accompaniment and care of people with severe mental and physical impairments • Interdisciplinary and interprofessional cooperation • Assistance in managing the group budget You fit in with us and with this position, if you also put our residents and our supervised employees at the center of your activities and you bring the following: • Professional / expert support EFZ • Sound professional experience in accompanying and caring for severely impaired people • Differentiated perception and reflection • Good PC skills and good German skills in words and writing This makes us the place for your work • Meaningful tasks with a lot of creative scope • Appreciative, human interaction as part of a well-established team • Professional support through an interdisciplinary and professional environment • Good accessibility with public transport For questions please contact Susanne Scherrer, Human Resources, 044 905 13 91.
