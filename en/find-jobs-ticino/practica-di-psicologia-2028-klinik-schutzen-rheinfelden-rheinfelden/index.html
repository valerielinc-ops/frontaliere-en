Role overview

2028 The Klinik Schützen Rheinfelden is a private clinic for psychosomatics, psychiatry and psychotherapy with 108 beds, a daily link with 24 seats and an ambulatory in Rheinfelden and Aarau.

The stationary departments are integrated in the hotels of Schützen Rheinfelden AG.

The clinic specializes in the treatment of adults with illnesses such as depression, anxiety, trauma disorders, psychosomatic oncology, somatoforme and pain disorders, eating disorders and personality disorders.
