Industry Partnership Lead 80%-100%, Zurich, fixed-term print Drucken The ETH AI Center is one of the largest university centers in the world for artificial intelligence with over 110 Professors from all 16 departments of ETH Zurich and over associated 1500 PhD students & postdocs. Our purpose is to amplify human potential by fostering breakthrough ideas with interdisciplinary AI research and enabling the path from science to society. We strive to push the boundaries of AI research, foster innovation, and drive impactful projects. As the Industry Partnership Lead, you will have the unique opportunity to work at the forefront of AI research and industry applications. Project background We are looking for a hands-on leader to own and scale the ETH AI Center’s industry engagement function and develop long-term, impactful partnerships. You will operate as a player-coach: driving high-value partnerships end-to-end yourself while building pragmatic structures, processes, and the team setup needed to scale. You should be equally comfortable in a boardroom closing a six-figure collaboration as you are refining a pitch deck, structuring a CRM, or unblocking an internal stakeholder. At the intersection of academia, industry, and entrepreneurship, you will translate cutting-edge AI research into compelling partnership models and build long-term relationships with leading global organizations. You will collaborate closely with ETH AI Center leadership, Swiss National AI Institute (SNAI), and partner institutions such as the EPFL AI Center - engaging daily with ETH professors and researchers, C-suite leaders at partner companies, SNAI and EPFL AI Center counterparts, and innovation leaders across the Swiss and European AI ecosystem.
