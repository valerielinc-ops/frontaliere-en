Description

PROFILE

  • Completed technical or IT-related training or Studies and experience in the Teamcenter environment
  • In-depth knowledge of Teamcenter AWC, BMIDE, workflows & lifecycles, authorization models and Active Workspace Extensions
  • Experience in the development and expansion of Teamcenter solutions with ITC, C++, SOA and REST services as well as workflow handlers and business rules
  • Understanding of standard PLM solutions and upgradeable customizing according to the “Configuration First” principle
  • Experience with system architectures, interface definitions and integration of CAD, ERP/SAP and ALM systems
  • Confident handling of Oracle databases, data imports, PowerShell & batch scripting as well as deployment and release processes
  • Structured, communicative and team-oriented way of working as well as the ability to communicate technical content in an understandable manner to different stakeholders TASKS
  • Responsibility for installation, configuration and maintenance of PLM system landscapes including server operation, updates, patches and hotfixes
  • Analysis of technical requirements and transfer to sustainable, Teamcenter-compliant technical concepts based on the principle of “standard before customizing”
  • Modeling and configuration of data structures, processes, lifecycles, workflows and authorizations with BMIDE and Active Workspace
  • Development and implementation of extensions such as ITC, AWC extensions, workflow handlers and services with a focus on upgradeability and clean architecture
  • Integration of Teamcenter into adjacent systems such as CAD, ERP/SAP or ALM including import, export and synchronization processes
  • Support of Deployment, release and operational processes including testing, error analysis and technical documentation
  • Close collaboration with IT and specialist departments as well as active participation in the further development of the PLM system landscape
