Original description

Do you have an eye for detail, enjoy precise manual work, and have an interest in architecture and design? Then you’ve come to the right place! The Fachhochschule Graubünden is offering you an exciting and varied apprenticeship as an Architectural Model Maker EFZ starting on August 1, 2026. ## Tasks - During your apprenticeship, you will create scale and true-to-life models of planned buildings and terrain. - You will work with various materials such as wood, plastic, metals, cork, and plaster, giving the models a realistic appearance with colors and plant-based materials. - In our modern model-making workshop, you will also work with various stationary machines and CNC-controlled machines. ## • Requirements - Spatial imagination and a keen sense of materials - Enjoyment of practical and planning work, as well as collaboration with others - Systematic thinking and precise work methods - A motivated, eager-to-learn, and creative personality - Good academic performance in mathematics, drawing, and manual work ## • What we offer - 6 weeks of vacation and fair pay, including a 13th-month salary - A solid, practice-oriented education in an exciting field - A modern workshop, including a laptop - Performance bonuses - Active support for attending vocational high school (optional) - Great sports offerings and employee outings ## Contact Interested? You can find all the information about your training and our benefits at www.fhgr.ch/lernende.
