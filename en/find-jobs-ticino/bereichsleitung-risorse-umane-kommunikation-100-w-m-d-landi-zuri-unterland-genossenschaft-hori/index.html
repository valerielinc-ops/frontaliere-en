Role overview

Benefits - Education and training We invest in the development of our employees.

A wide range of internal education is open to all and we support external training.

Our holiday scheme guarantees all employees at least 5 weeks of holidays.

For learners and employees under 20 years of age, it is 6 weeks.

In addition, the purchase of unpaid leave is possible.

Mothers receive 100% of their insured wages during 14 to 16 weeks of maternity leave.

The other parent enjoys 3 weeks of family time at full wage.

We provide our employees with above-average social and pension benefits and offer special conditions on supplementary insurance with various health insurance funds for their entire family.

Discounts and discounts Our employees benefit, among other things, from discounts when buying furniture and new cars, special conditions on private insurance as well as discounted mobile subscriptions for them and their families.

Every five years we thank our employees for their loyalty and great commitment.
