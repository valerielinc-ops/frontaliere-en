Company and context

  • The Position The Opportunity Roche Diagnostics Quality, Regulatory & External Affairs (QR&E) is a fully global organization of approximately 1,500 employees present across 18 countries.
  • This organization is engaged across the entire diagnostic value chain - ensuring our right to operate, shaping the global regulatory environment, and accelerating access to our diagnostic solutions for customers and patients worldwide.
  • In People & Culture (HR) our vision is to bring our Roche purpose to life by unlocking the greatness in all our people and organisation.
  • We are a catalyst for enabling high performance while nurturing a sense of belonging and an inclusive culture where we can all thrive as we discover the best ideas and develop truly innovative solutions for patients and Roche’s future success.
  • This position embodies the mindset of a business leader and entrepreneur, operating flexibly within a highly networked, cross-border structure to support multiple global leadership teams.
  • As the P&C Business Partner for QR&E, you will be based in the USA but possess a deeply global mindset.
  • This role is designed for an HR professional who is energized by engaging and supporting diverse, global teams and who wants to expand their expertise by learning how P&C and employment landscapes operate in other countries.
  • You will serve as a strategic partner to senior leaders located both inside and outside the USA, navigating a complex matrix organization to translate business priorities into impactful global people strategies.
  • You will be an active member of a dedicated, international team of P&C Business Partners.
  • Together, you will collaborate closely across borders and time zones to share insights, align initiatives, and provide seamless, unified support to the global QR&E organization.
