Description

Collaboration in ongoing international cooperation projects at MeteoSwiss in the field of meteorological measurements and data, combined with regular trips abroad Design, recommendation and implementation of operational and sustainable solutions in the areas of meteorological measurement network deployment, meter maintenance and calibration, data management and staff training in cooperation with international partners preparing reports, concept papers and other technical documents (e.g. calls for tenders), depending on:

  • Requirements of the various projects Support for the acquisition of new projects abroad presentation of contributions at international conferences
  • Submission of articles in peer-reviewed journals Master in Natural Sciences or Engineering; A promotion is beneficial Experience with meteorological measurement systems (including ground and atmospheric observations, in particular for GBON variables); additional experience with hydrological measurement techniques is an advantage experience in the processing, storage and dissemination of meteorological data
  • Experience with WMO-relevant open source tools (wis2box, ADL, climweb, surface CDMS, ...) is an advantage Agile and solution-oriented thinking with analytical and independent working methods; a strong sense of responsibility and the ability to work efficiently in a team of experts
  • Practical experience with databases, virtual machines/cloud computing and Python programming is beneficial Strong intercultural awareness and empathetic thinking, safe in dealing with a large number of international scientific, technical and administrative partners You have a good knowledge of two official languages and fluent knowledge of English
