Original description

The Possibility We are looking for a dynamic and experienced executive for purchasing that strengthens our dedicated service team at Hitachi, Ltd., where speed and quality are of the utmost importance. In this key position, you conduct strategic purchasing initiatives, optimize procurement processes and run a talented team of around 12 shopping experts. Your expertise ensures a fast and cost-effective procurement of goods and services while maintaining the highest quality standards and promoting long-term, partnership relationships with suppliers. This allows you to take over the leadership, promotion and further development of our purchasing team and create a performance-oriented and responsible corporate culture. They develop and implement innovative procurement concepts in accordance with our service strategy. In doing so, you place particular emphasis on timely delivery, continuous quality improvement, efficiency improvement in the supply chain and sustainable cost reduction. The application of the Critical-Path method to ensure timely procurement processes and to minimize delivery delays is one of your core tasks. They ensure consistent excellence throughout the entire purchasing process – from demand determination to payment – and ensure clear processes and maximum transparency. They define and control supplier segmentation and develop targeted procurement strategies. In addition, you are responsible for the selection, qualification and contract negotiations with suppliers. By building strategic partnerships at leadership level and establishing robust supplier management systems with KPIs, scorecards, regular reviews and escalation processes, you can ensure the sustainable performance of our supplier network. They work closely with cross-departmental teams such as material planning, engineering, quality and controlling, in order to optimally tune the purchase to the corporate requirements. They control purchase-related risks, including supply chain disruptions
