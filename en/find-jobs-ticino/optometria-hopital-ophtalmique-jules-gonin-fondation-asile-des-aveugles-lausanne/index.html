Role overview

Founded in Lausanne in 1843, the Asylum for the Blind Foundation includes the Jules-Gonin Ophthalmic Hospital, a university ophthalmology service, a research centre, the Pedagogical Centre for Students with Visual Impairment (CPHV), the social, rehabilitation and low vision service and the Clair-Soleil medical and social institution.

More than 650 employees are mobilized every day in the service of visual health to offer diagnostic, treatment and support services to people with vision problems.
