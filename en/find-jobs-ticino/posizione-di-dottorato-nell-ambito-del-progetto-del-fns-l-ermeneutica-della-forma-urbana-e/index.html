## Posizione Aperta Posizione aperta presso Università della Svizzera italiana. ## Informazioni Generali - Dipartimento/Istituto: Institute for the History and Theory of Art and Architecture (ISA). - Ruolo: PhD position as part of SNSF project “André Corboz’s hermeneutics of urban and territorial form: research practices, concepts and methodologies”. - Sede: Mendrisio, Svizzera (Canton Ticino). ## Descrizione PhD position as part of SNSF project “André Corboz’s hermeneutics of urban and territorial form: research practices, concepts and methodologies” (100%) The Università della Svizzera italiana (USI) is a young and lively university, a hub of opportunities open to the world where students are offered a quality interdisciplinary education in which they can be fully engaged and take centre stage, and where our researchers can count on having the space to pursue their initiatives freely. Established in 1996, USI is in constant transformation, always taking on new challenges while remaining true to its three guiding principles: quality, openness, and responsibility. The Institute for the History and Theory of Art and Architecture (ISA) has a profile inspired by long-established institutions, such as the History Theory and Criticism of Architecture and Art of the MIT in Boston or the Institute for the History and Theory of Architecture (gta) of the ETH Zurich. Without limiting their fields of study to any period or geographical area in particular, the purpose of ISA is to foster a fuller understanding of the complex meanings of artistic and architectural action. ## Il Progetto SNSF The SNFS project “André Corboz’s hermeneutics of urban and territorial form: research practices, concepts and methodologies” will investigate the research methodologies and practices of André Corboz (1928-2012), one of the most distinguished personalities in the field of urban and territorial studies.
