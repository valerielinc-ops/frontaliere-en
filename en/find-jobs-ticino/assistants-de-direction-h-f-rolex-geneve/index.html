Role overview

Introduction We are looking for Executive Assistants (M/F) whose main mission will be to assume a central and leading role with management by providing operational support in the fulfillment of their responsibilities.

Responsibilities

  • Ensure the coordination and monitoring of all administrative activities
  • Anticipate, organize and monitor workflows, information and communication in complete autonomy, particularly through the management of emails, the agenda, calls and correspondence
  • Act as a high-quality interface between management and internal and external contacts
  • Demonstrate anticipation and availability to identify and implement solutions adapted to various situations
  • Directly manage certain files and specific projects
  • Write meeting reports, coordinate and monitor decisions taken
  • Prepare presentations and produce reports by collecting and analyzing data and results Profile
  • Commercial maturity or equivalent training, federal executive assistant certificate, an asset
  • Proven experience of more than 10 years as a management assistant within a large structure
  • Native French speaker with excellent spelling and perfect command of syntax
  • Excellent written and oral English
  • Mastery of common IT tools
  • Strong capacity for anticipation, taking initiative and very good management of priorities
  • Personality with interpersonal skills, sense of service and welcome
  • High sense of discretion and confidentiality Conclusion We offer you a stimulating environment, excellent working conditions and first-rate social benefits.

We thank you for sending a complete application file (CV, cover letter, certificates and diplomas).
