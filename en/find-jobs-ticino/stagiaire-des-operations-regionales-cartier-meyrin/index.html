MAIN OBJECTIVE The Regional Operations intern is a key contributor within our Cartier Europe Operations department. In this role, the intern will take part in the distribution of Cartier products from our regional distribution center to our point of sales and be responsible for efficiently ensuring the administration of staff orders and deliveries to offer the highest service. The position is based in Meyrin, Geneva within our "Campus Genevois de Haute Horlogerie".
