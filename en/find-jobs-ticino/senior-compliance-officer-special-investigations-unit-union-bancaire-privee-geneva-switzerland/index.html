Original description

Mission & Main duties of SIU team Main unit of WM Compliance responsible for internal investigative efforts and handling of cases with enhanced FCC risk. Team’s main duty is to handle internal investigations with the responsibility to conclude them in the timely manner and observance of Bank’s regulatory and reporting duties. Key responsibilities Senior Compliance Officer in SIU : Handling of internal investigations, internal escalations, submission of files to AML Committee for the decision regarding SAR filing and answers to Authority Requests. Additional responsibilities include daily screening of client names, handling Request for Information (RFI) for correspondent banks and media watch. Treatment of internal and external audit requests as well as analysis of files with high management attention. Main Committees: AMLC ARC Sanctions Committee Core competencies: Knowledge of Swiss Banking regulations, provisions of Swiss AML law, Swiss Criminal Code and MROS practice Experience in Financial Crime Compliance and AML Strong adherence to the company’s values: Dedication, Conviction, Agility, and Responsibility. Compliance with regulations and internal directives. Specific Competencies and other Skills/Abilities: Curiosity, adaptability, analytical and holistic approach, ability to work in a team and drawing conclusions upon collected facts.
