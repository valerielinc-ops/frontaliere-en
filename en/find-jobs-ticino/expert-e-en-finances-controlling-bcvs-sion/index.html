Original description

As an expert in finance and controlling, you will contribute to the financial management of the Bank and participate in various projects of the service. You will ensure the reliability of financial information and the respect of regulatory requirements. ## • Responsibilities - You will be responsible for the follow-up of investments and costs (CAPEX / OPEX) for the entire bank - You will contribute to the development of dashboards and financial analysis to reach your audience with impact and support strategic decision-making - You will participate in the elaboration and improvement of financial forecasts - You will contribute to the reform of the investment process (real estate / IT) - You will participate in the implementation of critical regulatory reporting for supervisory authorities (BNS – FINMA) ## • Requirements - You stand out with solid analytical skills combined with a deep knowledge of banking regulations - You have a tertiary education in economics (HEC, Federal Diploma in Expert in Finance and Controlling) coupled with experience in the field of banking risk management, banking accounting, or banking audit - You master banking regulations, and your interest in IT brings you ease with software (ideally Fire and Finnova) and reporting tools (e.g. PowerBi) - As a native French or German speaker, your good knowledge of the second language represents a certain advantage - Agile and sociable person, you bring a sharp analytical spirit and the dynamism necessary for an entrepreneurial management of your files - Finally, you excel in an evolving environment and demonstrate initiative, rigor, and autonomy. Your curiosity and proactivity allow you to identify relevant solutions. ## Contact We look forward to discovering your application. To do so, send your complete file to Marisa Berclaz, our HR Business Partner, to the address: recrutement@bcvs.ch, by the latest 13 March 2026.
