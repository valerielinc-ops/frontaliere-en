Role overview

COMMERCIAL MANAGER SWISS ENERGY PROGRAM PROJECT GIG Europe SA, a company of the Tarchini Group, which deals with the design, development and management of 'green' energy plants, is looking to expand its activities in the national territory in the field of energy storage with the realization of BESS plants, and is searching for a commercial figure to manage relationships and negotiate land agreements for the realization of BESS plants, within the context of the Swiss Energy Program.

The selected person will have to identify portions of land, dialogue with landowners and with electricity network managers, to finalize agreements on surface rights for the installation of BESS plants. Requirements Age between 30 and 40 years Swiss citizenship

Perfect knowledge of German and English (possibly bilingual).
