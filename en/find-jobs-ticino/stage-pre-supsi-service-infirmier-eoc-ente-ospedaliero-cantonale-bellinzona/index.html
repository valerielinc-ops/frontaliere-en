Application process

  • The following position is addressed to candidates who wish to apply for a spontaneous application as a pre-SUPSI training course – nursery service Profile The pre-SUPSI internship is required by the Italian University School of Switzerland and is aimed at students who wish to pursue a Bachelor in nursing.
  • Requirements To be enrolled and attend complementary modules of the Specialised School for Health and Social Professions (SSPSS).
  • Prepared for human contact, motivation to work with patients, communication skills and flexibility.
  • Excellent knowledge of the Italian language spoken and written and possibly of a second national language.
  • Indispensable knowledge of the main computer means (office package).
  • Date of entry Educational periods established by the school (1st period: January-February and 2nd period: April-May).
  • Notes --> Applications of those who do not have the above requirements will not be taken into account in any case and will therefore be deleted from our database.
  • Applications will be assessed only in case of service requirements.

Company and context

  • The organization allows to effectively combine local approach and overall vision, ensuring the population a global and proximity hospital offer, regardless of where the services are required.
  • Thanks to the commitment and expertise of more than 6,500 employees and their attention to the human relationship, EOC ensures quality health care by taking care of more than 41,000 patients per year and providing more than 600,000 outpatients.
