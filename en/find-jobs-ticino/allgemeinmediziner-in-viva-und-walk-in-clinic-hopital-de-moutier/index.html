Company and context

  • Start date: By immediately or by agreement Company description The Réseau de l’Arc is a health organization firmly anchored in the region with around 1’250 employees, which ensures comprehensive care for about 80’000 people.
  • It includes two hospitals in Moutier and Saint-Imier, five Medicentres as well as the Medical Center Biel (MZB), supplemented by an outpatient network, two nursing homes, two radiology institutes, a center for physical and cognitive therapies and a hospital care service at home.
  • As the first fully integrated health organization in Switzerland, the Réseau de l’Arc offers its own health insurance plan within the framework of the VIVA project, which guarantees the residents: an optimal and coordinated supply within the Jura arch.
  • The Medical Center Biel (MZB) is located in the centre of this integrated model and combines an interdisciplinary team from Hausärzt:innen, Fachärzt:innen, Pflegefachpersonen, Psycholog:innen und Therapeut:innen.
  • It offers a wide range of services – from general medicine and emergency care through specialist consultations to radiology and laboratory.
  • The medical centre Biel (MZB) and the medical practice VIVA together form an integrated care system that guarantees comprehensive and continuous care for the patient.
  • While the MZB as a regional competence centre offers a wide range of medical services – from basic care to specialized examinations and emergency treatments – practice VIVA complements this offer by a long-term, personal support of the insured persons of the VIVA health plan.
  • The close interconnection of MZB and practice VIVA enables coordinated and patient-centered care: Acute complaints can be treated easily and quickly via the Walk-in-Clinic of the MZB, while chronic and preventive health aspects are closely followed in the context of home care in the VIVA practice.
