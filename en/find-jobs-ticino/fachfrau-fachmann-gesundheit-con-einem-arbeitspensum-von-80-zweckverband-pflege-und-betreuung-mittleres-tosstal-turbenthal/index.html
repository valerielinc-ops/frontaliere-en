Original description

The Zweckverband Pflege und Betreuung Mittleres Tösstal provides the population of the four association municipalities Turbenthal, Wila, Wildberg and Zell with inpatient and ambulatory care, care and residential services according to the service mandate. Spitex Mittleres Tösstal provides professional hospital care and support for the inhabitants of the mentioned communities. We are a progressive and dynamic organization with over 90 employees and integrate interdisciplinary specialist teams, such as acute and long-term care, psychosocial care, palliative care, housekeeping and care as well as Spitex Plus services. Weekend service Self-employed, responsible and team-oriented working methods High level of social and professional competence Flexibility and resilience Computer skills Cat B driving licence, own car or e-bike This position is also suitable for re-entry into care.
