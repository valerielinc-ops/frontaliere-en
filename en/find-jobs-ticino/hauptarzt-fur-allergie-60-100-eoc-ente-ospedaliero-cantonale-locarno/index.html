Company and context

  • The organization allows to effectively combine local approach and overall vision, ensuring the population a global and proximity hospital offer, regardless of where the services are required.
  • Thanks to the commitment and expertise of more than 6,500 employees and their attention to the human relationship, EOC ensures quality health care by taking care of more than 41,000 patients per year and providing more than 600,000 outpatients.
  • To complete our team of EOC Medicine, and more precisely at the service of Allergology of the Regional Hospital of Locarno "La Carità" (main seat) we look for a(a) Head doctor of Allergology (60-100%) Profile: The person we seek has professional experience and autonomy, as well as motivation, own initiative and spirit of collaboration.
  • He believes, like us, in the importance of putting the patient and his family at the center of all our attentions.
  • For medical practitioners with foreign qualifications, the Federal Commission’s recognition for medical professions MEBEKO is required, and its registration with MedReg before the start of the activity.
  • Comprehensive knowledge of the Italian language (level required at least B2) with relative registration to MedReg before the start of the activity.
  • Extensive knowledge of another national language and English.
  • Obtaining the authorization of Libero Esercizio issued by DSS Canton Ticino.
  • Experience in Allergology and Immunology Experience in the formation of young colleagues (medici assistants) Spiccate relational and work skills in teams Interest in research and teaching.
  • Date of entry: from immediately or date to be agreed.
