Role overview

TL DR We are expanding Duferco’s Corporate Innovation Team: a fast-moving engineering team operating in a startup-like environment, backed by the stability and scale of a global diversified group.

You will work across multiple companies and domains inside the group — including energy, trading, logistics, steel, insurance, sports, and other sectors — helping build and operate the cloud infrastructure, deployment systems, automation platforms, and internal developer tooling that power our next generation of software and AI-enabled systems.

We are looking for a DevOps / Platform Engineer with strong infrastructure and automation skills who wants to help design highly autonomous, resilient, and scalable systems.
