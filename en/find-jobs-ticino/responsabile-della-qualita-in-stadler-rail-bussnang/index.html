Description

PROFILE

  • You have several years of experience in quality and process management, ideally in an industrial environment
  • You can prove a basic education at university level (FH/uni) in the field of business administration, engineering or a comparable discipline
  • You have in-depth knowledge of the ISO 9001 (22163), ISO 14001 and ISO 45001 standards
  • You have experience in planning and conducting internal and external audits
  • You have practical knowledge of quality planning in projects and you know the common quality control and quality assurance methods of a manufacturing company
  • Ideally, you have experience with Lean Management, Kaizen and CIP methods
  • You have knowledge of modeling processes, preferably with BPMN 2.0
  • You convince through a structured, analytical and solution-oriented working method and communicate safely and addressable in German and English TASKS
  • You plan and lead internal audits to ensure the effectiveness of our integrated management system
  • Prepare external audits and supervise them vis-à-vis customers, certification bodies and authorities
  • You ensure the further development and maintenance of the management system according to ISO 9001 (including IRIS/ISO 22163), ISO 14001 and ISO 45001
  • You carry out conformity checks and derive effective measures
  • You analyze, optimize and standardize existing processes in the sense of efficiency and quality
  • You support projects in quality planning and ensure robust quality assurance
  • You work closely with different departments and advise them on quality-relevant questions
