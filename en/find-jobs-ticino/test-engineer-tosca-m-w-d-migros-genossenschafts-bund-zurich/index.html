Role overview

As a test engineer, together with the agile teams, you will receive test automation to the next level.

With your technical expertise in Tricentis Tosca you implement robust, automated regression tests and integrate them into the existing delivery processes.

You will be able to run, maintain and further develop a team buddy in each case.

Main responsibilities

  • Automating regression tests and testing processes with the Testing Tool Tosca
  • Advice and support of the agile teams related to Testing Tool and Automation
  • Analyze, check and evaluate user requirements, specifications and models with regard to testability
  • Analyze product risk analysis to determine the test depth and determine residual risks after performing the tests
  • Creating test specifications based on a detailed technical analysis of both functional and non-functional requirements (reliability, efficiency, usability, etc.)

Key requirements

  • Found professional experience: in the field of software testing (Tricentis Tosca) Eidg.
  • ID: at least Higher vocational training in the field of computer science
  • Analytical and conceptual skills as well as a high degree of autonomy
  • Sovereign use of relevant digital tools as well as interest in future technologies
  • Open for cross-departmental cooperation with teams and units with an agile mindset (SAFe, Scrum)
  • Strong solution orientation – challenges proactively tackle rather than postpone
