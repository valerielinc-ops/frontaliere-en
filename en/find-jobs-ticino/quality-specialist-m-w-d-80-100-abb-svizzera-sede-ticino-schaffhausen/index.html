Main responsibilities

  • Your role and responsibilities
  • In this role, you will support the implementation of the local quality strategy, driving a strong quality culture, continuous improvement, and sustainable problem-solving.
  • As a Supplier Quality Engineer, you will ensure that all purchased materials and services meet ABB standards by qualifying suppliers, monitoring performance, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to achieve world-class results. The work model for the role is: hybrid
  • This role is contributing to the Electrification, Smart Buildings / Supplier Business Excellence & Development in Schaffhausen, Switzerland. You will be mainly accountable for:
  • Supplier Qualification & Audits: Qualify and approve new suppliers and conduct regular process, system, and product audits. Quality
  • Requirements & FAI: Define and communicate quality requirements and lead First Article Inspections and qualification processes.
  • Performance Monitoring & Improvement: Track supplier performance using KPIs (e.g., PPM, OTD) and drive continuous improvement initiatives.
  • Non-Conformance Management: Investigate quality issues, perform root cause analysis, and ensure effective corrective actions (8D/CAPA).

Key requirements

  • for the role
  • Bachelor’s degree in engineering (Mechanical, Electrical, Industrial, or related field) or equivalent professional experience
  • Good knowledge of English and German (spoken and written); additional languages are a plus
  • Experience in supplier quality management and supplier audits (ISO 9001, VDA 6.3, IATF 16949 or similar)
  • Knowledge of APQP/PPAP and First Article Inspection (FAI)
  • Strong problem-solving skills using root cause analysis methods (8D, 5-Why, Ishikawa)
  • Understanding of statistical quality methods (SPC, MSA, Gauge R&R)
  • Knowledge of technical drawings, GD&T, and material/component specifications
