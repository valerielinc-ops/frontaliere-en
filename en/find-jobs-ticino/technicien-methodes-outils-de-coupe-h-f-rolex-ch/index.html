Introduction For our Production division, we are looking for a Cutting Tool Methods Technician (M/F) whose main mission will be to guarantee the qualification and provision of all cutting tools used for machining Box components.

  • Introduction For our Production division, we are looking for a Cutting Tool Methods Technician (M/F) whose main mission will be to guarantee the qualification and provision of all cutting tools used for machining Box components.
  • Define and make available the cutting tools necessary for the industrialization of new products and new machining means

Main responsibilities

  • Define and make available the cutting tools necessary for the industrialization of new products and new machining means
  • Maintain the technical link and proximity with suppliers in order to guarantee the performance of the tools
  • Sustain the know-how by ensuring the management and updating of technical data and associated documents (plans, reference reports and standardization)
  • Pilot continuous improvement by identifying and carrying out optimization actions
  • Ensure the role of technical respondent for cutting tools with internal and external partners
  • Participate in technical exchanges with production to ensure support on cutting tools
  • Ensure technological monitoring of cutting tools by participating in trade shows and regularly carrying out feasibility studies Profile
  • CFC of polymechanic, ES Technician or equivalent
  • Recognized skills in machining and management of cutting tools, with expertise on stainless steels and precious metals Mastery of software CAD/CAM (ideally CREO)
  • Ability to work in a team with a taste for the field
