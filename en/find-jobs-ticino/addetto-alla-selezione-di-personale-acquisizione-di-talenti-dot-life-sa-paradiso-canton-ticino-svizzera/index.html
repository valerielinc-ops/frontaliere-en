Original description

Company Description DOT LIFE is the management company of an exclusive selection of four hotels located on Lake Lugano, Switzerland. With a team of around 300 employees, its hotels – Villa Principe Leopoldo, Park Hotel Principe, Kurhaus Cademario and Villa Sassa – offer refined and refined stay experiences. DOT LIFE's mission is to embody the DOT Lifestyle philosophy, promoting holistic wellness and redefining the standards of hospitality. The hotels guarantee excellence in Swiss luxury, combining unique experiences, high-quality services and personalized assistance to meet every guest need. Role description We are looking for a talent acquisition recruiter to be placed at our headquarters in Paradiso. This is a part-time and face-to-face role. The selected candidate will be responsible for managing the entire personnel selection process, from the identification of company needs to the conclusion of the recruitment. Day-to-day tasks will include posting job openings, interviewing candidates, and coordinating hiring procedures, ensuring a positive candidate experience while promoting the company's values. Qualifications - Skills in recruiting and ability to manage the entire selection cycle, with experience in writing and publishing job offers and in the search for qualified candidates. - Experience and skills in hiring and interviewing, with the ability to conduct interviews in a professional manner oriented towards the evaluation of candidates. - Excellent communication skills and interpersonal skills, with an empathetic and proactive approach to candidates. - Management skills such as attention to detail, time management and the organization of personnel selection processes. - Knowledge of local regulations and best practices related to the hiring process will be considered a preferential requirement.
