Original description

The Civil Engineering Office is responsible for the planning, construction, maintenance and operation of cantonal roads as well as hydraulic engineering and non-motorized traffic in Graubünden. We are also responsible for the operational maintenance of the national roads in the canton. With the introduction of operational mobility management, the management of the cantonal vehicle fleet was also centralized in order to ensure more economical and environmentally friendly operation. As the specialist department for non-motorized traffic, we coordinate the planning and construction of non-motorized traffic facilities. This includes, in particular, walking, hiking, cycling and mountain biking as well as transportation with vehicle-like devices. As a Friendly Work Space certified office, the health and satisfaction of our approximately 480 employees is particularly important to us. For this reason, we are committed to systematic occupational health management (OHM).
