Main responsibilities

  • At UHR (maintenance rolling stock) you will have a key position in the Olten plant, with many tasks and responsibilities in order to support our customers with your full commitment.
  • Together with your teammates, you will develop solutions to current challenges and implement them as a team.
  • Further training is also not neglected for individual professional development.
  • At the Olten plant you are currently working in two shifts from Monday to Friday between 4:45 a.m. and 5:45 p.m.
  • You can move that.
  • As a shunting specialist, you move passenger and freight train cars under the guidance of the maneuver coordinator.
  • Your area of ​​activity includes, among other things, attaching and detaching the various wagons and operating the manual switches and the signal box.
  • You work in accordance with the transport service regulations and the prescribed standards of the SBB.
  • Commissioning and driving multiple units and articulated trains are also part of your area of ​​responsibility.
  • You drive shunting locomotives, road-rail vehicles, carry out brake tests and ensure that they are ready to drive.
