Original description

Reference code: JR128124 Richemont owns some of the world’s leading luxury goods Maisons, with particular strengths in jewellery, fine watches and premium accessories. Each Maison represents a proud tradition of style, quality and craftsmanship and Richemont seeks to preserve the heritage and identity of each of its Maisons. At the same time, we are committed to innovation and designing new products which are in keeping with our Maisons’ values, through a process of continuous creativity. F&A Industrial department at Richemont aims to contribute to the industrial transformation of the F&A segment of the Group. It operates the Group F&A production entities and aims to define and lead the Group F&A Industrial strategy to support F&A Maisons growth and accelerate internal savoir-faire. HOW WILL YOU MAKE AN IMPACT? The role of F&A Industrial Project Coordinator will be part of the F&A Industrial team, within Group Operations, reporting to F&A Industrial Transformation Senior Manager. The role will actively support on a wide variety of strategic and operational initiatives that will impact transversal initiatives with Maisons, production entities, F&A division and other Group teams (Responsible Sourcing, HR, Sustainability…). The key responsibilities will be to: Support the F&A Industrial team in consolidating the F&A industrial strategy and roadmap, working with Maisons’ teams and industrial entities to gather inputs and support decision-making Support the team and the division in coordinating transformation projects Formalize department outputs and document progress status with presentations for upwards and downwards communication - within the department and with the rest of the Group Support key moments during the year (budget, financial updates, manufactures visits, reporting to management, …) Organize the interactions with key stakeholders from the Group and Maisons HOW WILL YOU EXPERIENCE SUCCESS WITH US?
