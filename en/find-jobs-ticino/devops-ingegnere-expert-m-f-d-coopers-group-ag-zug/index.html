Role overview

For our client, we are looking for an experienced DevOps / Infrastructure Engineer who confidently designs, builds, and operates scalable on-premises and cloud infrastructure platforms, effectively collaborates with software engineers, solution architects, platform teams, and customer-facing stakeholders, and brings strong expertise in infrastructure architecture, reliability engineering, automation, infrastructure as code, CI/CD, and high-availability environments, in order to ensure a robust, secure, and efficient infrastructure ecosystem that supports software development, hardware integration, testing environments, and business-critical services while maintaining operational excellence, resilience, lifecycle management, and compliance with organizational and industry requirements throughout the platform lifecycle.

Start date: asap Planed Durration: 12 Months (extension possible) Workload: 80-100% Contract Type: Contracting Location: Rotkreuz Remote: up to 50% Your Tasks:

  • Contribute to RLX infrastructure from all the way to hardware and physical network to buildind and maintaining on-premises HA clusters with all their infrastructure services
  • Select, order, assemble and integrate hardware for the infrastructure
  • Contribute to the RLX hardware in the loop testing infrastructure
  • Create and operate the platform for our high level services (build servers, ci runners, artifact repositories)
  • Integrate the infrastructure with external dependencies (compute, storage, network provided by the company and facilities available on site, and also with service dependencies, such as GitLab, GitHub, Artifactory, Slack)
  • Create declarative infrastructure as code solution for infrastructure management
  • Build and maintain monitoring, alerting and inventory management services
  • Provide a platform for developer self service, that allows devs to experiment or solution engineers to take over a hardware in a loop device for debugging and testing
  • Design a lifecycle management solution for the infrastructure and the hardware in the loop hardware components
  • Design, implement, test and maintain the backup, emergency and disaster recovery procedures for the infrastructure and the services
  • Integrate cloud and on premises infrastructure and service and Design, build and maintain pipelines (GitHub) Your Profile:
  • 8+ years of professional experience in Software Engineering, Infrastructure Engineering, Site Reliability Engineering (SRE), DevOps, or a related field
  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology, or a related engineering discipline
  • Proven track record of designing, building, and operating infrastructure platforms from the ground up, including user-facing services and production environments
  • Strong understanding of infrastructure architecture, reliability, security, and operational excellence
  • Experience collaborating closely with Software Engineers, Solution Architects, Product and Platform teams and Customer-facing technical stakeholders
  • Demonstrated ability to lead technical initiatives, drive infrastructure improvements, and influence engineering best practices
  • Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to work independently in fast-paced environments
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to explain complex technical concepts to both technical and non-technical audiences
  • Professional proficiency in English (written and spoken) Nice to Have:
  • Experience in Virtualization / Container technologies, Kubernetes, CI/CD pipelines
  • Experience with cloud providers (e.g.
