Founded in 1913 in Italy, the Prada Group was built on a tradition of excellence and with a vision of innovation. The Group, a world leader in luxury, operates in over 45 countries with the brands PRADA, Miu Miu, Church's and Car Shoe, and employs over 100 nationalities. SCOPE The researched figure has a strong sensitivity to the product and a technical background in the area Luxury Clothing with proven experience on technical outerwear. The role involves the coordination of the chain in the stages of prototyping, sampling and production, ensuring efficiency, quality and alignment with the Group's stylistic and industrial needs.
