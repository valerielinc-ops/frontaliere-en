LANDI Graubünden AG is an agricultural, retail and energy trading company, anchored and networked in the canton of Graubünden. The forklift test is an advantage. ## Your advantages - familiar working atmosphere with a first-name culture - individual training and development opportunities - at least 5 weeks of vacation as well as very good social benefits - benefit from various discounts and attractive benefits - careful introduction together in a strong team with passion
