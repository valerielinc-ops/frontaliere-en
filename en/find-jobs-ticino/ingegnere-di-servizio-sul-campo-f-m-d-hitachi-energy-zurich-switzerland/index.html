Original description

## Descrizione The opportunity Hitachi Energy, High Voltage Products, is renowned worldwide as a specialist for power transmission and distribution. The Service unit is responsible for the worldwide after sales support of generator circuit-breaker (GCB) systems and gas insulated switchgears (GIS), including training, installation and commissioning, scheduled maintenance, added value services, troubleshooting, extensions, retrofits and replacements. Service is the key to success. As a colleague in our globally active service team, you will appreciate the independent, varied and solution-oriented work. After a versatile, practice-oriented training in Switzerland and abroad, you will lead new GIS installations around the globe. A technically challenging position in a multicultural environment awaits you. You can expect an environment where all employees can develop their skills and grow in it. Senior as well as junior applications are welcome! ## Mansioni - Managing multicultural construction sites worldwide for gas insulated switchgear for the voltage level of 110KV - 550KV. - Responsible for the successful execution of on-site installation / maintenance / repairs in compliance with contractual deadlines and in compliance with local legal requirements. - Responsible for the quality of work performed on site through quality control and assessment of skills of local support staff. - Conduct on-site product training on the product and during installation (optional). - Coordination of local labor. - Cost control of resources used and regular reporting to project management. - Actively contribute to process improvements and proactively take measures to increase the efficiency of on-site activities. ## Requisiti - Completed apprenticeship in the metal construction, electrical or automotive industry, or similar - Basic knowledge in electrical engineering is a desirable must, further training in electrical engineering is an advantage - Work experience in assembly & commission
