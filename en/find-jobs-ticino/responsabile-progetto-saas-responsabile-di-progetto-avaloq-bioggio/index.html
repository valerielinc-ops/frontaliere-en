Role overview

We are looking for an experienced Project Manager to lead and manage core projects to our SaaS community, focusing primarily on annual software updates for banking solutions.

In this role, you will be responsible for overseeing the planning, execution, and successful delivery of these projects, ensuring that the software updates are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

In addition to managing the core upgrade projects, you will collaborate with other Project Managers overseeing "on top" initiatives.
