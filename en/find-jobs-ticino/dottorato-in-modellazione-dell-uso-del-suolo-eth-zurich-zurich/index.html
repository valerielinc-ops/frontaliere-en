PhD in land use modeling 100%, Zurich, fixed-term print Drucken The Chair for Planning of Landscape and Urban Systems (PLUS) at the Institute for Spatial and Landscape Planning, ETH Zurich, is a young, dynamic and interdisciplinary research team addressing problems of sustainable spatial and landscape development.

Starting in September 2026 or by mutual agreement, we are looking for a PhD in land use modeling.

Project background The 4-year PhD position is funded by the NCCR CLIM+ program ( NCCR CLIM+ — Climate Extremes and Society ), financed by the Swiss National Science Foundation.
