Main responsibilities

  • The opportunity We are looking for an ambitious personality, who will have the following main responsibilities:
  • Collect and consolidate data from a variety of sources (inc. internal systems, other teams at Roche) to ensure relevant information is available to the team
  • Perform data audits and cleansing to improve the quality of the team’s different databases (e.g., global portfolio asset database), ensuring they are consistent and accurate
  • Analyse data (e.g., owned and leased asset data, workplace quantitative and qualitative data, fit-out cost benchmarking data) to identify key trends and gaps for further review
  • Visualise meaningful conclusions from data analysis by creating summaries, tables, and presentation slides based on prepared data
  • Assist with subteam-specific recurring reporting needs (e.g., lease administration reports) as well as team-wide reporting needs such as performance (KPIs) reports for periodic reviews
  • Support the administration of team’s internal systems (e.g., user management in portfolio asset database and project management platform) and assist in updating and maintaining analytical solutions and visualisations (e.g., dashboards)
  • Coordinate internal team meetings (incl. helping prepare agendas, collect discussion topics), and maintain the team’s website, which includes collaborating with team members to source and draft project stories for publishing
  • Act as a connective tissue between sub-teams, helping share best practices, align data standards, and coordinate joint initiatives Who you are For this position you bring the following qualifications:
  • Currently enrolled in a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Business, Management, Real Estate, Facility Management, Data Analytics or related field of work (or recently graduated within the past 12 months)
