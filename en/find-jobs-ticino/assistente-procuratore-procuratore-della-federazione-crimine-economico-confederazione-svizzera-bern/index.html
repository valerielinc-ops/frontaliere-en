In extensive and partly media-exposed criminal investigations as well as in international legal assistance proceedings, which support case-leading prosecutors mainly in German

  • Structured and result-oriented working methods as well as networked thinking and solution-oriented, pragmatic and assertive personality with a positive attitude and strong team spirit
  • Good active knowledge of at least one second official language and in English; passive knowledge of the third official language
