Role overview

Your Responsibilities

  • You actively contribute to the industrialization of our products through the analysis of Digital Mock-Ups (DMU) and by anticipating future production needs
  • You lead the definition and optimization of assembly and pre-assembly sequences for our engines
  • You ensure the feasibility and efficiency of assembly operations by studying the accessibility of assembly areas and tooling
  • You identify and prevent collision risks between components, installations, and tools to ensure smooth and safe production operations
  • You participate in engine assembly activities to gain in-depth knowledge of production techniques and relay improvement requests from the shop floor
  • You translate your analyses into concrete recommendations and high value-added technical reports
  • You contribute to the continuous improvement of our production methods and the deployment of new industrial management tools
  • You carry out strategic studies and provide key analyses to support management decision-making
  • You collaborate with production teams in conducting tests on assembly lines and validate analyses against real production conditions Your Profile
  • You hold an Engineering degree or an equivalent qualification in Industrial Engineering or Mechanical Engineering, with a specialization in design or a related field
  • You have a minimum of 2 to 5 years of experience in mechanical design or simulation
  • You are fluent in English, both spoken and written, and have a good level of French.

Knowledge of German is considered an asset

  • You are proficient in MS Office and have a solid basic knowledge of Pro/Engineer – CREO and ideally Teamcenter Visual Mockup
  • You are passionate about technology and have a particular interest in Diesel engines and complex industrial environments
  • You demonstrate flexibility, agility, and a strong sense of responsibility
  • You stand out through your positive mindset, openness to change, and strong communication skills Our Offer Can we inspire you to take on a responsible position in a dynamic and independent family business?

The company ​Liebherr Machines Bulle SA is the centre of excellence for combustion engines (diesel and gas engines) and hydraulic components (axial piston pumps and motors).
