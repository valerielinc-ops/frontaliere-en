Role overview

Competition: Director/Director Sanitario at Clinica Varini, Orselina (Locarnese, Canton Ticino) Description The Clinical Varini Foundation of Orselina, with its 130 employees, offers stationary and outpatient performance in the fields of specialist medicine and dermatology for adult patients.

The structure is a reference point for the taking and care of different fragility, with 75 beds in various sectors: Acute repared with less intensity (RAMI), geriatric palliative care, House for the elderly, temporary stay and adult invalid Foyers.

In anticipation of the integration with the Casa per Anziani Montesano, for a total of 110 beds from January 2028, the contest for the recruitment of an FMH Specialist in general internal medicine with in-depth training preferably in geriatrics.
