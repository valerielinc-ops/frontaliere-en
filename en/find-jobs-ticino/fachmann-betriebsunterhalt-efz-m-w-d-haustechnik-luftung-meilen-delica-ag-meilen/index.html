Role overview

With us, you keep operations running in the truest sense of the word: from ventilation and waste disposal to the sewage system, you ensure that everything runs smoothly – and you help us build up vocational training in facility maintenance.

You maintain and clean our ventilation systems, replace filters, and ensure that motors and building services run reliably.

Key requirements

  • Several years of professional experience (3-5 years) in maintenance or building services.
  • Completed vocational training (EFZ) in facility maintenance or equivalent technical field.
  • Mechanical affinity, technical understanding, and proficiency in MS Office.
  • High social competence, team-oriented, and interest in mentoring apprentices.
  • Willingness for on-call duty (6-7 times/year) and fault hotline availability.
  • Resilient and reliable, comfortable working in heat, cold, or at heights.
