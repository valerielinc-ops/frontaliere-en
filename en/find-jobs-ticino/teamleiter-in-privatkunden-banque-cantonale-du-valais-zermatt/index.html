Private Customer Team Leader Zermatt 80-100% Do you have a commercial education and experience in the banking sector and leadership? Are you looking for an opportunity to put your skills into practice? This is your chance. What does that mean concretely? - Your task is to lead and develop the private customer advisor team, both technically and personally. - You accompany and coach the advisors. - You control sales activities and drive the acquisition of new customers. - You manage your own private customer portfolio and identify the potential of existing and potential customers. - You build and maintain a relationship of trust with customers and serve them proactively. - You implement and maintain regulatory requirements and internal guidelines. Do you like challenges? This role is perfect for you if... - You have a commercial education and experience in the banking sector, preferably in a leadership position. - You already have experience in customer management and good sales skills. - You have a regional network that can help expand our customer base. - You stand out for your high customer orientation and entrepreneurial thinking. - You can easily communicate with customers thanks to your good language skills. Then we should get to know each other! Do you want to be part of our team? - We are happy to receive your application. - Send your complete documents to our HR Business Partner Yolanda Fernandez at the address: rekrutierung@wkb.ch.
