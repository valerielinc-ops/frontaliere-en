Role overview

The Dielsdorf Health Center is a modern geriatric competence center with around 500 employees from 35 nations.

Our offering ranges from advice for older people and their relatives to general and specialized long-term care through to care and support at home.

With over 200 beds, the inpatient offering of general long-term care at the Dielsdorf location is supplemented by specialized treatment paths for rehabilitative acute and transitional care, palliative care, psychiatric geriatrics and a focus on dementia.
