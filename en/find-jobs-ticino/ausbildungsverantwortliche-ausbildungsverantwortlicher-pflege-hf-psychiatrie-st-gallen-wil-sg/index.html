Company and context

  • The responsibility for the selection, organization and implementation of internships and qualification procedures lies with you.
  • You coordinate and plan the assignments of learners and students in cooperation with the nursing services and department management.
  • You are the contact person for educationally relevant external positions as well as for guardians and lead practice-oriented introductory interviews, learning support and evaluation interviews.
  • You represent the management of education and training nursing, therapies and social work in various committees and at external events.
  • You have completed tertiary nursing training and at least two years of professional experience, ideally in the field of training.

  • In-depth knowledge of the Swiss education system as well as educational theories and didactics is available.
  • You convince with strong social, communication and leadership skills as well as your conflict resolution ability.
  • An independent, responsible working method with a high level of initiative and innovative analytical thinking characterizes you.
  • You bring experience in interdisciplinary cooperation, project work and active participation in committees and training concepts.
