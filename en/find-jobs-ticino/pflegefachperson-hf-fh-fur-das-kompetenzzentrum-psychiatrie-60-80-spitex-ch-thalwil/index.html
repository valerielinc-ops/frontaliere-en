Role overview

As a non-profit organisation, Spitex Zimmerberg AG is responsible for the care, advice, care and support of people of different age groups who are dependent on external help due to health restrictions.

We enable the inhabitants of Adliswil, Thalwil, Kilchberg, Rüschlikon and Langnau am Albis to live a self-determined life in their usual environment.
