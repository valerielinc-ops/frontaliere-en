Role overview

We are looking for a E-commerce Merchandising Specialist to join the E-commerce team, with the goal of supporting the management and optimization of the online assortment, contributing to commercial performance and delivering an excellent user experience across the digital channel.

The role will work closely with the Merchandising, CRM, and Marketing teams, ensuring a consistent and elevated online product presentation in line with the brand’s luxury positioning.
