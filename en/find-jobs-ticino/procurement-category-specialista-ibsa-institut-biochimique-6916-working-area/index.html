Original description

The Procurement Category Specialist supports the execution of category strategies and manages day-to-day sourcing activities in close collaboration with the Category Manager and internal stakeholders. The role focuses on operational sourcing, supplier coordination, and procurement data management, ensuring efficient purchasing processes, supplier performance monitoring, and continuous improvement in procurement operations. The position reports directly to the Category Manager. ## Mansioni - Support the execution of category strategies defined by the Category Manager in alignment with business functions - Manage operational sourcing activities including RFx processes, quotation analysis, and supplier follow-up - Implement supplier agreements in line with defined category plans - Maintain accurate procurement data in ERP systems (pricing, contracts, lead times, supplier information) - Collaborate with internal stakeholders to collect requirements and ensure specification alignment - Monitor supplier performance using defined KPIs and escalate deviations when necessary - Support contract implementation and compliance monitoring at local or regional level - Coordinate supplier onboarding and qualification processes, including documentation and system setup - Manage and resolve day-to-day procurement issues such as delivery delays, order discrepancies, and quality claims - Conduct basic market and benchmark analysis to support supplier selection and negotiations - Contribute to continuous improvement initiatives by identifying operational inefficiencies and proposing corrective actions ## • Requisiti - Degree in Business Administration, Supply Chain, Engineering, o un campo correlato - Esperienza in procurement, sourcing, o operazioni di supply chain - Buona comprensione dei processi RFx e della gestione dei fornitori - Esperienza di lavoro con sistemi ERP (ad esempio SAP o simili) - Abilità analitiche e organizzative forti - Capacità di collaborare con team cross-funzio
