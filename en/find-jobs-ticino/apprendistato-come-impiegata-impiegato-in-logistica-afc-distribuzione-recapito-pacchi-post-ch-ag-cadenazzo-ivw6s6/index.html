Original description

Do you like starting the day early, being outdoors and giving a smile to the people you meet? Then training in delivery (Distribution specialization) is just what you need! In the three years of basic training you will acquire the tools necessary to successfully enter the varied world of logistics. Your training Early in the morning you will sort the parcels assigned to you and load your yellow vehicle for the next distribution round. You will then travel independently, deliver and take delivery of all types of items and take care of our customers' requests. You will conclude the working day by completing several subsequent jobs at your training location. During the training you will receive the support of professionals who will introduce you to the practical part. With financial support from Swiss Post, you will also obtain a category B driving license and learn to drive a forklift. You will complement your extensive logistics training with assignments in various internal functions, employment in a warehouse service and individual optional modules in line with your needs. Your skills We are looking for young people who are enthusiastic, positive and motivated to make a difference with their actions. You meet the following requirements: you like working outdoors and physical activity (sturdyness is a fundamental requirement) you love working in contact with others and you are a reliable, honest and discreet person at the start of the apprenticeship you are at least 17 years old you are available, before the start of the apprenticeship, to obtain the learner/learner driver license for category B motor vehicles you have completed compulsory schooling with a sufficient grade average at the lowest level at the start of the apprenticeship you have a B2 language level in the national language of the training place. Do you recognize yourself in this profile? Then you are exactly the person we are looking for! We will be happy to receive your online application accompanied
