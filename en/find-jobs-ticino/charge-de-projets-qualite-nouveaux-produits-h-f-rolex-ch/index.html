Main responsibilities

  • Pilot and animate the preventive quality process of new products (risk analyses, life analysis series)
  • Define with the project team the qualification plan and the monitoring plan produced, accompanying the qualification of articles
  • To drive the quality performance of new products, and to manage the treatment of internal non-compliances
  • To pilot with the support of multidisciplinary teams projects of continuous improvement (intention framing, analysis and deployment in accordance with planning and objectives)
  • To propose improvement projects based on the results of exploitation
  • Provide methodological support on tools and approaches Quality
  • Develop and adapt, if necessary, Quality tools to the needs of users Profile
  • Professional experience confirmed in project management
  • Desired knowledge of manufacturing processes: machining and surface processing
  • Confirmed mastery of statistics and quality tools (QRQC, 8D, AMDEC, MSP, Audit...)

Key requirements

  • University diploma, EPF/HES engineer or equivalent training
