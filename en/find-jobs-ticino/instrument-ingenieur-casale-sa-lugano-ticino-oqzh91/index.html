Main responsibilities

  • The position is within the Procurement Department and reports to the Section Manager. Main duties and Responsibilities:
  • Act as the main interface with suppliers regarding supply progress and delivery status
  • Ensure material delivery to be in accordance with Purchase Order terms by monitoring and controlling vendor progress
  • Perform desk expediting to monitor production and financial progress and identify potential risks impacting delivery dates
  • Collect Monthly Progress Reports (MPRs) from suppliers
  • Review and analyze MPRs to assess actual progress, identify actual/potential delay and define realistic forecast delivery dates
  • Interface with Quality Inspectors to follow up and monitor supply progress
  • Regularly update the supply status report to inform the Project Manager and/or Procurement Coordinator on supply progress, delays, and critical issues
  • Coordinate with suppliers to define and implement recovery plans to maintain delivery schedules
  • Inform and update the Expediting Coordinator Manager and the QC & Expediting Section Manager on critical supply issues and propose corrective actions
