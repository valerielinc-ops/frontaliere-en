Arxada is a global leader in innovative solutions to protect our world.

Application process

  • As a member of our composite material laboratory at Arxada in Visp, Switzerland, you plan and carry out laboratory experiments and application testing in the field of polymers and their composites in close cooperation with the project teams.
  • Preparation and development of product formulations
  • Laboratory syntheses in gram to kilogram scale
  • Analysis and characterization of raw materials and end products
  • Implementation of application tests on polymer and composite materials
  • Ensuring high safety and quality standards in the laboratory
  • Careful documentation of results and active knowledge exchange in the team Very good knowledge of German;
  • Good MS-Office knowledge (Excel, Word, PowerPoint) as well as affinity for computer-assisted laboratory systems
