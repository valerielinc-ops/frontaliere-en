or with our online form susanne.musy@lhm.ch The Lucerne Altitude Clinic Montana is the rehabilitation clinic of the Lucerne Cantonal Hospital, with accredited programs for pulmonary, cardiovascular, musculoskeletal and internal rehabilitation as well as a department for psychosomatics.

Another focus is the certified sleep analysis centre.

The clinic is located at an altitude of 1500 m on the sunny plateau of Crans-Montana in central Valais.

For 01.08.2026, we are again offering apprenticeships for training as a specialist in health (FaGe).

Health (FaGe) is a three-year apprenticeship with federal Certificate of proficiency.

Individual reductions are possible, taking into account already acquired basic vocational training EBA or EFZ.

The FaGe accompany and care for patients and support them in the activities of daily life, as well as take on housekeeping and administrative-logistic and medical-technical tasks.

Nursing and medical work is carried out on behalf of and under the responsibility of a qualified nurse.
