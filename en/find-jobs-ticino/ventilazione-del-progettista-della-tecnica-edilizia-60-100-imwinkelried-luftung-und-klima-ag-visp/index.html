Original description

Vacancies Building technology planner, ventilation Building technology planner, ventilation 60 until 100% We are a Swiss company with expertise in ventilation and air conditioning technology. With headquarters in Visp and a location in Burgdorf and around 70 employees, we operate in the cantons of Valais and Bern. We are part of the Burkhalter Group, the leading provider of cross-discipline building technology in Switzerland and the Principality of Liechtenstein. Are you ready for the next career step? We are looking for you as a building technology planner for ventilation (60–100%). Entry immediately or by appointment. Your tasks As a building technology planner, you will support our project managers in interesting projects. You support project teams in creating project planning, submission and execution documents as well as plan and schematic drawings using CAD in 2D and 3D. You will also independently develop smaller ventilation, heating or sanitary systems “from the initial idea to the handover of the system to the client”. Your profile In order to be able to carry out this demanding and varied job, you must have a qualification as a building/technical planner or similar. You have good knowledge of CAD and Microsoft Office, are open to new things and enjoy working in a team. Reliability and flexibility are your other strengths. Foreign language skills, French an advantage, round off your profile. Our offer We offer you an attractive workplace, independent work with a lot of customer contact in a dynamic team, a pleasant working atmosphere and other attractive conditions. Your contact Have we aroused your interest? Then send us your complete application documents online. We look forward to getting to know you.
