Role overview

The Psychiatric University Hospital Zurich treats and cares for mentally ill people of all ages.

The more than 2,500 employees from 55 different professional profiles work in interdisciplinary teams to contribute to the holistic care of patients, from assessment to therapy to aftercare.

Our work is based on mutual trust and personal responsibility and together we promote an innovative corporate culture.
