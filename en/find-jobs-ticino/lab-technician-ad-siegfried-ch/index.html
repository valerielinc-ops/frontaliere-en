Main responsibilities

  • In the context of implementing new manufacturing processes for pharmaceutical active ingredients, you will perform, as part of the laboratory team, the necessary chemical analyses of laboratory experiments to set up an optimized manufacturing process for the factory.
  • In accordance with GMP requirements, you will actively participate in analytical validations/transfers.
  • You will also be responsible for laboratory experiments conducted in the context of investigations following non-conformity issues at the factory.
