Role overview

The International Payroll Specialist will be a key resource within an evolving global payroll structure. The role ensures accurate salary processing, full compliance with local and international regulations, and effective collaboration with internal and external partners to guarantee efficient and compliant payroll operations.

This position will primarily focus on EFG locations outside Switzerland and will serve as the main point of contact for payroll‑related matters across these locations. At the same time, the role will also be involved in payroll operations for Switzerland, providing support and backup to the team as needed. This role will provide guidance on payroll topics, including allowances, tax regulations, social security and reporting requirements, supporting the organisation in maintaining high standards of compliance and service quality.

The role also offers the opportunity to contribute to the implementation of a global payroll platform for employees of EFG international locations and to progressively take ownership of multi‑country payroll operations from Switzerland

  • Manage the global payroll process from Switzerland for selected international locations, ensuring accurate and timely salary payments.
  • Support and act as backup for payroll activities for Switzerland‑based employees.
  • Coordinate with local payroll providers, ensuring compliance with tax, social security and labour regulations in each jurisdiction.
  • Proven experience in multi‑country payroll management, ideally within an international or financial services environment.
  • Strong knowledge of international payroll processes and compliance requirements (tax, social security, labour law) across multiple jurisdictions.
  • Experience with payroll platforms and HR systems, with strong attention to data quality and process reliability.
