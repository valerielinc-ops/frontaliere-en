PUBLIC COMPETITION SPECIFICATIONS FOR THE PART-TIME HIRING (70%/28 HOURS PER WEEK) OF AN EVENT LOGISTICS COORDINATOR AT THE EVENTS AND CONGRESS DIVISION OF THE CITY OF LUGANO * * * * * The MUNICIPALITY OF LUGANO announces a public competition for the part-time hiring (70%/28 hours per week) of an event logistics coordinator at the Events and Congress Division of the City of Lugano, under the conditions of the Organizational Regulations for the employees of the City of Lugano (ROCCL) and the specifications of this competition.
