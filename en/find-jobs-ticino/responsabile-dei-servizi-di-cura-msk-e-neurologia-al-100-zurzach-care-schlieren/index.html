Original description

Our Limmattal rehabilitation clinic offers specialized musculoskeletal and neurological rehabilitation, including surveillance rehabilitation and trauma rehabilitation at a high medical level. Our interdisciplinary team ensures optimal care for our patients through targeted diagnostics and individually tailored therapy programs. The close collaboration with the Limmattal hospital and the direct connection to the medical emergency station ensures timely acute treatment in case of urgent complications. Your tasks You will be responsible for the personnel, professional and administrative leadership of our nursing team in the Limmattal rehabilitation clinic together with your two group leaders over two floors (musculoskeletal and neurological rehabilitation). You will actively contribute to the further development of the newly expanded site in Schlieren as a member of the management team. You will work in an interprofessional team with other nurses, therapists and doctors. Together with the nursing team, you will be responsible for high-quality care. Your profile You have completed your training as a registered nurse or have an equivalent recognition with at least one positive SRK-PreCheck. You have leadership experience and are willing to further develop this area. You have a permit to practice your profession or are willing to apply for one. You have experience in the field and preferably also leadership experience. You enjoy working in an interprofessional team, think holistically and always look for solutions. You are positive, communicative and have a strong sense of quality. You are comfortable with common computer programs (Outlook, clinical information system, planning software Polypoint). What we offer You will work in a modern working environment and can actively participate. You will work in a supportive and respectful team that works together for the best possible patient care. You can voluntarily participate in our internal nursing pool for additional shift
