Original description

In the Indirect Tax sector, we advise nationally and internationally active companies on current developments and relevant VAT regulations. Why is VAT so exciting? As a consumption tax, it requires detailed analysis of services and products. Our work is broadly divided into two areas: VAT services (consulting and compliance) and VAT technologies and processes. ## Responsibilities - Support Swiss and international companies with VAT-related questions (e.g., place of supply, exemptions, input tax deduction). - Research facts, analyze data, and draft memos, presentations, and expert opinions. - Participate in submissions to authorities (e.g., binding rulings) and manage correspondence with authorities. - Support registrations, declarations, and internal controls and help improve VAT compliance processes. - Monitor legal developments in Switzerland and the EU and prepare concise briefings for the team. - Work in interdisciplinary project teams, take on clearly defined work packages, and contribute to quality and deadlines. ## Requirements - Completed bachelor's degree or equivalent qualification - Interest in legal and economic issues - First practical experience in indirect taxes, financial services, auditing, or consulting is an advantage - Proficient in German and English, both written and spoken; Italian and French are an advantage - You are curious, flexible, eager to learn, and have an entrepreneurial spirit - Team player, clear communication, and enjoyment of collaboration in interdisciplinary, international teams.
