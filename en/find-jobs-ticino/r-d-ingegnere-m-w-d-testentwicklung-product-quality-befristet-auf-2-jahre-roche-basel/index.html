Main responsibilities

  • Your responsibilities/position As a R&D Engineer (w/m/d), you are critically involved in the development and preservation of the product quality of our fully automated in vitro diagnostics.
  • Project highlights: You are actively working on exciting reagent projects focusing on the clinical.
  • Chemistry and are involved in the work of our PartnerChannels, which expand our diagnostic solutions.
  • Developer-Mindset: You work with the development, optimization and revision of in vitro diagnostics for clinical chemistry.
  • Scientific Depth: You plan and conduct independently challenging analytical experiments to develop, characterize and optimize assays.
  • Technological excellence: The independent adaptation and validation of test methods on modern analysers (e.g. cobas c / Integra systems) is your core area.
  • Data-based decisions: You conduct experimental data independently, judge them critically and prepare the results graphically and in writing using statistical methods.
  • Responsibility and quality: You are responsible for the maintenance and maintenance of laboratory equipment and ensure compliance with applicable safety and quality regulations.
  • Interface work: The careful documentation of your work as well as the development and presentation of solution suggestions in the team and at internal interfaces complete your task area.
