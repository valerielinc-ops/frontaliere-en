Original description

Directly linked to the Deputy Director General, you lead and coordinate all the missions of the new Risk Management Directorate (RMD) and ensure that the services provided are efficient. As a director, you propose the specific risk management and internal control strategy, taking into account the institutional issues and the needs of the partner entities. You ensure the implementation of this strategy and develop a risk culture, in consultation with the Directorate General and the Board of Directors, and with the teams of the directorates and departments. In particular, you design and maintain a cross-cutting and sectoral risk mapping, ensuring a systematic and comprehensive view of institutional exposures. You structure and lead risk management bodies to ensure effective and shared governance. To achieve your mission, you are setting up a central risk management team that you lead and that is functionally linked to decentralized risk and internal control teams in the directorates and departments. You define performance indicators and adapt criteria and approaches to hospital and medical specificities, and deploy a digital tool dedicated to risk management, crisis management, and internal control. In an integrated vision, you maintain a consolidated risk portfolio by identifying interdependencies. You also ensure consistency of risk coverage plans and intervene in crisis management to ensure appropriate action and coordination with relevant stakeholders. Through your client orientation and proactive consultation approach, you create a relationship of collaboration and trust that is conducive to managing risks, implementing and supervising institutional internal control, without ever losing sight of the independence and rigour inherent in your function. Promoting innovation and continuous improvement, you are deploying an institutional risk culture, crisis management, and internal control program to strengthen institutional maturity.
