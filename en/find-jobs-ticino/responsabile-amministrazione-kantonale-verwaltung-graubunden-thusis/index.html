Original description

The Office for Real Estate Assessment (AIB) carries out official reviews of properties in the canton at six locations. The reviews are opened to the owners. The data are also used by the cantonal and municipal tax authorities, the building insurance company Graubünden and the land register offices. The common revision reviews take place every 10 years. In addition, owners may request an assessment at any time. We are looking for a motivated/n team leader/in administration for the Sois and Ilanz sites on September 1, 2026 or by agreement. Evidence - Personnel and professional management of 5 employees at the locations Thusis and Ilanz - preparation, cleaning and collection of property data for real estate valuers in the assessment system - opening of valuation instructions and bills - First contact point for written and telephone requests - Efficient handling of various administration tasks - Support of the evaluation team in administrative matters
