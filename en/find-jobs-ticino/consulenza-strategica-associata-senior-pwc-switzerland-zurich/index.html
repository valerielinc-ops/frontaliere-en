Original description

Strategy& is PwC's global strategy consulting arm. In Switzerland, we primarily support leading Swiss companies in shaping their international presence - from headquarters strategy to transforming global business units. We develop tailored strategies for leading companies based on clearly differentiated competitive advantages. Our team is characterized by a particularly strong sense of camaraderie: from office events at exciting locations to regular team dinners and team-building events - we don't forget to have fun at work. As a Senior Associate in the Strategy& team, you work with leading national and international organizations from various industries. Together with your team, you develop analyses, strategies, and concepts along the entire value chain - from business and operating models to technology and digital strategies to transformation programs. You take responsibility for clearly defined project modules or workstreams and drive their content processing independently forward. Structuring problem statements, conducting sound analyses, and leading data-based action recommendations are part of your tasks. In close exchange with project management and the team, you ensure that results are consistent, sustainable, and implementation-oriented. You also work closely with clients, prepare results in a targeted manner, and support the presentation of key findings to management and decision-making bodies. In this way, you receive early responsibility for central analyses and content within project work. In addition, you actively participate in team work, support junior colleagues, and contribute to an open and collaborative team culture. Through training and project work, you continuously develop your methodological, industry, and consulting skills. In your analyses, you examine complex questions of our clients and develop structured solution approaches with a lasting value.
