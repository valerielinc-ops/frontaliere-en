  • Requirements Degree/diploma in medicine and surgery; for doctors with foreign qualifications, recognition/recording by the Federal Commission for medical professions MEBEKO is required and registration with MedReg before the start of the activity.

Application process

  • The following position is addressed to candidates wishing to apply spontaneously to the medical Oncology service of the Italian Oncology Institute as a doctor in Medical Oncology Profile IOSI employs doctors in training in the department degenti in Bellinzona and in the 4 outpatients of Bellinzona, Locarno, Lugano and Mendrisio.

Company and context

  • The organization allows to effectively combine local approach and overall vision, ensuring the population a global and proximity hospital offer, regardless of where the services are required.
  • Thanks to the commitment and expertise of more than 6,500 employees and their attention to the human relationship, EOC ensures quality health care by taking care of over 41, 000 patients who are deceased a year and providing over 600,000 outpatients.
  • The Institute is recognized as a type A training centre for obtaining an FMH medical oncology specialist and is affiliated to the Faculty of Biomedicine of the Università della Svizzera Italiana (USI).
  • In addition, the Oncology Clinic cultivates regular collaborations in the academic field with the Oncological Research Institute (IOR), the Biomedicine Research Institute (IRB), the Zurich Polytechnic (ETHZ) and the Swiss Research Group Against Cancer (SAKK).
  • In addition to the daily clinical activity, the educational offer of our Institute provides, with a close collaboration with the Hematology Clinic, a rich weekly training program with Journal Club, educational conferences and oncolunch.
  • The discussion of clinical cases is multidisciplinary and takes place within the numerous tumor boards that are held weekly and to which each assistant doctor is invited to participate actively.
  • The doctor we seek must possess motivation, initiative and spirit of collaboration.
  • He believes, like us, in the importance of putting the patient and his family at the center of all our attentions.
  • Comprehensive knowledge of the Italian language (level required at least B2) with relative registration to MedReg before the start of the activity.
  • They are also hoped, but not indispensable, good knowledge of another national language as well as Italian (French, German) and possibly English.
