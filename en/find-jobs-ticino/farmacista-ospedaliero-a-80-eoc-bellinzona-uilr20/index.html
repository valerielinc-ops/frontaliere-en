Role overview

The EOC the multisite Ticino hospital is present with its institutes on the whole cantonal territory for a total of 1’000 beds. The organization allows to effectively combine local approach and overall vision, ensuring the population a global and proximity hospital offer, regardless of where the services are required. Thanks to the commitment and expertise of over 6,500 employees and their attention to the human relationship, the EOC ensures quality health care by taking care of over 41, 000 patients who are deceased a year and providing over 600,000 outpatients.

To complete our team of the Farmacia Service (Sector Farmacia Ospedaliera) of the Institute of Pharmacological Sciences of Italian Switzerland (ISFSI) of the Regional Hospital of Bellinzona and Valli, San Giovanni headquarters, we look for a(a) Hospital Pharmacist (80%).

The person we are looking for will take care of all the pharmaceutical aspects related to the supply and supply of medicines, as well as its specialist advice to doctors and nurses. It will also be called to participate in the management of the hospital pharmacy and to collaborate in the operation of the production service of cytostatics.

  • Federal diploma of pharmacist or equivalent foreign title
  • Ideally completed by an FPH specialization in hospital pharmacy or clinical pharmacy
  • Excellent mastery of the Italian language
  • Good knowledge of at least another national language
  • Good computer skills
  • Good knowledge of the Swiss health system
  • Explain organizational, relational and communication skills
  • Spirit of initiative
  • Strong willingness to work both in a multidisciplinary team and independently
  • Comprehensive experience in hospital pharmacy and/or in the production of sterile medicines (preferential title)
  • Date of entry: 1 September 2026 or date to be agreed
  • Opportunities to work in a multidisciplinary team
  • Possibility to develop your skills and grow professionally
  • Dynamic and stimulating working environment
