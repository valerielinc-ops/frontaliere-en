Your Responsibilities

  • You act as the technical interface between Liebherr Ettlingen and Liebherr Machines Bulle to support coordination, alignment, and knowledge exchange for all remanufacturing topics
  • You ensure the consistent integration of remanufacturing requirements throughout the product development process
  • You design, document, and maintain remanufacturing products and components including CAD models, technical drawings, BOMs, and PLM data, while meeting technical, cost, quality, and schedule targets
  • You work in close cooperation with interdisciplinary internal departments and suppliers to ensure technical alignment, clarification, and co‑design for remanufacturing applications
  • You establish and continuously improve remanufacturing engineering processes through guidelines, best practices, and effective knowledge management to ensure efficient remanufacturing development Your Profile
  • You have successfully completed engineering studies in a relevant field
  • You have previous experience in combustion engine design
  • You bring strong technical expertise in combustion engines (diesel required, gasoline and/or hydrogen is a plus) and a basic ability to interpret simulation results (1D, CFD, FEA, NVH…)
  • You have advanced proficiency in IT tools, including MS Office, PTC Creo, and PLM Albatros, as well as basic knowledge of ERP systems
  • You are autonomous, innovative, goal‑driven, and comfortable working in an international environment
  • You are able and willing to travel regularly to Liebherr Ettlingen, approximately once per month

The company ​Liebherr Machines Bulle SA is the centre of excellence for combustion engines (diesel and gas engines) and hydraulic components (axial piston pumps and motors).

The company develops and produces various products, which find their use in building construction and civil engineering, mining, agriculture, decentralised energy, machine and plant engineering, as well as aerospace.
