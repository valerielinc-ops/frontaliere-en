Main responsibilities

  • and activities
  • The selected educator will work within an educational team, with the aim of promoting the physical, cognitive, emotional and social development of children.
  • The professional figure will be responsible for the following activities:
  • Educational planning: development of spaces and environments aimed at the well-being and growth of the children welcomed, in line with the Service guidelines, ensuring an inclusive and personalized approach.
  • Independent management of a group: encouraging interaction between children, stimulating collaboration, communication and mutual respect, helping to create a peaceful and welcoming context.
  • Observation and documentation: monitoring children's growth and learning progress, drafting evaluation documents and reports and maintaining continuous communication with families.
  • Collaboration with the educational team: work in synergy with the other professional figures of the Service, to guarantee a co-participatory and cohesive approach.
  • Care and assistance activities: support in daily child care activities, such as feeding, hygiene and rest, ensuring a safe and protected environment. Requirements
  • SERI recognition required if the qualification was obtained abroad.
  • Previous experience in a nursery and/or in a similar context is a preferential requirement.

Key requirements

  • SUP diploma or university qualification in the educational sector (Bachelor in social work with Educator curriculum, Degree in Educational Sciences or similar), or diploma from a tertiary high school (SSS diploma of Childhood Educator) or Federal Certificate of Ability as OSA Childhood.
  • Diploma SUP o titolo universitario nel settore educativo (Bachelor in lavoro sociale con curriculum Educatore, Laurea in Scienze dell’educazione o analogo), oppure diploma di una Scuola superiore di grado terziario (diploma SSS di Educatrice/tore dell’infanzia) oppure Attestato Federale di Capacità come OSA Infanzia.
  • Riconoscimento SEFRI richiesto nel caso in cui il titolo di studio fosse stato conseguito all’estero.
  • Esperienza pregressa in un nido dell’infanzia e/o in un contesto analogo costituisce requisito preferenziale.
  • Conoscenza dei principi pedagogici e delle metodologie educative applicabili al contesto della prima infanzia.
  • Capacità relazionale e comunicativa con i bambini e le famiglie.
  • Attitudine al lavoro di gruppo e alla collaborazione.
  • Approccio partecipativo rivolto all’implementazione del progetto pedagogico.
  • Flessibilità rivolta al buon funzionamento del Servizio.

What the company offers

  • Fixed-term or indefinite contract (depending on the need for the Service) with classification according to the CCLATAN located in band 4 of the Cantonal Scale, with the benefit of further improved conditions deriving from the CCLSUPSI.
