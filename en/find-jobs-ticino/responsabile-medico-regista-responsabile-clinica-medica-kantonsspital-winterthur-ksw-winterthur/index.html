Role overview

The Medical Polyclinic at KSW is the central interdisciplinary contact point for complex and unclear diseases in the region.

We combine broad internalist expertise with close cooperation across specialist boundaries – both at home and at home physicians in the region.

In addition to focal points in inflammatory and autoimmune diseases, we offer specialized speaking hours (including hypertension, osteology, vaccination and travel medicine), perform sonographies and punctures and also integrate the personal medical service for internal and external institutions.
