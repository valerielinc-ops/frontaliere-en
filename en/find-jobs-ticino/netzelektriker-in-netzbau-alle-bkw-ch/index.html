Original description

Your tasks • As a network electrician, you build, operate and maintain BKW's distribution network together with your colleagues. • You carry out assembly work and are responsible for maintaining and troubleshooting our systems in the low and medium voltage distribution network. • You lay cable lines, maintain overhead lines and are also there when damage needs to be repaired (on-call and fault service). What you bring with you • Completed vocational training as a network electrician EFZ, electrician EFZ, or assembly electrician EFZ. • You have experience with electrical installations or network construction. • Independent way of working and a reliable personality who values ​​working in a team. • You have a category B driving license. • Very good knowledge of spoken and written German, any additional language is a plus. At BKW Power Grid we are shaping a reliable energy future through innovative approaches, responsibility and close collaboration. Diversity is a central success factor: different backgrounds, perspectives and skills strengthen our teams and our solutions. Find out more about how we live diversity at Power Grid here. About us BKW offers its customers international complete solutions in the areas of energy, buildings and infrastructure. We employ around 11,500 people. Together we build networks, produce energy, plan and implement energy-efficient buildings and design sustainable living spaces of tomorrow. For this we need you: people with ideas and commitment. Join us and make a valuable contribution to society and our environment!
