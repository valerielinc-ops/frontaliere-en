Role overview

Polyclinic, emergencies, outpatient care, hospitalizations: from birth to old age, the Morges Hospital offers a wide range of healthcare services.

The facility has 240 acute care beds, a maternity ward with approximately 1,500 births per year, and nearly 30,000 emergency consultations.

In total, over 40 medical disciplines have been practiced within its walls for more than 150 years.
