Role overview

The Office for Imprisonment (AJV) Graubünden is a department of the Department of Justice, Security and Health of the Canton of Graubünden.

The AJV is responsible for the execution of penalties and measures as well as the implementation of the various types of detention.

In Graubünden, the execution of penalties and measures is thus regulated as a uniform topic and holistic task, in which different professions work interdisciplinary with people who have become delinquent in order to prevent further delinquency.
