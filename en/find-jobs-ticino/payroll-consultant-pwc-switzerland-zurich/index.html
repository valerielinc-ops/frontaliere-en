Original description

Responsibilities - Perform the monthly payroll on a mandate basis, taking into account all applicable regulations and laws. - Prepare the withholding tax and social security declarations as well as the payslips. - Be the primary point of contact for clients of various sizes and industries—from small SMEs to larger branches of internationally operating companies. - Support clients with their personnel administration and proactively inform them about professional updates. - Maintain contact with clients, social security authorities and withholding tax offices. - Assist with the payroll of expatriates and create the shadow payroll. - Reconcile pension fund invoices, financial accounting and payroll accounting. ## Requirements - Hold a completed commercial basic training, preferably in the fiduciary sector. - Have at least two years of experience in payroll accounting and are familiar with personnel administrative tasks. - Deepen your expertise in payroll/social security and pursue further training to become a Payroll Specialist; aim for the federal certificate as an HR or social security professional as a long‑term goal. - Proficient in German and possess very good English skills. - Have good knowledge of SAP or the Abacus payroll module and strong Excel skills. - Work accurately and efficiently and are keen to continuously expand your knowledge. - Be motivated to work in a dynamic environment and gladly apply your payroll expertise as a consultant for our clients and their interesting requests.
