Original description

Integrated or integrated into the performance and clinical excellence division of the Quality Care Service, you join the DynamO interprofessional team and participate in structuring work in support of the piloting and deployment of the program. • Your missions are structured around four essential axes Program Tools and Documentation: You identify, organize and harmonize and update program tools, media, fact sheets, records and reference documents. Development and implementation of programme reporting: You contribute to the identification of needs, the structuring of information useful for piloting, the design of monitoring media and the progressive implementation of a legible reporting for the various stakeholders. Support for communication and ownership of tools: You are involved in the design of materials that facilitate the understanding and use of the program's tools. You are contributing to the preparation of presentations and dissemination documents. Cross-cutting support to the operation of the program: You are involved in analysis, synthesis and continuous improvement, as well as in the ad hoc preparation of sessions, workshops or refunds.
