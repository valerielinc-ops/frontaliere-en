Apply Division Tool Function Sales/Marketing Related location Switzerland, Agno More than Tools Mikron Tool is an international leader in the development and production of metal cutting tools.

We are a Swiss company in continuous growth, enthusiastic about innovation and technology.

We specialize in the production of high-performance tools of small and medium size for the processing of difficult materials such as stainless steel, heat-resistant alloys, and titanium.

In one of the most modern technology centers for micromachining, holistic processing processes are also developed - including in the context of customer projects.

Thanks to our know-how, we develop sustainable, efficient, and somewhat "crazy" processing solutions with high added value for customers.
