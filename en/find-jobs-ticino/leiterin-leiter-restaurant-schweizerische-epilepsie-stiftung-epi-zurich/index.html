Role overview

The Swiss Epilepsy Foundation (EPI) is a multi-saving company.

It includes the EPI WohnWerk, the Lengg upper school, the donation Dapples, a seminar centre with restaurant and the EPI central services.

In addition, the Swiss Epilepsy Foundation, together with another foundation, forms the sponsorship of the Lengg AG Clinic, each with a clinic for epileptology and neurorehabilitation.
