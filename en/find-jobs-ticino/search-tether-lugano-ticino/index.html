More about this search for cross-border applicants

Ticino, Switzerland's sunniest canton, offers a dynamic job market with opportunities in finance, tourism, and technology. Lugano, its largest city, is a hub for cross-border workers, or 'frontalieri', commuting from northern Italy. The region's proximity to Italy and its Italian-speaking environment make it an attractive destination for professionals seeking to work in Switzerland while living in Italy. With a strong emphasis on bilingualism and a high quality of life, Ticino provides a unique blend of cultural and professional opportunities.
