You are part of a team that not only checks numbers, but also recognizes connections. At PwC in the Audit - Financial Services department (banks, asset managers, insurance companies) you analyze companies: How successful are they? Where are the opportunities and risks? What counts for us is your personality, your skills and your desire to make the world a better place.
