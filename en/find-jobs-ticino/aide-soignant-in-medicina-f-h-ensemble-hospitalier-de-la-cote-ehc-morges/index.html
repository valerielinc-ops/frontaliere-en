Role overview

Hospital, emergencies, outpatient care, hospitalizations: from birth to old age, the Morges Hospital offers a wide range of healthcare services.

The establishment has 240 acute care beds, a maternity ward, with approximately 1,500 births per year, and around 30,000 emergency consultations.

In total, more than 40 medical disciplines are practiced within its walls for over 150 years.

Surrounded by vineyards, with an incredible view of the mountains and the lake, the Morges Hospital welcomes you in a warm and dynamic environment.

  • Requirements We are looking for the Morges Hospital a: - Nurse Assistant in Medicine (F/M) at 80-100% - Start of service as soon as possible or to be agreed.

The Medicine Service on the 1st floor has 40 beds, including 6 dedicated to palliative care, and ensures an interdisciplinary care of patients with palliative care in general and acute.

The Medicine Service on the 2nd floor includes 43 beds, half of which are dedicated to acute care for the elderly, with an interdisciplinary care in an adapted environment that aims to promote and maintain autonomy in daily activities.

  • What we offer - 5 weeks of annual leave, 6 weeks from the age of 50.

Possibility of buying an additional week of leave, as well as 10 paid holidays.

  • Several proposals offering flexibility (unpaid leave, part-time hours, paid hours of change, etc.).
  • Diploma of auxiliary health, of nurse assistant recognized by the CRS or a AFP of ASA
  • 4 years of experience in CTR, EMS, home care, acute care Good organizational skills A sense of listening and excellent relational skills A good emotional control, of proactivity and flexibility A flexibility and availability to work day and night Good communication skills Good teamwork skills
