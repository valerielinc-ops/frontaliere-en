Role overview

Hospital Intercantonal de la Broye (HIB) With its 800 staff members, the Hospital Intercantonal de la Broye (HIB) ensures a mission of acute care and rehabilitation.

Its ambition is to be a dynamic, innovative and excellence-oriented healthcare institution for the benefit of the Broye population.

To tackle the numerous challenges ahead, the HIB is looking for a full-time physiotherapist: Full-time Physiotherapist Your mission The physiotherapy service ensures the welcome and clinical care of hospitalized and ambulatory patients in the fields of orthopedic and trauma surgery, physical medicine and rehabilitation as well as in the field of acute care.

Missions

  • Evaluate, perform and adapt care according to a specific treatment plan for each patient
  • Prevent complications related to the patient's health by implementing relevant intervention measures
  • Work in a team while respecting interdisciplinarity and collegiality
  • Participate in the supervision of students
  • Participate in research projects Your strengths Professional requirements
