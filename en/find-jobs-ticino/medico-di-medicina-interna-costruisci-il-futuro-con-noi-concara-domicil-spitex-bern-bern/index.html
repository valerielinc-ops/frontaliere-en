Role overview

Tasks: Are you looking for a job in which meaningful and patient-centered medicine is the focus and teamwork is important?

The medical service of Domicil Bern AG was founded in spring 2024 and is still being developed - with room for participation and new ideas.

If you want to live your passion for clinical work, are looking for an alternative to everyday hospital life, or if the traditional general practice doesn't suit you right now, this position could be the right next step for you!

As a doctor, you and an Advanced Practice Nurse (APN) look after the residents at the locations assigned to you and carry out regular visits there, while being mobile.

In addition, you will be responsible, alternately with your colleagues, for the medical telephone background service, for which there is also the option of working from home.

As a medical contact for nurses and APNs, you will ensure high-quality, patient-centered care for our residents and make informed decisions quickly in urgent situations.

Shape the future of medical care with our young, dynamic team - we look forward to getting to know you!

You can find further information and an insight into the everyday working life of our doctors at jobs.concara/medcara and domicilbern.ch/mediziner-dienst Requirements:

  • Specialist in general internal medicine or general practitioner
  • Professional experience in geriatrics or palliative medicine is an advantage
  • Category B driving license desired
  • Team player, dynamic and solution-oriented personality
  • Interest in helping to shape and develop We offer:
  • 42-hour week at 100% and the possibility of working half days
  • No late, night and weekend shifts
  • Flexible annual working hours, innovative working time models and the possibility of working from home
  • Workload can be selected between 30-100%
  • Employer share of premiums for daily sickness benefit insurance and pension fund higher than statutory regulations
  • Lots of design options in an innovative and dynamic environment Domicil offers seniors inpatient care and support services.

With more than 20 locations and around 1,600 employees, Domicil is the number 1 in long-term care in the canton of Bern.

Under the umbrella of Concara, together with Spitex Bern, we are committed to providing integrated all-round care.
