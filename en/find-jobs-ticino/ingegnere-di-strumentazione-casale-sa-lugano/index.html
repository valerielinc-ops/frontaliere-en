Original description

Casale SA is a leading company specializing in the design and implementation of innovative plants for the production of green ammonia, ammonia, nitrates, phosphates, urea, methanol, melamine, and syngas. Committed to sustainability and cutting-edge technology, we strive to deliver high-quality solutions to our global clients. As part of this vision, we are seeking a **Instrument Engineer** to join our Instrument & Electrical Section. This role offers the opportunity to work in an international, detail-oriented, and passionate environment, contributing to the success of our projects. The **Instrument Engineer**is responsible for the development and integration of instrumentation and automation systems for Casale plants. This includes leading the design, specification, and follow-up of instrumentation activities to ensure optimal plant functionality, safety, and reliability across all project phases. **Project & Technical Coordination** - Lead and coordinate instrumentation engineering activities for complex projects, ensuring alignment with project requirements and timelines. Provide technical support during the bidding phase, ensuring a clear understanding of client expectations and project scope. - Define technical requisitions and specifications for instrumentation and automation systems to ensure performance, compliance, and safety of Casale plants. **Procurement & Vendor Interaction** - Support the Procurement Department in the supplier selection process through technical alignment and bid evaluations. - Review and approve vendor drawings, documentation, and testing procedures related to instrumentation equipment. - Conduct technical follow-up on purchasing orders, resolving any technical issues that may arise. **Engineering Deliverables** - Prepare and verify general specifications, instrument data sheets, and control system documentation during the executive project phase. - Ensure compliance with applicable codes, standards, and project-specific requirements.
