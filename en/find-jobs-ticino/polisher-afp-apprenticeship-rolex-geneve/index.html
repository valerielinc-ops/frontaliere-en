Original description

Motivated by a Polisher apprenticeship? Ready to become a reference in your trade thanks to high-quality training in a leading company? Join us! The Polisher intervenes at the end of the production of watchmaking components. They perform various finishing operations (polishing, satin-finishing, brightening, etc.) designed to give their surface the final aesthetic appearance desired by the client. They carry out all the operations specific to their trade in an autonomous and reliable manner, ensuring that they meet the aesthetic quality, dimensional and productivity criteria expected in their production environment. Profile • Visual acuity and manual dexterity • Ability to concentrate and be patient • Interest in working with metals To apply If you are motivated to take on the challenge, do not hesitate to apply. Your application must include: • a curriculum vitae • a cover letter • your school reports for the last three years Guided by experienced trainers, you will be able to evolve in an exceptional working environment. This training will ensure you excellent prospects. Rolex is committed to offering you more than just an apprenticeship: a real opportunity for personal and professional development. #LI-DNI
