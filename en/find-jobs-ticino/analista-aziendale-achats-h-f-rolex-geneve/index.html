Role overview

Introduction For our Information Systems division , we are looking for a Business Analyst (H/F) whose main mission will be to design applications and maintain processes related to Purchasing activities.

Responsibilities

  • Ensure the operational link between the business and the Build teams
  • Contribute to the transformation programme aimed at implementing the management processes and tools between Rolex and its suppliers through a SRM portal (Ivalua)
  • Monitor business needs and requirements (functional specifications), in collaboration with the Business Process Owners
  • Participate in the implementation of the solution, including the testing and business support phases
  • Ensure compliance with the established processes, including interfaces between Ivalua and several SAP instances
  • Coordinate actions with SI teams
  • Ensure that the standard good practices of the solution are respected
  • Develop and maintain applications
  • Manage incidents
  • Analyze, encrypt and plan evolution requests related to the Purchasing function Profile
  • University training in management computing, or equivalent training
  • Technical and functional experience in the field of SRM (Ivalua) and/or ERP (SAP)
  • Ability to reformulate users' needs for computer functionality
  • Methodology and good knowledge of session and communication facilitation tools
  • Rigor and good analysis capacity
  • A sense of communication and ability to work in a team
  • Good relational and adaptive skills
  • Initiative
  • Respect for confidentiality Conclusion We offer you a stimulating environment, excellent working conditions, flexible working time solutions and top social benefits.

Thank you for sending a complete application package (CV, cover letter, certificates and diplomas).
