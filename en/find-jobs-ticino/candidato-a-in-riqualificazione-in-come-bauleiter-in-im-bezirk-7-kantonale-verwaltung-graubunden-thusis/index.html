Role overview

The civil engineering office is responsible for the design, construction, maintenance and operation of the cantonal roads as well as the hydraulic engineering and slow transport in Graubünden.

We also take care of the operational maintenance of the national roads on the cantonal territory.

The canton’s road infrastructure comprises over 1,450 artificial buildings, including bridges at airy heights of up to 90 meters and tunnels of up to 3 kilometers in length.
