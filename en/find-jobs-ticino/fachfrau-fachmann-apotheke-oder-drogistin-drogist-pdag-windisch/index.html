Role overview

The Aargau Psychiatric Services (PDAG) is one of the most modern and largest psychiatric hospitals in Switzerland and has been a stock corporation owned by the Canton of Aargau since 2004.

As a specialist center for the entire PDAG, the hospital pharmacy ensures the needs-based procurement and supply of all inpatient departments at the Windisch location (around 500 beds) as well as the outpatient clinics, centers and consultation hours of the PDAG with medicines, medical devices, protective materials, disinfectants, drinking and tube feeding as well as infusion solutions.
