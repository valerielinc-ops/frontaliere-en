Original description

The Zweckverband Pflege und Betreuung Mittleres Tösstal provides the population of the four association municipalities Turbenthal, Wila, Wildberg and Zell with inpatient and ambulatory care, care and residential services according to the service mandate. Spitex Mittleres Tösstal provides professional outpatient care and care for the inhabitants of the mentioned communities. We are a progressive and dynamic organization with over 90 employees and integrate interdisciplinary specialist teams, such as acute and long-term care, psychosocial care, palliative care, housekeeping and care as well as Spitex Plus services. To strengthen our dedicated team, we are looking for an Nurse FaGe or HF for the evening service with a workload of approx. 20-40 % • We offer: A dedicated, far-sighted and interdisciplinary team Open, warm and human team culture A responsible, varied and independent work Careful training Digital working instruments Individual, vocational and subject-specific training opportunities Monthly team meetings Good pay and above-average social benefits and at least. 5 weeks holiday • Your profile: Completed training as a nurse Health or HF - or study Evening duty Self-employed, responsible and team-oriented working methods High level of social and professional competence Flexibility and resilience Computer skills Cat B driving licence, own car or e-bike This position is also suitable for re-entry into care and for family managers and students.
