  • The work model for the role is: hybrid (ideally within presence of current business locations ) You will be mainly accountable for:
  • Technical support for ABB experts worldwide, ABB business partners and End customers with Salesforce as the CRM (Customer relationship management) platform and participation in ensuring 24/7 availability of remote support line
  • Leading resolution teams and handling customer communication for support cases where different stakeholders (e.g., System experts, R&D, Quality etc.) build a resolution team according to severity, complexity, and urgency
  • Leading and support with type test activities, component specifications, life cycle solutions and product engineering etc.
  • Supporting customers with grid code compliance studies (e.g.
  • Power Factory, PSS/E, PSCAD) and digital transformation (e.g. cyber security, digital twin).
  • Training and onboarding support can be offered in this matter.
