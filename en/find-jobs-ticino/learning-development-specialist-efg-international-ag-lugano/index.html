Role overview

As a key resource of the Learning & Development team, the <i>Learning & Development Specialist</i> will manage end-to-end the apprentices and interns programmes at EFG, ensuring a best-in-class onboarding and development experience, and provide support and coordinate selected global learning initiatives.

The resource will interact with all HR functional areas and expected to become a reference person within the organization on the assigned activities.

This is an excellent opportunity for someone willing to work in an international, fast-paced and client-oriented HR team within a global private banking group.

Learning & Development Program Delivery (Apprentices & Interns)

  • Design, implement, and continuously improve apprentices and interns programmes, ensuring alignment with EFG’s standards and local requirements.
  • Manage the end-to-end hiring process for apprentices in Switzerland and interns globally, partnering with line managers and HR Business Partners/local HR.
  • Coordinate and facilitate onboarding and training sessions to build readiness and engagement for apprentices.
  • University degree, preferably in human resources, organizational development, economics, or a related field
  • 3+ of relevant experience in early career talent acquisition or development within structured HR departments of international companies
