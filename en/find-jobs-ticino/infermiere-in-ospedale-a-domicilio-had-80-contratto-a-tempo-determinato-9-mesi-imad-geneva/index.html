Role overview

In order to avoid a stay in the hospital or to promote an early exit, home hospitalisation (HAD) is intended for anyone, from infant to adult, for whom technical and/or complex hospital-type care is required.

Thus, HAD operates throughout the canton 365d/year and 24h/24, when care is deemed possible at home and specific expertise in a specialized field is required (pediatry, adult and paediatric oncology, nephrology, wound care, etc.).

The offer of HAD services is part of the relay and complement of the provision of services by the IMAD home maintenance teams.
