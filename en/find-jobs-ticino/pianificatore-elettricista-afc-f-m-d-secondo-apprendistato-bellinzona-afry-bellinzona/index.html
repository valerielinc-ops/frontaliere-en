Job description Starting from August 2026 we offer additional training in our subsidiary in Bellinzona: AFC (f/m/d) (second apprenticeship)

BELLINZONA During the apprenticeship you will learn, among other things, the following activities: Processing of projects of plants and electrical schematics with the help of CAD software Development of solutions and the preparation of the relative plans of detail Dimensionment of electrical lines and electro complex systems Technical coordination with the participants of the project, internal and external to the company Conduction of the execution on site and coordination with the other disciplines Independent design of installations For the apprenticeship, you should preferably bring the following profile: Completion of primary school Ended training as an AFC electrician or AFC mounting electrician Interests / strengths in geometry, mathematics and physics Interest in technical design and ability to work accurately Good awareness of space and technical understanding Interest in building processes of infrastructure, tunnels, railway and road installations.
