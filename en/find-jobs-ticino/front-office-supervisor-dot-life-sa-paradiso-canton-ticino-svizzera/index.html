Original description

DOT LIFE is a management company that takes care of an exclusive selection of four hotels located on Lake Lugano, Switzerland. With a team of around 300 employees, the company is responsible for prestigious structures such as Villa Principe Leopoldo, Park Hotel Principe, Kurhaus Cademario and Villa Sassa. Each property embodies the DOT Lifestyle philosophy, offering a stay experience that promotes holistic well-being and redefines hospitality standards. The hotels combine Swiss luxury, refined experiences, wellness and haute cuisine, ensuring personalized assistance and excellent services. We are looking for a full-time front office supervisor for our structure located in Paradiso, Switzerland. In this role, the candidate will organize and supervise the front office team, ensuring excellent customer service and correct management of daily reception activities. They will be responsible for managing bookings, welcoming and catering to guests' requests, ensuring high hospitality standards and efficient and professional communication with all staff and guests. Requirements: - Proven experience in supervisory roles in the hospitality industry and developed team coordination skills. - Practical skills to manage reception and booking tasks with precision and punctuality. - Advanced customer service and communication skills to ensure a great guest experience and collaborate with staff. - Knowledge of best practices in the hospitality industry, with possible use of management software for structure management. - The ability to fluently speak multiple languages, including Italian, English, and German.
