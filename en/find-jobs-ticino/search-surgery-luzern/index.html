More about this search for cross-border applicants

Luzern, nestled between the Swiss Alps and the Italian border, offers a dynamic job market for professionals in surgery, particularly for those who work as **frontalieri**—cross-border workers who live in northern Italy and commute daily to Switzerland. With eight active listings in the field, the region provides a mix of public hospitals, private clinics, and specialized surgical centers, many of which value experience in trauma, orthopedics, or general surgery. The demand for skilled surgeons remains strong, driven by Switzerland’s reputation for advanced medical care and a steady influx of patients. However, candidates should verify residency requirements, work permits, and commuting logistics before applying, as these factors influence both stability and quality of life in the area.
