Job Description

The Strategic Planning Officer will be part of the Strategy & Corporate Development Team in Lugano and will contribute to run an integrated planning, reporting and performance review process to facilitate value-driven business decision-making. In his role as a business partner and challenger of business performance he will focus on value-added decision support through granular analysis of performance root causes and by providing insights on value creation levers.

The Strategic Planning Officer will play an active role in the business performance dialogues, support business development and provide value-added decision support using data modelling and forecasting intelligence.

Main responsibilities

  • Business analyst and power user of MI data
  • Establish and compile presentations to senior management
  • Take active role in the business performance dialogues
  • Design and maintain data models for analysis and forecasting
  • Active collaboration with Planning & Controlling unit during budgeting and forecasting process
  • Develop strong relationship with business management and business controllers to build a role as business partner
  • Develop recurring benchmarking analysis in collaboration with external advisors

  • Key collaboration in corporate development projects and strategic initiatives, including M&A transactions

Skills and experience

  • Have professional experience in Financial Services Industry, in particular in Corporate Finance activities
  • Ability to understand large datasets and experience of data modelling, providing accurate data analysis
  • Power user of MS-Excel and MS-PowerPoint; Knowledge of using Excel Macros, VBA, Python, Power BI as a plus
  • Quick learner, accurate and efficient
  • Proactive attitude and willingness to take on new knowledge and skills
  • Ability to work under pressure and adapt to unforeseen work demands
  • Good communication skills to communicate effectively with internal stakeholders at all levels

  • Fluent in English and fluent in Italian
