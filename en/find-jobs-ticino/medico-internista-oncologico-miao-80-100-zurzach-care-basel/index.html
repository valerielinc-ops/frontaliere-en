Original description

We are expanding our modern internal-oncological rehabilitation clinic at our location in Basel. What makes this position special is the exciting combination of internal and oncological rehabilitation and post-acute care. Your work will remain varied and interesting. Your profile: - Swiss or MEBEKO-recognized medical degree, preferably with experience in the field of general internal medicine. - Personality: Bring professional and social competence for good interdisciplinary collaboration and work structured and independently.
