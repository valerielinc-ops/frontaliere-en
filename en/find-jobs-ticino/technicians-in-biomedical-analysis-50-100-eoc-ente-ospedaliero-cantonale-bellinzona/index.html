  • Ausschreibung L’EOC the multisite hospital of Ticino is present with its institutes on the entire cantonal territory for a total of 1’000 beds.

Company and context

  • The organization allows to effectively combine local approach and overall vision, ensuring the population a global and proximity hospital offer, regardless of where the services are required.
  • Thanks to the commitment and expertise of more than 6,500 employees and their attention to the human relationship, EOC ensures quality health care by taking care of more than 41,000 patients per year and providing more than 600,000 outpatients.
  • The Institute of Integrated Diagnostics of Italian Switzerland (IDISI) brings together the Clinics of Radiology, Nuclear Medicine, Pathology and Laboratory Medicine and, with its approximately 600 collaborators and almost 4 million examinations and analysis carried out each year, offers a comprehensive and high-quality diagnostic service, responding to the needs of patients resident throughout the cantonal territory.
  • Overcoming traditional specialist barriers, IDISI adopts a multidisciplinary approach to favor more efficient and personalized diagnostic-therapeutic paths, capable of responding to the specific needs of each patient.
  • In addition, the Institute is oriented towards university education and promoting biomedical research.
  • The Laboratory Medicine Clinic offers on several locations (Mendrisio, Lugano, Locarno, Bellinzona, Faido, Acquarossa) complete analysis services on biological samples whose results can serve as support for diagnosis and monitoring of diseases.
  • Most of the more than 3 million tests carried out each year are carried out on human, environmental and veterinary biological samples.
  • The Pathology and cytology Clinic provides the service of pathological anatomy for all EOC as well as private clinics and medical studies throughout the canton.
  • More than 80,000 cases are treated each year, covering about 50,000 patients resident in the canton.
  • To complete our teams of the Laboratory Medicine Clinic and the Pathology and cytology Clinic we look for Technicians in biomedical analysis (50-100%) year graduates 2026 Profile: Clinical staff can manage time efficiently and effectively.
