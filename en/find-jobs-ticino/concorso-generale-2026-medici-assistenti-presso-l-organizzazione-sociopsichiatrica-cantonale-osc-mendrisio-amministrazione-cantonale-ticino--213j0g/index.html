Tasks

  • performs, organizes and checks the individual therapeutic plan for the patient according to the indications received by the direct/upper of the patient, referring to the diagnostic and pharmacotherapeutic framework, guaranteeing in collaboration with the multidisciplinary team, the patient's taking charge
  • performs and realizes medical-psychiatric or pedopsichiatric visits, where necessary in collaboration with psychologists/hes, for patients, following the therapeutic plan and implementing it in compliance with its professional skills
  • actively collaborates with doctors of psychiatric and somatic hospitals and doctors with private practice, in accordance with the medical psychiatrist head of the OSC service, regarding the somatic care of the patients and according to the indications of the direct/higher
  • is required to train in the field of specialty in psychiatry and psychotherapy or pedopsichiatry according to FMH provisions
  • realizes, in accordance with the direct/upper, relations with other public and private structures operating on the territory in order to create the ideal conditions for network intervention

  • drafts in collaboration with his/her superiors direct/and psychiatric medical expert to him/her entrusted and supervised
  • guarantees the correct editing of TARDOC and TARPSY performance
