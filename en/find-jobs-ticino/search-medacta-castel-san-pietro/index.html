More about this search for cross-border applicants

In Ticino, Italian-speaking Switzerland, job opportunities span from healthcare and engineering to hospitality and administrative roles. For cross-border workers—known as *frontalieri*—this region offers a blend of high wages, tax advantages, and proximity to northern Italy, making it an attractive destination for professionals seeking stability and quality of life. The local labor market thrives in sectors like technology, finance, and skilled trades, with many listings tailored to those who commute daily between Italy and Switzerland. Whether you’re drawn to urban hubs like Lugano or smaller towns with strong industry ties, Ticino’s job market provides diverse pathways for career advancement.
