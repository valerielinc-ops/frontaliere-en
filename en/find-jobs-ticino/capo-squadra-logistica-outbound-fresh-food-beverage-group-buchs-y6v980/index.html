Main responsibilities

  • You lead and motivate a team consisting of 3 employees and 3 learners
  • You monitor and optimize goods receipt and goods exit processes
  • With your expertise, you will ensure the timely acceptance of goods, testing, commissioning and shipping
  • Compliance with customs and export regulations and the optimization of export processes are also your responsibility
  • You are the interface between different departments such as purchasing, warehouse, production and external partners
  • You are SAP Key User in the Logistics Area and the first contact point for questions and uncertainties
