Role overview

The IT Treasury and Trading team delivers efficient, effective support and IT solutions for applications and processes in client order execution, trading, treasury, and lending/collateral management. The team comprises both functional and technical experts supporting a Trading/Treasury room of approximately 50 traders. This role ensures continuity in client services and drives ongoing improvements in deliverable quality.

  • Run the Bank: end-to-end support on the IT trading platforms used for Securities Lending & Collateral, Islamic Finance, Securities and Derivatives execution and trading, spanning from application maintenance to analysis and resolution on daily incidents, liaising with external vendors where applicable.
  • Run the Bank: act as entry point to trading desk for broader issues that require liaising with other IT departments to cover the full stack (core banking and other linked applications, IT infa server, database, network, …) or with Operations teams (Securities, Derivatives, FX back office, middle office)
  • Change the Bank: coordinate and execute various IT projects aimed at evolving or upgrading the IT trading platforms, either developed and released internally or by application providers: collection of business needs, solution design and documentation, development where applicable and/or vendor coordination, creation of test cases catalogue and execution of IT tests, support to user acceptance test, rollout and post production support
  • A university degree, preferably in computer science, finance or an equivalent educational background
  • Very good knowledge of programming languages (Java; Pyton; C++, SQL, Powershell, …), application server (Windows mainly, Linux) and database systems (Oracle, MS SQL)
  • Good knowledge of financial products (Forex, Securities, Derivatives, Sec Lending) and main bank processes (order/execution on the market of the plain vanilla products)
