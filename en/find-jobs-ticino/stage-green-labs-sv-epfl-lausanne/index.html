Original description

The School of Life Sciences (SV) Sustainability Office is responsible for environmental sustainability within this faculty in EPFL. Working closely with the Dean’s office, we collaborate with the scientific, administrative, and student community to implement the “Green Lab” vision. This vision promotes environmentally and socially responsible research without compromising scientific excellence. Our strong partnership with EPFL Sustainability enables us to develop impactful projects tailored to the specific needs of life sciences research. In this context, we are looking for a motivated intern to contribute to the many Green Labs SV projects and events. Mission Your mission will be to support the SV Sustainability Office in implementing EPFL’s Green Labs vision.
