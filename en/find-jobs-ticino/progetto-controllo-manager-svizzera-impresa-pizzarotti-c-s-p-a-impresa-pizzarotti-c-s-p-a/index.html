Impresa Pizzarotti & C. S.p.A. was founded in Parma in 1910 as an individual company by Gino Pizzarotti, later transformed in 1945 into a limited liability company by geom. Pietro Pizzarotti, father of current President Cav. I work Paolo Pizzarotti. It became in 1961 Company for shares with a share capital of 250 million euros. Today it is at the fourth generation. Over the years, the Pizzarotti Company has been the protagonist of a continuous evolution, until it becomes one of the main Italian contractors. The company has always aimed at a constant development in the construction of complex works: road and highway infrastructure also in concession, work in tunnels, airports, dams, industrial infrastructures, railway constructions and sanitary and residential construction.
