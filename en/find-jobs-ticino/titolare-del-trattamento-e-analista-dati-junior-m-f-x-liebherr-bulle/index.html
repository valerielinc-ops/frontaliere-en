Your Responsibilities

  • As a Junior Controller & Data Analyst (m/f/x), you monitor expenses by cost center,
  • You analyze and comment on profit and loss variances (financial and analytical),
  • You support the preparation of budgets by cost center,
  • You participate in monthly closings as well as in the preparation of future plans,
  • You develop KPIs and performance BI reports/tools in collaboration with the company’s functional managers,
  • You carry out specific analyses and drive optimization initiatives, Your Profil
  • You hold a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Business Administration, Finance, or a related field,
  • You have at least 2 to 3 years of experience in controlling or data analysis, ideally in an industrial environment,
  • You are fluent in French and English and have good knowledge of German,
  • You are proficient in the MS Office suite and have solid knowledge of an ERP system,
  • You are autonomous, flexible, and able to work effectively within a team,
  • You stand out through your analytical skills, communication abilities, and your capacity to assert yourself.
