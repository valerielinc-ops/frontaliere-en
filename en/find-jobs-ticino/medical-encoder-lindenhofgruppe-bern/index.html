Original description

Are your sense of responsibility and self-initiative on your strengths? Would you like to be a member of a leading and successful team? Then fit us perfectly. As a Lindenhof group, we are only so successful because more than 800 nursing professionals, about 100 hospital doctors, 300 staff doctors and 1300 other employees work in hand – and can always rely on each other. The focus of high-quality medicine is always a strong team. And to strengthen this team we are looking for you! Tasks: Exciting challenges await you • Self-employed encoding of stationary cases of all specialist departments according to SwissDRG • Close cooperation with doctors, care and financial department • Analysis and clarification of complex coding cases • Processing of cost-carrying remittances • Participation in further development of coding standards and internal processes Requirements: What distinguishes you for the job • Multiannual professional experience in medical encoding (SwissDRG), preferably with completed additional qualification as idgenössische/r Medizinische/r Codierer/in • Basic medical education (e.g. care, NDS HF, doctor or the like) • Very good knowledge of medical terminology • High personal responsibility and team orientation • Very good IT user knowledge (ID-Diacos, BC, KISIM, MS-Office, etc.) We offer: In the Lindenhof group you can take early responsibility and participate in pioneering solutions. Thanks to your functional flair and your fine feeling for people, you act skillfully as a link and offer comprehensive services for the entire group. With your strong service orientation in the sense of our customers, you relieve the teams in our hospitals and thus provide immediate added value for our patients.
