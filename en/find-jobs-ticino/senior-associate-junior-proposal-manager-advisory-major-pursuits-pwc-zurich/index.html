Role overview

The Advisory Activation Hub is PwC Switzerland's centralised proposal and pursuit function supporting our Advisory practice.

The team works closely with partners, directors, and engagement teams across industries to shape winning client proposals.

Combining project management, storytelling and digital expertise, the team supports complex and strategic pursuits end to end, while continuously improving tools, processes and ways of working to enhance proposal quality and efficiency.
