Legal Counsel (m/w/d) 80%-100%, Zurich, unlimited ETH Zurich stands for excellent teaching, groundbreaking basic research and the application of the results for the benefit of society. Founded in 1855, it is now one of the world's best scientific and technical universities. The legal service of ETH Zurich is part of the General Secretariat of ETH Zurich, which is assigned to the school management with the tasks of a general staff office. To strengthen our team, we are looking for a Legal Counsel with a focus on data protection and the principle of publicity. In addition, the public principle has established itself in the federal administration and the great interest of the public in ETH Zurich leads to increasing numbers of requests based on the public law. Therefore, we are looking for a resilient person with a very pragmatic way of working, who has experiences in data protection and ideally with information/publicity laws and wants to deepen this in close cooperation with ETH Zurich's data protection and public policy officers. At the same time, a suitable person should be interested in the diverse further legal issues of a leading university. Its versatile task therefore includes, among other things, the examination and writing of contracts, regulations, instructions, orders, legal regulations and rules of procedure and the advice on various governance and compliance issues.
