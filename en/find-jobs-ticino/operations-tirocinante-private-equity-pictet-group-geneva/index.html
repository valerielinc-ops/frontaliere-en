Role overview

Your team Pictet Alternative Advisors is a specialist in alternative investments, recognized for its expertise in hedge funds, private equity, and global real estate.

Based in Geneva and London, the team designs, manages, and advises on portfolios of alternative investment solutions for institutional and private clients.

As part of its ongoing development, Pictet Alternative Advisors is looking to strengthen its team by recruiting a Junior Operations Intern for a 6-month internship, starting as soon as possible, to support the growth of its private assets funds.
