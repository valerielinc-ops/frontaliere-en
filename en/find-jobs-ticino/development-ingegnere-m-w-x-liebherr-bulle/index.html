Role overview

Your Responsibilities

  • You design and analyze new engine technologies and components
  • You perform engine 1D-Simulation using AMESim or GT-Power
  • You post-treat measurement results with Matlab, Concerto, and other tools
  • You carry out mechanical conception and create CAD models of components for prototyping
  • You design and dimension components for engines and pumps
  • You define the system part list and supplier management
  • You present simulation and measurement results
  • You develop tools and models for data post-treatment
  • You test the engine and complete power train
  • You document the developed library for component modeling
  • You ensure continuous optimization and validation of the models using measurement data Your Profile
  • You hold a Bachelor’s or a Master’s degree in mechanical engineering or similar
  • You have at least 3 years of professional experience in combustion engines or a related field
  • You bring strong expertise in simulation tools such as Matlab/Simulink, AMESim & Simulation X along with proficiency in Creo and Concerto
  • You are analytical, rigorous, and proactive, and you excel in both independent work and collaborative environments
