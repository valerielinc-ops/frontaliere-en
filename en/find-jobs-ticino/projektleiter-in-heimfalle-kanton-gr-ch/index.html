The Office for Energy and Transport is the Cantonal Competence Center for Energy and Public Transport Issues. The use of hydropower and other renewable energy sources as well as a sustainable and efficient use of energy are among the core topics of the Energy and Transport Office. We primarily process the approval of concessions and projects to use hydropower. ## Mansioni - Cooperation in the implementation of the cantonal hydropower strategy - Project management of the various homefalls, on the one hand as project manager, on the other hand as representative of the cantonal in the home commissions - Thematic leadership in matters of the recovery and operation of the cantonal power plant portfolio, and in the construction of the corresponding structures - Development of decision bases in connection with home cases or Concession renewals - Economic evaluations of power plant companies - consultation of municipalities in home affairs, communication with municipalities - coordination with external service providers and industry partners in matters of home affairs - data management of individual power plant installations (procurement, evaluation and management) with regard to home cases ## • Requisiti - Higher education or technical college degree in the field of construction or energy July 2026 or by agreement a project manager/in home cases.
