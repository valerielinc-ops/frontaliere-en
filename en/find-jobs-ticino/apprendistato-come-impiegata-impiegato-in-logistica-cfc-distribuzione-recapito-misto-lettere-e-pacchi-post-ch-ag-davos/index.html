Original description

Do you like to start the day early, do you love being in the fresh air and do you enjoy putting a smile on people's faces? Then training in delivery (specializing in distribution) is just right for you! During the three-year basic training, you will acquire the tools you need to successfully enter the diverse world of logistics. Your training Early in the morning you sort your letters and packages and load your delivery vehicle for the subsequent delivery tour. After that, you'll be out and about independently, bringing and picking up all kinds of shipments and taking care of our customers' concerns. Before you start your well-deserved end of work, you will complete various follow-up work at your training position. During your training, you will be supervised by professionals, introduced into practice and continually developed further. In addition, with financial support from the Swiss Post, you will either acquire a category A1 motorcycle driving license or a category B motor vehicle driving license and learn how to drive a forklift truck. With various internal stations, a placement in a warehouse, as well as individual elective modules according to your wishes, we complete your broad logistics training. This is what you bring with you We are looking for young people with enthusiasm, joy and motivation who want to make a difference. You should fulfill the following points: You are enthusiastic about physical activity and working in the fresh air. In addition, you enjoy dealing with people, are reliable, honest and secretive. You are ready to obtain your learner's license A1 or B by the start of your training at the latest. Compulsory schooling must be completed with a sufficient grade point average at the lowest level. When you start your course you will have language level B2 in German. Does this apply to you? Then we are looking for you!
