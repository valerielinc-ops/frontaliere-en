Original description

The digital audit consists of analyzing and optimizing our clients' business and IT processes, in order to help them create sustainable value for a digital future. Our job comes down to building trust: helping customers understand the benefits – not just the risks – that new technologies offer and giving them the knowledge and confidence to entrust bright minds to lead their technology transformation. Evaluation of internal controls of business and IT processes during the audit of the annual accounts of national and international clients, as part of the "Audit 4.0" approach. Analysis of transactions, master data and accounts using tools to detect irregularities, contribution to process mining projects. Active participation in consulting activities aimed at optimizing internal control systems and risk management. Collaboration on special IT and process related projects for our clients. Development within a dynamic work environment, promoting team spirit, trust and mutual support. Regular receipt of constructive feedback from colleagues and superiors. Potential employment opportunities as an Associate thereafter. Holder, or in the process of being so, of a HES Bachelor's degree (business informatics) or a Master's degree (information systems) Native French speaker with excellent English skills. Excellent synthesis and analysis skills. With a great desire to learn, with the objective of continuing training with a view to obtaining the CISA (Certified Information Systems Auditor) diploma. Significant communication and teamwork skills, while demonstrating a strong sense of customer service.
