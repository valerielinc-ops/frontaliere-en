Company and context

  • The Position In Roche’s Pharmaceutical Research and Early Development organization (pRED ) , we make transformative medicines for patients in order to tackle some of the world’s toughest unmet healthcare needs.
  • At pRED, we are united by our mission to transform science into medicines.
  • Together, we create a culture defined by curiosity, responsibility and humility, where our talented people are empowered and inspired to bring forward extraordinary life-changing innovation at speed.
  • The pRED Oncology team is focused on discovering and developing step-change innovations in cancer immunotherapy and molecular targeted therapy for cancers where there are few treatment options, or where treatment choices simply don’t exist.
  • The position is located in the department of Early Clinical Development Oncology (ECDO) The opportunity You are leading a team of highly qualified clinical scientists and medical writers and report to the Head of Oncology Early Clinical Development.
  • You are mentoring and enabling the clinical scientists and medical writers to support the planning, implementation and execution of the clinical development plans of the pRED Oncology programs.
  • The role contributes to the strategic direction and culture of Oncology Early Clinical Development as part of the Oncology Early Clinical Development Leadership Team as well as a member of other guidance or governance bodies and committees (within and/or outside the Oncology Disease Therapeutic Area). In your role, you will:
  • Lead and develop a high-performing team of Clinical Scientists and Medical Writers, fostering a culture of ownership and coaching to ensure talent reaches its full potential.
  • Strategically oversee clinical and regulatory excellence across the Oncology Early Development portfolio, ensuring high-quality documentation (Protocols, IBs, CSRs) and optimal resource allocation.
  • Drive innovative trial design and data interpretation in close partnership with Medical and Biomarker leadership to optimize clinical development plans and dose selection.
