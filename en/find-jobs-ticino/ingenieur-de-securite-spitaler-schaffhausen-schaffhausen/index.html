The hospitals Schaffhausen are in the midst of the digital transformation.

As an ICT Security Engineer, you play a central role in protecting our critical infrastructure and make a direct contribution to safe hospital operations.

Main responsibilities

  • Responsibility for the operation and further development of our ICT security solutions
  • Monitoring security systems, early detection of threats and initiating targeted measures
  • Analysis and handling of security incidents, including ensuring sustainable resolution
  • Identification of vulnerabilities, assessment of risks and coordination of appropriate countermeasures
  • In your role, you advise ICT projects and accompany new technologies from a security point of view
  • Active further development of our security processes and architecture
  • Support for risk analysis and the expansion and operation of the ISMS

Company and context

  • Promotion of security awareness within the entire organization Requirements

  • Several years of experience in a similar function
