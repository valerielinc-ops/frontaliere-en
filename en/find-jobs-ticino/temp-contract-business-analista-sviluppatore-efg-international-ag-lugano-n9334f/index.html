Role overview

Our team delivers solutions and provides support for internal and third-party systems in place for the Investment Client Solution (ICS) business. Support, business analysis, testing and development is provided for all business lines of the ICS including Advisory, Discretionary and New Capital Funds. The main aspect of this role is Support and Business Analyst functions. The role involves interaction with a wide range of end users from all aspects of the business (e.g. Front office, Middle office, Back office, Risk and Compliance as well as IT). The role involves providing support for Production critical issues, understanding and challenging business change requirements, testing changes, co-ordinating delivery workstreams, devising workable efficient scalable solutions to meet business requirements.
