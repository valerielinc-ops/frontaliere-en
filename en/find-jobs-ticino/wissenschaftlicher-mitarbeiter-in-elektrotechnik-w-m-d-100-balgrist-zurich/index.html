The University Hospital of Balgrist is one of the most renowned orthopedic clinics in Switzerland with international recognition and attached centre for paraplegia and rehabilitation.

The University Hospital Balgrist offers one of the most modern and attractive research centres in Switzerland, where researchers from the University and ETH Zurich, medical professionals, developers and industry work together to reduce problems of patients suffering from musculoskeletal diseases.

The Balgrist SPINE BIOMECHANICS Group is active in biomechanics and computer-assisted orthopedics and develops innovative solutions for the optimization of surgical treatments.
