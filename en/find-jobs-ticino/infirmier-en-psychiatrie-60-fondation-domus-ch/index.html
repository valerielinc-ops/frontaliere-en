Original description

The Domus Foundation, a Valaisan institution for psychosocial rehabilitation, accompanies adults facing major mental disorders. Nearly 150 employees are committed to it with conviction and professionalism. Our vision: self-determination for everyone, a solution-centric approach and professional practices marked by benevolence and responsibility. We seek for our home in Ardon a.e.: Nurse in Psychiatry (60%) from July 1 or to agree ## Mansioni • ensure daily nursing care of the persons accompanied in their entirety, at the preventive, curative, medico-educational and palliative level, if necessary; • managing all medical consultations and continuity of care; • providing support to beneficiaries within the Foyer d'Ardon in their daily activities with a view to psychosocial rehabilitation based on self-determination; • support residents in their individual projects and objectives; • develop a privileged relationship with the network of accompanying persons; • collaborate in interprofessionality with social workers, ESAs, ASSCs, day centres and workshops. ## Cosa offeriamo • join a foundation guided by participatory management, positive dynamics and a strong spirit of openness; • to integrate an institution certified by Promotion Santé Suisse, concerned with the well-being of everyone and the teams where listening, collaboration, mutual assistance and friendliness are cultivated; • to take your place in a structure that regularly carries out many projects to which everyone can contribute; • to benefit from annual scheduled working hours and collaboration within a multidisciplinary team; • to benefit from a pleasant working environment and good social and wage conditions; • Continuing training every year via our training catalogue "Acting Together". ## Requisiti • you benefit from a Bachelor of Nursing or equivalent, CAS psychiatrist an asset; • you have at least 2 years' professional experience.
