Main responsibilities

  • You will assume joint strategic and operational responsibility for the department, in a co-management setup.

Key requirements

  • University degree in civil, hydraulic, or related engineering (ETH/FH/UNI), with additional qualifications in management, finance, and team leadership (leadership and people management), as well as technical expertise in hydropower plants or underground works.
