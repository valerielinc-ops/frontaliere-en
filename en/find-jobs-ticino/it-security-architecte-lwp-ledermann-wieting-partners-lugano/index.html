Indicative location: Lugano. On behalf of an important client, we are looking for the following: IT SECURITY ARCHITECT Responsibilities: • Define and maintain enterprise IT security architecture standards, frameworks, and guidelines. • Ensure compliance with regulatory, and industry best practice standards (PCI-DSS, NIST CSF). • Design and review IT systems, applications, and infrastructure by translating business requirements into secure technical solutions. • Apply architectural principles such as Zero Trust, Defense in Depth, and integration with Automated Detect & Response. Requirements: • Degree in computer science, cybersecurity, IT architecture, or equivalent specialization. • 5 years of proven experience as an IT Security Architect or in a similar senior security or Architecture role. • Experience with risk assessments, threat modeling, and security architecture reviews. • Strong knowledge of security frameworks and standards such as NIST CSF. Experience with PCI-DSS would be a plus. • Security certifications such as (CISSP, CISM, SABSA, TOGAF, or equivalent) are a plus. • English is mandatory, with Italian being a strong advantage. German is a plus. Soft Skills: • Strong communication capabilities and teamwork skills. • Leadership personality and capability at structured analysis and abstraction. We invite candidates to send their application directly by e-mail to our consultant Mr. Alberto Largader (largader@lwphr.ch).
