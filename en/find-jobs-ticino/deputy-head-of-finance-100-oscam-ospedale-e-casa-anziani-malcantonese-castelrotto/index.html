Job Posting OSCAM – Ospedale e Casa Anziani Malcantonese is publishing the following job posting: 100% Finance Deputy Head Job. Full-time finance deputy head job The Giuseppe Rossi Foundation manages a 56-bed hospital in Castelrotto. Of these, 26 beds are for patients with psychiatric illnesses and 30 beds are for a medicine department (RAMI – acute care department with lower intensity). The hospital also has a first aid center and various outpatient clinics (cardiology, acupuncture, hepatology, radiology, occupational therapy, physiotherapy, laboratory, dietary service). In addition to the hospital, we manage an elderly home with 105 beds in Castelrotto and a second elderly home with 70 beds in Caslano, as well as a day/night therapeutic center that can accommodate up to 20 patients per day. The Malcantonese Hospital is looking to complete its team with a full-time finance deputy head with experience and prospects for promotion. Responsibilities The person we are looking for will deal with the following tasks under the supervision of the finance head and in collaboration with the area heads of the various services: * general/analytical accounting management of the institutions; * management/supervision of different types of performance invoicing; * management/supervision of creditor/debtor accounts and litigation; * supervision of payroll accounting; * management/supervision of personnel, resident and patient statistics and those required by the competent authorities; * preparation of periodic reports, budgets, forecasts and accounting closures; * management of relations with the services of the cantonal administration, institutional and operational partners. Requirements - Swiss citizenship or foreign nationals with a work permit - Federal certificate of specialist in finance and accounting, Bachelor, Master or license in business administration or equivalent title. - Good knowledge of the main MS Office and accounting software applications (if possible ISAWin). -
