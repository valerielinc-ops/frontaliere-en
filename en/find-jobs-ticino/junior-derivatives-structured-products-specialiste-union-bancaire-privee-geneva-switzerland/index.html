Environment: The Structured Products Team of the Treasury and Trading Department is in charge of the issuance process, the hedging and the monitoring of the structured products issued by the bank. This junior position provides a great opportunity to join a dynamic team on the trading floor and to strengthen your skills in OTC Equity Derivatives and Structured Products trading.
