Original description

# Teacher of educational sciences The University of Applied Sciences of Southern Switzerland (SUPSI) is offering a competitive position at the Department of Education and Learning (DFA) / Alta scuola pedagogica (ASP). Teacher of educational sciences for the fields of developmental psychology (with particular attention to the developmental age 0-10 years) and/or educational psychology . Degree of part-time employment (50%) permanent. Entry into service: 1 September 2026. ## Professional context The DFA/ASP ensures the professional training of teachers of all school levels (nursery school, elementary school, middle school, high school and special schools). Conducts research, innovation and development work focused on professional training, education, cultural development and learning in formal and informal learning contexts. It offers continuous and advanced training, as well as a wide range of services intended for teaching and training professionals or other audiences. It also plays an important role on a regional and national level at a cultural level and in the coordination and development of the education and training system. ## Duties The teacher works within the Education, Learning and Teaching (EAI) Competence Center, actively contributing to the initial training activities of teachers in training and to the ongoing training activities of teachers. In particular, the teacher is active in Bachelor training for nursery schools and elementary schools by designing, implementing and evaluating large group courses and/or class group seminars in the various training devices provided. The teacher also contributes to the professionalization of teachers by promoting the development of psycho-pedagogical skills through professional support and continuous training devices. The teacher also participates in the academic and institutional life of the DFA/ASP and the Education, Learning and Teaching Competence Center, contributing to the development of the Centre's activities
