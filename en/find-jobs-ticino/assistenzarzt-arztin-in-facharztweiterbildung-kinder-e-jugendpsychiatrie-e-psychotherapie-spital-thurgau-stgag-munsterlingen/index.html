The paediatric and youth psychiatric service seeks for agreement a/n assistant physician in specialist training child and youth psychiatry and psychotherapy 100% or part time In the course of further training, you will have the opportunity to rotate in our wide clinical offer between the various internships and thus benefit from a comprehensive specialist training. Interest in medical training in children and youth psychiatry Commitment, flexibility, humour and enthusiasm for work with mentally ill children and their families as well as interdisciplinary cooperation 8596 Münsterlingen, Switzerland The Children's and Youth Psychiatric Service (KJPD) Thurgau offers excellent framework conditions for classical child- and youth-psychiatric and comprehensive psychotherapeutic training.
