Role overview

The role bridges business needs with regulatory standards and ensures the smooth execution of regulatory, compliance, and operational processes for activities in the Americas region.

Main responsibilities

  • Support the Client Relationship Officers in proactively identifying, managing and mitigating risks arising from day-to-day business activities, applying advanced professional expertise and conceptual thinking to complex cases

  • Distribute market knowledge and best practices among Client Relationship Officers, Client Service Officers and Account Managers, defining and embedding standard around which others will operate
  • Ensure timely completion and remediation of pending follow-ups relating to client lifecycle activities, being accountable for delivery of assigned processes/programmes impacting the department
  • Approve clients and business introducers new accounts, oversee KYC lifecycle and periodic reviews within the authority delegated by HoPB, ensuring oversight and compliance standards are met through in-depth professional expertise
  • Act as a key senior partner to Front Office and control Functions and as a key link between FLOD and SLOD, ensuring consistent communication and escalation paths, and networking with key contacts across multipe levels and teams internally and externally
  • Review account openings, CIP modifications and periodic reviews for the local PBS teams across the Region, while providing guidance to strengthen their understanding of requirements and control standards. Through continuous support and best practice sharing, provide guidance to more junior members of the team and help develop the capability to perform these reviews independently.
