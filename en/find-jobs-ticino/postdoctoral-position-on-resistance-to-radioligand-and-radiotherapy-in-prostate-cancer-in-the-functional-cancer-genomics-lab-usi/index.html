Role overview

The Institute of Oncology Research (IOR), affiliated with the Università della Svizzera italiana (USI), is located in a brand-new, high-tech research building that hosts more than 20 different research groups.

It offers state-of-the-art facilities together with the neighboring Institute for Research in Biomedicine (IRB).

It offers a thriving environment with leading edge research projects and multiple occasions of interaction with world leaders in the fields of cancer, immunology, and DNA biology (see Ph.D. lecture series at PhD Program).
