Original description

As head of the asylum and refugee health unit (USAR), you carry out and supervise clinical, academic and administrative activities. You ensure and participate in the proper functioning of the unit by coordinating and collaborating with your multidisciplinary team of doctors, social assistants, nurses and secretaries. You are also responsible for the medical consultations offered to migrant patients within the PMPRS. You ensure the medical coordination of the Asylum Health Care Network (AHC) in collaboration with the AMG and the General Hospital. You are concerned or anxious to provide a dynamic and caring working environment by valuing and developing your team's skills. Through your leadership, you have the ability to unite your team around common goals and involve them in development, research and institutional projects. You assist management in the design, development and delivery of pre- and post-graduate education. You also contribute to the development of quality research within your unit. You have developed research axes and projects in the field of asylum and refugee health, with a significant academic production (including publications, funding). Finally, as a specialist in the health of migrants, you represent the Unit (USAR), the Service (SMPR) and the Department (DMCPR) both within the HUGs and against any external contact.
