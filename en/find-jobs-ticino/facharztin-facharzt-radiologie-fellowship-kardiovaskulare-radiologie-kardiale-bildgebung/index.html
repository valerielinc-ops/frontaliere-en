Main responsibilities

  • You will receive structured training and supervision by experienced specialists in the Cardiothoracic Radiology section.
  • You will deepen your knowledge of indications, patient preparation, protocol selection, image acquisition, post-processing, image interpretation and diagnosis of cardiovascular examinations
