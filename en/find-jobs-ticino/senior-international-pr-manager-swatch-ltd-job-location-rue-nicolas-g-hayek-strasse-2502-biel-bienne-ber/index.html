Main responsibilities

  • Develop and execute PR campaigns that build awareness and credibility with key audiences.
  • Shape and refine messaging that reflects brand voice and values.
  • Oversee day-to-day work with local PR Managers to ensure aligned, high-quality execution.
  • Track and analyze media coverage and campaign performance metrics to report on ROI, draft appropriate responses.
  • Coordinate press attendance at global and glocal press events.
  • Manage media visits and spokesperson interview opportunities.
  • Identify and manage potential reputational risks and contribute to respective communications planning and response.
  • Partner with internal teams to identify and amplify compelling product and brand stories.
  • Oversee PR budgets and external partner relationships, where applicable.

Key requirements

  • Strong experience pitching and placing stories with tier-one media
  • Experience collaborating with c-level executives and cross-functional teams
  • Ability to work well in extremely fast-paced environment with high volume projects and quick turn-around
  • Ability to handle multiple projects, coordination with various departments both internally and externally, with the flexibility to re-prioritize when needed, keeping timing, quality, and budget in mind while driving projects forward pragmatically
  • Organized, reliable, flexible and team-oriented work ethic professionali
