Original description

Digital audit involves analyzing and optimizing our clients' business and IT processes to help them create lasting value for a digital future. Our work boils down to building trust: helping clients understand the benefits - not just the risks - of new technologies and giving them the knowledge and confidence to entrust brilliant minds with carrying out their technological transformation. ## Responsibilities - Evaluating internal controls of business and IT processes during the audit of annual accounts of national and international clients, within the "Audit 4.0" approach. - Analyzing transactions, master data and accounts using tools to detect irregularities, contributing to "process mining" projects. - Actively participating in advisory activities aimed at optimizing internal control systems and risk management. - Collaborating on special IT and process-related projects for our clients. - Evolving in a dynamic work environment that fosters teamwork, trust and mutual support. - Regularly receiving constructive feedback from colleagues and superiors. - Potential opportunities for hiring as an Associate afterwards. ## Requirements - Holder, or in the process of obtaining, a HES Bachelor's degree (business informatics) or Master's degree (information systems) - Native French speaker with excellent English skills. - Excellent synthesis and analytical skills. - Highly motivated to learn, with the aim of continuing training to obtain the CISA (Certified Information Systems Auditor) auditor diploma. - Significant aptitude for communication and teamwork, while demonstrating a strong customer service orientation.
