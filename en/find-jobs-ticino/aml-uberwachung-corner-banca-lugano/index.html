Original description

We are looking for an experienced and detail-oriented Compliance professional to join our team within a dynamic banking environment. The role involves supporting the Front Office on compliance-related matters, ensuring adherence to regulatory requirements, and contributing to the effective management of financial crime risks. The ideal candidate brings strong analytical skills, a proactive mindset, and the ability to handle complex cases with accuracy and professionalism.
