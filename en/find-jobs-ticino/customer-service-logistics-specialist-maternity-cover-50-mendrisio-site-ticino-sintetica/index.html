Main responsibilities

  • Key responsibilities
  • Maintain in the system orders received from domestic customers, transmit them to external warehouse and monitor delivery punctuality, issue related invoices on daily basis.
  • Communicate critical supply issues to customers.
  • Manage and monitor orders received from international partners, ensuring the ERP system is consistently updated; transfer orders to Production Planning team and confirm feasibility to partners in terms of delivery timelines.
  • Maintain and update the order portfolio and related revenue plan, preparing reports and analyses.
  • Issue invoices and logistics documentation and coordinate product collection including compliance with export procedures and order fulfilment requirements.
  • Oversee import and export logistics activities requested by internal departments.
