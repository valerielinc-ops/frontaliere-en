Job Description

We are looking for a competent university student to gain hands-on experience in a dynamic financial services environment. If you have the skills and enthusiasm we are looking for and are excited about the opportunity to enhance our Finance - Corporate Tax functions, please apply today.

Key areas of responsibilities:

  • Tax audits preparation including analysing any potential mismatches

  • GloBE/Pillar II implementation of the dedicated IT tool

  • Extraction and analysis of accounting and other data that has an impact on VAT

  • Other useful tasks/extractions from Power BI

  • TP Genie (IT tool) implementation – intra-group financial flows

  • TP (Transfer Pricing) analysis – reconciliations and Globus/HFM checks

  • Tracking unpaid invoices

  • Tracking unsigned intra group agreements

  • Any ad-hoc question or urgency which would arise during the assignment

Requirements and Qualifications

  • University student, preferably in Corporate Finance.
  • Fluent in English, French is a plus.

  • Excellent analytical and research skills with detail orientation

  • Good knowledge of accounting and bookkeeping procedures

  • Strong ability to organize and prioritize tasks

  • Reporting and numeracy skills

  • Good at meeting deadlines and solving problems

  • Ability to work well independently as well as the ability to work well with stakeholders
