Role overview

Mansioni - HF nurses are responsible for the entire nursing process.

  • You are responsible for the planning, execution, delegation and monitoring of nursing measures.
  • After obtaining detailed information about the care needs and the needs of the patients, they create a care concept.
  • As a team, they carry out the care measures and monitor their effect and success.
  • You closely monitor the course of the disease and the recovery process.
  • In the nursing documentation, they record the latest developments on a daily basis and share their knowledge during ward reports and doctor visits.
  • HF nurses are repeatedly confronted with challenging situations in their work: they assist patients who are in different physical and mental states and accompany people in all life situations.
  • Requirements - For the shortened course of study (start of winter & spring), a degree as a specialist in health EFZ, as well as a trial internship in the acute area, is required.
  • For the 3-year training (start winter), we are looking for persons without a FaGe degree, with the following requirements: - Completion of at least 3 years of basic vocational training with federal Certificate of Competence (EFZ) or - Secondary school certificate or high school matriculation Interested?
