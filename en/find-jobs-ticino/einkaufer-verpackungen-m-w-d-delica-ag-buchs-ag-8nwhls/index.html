Main responsibilities

  • You support the central material group managers in implementing their strategies and contribute to achieving the higher-level objectives of the Migros industry.
  • You are responsible for the precise master data management and prepare product specifications to make informed purchasing decisions.
  • You carry out the order and contract processing while respecting the agreed framework contracts.
  • You have strong relationships with suppliers to ensure a reliable and high-quality supply.
  • You work intensively in cross-functional teams and coordinate our procurement network by effectively combining local and central requirements and using synergies.
  • You accompany tendering and awarding processes, analyze offers and conduct negotiations to ensure the best conditions for our company.

Key requirements

  • 5 years: in purchasing food or consumer goods production
  • Higher technical school: as a shopping specialist or shopping manager - experience in purchasing packaging or raw materials and very good IT skills (MS-Office, SAP MM-Modul)
  • Strong communication and team skills, analytical thinking and pronounced negotiation skills
  • Proactive approach with high process understanding
  • German (insurance) Special offers for your stay
