Original description

Mansioni The Metal & Inventory Controller is responsible for ensuring robust control design and operating effectiveness across metal and material movements, inventory accuracy, and related processes within the factory environment. Acting as a second line of control, the role independently reviews, validates, and tests material flows and inventory transactions to ensure compliance with internal policies and procedures. The position contributes to continuous improvement of inventory and movement controls, supports bi-annual physical inventory counts, and provides actionable insights to strengthen operational integrity, transparency, and risk mitigation. ## Principali attività e responsabilità - Control Design & Operating Effectiveness Support in design, document, audit and maintain controls related to metal/material movements and inventory tracking. - Conduct regular reviews of control operating effectiveness, identifying gaps or weaknesses. - Perform detailed tests of movements and adjustments to confirm adherence to documented procedures. - Coordinate with production, operations and logistics to clarify movement discrepancies and enforce control standards. - Inventory Monitoring & Reconciliation Monitor inbound/outbound metal movements and validate accuracy in inventory systems. - Reconcile inventory records with physical counts and system transactions to identify variances. - Perform cause analysis of discrepancies and coordinate corrective actions. - Support cycle count programs and lead investigations of root causes in count differences. - Physical Inventory Support Support to plan, execute and document physical inventory counts. - Support to prepare inventory count procedures, control test criteria, and reports. - Process Improvement & Governance Propose improvements to policies, procedures, and system controls to strengthen inventory accuracy and control design. - Collaborate with Production, Logistics, and IT teams to streamline processes and reduce error rate
