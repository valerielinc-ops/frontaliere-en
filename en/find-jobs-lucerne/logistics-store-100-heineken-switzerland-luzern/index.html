Role overview

For our subsidiary Stardrinks AG in the Depot in Lucerne, we are looking for a communicative, independent and interested personality as a logistics provider in the beverage warehouse (100%) You are responsible for the timely, efficient and qualitatively perfect supply of beverages, food gases and POS material.

You also take the goods receipt and the defined input control of own and foreign products.

In addition, you carry out general storage and cleaning work, assist with recurring inventory and assist in transportation as well as in other depots if necessary.
