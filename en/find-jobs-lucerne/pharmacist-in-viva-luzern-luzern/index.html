Role overview

Holistic supervision of the medication process: You accompany our residents through all phases of medical care – from prescription to safe application – and ensure an individual, professional care

  • Pharmaceutical advice and expertise: You are a competent contact person for home doctors as well as the nursing and therapy team and handle pharmaceutical inquiries with high professionalism and commitment

Interface management: You ensure safe and coordinated medication supply in the transition between inpatient and outpatient care and promote efficient communication between all parties involved

  • Quality-oriented cooperation: You are actively involved in the further development of our quality management and work closely with different professional groups to continuously improve the supply of medicines

Deputy Head of Pharmacy: During the absence of the Head of Pharmacy, you take over the professional deputy and ensure that the medication supply and team run smoothly
