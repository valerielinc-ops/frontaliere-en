Main responsibilities

  • Together with your colleagues, you are responsible for the diagnostic and interventional radiological care of patients in the largest radiological institute in Switzerland (LUKS).
  • You work in the "Body" radiology section, where you are responsible for the diagnosis of abdominal and urogenital imaging (MRI, CT, (CE)US, X-ray and fluoroscopy) and you are the contact person in our clinic for the demonstration of findings in the clinical reports and the multidisciplinary tumor boards
  • You participate in the implementation of abdominal interventions (sonographic and CT-guided biopsies and drainages)
  • You accompany the training of the interns, participate in the radiology operations and, if interested, there is the opportunity to participate in clinical research Requirements:
  • You have the title of specialist in radiology
  • You have a great interest in abdominal and urogenital radiology or have already acquired a specialization in this area
  • Your An initiative, team-oriented and independent way of working rounds off your profile. We offer:
  • Look forward to working in a collegial and professional environment with the most modern equipment (14 MRI 1.5 and 3T), 11 CT (incl. dual source and cone beam), digital X-ray, ultrasound, angiography (monoplane and biplane incl. hybrid room "Angio-CT"), PET-CT and SPECT/CT
  • You can expect a demanding and varied work in abdominal radiology, where your commitment and initiative are desired and valued
  • Further and advanced radiological training is of course part of our job offer
