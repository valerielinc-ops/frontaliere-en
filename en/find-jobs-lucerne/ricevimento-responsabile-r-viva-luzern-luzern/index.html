Role overview

You accompany people when entering, leaving or changing within the age center – and design these transitions with heart, organizational talent and vision, always with a focus on the needs of the residents and their relatives as well as in close cooperation with care, administration and external partners.

  • You advise residents and relatives individually and empathetically as part of a case management
  • You organize visits and information discussions with (potential) residents and their relatives
  • You lead the bed mirror and ensure a forward-looking and needs-based room occupancy
  • You support with applications for supplementary services, IV and follow-up solutions
  • You maintain contact with institutions such as KESB, AHV, Pro Senectute and other relevant bodies
  • Requirements
  • Completed nursing or medical training with a tertiary degree – ideally supplemented by further training in case management
  • Empathy, communication strength and the ability to act clearly and solution-oriented even in challenging situations Good IT user knowledge as well as a networked, entrepreneurial thinking High flexibility and resilience in a dynamic environment
  • Very good knowledge of German (minor level B2) and a good understanding of the Swiss German language Cosa of Friamo Generous participation in external education and training
  • Extensive internal training offer
  • Individual advice on career planning Contatto
