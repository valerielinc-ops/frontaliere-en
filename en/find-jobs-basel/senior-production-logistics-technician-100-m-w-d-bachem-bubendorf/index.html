Role overview

In order to drive our continued organic growth, we are constantly looking for highly qualified specialists.

To strengthen our team in "Peptide Manufacturing Support" we are looking for a dedicated Senior Production Logistics Technician 100% (m/f/d)

Your tasks: Ensuring the timely supply of the production lines with materials (raw materials, semi-finished and finished goods) Picking production orders in different storage zones (frozen, chilled, uncooled) Carrying out warehouse movements, rebookings and transfers between storage and production areas GMP/cGMP-compliant documentation as well as support with internal and external audits Operation, maintenance and safe use of logistical tools in compliance with safety and quality standards Representation of the team leader in day-to-day business (material flow, prioritization, escalations) Analysis and optimization of logistics processes Training, induction and coaching of employees in logistics processes
