Role overview

The bed management functions as a central point for all stationary patient recordings and internal layings within the Claraspital and the USB, with the exception of patient recordings in high-installed or specialized beds (Bsp.: INT, IMC, SU, NCW, isolation station).

It acts as a contact person for all departments of the USB, for all beds of the Claraspital and for external instructors.

The task of bed management is to ensure and implement a patient-oriented and co-worker-oriented and economical control of the internal patient flow taking into account an optimal bed utilization while performing the interface management required for this purpose.
