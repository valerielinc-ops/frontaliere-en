Main responsibilities

  • Design, implement, and maintain the Digital Manufacturing & Data Lab infrastructure, including automation systems, virtualized environments, and network setups
  • Develop, configure, and support Siemens PCS7 systems (incl.
  • WinCC, Simatic Batch) for lab and pilot environments
  • Build and maintain simulation capabilities and digital twins using SIMIT, enabling virtual commissioning, testing, and training scenarios
  • Administer and operate lab IT/OT infrastructure, including virtualization, systems, and network components
  • Integrate controllers, field devices, and lab equipment into cohesive automation and data environments
  • Provide and maintain internal tools, templates, and automation libraries for users of the lab
  • Support users (engineers, data scientists) in utilizing lab systems for experiments, testing, and troubleshooting
  • Participate in automation and digitalization projects, from requirements gathering to prototyping and implementation
  • Maintain system documentation, ensure configuration management, and support testing activities where applicable

Key requirements

  • University degree in Automation Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, or a related technical field
  • Hands‑on experience with process control systems, ideally Siemens PCS7, WinCC, and Simatic Batch, as well as simulation tools such as SIMIT or similar platforms
  • Solid understanding of automation hardware, PLCs, controllers, field instrumentation, system integration, and industrial communication protocols (e.g., OPC UA, Profinet, Modbus)
  • Practical knowledge of IT/OT infrastructure, including virtualization, Windows administration, TCP/IP networking, virtual environments (e.g., VMware, Proxmox), and network segmentation in lab setups
  • Exposure to data systems (e.g., historians, MES, analytics platforms), their integration with automation systems, and experience in virtual commissioning and digital twin approaches
  • Strong problem‑solving skills with the ability to work across disciplines, act as a technical allrounder, and translate user needs into practical technical solutions
  • Dedicated, self‑driven, structured, and collaborative personality with a hands‑on mindset; solid knowledge of German is considered a strong plus
