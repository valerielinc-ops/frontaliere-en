Engineer Measurement Technology / Electrical & Physical Testing of Plastics (m/f) Role Overview : Huntsman is seeking an Engineer in Measurement Technology / Electrical & Physical Testing of Plastics to join our Technology Center in Basel, within the Advanced Materials division.
