Role overview

We look for a motivated and flexible person as CNC maintenance technician Your responsibilities

  • Carry out regular maintenance work on machines, equipment and equipment to ensure their proper functioning and to minimize downtime
  • Independent analysis and correction of faults (mechanical, electrical, pneumatic)
  • Ensuring high machine availability and minimizing downtime
  • Planning and implementation of preventive maintenance
  • Support for continuous improvement of plant performance (OEE, quality, stability)
  • Documentation of disruptions, causes and measures taken
  • Close cooperation with production and internal specialist departments
  • Development and implementation of preventive maintenance plans for early detection and prevention of potential problems
  • Compliance and implementation of all relevant safety regulations and guidelines during maintenance work to prevent accidents and injuries
  • Supporting the team in ensuring and monitoring technical and infrastructural building management
  • Implementation of projects to improve maintenance processes and increase efficiency.
  • Introduction and management of maintenance and maintenance software and training of the team in handling new systems Your qualifications
  • Completed technical training, preferably in electrical engineering, mechanics or mechatronics
  • NIV 15 connection approval
  • Several years of experience in the maintenance of CNC machines (ideally milling machines)
  • In-depth knowledge of mechanics, electrics and pneumatics
  • Experience in systematic error analysis and troubleshooting
  • Good knowledge of German, English and French are advantageous
  • Independent, structured and solution-oriented working method
  • Teamwork and effective communication skills About Medartis Medartis is one of the leading manufacturers of medical products for osteosynthesis in the area of the facial skull and extremities.

For the benefit of the patient, Medartis is committed to providing surgeons and surgical staff with the most innovative titanium implants and instruments and providing the best service.

We offer you a responsible and varied activity with high quality requirements in a growing, international company with progressive social benefits.

We look forward to your complete online application.
