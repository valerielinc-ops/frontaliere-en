Company and context

  • The Position In Roche´s Pharmaceutical Research and Early Development organization (pRED), we make transformative medicines for patients in order to tackle some of the world’s toughest unmet healthcare needs.
  • At pRED, we are united by our mission to transform science into medicines.
  • Together, we create a culture defined by curiosity, responsibility and humility, where our talented people are empowered and inspired to bring forward extraordinary life-changing innovation at speed.
  • This position is located in Preclinical Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls (pCMC), a department within the small molecule research organization in pRED Therapeutic Modalities, where we pave, in a diverse, ambitious and innovative environment, the path to bring drug candidates from early research into development.
  • The Opportunity As an Intern in our team your main tasks will include:
  • Execute and support the optimization of screening assays using liquid handling systems to accelerate the pre-formulation pipeline.
  • Perform "head-to-head" manual assays at the bench to compare, validate, and troubleshoot results against automated processes, ensuring consistency and accuracy.
  • Interpret complex datasets to provide recommendations on process improvements and formulation viability. Who You Are
  • You are a meticulous and independent experimentalist who enjoys troubleshooting
  • You hold a Master’s degree in Chemistry, Pharmaceutical Sciences, Chemical Engineering, or Automation with lab experience, degree completed no more than 12 months prior to the internship start date
