More about this search for cross-border applicants

Basel offers a dynamic job market in life sciences, finance, logistics and professional services, with over 300,000 jobs across the region. The city’s pharmaceutical clusters around Novartis and Roche, along with global trading and banking firms, provide steady demand for skilled workers. Neighborhoods like Kleinhüningen and Dreispitz host logistics hubs, while the city center concentrates finance and consulting roles. Public transport links to France and Germany make Basel accessible, and the canton’s unemployment rate remains below the Swiss average. Whether you specialize in IT, engineering, healthcare or administration, Basel’s labor market balances international firms with local employers.
