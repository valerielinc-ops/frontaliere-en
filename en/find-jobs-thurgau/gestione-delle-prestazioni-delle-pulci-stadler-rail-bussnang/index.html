Description

PROFIL

  • University degree (ETH/UNI/FH) in engineering or comparable
  • Professional background in systems engineering, service engineering or maintenance
  • Experience in interface role (customer, project management, engineering) desirable
  • Very good system understanding (mechanics, electrics and software)
  • Analytical thinking and ability to understand error images systemically
  • Experience with error analysis (root cause analysis) or reliability methods (RAMS, FMEA, FRACAS) is advantageous
  • Ability to produce clear and addressable reports
  • Ability to present technically complex topics in a comprehensible way TASKS
  • Analysis and categorization of malfunctions occurring during operation of rail vehicles
  • Detection of systematic errors in operation and communication to Engineering
  • Regular production of reliability reports (FRACAS Report)
  • Track fleet reliability and define measures for achieving the contractually promised reliability values
  • Ensure progress in solving remedial measures in engineering
  • Interface with the customer for discussion of malfunctions occurring during operation
