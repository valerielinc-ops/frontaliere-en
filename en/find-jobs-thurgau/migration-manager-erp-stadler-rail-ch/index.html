Description

PROFILE

  • Completed studies in (business) informatics, data management, engineering, business administration or comparable
  • Several years of experience in ERP transformations and/or data migrations (ideally SAP)
  • Sound understanding of data models, master data/transaction data as well as migration procedures (e.g. data profiling, cleansing, mapping, cutover)
  • Experience in planning and controlling migrations including migration tests and Go-live/Cutover
  • Structured, quality-oriented way of working and ability to manage several projects in parallel
  • Very good knowledge of German and English TASKS
  • Responsibility for the migration workstream within the ERP transformation program including governance, planning and roadmap per project/wave
  • Control of migration teams and coordination with specialist departments, IT and test/integration teams
  • Development and coordination of migration concepts including scoping, mapping and cutover
  • Ensuring data Profiling, data cleansing and data quality
  • Planning and coordination of migration tests (unit/integration/UAT support) as well as cutover and go-live activities
  • Management of risks, issues and decisions in the migration environment
  • Establishment of transparent migration reporting (status, progress, data quality, readiness)
  • Definition and monitoring of relevant migration KPIs
  • Close collaboration with the local projects within the program to implement migration requirements, cutover plans and Ensure consistent go-live acceptances
