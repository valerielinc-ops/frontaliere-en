PROFILE

  • Completed studies in electronics, embedded systems or comparable field
  • Several years of experience in electronics development, circuit analysis or assembly diagnosis
  • Very good knowledge of analogue and digital circuit technology
  • Safe handling of measuring devices such as oscilloscope, multimeter, logic analysis
  • Experience in PCB analysis, component selection, soldering technology and rework
  • Experience in systematic fault analysis and documentation
  • Very good knowledge of German and good English for supplier communication TASKS
  • Analysis of faulty or failed electronic assemblies at component and circuit level
  • Derivation of the cause of the failure based on the failure pattern
  • Conducting electrical measurements, optical inspections and circuit analyses
  • Evaluation of component failures, thermal or electrical overloads as well as layout or design problems
  • Creation of detailed reports on findings for internal and external further use
  • Close cooperation with device and component suppliers
  • Analysis and processing of technical faults in the operational testing and warranty phase (use of internal tools for vehicle diagnosis)
  • Systematic investigation and evaluation of faults across several vehicle fleets
