Description

PROFILE

  • You have a completed HF or university degree in electrical engineering or a comparable field
  • You have already gained initial experience in power distribution, drive systems or control and control technology, ideally even in rail vehicle construction
  • You communicate fluently in German (level C2) and have good English skills (level B2)
  • You enjoy working in interdisciplinary teams and have knowledge of using E-CAD programs
  • You value working with high level of personal responsibility and has strong communication and coordination skills TASKS
  • You are responsible for the various subsystems in electrical engineering as well as the design and integration of the electrical systems in our vehicles
  • You accompany the vehicle development process by creating concepts and specifications
  • You design the electrical components according to customer requirements and currently applicable standards
  • You take over the technical coordination with other disciplines (software creation, installation planning, mechanical engineering, fire protection, etc.)
  • You create the Electrical function overviews in E-CAD as a basis for the schematic draftsmen
  • You oversee the assembly and initial commissioning of the vehicles in collaboration with our customers
  • You maintain collaboration with our suppliers and ensure timely and high-quality implementation
