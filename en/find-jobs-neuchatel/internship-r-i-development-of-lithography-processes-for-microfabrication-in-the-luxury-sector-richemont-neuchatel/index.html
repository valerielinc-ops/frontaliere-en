Main responsibilities

  • In collaboration with the Microfabrication team and under the supervision of your internship mentor, your tasks will include:
  • Understanding the technical challenges of existing manufacturing processes; Identifying new manufacturing processes;
  • Managing the design and implementation of experimental studies; Performing metrological controls;
  • Analyzing results and identifying areas for improvement;
  • Writing scientific reports.
  • PROFILE You are a Master’s level student at EPF, Grandes Ecoles, or equivalent, with specialization in microtechnology, physics, or materials.
  • You have some experience in microfabrication and a desire to work in a cleanroom environment.
  • Experience in photolithography and mask design (Klayout) is a plus.
  • Rigorous, methodical, and with a critical mind, you enjoy teamwork.
  • Your sense of responsibility and autonomy will be valuable assets to successfully complete this mission.
