Postulazioni online: www.nant.ch Descrizione The Riviera-Chablais Vaud-Valais Hospital, in collaboration with all its partners, has set up a mobile rapid response team (EMIR) to help keep the person in an emergency situation in his or her home.

This team intervenes on medical warrants at home, to assess the situation within two hours and/or provide care to persons over 18 years of age, 24 hours a day and 7 days a week.
