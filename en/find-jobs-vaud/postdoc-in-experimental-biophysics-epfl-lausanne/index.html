Role overview

Mission Our group is a curiosity-driven experimental biophysics lab aiming to understand how fluid flows and mechanical properties emerge from the collective activity of molecular assemblies in living cells.

We also investigate the biophysical mechanisms by which cells sense, adapt, and respond to their environment.

We study free-living unicellular organisms as model systems.
