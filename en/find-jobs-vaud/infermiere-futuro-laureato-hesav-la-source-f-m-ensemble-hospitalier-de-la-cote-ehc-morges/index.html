Role overview

The Morges Hospital offers a wide range of care, from outpatient care to acute care, passing through emergencies and long-term treatments.

The institution has 240 acute care beds, a maternity ward with approximately 1,500 births per year, and around 30,000 emergency consultations.

More than 40 medical disciplines have been practiced within its walls for over 150 years.
