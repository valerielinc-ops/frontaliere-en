  • As part of our medical team, you will play a key role in the reception and administrative care of our patients:
  • Management of appointments for various medical imaging examinations (radiography, MRI, scanner, ultrasound, mammography...)
  • Administrative preparation and organisation of patient records prior to examinations
  • Verification of medical requirements and coordination with radiologists and radiologists
  • Management of patient consents and information on the conduct of examinations
  • Seizing and archiving imaging results, secure transmission to treating physicians
  • Monitoring of radiology-specific protocols and procedures (safety, radiation protection, confidentiality)
  • Coordination with laboratories and ancillary services for combined examinations
