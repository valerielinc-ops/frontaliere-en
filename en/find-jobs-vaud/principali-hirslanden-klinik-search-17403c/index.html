Application process

  • MAIN TASKS Attached to the Human Resources department, you will be the preferred contact for employees for questions related to staff health policy and their application.
  • You will work in an interdisciplinary manner with other occupational safety and health specialists such as occupational therapists, HPCI nurses, the safety officer as well as human resources and management and will be responsible for coordinating all related activities.
  • Organize and ensure the application of the occupational health policy.
  • Establish and/or maintain working conditions allowing employees to remain in good health by promoting their physical, mental and social well-being.
  • Inform employees and assist department managers in matters of health and safety at work.
  • In collaboration with the MSST manager, identify the health risks to which employees are exposed and keep them informed.
  • Provide advice for the prevention of professional and non-professional accidents through awareness campaigns.
  • Collaborate with the human resources department in the management of absences by implementing measures to promote return to work.
