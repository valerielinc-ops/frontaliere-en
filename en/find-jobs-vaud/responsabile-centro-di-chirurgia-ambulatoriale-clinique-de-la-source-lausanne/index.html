Role overview

La Clinique de La Source is a unique multidisciplinary acute care facility.

It belongs to the Fondation La Source, an independent and private non-profit institution that also oversees the Institut et Haute École de la Santé La Source.

The independence granted by this status allows it to continually invest in human and technological aspects to ensure the best possible care for its patients.
