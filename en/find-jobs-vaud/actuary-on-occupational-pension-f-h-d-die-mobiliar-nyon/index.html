Main responsibilities

  • You make an essential contribution to the proper functioning of foresight and your responsibilities require both actuarial, programming and database skills.
  • What you can achieve with us: Your main task is to develop and maintain the actuarial components of the software used for pension fund reinsurance.
  • You work in a cross-cutting way with the teams in charge of pricing, balance sheet, risk management, as well as with the IT teams, in order to design and optimize the tools necessary for the proper functioning of foresight.
  • In addition, you are involved in the modelling of our disability, death and retirement products, whether for pricing, setting up reserves or conducting ad hoc analyses. What you bring:
  • ASA actuary training or equivalent training
  • Experience of at least 3 to 5 years in life insurance
  • Strong programming experience, especially in Python and R
  • Excellent analytical and synthesis capabilities, high curiosity
  • Want to collaborate in an interdisciplinary team and actively contribute to change Very good mastery of French and German
  • Working with us: The Nyon Actuariate team is a team of actuaries, where everyone has a high level of responsibility, while working in a resolutely collaborative atmosphere.
