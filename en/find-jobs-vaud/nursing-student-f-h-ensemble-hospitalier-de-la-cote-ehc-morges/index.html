Role overview

Within the Accommodation department of the EHC, our three EMS — Nelty de Beausobre, Parc de Beausobre and Les Clos d'Aubonne — welcome residents in complementary living environments, between the centre of Morges, an intergenerational park and the heights of Aubonne overlooking the vineyards and the lake.

These institutions offer personalized support, in geriatrics and psychiatry of advanced age, with particular attention to quality of life, human relationship and the maintenance of social ties.

By joining our teams, you will evolve in learning environments that are rich in learning, with the contact of professionals involved in the accompaniment of older people.
