Role overview

Integrated within a renowned large institution, the Lavigny Institution’s hospital is one of the key centers in the Romandy region for patients with brain injuries requiring neurological rehabilitation, or suffering from difficult-to-treat epilepsy.

Open since July 1, 2024, the University Neurorehabilitation Service (SUN) is an innovative structure linking two institutions, the CHUV and Lavigny Hospital.

With 75 beds and nearly 200 staff members, SUN provides highly specialized and interdisciplinary care in academic neurorehabilitation, serving over 500 hospitalized patients and nearly 2000 outpatient patients annually.
