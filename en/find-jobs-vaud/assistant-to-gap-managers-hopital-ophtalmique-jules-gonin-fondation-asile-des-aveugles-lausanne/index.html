Role overview

Founded in Lausanne in 1843, the Asylum Foundation of the Blind includes the Jules-Gonin Ophthalmic Hospital, a university department of ophthalmology, a research centre, the Centre pédagogique pour écoles handicapées de la vue (CPHV), the social service, rehabilitation and low vision and the medical-social establishment Clair-Soleil.

More than 650 employees work every day in the service of visual health to offer diagnostic, treatment and accompaniment services to people with vision.

To complement our team, we are looking for a Assistant to GAP Your Mission Co-organising the activities of the secretariats, units without reference, with the GAP officials Ensure planning and management of schedules for all PIP secretaries and ensure communication with PIP officials Pilot and/or support PAG projects and contribute to cross-cutting projects Establish , standardize and manage control and monitoring statistics within the PAG, ensuring timeliness and reliability of information Develop and manage the internal communication of the PAM in order to ensure an efficient and consistent flow of information (presentation, process, SMALA,...) Represent PAG officials in internal sessions, working groups, and external partners.
