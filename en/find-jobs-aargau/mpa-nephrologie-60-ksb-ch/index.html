Role overview

They appreciate the combination of direct patient contact, organizational tasks and medical activities.

In the nephrological ambulatorium, you will accompany patients through their appointment and provide a smooth process together with the team.

In doing so, you can use your expertise, self-employment and service orientation in a targeted manner.

The place is available by immediately or by agreement.

Your tasks

  • They receive patients and are the first respondent for internal and external concerns.
  • They plan and coordinate the nephrological speaking hours and take over the administrative tasks involved.
  • They carry out venous blood withdrawals and assist in medicamental administrations, in particular intravenous, intramuscular and subcutaneous therapies.
  • They maintain and process 24-hour urine collections according to the applicable requirements.
  • They work closely with the medical team and contribute to a smooth run in the outpatient clinic.
  • You take responsibility in your area of responsibility and actively engage in team cooperation.
  • You have training as MPA EFZ, dipl.
  • Care specialist or doctor's secretary with appropriate training for venous blood withdrawals.
