Description

For the Centre for Inpatient Forensic Psychiatry (ZFPS), we are looking for you as part of an interdisciplinary management team (psychiatry-psychology nursing/care) as of 01.11.2025 or by agreement:

  • As part of an interdisciplinary management team (psychiatry-psychology nursing/care), you will design the therapeutic environment and the individual treatment of patients at the KFP-1 safety ward
  • You will supervise the psychiatric-psychotherapeutic training of the resident in their ward
  • For some of the intervention patients, you will initiate therapy under highly secure conditions.
  • They carry out inpatient clarifications on differential diagnostic questions and on the question of the treatability of mentally ill offenders.
  • As part of inpatient acute treatment of prison inmates, you will work closely with the Psychiatric-Psychological Service (PPD) of the Lenzburg Prison and the Health Service of the Aargau Prisons.
