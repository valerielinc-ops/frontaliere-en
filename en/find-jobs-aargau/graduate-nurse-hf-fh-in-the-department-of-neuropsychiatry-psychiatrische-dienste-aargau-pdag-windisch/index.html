Description

For the neuropsychiatric acute ward, our very well-rehearsed and humorous team is looking for you immediately or by appointment as tasks:

  • Inpatient acute care of people with an intellectual developmental disorder with various psychiatric illnesses in close cooperation with the highly qualified interdisciplinary team
  • You competently implement the care process within the framework of caregiver work and share responsibility for the implementation of the therapeutic, nursing and educational measures
  • You support people in acute crises, while supporting relatives and actively include the individual life situation of the patients
  • Thanks to early planning in shift work, you have the opportunity to plan your free time and work assignments in advance Requirements:
  • Completed training as a qualified nurse HF or FH, an advantage with experience in psychiatry, acute geriatrics, acute medicine or with people with intellectual development disorders
  • Willingness and interest to familiarize yourself with a highly specialized treatment concept and develop it further
  • Strong personality Strong social and self-competence, as well as enjoyment of complex challenges
  • Interest in continuous professional development We offer: Our employees are the heart of our company.

That's exactly why we offer you a lot: we strongly encourage further training.

We also invest a lot in team cohesion and offer all of our teams access to the “Moodtalk” software, which strengthens team communication.
