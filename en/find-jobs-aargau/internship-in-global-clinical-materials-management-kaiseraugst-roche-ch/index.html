Main responsibilities

  • The Opportunity In this role, you will have the chance to make a meaningful impact by taking on the following key responsibilities:
  • Ensure seamless availability of drug products and packaging materials to meet clinical manufacturing requirements, guaranteeing timely production of investigational medical products.
  • Maintain accurate master data for drug products in SAP systems, including defining, compiling, and continuously updating product-specific data and documents.
  • Manage purchase orders for development, partner, and commercial drug products from both internal and external suppliers using the appropriate procurement systems.
  • Lead supplier relationship management by monitoring supplier performance through KPIs, driving continuous improvements, and fostering transparent collaborations.
  • Ensure batch availability and readiness of drug products and packaging materials for downstream manufacturing processes.
  • Proactively identify, address, and resolve supply chain and procurement issues to ensure smooth global clinical supply operations.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams in an inclusive, innovative, and international environment to drive excellence in clinical supply chain management.
  • Contribute to a high-performing team culture where teamwork, professional growth, and delivering impact for patients are prioritized.
