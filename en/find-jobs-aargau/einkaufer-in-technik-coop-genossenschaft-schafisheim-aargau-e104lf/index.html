Role overview

Tasks

  • You analyze the global procurement markets for technical components, machines and services and develop sustainable commodity group and supplier strategies
  • You negotiate framework contracts, prices, service-level agreements (SLAs) and conditions with national and international technology partners in coordination with your supervisors or specialist centres
  • You evaluate and audit existing suppliers from the technical environment and build new, strategic partnerships
  • You identify cost-cutting potentials (TCO approach to machines and plants) and secure our technical supply chains through proactive risk management
  • You trigger orders on time and monitor them so that the plants never stand still
  • You have delivery dates under control, initiate payment procedures in the event of delay and clarify quantity or price deviations
  • You are in close contact with the specialist centres of technology such as maintenance and incoming goods to guarantee optimal material availability
  • You keep article and supplier master data in the ERP system clean and optimize them on an ongoing basis
  • Successfully completed commercial training with further training in purchasing
  • Negotiation skills, assertive thinking skills
  • Secure handling of common ERP systems (e.g. SAP, MS Dynamics, Infor) and MS Office (especially Excel
