Application process

  • How you'll make an impact In this challenging position, you will design sub-station control systems (SCADA) for high- and medium-voltage switchgear in accordance with the latest technology standards such as IEC 61850, IEC 104, and other protocols You will integrate the systems, including the parameterization of the Ethernet-based communication structure Furthermore, you will implement project-specific functions and software applications, carry out tests, and coordinate interfaces Supporting on-site commissioning at facilities throughout Switzerland is also part of your role You will support the project manager in technical matters and coordinate both customers and in-ternal partners You will create operating manuals tailored to customer requirements Your background Bachelor's or master's degree in power engineering, electrical engineering, or an equivalent qualification Basic knowledge of control and protection systems, RTU systems, or process automation systems is an advantage You have basic knowledge of cybersecurity and network architecture Alongside strong communication skills and team spirit, you must demonstrate the ability to work independently and take responsibility Fluent German (spoken and written) is required.
