Role overview

Eidg. recognised psychotherapists / psychotherapists (60% annual penum) for flexible representational missions in stationary and semi-stationary setting.

We are a private clinic for psychosomatics, psychiatry and psychotherapy with 108 beds in a central location in Rheinfelden and two outpatients in Rheinfelden and Aarau.

The clinic is recognized by FMH as a training centre for the specialist in psychiatry and psychotherapy (2 years inpatient, 2 years outpatient), as well as by the SAPPM (2 years).
