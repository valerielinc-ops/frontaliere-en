Original description

For the Center for Inpatient Forensic Psychiatry (ZFPS), we are looking for you as tasks immediately or by appointment: • You will work on one of our medium/low security wards in a group setting • You will treat and look after patients from the prison system for measures • You will support and promote the social abilities and skills of the patients in the milieu therapy setting as part of caregiver work • You will be part of the interdisciplinary treatment team and will be involved in treatment planning, day structuring and the design of the living environment • You work both rehabilitatively and preventively in order to avoid future crimes • You maintain contact with relatives and authorities • You are jointly responsible for the training of learners as part of the training assignment Requirements: • You have completed training as a qualified nurse HF/FH and preferably have professional experience in psychiatry • SRK recognition is mandatory for foreign diplomas • Your attitude towards delinquent people is appreciative and risk-aware • You enjoy working in an interdisciplinary team • You have exceptionally high social skills • You have strong communication skills • You are characterized by a very high level of self-reflection and the ability to criticize • You work in a three-shift system We offer: Our employees are the heart of our company.
