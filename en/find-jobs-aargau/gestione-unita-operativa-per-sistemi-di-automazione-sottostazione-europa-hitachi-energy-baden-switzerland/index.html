Role overview

The Opportunity As the Operating Unit Manager for Substation Automation Systems HUB Europe, you will play a pivotal leadership role in shaping the strategic direction, performance, and growth of our Substation Automation portfolio across the region.

You will oversee all commercial, operational, and technical aspects of the HUB, ensuring excellence in project execution, customer satisfaction, and business results.
