Dettagli operativi

Cantonal Taxes and Deductions The tax rate varies significantly from one canton to another. For example, the Canton of Zug has lower tax rates compared to the Canton of Geneva. The deductions for AVS are 6% of the gross salary (3% borne by the employee and 3% borne by the company), while for health insurance, they vary depending on the provider and the type of policy chosen, but on average, they add up to between 7% and 10% of the gross salary (part of which is borne by the company). Frequently Asked Questions
How is the net salary calculated for a cross-border worker in Switzerland?
The net salary is calculated by subtracting cantonal/federal taxes, AVS contributions, and health insurance from the gross income. Use our calculator for a personalized result.
Are AVS contributions borne solely by the worker?
No, AVS contributions are split between the worker (3%) and the employer (3%).
Where can I find the tax rate for my canton of residence?
You can find tax rate information by visiting your canton of residence's website or contacting the local tax office.
