Analysis of the situation The situation of restaurants in Switzerland is critical. The closure of establishments would have a significant impact on workers in the sector and on suppliers. This would have a significant impact on their economic situation and their ability to cover their expenses. ### Alternative solutions It is important to seek alternative solutions to mitigate the impact of a possible closure of restaurants.
