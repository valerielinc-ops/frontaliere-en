Working in the canton of Zug as a cross-border worker

The canton of Zug is one of the main destinations for Italian cross-border workers (frontalieri) chasing salaries that exceed the Italian average. The positions listed here cover every sector — healthcare, construction, manufacturing, services, IT, logistics — and include both full-time (100 %) and part-time (50–80 %) contracts that are standard in Switzerland.

To qualify for the cross-border tax regime under the 2026 New Agreement you must reside in Italy within 20 km of the Swiss border, return home daily (with up to 25 % of days allowed in smart-working from Italy), and hold a G-type work permit.
