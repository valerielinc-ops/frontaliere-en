Role overview

Your field of activity You attach assembled ventilation ducts in the masonry and, where necessary, cover them with insulating material, install motors, fans and filters as well as the control and regulation devices.

After completing the assembly work, you put the systems into operation according to the specified process steps and check whether everything is set correctly.

You are also responsible for the high-quality maintenance, repair and dismantling of ventilation systems and carry out orders that are part of an overall construction process.
