With our in-house crèche right on the area of the cantonal hospital, we support you for a healthy work-life balance that not only works but does well.

The Spitäler Schaffhausen include the Cantonal Hospital, the Psychiatric Services, the Praxen Radiology and Cardiology Posthof Neuhausen and the Practice Neurology Schaffhausen Center.

With over 1'700 employees, we secure the extended medical and nursing basic care of the Schaffhausen region.

Every year we care about 11'000 stationary and 90'000 outpatient patients.

We are also an important training centre in the canton.
