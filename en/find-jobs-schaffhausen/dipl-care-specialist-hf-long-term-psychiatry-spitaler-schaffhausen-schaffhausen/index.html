Role overview

On our 4 stations of long-term psychiatry with 18 beds, chronically mentally ill women and men with cognitive impairments, limitations of independent life and everyday design or pronounced behavioral disorders are supervised.

In addition, the stations offer low-swelling rehabilitation programs for patients who subsequently return to their native conditions or transfer to another institution.
