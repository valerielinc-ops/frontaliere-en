With our in-house crèche directly on the site of the Cantonal Hospital, we support you for a healthy work-life balance that not only works, but also does well.

The hospitals in Schaffhausen include the Canton Hospital, the psychiatric services, the radiology and cardiology practices Posthof Neuhausen and the practice Neurology Schaffhausen Center.

With more than 1,700 employees, we ensure the extended basic medical and nursing care in the Schaffhausen region.

Every year, we look after approximately 11,000 inpatient and 90,000 outpatient patients.

We are also an important training centre in the canton.
