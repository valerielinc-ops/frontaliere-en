Role overview

Post "Infirmary, RFSM Marsens and Villars-sur-Glâne - for spontaneous applications" to the RFSM - Fribourg mental health network, Marsens (FR).

The RFSM is the mental health centre of the canton of Fribourg: adult psychiatry, children/adolescents, elderly people, addictions.

The Friburg Network for Mental Health (RFSM) is attached to the Directorate of Health and Social Affairs (DSAS).

The RFSM is a centre of medical, nursing and psychosocial skills specialized in mental health.

The State-employer encourages the reconciliation of work and private life and offers an attractive working environment and working conditions to its employees.
