Role overview

Your Responsibilities

  • You lead and develop the members of the Subsystems Engineering team by supporting employees, developing and allocating competencies, and ensuring effective team organization
  • You estimate the effort and budget required for Subsystem Owners’ tasks related to projects, serial changes, and problem-solving activities
  • You are responsible for quality, consistency, and timely delivery of development activities and deliverables for the team, while monitoring budget status
  • You are responsible for operational stability, prioritization, and coordination of all Subsystem Owner activities across development and series support
  • You own and develop the subsystem knowledge management system, including failure modes, validation and design guidelines
  • You lead and support 8D problem-solving activities and ensure the capitalization of lessons learned
  • You are responsible for correct definition of Subsystems DVP&R for new projects and serial changes
  • You act as the first-level escalation owner for technical issues, project conflicts, and supplier-related issues, while supporting Subsystems Owners, Project Management, and the Sales department in discussions with suppliers and customers
  • You report to various stakeholders, such as the Department Leader and the Technical Core Team Your Profile
  • You have successfully completed a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering, specializing in internal combustion engines and powertrain engineering
  • You have strong experience and expertise in combustion engine development, subsystem development and validation (DVP&R), a broad understanding of engine subsystems, as well as project management
  • You have an excellent command of English and French
  • You have an excellent command of MS Office
  • You possess proven technical and disciplinary leadership skills, along with strong potential for further development
  • You are able to manage conflicts and escalations effectively, demonstrating strong communication skills within cross-functional teams
  • You are a highly resilient professional with a strong sense of responsibility, capable of working in high-pressure environments while remaining mindful of costs, resources, and strategy Our Offer Can we inspire you to take on a responsible position in a dynamic and independent family business?
