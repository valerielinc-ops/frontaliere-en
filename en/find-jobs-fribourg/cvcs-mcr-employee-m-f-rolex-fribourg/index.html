Main responsibilities

  • Monitor the periodic operation of CVCS installations
  • Carry out preventive and corrective maintenance actions on heating, ventilation, air conditioning and sanitary installations
  • Analyze, diagnose and intervene on various technical faults
  • Manage technical alarms on the supervision tool
  • Optimize the operation of CVCS installation control loops
  • Carry out measurement and balancing campaigns on hydraulic and aeronautical installations
  • Participate in functional tests of critical installation servitudes
  • Manage subcontracting companies to ensure the quality and safety of interventions
  • Ensure follow-up of a team of suppliers in charge of CV installation maintenance
  • Develop descriptions and small specifications for the implementation of technical projects

Key requirements

  • HVAC installer or plumber certification, ideally complemented by a diploma as a Building Technician or equivalent recognized title
  • Confirmed experience in the maintenance of complex CVCS installations, ideally in the industrial sector
  • Mastery of centralized technical management (CTM) tools and associated settings
  • Computer skills (Office Suite and technical tools)
  • Rigor, autonomy and ability to manage priorities in a dynamic environment
  • Team spirit, service orientation and customer focus developed and very good communication skills
