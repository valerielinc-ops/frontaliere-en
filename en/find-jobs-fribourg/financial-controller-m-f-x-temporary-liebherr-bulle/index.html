Role overview

Your Responsibilities

  • As financial controller , you prepare the monthly analytical profit and loss reporting, ensure data consistency, and perform the necessary controls,
  • You conduct variance analyses and prepare the related financial assumptions,
  • You maintain and update the financial databases required for the division’s BI tool,
  • You monitor ongoing projects, analyze variances, and manage the annual investment budget while ensuring compliance with budget constraints,
  • You are responsible for monitoring and analyzing general costs for various departments such as Logistics, Quality, IT, and Facility Management,
  • You support managers in preparing annual GK budgets and in understanding cost drivers and deviations,
  • You analyze the financial aspects related to Excess & Obsolete inventory in close collaboration with the Controlling teams,
  • You perform ad hoc analyses and provide support to stakeholders as needed.

Your Profil

  • You hold a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Business Administration, Economics, or a similar field,
  • You have at least 4 to 5 years of experience in accounting or industrial controlling,
  • You are fluent in French and English and have good knowledge of German,
  • You are proficient in MS Office tools,
  • You are autonomous, organized, and have strong teamwork skills,
  • You stand out for your analytical mindset, proactivity, and discretion,
  • You are available until May 31, 2027.
