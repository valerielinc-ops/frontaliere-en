Role overview

Your Responsibilities

  • You take ownership of system-level control design (design, simulation, V&V, …), contributing to next-generation engine control architectures and digital engineering capabilities
  • You collaborate with multidisciplinary teams, experts, and customers
  • You design, implement, and maintain model-based plant/system models and Model‑in‑the‑Loop (MiL) simulation environments to systematically test, analyze, and validate control functions
  • You perform evidence-based trade‑off analyses to evaluate and optimize alternative technical solutions
  • You support application engineers during commissioning activities at test benches and in real-world field applications
  • You define system-level control specifications, including failure mode analysis, safety and cybersecurity considerations
  • You analyze, evaluate and document the dynamic behavior and interactions of engine control system elements
  • You design, develop and maintain engine control system architectures (hardware, software and services) with a focus on performance, quality and reusability
  • You coordinate with project management and software/hardware development teams to integrate new functions and components, and continuously improve model-based systems engineering methods, processes and toolchains Your Profile
  • You have successfully completed engineering studies in the field of automotive, powertrain, mechanical, mechatronics, electronics engineering, or similar
  • You have solid experience in combustion engine control system development, including multi‑physics modeling, and ideally engine calibration (performance and emissions)
  • You are proficient in Matlab/Simulink and embedded software engineering, with strong skills in model‑based systems engineering (MBSE) and signal processing
  • You are familiar with ALM/PLM and requirements authoring tools, and MBSE environments such as System Composer, Cameo, Rhapsody or similar
  • You bring an analytical mindset, high learnability, agile methods, and collaborate effectively in cross‑functional engineering teams
  • You are willing and able to occasionally travel
  • You are fluent in English Our Offer Can we inspire you to take on a responsible position in a dynamic and independent family business?
