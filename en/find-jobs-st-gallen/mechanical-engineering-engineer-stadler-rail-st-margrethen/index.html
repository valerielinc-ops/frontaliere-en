Description

PROFILE

  • Completed technical training at university level (FH/University)
  • Several years of professional experience in vehicle construction is an advantage
  • Good knowledge of a current 3D CAD system, preferably Catia V5
  • Knowledge of German (level C1) and English (level B2), spoken and written TASKS
  • Responsibility for the technically correct design and development of mechanical assemblies in the interior and exterior of our vehicles
  • Creation, construction and preparation of production-ready documents (3D models, drawings and parts lists) and technical specifications
  • Carrying out technical feasibility studies and concepts as well as developing solutions
  • Collaboration across departments and locations as well as exchange with other departments (e.g. production, purchasing, project management, approval, surface technology)
  • Supporting our suppliers and customers with regard to technical issues
  • Supporting our production during the evaluation and initial use of new products
  • Responsible as the responsible specialist for advising and supporting engineering and project management throughout the entire project phase
