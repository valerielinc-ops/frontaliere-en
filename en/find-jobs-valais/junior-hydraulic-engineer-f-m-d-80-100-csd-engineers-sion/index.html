Main responsibilities

  • Your responsibilities:
  • Primarily work in river hydraulics while contributing to various urban hydraulics projects
  • Analyze and manage hydrological risks in an integrated manner: river flooding, erosion, surface runoff
  • Design, dimension, and oversee projects for river management, safety measures, and renaturation (SIA 31–53 standards), particularly on multiple watercourses and within projects related to the third Rhône correction Support urban hydraulics projects:
  • Develop and update municipal water drainage plans (PGEE)
  • Plan and dimension pressurized networks and infrastructure (collectors, pumping stations, discharge structures) according to SIA 31–53 standards
  • Actively participate in several municipal PGEE projects and the planning of new networks

Key requirements

  • Engineering degree with a specialization in river and urban hydraulics (hydrology, sediment transport, river management, hydraulic structures)
  • Strong interest in hydrological risks, mountain watercourses, and urban runoff, with a genuine desire to learn
  • Knowledge of Swiss norms and regulations (EPFL, EPFZ, or equivalent; experience in Switzerland is an asset)
  • Autonomy, critical thinking, versatility, and strong writing skills
  • Proficiency in QGIS and hydraulic modeling software (Basement, HEC-RAS, and/or SWMM) Fluency in French; German and English are assets Valid driver’s license
