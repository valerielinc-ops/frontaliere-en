Original description

The Plantahof is a service in the Department of Economics and Social Affairs and the Centre for Agricultural Education and Consulting in the Canton of Graubünden. In addition to the agricultural professional school, the Plantahof offers various further training courses, training courses and conferences and is responsible for the comprehensive consultation and the placement of specialist knowledge for the Bündner and Glarner farms and alpine farms and other circles that move on agricultural topics. For these purposes he also conducts his own trial and estate operations at four different locations including two court shops. Finally, it has a conference centre with boarding school, lecture hall and a catering facility. - Development of individual operating concepts as well as strategic basis for decision-making for family farms - preparation of viability calculations and financial reports - consultation of business management families in the process of transfer of farms - consultation of private individuals, communities and other private and public institutions in agricultural matters - organisation and implementation of training events in the field of business development - networking within the development of farms and beyond the areas of training The general working language is German. Knowledge in another cantonal language is advantageous
