Role overview

Responsibilities: Volunteers will be deployed in the following areas:

  • Reception: Accompany newly admitted patients to their respective wards
  • Visiting: Ease the hospital stay for patients through conversation
  • Sunday service: Accompany patients to religious services and bring them back to their wards
  • Orientation assistance: Provide directions or accompany patients, guests, and visitors to their desired locations in the entrance area
  • Requirements: - No special training is required to work with us.

What matters is your willingness to help and your enthusiasm for interacting with people in the hospital.

As a volunteer, you commit to regularly dedicating two to four hours per week to a specific task — excluding nursing duties — and to maintaining confidentiality.

  • The focus is not on performance but on your willingness to take time for both patients and visitors.

This role is based on voluntary work and is unpaid.

However, you will benefit from various perks offered by Kantonsspital Graubünden and gain the rewarding feeling of doing meaningful work.
