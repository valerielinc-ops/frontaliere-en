Role overview

In the canton of Solothurn, BACHTELEN is committed to the individual promotion and integration of children, young people and young adults.

In order to achieve our goals, we guide day care centers, day care schools, housing offers, offers of individual promotion as well as therapeutic and advisory offers.

We are looking for the school year 2026/2027 for the day school Grenchen from 01.08.2026 School teachers for cycle 1 and 2 (40 – 60 %) or an instructor with willingness, training within the next two years Tasks Teaching and funding of normal-skilled pupils with special educational needs in the areas of behaviour, learning and communication Class management of an age-mixed class of 8 – 9 pupils in cycle 1 and 2 Close cooperation in a multi-professional team consisting of the disciplines of medical education, social education, therapy & counseling We are looking forward to the It is also a pleasure to take new educational paths, for example through learning in nature or action-oriented teaching.
