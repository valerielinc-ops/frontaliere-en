Company and context

  • As part of the VIVA project, the Arc Network has become Switzerland's first fully integrated care organization to propose a health insurance plan.
  • The VIVA health plan is accessible to the inhabitants of the Jura Arc, ensuring optimal and coordinated care.
  • At the heart of this dynamic, the Biel Medical Centre (CMB) fully embodies the values of accessibility, continuity of care and coordination.
  • A true centre of first-recourse medicine, it brings together a multidisciplinary team of general practitioners, specialists, nurses, psychologists, therapists and healthcare staff.
  • Each patient is accompanied by a referral physician, who guarantees a personalized follow-up in the long term.
  • The CMB is distinguished by its integrated approach, in close association with partner hospitals and other entities of the Arc Network.
  • It offers a wide range of services: medical consultations, outpatient emergencies, specialist care, paediatric follow-up, mental health, physiotherapy, wound care, dental medicine, technical examinations (radiology, laboratory, etc.).
  • Thanks to its Walk-in Clinic, the CMB also responds to urgent needs without an appointment, Monday to Saturday.
  • It offers fast, seamless and coordinated access to care, reinforced by the expertise of the Swiss Medical Network to which it has been linked since 2020.
  • Its general medical practice VIVA completes this offer, to the insured of the VIVA health plan a personalized accompaniment, integrated within the very medical center.
