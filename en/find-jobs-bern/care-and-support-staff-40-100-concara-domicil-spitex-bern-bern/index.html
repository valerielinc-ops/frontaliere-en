Role overview

Tasks: Are you ready to actively shape the future of nursing?

We are looking for a committed and innovative nursing and support staff member to support our team in the further development and implementation of our modern role organisation.

You see caring for and caring for people with dementia as an enriching challenge.

You deal with relatives and confidants in a professional and empathetic manner.

Our newly introduced role model focuses on strengthening and promoting each team member's individual resources.

The aim is to relieve the burden on the entire care team in the long term.

Depending on your skills and interests, you have the opportunity to take on additional tasks in departments or in coordination.

Requirements

  • Training at assistant level (assistant:in health/social affairs EBA, nursing assistant FA), course SRK/basic course VBB
  • You have experience in the care of people with dementia and are familiar with the relevant requirements for the implementation of professional care and support
  • You like to work in a team, are solution-oriented and attach great importance to further development.

We offer

  • Appreciative corporate culture and value-oriented leadership culture
  • Independent action in a motivated and supportive working environment
