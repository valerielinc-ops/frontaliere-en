Application process

  • As an ICT application manager, you not only take responsibility – you create real impact.
  • Together with your dedicated colleagues, specialists and external partners, you shape the digital future of our hospital – close to the human being, close to the pulse of medicine.
  • Your everyday life is as diverse as our clinic operation – and as important.
  • You are co-responsible for the clinical information system (CGM Phönix) and, together with the responsible medical informant, ensure a stable operation
  • You are also responsible for various other medical applications (LIS, RIS, PACS, HCM, etc.) and monitor them throughout their life cycle
  • You analyse requirements, accompany their implementation and coordinate internal and external partners
  • You carry out smaller projects, subprojects and actively work on larger projects
  • You work closely with specialist areas, ICT infrastructure & services, ICT support, external service providers and manufacturers
