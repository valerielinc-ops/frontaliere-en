Role overview

Introduction For our Information Systems department, we are looking for various Business Analysts "CFAO" whose main mission will be to have functional responsibility for part of the application scope meeting the business needs in the areas of either Control Programming (CAI) or Digital Model Modelling (CAD).

Responsibilities

  • Identify and analyse the needs of the trade either for the CAI part or for the CAD part
  • Propose software solutions to meet the needs in line with the SI strategy to be deployed (rationalisation and integration)
  • Ensure the digital chain of the product development cycle and software developments in partnership with our suppliers
  • To ensure the operational maintenance of the applications for which you are responsible in partnership with the other internal actors (Infrastructure / Security / Network / Customer Post) Profile
  • Engineering school with specialization on the industrial perimeter
  • 5 years of seniority in the field of software administration
  • Master either the METROLOG (Metrologic Group) application for the CAI part or the CREO application (PTC) for the CAD part
  • Skills on software integration in an industrial IT environment
  • Good knowledge of English (B2)
  • B one ability for synthesis and stress management
  • Communicating and reconciling Conclusion We offer you a stimulating environment, excellent working conditions, flexible working time solutions and top social benefits.
