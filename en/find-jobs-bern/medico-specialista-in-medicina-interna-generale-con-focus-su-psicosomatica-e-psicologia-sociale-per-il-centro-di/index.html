Role overview

The Hasliberg Rehabilitation Clinic is a renowned Swiss clinic in the field of internal oncology, psychosomatic and musculoskeletal rehabilitation.

With the Center for Pain and Stress Medicine in Bern, we run a specialized outpatient competence center for the integrative treatment of chronic pain and stress sequelae – an offer with a consistently biopsychosocial approach that is unique in the region.
