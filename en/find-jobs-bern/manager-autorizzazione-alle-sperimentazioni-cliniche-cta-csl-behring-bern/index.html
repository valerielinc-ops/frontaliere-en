Role overview

The Manager, Clinical Trial Authorization (CTA) is responsible for ensuring that CTA activities for assigned clinical studies are planned, governed, and overseen in line with CSL regulatory expectations, quality standards, and development timelines.

Reporting to the Director, Global Regulatory CTA, the role translates global CTA direction into clear, study‑specific regulatory approaches, provides guidance to Study Teams and partners, and acts as the first line CTA point of contact across functions.

The position ensures that CTA activities delivered by external partners are appropriately overseen, risks are identified early and managed proactively.
