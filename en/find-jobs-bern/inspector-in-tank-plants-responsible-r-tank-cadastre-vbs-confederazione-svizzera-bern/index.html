Description

Responsible for the safe operation and operational readiness of tank plants in the allocated regions through periodic operational inspections, the implementation of specifications and the ongoing technical support of plant operators Working on new and renovation projects as well as providing technical support for their acceptance and approval of operations

  • Planning and conducting the various training courses for the specialist personnel of tank plants as a course instructor and/or specialist teacher The management of the tank cadastre VBS, the planning of the legally required maintenance of tank plants and the ongoing mutations are responsible
  • The processing of technical instructions, regulations and regulations for the operation and maintenance of tank plants supported
  • Basic technical training as a mechatronics technician, electrical specialist or machine drawing technician as well as a further training as a technician, master examination automotive clerk or similar
  • Training as a specialist in operational water protection, fire protection and hazardous goods officer / r
  • Experience in the areas of filling stations, tank and emergency power systems as well as pipelines for the transport of liquid fuels
  • Team players with business thinking and acting
  • Good didactic, editorial and rhetorical skills and active knowledge of a second official language
