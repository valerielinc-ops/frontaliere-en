Role overview

Tasks: Together with the HR Services team (4 employees), you ensure correct wage processing for around 2,200 employees.

You independently carry out the monthly payment and accounting runs, including controls and mutations.

You are jointly responsible for the comprehensive handling of issues relating to wages and handle social security cases.

You advise employees and managers on questions relating to payroll accounting and time management.

You will also be actively involved in system maintenance and the further development of payroll and time management processes.

Our aim is to harmonize the wage systems and employment conditions (including pension fund) of Domicil and Spitex.

If you have (project) experience in this area, that also speaks for you.

An exciting and varied job awaits you in a dynamic and appreciative environment with plenty of room for development and design.
