Role overview

Tasks: Are you ready to actively shape the future of care?

We are looking for a committed and innovative nursing and care employee who will support our team in the further development and implementation of our modern role organization.

Caring for and accompanying people fulfills you and you are committed to their well-being.

When dealing with relatives and people you trust, you always act sensitively and professionally.

Our newly introduced role model focuses on strengthening and promoting the individual resources of each team member.

The aim is to sustainably relieve the burden on the entire nursing team.

Depending on your skills and interests, you have the opportunity to take on additional tasks in specialist areas or in coordination.

Requirements

  • Training at assistant level (Health/Social Assistant EBA, Nursing Assistant FA), SRK course/VBB basic course
  • You have experience in caring for people with dementia and are familiar with the relevant requirements for providing professional care and support
  • You enjoy working in a team, are solution-oriented and attach great importance to further development.

We offer
