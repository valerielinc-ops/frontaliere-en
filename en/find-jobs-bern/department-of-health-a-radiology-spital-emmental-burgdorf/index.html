Role overview

The Spital Emmental offers specialists at the two hospital locations Burgdorf and Langnau for the high-standing medical care of 150 000 inhabitants – around the clock.

Our Institute for Radiology and Neuroradiology is responsible for imaging diagnostics at both hospital locations and is responsible for the detection of conventional X-ray examinations of several large-scale practices in the catchment area.

Thanks to the subspecialization of radiology and an independent neuroradiology department, we guarantee highest diagnostic quality.
