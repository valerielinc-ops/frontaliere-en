Role overview

The pain clinic treats outpatient patients with chronic pain syndromes.

They work in an interdisciplinary team consisting of psychology, anaesthesiology, nursing and other disciplines.

For our pain clinic at the Langenthal site, we are looking for a dedicated personality as what you can do with us

  • The pain clinic treats outpatient patients with chronic pain syndromes.
  • They work in an interdisciplinary team consisting of psychology, anaesthesiology, nursing and other disciplines.

Application process

  • Application of relaxation methods (e.g. progressive muscle relaxation, biofeedback, MBSR)
  • Implementation of assessments incl. Questionnaire diagnosis
  • Compiling progress reports and final reports
  • Presentation of the pain clinic after reading about e.g. lectures/posters
  • Close interdisciplinary cooperation (including physiotherapy and psychiatry) This will bring you
  • University degree in Psychology (University or University of Applied Sciences)
  • Complete training in recognised psychotherapy, preferably behavioral therapy
  • Experience in the field of pain psychology or pronounced affinity to the subject area Experience in group therapy
