Role overview

This role is a position in the Drug Delivery Systems & Packaging (DDSP) department within CSL Technical Operations.

The ideal candidate has a solid track record in the development of drug delivery devices, from concept development through commercialization and product maintenance.

This role requires experience in leading user interface development and a passion for making the right product that meets the needs of the patients.

Main responsibilities

  • Act as Lead Human Factors Engineer
