Role overview

At PostFinance, we develop high-quality applications that reach our customers as quickly as possible with modern technologies and high quality.

Our test automation team provides the right tools to ensure efficient test processes from system level onwards.

As a Lead Developer (f/m/d), you not only actively shape the end-to-end test automation solution, but also lead the team in technical decisions, promote best practices and drive innovation – with expertise, team spirit and strategic vision.

We meet twice a month in Bern and also twice a month at another location (Zofingen, Olten or Zurich).

As a lead developer (f/m/d), you and the team shape the implementation of

Appium, Selenium) In-depth knowledge of software architecture as well as test automation of web UIs, mobile apps and APIs is advantageous Experience with Kubernetes, Docker, GitLab and CI/CD pipelines as well as in the development, analysis and troubleshooting of large, decentralized systems Very good German and English skills
