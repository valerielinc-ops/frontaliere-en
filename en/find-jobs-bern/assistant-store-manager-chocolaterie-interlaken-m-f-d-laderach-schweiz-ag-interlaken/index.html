Original description

High-quality fresh chocolate - that's what Swiss chocolate manufacturer Läderach stands for. Founded in 1962, the family-owned company now employs over 2,500 people from more than 80 countries, with a growing trend. The company's headquarters are located in the canton of Glarus. Against a picturesque Alpine backdrop, we produce our fresh chocolates and develop new, innovative ideas in agile teams. The 'Retail' department comprises over 250 chocolateries in more than 30 countries. Here, we offer our chocolate specialties and invite customers to experience fresh chocolate again and again. Do you also want to be part of it? Then come to us. You will be expected to. You support the store management in the daily management and organization of the store. You offer competent and customer-oriented advice and support in the selection of gifts from our extensive product range. You take care of the attractive presentation of our extensive confectionery and chocolate assortment, including creative store design. You guarantee top quality and consistently apply our high standards. You are an important contact person for the team and for customers. You contribute to the optimization of work processes and ensure that the store operates efficiently and profitably. This is what you bring with you. You bring first-hand experience in store management in the fashion retail sector. You have completed a training course in retail and/or have comparable experience in working with food products. You are proficient in German and English; additional languages are an additional advantage. You love a hands-on mentality and are technologically savvy. You are enthusiastic about optimization and have an entrepreneurial mindset. You have an exceptional sense of quality and hygiene. You are not afraid of flexible working hours (365 days open) and are also available on weekends, Saturdays, Sundays, or public holidays. This is what we offer you. An exciting working environment with motivated colleagues
