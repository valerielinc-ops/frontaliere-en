Company and context

  • For our ICT at the Burgdorf location, we are looking for someone immediately or by appointment: Your scope: We are specifically expanding our ICT & digitalization organization and creating the role of Business Analyst for Digital Transformation.
  • In this key position, you are the link between departments, the clinic and ICT - you translate technical requirements into viable digital solutions and actively drive the transformation of our hospital.
  • You will start with a focus on migrating our ERP platform (Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central)
  • especially in the areas of patient administration, service recording and pharmacy
  • and will expand your scope to include further digitalization projects across clinical and administrative processes.
  • You report directly to the Head of Transformation & Digitalization and work closely with the Digitalization Board, the specialists, external partners and ICT managers.
  • Everyday work is varied, demanding and makes an important contribution to the smooth functioning and future viability of our hospital.
  • Collection, analysis and structuring of technical requirements from clinics, care and administration - with a consistent focus on benefits, feasibility and profitability
  • Analysis, modeling and further development of business processes (ACTUAL/TARGET analysis, BPMN, weak point and potential analysis), especially in the context of ERP migration
  • Development of specifications, user stories, epics and solution concepts in close coordination with specialist departments, architecture board and ICT
