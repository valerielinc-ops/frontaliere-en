Role overview

The Global Head of Maintenance and Innovation Strategy is a senior leadership position responsible for defining, leading, and executing the organization’s global maintenance vision and innovation roadmap.

This role is essential for ensuring world-class asset reliability, operational efficiency, and long-term competitiveness by utilizing advanced technologies, data-driven insights, and continuous improvement methodologies.

This position oversees global teams, establishes strategic direction, global maintenance procedures, standardizes maintenance frameworks, and drives innovation programs across all sites and business units.
