Role overview

The SRO Psychiatric Services ensure regional, community and patient-oriented psychiatric care for 80 000 inhabitants.

We have modern, integrated supply structures such as emergency trip, station-equivalent mobile acute treatment, integrative psychiatry care, social psychiatric and psychotherapeutic outpatients at 4 locations with special offers, as well as rehabilitation day clinic, acute day clinic, age psychiatry with ambulatory and day clinic, and a stationary offer with 34 beds in Spital Langenthal.

We have the SIWF recognition as a further education in category A for 3 years, both outpatient and stationary.
