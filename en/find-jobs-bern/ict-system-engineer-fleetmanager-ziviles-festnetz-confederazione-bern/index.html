Description

  • Coordinate the use and further development of the civilian fiber optic networks and connections for military operations with the civilian partner
  • Build and maintain the inventory of the available network connection points from the military to the civilian networks and the civilian connection points that can be used for military purposes
  • Build and further develop the ordering, operation and support processes for the military use of civilian connections together with the civilian partner
  • Collaborate in projects to realize the use of civilian mobile phone and fiber optic networks and connections for military use
  • 2nd Level Operations: Troubleshooting and escalation management in the event of disruptions to civil network connections as well as changes at the civil/military interfaces
  • Create documentation as well as knowledge building and training for the operations team and users
  • Training as a computer scientist (HF/FH), technician HF or engineer (HF/FH) in the field of telecommunications or IT
  • Sound knowledge of fiber optic networks as well as a basic understanding of mobile communications technologies (4G/LTE, 5G, Mobile Core, VoLTE, IMS)
  • Very good knowledge of IP networks (TCP/IP, routing, switching, DNS, VPN, firewalls)
  • Knowledge of monitoring, troubleshooting and quality assurance of communication systems
  • Basic knowledge of security aspects of communication networks (e.g. encryption, authentication, certificate management)
  • Structured and independent way of working in operations and project environments, including cooperation with suppliers
