Role overview

Support and take responsibility for the operational implementation of domotics/digitalization (building automation/security systems/OT networks/directional and management applications) in the area of technical building management in the LBA

  • Participate in the development, implementation and implementation of technical specifications in the field of domotics/digitalization
  • Support technical operator organisations in the army logistics centres throughout Switzerland and the specialist responsible for domotics/digitisation
  • carry out technical inspections and audits in the real estate defense and prepare the relevant inspection reports
  • Support construction projects in planning and implementation as well as ensuring the operation, maintenance and management of the life cycle management of building technology facilities
  • Contact person for internal and external partners in the field of domotics/digitalization
  • Completed basic training as an automatist, building informatician or in the electrotechnical sector with EFZ
  • Higher education in the field of domotics/digitalization, technician/in HF/FH (automation, building automation, electrical and safety technology), project manager/in building technology BP/building automation BP
  • Experience with complex and security-relevant projects
  • Communication talent, strong analytical and conceptual thinking as well as goal- and result-oriented
  • oral and written knowledge of a second official language
