Role overview

Are you skilled in organization and planning? In this exciting pivotal role, you will support our transport team with nightly deliveries to branches, making a valuable contribution to a satisfying customer experience. As part of the night team, you will ensure that all preparations for the delivery day are completed and that branches are supplied on time.

Responsibilities

  • Ensure the correct and punctual preparation of vehicles and trailer compositions
  • Active collaboration with various internal departments such as dispatch, loading, and return logistics
  • Strict adherence to safety and operational guidelines to ensure workplace safety and compliance with operational standards
  • Perform minor maintenance tasks on vehicles and equipment to ensure their operational readiness and longevity

Requirements

  • Professional experience: as a driver
  • Willingness to work night shifts. Working hours: 21:30-06:30 / 22:30-07:45 / 23:55 – 08:30
  • Possession of a category CE driving license
  • Personality characterized by teamwork, resilience, and flexibility
  • Strong safety awareness and responsible behavior
  • No training/continuing education required
  • German (business fluent)

What we offer

  • Healthy working: Corporate health management including Friendly Workspace certification
  • Occupational pension: Above-average benefits ensure financial security for employees in retirement
  • Social benefits: Attractive working conditions with excellent social benefits
  • Cumulus points: Multiple Cumulus points on food and non-food items
  • Leisure and cultural offers: Various discounts on holiday offers, language stays, Migros Ferien, Interhome, and events
  • Catering: Extensive culinary offerings at discounted prices
