Role overview

The social paediatric centre (SPZ) is an organizational unit at the center of the cantonal hospital Winterthur.

In the stationary area, the SPZ leads a therapy station for 12 patients (alternatively mixed).

Children and adolescents receive a multimodal stationary treatment due to psychosomatic and psychiatric illnesses (key points: eating disorders, depression, anxiety, pain and forced disorders).

They are accompanied by milieu-therapeutic and visit the clinic school daily.
