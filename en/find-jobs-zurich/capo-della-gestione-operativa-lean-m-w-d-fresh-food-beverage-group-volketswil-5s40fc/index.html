Main responsibilities

  • Management and further development of the operational lean team with a clear OPEX orientation
  • Control and implementation of improvement initiatives to sustainably increase efficiency, quality and productivity
  • Further development of the lean management system (e.g. shop floor management, KPI logic, problem-solving routines, 5S, standards)
  • Responsibility for structured improvement projects including Six Sigma / DMAIC (analysis, implementation, stabilization, impact measurement)
  • Coaching of managers and teams in the company (from team leaders to operations managers) to anchor lean principles in everyday management
  • Conception and implementation of training, workshops and Kaizen formats to ensure knowledge transfer
  • Ensuring measurable results (e.g.
  • OEE, output, scrap, disruptions, set-up times) and establishing transparent performance control
  • Active participation in reporting, incl. Provision of relevant data and key figures
  • Collaboration across locations with other companies to harmonize standards and best practices Requirements
