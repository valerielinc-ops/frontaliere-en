Role overview

Our rehabilitation clinic Limmattal offers specialized musculoskeletal and neurological rehabilitation including surveillance rehabilitation as well as accident rehabilitation at a high medical level.

Our interdisciplinary team ensures, through targeted diagnostics and individually coordinated therapy programs, that our patients are optimally supervised on their way back to more health and quality of life.

Due to the close cooperation with the hospital Limmattal and the direct connection to the medical emergency station, a timely acute-somatic treatment is always ensured even in case of emergency complications.
