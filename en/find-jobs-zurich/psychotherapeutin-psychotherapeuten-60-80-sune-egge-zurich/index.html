Role overview

Our specialist hospital for social medicine and dependency diseases Sune-Egge offers stationary and outpatient treatment of patients with combined somatic and psychiatric and diseases.

For them we have 22 acute and 20 long-term care beds in Zurich and work closely with public hospitals, doctors, local departments and addiction institutions.

They take psychotherapeutic and psychosocial treatments in stationary and outpatient settings.
