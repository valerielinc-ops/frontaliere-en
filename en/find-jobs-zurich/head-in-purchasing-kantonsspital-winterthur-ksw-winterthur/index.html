Role overview

In this responsible management function, you guide the purchase of the Cantonal Hospital Winterthur and, together with your team, ensure an economical, legally secure and reliable procurement for a modern centre hospital.

You are responsible for the operational and strategic development of the purchase, guide your team professionally and personnelly, ensuring professional procurement processes, a high quality of service compared to the departments, and effective cooperation with internal and external partners.

A special focus is on the further development of the purchasing processes, the safe operation and the optimization of the ERP and P2P systems used and the correct implementation of the requirements of public procurement law.
