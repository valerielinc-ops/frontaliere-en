Role overview

Tasks

  • As a HF student, you are jointly responsible for the planning, implementation and evaluation of the nursing process
  • In an interdisciplinary team, you will work together to ensure that the residents feel safe and comfortable in everyday life.

In doing so, you will build a professional relationship with the residents and represent their concerns

  • If there are medical concerns, you will arrange targeted care and treatment, in close consultation with the vocational trainers
  • Other central topics include the development of coping strategies and the targeted promotion of resources based on care planning for specific clinical pictures Requirements:
  • Federal certificate of competency, technical or high school diploma, vocational secondary school diploma or comparable qualification
  • Enjoy accompanying and supporting people Impairments
  • Process- and solution-oriented and independent way of working
  • Good knowledge of spoken and written German
  • You are responsible, a team player and sociable, have empathy and good observation skills We offer:
  • Weekly learning day for good theory - practical transfer together with the students of the Lengg Clinic
  • Assignments in the various areas of the Swiss Epilepsy Foundation
  • Professional support in practice by vocational trainers
  • 5 weeks of vacation
  • Discounts in the EPI Park Restaurant, in the pharmacy and with a half-fare or general season ticket.

Sometimes quite challenging, but never boring: at EPI WohnWerk, no two days are the same.

Because the people we support each have their own unique life story.

Thanks to the collaboration in the interprofessional team, we are ideally positioned for our working day.
