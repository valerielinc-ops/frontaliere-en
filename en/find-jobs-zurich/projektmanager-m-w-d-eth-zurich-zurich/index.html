Role overview

Projektmanager (m/w/d) 80%-100%, Zurich, unlimited print Print To support research and teaching, ETH Zurich's computer science services act as a service organization in a large and complex IT environment.

We offer high-quality, flexible and scalable computer science services to members of ETH Zurich and support them in competition with the best universities in the world.

Do you want to actively shape the future of a powerful, secure and sustainable IT infrastructure?

In this key role, you take over the cross-border responsibility for our compute and storage services, perform several teams and shape the strategic further development of our platforms.

You combine entrepreneurial thinking, leadership and technological vision.

We are looking for a group management Compute & Storage (m/w/d) immediately or by agreement.

Description They run the Compute & Storage group independently and are responsible for the entire operation of the associated services.

Ensure that our systems meet the highest requirements in terms of availability, stability, security and compliance and at the same time are responsible for the further development of standards and guidelines.

In close coordination with internal stakeholders and interface partners, you take over the escalation and requirement management, control external service providers and sourcing partners and drive the modernisation of the platforms – also in the context of multi-vendor service designs – consistently ahead.

In addition, you represent your services in strategic IT infrastructure projects and make decisions in corresponding Steering Committees.
