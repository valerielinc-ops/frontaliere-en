Postdoctoral position – Co-evaluating bio-inputs for Smallholder Farmers in Kenya 100%, Zurich, fixed-term print Drucken The Sustainable Agroecosystem group at ETH Zurich is looking for a highly motivated Postdoc for a project on smallholder farmer relevant agroecological innovations in Kenya.

The Sustainable Agroecosystem Group focuses on the feedbacks between agroecosystem management options, plant-soil interactions, nutrient cycling, and soil microbiome functioning within a transdisciplinary framework to ensure relevance and viability of the research outcomes for a farmer-led transition to sustainable agriculture.
