chevron left Overview chevron left Overview Scientific project management/coordination pilot project “PolyCenter” 40%-50%, Zurich, limited print Print For the construction, management and content-didactic support of the pilot phase of the PolyCenter, we are looking for a dedicated, organizational personality.

The PolyCenter is a strategic pilot project at ETH Zurich as part of the PAKETH reform.

The aim is to develop the methodological basic training in mathematics and computer science for BSc students of natural and system-oriented sciences (especially courses at D‐BIOL, D‐CHAB, D-EAPS, D‐HEST and D‐USYS) in a future-oriented and inclusive manner.

Innovative teaching and learning formats break up existing technical and curricular silos and create bridges between abstract theory and concrete application.

As a scientific project manager, you are the operational engine of the PolyCenter and act as an interface between lecturers, students and administration.

  • Overall coordination of the project including milestone and time planning
  • Content-didactic support and coordination of the development of the learning material
  • • Recruitment and professional support of the assistants (Senior-Student-Coaches)
  • Project communication, stakeholder management and planning and evaluation of evaluation measures Profile
  • Universitäts-MSc degree in an MINT-Disciplin
  • Experience in higher education and/or discipline The possibility to actively shape a visionary, ETH-wide teaching project from conception to implementation
  • A varied activity at the interface of science, teaching and management
  • A dedicated, interdisciplinary environment at D‐MATH and in the departments involved
