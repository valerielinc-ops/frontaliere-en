Key requirements

  • They have legal training.
  • They have very good knowledge of German and English;
  • Other languages are advantageous.
  • They have experience in dealing with the various stakeholders, preferably a university environment, especially with the younger generation (young professionals, researchers and students) and with different cultures.
  • They are an intimate personality with pronounced communication skills and an unbiased, positive and respectful basic attitude.
  • A training and/or experience in coaching, conflict resolution and/or mediation is advantageous.
  • The composition of the ombuds office is based on gender balance and reference to ETH Zurich.
  • As an ombudsperson at ETH Zurich, you will become part of a small team of experts who value respect, support and open communication.
  • Our ombudspersons have access to training on relevant topics such as conflict and respect management.
  • This responsible task is an excellent opportunity to introduce your skills in a multicultural and dynamic environment.
