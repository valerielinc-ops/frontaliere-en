Role overview

The Klinik Lengg AG in Zurich is the competence center for epileptology and neurological rehabilitation.

It is our goal to enable patients with neurological diseases to undergo as extensive rehabilitation as possible and to ensure accurate diagnosis and sustainable treatment of patients with epilepsy.

Around 300 employees are engaged humanly, competently, innovatively and reliably in regaining the greatest possible self-employment and thus their quality of life
