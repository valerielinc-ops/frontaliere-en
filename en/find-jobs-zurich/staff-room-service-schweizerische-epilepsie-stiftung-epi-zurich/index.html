Role overview

The Swiss Epilepsy Foundation (EPI) is a multi-saving company.

It includes the EPI WohnWerk, the EPI Spitalschule, the Lengg High School, the donation Dapples, a seminar center with restaurant and the EPI Central Services.

In addition, the Swiss Epilepsie Foundation, together with another foundation, forms the body of the Lengg AG Clinic, a clinic for epilepsy and neurorehabilitation.
