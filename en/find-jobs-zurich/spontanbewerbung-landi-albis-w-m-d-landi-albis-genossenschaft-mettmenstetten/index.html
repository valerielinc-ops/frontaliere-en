Role overview

The LANDI Albis cooperative is active in the fields of agricultural trade, retail trade, bakery, energy and real estate.

In the Knonaueramt and in the Limmat valley, LANDI Albis is present on the market with an agricultural competence center, 2 grain collection points, eleven Volg shops, a primary shop, a LANDI shop, the bakery confectionery "Albis Beck" with four cafés, petrol stations and residential and commercial properties as a company with around 200 employees.
