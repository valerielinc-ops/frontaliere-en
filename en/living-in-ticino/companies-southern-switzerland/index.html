The companies directory lists the major employers in southern Switzerland (Canton Ticino), organized by sector: banking, insurance, pharmaceutical, IT, manufacturing, retail, public administration, and international organizations.

Frequently asked questions

Which are the main Swiss companies that hire cross-border workers?
In 2026 the main Italian-speaking Swiss companies hiring cross-border workers are concentrated in 5 sectors: banking-finance (UBS, BancaStato, Raiffeisen Svizzera italiana, EFG), pharmaceutical/biotech (Helsinn in Lugano, IBSA in Lamone, Cosmo Pharmaceuticals in Lainate-Mendrisio, Cerbios-Pharma in Barbengo), medtech industry (Medacta International in Castel San Pietro, Daetwyler), ICT and services (PostFinance, SUPSI, Swisscom business, Rhenus logistics in Stabio), luxury retail (VF International in Stabio, Gucci Logistics in Cadempino). For the full official list of authorised cross-border employers see [SECO — Authorised staffing agencies](https://www.sem.admin.ch/sem/en/home/themen/arbeit/berufsvermittlung-und-personalverleih/zugelassene_personalverleiher.html).
How many companies are based in Canton Ticino and where can I find them in 2026?
About 36,700 active companies are registered in Canton Ticino (source: [USTAT — 2024 company census](https://www3.ti.ch/DFE/DR/USTAT/index.php?fuseaction=temi.dati&proID=28)), of which more than 5,300 have at least 10 employees. 52% are based in the Lugano district, 22% in the Mendrisiotto, 11% in Luganese-Malcantone and 15% in Bellinzonese-Locarnese. Where to find them: Register of Commerce (zefix.ch) for official data, Orell-Fuessli for balance sheets, Moneyhouse for corporate networks, SUPSI Alumni for direct contacts. Corporate job openings are aggregated on JobUp, JobScout24 and our [Frontaliere Ticino — Job Board](/cerca-lavoro-ticino). Italian cross-border workers (over 74,000 according to UST) make up nearly 30% of the Ticino workforce.
How to apply to Italian-Swiss companies hiring cross-border workers?
To apply effectively to Italian-Swiss companies in 2026 you need 4 documents: CV in Europass or Swiss format (1-2 pages, ID photo, detailed education EFZ/matura, languages with CEFR level), motivation letter tailored to the company, copy of the valid G permit (or declaration of application), Italian criminal-record extract translated. Main channels: corporate career pages (UBS Careers, Medacta Careers, Helsinn Careers), LinkedIn filtered on 'Canton Ticino', JobUp/JobScout24, SECO-authorised temp agencies (Adecco, Manpower, Randstad, Kelly Services). For healthcare workers (OSS/nurses) applications go through MEBEKO diploma recognition. Average selection time: 3-6 weeks. Source: [SECO — Labour market guidance](https://www.sem.admin.ch/sem/en/home/themen/arbeit.html).
