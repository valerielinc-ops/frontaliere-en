Ticino school calendar 2026-2027

The school holiday calendar in Canton Ticino is set annually by the Department of Education, Culture and Sport (DECS) and applies to public kindergartens (ages 3-6), primary schools (6-11) and lower secondary schools (11-15). Private schools generally follow the same calendar with 1-2 day deviations.

2026-2027 holiday dates

For the 2026-2027 school year the official DECS dates in Canton Ticino are: school start Monday 31 August 2026; autumn break Saturday 31 October - Sunday 8 November 2026; Christmas holidays Thursday 24 December 2026 - Wednesday 6 January 2027; carnival Saturday 6 - Sunday 14 February 2027; Easter Friday 26 March - Sunday 4 April 2027; Ascension Thursday 6 May 2027 with a long weekend to Sunday 9 May (Friday 7 May is a day off); Whit Monday 17 May 2027; Corpus Christi Thursday 27 May 2027; end of school year Wednesday 16 June 2027. Summer holidays start on Thursday 17 June 2027. The Immaculate Conception (Tuesday 8 December 2026) and St Joseph (Friday 19 March 2027) are additional days off.

Enrolment and cost

To enrol a child in a Ticino public school the child must be resident in Ticino (so only for B-permit cross-border workers, not G). Enrolment opens in January-February. For G-permit children there are bilingual private schools (SEL Sorengo, Liceo di Lugano) charging CHF 15,000-35,000 per year. School meals cost about CHF 8-12 per meal, subsidies available for low-income families.

Frequently asked questions

When does school start in Ticino in 2026?
For the 2026-2027 school year, classes in all schools of Canton Ticino start on Monday 31 August 2026, according to the official DECS calendar. Some vocational schools with specific calendars are an exception.
When does school end in Ticino in 2027?
The 2026-2027 school year ends on Wednesday 16 June 2027 for all compulsory schools in Canton Ticino. The summer holidays therefore begin on Thursday 17 June 2027.
What are the Carnival holidays in Ticino in 2027?
The 2027 Carnival holidays in Ticino run from Saturday 6 February to Sunday 14 February 2027 (one week), according to the DECS calendar.
When are the 2027 Easter holidays for Ticino schools?
The 2027 Easter holidays in Canton Ticino schools run from Friday 26 March to Sunday 4 April 2027. Easter Sunday falls on 28 March 2027.
When are the Christmas and autumn holidays in Ticino 2026-2027?
The autumn holidays run from Saturday 31 October to Sunday 8 November 2026; the Christmas holidays from Thursday 24 December 2026 to Wednesday 6 January 2027. All dates are set by the DECS of Canton Ticino.
