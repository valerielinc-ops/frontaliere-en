2026-05 archive FAQ

How is the hourly mean for Ponte Tresa computed?

For every hour of the day we pool all TomTom samples taken in the 30 days of 2026-05 at that hour (one sample every 10–15 minutes during peak windows, every 30–60 minutes off peak) and average them. Hours without enough samples stay at "—" instead of surfacing an unreliable value.
